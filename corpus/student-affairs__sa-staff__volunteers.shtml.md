# Volunteers

## Volunteer Process

*This process is for Student Affairs departments only. Volunteers are managed by Risk Management and for further information please refer to the [Risk Management site.](https://www.cpp.edu/rms/risk-management-programs/volunteers.shtml)*

**Definition**

* A Volunteer is an individual who performs work or provides services without compensation for a semester, academic year, calendar year, session or any part thereof, not to exceed one year. Volunteers should not replace or supplant the work performed by employees (represented or non-represented) and may not be used in full-time, long-term assignments.

**Volunteer Information**

* All volunteers are required to fill out the [Volunteer Identification Form](https://www.cpp.edu/rms/volunteer-identification-form.pdf). This will include identifying a summary of assignments and duties.
* Volunteers are required to have a background check.
* Any volunteers driving on the behalf of the University are required to take [Defensive Driver Training](https://www.cpp.edu/rms/driving.shtml).

**All Volunteer Identification Forms must be kept on file.****Please submit completed form by clicking the button below:**

[Submit Completed Volunteer Identification Form](https://cpp.az1.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_1Us1ce6aWhtEgWG)
