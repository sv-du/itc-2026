# Alumni Stories

## Share Your Alumni Story

![A alumna poses with her kids at a college display](../img/2022-world-ag-expo-920x570.jpg)

Learn what's happening with your classmates and other Cal Poly Pomona alumni. Want to share your own class note? Simply email your latest events and achievements in your life since graduation to alumni@cpp.edu. Don't forget to include your most recent photo so we can post it along with your class note.

You can also visit our Impact Map to share your Cal Poly Pomona experience and to see how others are creating an impact around the world. [#WeAreCPP](https://weare.cpp.edu/submit-a-story/)

## Distinguished Alumni

![Alumnus Mike Mellano receives the Distinguished Alumni Award.](../img/2023-alumni-awards-mellano-920x570.jpg)

The Distinguished Alumni Awards are the highest honor presented by the Cal Poly Alumni Association. Selected through a highly competitive process, each recipient has made a lasting impact on society and Cal Poly Pomona through outstanding professional, philanthropic, or volunteer accomplishments.

* Learn more about the [**Distinguished Alumni Awards**](https://www.cpp.edu/alumni/outstanding-alumni/about-distinguished.shtml) criteria and nomination process.
* Each year, the Cal Poly Pomona community gathers to celebrate the achievements of our alumni community at an [**Alumni Awards Gala**](https://www.cpp.edu/alumni/outstanding-alumni/distinguishedalumnigala.shtml).
* Learn more about the Distinguished Alumni of Cal Poly Pomona!
  + [Current honorees](https://www.cpp.edu/alumni/outstanding-alumni/meet2021honorees.shtml)
  + [Don B. Huntley College of Agriculture recipients](https://www.cpp.edu/alumni/outstanding-alumni/agriculture-recipients.shtml)
  + [All past recipients](https://www.cpp.edu/alumni/outstanding-alumni/past-recipients.shtml)

At Cal Poly Pomona, we are always proud of the amazing work of our alumni. Our [**Alumni Spotlight**](https://www.cpp.edu/alumni/spotlight/) series highlights the work of individuals who are using their talents to make a difference in the world.

For more information, contact the Office of Alumni & External Relations at (909) 869-2963 or alumni@cpp.edu**.**
