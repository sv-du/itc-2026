# The Lyle Center Presents:

## A Green New Deal for Climate Justice

![](../../)

**Date:** November 04, 2020
**Time:** 02:30pm
**Location:** Virtual

![November 4th. 2:30pm.  A Green New Deal for Climate Justice.  Dr. Thea Riofrancos.  Cal Poly Pomona College of Environmental Design Lyle Center for Regenerative Studies.  RSVP to receive a link to attend for free.  Please email:  crs@cpp.edu](../img/a-green-new-deal.jpg)

*Lyle Center Guest Lecture: "A Green New Deal for Climate Justice" featuring Thea Riofrancos, Ph.D.*

The Lyle Center for Regenerative Studies invites the campus community to a virtual conversation with Thea Riofrancos, Ph.D.

An assistant professor of political science at Providence College in Rhode Island, Riofrancos is also an Andrew Carnegie Fellow (2020-22), co-author of “A Planet to Win: Why We Need a New Green Deal” and author of “Resource Radicals: From Petro-Nationalism to Post-Extractivism in Ecuador.” Her research focuses on resource extraction, renewable energy, climate change, green technology and social movements.

The event is free and open to the public. RSVP to crs@cpp.edu to receive the Zoom link.
