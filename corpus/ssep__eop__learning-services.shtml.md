# EOP Learning Center Services

## EOP Learning Center Services

The EOP Learning Center (EOPLC) provides an inclusive learning community and academic support by combining 1-1 tutoring and academic coaching through a holistic approach. We specialize in supporting students through learning, empowerment, and belonging.

### Tutoring Subjects

**Check [CPP Connect](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/cppconnect/index.shtml) to view available subjects and make your appointment today!**

The EOP Learning Center provides two types of academic support:

1. **Writing & Presentation Support**
2. **Course-Based Tutoring** for select courses like Biology, Business, Engineering and Spanish.

**Writing & Presentation Support**
Our Writing & Presentations Learning Strategists can help you strengthen your writing and communication skills for any class or project.

> You can meet with them for:
>
> * Academic papers and essays
> * Presentations and speeches
> * Scholarship or personal statements

*You do not need to be enrolled in a specific course to schedule these appointments.*

### **Course-Based Tutoring**

Our Course-Based Learning Strategists provide tutoring for select subjects such as Chemistry, Math, Physics, Engineering, and Business.
 To book a session:

* You must be enrolled in one of the supported courses listed below.
* You must match with a Learning Strategist’s availability on CPP Connect.

### Learning Strategist Schedule

### **How to Get Started**

* **Step 1:** Review our list of [**Tutoring Subjects**](img/documents-accessible/eoplc-availability-in-accessible.pdf)by Learning Strategists to find support that matches your needs.
* **Step 2:** Check the **[Learning Strategist Schedule](img/documents-accessible/eoplc-availability-in-accessible.pdf)** to see who’s available at a time that works for you.
* **Step 3: Make appointment on [CPP Connect](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/cppconnect/index.shtml).** Need help? Use our [**CPP Connect Guide**](img/documents-accessible/eoplc-cpp-connect-guide-accessible.pdf) for step-by-step instructions.

### What is an EOP Learning Strategist?

An EOP Learning Strategist is the official name of EOP tutors who are students equipped to provide guidance by building on a student's academic skills. EOP Learning Strategist's focus is not just on grades but on sharing the skills and knowledge needed to learn meaningfully and independently.

### 1-to-1 Appointments

You can book a meeting with an **EOP** **Learning Strategist**for 1-hour appointments on **[CPP Connect](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/cppconnect/index.shtml)**.

You can choose to meet in-person or online with a Learning Strategist.

* **In-person** meetings are in the**EOP Learning Center**in [Building 1, Room 200](https://www.cpp.edu/maps/) located on the second floor in Building 1.
* **Virtual** meetings are offered via Zoom.
* To book an appointment, check out our step-by-step [**CPP Connect Guide**](img/documents-accessible/eoplc-cpp-connect-guide-accessible.pdf).

You can make an appointment to meet with an EOP Learning Strategist for STEM course-based tutoring or choose to meet with a Writing and Presentations Learning Strategist. See [**EOP Tutoring Subjects**](img/documents-accessible/eoplc-availability-in-accessible.pdf) to view current list of course subjects we offer.

You must be enrolled in the course you currently seeking tutoring for. If you do not see a course you are seeking tutoring for, please feel free to contact us at eoplc@cpp.edu.

### Drop-in Session

**Drop by the EOP Learning Center located in Building 1, Room 200** **(2nd floor)** to meet with an EOP Learning Strategist (LS) or to find a quiet place to focus and get stuff done.

Visit **[EOP Tutoring Subjects & Learning Strategist Schedules](img/documents-accessible/eoplc-availability-in-accessible.pdf)** for a quick view of current course subjects we offer and the Learning Strategist schedule.

Drop-in sessions are currently available in-person on a first come first serve basis.

### How to Make an Appointment

* All EOP students have access to book an appointment through [**CPP Connect.**](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/cppconnect/index.shtml)
* **You can choose to meet in person or virtually with a Learning Strategist.**
* **Course Based Tutoring:** You must be enrolled in the course you currently seeking tutoring for or it may not appear in CPP Connect as an option.
* **Writing and Presentations Tutoring:** All students can access tutoring for general assistants with writing and presentations. No specific course is required.
* Check out our step-by-step [**EOPLC CPP Connect Guide**](img/documents-accessible/eoplc-cpp-connect-guide-accessible.pdf).

**Need a subject you don't see?**Please email us at eoplc@cpp.edu to request tutoring for a specific subject.
