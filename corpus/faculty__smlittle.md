## About Me

Dr. Little received his Ph.D. in Applied Math and Computer Science from the School of Business and Technology, Northcentral University. He completed an Advanced Computer Security Professional from Stanford, Project Management Program at Cal Tech, Masters Learning Technology from Western Governor’s Teacher’s College, and holds a B.S. in Economics from Cal Poly Pomona and a B.A. in Applied Mathematics.

His teaching interests are operations research, statistics, analytics, and machine learning-artificial intelligence. He has presented research talks and papers at mathematics and scientific conferences. In addition, he currently works as a Senior Data Scientist at Edison Cal Poly Innovation Village.
