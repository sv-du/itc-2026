## Recent Scholarly Activities

* Aragon, Corwin. 2019. “Global Gender Justice and Epistemic Oppression: A Response to an Epistemic Dilemma.” *Feminist Philosophy Quarterly* 5 (2). <https://doi.org/10.5206/fpq/2019.2.7294>.
* Aragon, Corwin, and Alison M. Jaggar. 2018. “Agency, Complicity, and the Responsibility to Resist Structural Injustice.” *Journal of Social Philosophy* 49 (3): 439–60. <https://doi.org/10.1111/josp.12251>.
* “Building a Case for Social Justice: Situating Case Studies in Nonideal Theory” (forthcoming in an edited volume)
* “Resisting and Resistant Amusement” (forthcoming in an edited volume)
* Works in progress: “Faces of Epistemic Oppression,” “Responsibility for Migration Injustice,” “The Moral Basis of Structural Responsibility,” “The Politics of Racial Humor,”  *Seeking Justice in an Unjust World* (book-length manuscript)

## Research Areas

* Social and political philosophy (including issues of global justice)
* Normative ethics
* Feminist philosophy
* Critical philosophy of race
