# Alumni Success Stories

![Fabiola Perez](img/2017-fabiola-perez-920x920.jpg)

## Alumni Success Story

> “Working in a global company entails working with people from different cultures all over the world. At Cal Poly Pomona, there is a lot of diversity, which has helped me in my current role, especially my travels.”

Fabiola Perez ('17, agribusiness and food industry management, plant science)
global planters produce planner, John Deere

![Andrea Zuniga](img/2024-andrea-zuniga-920x920.jpg)

## Alumni Success Story

> “My agribusiness and food industry management major has been truly transformative, molding my path to success and enriching my perception of the indispensable role that food plays in our lives."

Andrea Zuniga ('16, agribusiness & food industry management)
learning and development consultant, Acosta Group

![Garrett Matt](img/2024-garrett-matt-920x920.jpg)

## Alumni Success Story

> “With a Cal Poly Pomona education as an agribusiness and food industry management major student and 15+ years developing high-tech vehicles, I’m able to manage teams to bring napkin sketch ag tech ideas into reality.”

Garrett Matt ('06, agribusiness & food industry management)
engineering manager, Blue River Technology

![Heather Palmer](img/2024-heather-palmer-920x920.jpg)

## Alumni Success Stories

> “My advice to students would be to stay open about your career path and try new things. My favorite thing about the industry I work in is the people.”

Heather Palmer ('08, agribusiness & food industry management)
director of sales, San Agrow

![Tatiana Assemian](img/2023-tatiana-assemian-920x920.jpg)

> “I have always loved that Cal Poly Pomona has a ‘learn-by-doing’ model and culture. I was able to deepen the discovery of my interests and gain a level of confidence that I’ve carried with me into the marketplace upon graduation.”

Tatiana Assemian (’11, agribusiness and food industry management)
distributor sales rep, Nestlé
