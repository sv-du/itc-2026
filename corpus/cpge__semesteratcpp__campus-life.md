## On Campus Activities

Cal Poly Pomona offers many opportunities for students to participate in academic, social, sporting and physical fitness activities.

These include:

* [Cal Poly Pomona Library](http://www.cpp.edu/~library/index.shtml)
* [Campus Dining](https://www.cpp.edu/~aboutcpp/visitor-information/dining.shtml)
* [Student Health Center\*](http://www.cpp.edu/~healthcounseling/)
* [Bronco Student Center](http://asi.cpp.edu/services/bronco-student-center/)
* [NEW! Bronco Recreation and Intramural Complex (BRIC)\*](http://asi.cpp.edu/campusrec/)
* [Intramural Sports Leagues\*](http://asi.cpp.edu/campusrec/programs/intramural-sports/)
* [Swimming Pool\*](https://asi.cpp.edu/campusrec/facilities/indooroutdoor-facilities/pool/)
* [Recreational, social, and cultural organizations](https://calpolypomona.campuslabs.com/engage/)
* [University & Foundation Housing Services](http://www.cpp.edu/~housing/)
* [University Computer Labs](http://libanswers.library.cpp.edu/faq/126933)

*\*Additional fees may apply for using these facilities/services.*
