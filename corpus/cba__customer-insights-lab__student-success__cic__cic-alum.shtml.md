# Customer Insights Consultancy Program

![Consulting class](../../img/banners/banner-consulting-class.jpg)

Since 2012, students who have taken our client consulting classes have been hired by world-class market research companies such as Kantar Research and Lieberman Research Worldwide, in part, thanks to the hands-on nature of the program. For this program, we select a small number of promising students. Outstanding students with strong detail-orientation and excellent communication skills are strongly encouraged to contact the professors with (1) resume with GPA, (2) a cover letter stating why taking this class will enhance your career preparation, and (3) a writing sample. Applicants will be vetted and admitted based on merit. [Click here to find out where our graduates are](https://www.cpp.edu/cba/customer-insights-lab/student-success/outcomes/placement-record.shtml).
