# Injury Response Procedure

![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white-mobile.svg)
![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white.svg)

## Injury Response Procedures

At Cal Poly Pomona, the safety and well-being of our campus community are our top priorities. Our Injury Response Procedures provide a clear and effective framework for managing injuries that may arise on campus. This comprehensive plan details the necessary steps to ensure a prompt and coordinated response from trained personnel. By promoting a proactive approach to injury management, we strive to minimize risks, create a safe learning environment, and equip all community members with the knowledge and preparedness to respond effectively in emergencies.

![Warehouse Worker Injury Response ](../img/injury-response-warehouse-worker-accident.jpeg)

## Role

### Students

If a student is seriously injured or ill and needs medical assistance please follow the procedure below. The student should always be referred to Student Health Services (SHS) for professional evaluation.

1. **Get Help -**Dial 911 FROM ANY LANDLINE PHONE. If using a cell phone in an emergency, dial (909) 869-**3070**.
2. **Student Health Services** - [Student Health Services](https://www.cpp.edu/health/index.shtml) is used for the student's initial treatment when possible.
   * Student must be currently enrolled in classes.
     + If not enrolled you must seek your personal medical provider for further assistance.
     + Student Health Services is open Mon – Fri, 8AM – 5PM. If injury is after hours you may call the After Hours Nurse Advice Line (855) 272-1723
3. **Reporting** - The supervisor in charge of the area must file a [Report Safety Concern](https://app.smartsheet.com/b/form/96e7d42644924b319280fb55742f97f1). Contact EH&S if you need any assistance (909-869-**4697**).

### Staff/Employee/Volunteer

Staff/Employee/Volunteer who sustain injuries while at work should report the incident to their supervisor as soon as possible. Worker’s compensation benefits are designed to provide you with medical treatment you need to recover from your work-related injury or illness. Please follow the procedures below:

1. **Get Help -**Dial 911 FROM ANY LANDLINE PHONE. If using a cell phone in an emergency, dial (909) 869-**3070**.
2. **Report Injury to EH&S** – Dial (909) 869- **4697**.
   * Cal/OSHA regulations require that employers must immediately report any work-related death or serious injury or illness. Immediately means as soon as possible but no longer than 8 hours after the employer knows or with diligent inquiry would have known of the death or serious injury or illness.
3. **Notify your supervisor** - The supervisor in charge of the area must file a Manager’s Report of Injury. Get help from department administration or the EH&S office if necessary. Turn in the completed form to your Human Resources department.
4. **Questions** - Please contact the CPP State Workers' Compensation office via workerscomp@cpp.edu or call (909)-869-**3725** for assistance or if you have any questions about work-related injuries or illnesses.

**State Workers' Compensation**: More information can be found [here](https://www.cpp.edu/eoda-hr/departments/wc/index.shtml).

**Foundation Workers' Compensation**: More information can be found [here](https://foundation.cpp.edu/es/safety-workers-compensation.aspx).

**ASI Workers' Compensation**: More information can be found[here.](https://asi.cpp.edu/facilities/bric/bric-info/)
