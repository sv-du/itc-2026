# STEM Student Success

## Mentoring Opportunities

[### Achieve Scholars

Designed to help students in all majors in the areas of research readiness, mentorship, and academic success through the help of dedicated faculty advising and peer mentoring. Click to learn more!](https://www.cpp.edu/our-cpp/students/asp/index.shtml "opens a new window")

[### BASES

The BASES program builds a community of support for Black/African American student scholars by providing financial, academic, and mentoring resources. Click to learn more!](https://www.cpp.edu/sci/bases/index.shtml "opens a new window")

[### Cal-Bridge

Cal-Bridge creates opportunities for historically underrepresented to participate and advance in STEM fields including physics, astronomy, computer science, and computer engineering, to increase their numbers in PhD programs. Click to learn more!](https://www.cpp.edu/calbridge/index.shtml "opens a new window")

[### McNair Scholars Program

The Cal Poly Pomona McNair Scholars Program prepares eligible undergraduate students for PhD programs by promoting student success through undergraduate research, faculty mentorship, and a series of skill building workshops. The program aims to increase the number of first-generation, low-income and/or underrepresented students in PhD programs. Click to learn more!](https://www.cpp.edu/mcnair/index.shtml "opens a new window")

[### MEP

MEP students have access to Academic Advising, Industry Events, Peer Mentoring, Scholarships, Supplemental Instruction, Tutoring, Workshops. College of Science majors are welcome to attend their tutoring. Click to learn more!](https://www.cpp.edu/engineering/mep/index.shtml "opens a new window")

[### SEES

SEES, or Science Educational Enhancement Services, offers a range of opportunities designed to help students succeed in their majors and future careers. Through community building, mentoring, professional development, and academic and financial support, SEES empowers students to progress and thrive. Click to learn more!](https://www.cpp.edu/sci/sees/index.shtml "opens a new window")

[### Student Innovation Idea Labs

The iLab has also evolved into a space for students to work on class projects, attend workshops and seminars as well as work on their startup or creative projects.](https://www.cpp.edu/ilab/ "opens a new window")

## Homework Help & Learning Assistant Program

[### Learning Assistant Program

The Biology Learning Assistant (Bio-LA) Program is dedicated to helping biology students succeed and feel like they belong at Cal Poly and in the biology major. Bio-LAs help facilitate student learning in the classroom and serve as peer-tutors in their dedicated classrooms to build their training as future middle and high school biology teachers.](https://www.cpp.edu/sci/cemast/lap/index.shtml "opens a new window")
