# Freshmen Orientation Details

## Fall 2026 Admits

**Orientation reservations for Fall 2026 Admits will be available beginning April 2, 2026.**

Orientation is a mandatory enrollment requirement. Please pay close attention to all Orientation details and deadlines as failure to complete your orientation requirement will result in your admission being withdrawn.

## Orientation Components

[### Make Your Reservation

As a Fall 2026 admit, you will be able to make your Orientation reservation via the Bronco Applicant Portal beginning April 2, 2026. Orientation day will be held in-person. You will receive written communication about Orientation via your CPP email address. Please continue to check your email daily.](https://applicant.cpp.edu "opens a new window")

[### Pre-Orientation Modules

The CPP pre-orientation modules will be available to you prior to your Orientation day. You must complete the first two modules before your Orientation day in order for your Orientation Registration (ROS) hold to be removed and allow for course registration.](https://orientation.cpp.edu/)

### Attend Orientation Day

Attend your Orientation Day! At your orientation, you will meet other new students, get connected with campus resources and register for your first-semester courses.

## Why Do I Need to Attend Orientation?

* Orientation prepares you for academic success by introducing you to faculty and staff, teaching you about registration tools and academic culture, and connecting you to important academic support resources.
* Orientation helps you connect to the campus community by providing opportunities to meet other students.
* Orientation empowers you to be an active learner by teaching you about CPP policies, introducing you to resources to help when times are tough, and giving you the tools you need to become a critical thinker.
