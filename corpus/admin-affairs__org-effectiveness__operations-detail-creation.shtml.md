# Operations Detail Creation

![](pictures/opsde.jpg)**Creating detailed operational records for departmental processes is crucial for ensuring consistency, efficiency, and accountability across the Division of Administrative Affairs. These records serve as a foundational blueprint that guides daily operations, facilitates training, and supports the seamless transition of staff.**

**One of the responsibilities of Operational Excellence (OE) is to provide Administrative Affairs Departments with tools and resources for process improvements. As part of this effort, OE has established an Operations Detail Creation Process to facilitate the creation and retention of Divisional operations details.**

## Operations Detail Creation Process

**Review the operations detail creation process by downloading the following PDF: OE-01, Operations Detail Creation Process (PDF)**

![](pictures/additionalresources.jpg)

## Supporting Documentation

**The Operations Detail Request Form requires end users to provide any supporting documents including guides, manuals, standard operating procedures (SOPs), operations detail drafts, memos, notes, or other resources pertaining to the topic of the operations detail.**

**Requesting departments should submit a preliminary draft with their request form. Preliminary drafts should follow the CPP Operations Detail Template. An example of the CPP Operations Detail Template can be downloaded by clicking the link below.**

[**CPP Operations Detail Template [DOCX]**](operationsdetails/opsdetailtemplate00.docx)

**If inadequate information and/or documentation is provided, the Divisional Policy Analyst will email the Requestor/Primary Contact to request additional information.**

![](pictures/1002149.png)

## Operations Detail Request Form

**For assistance in creating a departmental operations detail, please fill out all of the information in the Operations Detail Request Form.**

**The operations detail creation process can be lengthy and the priority of a request may change depending on Divisional needs. Please submit requests a minimum of 1 month before their due dates. Requestors will be kept informed of any updates/status changes to their request.**

**To go to the Operations Detail Request Form, click the link below.**

**[Operations Detail Request Form](https://app.smartsheet.com/b/form/955c09f7434a4ade945400450831a460)**
