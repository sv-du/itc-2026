# UB | UBMS Services

![Decorative Banner](../../img/banner.png)

**Program services consist of:** leadership and academic ownership workshops, prescriptive advising sessions, tutoring and mentoring components, family involvement and support system development, summer residential element, and other services that support students’ goals of attending a college or university.

![](../../img/satacad_1.jpg)

## Saturday Academies

Program scholars participate in workshops covering essential topics such as college applications, financial literacy, funding higher education, goal setting, leadership development, life skills, time management, effective study techniques, and hands-on career exploration opportunities such as robotics.

[Links to register](https://www.cpp.edu/pre-college/ub-classic/saturday-academy.shtml "Links to register. ")

## Advising

Program Coordinators/Advisors provide students with guidance and assistance in crafting college preparatory high school programs tailored to bolster academic performance and optimize their college admissions applications.

[Make an appointment](https://www.cpp.edu/pre-college/ub-classic/services1/schools.shtml "Make an appointment. ")

![](../../img/advising.jpg)

![](../../img/tutoring_1.jpg)

## Study Hall

These sessions provide students with a conducive environment for studying, where college tutors and mentors empower students to excel in their coursework and achieve academic success.

[Dates | Times | Locations](https://www.cpp.edu/pre-college/resources/studyhall.jpg "Dates | Times | Locations. ")

## Family Involvement

Quarterly meetings designed to empower families with comprehensive insights into the college application process, equipping them with working knowledge of college admissions procedures, financial aid options, academic readiness, and supporting their student through the high school journey.

[Events Calendar](https://www.cpp.edu/pre-college/ub-classic/resources/ub-calendar.shtml "Events Calendar. ")

![](../../img/parent_1.jpg)

![](../../img/summer_2023.jpg)

## Summer Program

The 6-week residential program at Cal Poly Pomona offers structured support, enhancing students' academic readiness for the next school year. Activities include rigorous courses and diverse recreational outings such as campus visits, museum tours, and community events.

[Summer Video](https://www.cpp.edu/pre-college/ub-classic/services1/ub-summer.shtml "Summer Video. ")
