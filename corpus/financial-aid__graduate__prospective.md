# Graduate Students

## Prospective Graduate Students

Pursuing a graduate education is a valuable investment in your future, and Cal Poly Pomona is here to support you every step of the way. We offer a variety of financial aid options to help cover expenses, from tuition to full cost of attendance, so you can focus on your studies.

This site will guide you through available financial aid options, eligibility requirements, and the application process. We also provide information on the average costs of a graduate degree at Cal Poly Pomona to help you understand your investment.

Our team is ready to assist with any questions—don’t hesitate to reach out!

## 2026-27 FAFSA and CADAA are Now Open - Apply Today!

The **2026-27 [FAFSA](https://studentaid.gov/h/apply-for-aid/fafsa) and [CADAA](https://dream.csac.ca.gov/landing)** are now available. These are the applications for the 2026-27 academic year (Fall 2026, Spring 2027, Summer 2027).

**Priority Deadline**: **9pm, March 2, 2026**

Broncos become by Doing - which includes securing your financial aid.

![2026-27 FAFSA and CADAA are now open - apply by 9pm on March 2, 2026 for Fall 2026 and Spring 2027 financial aid](../../img/fafsa-cadaa-homepage.png)

**Still need to apply for aid for 2025-26?**

* **[FAFSA](https://studentaid.gov/h/apply-for-aid/fafsa)**
* [**Dream Act Application**](https://dream.csac.ca.gov/)

Students who haven't yet submitted a FAFSA or CADAA are still encouraged to apply for aid for the 2025-26 academic year (through June 30, 2026). Keep in mind you may not be eligible for certain types of aid (including CPP or CA state-funded aid) since the priority deadline for 2025-26 has passed.
