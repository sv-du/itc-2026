# College of Science Advising Center

![Three students walk by Biotechnology building](img/science-largehero.png)

## About Us

We help undergraduate College of Science students with:

* General Academic Advising & Course Selection (GE & Core)
* Registration and Graduation Questions
* Petition Assistance & Information
* Degree Progress Review (DPR) Review
* Transfer Credit Review (TCR) Review
* Graduation Pledge Advising
* Academic Planner Review
* Academic Warning, Academic Notice and Academic Notice with Contract Advising
* Resource Referral

## Get In Touch with Us

[### Schedule An Appointment on CPP Connect

We offer appointments virtually or in-person. Appointments are scheduled in 30-minute increments with your assigned advisor. You may not be able to switch from an in-person appointment to a virtual appointment after scheduling. If you are more than 10 minutes late, you will need to reschedule. Appointments can be scheduled no more than 2 weeks into the future. If we are fully booked, please check on Friday afternoons for new appointments and/or check for cancellations.](#modal-0 "CPP Connect Appointment Scheduling Links")

### Meet with a Peer Advisor

Knowledgeable Student Success Ambassadors are available weekdays to provide in-person peer mentoring and answer basic questions. Visit the center to meet with one.
