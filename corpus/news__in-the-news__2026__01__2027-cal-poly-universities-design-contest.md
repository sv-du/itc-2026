# Design the next award-winning Cal Poly Universities Rose Parade float

Posted on January 30, 2026

Cal Poly Pomona and Cal Poly San Luis Obispo have opened a design contest for the 2027 Rose Parade float, inviting the public to submit original concepts for the next student‑built entry by Feb. 5 — with the winner’s idea showcased before millions of viewers and a choice of parade tickets or a $500 prize. The call for designs follows the Cal Poly Universities team’s historic Sweepstakes Award win for their 2026 float, “Jungle Jumpstart.” **Allyson Jane Castillo** (‘26, mechanical engineering), president of the CPP Rose Float team, is quoted in the article.

This article was originally published by [LAist](https://laist.com/news/los-angeles-activities/pasadena-rose-parade-cal-poly-pomona-san-luis-obispo-float-design-contest) on January 30, 2026.
