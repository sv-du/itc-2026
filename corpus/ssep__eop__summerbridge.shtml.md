# Summer Bridge

## About Summer Bridge (SB)

Summer Bridge is a ***FREE*** comprehensive, 3-week EOP summer transition program designed to assist incoming EOP first-year students in their transition from high school to the university setting by providing a wide range of educational, personal, social, and cultural enrichment opportunities.

EOP students ARRM yourself for the college challenge and join us for this once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to Connect, Learn, and Grow!

**Summer Bridge 2026 Program Dates:**

**Monday, July 6, 2026 through Thursday, July 23, 2026**

*(3-week program; Sunday - Thursday)*

**NOTE: Fall 2026 Incoming EOP students will be emailed an invitation in early May with additional details regarding Summer Bridge 2026.***Summer Bridge spaces are limited. To be considered students are encouraged to apply before June 1st at 12:00pm (noon). Once spaces are filled, applicants will be placed on our waitlist.*

**Transfer Students:** If you are an incoming EOP transfer student, check out our[EOP Transfer Bridge program](https://www.cpp.edu/ssep/eop/transferbridge.shtml), which specifically designed for you!

## Program Model

**Program Schedule:** Summer Bridge (SB) offers a structured program schedule that begins with students checking-in on Sundays. From Monday to Thursday, students participate in various activities to learn more about campus services, access program resources, and connect with peers and staff, helping them build their network at Cal Poly Pomona. On Thursdays, students check out and leave for the weekend.

**To give students a comprehensive experience of both living and commuting to campus, our 3-week program is divided into two components and must participate in both components:**

1. **Residential Component (Weeks 1 & 2):** Students live in the residence halls under the supervision of the SB residence life team.
2. **Commuter Component (Week 3):** Students commute to and from their residence, allowing them to experience what commuting or taking public transportation will be like in preparation for the academic year.

**Weekend Stayover and Extended Stay Request:** In the SB application, students can share any special circumstances that might prevent them from going home for the weekend or commuting during the last week of the program, such as living outside the local area.

Please note, resources are limited and not all Weekend Stayover or Extended Stay requests will be approved.

* Be as detailed as possible in the SB application to help staff address your needs.
* If admitted to SB but not approved for Weekend or Extended stay, students will receive information on resources, such as how to access a bus pass.

**Closed Program:** For the safety of our community, SB is a closed program. No guests are allowed during the entire 3 weeks of SB, except during check-in and check-out times or with prior professional staff approval.

### Why Participate in Summer Bridge?

×

**Program Goals:** Summer Bridge is designed to assist participants with getting a head start in their college journey, building support systems with faculty, staff, and peers, and gaining essential skills that will facilitate their transition and success at Cal Poly Pomona.

**Program Cost:** **FREE** to the student. The program offers a range of resources and support services at no cost to students, as fees are covered by university funding. To fully benefit from SB, we encourage students to actively engage in all that SB has to offer.

*"One thing I’ll be walking away with by participating in the SB program is confidence. In the beginning I felt very anxious to start college and lacked resources and now I feel more prepared to start college." - SB 2025 Participant*

Program Benefits Include: Educational Support, Student Development, Community and Social Events, and Cultural and Academic Enrichment Opportunities.

[### Summer Bridge Eligibility](#modal-1 "How am I eligible for Summer Bridge?")

### How am I eligible for Summer Bridge?

×

Admitted incoming EOP first-year students are invited to learn more about Summer Bridge via email in early May and are provided the Summer Bridge Information Module. Only after the student has completed the SB Information Module, they are provided with additional information regarding the SB program application process.

Selection to Summer Bridge is based on students responses and timely submission of required documents. To be fully considered, all CPP, EOP, and SB deadlines must be met.

**ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA**

1. **Submit EOP Partnership Agreement**
   1. If admitted to EOP at Cal Poly Pomona, you will receive an email notification with instructions on how to submit your EOP Partnership Agreement (EPA). In order to be invited to Summer Bridge, students must be fully admitted to EOP and meet all EOP program deadlines.
      * *If you have any questions regarding your EOP admissions status, please email eopadmin@cpp.edu. Provide your 9-digit Bronco ID number and full name in the email.*
2. **Be available to attend all SB program dates**
   1. **SB 2026 Program Dates: July 6th - July 23rd (Sunday - Thursday)**
      * SB is a structured program, where students must live on campus during the Residential Component (Week 1 & Week 2: July 6th - July 16th) and commute during the Commuter Component (Week 3: July 20th - July 23rd) and must be fully committed to our program partnership, as failure to do so may result in program dismissal.
        + ***No Excused Absences:** Students will not be excused for any personal appointments and are encouraged to plan ahead of time to be present during all 3-weeks of SB.*
        + ***Attend a [Freshmen Orientation](https://www.cpp.edu/orientation/freshmen/start-here.shtml) date before the start of SB 2026 (July 6, 2026).** During Orientation Day students get to register for Fall classes, so it is recommended to attend one of the earliest available dates.*
3. **Complete Summer Bridge Information Module**
   1. **Deadline to complete Information Module: May 31, 2026 at 12:00pm (Noon)**
      * Access to SB Application will be given once the module is completed.
4. **Complete Summer Bridge Application**
   1. **Deadline to complete application: May 31, 2026 at 11:59pm (Midnight)**
      * Applicants must meet all university and EOP deadlines to be considered for acceptance into SB.

**PROVISIONALLY ADMITTANCE NOTIFICATION**

Provisionally admitted students will be notified by early June via their CPP and Personal email address. If admitted, students must do the following:

1. **Complete SB Partnership Agreement form:** In this form students can indicate whether they *accept* or *decline* their attendance to the SB program.
   * Only students who complete this step will be officially admitted as part of the SB 2026 cohort*.*
