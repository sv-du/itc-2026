# Studio 6

Studio 6, a service of CAFE, is an updated computing lab open to faculty and staff for [Learning Technology](../teaching-and-learning/learning-technologies.shtml) training and assistance.

Students should visit or contact the [IT Service Desk](https://www.cpp.edu/it/help/)

![retro phone](../img/icons/icon-classicphone.svg)

## Contact Us

**Submit a Ticket:** [Canvas request form](https://cpp.service-now.com/ehelp?id=sc_cat_item&sys_id=df0d4725dbffa0d0f0eed2e3ca9619b7&sysparm_category=d6f972c313ab130027a43ff18144b0a4)

**Email1:** studio6@cpp.edu | canvas@cpp.edu

**Phone2:** 909-869-3099, Mon - Fri, 8am - 5pm

**Location:** [Building 1](https://www.cpp.edu/maps/?id=1130#!m/276179), Room 205C

**Walk-ins:** Walk-in services are generally available Mon - Fri, 8am - 5pm, during fall and spring semester. Studio 6 has reduced walk-in hours during semester breaks and summer semester. We are currently short-staffed, so we recommend calling ahead to confirm staff are available.

**Appointments:** In-person and virtual appointments are available Mon - Fri, 8am - 5pm. Please call us during business hours or email us at studio6@cpp.edu to request and appointment.

1. Messages sent to these email addresses are automatically converted to ServiceNow tickets.
2. Calls are limited to 45 minutes.

## Multimedia Computing Lab

Our Studio 6 facility has Mac and PC computers, scanners (flatbed and slide), and printers, all available for use during in-person hours. See a list of software installed on our lab computers at our [Learning Technologies](../teaching-and-learning/learning-technologies.shtml) page.

Laptops, microphones, iClicker instructor kits, graphic tablets, and more are also available for check-out two weeks at a time.

Our technologies support accessibility for all users, including screen reading software, Optical Character Recognition (OCR) scanners, and an ADA-compliant, adjustable desk.

## Assistance with Learning Technologies

Knowledgeable Instructional Technology Assistants (ITAs) are available to provide support for a variety [Learning Technologies](../teaching-and-learning/learning-technologies.shtml).

For help with a pedagogical problem, a new teaching idea, or (re)designing a course using technology (including converting to an online or hybrid format), [request instructional design consultation](https://cpp.service-now.com/ehelp?id=sc_cat_item&sys_id=c2cfa1e26fe18b007996073aea3ee47e) with CAFE.
