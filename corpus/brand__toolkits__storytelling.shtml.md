# Storytelling Toolkit

## Storytelling Toolkit

[### Brand Guidelines

The Brand Guidelines provide rules and recommendations to craft a compelling and consistent experience that can be applied across all messaging and brand touchpoints.](https://www.cpp.edu/brand/authenticated/cpp-brand-guidelines.pdf)

[### Brand Quick Guide

Need a quick summary? The Brand Quick Guide provides a snapshot of the highlights of our Cal Poly Pomona brand.](https://cpp.canto.com/v/brand/landing?viewIndex=2)

[### Canto

Find official photography and images in Canto, our digital asset management system.](https://cpp.canto.com/allfiles?viewIndex=0)

[### College Branding Platform

This platform is a source of language applicable to each of Cal Poly Pomona’s colleges that aligns with the CPP Brand Platform and the Become by Doing campaign. This guide is meant to be a flexible tool that can guide your communications for different audiences.](https://www.cpp.edu/brand/authenticated/cal-poly-pomona-college-branding-platforms.pdf)

[### CPP Editorial Stylebook

To promote consistency and professionalism, the university’s Editorial Stylebook (PDF) provides guidance on written communications for general, external communications.](https://www.cpp.edu/brand/pdfs/cpp-stylebook.pdf)

[### CSU Editorial Style Guide

This guide provides recommended usage on words, phrases and punctuation when writing about and for the university. If a style is not noted, please refer to the Associated Press Stylebook.](https://www.calstate.edu/csu-system/csu-branding-standards/editorial-style-guide)

[### Op-ed Guidelines

Op-eds provide an opportunity for a writer to bring their experience, ideas, critical analyses, and insights to a larger audience. Our publications may publish op-eds from our community that support, illustrate or provide further insight into the university’s vision, mission, values and initiatives.](https://www.cpp.edu/stratcomm/op-ed-guidelines.shtml)
