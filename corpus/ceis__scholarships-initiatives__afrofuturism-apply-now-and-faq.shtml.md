# Applying and FAQ

## Applying & Requirements

### Where do I apply?

We are not accepting applications at this time. Keep an eye out in Fall 2026 for Cohort 4 applications.

### Who Can Apply?

The fellowship is available for those enrolled in CEIS academic programs including undergraduate, credential, master's, and doctoral levels. It welcomes participants from all backgrounds, regardless of race, sex, color, ethnicity, or national origin.

### Why should I apply?

If you're passionate about creativity, social change, and reimagining the future, the CEIS Afrofuturism Initiative offers a unique space to explore bold ideas, create interdisciplinary projects, and join a supportive community of change makers.

### Requirements

2.0 or Higher Cumulative GPA, enrolled part-time or full-time.
If accepted you must participate in community based meetings and project-based learning.

### Fellowship Benefits

Up to $5,000 over two semesters, leadership development, peer and faculty mentorship, and network opportunities.
