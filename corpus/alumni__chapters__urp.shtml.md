# Urban and Regional Planning Chapter

![group at dinner gathered around rectangular table](../img/urp-aboutpic.jpg)

## Mission Statement

The Urban and Regional Planning Alumni Chapter is dedicated to strengthening the connections between alumni and students, faculty, staff, industries and organizations that support those who work in planning. Chartered in 1994, the chapter was formed to assist alumni in furthering their personal and professional growth and development.
