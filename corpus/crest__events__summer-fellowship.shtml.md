# Summer Fellowship

![Three students working in the lab](../img/utrgvrailway.jpg)

## Summer 2026

### Summer Student Exchange Program

Funded by the National Science Foundation (Award #2514616), the CREST-RASM Center at Cal Poly Pomona is offering a Summer Student Exchange Program for CPP STEM students at The University Texas Rio Grande Valley. This 10-week fellowship will select three students to develop their expertise in emerging STEM fields.

[Apply Online](https://cpp.az1.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_7USfyrYCgqqJ16C%20 "opens a new window")

### When and Where

Ten weeks during the summer at The University Texas Rio Grande Valley.

[![Student at UTRGV posing](../img/utrgv-iam.jpg)

### What You Get

Selected students will receive a $8,200 in scholarship to support their participation in the program.](#modal-0 "Scholarship Information")

### Scholarship Information

×

Students are responsible for arranging their own lodging, airfare, and meals, allowing flexibility in planning their stay.

Scholarship funds are distributed in two parts:

* 50% awarded before travel, and
* 50% awarded upon successful completion of the fellowship.

Close

![Professor and student doing railway safety](../img/utrgv-railway-safety.jpg)

### What to Expect

This 10-week fellowship provides practical research experience, professional development, and opportunities to contribute to significant projects—helping students grow their skills, build collaborations, and prepare for future STEM careers.

## Texas Research Centers

### The University Texas Rio Grande Valley

Participants will have the opportunity to choose between two leading research centers at The University of Texas Rio Grande Valley. Both centers are part of the collaborative partnership providing students with hands-on research experience guided by expert faculty and researchers.

### Requirements

### Application Deadline: January 30th

* Applicants must complete and upload one **Statement of Purpose**
  + Write a one page describing how the Summer Fellowship will contribute to your academic and career goals (maximum 500 words).
* Applicants must upload one **Professional Reference** letter
* Applicants are expected to have a GPA of 3.0 or higher
* Applicants must be a U.S. citizen or permanent resident

Selected individuals will be notified by **February 15th**
