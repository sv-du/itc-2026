# Asphalt Repair on Two Roads to Start on Wednesday, Feb. 26

**![](https://www.cpp.edu/compass/img/mansion-lane.jpg)**

**Summary of Disruption:**Asphalt repair on two roads will commence on Wednesday, Feb. 26. The repairs are expected to be completed by Friday, Feb. 28. Repair work is scheduled from 6 a.m. to 3 p.m. each day.

Asphalt repairs will occur at:

**Mansion Lane:** Vehicle traffic will not be allowed on Feb. 26 and Feb. 27 at the upper section of the road leading to Kellogg House (Building 112). Portions of the road starting at University Drive also will be repaired. Limited vehicle traffic will be allowed on the lower section of Mansion Lane. (See map for repair areas marked in red.)

**Cypress Lane:** A section of the road in front of Police and Parking Services (Building 109) and adjacent to Parking Lot F5 will undergo repairs during the three-day project. Vehicle traffic and parking access could be limited by the repair work.

**Reason for Disruption:** Allow contractor crews to perform asphalt repairs on sections of the roads.

**How does this affect me?** Vehicle traffic will be blocked or limited on Mansion Lane. Parking spaces on the lower portion of Mansion Lane and adjacent to Cypress Lane could be obstructed.

**What do I need to do?** Use caution when approaching the repair sites. Please share this information with others who may have not received this message.

**For any questions or more information, please contact:**Stan Suh at Ext. 6706 or sjsuh@cpp.edu.
