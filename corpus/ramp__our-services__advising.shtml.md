# Advising

## Helping You Navigate CPP with Confidence

![Students studying in library](../img/study-ramp.png)

RAMP maintains two full-time supplemental academic advisors. In addition to determining individualized plans for tutorial sessions, the advisors are available to RAMP participants to help with a variety of concerns or questions students might have.

If you have questions about financial aid, graduate school opportunities, add/drop procedures, or other Cal Poly Pomona student and academic services, RAMP staff will help you discover the answers and refer you to other campus agencies when appropriate. Our goal is to help you have the best possible experience at Cal Poly Pomona.

[Make an Appointment on CPP Connect](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/cppconnect/index.shtml)
