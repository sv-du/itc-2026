## Guided by Purpose. Inspired by Possibility.

**![Professionals planning in collaboration](../img/mission_and_vision_img_1.jpg)**

At Cal Poly Pomona’s College of Professional and Global Education, Workforce Innovation & Career Engagement (WICE) exists to bridge **education** and **industry** through *purpose-driven* collaboration. Our mission and vision reflect a shared commitment to empowering people, strengthening organizations, and shaping a future where learning and work move hand in hand. Together with our partners, we’re building pathways that turn potential into progress and ideas into impact.

![decorative image](../img/our_mission.jpg)

## Our Mission

### Why We Exist

To advance equitable access to high-impact career pathways by fostering strategic industry partnerships, delivering responsive, employer-informed training and upskilling, and facilitating meaningful employment outcomes that promote lifelong learning, economic mobility, and student success.

![Excited professionals during training](../img/what_we_aspire_to_become.jpg)

## Our Vision

### What We Aspire to Become

A dynamic hub that connects students, employers, and industries to advance workforce readiness and drive regional economic growth.

## What We Do

### By Design

WICE designs and delivers programs that meet the needs of today’s workforce while preparing for tomorrow’s opportunities.

#### Workforce Upskilling & Reskilling

Industry-informed solutions that strengthen the region’s talent pipeline.

#### Career Engagement

Partnerships that connect Cal Poly Pomona students with paid, career-relevant experiences.

#### Training and Development Collaboration

Customized programs for employers through California’s Employer Training Panel (ETP).
