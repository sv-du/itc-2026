## About the Program

Add value to students’ lives and integrate your learnings into a classroom setting as you prepare to become a middle or high school math teacher, or delve into the theory and proofs behind pure mathematics.
With the program emphasis in secondary teacher preparation / pure mathematics, you can make an often-intimidating field accessible and provide students with problem-solving skills they can use for the rest of their lives, or strive to extend our knowledge of fundamental mathematics.

## The Polytechnic Approach

Build a strong foundation in mathematics by studying everything from geometry to number theory to topology. Gain an understanding of teaching and pedagogy at different educational levels. Then, transform from the student to the teacher with experience in actual secondary school classrooms.

### Secondary Education Teacher

Introduce middle and high school students to the fundamentals of pre-algebra, geometry, algebra, precalculus, statistics and calculus. Create lesson plans, present lectures, design hands-on activities, grade assignments and instill a love of mathematics.

### Professor

Teach at the college or university level and help students from all backgrounds develop their mathematical skills. Develop lesson plans, give assessments and mentor students while also conducting research. Propose new theories, seek to prove complex theorems or apply mathematical and statistical models to real-life scenarios.

## Courses

The Secondary Teacher Preparation / Pure Mathematics option provides you with a deep understanding of mathematical concepts and methods while preparing you for careers in secondary education, many sectors of industry and government. It also gives a foundation for graduate study in mathematics education and pure mathematics.
