# Comparison Guides

## Crosswalk Tables

### Comparison Guides (Crosswalk Tables)

Comparison Guides (also known as crosswalk tables) are documents that your college has made to show you the differences between your major's quarter and semester curriculum.

#### Huntley College of Agriculture

Log into your [Blackboard account](https://my.cpp.edu) to find the College of Agriculture Semester Conversion Advising course.

#### College of Business Administration

Visit the [College of Business Administrations Student Success Center's website](https://www.cpp.edu/cba/student-success-center/index.shtml).

#### College of Education & Integrative Studies

Log into your [Blackboard account](https://my.cpp.edu) to find the College of Education & Integrative Studies Advising course.

Visit the [College of Education & Integrative Studies Advising Center's website](https://www.cpp.edu/ceis/student-success-center/index.shtml).

#### College of Engineering

Log into your [Blackboard account](https://my.cpp.edu) to find the College of Engineering Advising course.

Visit [Aerospace Engineering department's website](https://www.cpp.edu/engineering/aro/index.shtml).

See [Chemical & Materials Engineering department](https://www.cpp.edu/engineering/cme/index.shtml).

Find [Civil Engineering department](https://www.cpp.edu/engineering/ce/index.shtml).

Visit [Electrical & Computer Engineering department website](https://www.cpp.edu/engineering/ece/index.shtml).

See [Electromechanical Engineering Technology department](https://www.cpp.edu/engineering/et/index.shtml).

Find [Industrial & Manufacturing Engineering department](https://www.cpp.edu/engineering/ime/index.shtml).
Visit [Mechanical Engineering department](https://www.cpp.edu/engineering/me/index.shtml).

#### College of Environmental Design

Log into your [Blackboard account](https://my.cpp.edu) to find the College of Environmental Design's Advising course.

Visit the [College of Environmental Design's website](https://www.cpp.edu/env/).

#### Collins College of Hospitality Management

Log into your [Blackboard account](https://my.cpp.edu) to find the Collins College of Hospitality Management Advising course.

Visit the [Collins College of Hospitality Management Advising Center's website](https://www.cpp.edu/collins/current-students/academic-advising.shtml).

#### College of Letters, Arts & Social Sciences

Log into your [Blackboard account](https://my.cpp.edu) to find the College of Letters, Arts & Social Sciences Advising course.

Visit the [CLASS Student Success Center's website](https://www.cpp.edu/class/student-success-center/index.shtml).

#### College of Science

Log into your [Blackboard account](https://my.cpp.edu) to find the College of Science Advising course.

Visit the [College of Science Advising Center's website](https://www.cpp.edu/sci/advising-center/semester-conversion.shtml).
