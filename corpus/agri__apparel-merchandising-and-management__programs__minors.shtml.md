# Minors

## Become by Displaying

![A shirt is displayed on a mannequin at an AMM boutique outside Building 2.](../img/2016-amm-boutique-3-920x570.jpg)

In the fashion and apparel industries, design is not just about what clothing and footwear will look like. It’s also about deciding how your products are promoted and displayed to attract customers.

Our department has two minors: **Fashion Merchandising** and **Footwear Design and Merchandising.**With these minors, you can learn to specialize in the field of fashion and footwear.

Find out more about the courses related to these minors at [catalog.cpp.edu](https://catalog.cpp.edu/).

[Fashion Merchandising](https://catalog.cpp.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=69&poid=19574&returnto=5890)

[Footwear Design & Merchandising](https://catalog.cpp.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=69&poid=19800&returnto=5890)

Make sure that you select the most recent edition of the catalog in the upper right corner (see the circled menu in the picture below).

![A screenshot of the online university catalog.](../img/2025-amm-minors-624x211.jpg)
