## Please review the following steps and their deadlines

### CSU Application

Complete and submit the online California State University Admissions application at [Cal State Apply](https://www2.calstate.edu/apply), and monitor the CSU website for updated deadlines.

### Optional: EOP Application via CSU Apply

Applying for the Educational Opportunity Program (EOP) is not required for our RS program, but it is strongly recommended. You can apply through Cal State Apply and select 'Yes' if you're interested in applying to the EOP program. The final deadline to submit the EOP application to Cal Poly Pomona is January 15, 2025.

### Financial Aid Application (FAFSA or CADAA)

The priority deadline for the FAFSA and CADAA is usually March 2nd each year. For the 2025-26 cycle, the priority deadline in California will be March 3rd, 2025, since March 2nd falls on a weekend.

We encourage you to complete your financial aid application as early as possible before the deadline and to regularly check the websites for updates.

* [Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA®)](https://studentaid.gov/#https://studentaid.gov/)
* [California Dream Act Application (CADAA)](https://dream.csac.ca.gov/landing#https://dream.csac.ca.gov/landing)

### RS Application

Complete and submit the [Online Renaissance Scholars Application](https://engage.cpp.edu/manage/login?realm=&r=/register/rs_programs) and verification documents. Please reach out to us if you need any assistance.

### RS Intake Meeting

Sign-up to complete a mandatory RS Intake Meeting

Purpose: RS Intake Meeting helps us understand how we can best support your success.

Information on how to sign-up for your RS Intake Meeting will be sent to your email once your application and verification documents have been processed.

### RS Partnership Agreement/Acknowledgement

After completing your RS Intake Meeting, you will be required to complete your RS Partnership Agreement/Acknowledgement before you can begin accessing RS program benefits & services.
