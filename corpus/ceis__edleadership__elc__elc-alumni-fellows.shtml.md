# ELC Alumni Fellows

![Students walking in their regalia on graduation day. Regalia is Black and Bright Blue.](../img/25ceis-grad_8-2.jpg)

## From Scholarship to Impact

ELC alumni fellows are graduates of CPP's Educational Leadership doctoral program who are working with faculty mentors to bring their equity-focused insights and scholarship to fruition through educational publications and practitioner-facing research briefs.
