## Continuing Students:

![students smiling](../img/students_smiling2.jpg)

## Registration

**Continuing students** registration for courses begins in April for the fall term, October for a winter or spring term, and March for a summer term. The registration windows for eligible continuing students are available online via BroncoDirect  before the start of the registration period. All registration holds must be cleared before registration.

[**New undergraduate students**](registration-new-students.shtml) will register for classes as part of their participation in mandatory orientation.

![Students studying](../img/students_study.jpg)

## Continuing Students ~

## Registration Window

* Registration windows are assigned based on class level (Senior, Junior, Sophomore, Freshmen) as well as units earned (not including currently enrolled units). If a group of students has the same number of units, registration windows are assigned randomly to ensure equity.
* Registration windows are fixed and cannot be changed. If you believe your registration window was assigned in error, you should contact the Registrar’s Office at registrar@cpp.edu.
* Unit limits apply to each of the registration periods and do not include waitlisted classes. For more detailed information, refer to the [Registration Schedule](https://www.cpp.edu/registrar/registration/registration-schedule.shtml "https://www.cpp.edu/registrar/registration/registration-schedule.shtml").
* You may waitlist up to a maximum of 10 units for Fall or Spring term. Waitlisted units do not count toward the total allowable unit limit. For detailed information, visit [Waitlisting](waitlisting.shtml "https://www.cpp.edu/registrar/registration/waitlisting.shtml").
* For additional information on registration, visit [Registering for Courses](https://www.cpp.edu/~studentsuccess/guides/registration/register.shtml "https://www.cpp.edu/%7Estudentsuccess/guides/registration/register.shtml")

* Please review your specific registration window online in your [BroncoDirect](https://my.cpp.edu/uPortal/p/broncodirect.ctf1/max/render.uP "https://my.cpp.edu/uPortal/p/broncodirect.ctf1/max/render.uP") - Student Center.
