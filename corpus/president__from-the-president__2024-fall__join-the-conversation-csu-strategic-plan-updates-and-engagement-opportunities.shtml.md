# Join the Conversation: CSU Strategic Plan Updates and Engagement Opportunities

## December 3, 2024

Dear Campus Community,

We are excited to continue the dialogue around the system-wide [CSU Strategic Plan](https://www.calstate.edu/Strategic-Planning-for-the-CSU/Pages/default.aspx "https://www.calstate.edu/Strategic-Planning-for-the-CSU/Pages/default.aspx"), which is central to shaping the future of our university system. This collective effort depends on your input, creativity and collaboration.

**Upcoming Town Halls: December 4, 5 & 6**I encourage you to participate in one of the three remaining virtual town halls. These sessions offer a valuable space to share your ideas and hear from others across the CSU. [Register here](https://www.calstate.edu/Strategic-Planning-for-the-CSU/Pages/attend-a-town-hall.aspx "https://www.calstate.edu/Strategic-Planning-for-the-CSU/Pages/attend-a-town-hall.aspx").

**Explore Our Engagement Platform**To ensure everyone has a chance to contribute, we’ve launched an ongoing engagement platform available at[csustrategicplan.mysocialpinpoint.com](https://nam11.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fcsustrategicplan.mysocialpinpoint.com%2F&data=05%7C02%7Cmpignotti%40cpp.edu%7Cf35fcf2d4f114875a97808dd13ebac3c%7C164ba61e39ec4f5d89ffaa1f00a521b4%7C0%7C0%7C638688625139679821%7CUnknown%7CTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJFbXB0eU1hcGkiOnRydWUsIlYiOiIwLjAuMDAwMCIsIlAiOiJXaW4zMiIsIkFOIjoiTWFpbCIsIldUIjoyfQ%3D%3D%7C0%7C%7C%7C&sdata=oGvMjd%2BxB3BqA4YHTPRswgTQU1XGjghc1Ac9kwIAMfA%3D&reserved=0 "Original URL: https://csustrategicplan.mysocialpinpoint.com/. Click or tap if you trust this link."). This platform allows you to:

* Share your thoughts and feedback on the CSU’s strategic direction.
* Interact and connect with other CSU stakeholders.
* Suggest documents or resources to inform the plan’s development.

In particular, I encourage you to contribute to the [social map for **Regional Specialties**](https://csustrategicplan.mysocialpinpoint.com/#jl_magic_tabs_regional_specialties_gix4 "https://csustrategicplan.mysocialpinpoint.com/#jl_magic_tabs_regional_specialties_gix4") to highlight the unique strengths and characteristics of Cal Poly Pomona and our surrounding community.

**Contribute, Share & Revisit Often**We invite you to explore the engagement platform, add your insights and share it widely with your colleagues, classmates and friends. The platform is dynamic, with new content and opportunities for engagement added regularly — so be sure to check back often.

Thank you for your commitment to this important process. Together, we will create a strategic plan that drives success for our students, campuses and communities across California.

Sincerely,

Soraya M. Coley, Ph.D.
President
