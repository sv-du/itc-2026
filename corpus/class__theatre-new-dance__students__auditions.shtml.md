## Are you an actor?

### Audition for a Department of Theatre Performance

We hold general auditions each semester for the upcoming semester's shows. Check this page for updates.

![Audition for a Department of Theatre Performance](../img/student-pages/auditions1.png)

## Upcoming Auditions

## Upcoming Fall 2025 General Auditions:

#### General Auditions: Tuesday, August 19, 3pm - 7pm

#### Callbacks: Thursday, August 21, 5:30pm - 8:30pm

*Auditions and callbacks will take place in the Theatre building (Bldg. 25)*

**Information about auditions can be [found on our Canvas page.](https://canvas.cpp.edu/courses/112605) If you are unable to access the page, or if you are interested in auditioning but are NOT a current CPP student, please contact Sarah Krainin.**

* All CPP students in any major/minor, and members of the CPP community, are eligible to audition
* **[Show scripts are available on the TND Canvas page HERE.](https://canvas.cpp.edu/courses/112605)**Please familiarize yourself with the shows!
* An announcement with cast breakdowns, sign-up links and what to prepare will be sent out soon and posted on the canvas link!

Shows open to auditions:

***Harbur Gate*by Kathleen Cahill**
Performance dates: October 10-19, 2025
Directed by Bernardo Solano

***Anon(ymous)*by Naomi Iizuka**
Performance dates: November 14-23, 2025
Directed by Sayda Trujillo
