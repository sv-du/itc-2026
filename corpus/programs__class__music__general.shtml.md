## About the Program

Ready to dive into the world of music? With our General Music option, you’ll thrive in a hands-on environment that combines performance, technology and industry knowledge. With expert faculty guiding you, you’ll explore everything from music theory and history to the latest trends, all while developing your artistic and professional potential. This option allows you to gain a solid foundation while having the freedom to explore interdisciplinary studies.

## The Polytechnic Approach

Here, you won’t just study music—you’ll experience it in action! Get hands-on experience in the performance, production and management side of music industry through internships, working with the latest music technology, producing and mixing your own projects. Perform with diverse ensembles and collaborate with students from other fields like theater and media, combining your musical skills with new technologies and creative approaches.

## Career Outcomes

### Audio/Visual (AV) Technician

Operate and manage audio and visual equipment for concerts, broadcasts, and live events. These technicians ensure sound, lighting, and other media equipment runs smoothly during productions.

Card 2 of 3

### Music Journalist

Write about the music world! Music journalists report on trends, review albums, interview artists, and cover concerts, festivals, and other music events for magazines, newspapers, and online platforms.

## Courses

The General Music option provides a comprehensive foundation in music within a liberal arts framework. This option offers coursework in music theory, history, and performance, giving you the flexibility to explore a variety of musical interests and interdisciplinary studies. You will benefit from diverse performance opportunities in ensembles and be introduced to essential topics like music technology and recording. Core courses such as "World of Music," "Careers in Music," and "History of American Popular Music" ensure a well-rounded musical education while allowing room for exploration and personal growth. This program is ideal for those seeking a broad understanding of music and its various applications, including potential paths in music-related careers or further academic pursuits.

For the most up-to-date information on courses for this program, visit the course catalog or contact an advisor.
