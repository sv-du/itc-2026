## Career Paths in Hospitality

![A hotel located next to a beach.](../img/lodgingandresorts.jpeg)

### Lodging and Resorts

This sector includes businesses that provide overnight accommodations, such as hotels, resorts and bed-and-breakfasts, focusing on guest comfort and service. Career paths include: Hotel Manager, Front Desk Supervisor, Housekeeping Manager, Concierge, Revenue Manager and Resort Activities Coordinator.

![An ice cream dessert with dried ice. ](../img/foodandbeveragesector.jpeg)

### Food and Beverage

This sector encompasses all establishments that prepare and serve food and drinks, including restaurants, bars, and catering services. Career paths include: Restaurant Manager: Executive Chef, Sommelier, Barista, Catering Manager, Food and Beverage Director.

![A state park with canyons ](../img/travelandtourismsector1.jpeg)

### Travel and Tourism

This sector covers services that facilitate travel and exploration, such as travel agencies, tour operators, and transportation services, aiming to enhance the travel experience. Career paths include: Travel Agent, Tour Guide, Destination Manager, Travel Consultant, Cruise Director, Airline Customer Service Representative.

![The colosseum in Los Angeles.  ](../img/sportsandrecreation.jpeg)

### Sports and Recreation

This sector involves facilities and services that promote physical activities and entertainment, including sport complexes, fitness centers and recreational parks. Career paths include: Guest Service Manager, Recreation Program Coordinator, Spa Manager, Activities Director, Event Services Manager, Sports Hospitality Coordinator.

![A country club and golf club](../img/countryandgolfclubssector.jpeg)

### Country Clubs and Golf Clubs

This sector includes private clubs that offer exclusive recreational and social amenities, such as golf courses, dining facilities, and event spaces, catering to members' leisure and networking needs. Career paths include: Club Manager, Golf Course Superintendent, Membership Director, Golf Pro, Clubhouse Manager, Food and Beverage Manager.

![Fans celebrate at a Baseball Field](../img/specialeventsandmeetings1.jpeg)

### Special Events and Meetings

This sector specializes in planning and executing events, from corporate meetings and conferences to weddings and social gatherings, ensuring seamless and memorable experiences. Career paths include: Event Planner, Meeting Coordinator, Wedding Planner, Convention Services Manager, Event Marketing Specialist, Audio-Visual Technician.
