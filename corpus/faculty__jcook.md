## About Me

John Cook received a Bachelor’s of Science degree in Chemical Engineering and Forestry from the University of Michigan at Ann Arbor.  He also received a Master degree in Engineering from Western Michigan University at Kalamazoo.

He worked for thirty years in the field of manufacturing. He was involved with the design, construction, start-up, and optimization of manufacturing at sites throughout the world. He led teams of professionals with an emphasis on product development and high volume manufacturing of consumer products. Scope included engineering and production with extensive collaboration with suppliers and design personnel from Corporate R&D. He has led Quality Improvement and Information Technologies teams to create world-class process monitoring systems.

After retiring, he became bored and decided to start a new career in teaching. He utilizes his experience from the private industry to provide students with practical, real-world techniques in operations management, supply chain management, project management, process optimization, quality control, and Lean. He continues to consult with consumer products companies, which helps him stay updated on the latest management practices.
