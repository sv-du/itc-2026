# Welcome to the College of Engineering

Solve problems. Engineer new solutions. Make everyday life better for millions.

![A liquid rocket built be engineering students being launched.](img/img_liquid-rocket_launch.jpg)

## Our Philosophy

### A True Polytechnic Experience

The difference between wishful thinking and real-world problem solving is all in the doing. At the College of Engineering, you'll learn to think beyond what’s already been done to make it better than ever. You'll put theory to work through hands-on exposure, real-world application and cutting-edge research — preparing you to enter an array of industries with utmost experience, confidence and problem-solving abilities. At Cal Poly Pomona, you don’t just learn by doing. You become by doing.

## College of Engineering

### Graduate Programs

Our hands-on approach extends to graduate education, blending theoretical knowledge with practical application to real-world problems. Work closely with faculty who are experts in their fields and actively engaged in research and industry partnerships. Plus, many of our programs offer evening classes, enabling you to work while studying in the evenings.
