# Vanpool

Let someone else do the driving.  Cal PolyPomona vanpools hold 7-10 people who share the commute to the campus.  Vanpools set their own route, operating schedule, and pick-up/ drop-off location.  Fares are determined by the number of passengers riding in the van, the monthly mileage, and fuel charges.  Drivers may also qualify for a free fare. All vanpool riders must complete a Passenger Agreement or Driver Agreement from the Rideshare Office before they ride the van.

[Click here](../docs/routes-for-web-2019-2020.pdf) for the current Cal Poly Pomona vanpools in service.

Full-time Foundation and ASI employees are eligible to ride vanpools.  **Students are not eligible for the vanpool program.**  Contact the Rideshare office for available seats on vanpools.
