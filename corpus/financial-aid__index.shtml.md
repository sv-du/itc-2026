# Office of Financial Aid & Scholarships

![Students walking outside on campus](https://www.cpp.edu/financial-aid/img/students_walking.jpg)

## Office of Financial Aid & Scholarships

The Cal Poly Pomona Office of Financial Aid and Scholarships provides students with the financial resources and related services necessary to achieve their educational goals. We are committed to providing accurate, user-friendly, responsive, and respectful service to students and their families.

We administer federal, state, and university aid and scholarship programs in accordance with applicable policies and regulations while striving to be an innovative and service-minded department. We also provide outreach and services in collaboration with other university departments and the community.

**School code: 001144**

## Discover The Opportunity to Fund Your Future

![2026-2027 Scholarship Season is Underway! Apply for CPP Scholarships by March 2, 2026!](img/2026-2027-scholarship-season-website-graphic.png)

Cal Poly Pomona offers hundreds of scholarships that recognize more than just academics. Many awards consider your story, your involvement, your ambitions, and the unique experiences that shape your Bronco journey.

Visit the **[Bronco Scholarship Portal (BSP)](https://csupomona.academicworks.com/ "https://csupomona.academicworks.com/")** to complete the General Scholarship Application, or click to learn more about **[scholarships](https://www.cpp.edu/financial-aid/scholarships/ "https://www.cpp.edu/financial-aid/scholarships/")** and how CPP supports students through **[Scholarship Season](https://www.cpp.edu/financial-aid/scholarships/cal-poly-pomona-scholarships.shtml "https://www.cpp.edu/financial-aid/scholarships/cal-poly-pomona-scholarships.shtml")**.

**Deadline: March 2, 2026**

## 2026-27 FAFSA and CADAA are Now Open - Apply Today!

![FAFSA & CADAA for 2026-27 now open! Apply now! Priority Deadline is 9pm, March 2, 2026](fafsa-cadaa-website.png)

The **2026-27 [FAFSA](https://studentaid.gov/h/apply-for-aid/fafsa) and [CADAA](https://dream.csac.ca.gov/landing)** are now available. These are the applications for the 2026-27 academic year (Fall 2026, Spring 2027, Summer 2027).

**Priority Deadline**: **9pm, March 2, 2026**

Complete your application as soon as possible - some aid is first-come, first-served.

## Credential

Students interested in pursuing teaching as a career, have chosen a noble profession. Like all college-bound students, however, they face the obstacle of paying for their education. ## Graduate

At Cal Poly Pomona, we understand that pursuing a graduate degree takes great commitment and sacrifice. That’s why we’re equally committed to helping make your graduate studies affordable. The scholarships, grants and loans available for graduate students vary; take a look at the information specific to you, including details on eligibility, deadlines and more.
