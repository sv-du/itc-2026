# Information for J-2 dependents

Information for J-2 dependents

## How to Request I-20?

### 1. Gather Documents

#### Documents Needed

###### Important: English translations must accompany any documents issued in a language other than English.

* **Sufficient financial documentation**
  + Bank statements, letter from a bank or sponsor, or financial guarantee
  + Financial documentation cannot be older than six (6) months from program start date
  + Evidence of adequate funding to meet the estimated tuition and living expenses for first year of study (see the Approximate Costs chart below).
* **Passport**
* **Valid I-20 from current institution (if applicable)**

#### Approximate Costs

* The estimation includes costs of campus fees, books, supplies, room and board, personal expense, transportation costs, and tuition for one academic year.
* For more information about tuition and fees, visit the Student Accounting & Cashiering Services [website](https://www.cpp.edu/student-accounting/tuition-fees/).

###### A spouse requires an additional $5000 and a child an additional $3000 of support.

|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
|  | **Undergraduate** | **Graduate** |
| Living Expenses | $20,732 | $20,732 |
| Tuition & Fees | $17,818 | $16,084 |
| Total | **$38,550** | **$36,816** |

### 2. Complete I-20 e-Request

1. Login to [I-20 e-Request](https://polyform.cpp.edu/Loginformproc/UnityForm.aspx?d1=AdpWqUVp5CMX6P0Pb%2fRJZ9tOGfu2GT0NWxGzz7zu2WOjAK8IYgZBVtUWKxUJz%2fv2NBiG4d8vFKK57jBzU%2brUsrfsESxyiOYUji6HDxnRfYjo1ZgO%2f%2fjIhGoul5vHwusmG39erZNFyzgFfNoIdw6Jank0QTM%2fijxSXHm9%2bnAP%2bdMy2wqU9VLF6ngQGBwsG1hgTvwjReaQauaHZu82IAxcKcLSqPitzzf3lwdwatFXWh6Y) using CPP username and password
2. Follow instructions and fill out the request form
3. Upload following:
   1. Sufficient financial documentation
   2. Passport
   3. Valid I-20 from current institution (if applicable)
4. Click "Save/Submit"
5. Allow 5 business days for ISSO processing

### 3. Check CPP Email

* After successfully submitting the I-20 e-Request form, you will receive an email confirmation.
* If your application needs additional documents, you will receive an email from the ISSO.

## After Submitting I-20 e-Request

### 1. Review Pre-Departure Checklist

Follow the Pre-Departure checklist and complete all required steps before traveling to the U.S.
