# Outstanding Alumni

## Aerospace Outstanding Alumni

Each year, during the Aerospace Engineering Department annual award banquet, we honor one of our outstanding alumni, who has demonstrated years of achievement in the aerospace industry.

![Ali Ahmadi](../img/alumni.008.jpeg)

## John C. Rose 2024

**Global Engineering for The Boeing Company**

John Rose serves as the Sr. Director – Global Engineering Strategy in the Engineering Strategy & Operations organization for The Boeing Company. John earned his B.S. and M.S. in Aerospace Engineering from Cal Poly Pomona, followed by an MBA from USC.
He has held various roles in his 25 years with The Boeing Company, from Loads & Dynamics Manager and Chief of Staff to Sr. Manager – Structures Technology.
John has been fortunate to support such exciting programs as the Space Shuttle, initial ISS construction, CST-100 certification, 767 winglets, proprietary platforms, and the USAF tanker. He also previously worked for The Walt Disney Company as a Mechanical Engineer in the Ride & Show Engineering group at The Disneyland Resort. John returned to Cal Poly Pomona to serve as a part-time instructor in the Aerospace Dept, teaching several of the freshman introduction classes.

![Ali Ahmadi](../img/‎alumni.‎001.jpeg)

## Ali Ahmadi 2023

**Cal Poly Pomona Aerospace Engineering**

Dr. Ali R. Ahmadi received his BS in Aerospace Engineering from Cal Poly Pomona and a Ph.D. from MIT. From 1980 to 1985, he worked as a Scientist at the consulting firm BBN. He joined Cal Poly Pomona in 1985, becoming a tenured Professor in 1988. He served as the Department Chair from 2003 to 2023 and oversaw the MS in Aeronautics with Northrop Corp (1986 - 2002) and the MS in Aerospace program in Lancaster with the US Air Force (2007 - 2021), awarding 150+ master's degrees. He's taught 15 Aerospace courses, conducted research with NASA and US Air Force, and secured $10M+ for Aerospace lab development. He received teaching awards from Northrop Grumman and Lockheed Martin and played a vital role in faculty recruitment and the success of the Aerospace Engineering program at Cal Poly Pomona.

![Manuel Beltran](../trash/‎alumni.‎002.jpeg)

## Manuel Beltran 2019

**Associate Technical Fellow, The Boeing Company**

Manuel Beltran graduated from Cal Poly Pomona in 1989 with a B.S. in Aerospace Engineering and a Minor in Computer Science. While at Cal Poly Pomona, he was part of the MEP program. Manuel is a distinguished Associate Technical Fellow and Chief Software Architect for Boeing AvionX. His career spans nearly 30 years of working with cutting-edge technologies across a wide range of aerospace programs in space vehicles, military ground vehicles, robotics, high-energy lasers, fighter jets, and commercial airplanes.

![Joe Yee](../img/‎alumni.‎003.jpeg)

## Joe Yee 2018

Joe Yee graduated with a degree in Aerospace Engineering from Cal Poly Pomona in 1972 and began his career with Fluor Corporation in 1973. He worked as a mechanical piping stress engineer/analyst on many energy-related projects like the Trans-Alaska Pipeline System – at the time the most expensive privately-funded construction project – as well as power plants and refineries. At Fluor, Joe enrolled in a Cal Poly external graduate program, earning a Master of Engineering in 1983. After Fluor, he has founded and work in multiple companies including Alysc Telecom, Ameriphone Inc, and Imagine-It-Concepts.

![Joe Salontai](../img/‎alumni.‎007.jpeg)

## Joe Salontai 2018

Joe Salontai graduated from California State Polytechnic University - Pomona with a degree in Aerospace Engineering. From 1963 -1974, employed as a Group Engineer at McDonnell Douglas before joining William Morsch in business. Bill Morsch retired in 1975 and the Salontai family became owners of Morsch Machine. The company employs modern manufacturing techniques, including the use of state-of-art computer-controlled robotic equipment and high-speed machining. Most of the facility production is aerospace oriented. Whether commercial or military, items produced are included within almost every current aircraft platform (jetliners, fighters, bombers) as well as various missiles, launch vehicles, and some space-based platforms.

![Michael E. Blackstone](../img/‎alumni.‎004.jpeg)

## Michael E. Blackstone 2017

**American Airlines, Blackstone Aerobatics**

Mike's passion for flying took him to Cal Poly Pomona, where he graduated with a Bachelor of Science Degree in Aerospace Engineering. In 1981, Mike bought a brand new factory-built Pitts S-2A and started Blackstone Aerobatics, where he became an amazing aerobatic pilot with over 1,300 hours in his Pitts. Captain Blackstone flew for American Airlines for 20 years and for AirCal for 14 years, and he retired at age 59 in 2007.
Michael E. Blackstone was born in Artesia, California, and resided in Fullerton, CA, for most of his life. He died of lung cancer after a 7-month battle on August 19, 2015, at the St Jude Heritage Hospital. He was 67 years of age.

![Jim Eshleman](../img/‎alumni.‎005.jpeg)

## Jim Eshleman 2016

**Northrop Grumman**

Jim Eshleman graduated Magna Cum Laude from Cal Poly Pomona in 1982 and began his aerospace career at the NASA Ames Research Center doing research wind tunnel testing on powered-lift aircraft in the National Full Scale Aerodynamics Complex. While there, he received his Master's degree in Aerospace Engineering from Stanford in 1985. Jim is currently the Air Vehicle lead on Northrop Grumman's Tactically Exploited Reconnaissance Node (TERN) Program in Rancho Bernardo, California, where he is responsible for all aspects of air vehicle design, development, and fabrication.

![Steve Cass](../img/‎alumni.‎006.jpeg)

## Steve Cass 2015

**Gulfstream Aerospace**

Steve Cass graduated in 1987 from Cal Poly, Pomona, with a degree in Aerospace Engineering. Steve is currently vice president, Technical Marketing and Communications at Gulfstream Aerospace in Savannah, Georgia. In this capacity, Steve is responsible for all internal and external communications, sales development, and sales support for the worldwide sales team; and assisting with new product development programs and product enhancements at Gulfstream.
