# Events & Campus Involvement

## Spring 2026 PolyTransfer Events

[### February 11: Healthy Relationships

Join Survivor Advocacy Services, PolyTransfer, and Male Success Initiatives for an engaging and educational workshop designed to test and strengthen your knowledge on healthy relationships, sexual assault awareness, and key terminology.](https://mybar.cpp.edu/event/12146254)

[### February 17: Writing Scholarship Essays

This workshop will focus on the scholarship essay writing process, helpful tips for making your essay more competitive, and referrals to campus resources. This event will be offered in hybrid format, both in person and via zoom. Snacks and beverages will be provided.](https://mybar.cpp.edu/event/11989976)

[### February 24: Advising 101 Workshop

Join us for an informative session designed to help continuing and newly admitted students understand CPP’s advising structure, resources, and policies. Learn how to make the most of your advising experience and set yourself up for academic success!](https://mybar.cpp.edu/event/12132381)

[### February 25: Speed Friending

Looking to meet new people and build genuine connections on campus? Join us for Speed Friending — a fast-paced, fun way to interact with fellow students!
This event is open to all current students and offers a welcoming space to engage in meaningful conversations about life goals, identity-shaping experiences, and quirky, random questions that help you get to know others better. It’s also a great opportunity to practice social skills in a relaxed, low-pressure environment. Come connect, share, and make new friends!](https://mybar.cpp.edu/event/12132383)

[### March 3: Navigating Your CPP Journey

This workshop is designed to help students build confidence and clarity as they move through their academic and personal journey at Cal Poly Pomona. This session provides a comprehensive overview of key strategies for success, including how to effectively use campus resources and services, and access academic support such as the Learning Resource Center and Writing Center. Tailored for all CPP students, this workshop equips participants with essential tools, guidance, and insights to help them thrive both inside and outside the classroom.](https://mybar.cpp.edu/event/12221412 "opens a new window")

[### March 17: Grad School Statements

Do you know the difference between a Personal Statement and Statement of Purpose? If not, this workshop is for you! Join us to learn helpful tips for drafting a quality essay and participate in a writing activity.](https://mybar.cpp.edu/event/12068389)

[### March 18: Undergraduate Research

Interested in getting involved in research at Cal Poly Pomona? This workshop will introduce transfer students to the Office of Undergraduate Research and the many opportunities available to participate in research across disciplines. Learn how to connect with faculty, explore research programs, and take the first step in your academic journey as a scholar.](https://mybar.cpp.edu/event/12132395)

[### March 24: Community Engagement Workshop

“Learn by Doing Good: An Introduction to Community-Engaged Learning at Cal Poly Pomona”
Join the Center for Community Engagement (CCE) for an interactive workshop designed to introduce students to the many ways they can connect their academic journey with meaningful, hands-on community engagement. Guided by our mission to inspire civic engagement, promote career exploration, and empower students through real-world learning, the CCE creates opportunities that make a positive impact on our local community, region, and beyond.](https://mybar.cpp.edu/event/12152004 "opens a new window")

[### March 26: Internship Strategies

Internships are an opportunity to further develop what you learn in your courses and prepare for your future career. This workshop will provide tips and strategies for securing an internship and making the most of the experience.](https://mybar.cpp.edu/event/12068473)

[### April 7: Investing & Major Purchases

Learn how to shape your financial future and important strategies when making your first major purchase like a car or home.](https://mybar.cpp.edu/event/11989978)

[### April 8: First-Gen on Tap

Stop by and learn how you can be connected with other first-generation students, faculty and staff in a fun and engaging environment. You can also feel good about eating and drinking for a cause! Proceeds from Innovation Brew Works purchases will go towards establishing a first-generation student scholarship.](https://mybar.cpp.edu/event/12132440)

[### April 21: Understanding Credit Score

Now is the time to establish and build credit for your future. Wise use of credit can take you to the front door of your first home. Just a few mistakes can make your goals harder (and more expensive) to reach! This session will introduce the basic building blocks of successful credit management: what your FICO score means, how it is determined, and how your behavior can make it go up or down.](https://mybar.cpp.edu/event/11989981)

[### April 23: Leading Through Strengths

This interactive workshop, hosted by the Career Center, is designed to help students strengthen their soft skills and elevate their leadership potential. Participants will explore practical strategies they can apply immediately in their job search and professional environments. Through guided activities, real-world examples, and hands-on practice, students will learn how to communicate effectively, collaborate with confidence, and demonstrate leadership in ways that make a lasting impression on employers.](https://mybar.cpp.edu/event/12213094)

[### April 30: PolyTransfer Tailgate

Join PolyTransfer to cheer on CPP's Baseball team as they take on Cal State San Marcos!](https://mybar.cpp.edu/event/12146175)

[### May 5: Graduation Celebration

Congrats Transfer Graduates!! To celebrate all of you, PolyTransfer will be hosting our graduation celebration! Come meet other graduating students, get an opportunity to decorate your cap, and even have a chance to take graduation photos!](https://mybar.cpp.edu/event/12132402)
