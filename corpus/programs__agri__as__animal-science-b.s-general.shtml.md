## About the Program

Get ready for a career in the thriving animal industry with hands-on learning and access to more animals than any other Southern California college. The Animal Science general option prepares you for animal-focused careers beyond veterinary medicine, offering broad coursework across species and specialties to build a strong foundation in animal sciences.

## The Polytechnic Experience

Explore the animal industry through hands-on work in labs and on our 330 acres of rangeland and 100 acres of pasture. Care for cattle, sheep, goats, swine and the famed Kellogg Arabian horses. With strong industry ties and real experience, you’ll prepare for careers in animal industries, feed companies, biotech and more.

### Animal Breeder

Breed animals for desirable traits including size, speed, meat production and more. Select animals based on health and genetics, assist with artificial insemination and keep records of breeding history.

### Livestock Buyer

Purchase livestock on behalf of individuals and companies. Identify opportunities to purchase animals, inspect livestock for desired characteristics and negotiate with suppliers.

### Animal Welfare Auditor

Monitor animal welfare and perform audits on farms, animal production facilities, and animal processing facilities to ensure proper animal care.

## Courses

Cal Poly Pomona's Animal Science option in the Bachelor of Science in Animal Science offers a broad range of career paths beyond veterinary school. Students study the entire animal, from cellular DNA to overall physiology, gaining expertise in genetics, breeding, immunology, and reproductive physiology. The program provides hands-on learning at the renowned Kellogg Arabian Horse Center and extensive teaching farms. Students can explore careers in animal industry management, food safety, public health, and more. Whether it's developing vaccines, improving pet food, or ensuring safer meat, the program prepares students to make significant contributions to the growing animal industry. The curriculum also includes unique experiences like performing artificial insemination and monitoring the birth of foals.

For the most up-to-date information on courses for this program, visit the course catalog or contact an advisor.
