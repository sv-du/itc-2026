# Teacher Training

**CPP Femineer Teacher Training Workshop**

Teachers who attend this professional development workshop will be instructed on how to teach a 30-hour curriculum project that focuses on real-world engineering skills that enable students to develop a finished product.  **Please note that all teachers who attend the training MUST have their own laptops and be in attendance for all training days**.

**Summer Training Dates**:

* Year 1 Creative Robotics: June 22-23, 2026
* Year 2 Wearable Technology: June 24-26, 2026
* Year 3 Pi Robotics: Not Available

[Register Online for Femineer Teacher Training](https://forms.gle/WJJxC8YoCx67SEvM8)
