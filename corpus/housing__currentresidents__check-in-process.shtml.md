# Check-In Information

## Welcome to Your New Home

Below is general information and standard procedures for checking into the Residential Halls, Residential Suites, or Traditional Halls during operating hours and after hours. Please do not hesitate to contact the Housing Office at (909) 869-3307 or housing@cpp.edu with any questions.

## Business Hours

### Check-In Procedures

University Housing Services has two check-in locations depending on the community residents are moving into. Business Hours for both locations are Monday-Friday from 8:00am-5:00pm. Below overviews the check-in locations for each community type.

[![Traditional Hall buildings](../documents/img/trad-halls/trad-ext-5.jpg)

### Traditional Halls

During Business Hours, residents moving in to the Traditional Halls (Encinitas, Montecito, Alamitos, and Aliso) can check-in at the 71 Housing Office Monday-Friday from 8:00am-5:00pm.](https://www.cpp.edu/maps?id=1130#!m/276507?share  "opens a new window")

[![Residential Suites buildings](../documents/img/suites/suites-ext-27.jpg)

### Residential Suites

During Business Hours, residents moving in to the Suites (Estrellas, Bonita, Sol, Montanas, or Luna) can check-in at the 74 Secoya Housing Office Monday-Friday from 8:00am-5:00pm.](https://www.cpp.edu/maps?id=1130#!m/773559?share  "opens a new window")

[![Residential Hall buildings](../documents/img/res-halls/res-ext-8.jpg)

### Residential Halls

During Business Hours, residents moving in to the Residential Halls (Sicomoro and Secoya) can check-in at the 74 Secoya Housing Office Monday-Friday from 8:00am-5:00pm.](https://www.cpp.edu/maps?id=1130#!m/773559?share  "opens a new window")

## Check-In Procedures: After Business Hours

Once the Housing Offices close, residents can check-in with their RA on Duty Monday - Friday after 6:00pm and all day Saturday and Sunday. View your view your RA's contact numbers below.
