# Happy Holidays, Cal Poly Pomona!

## December 15, 2025

[![President Levine playing piano while the CPP Choir sings behind her](../../img/holiday-video-email-thumbnail.png)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4QuwgCilCVk&t=1s)

[[Full Text of Video Message](../../pdfs/2025-holiday-video-message-transcript.pdf) (PDF)]

Dear Campus Community,

Lesili and I [extend our warmest wishes](https://nam11.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.youtube.com%2Fwatch%3Fv%3D4QuwgCilCVk&data=05%7C02%7Cmpignotti%40cpp.edu%7Ca6a4c66c84e246de347408de3c0b74aa%7C164ba61e39ec4f5d89ffaa1f00a521b4%7C0%7C0%7C639014229099116412%7CUnknown%7CTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJFbXB0eU1hcGkiOnRydWUsIlYiOiIwLjAuMDAwMCIsIlAiOiJXaW4zMiIsIkFOIjoiTWFpbCIsIldUIjoyfQ%3D%3D%7C0%7C%7C%7C&sdata=8rX9nX0YXR0Gz9DB%2BCvqcd9qC%2BWTonAJr9sQ4j7kly0%3D&reserved=0 "Original URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4QuwgCilCVk. Click or tap if you trust this link.") to you for a peaceful winter break and a joyful holiday season with those who matter most. We hope this time offers you opportunities to rest, recharge and reconnect after a busy and productive year.

We are deeply thankful for everything you contribute to our university, our students and each other. May this season bring you moments of reflection, good health and renewed energy for the year ahead. We wish you a safe, happy and prosperous new year.

*Happy holidays!*

Dr. Iris S. Levine
Interim President
