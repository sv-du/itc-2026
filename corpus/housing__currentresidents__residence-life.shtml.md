# Residence Life at UHS

## Campus Living at Cal Poly Pomona

Living on campus is an exciting part of the college experience. It's a place where you can build community, grow personally and academically, and make memories that last a lifetime. Our housing communities are designed to support your success, offering a safe, inclusive, and engaging environment. Whether you're joining us for the first time or returning for another year, you'll find opportunities to connect, get involved, and truly make this space your home. Our team is here to support you every step of the way. Welcome home, Broncos!

### **What is a Resident Advisor (RA)?**

A Resident Advisor (RA) is a student leader who lives in your community and is here to support your on-campus experience. RAs are trained to help foster a safe, inclusive, and welcoming environment for all residents.

### **What Do Resident Advisors Do?**

RAs build community by planning events, hosting floor meetings, and encouraging involvement. They also serve as a resource—answering questions, sharing campus info, and helping connect you to support services. Additionally, RAs help uphold community standards and are trained to respond to a variety of student concerns.

### **When Should You Contact Your RA?**

Reach out to your RA if you:

* Have questions about your building or campus life
* Need help navigating a roommate situation
* Are feeling homesick, stressed, or just need someone to talk to
* Want to get more involved in your community
* See or experience something that concerns you

Your RA is here to support you, no matter how big or small the situation may feel.

## support to keep your space comfortable

### Maintaining Your Home Away from Home

Our Facilities team works hard to keep your living space safe, clean, and in good working condition. If you experience any issues in your room or community areas, such as plumbing or lighting problems, be sure to submit a work order as soon as possible. Timely requests help us resolve problems quickly and maintain a comfortable environment for everyone.

[Submit a Work Order through your Housing Portal](../facilities/submit-a-housing-work-order.shtml)
