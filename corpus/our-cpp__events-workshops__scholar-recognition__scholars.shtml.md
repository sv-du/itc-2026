# Scholar Recognition

![2023 Scholar Recognition](../../img/events/scholar-recognition/banners/scholar_recognition_banner2023.png)

## Welcome

On May 2, 2024 Cal Poly Pomona students, faculty and family members gathered to celebrate the achievements of undergraduate researchers and mentors at the Scholar Recognition Ceremony hosted by the [Office of Undergraduate Research (OUR)](../../index.shtml).The hallmark of a Cal Poly Pomona education is learn-by-doing. On this day we took time to recognize and congratulate students for their initiative to learn-by-doing through identifying and implementing solutions to real world problems and opportunities.

The Scholar Recognition Ceremony is a banquet held during the spring quarter where we are able to recognize undergraduate researchers, mentors, and faculty for their hard work the past year. At this ceremony we celebrated research done by many programs, as well as those who received Research Distinction.
