# Attend a Formatting and Electronic Submission Workshop

If you are a graduate student working on your thesis or project, you are invited to attend the workshops offered by the Formatting/Uploading Review Team.

The workshops will take place through zoom. Once you register, you will get a thank you notification along with the zoom links. Please make sure you note it.

The workshop will cover how to submit electronically, and a review of formatting guidelines for preparing your paper for theses or your abstract for master projects. This will give you an opportunity to ask any questions you may have about the process for submitting your thesis report or project abstract to Graduate Studies.

For resources and where to obtain the PowerPoint presentations please follow the [Formatting Guide.](https://libguides.library.cpp.edu/formatreview%20)

\*\*The following days have been scheduled for the Formatting/Uploading Review Team to host the Formatting and Electronic Submission Workshops in Spring 2025:

* [March 5th, 4-5 pm](https://forms.office.com/pages/responsepage.aspx?id=HqZLFuw5XU-J_6ofAKUhtDSi_LEhMSFEk19g15ng8cpUQzlDRUhMMUhPMU1IVUZDQlNMUTk1T0ZVQy4u&route=shorturl)
* [April 17th, 4-5 pm](https://cpp.libcal.com/event/14080613)

If you have any questions, please contact the Formatting/Upload Review Team at formatreview@cpp.edu.

Take the [workshop survey](https://cpp.libwizard.com/f/libraryformattingworkshop) after you have attended the workshop.

\*\*Please note this registration is for the workshops only. If you want to schedule an appointment to have your paper reviewed, please contact formatreview@cpp.edu.
