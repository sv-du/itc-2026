# Application

![](../../../img/summer/slimbannercars.jpg)

The **Cal Poly Pomona Creative Activities & Research Symposium (CPP CARS)** is an annual event hosted by the Office of Undergraduate Research. This event is held in the Summer and provides an opportunity for our undergraduate and graduate students to share their original research, scholarly and creative activities project they completed during the last academic year or summer.

This event includes creative activities such as dance performances, poetry reading, and entrepreneurial pitches for budding startups. CARS is a non-competitive symposium.

**![Step 1 on a blue background](https://i.imgur.com/SSCmSlH.png)**

## Submit Application

Submit your application to present at the Annual Creative Activities and Research Symposium (CARS). Each project team should submit only **one application**. There is the option to pause and resume your application. The list below details information you will need to have to complete your application.

To view the CARS Application Information Session, please see the [“Resources”](#resources) section below.

1. Bronco IDs for all Cal Poly Pomona Students
2. Full Name of each group member (please check spelling)
3. Program Name or Lab Assignment for both Cal Poly Pomona and non–Cal Poly Pomona Students
4. Abstract (250 words or less)
5. Faculty Mentor's name and email address
6. Institutional Review Board (IRB) or Animal Use and Care Committee (ACUC) Protocol ID (if applicable)

**The deadline to apply is July 16, 2021 at 11:59 pm.**

## RSVP for CARS

**The OUR will send acceptance notification emails to all presenters by July 22, 2021. The acceptance notification will have a “Submission ID” that presenters must use to RSVP for the event. Presenters must RSVP for the event by July 30, 2021.** **Presenters who do not RSVP will not be included in the event program or schedule.**
