# High Fives

![characters applauding](/admin-affairs/img/high-five-banner2.jpg)

## What is a High Five?

"High fives" are given to staff in the Administrative Affairs division by anyone who would like to share appreciation or recognition for outstanding work. Submitted "high fives" are reviewed and posted to this web page on a monthly basis. Take a look below at some of the outstanding work our staff has provided.

If you would like to give someone a "High Five" please use the link below.

* [Online form to submit a high five!](https://cpp.az1.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_bO7FgvAeCG2pkTI)

High fives updated: February 3, 2025

![silver medal](img/silver-medal-high-five.jpeg)

## **High Fives!**

**Danielle Murillo, Cal Poly Pomona Enterprise Foundation**

Your amazing smile and laughter are contagious. You're an amazing worker. Cal Poly Pomona is very grateful to have you. You care so much for the students, students are grateful to have you.

-Anonymous

**Justin Alvarez, Cal Poly Pomona Enterprise Foundation**

Justin's ability to jump in and problem solve has had a significant positive impact on our department and overall team success. One of the qualities I admire most about Justin is their collaborative spirit. They excel at fostering teamwork, encouraging open communication, and ensuring that every voice is heard. In addition to their technical expertise, Justin consistently goes above and beyond to ensure that tasks are completed efficiently and effectively. Their proactive approach and attention to detail have helped us avoid potential challenges and meet deadlines with confidence.

-Melody Young, Cal Poly Pomona Foundation Inc

**Zaria Towery, Cal Poly Pomona Enterprise Foundation**

I wanted to nominate one of our University Village Graduate Coordinators Zaza Towery for the incredible work she did in facilitating our spring semester evacuation drill at the University Village last night. This was her first time coordinating the process and while a bit nervous to start, she led our team confidently and directly. Through her facilitation, we were able to evacuate all of our residents from their apartments 30 minutes faster then our Fall semester evacuation drill! Great job Zaza!

-Kyle Sirowy, Foundation Enterprises

**William Chung, Cal Poly Pomona Enterprise Foundation**

William created and presented on an excellent modeling tool used to prepare the Teaching Horse proforma. Compliments came in from the Provost, consultants, and committee.

-Jared Ceja, Foundation Enterprises

**Edith Ramirez, Bronco Bookstore**

Edith was our main resource for BroncoBound an incredibly successful and popular event that that is the biggest sales day of CPP Logo merchandise of the year. Her partnership is much appreciated.

-Clint Aase, Bronco Bookstore

**Joseph Phillpy, Facilities Planning & Management**

Last week rainstorm and brief power outage had taken down the SCADA system that controls the production and distribution of potable water on campus. Joe worked feverishly with a contractor and the Division of Drinking Water to maintain delivery of water the campus while troubleshooting to resolve the issues from Friday through Saturday. Thanks to Joseph's efforts, the water system was back in operation on Saturday afternoon, and he deserves a big High Five.

-George Lwin, Facilities Planning & Management
