## QuickStart: Security and Privacy for Teaching Remotely

**When using Zoom for teaching remotely keep privacy in mind. Protect your student's privacy and your own by using the following tips:**

| Tutorial | Format |
| --- | --- |
| Start or schedule a secured meeting with a password: [Meeting Passwords](https://cpp.service-now.com/ehelp?id=kb_article&sys_id=10879ff0db3f8410ae3a567b4b96191c#Meeting_Passwords) | eHelp page |
| Don't show meeting passwords on your calendar invite: [Using Your Outlook Calendar Securely with Zoom](https://cpp.service-now.com/ehelp?id=kb_article&sys_id=10879ff0db3f8410ae3a567b4b96191c#Using_Your_Outlook_Calendar_Securely_with_Zoom) | eHelp page |
| Allow participants to only join the meeting after the host: [Disable the "Join Before Host" Meeting Option](https://cpp.service-now.com/ehelp?id=kb_article&sys_id=10879ff0db3f8410ae3a567b4b96191c#Disable_the_%22Join_Before_Host%22_Meeting_Option) | eHelp page |
| Lock a meeting after all your students have joined: [Lock Your Zoom Session](https://cpp.service-now.com/ehelp?id=kb_article&sys_id=10879ff0db3f8410ae3a567b4b96191c#Lock_Your_Zoom_Session) | eHelp page |
| Expel a participant or all participants: [Remove a Participant from Your Zoom Meeting or Webinar](https://cpp.service-now.com/ehelp?id=kb_article&sys_id=10879ff0db3f8410ae3a567b4b96191c#Remove_a_Participant_from_Your_Zoom_Meeting_or_Webinar) | eHelp page |
| Mute/unmute a participant or all participants: [Mute All And Unmute All](https://support.zoom.us/hc/en-us/articles/203435537-Mute-All-And-Unmute-All) | eHelp page |
| Enable/disable a participant or all participants to record: [Managing participants in a meeting](https://support.zoom.us/hc/en-us/articles/115005759423-Managing-participants-in-a-meeting) | eHelp page |
| Meetings and Webinar recordings can be stored on the host's local device: [Local Recording](https://support.zoom.us/hc/en-us/articles/201362473-Local-Recording) | eHelp page |
| Upload to Kaltura (read [Kaltura's privacy advantage over Youtube](https://www.cpp.edu/facultycenter/teaching-continuity/deliver-content-online/#kaltura_over_youtube)): [Secure Your Meeting Recording](https://cpp.service-now.com/ehelp?id=kb_article&sys_id=10879ff0db3f8410ae3a567b4b96191c#Secure_Your_Meeting_Recording) | eHelp page |

**In-Room Amplification for Most Classrooms:**

There is great news for our faculty struggling to be heard through your masks! Thanks to [MediaVision](https://www.cpp.edu/mediavision/) & [IT's Classroom Technology Support](https://www.cpp.edu/it/) group, Cal Poly Pomona is now offering in-room voice amplification for all PC smart classrooms. The in-room amplification utilizes classroom’s built in speaker system but does require that you bring your own device (BYOD) for the microphone.

Although any usb microphone will work, headset style is strongly recommended. MediaVision's pick is the [FIFINE Wireless Microphone K031B](https://www.amazon.com/Wireless-Microphones-FIFINE-Microphone-Transmitter/dp/B074C125TN/ref%3Dsr_1_1_sspa?gclid=Cj0KCQiAxoiQBhCRARIsAPsvo-wmI0EZrODjCi3slP8-_p0niqDzHvDPmriwiSKw52XV5g376-5hCxQaAvtTEALw_wcB&hvadid=409979365839&hvdev=c&hvlocphy=9031245&hvnetw=g&hvqmt=e&hvrand=1600901072189515135&hvtargid=kwd-383507056239&hydadcr=29835_12016159&keywords=fifine+k031&qid=1644366377&sr=8-1-spons&psc=1&spLa=ZW5jcnlwdGVkUXVhbGlmaWVyPUEzM0NQQlExOUw0VkxaJmVuY3J5cHRlZElkPUEwODQ0MDY0MVA2ODM3M1dCODFJSCZlbmNyeXB0ZWRBZElkPUEwODU1ODU3SDFWTjVXTTlRNThHJndpZGdldE5hbWU9c3BfYXRmJmFjdGlvbj1jbGlja1JlZGlyZWN0JmRvTm90TG9nQ2xpY2s9dHJ1ZQ==).
