## Welcome

Dr. Holly He joins the Accounting Department at California State Polytechnic University, Pomona in 2024. She currently teaches financial accounting. Prior to joining Cal Poly Pomona, Dr. He taught in University of California- Irvine (UCI). Dr. He holds a bachelor's degree in accounting and a master's degree in Accounting from Renmin University of China, a Master in Finance from University of Rochester, and a PhD in Accounting from UCI. Her research interests include financial accounting and auditing.
