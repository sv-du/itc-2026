# Community Innovation Hub Reservations

![downtown pomona hub](img/banners/contact-us/hub_1.jpg)

## Create. Collaborate. Thrive. Where community and innovation meet.

Located in the heart of downtown Pomona, the Community Innovation Hub at [101 W. Mission Blvd., Suite 111](https://www.google.com/maps?ll=34.055612,-117.750482&z=15&t=m&hl=en&gl=US&mapclient=embed&q=101+W+Mission+Blvd+%23111+Pomona,+CA+91766), is a welcoming space designed to inspire collaboration, creativity, and community impact. Whether you're hosting a workshop, meeting, or event, we’re here to support your vision.

Please note: Submitting this form is a **request only** and does **not** guarantee a reservation. A member of our Hub staff will review your submission and respond within **2–3 business days** to confirm availability or request additional information.

We appreciate your interest and look forward to helping you bring your ideas to life.
