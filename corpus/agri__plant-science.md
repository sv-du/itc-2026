# Department of Plant Science

![Students use a tractor to plant radishes at Spadra Farm.](img/2019-spadra-radishes-1160x928.jpg)

## Become by Growing

Come grow with us. Discover the variety of career paths that are possible, from agronomy and horticulture to urban agriculture, arboriculture, and sports turf management. Get hands-on experience growing crops on the campus farm or in greenhouses. Explore hydroponic farming or beekeeping. The possibilities are endless.

Ready

The plant science degree meets all coursework requirements for the California pest control advisor license. Graduating students can take the exam immediately and earn their license.

### Grow Crops

From field production to greenhouses, hydroponics to organic, controlled environment, and sustainable methods, learn about the many ways to grow crops.

Card 2 of 9

![Students and an instructor in protective gear examine a section of a beehive.](img/2018-bee-science-920x702.jpg)

### Work with Bees

Learn how to raise bees and their importance as pollinators.

Card 3 of 9

![An instructor uses a water probe on a section of turf.](img/2014-turf-moss-920x702.jpg)

### Learn About Turf Management

Turf management paves the way for careers in sports, managing golf courses and athletic fields. Our students have worked at Dodger Stadium, the Rose Bowl, the Los Angeles Memorial Coliseum ─ and even interned at the Super Bowl.

Card 4 of 9

![A student with safety harness and ropes in a tree.](img/2019-arboriculture-powell-920x702.jpg)

### Discover Arboriculture

Learn how to trees are maintained and cared for.

Card 5 of 9

![A female student harvests grapes.](img/2018-grape-harvest-920x702.jpg)

### Harvest Grapes

Our campus farm includes three vineyards that are harvested annually. The grapes are sent to a winery to be made into Horsehill Vineyards wine, which is sold in the Farm Store.

Card 6 of 9

![Professor Anna Soper and a student examine a tube of insects collected on the campus farm.](img/2018-insect-collection-soper-920x570.jpg)

### Collect Insects

Study insects. Learn which ones are beneficial and which ones are pests.

Card 7 of 9

![Professor Aaron Fox speaks about healthy soils at a Spadra Farm event.](img/2019-spadra-soils-fox-920x702.jpg)

### Study Soil Science

Healthy soils are essential for growing crops. Discover how to make soil healthier.

Card 8 of 9

![A student tills the Horsehill MicroFarm](img/2022-micro-farm-920x702.jpg)

### Urban Agriculture

The Greater Los Angeles area is a sprawling metropolis whose denizens still need food. Discover about small-scale crop production in urban areas.

### Clubs, Field Trips, Competitions

Get involved. Demonstrate leadership. Participate in competitions. Explore and connect with industry.
