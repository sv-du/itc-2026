# Immersive Learning Experience in Tokyo, Spring Break 2026

![picture of the city in Japan](../../img/tokyo-japan-spring-2026.jpg)

## About the Program

Join us for an unforgettable educational journey through one of the world’s most vibrant and inspiring cities, **Tokyo, Japan**. Immerse yourself in the heart of Tokyo during one of Japan’s most breathtaking seasons - Sakura (Cherry) Blossom. This 9-day study abroad program offers students across all majors a unique opportunity to explore Japan’s rich traditions and cutting-edge innovations through cultural fieldwork and guided experiences.

From the elegance of Kabuki theater and historic temples in Asakusa, to modern marvels like Shibuya Sky and Tokyo Skytree, students will witness firsthand how Japan harmoniously blends the past and the future. Engage with Cal Poly Pomona alumni working in Tokyo’s world-class hospitality scene, enjoy a day at Tokyo Disney or DisneySea, cruise through Tokyo Bay, and stroll through cherry blossom-lined parks.

Embark on an enriching educational experience in one of the world’s most dynamic and inspiring cities. This is an opportunity that will undoubtedly become a defining highlight of your academic journey at Cal Poly Pomona.

![Zebra crossing in Shinjuku, Tokyo at sunset](../../img/japan-image-2.jpg)

## Program Information

**Course Name:** CLS 4820: Study Abroad – International Fieldwork

**Open to all majors**

**Earn 3 GE (Area 3C or 4C) credits** in CLS 4820

**Dates:** March 28, 2026 to April 5, 2026

![picture of imperial palace in Tokyo ](../../img/japan-image-3.jpg)

## Program Highlights

* Experience Japan during Cherry Blossom season
* Visit Kabuki theater & historic temples in Asakusa
* Visit Tokyo Skytree & Shibuya Sky
* Tokyo Bay cruise and DisneySea/Disney
* Alumni networking in Tokyo’s hospitality scene
* Sakura-lined parks & cultural fieldwork

To learn more about this program, contact Professor Linchi Kwok at lkwok@cpp.edu.
