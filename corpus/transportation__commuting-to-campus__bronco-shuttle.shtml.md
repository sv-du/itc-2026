# Bronco Express Shuttle

## ![Bronco Shuttle](../img/shuttle.jpg)

For questions regarding the Bronco Express Shuttles please contact us at broncoshuttle@cpp.edu.

## Shuttle Routes

### The Current Apartments & Main Campus/Village Shuttles

**THE CURRENT APARTMENTS SHUTTLE**

**Bronco ID Required**

This free service is provided exclusively for Cal Poly Pomona students, faculty, and staff. A Bronco ID is required to ride the shuttle.

**Monday - Thursday**

7:30 am - 10:00pm

**Friday**

7:30 am - 6:00pm

**Shuttle Stops**

* Student Services Building
* The Current Apartments

**NOTE:** *Shuttle not in service during winter intercession, spring break, summer break and campus holidays.*

**MAIN CAMPUS/VILLAGE SHUTTLE**

**Bronco ID Required**

This free service is provided exclusively for Cal Poly Pomona students, faculty, and staff. A Bronco ID is required to ride the shuttle.

**Monday - Thursday**

7:30 am - 11:00pm

**Friday**

7:30 am - 6:00pm

**Shuttle Stops in Order Clockwise (M1)**

* Student Services Building
* Lot E2 near Building 89
* Student Services Building
* Lot B
* Temple/University SW Corner
* Lyle Center @ Top of Hill (Until 5:30PM)
* Farm Store
* The Village
* Parking Structure 2 South Side
* Collins College near Building 79B
* University Dr near Building 7
* University Dr near Building 94
* University Dr near Rose Garden
* Parking Structure 1 West Side
* Student Services Building (Repeat Loop)

**NOTE:** *Shuttle not in service during winter intercession, spring break, summer break and campus holidays.*

### Pomona North Metrolink Shuttle

**METROLINK & METRO A LINE SHUTTLE**

**Bronco ID Required**

This free service is provided exclusively for Cal Poly Pomona students, faculty, and staff. A Bronco ID is required to ride the shuttle.

**From Pomona North Station to CPP Building 94**

**Monday thru Thursday**

Last pickup from Pomona North Station is 9:40pm.

**Friday**

Last pickup from Pomona North Station is 5:40pm

Departs [Pomona North Station](https://maps.app.goo.gl/AF43jcjELFQ6qwma7) at 7:40am 8:40am, 9:40am, 10:40am, 11:40am,    1:40pm, 2:40pm, 3:40pm, 4:40pm, 5:40pm, *6:40pm, 7:40pm, 8:40pm, 9:40pm.*

***NOTE:** Shuttle not in service during campus holidays.*

**From CPP Building 94 to Pomona North Station**

**Monday thru Thursday**

Last pickup from CPP Building 94 is 10:05pm.

**Friday**

Last pickup from CPP Building 94 is 6:05pm.

Departs from [CPP Building 94](https://maps.app.goo.gl/jhVzmmKzKbmi27Mt7) at 8:05am, 9:05am, 10:05am, 11:05am, 12:05pm, 1:05pm, 2:05pm, 3:05pm, 4:05pm, 5:05pm, 6:05pm, *7:05pm, 8:05pm, 9:05pm, 10:05pm.*

****NOTE:** Shuttle not in service during campus holidays.**
