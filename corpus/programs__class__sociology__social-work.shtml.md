## About the Program

Do you dream of making a real impact on people’s lives and strengthening communities? Our Social Work Option combines the power of sociology with practical tools to help you do just that.
You’ll dive into important real-world issues like mental health, child welfare and elder care and gain valuable hands-on experience to prepare you for graduate school or a rewarding career in social services.

## The Polytechnic Approach

Following a broad foundation in sociological methodologies and theories, you will engage in coursework that introduces you to and immerses you in the social work profession. From internships at local agencies to designing community intervention programs, you’ll make a difference right away and gain the experience needed to enter the social work field with confidence.

## Courses

The Social Work option is designed for those who are passionate about making a direct impact on individuals, families, and communities. This program emphasizes the practical application of sociological theories and research methods to social work practice, providing you with the skills and knowledge needed to address social problems and improve the well-being of diverse populations. You will be well-prepared for careers in social work, case management, community organizing, or advocacy, and equipped to pursue advanced degrees in Social Work, Sociology, or related fields, leading to licensure or specialized roles within the field of social services.

For the most up-to-date information on courses for this program, visit the course catalog or contact an advisor.
