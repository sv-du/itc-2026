# Mitchell Robertson

![](../../img/members/mitch-member.png)

## Mitchell Robertson

## **AMI Club Liaison**

**Major:** Technology Operations Management

**Interest:** Data Analysis, Forecasting Analysis, Data Visualization

**Skills:** Data Analysis, MS Excel, SPSS, Public Speaking

Mitch, originally from Orange County, is currently a 4th-year student studying Technology Operations Management (TOM). He specializes in data analysis using Microsoft Excel and spends time visualizing data to present the findings in an informative way. He also has skills in public speaking to present the analytical findings. On the side, Mitch loves to watch the Los Angeles Angels and surf at beaches. He plans to become a data analyst with a specialization in demand forecasting after graduation.
