## High Performance Computing

Whether you’re working with large datasets, running complex calculations, or getting started, we understand the role of technology in modern research and are here to help.

[Explore HPC Resources](high-performance-computing.shtml)

## Advanced Computing Initiatives

[### AI at Cal Poly Pomona

A leader in academics, research, and workforce development, the university is positioned at the forefront of the artificial intelligence revolution.](artificial-intelligence-at-cpp.shtml)

[### Cybersecurity

Cal Poly Pomona offers a nationally recognized cyber education experience, our students learn skills outside of their core disciplines preparing them for in-demand careers.](/cyber/)

[### GIS Research

CPP is a hub for spatial and geospatial scholarly and creative activities.](/class/cgisr/index.shtml)

[### High Performance Computing

The HPC initiative supports research and instruction within the colleges.](index.shtml)

[### Red Hat Academy

Red Hat Academy courses are available to all currently enrolled students at no cost. Students prepare foundational skills in cloud computing, software, administration, or IT services; not just Linux.](red-hat-academy.shtml)

[### AI and ML Webinar Series

Recently, NVIDIA, Mark III Systems, and Cal Poly Pomona hosted an Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) webinar series. Features industry experts who dive into current trends.](ai-ml-webinar-series.shtml)

## Training Resources

[### What is Generative AI

Learn the fundamentals of GenAI and how these models automate repetitive, general, or computational tasks, allowing professionals time to focus on quality work and reduce administrative burden.](https://www.linkedin.com/learning/what-is-generative-ai/generative-ai-is-a-tool-in-service-of-humanity "opens a new window")

[### Introduction to Prompt Design

Develop an understanding of prompting conversational chatbots and large language models (LLMs). Participants learn the types, patterns, and best practices to work efficiently with GenAI.](https://CSU.sumtotal.host/core/pillarRedirect?relyingParty=LM&url=app%2Fmanagement%2FLMS_ActDetails.aspx%3FActivityId%3D142171%26UserMode%3D0)

[### Responsible AI

Learn and build trust in AI tools while remaining skeptical of its recommendations; Responsible AI focuses on the positive impact Copilot, ChatGPT, and others can provide, whether designing and developing AI applications or using GenAI in our everyday work and study.](https://CSU.sumtotal.host/core/pillarRedirect?relyingParty=LM&url=app%2Fmanagement%2FLMS_ActDetails.aspx%3FActivityId%3D142170%26UserMode%3D0 "opens a new window")
