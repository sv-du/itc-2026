[Search Giving Options](https://www.cpp.edu/giving/search.shtml)

## Planned Giving

[![Group of CPP students](https://www.cpp.edu/giving/img/gift-plan1.jpg)](https://cpp.giftlegacy.com/)

Planned gifts can be designed to meet your personal philanthropic and financial goals.

[Explore Gift Planning](https://cpp.giftlegacy.com/)

## Tribute & Memorial Gifts

Is your gift in memory or honor of a loved one? [You can make a lasting tribute to honor a loved one or commemorate a special occasion/milestone.](https://www.cpp.edu/giving/cpp/tributes.shtml)

## Matching Gifts

Not sure if your company matches donations? [Check our list of matching companies](https://www.cpp.edu/~giving/ways-to-give/matching-gifts.shtml).

## Recurring Gifts

To establish, amend, or cancel a recurring gift, please contact gifts@cpp.edu or call (909) 869-2914.
