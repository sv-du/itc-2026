# Kellogg Honors College

![Students participate in a club meeting, smiling and working together at a large table covered with notebooks, snacks, and devices.](img/homepage/khc-1.jpg)

## Join a Community of Scholars

The Kellogg Honors College (KHC) is a community of scholars at Cal Poly Pomona that promotes inclusive academic excellence and intellectual exploration through honors courses, civic engagement, and special events. Students can join the KHC as incoming freshmen, current first-year freshmen finishing one year at CPP, and incoming transfers.

## What do Honors Students do?

![A professor engages with a full classroom of attentive students during a lecture or seminar in a modern academic setting.](img/homepage/program-1.jpg)

### Take Honors Courses

These are taught by some of our most talented faculty and are capped at 15-20 students per section. Our courses are part of the General Education curriculum. Student must maintain an average cumulative 3.3 GPA. Freshman must take 7 honors courses, Sophomores must take 5 honors courses, Transfers must take 4 honors courses.

![Two students collaborate on a Día de los Muertos altar decoration, arranging marigold flowers and colorful paper crafts.](img/homepage/program-2.jpg)

### Civic Engagement

Students will be required to participate in community service activities every year. We provide many opportunities both on and off campus.

![ student presenting their poster on scientific research explains findings to an attendee during a research showcase event.](img/homepage/program-3.jpg)

### Engaged Learning Experience

Students will conduct an independent research project under the guidance of a
faculty mentor and present their research at our annual symposium.

## Why Become an Kellogg Honors Student?

1. ### Scholarship Opportunities

   Scholarships are available for Honors Students, apply through the Bronco Scholarship Portal.
2. ### Access to Priority Registration

   KHC students in good standing receive priority registration when they take honors courses.
3. ### Research And Career Preparedness Opportunities for Students

   Present your research project at the annual symposium, regional or national conferences! Enrichment grants provided by KHC for students presenting their research or participating in study abroad.
4. ### Engage in a Community of Scholars

   At KHC, we center community. Get access to the honors commons, a student gathering space with computers and printing services. Honors students participate in community service opportunities throughout the year. Free field trips and cultural events are also available to honors students throughout the academic year
5. ### KHC is a signature PolyX experience

   Students who complete the program are guaranteed involvement in high impact, learn-by-doing practices--key components of a polytechnic education.
