## California Tastemakers

The Golden State boasts some of the best restaurants, wineries and cafes. CPP alumni and students are elevating the flavors and experiences across the state.

[Read California Tastemakers](https://www.cpp.edu/cppmag/crafting-the-flavors-of-california/california-tastemakers/index.shtml "Read California Tastemakers. ")

Engineering graduate builds strength, smarts and resolve through competitive weightlifting club.
