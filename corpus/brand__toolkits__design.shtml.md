# Design Toolkit

## Tools and Resources for Designers

[### Brand Guidelines

The Brand Guidelines provide details, specifications and considerations for designers.](https://www.cpp.edu/brand/authenticated/cpp-brand-guidelines.pdf)

### Canva

StratComm maintains a Canva team site that includes a brand kit and design templates for print and digital applications. Email brand@cpp.edu for user access.

[### Logos

Cal Poly Pomona's primary and secondary logos can be downloaded through Canto, our digital asset management system.](https://cpp.canto.com/v/brand/)

[### Transducer CPP Font

Transducer CPP Extended Medium and Transducer CPP Extended Bold are our primary headline fonts. Download via Canto, our digital asset management system.](https://cpp.canto.com/v/brand/)

[### Proxima Sera and Usual

Proxima Sera is a serif font for headlines and subheads. Usual is a practical font primarily for body copy and small information. Both are available via Adobe Fonts.](https://fonts.adobe.com/)

[### Athletics

If you’re designing for Bronco Athletics, please refer to the Cal Poly Pomona Athletics Style Guide (PDF).](https://broncoathletics.com/documents/2020/8/27/Cal_Poly_Pomona.pdf)

## Color

### Primary and Secondary Colors

Cal Poly Pomona has two primary colors and nine secondary colors. RGB and HEX codes are optimized for digital use, while CMYK and PMS are for print materials. Color accessibility is critical. The approved background and type color combinations are shown below and listed in each pop-up card. Refer to the Brand Guidelines PDF for more details.
