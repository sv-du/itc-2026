# Learning Goals

**PO 1:  Students completing the BSBA program with the FRL option will be able to communicate effectively in a business environment.**

*Corresponding student learning outcomes (SLOs):*

**SLO 1a**: FRL students will demonstrate oral communication skills during individual or group presentations.

**SLO 1b**: FRL students will demonstrate written communication skills by producing quality business documents.

**PO 2:  Students completing the BSBA program with the FRL option will be able to use critical thinking in making financial decisions.**

*Corresponding student learning outcomes (SLOs):*

**SLO 2a**: FRL students will demonstrate the ability to identify key issues.

**SLO 2b:** FRL students will demonstrate the ability to find, read and evaluate information sources.

**SLO 2c:** FRL students will demonstrate the ability to solve unstructured problems using appropriate quantitative and qualitative skills.

**PO 3:  Students completing the BSBA program with the FRL option will be able to use the Technological skill required in the finance, real estate and Law industry.**

*Corresponding student learning outcomes (SLOs):*

**SLO 3a:** FRL students will be able to use computer software such as Excel, investment related software, and real estate related software to solve related problems

**PO 4:  Students completing the BSBA program with the FRL option will be able to demonstrate an understanding of international aspects of finance.**

 C *orresponding student learning outcomes (SLOs):*

**SLO 4a:** Students will be able to analyze the effect of international financial changes and their risk.

**PO 5:**   **Students completing the BSBA program with the FRL option will be able to demonstrate awareness of the ethical and legal aspects of the global and diverse business environment.**

*Corresponding student learning outcomes (SLOs):*

**SLO 5a:** Students will demonstrate the ability to recognize key aspects of ethical issues.
