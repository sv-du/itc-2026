# Undeclared and Exploratory Advising: Your Path Starts Here!

![An advisor works with a student on academic planning](../img/bac/undc-hero.png)

## Get In Touch

[### Schedule an Appointment on CPP Connect

Make an appointment to meet with us. When you make an appointment, choose Advising and then scroll to the Undeclared Advising section.](../cppconnect/index.shtml "opens a new window")

### Email Us

Email us at undeclared@cpp.edu. Please email us from your CPP email address and include your Bronco ID number in your message.

### Visit Us In Person

Our office is in the Student Services Building, First Floor (Blue Counter).

## Welcome!

Joining Cal Poly Pomona as an undeclared student will help you embrace the unknown and the journey of self-discovery. This is the time to explore and grow.

As an Undeclared student, you will have a wide array of opportunities to critically think about your career and life goals. We are here to assist you with academic advising, career and major exploration. We hope that you will be open to explore all of the different options and paths each major has to offer. Our goal is to help you declare a major within your first year. We look forward to joining you in this exploration path.

If you are currently exploring majors and thinking about switching majors, we advise you to utilize the resources or schedule an appointment with the Undeclared, Exploratory, and Pre-Health Professional Advising Coordinator. We are here to support you throughout your exploration process!

## About Our Programs

### Undeclared Advising

The Undeclared Advising Program is a program that serves incoming first-time freshmen with the opportunity to engage students with the major and career exploration process. You must apply to Cal Poly Pomona as an Undeclared student.

### Exploratory Advising

Interested in exploring other majors and do not know where to start? We can help you with finding the best fit for you through resources and advising.
