![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white.svg)

## The 2025-2026 Essay Contest Is Now Closed

FYE hosts an annual essay contest, giving students the chance to share their reflections on the Common Read. The winners of the 2025-26 writing contest will be announced at the Common Read Keynote Event with Mónica Guzmán on Tuesday, March 10.

[Learn More and Register Here!](common-read-events.shtml "opens a new window")
