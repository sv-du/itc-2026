# Fueling Innovation, Empowering Entrepreneurs

## Accelerating Impact

The Center for Entrepreneurship and Innovation (CEI) at Cal Poly Pomona’s College of Business is a dynamic ecosystem where big ideas meet bold action. As a hub of creativity and collaboration, CEI cultivates entrepreneurial talent, supports emerging ventures, and fosters meaningful partnerships that drive innovation across the region.

Whether you’re a student dreaming of launching a startup, a faculty innovator looking for support, or a community member ready to spark change, CEI offers the resources, mentorship, and network to turn vision into venture.

## Executive Summary

CEI is a vibrant platform for developing entrepreneurial leaders and catalyzing business growth. Rooted in experiential learning and real-world impact, the Center connects students and stakeholders to innovation pipelines, startup incubation, growth acceleration, and commercialization opportunities. With an emphasis on diversity, inclusion, and economic mobility, CEI is poised to become a regional leader in entrepreneurship education and innovation support.

## What We Offer

### Startup Incubation & Investment-Ready Acceleration

Structured support to transform early-stage ideas into investor-ready ventures. From business modeling to pitch refinement and market validation, we help startups scale strategically and sustainably.

### Experiential Learning & Innovation Labs

Immersive, hands-on environments that empower students and founders to ideate, prototype, and problem-solve in real-time—bridging classroom knowledge with real-world impact.

### Mentorship & Strategic Partnerships

Access a powerful network of successful entrepreneurs, industry veterans, corporate partners, and faculty experts who provide guidance, industry insights, and growth-oriented collaboration opportunities.

### Workshops, Pitch Competitions & Executive Speaker Series

Dynamic, high-impact programming that hones entrepreneurial skill sets, sparks innovation, and opens doors to capital, mentorship, and visibility.

### Professional Development & Founder Bootcamps

Accelerate your venture and your career through targeted training in business strategy, leadership, investor relations, and startup scaling—delivered via interactive bootcamps and executive education modules.

### Customized Certificate Programs

Flexible, stackable credentials tailored for emerging founders, working professionals, and corporate innovators—developed in collaboration with Cal Poly Pomona faculty and industry partners.

### Funding Access & Capital Navigation

Guidance and support to pursue seed funding, angel investment, venture capital, private equity, and grant opportunities—helping founders secure the resources they need to launch and grow.

## Join the Movement

### Be Part

Of a thriving entrepreneurial community that’s redefining the future of business and innovation in Southern California. Whether you're a student ready to launch your first venture, a professional eager to mentor the next generation of innovators, or a partner looking to invest in high-impact initiatives—CEI is your gateway to growth, connection, and change.
