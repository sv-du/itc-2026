# Internships

![Martinez and Rojas](../img/hacu-2.jpg)

## New Possibilities

Internships and fellowships are typically short-term opportunities for students to gain professional or academic experience. Most of the opportunities highlighted below are paid, or offer academic credit; they range from internships at national museums to fellowships here on campus to cultivate research skills and to prepare students for future academic and professional opportunities....

## Featured Internships

[![Getty interns](../img/gettyv3.jpg)

### Getty Marrow Undergraduate Internships

This program funds 10-week paid internships at cultural organizations (particularly museums) across Los Angeles for outstanding students who are members of underrepresented groups. Click for program details!](https://www.getty.edu/projects/getty-marrow-undergraduate-internships/%20 "opens a new window")

[![Justice Corps](../img/justicecorpsoriginalv2.jpg)

### JusticeCorps

This organization offers both minimum-time and (compensated) full-time positions that place students in California’s court-based self-help centers for self-represented litigants.](https://courts.ca.gov/programs-initiatives/justicecorps%20 "opens a new window")

[![HACU Intern Program ](../img/hacu3.png)

### Hispanic Association of Colleges and Universities

This internship program places students from the Hispanic community in paid semester-long internships within federal agencies, corporations and non-profit organizations in Washington, D.C. Recent CPP alumni have interned at the Library of Congress through this program.](https://hacu.net/programs/hacu-apply-asp/%20 "opens a new window")

## Other Internships

Click the drop-down menu items below to find out more about internship opportunities!

### Government and Public Policy

[**Capital Fellows Program**](https://www.csus.edu/center/center-california-studies/capital-fellows/)

These are public policy fellowships, most of which seem to run the academic year. Fellows are usually based in Sacramento and are paid a monthly stipend.

[**Defense Intelligence Agency**](https://www.dia.mil/Careers/Internships/ "Defense Intelligence Agency")

These are paid summer internships in various branches of the intelligence community (most likely in Washington, D.C.)

### Social Justice and Activism

[**Lutheran Volunteer Corps**](http://lutheranvolunteercorps.org "Lutheran Volunteer Corps (website)")

This organization places volunteers for full-time one-year positions in non-profit organizations located in a variety of major cities around the country. Volunteers are not paid, but are provided with housing and food (room and board).

[**Dream Summer and Immigrant Justice Fellowships**](https://labor.ucla.edu/our-projects/dream-resource-center/dream-summer/ "Dream Summer (website)")

These are paid social-justice fellowships in Los Angeles of varying durations (summer, year-long) designed to empower immigrant youth to “be the next generation of social justice leaders through professional development.”

### Museums, Archives and Public History

[**Los Angeles County Arts Internships**](https://www.lacountyarts.org/opportunities/arts-internship-program-students/about-arts-internship-program-students "LA County Arts Internships (website)")

This program provides paid 10-week summer internships at nonprofit performing, presenting, literary and municipal arts organizations in Los Angeles County.

[**National Council on Public History**](http://ncph.org/jobs/ "NCPH (website)")

This is a listing of jobs and opportunities in the public history profession; click on “internships” on the left side of the page for more specific listings about fellowships and internships.

[**National Museum of the American Indian**](https://americanindian.si.edu/about/internships "NMAI (website)")

These are paid 10-week internships (summer, fall and spring) in Washington, D.C. for students interested in the museum profession and related fields.

[**Smithsonian Internships**](https://internships.si.edu/ "Smithsonian (website)")

This is an overall listing of the numerous internship opportunities (paid and unpaid, of varying durations) at all of the museums that fall under the umbrella of the Smithsonian Institute. (Most of the internships are in Washington, D.C.)

[**National Park Service**](https://www.nps.gov/subjects/youthprograms/jobs-and-internships.htm "NPS (website)")

This page lists the numerous job and paid internship opportunities within the National Park Service. There are many specialized internship programs listed here; see, for instance, the [Latino Heritage Internship Program](https://latinoheritageintern.org/ "LHIP (website)").

### Research Awards and Fellowships

[**McNair Scholarships (Cal Poly Pomona)**](https://www.cpp.edu/mcnair/ "McNair (website)")

This year-long program prepares students for Ph.D. programs by promoting student success through undergraduate research, faculty membership and skill-building workshops.

*\*Numerous CPP history students have received McNair scholarships over the past 10 years.*

[**Michigan Humanities Emerging Research Scholars Program (MICHHERS)**](http://www.rackham.umich.edu/diversity-equity-inclusion/michhers "MICHHERS (website)")

This two-week summer program at the University of Michigan is designed to encourage rising seniors and recent B.A.s to consider pursuing a doctoral degree in the humanities; it allows students to develop a research project in consultation with faculty and graduate students from the University of Michigan. The program (which includes a stipend and covers transportation and housing costs) specifically seeks students who come from an “educational, cultural or geographic background that is underrepresented in graduate study in their discipline in the United States or at the University of Michigan.”
