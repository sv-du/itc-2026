# Virtual Field Trips

Can't make it to AGRIscapes for a field trip? Have no fear. You can access our virtual field trip materials here.

These lessons contain videos, games, and worksheets that are educational and entertaining. Your schoolchildren will learn more about plants, gardening, insects, nutrition, and healthy eating in these lessons.

We hope you enjoy them and make good use of them!
