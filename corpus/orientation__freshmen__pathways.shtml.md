# Learning Pathways

## Which Pathway is Right For You?

## As a part of orientation, we value your voice as an incoming student. We understand that your needs are unique, so we have included learning pathways for you to have a choice in your orientation experience. Below are four learning pathways, please read through each description and select the one that resonates with you the most. Each pathway will include representatives from campus resources and support services that will help you as you transition to Cal Poly Pomona.
