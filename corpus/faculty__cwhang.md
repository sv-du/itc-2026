## Bibliography

Dr. Claire Whang holds a Ph.D. in the area of Retail Merchandising and Consumer Studies from the University of Minnesota. Her primary research interest lies in understanding "how the new technology influences the way consumers think, feel, and behave." Her research topic includes social media marketing, personalized advertising, and voice shopping. Her research is published in several peer-reviewed journals.

## Research Interest

**Retail Technology and Consumer Behavior:**

* How new technology shapes the way people think, feel, and behave
* Understanding how consumers utilize the new shopping-related platforms such as social media, mobile, website, and voice assistants
* Understanding the beneficial and detrimental effects of new technology on consumers
* Personalized retail experience
* Topics include voice shopping, online behavioral targeted ad, 3D virtual stores, AR marketing, Influencer marketing
