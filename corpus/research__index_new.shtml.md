## Vision Statement

Advancing excellence and expanding opportunity in scholarship and innovation to deliver meaningful and transformative impact for our community, California, and the world.

## Mission Statement

We support our polytechnic university in advancing research, securing funding, safeguarding the highest standards in human and animal studies, and translating discoveries into real-world applications and enduring knowledge.
