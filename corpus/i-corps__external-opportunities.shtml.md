I-Corps™ Hub is a network of universities dedicated to helping researchers successfully take their research and translate it into viable Deep Technology in the marketplace.

[**Learn more**](https://www.icorpshubwest.org/about)

## Community Energy Innovation Prize

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) has launched a new American Made Challenge Competition: The Community Energy Innovation Prize, with a dedicated track to support student-led community energy innovation initiatives. Program staff, faculty, or student club/group leaders (with their institution's support) are eligible to apply.

[**Learn more**](https://www.herox.com/CommunityEnergyInnovation)  [**Register**](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/community-energy-innovation-prize-informational-webinar-tickets-721575169757?aff=oddtdtcreator)
