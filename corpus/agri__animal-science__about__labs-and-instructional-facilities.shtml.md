# Where You'll Learn by Doing

## Facilities, Maps, and Pictures

[![Photo of the Building 2 Exterior](../img/2022-building-2-920x702.jpg)

### Building 2

The department is based in the Huntley College of Agriculture in Building 2. Tenure-track faculty and the Student Success Center have their offices here. In addition, several labs are held in Room 111.](/maps?id=1130#!m/276180?share  "opens a new window")

[![A cow stands in the Cattle Unit](../img/2013-cow-920x702.jpg)

### Cattle Unit

Our livestock at the Cattle Unit includes a breeding herd of Angus and Angus Crossbred cattle.](/maps?id=1130#!m/276194?share  "opens a new window")

[![A pig walks by a shelter at the Swine Unit](../img/2019-swine-unit-920x702.jpg)

### Swine Unit

We have various commercial breeds of swine in our animal units.](/maps?id=1130#!m/276194?share  "opens a new window")

[![Sheep eat hay from a feeder at the Sheep Unit.](../img/2023-sheep-unit-feeders-920x702.jpg)

### Sheep Unit

Our sheep unit includes goats and flocks of purebred Rambouillet and Suffolk sheep.](/maps?id=1130#!m/276485?share  "opens a new window")

[![Photo of the Building 67 exterior](../img/2018-ahs-building-920x702.jpg)

### Building 67 (Animal Health Science)

Building 67 is home to our animal health science program, which trains students to become veterinary technicians — the equivalent of nurses in veterinary medicine. The program holds its lectures and laboratory classes (e.g., surgery, radiology, and pharmacology) here.](/maps?id=1130#!m/276535?share  "opens a new window")

[![Female students pose with an Arabian horse at the W.K. Kellogg Arabian Horse Center.](../img/2023-horse-center-920x702.jpg)

### W.K. Kellogg Arabian Horse Center (Building 29)

Learn about equine science or watch a Sunday Horse Show here!](/maps?id=1130#!m/276485?share  "opens a new window")
