# Professional Development

## Tools for Success

Facilities Planning & Management is committed to the professional growth and development of staff members. To support the Campus Strategic Plan (Initiative 5: Advance organizational development and employee excellence), the department invests in personnel by expanding professional development opportunities. This is achieved by offering a range of personal and professional development opportunities for employees tailored to specific roles and pathways for career advancement.

**Tuition Reduction and Fee Waiver Programs**

Tuition reduction and fee waiver are programs designed to reduce or waive state fees for eligible employees to take classes at a CSU campus to improve their job skills and abilities (work-related training) or to obtain the education needed to pursue CSU career goals (career development).

Approved CSU Fee Waivers are only applicable for Regular Academic Session (state-funded) courses and cannot be applied to courses that are Open University/Extension Programs. Please check the [Schedule of Classes](https://schedule.cpp.edu/)for the applicable academic term to determine if the class is “Regular Academic Session.”

For more information, please visit the [CPP benefits website](https://www.cpp.edu/eoda-hr/departments/benefits/index.shtml).

**CSU Professional Development**

The California State University website provides options for staff members to expand their job-related learning.

Visit the [Systemwide Learning and Development website](https://csyou.calstate.edu/Divisions-Orgs/bus-fin/cpdc/facilities-management-institute/Pages/default.aspx) and the [Facilities Management Institute Capital Training Program website](https://csyou.calstate.edu/Divisions-Orgs/bus-fin/cpdc/facilities-management-institute/Pages/default.aspx).

**Linkedin Learning**

View [online video and training tutorials](https://www.linkedin.com/learning/?u=56973593).

Also available on the [my.cpp.edu](https://my.cpp.edu/uPortal/f/home/normal/render.uP) portal

**APPA Training**

Learn about courses and certification requirements at the [APPA Certification for Certified Educational Facilities Professional (CEEP) website](https://www.appa.org/programs).

**College of Professional and Global Education**

Offers a diverse array of continuing education and certificate programs to enhance your career skill set.
