# Educational Opportunity Program

![Former EOP Student smiling in a computer lab](img/milca-ramos.jpg)

## About EOP

At Cal Poly Pomona, EOP continues to operate as the first and most comprehensive post-secondary access and retention program. Established in 1969, the program serves low-income, typically first-generation students, who demonstrate the motivation and potential to succeed in college. Through our holistic model, we provide support to students through our four unit areas: outreach & admissions, transitions programs, advising services and EOP Learning Center.

Cal Poly Pomona is a diverse community of individuals who represent many perspectives, beliefs and identities, committed to fostering an inclusive, respectful, and intellectually vibrant environment. All university programs and activities are open and available to all regardless of race, sex, color, ethnicity or national origin.
