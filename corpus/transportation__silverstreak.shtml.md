# Silver Streak at CPP

![Silver Streak is at CPP](img/2023_silverstreak.png)

![](img/silver_streak_cell_phone.png)

## An Easier Way to Get Around

Get to top destinations, including Downtown LA, faster than ever with the Foothill Transit Silver Streak bus, now boarding on campus at the Student Services Building!

Silver Streak buses stop on campus **between 7 a.m. and 9 p.m. on weekdays** at the stop located at the Student Services Building (Bldg. 121) drop-off circle.

You can track the Silver Streak in real time using the **myStop Mobile app**, available on both the App Store and Google Play Store. Just select 'Foothill Transit' as the agency and select Silver Streak. The app also has service alerts on detours or delays.

Learn more about Silver Streak on the [Foothill Transit website](http://foothilltransit.org/silverstreak/).

[See Silver Streak Map & Schedule](silver-streak-map-and-schedule.pdf)

## Class Pass Works on Silver Streak

Yes, the CPP Class Pass works on Silver Streak bus lines. If you already have your free Class Pass, there's nothing you need to do. Just board a Silver Streak bus and tap to board!

If you still need to pick up your Class Pass at the **BRIC front desk (Bldg. 42).,** click the link below for more info.

## Silver Streak Stop Location SSB Drop Off Circle

Look for the Silver Streak bus stop sign located at the drop off circle right outside of the Student Services Building (SSB #121). Silver Streak buses will stop on campus **between 7 a.m. and 9:30 p.m. on weekdays** at this stop.

You can track the Silver Streak in real time using the **myStop Mobile app**, available on both the App Store and Google Play Store. Just select 'Foothill Transit' as the agency and select Silver Streak. The app also has service alerts on detours or delays.

## Frequently Asked Questions

Expand/Collapse All Frequently Asked Questions

### When does the bus stop on campus?

Silver Streak buses will stop at Cal Poly Pomona on weekdays between 7am and 7pm, except on holidays. Visit www.foothilltransit.org for complete schedules.

### How long does it take to get to DTLA?

Trips on the Silver Streak to downtown Los Angeles can take between 45 minutes to an hour depending on things such as traffic, boardings, and detours.

### Does the Class Pass work on Silver Streak?

Yes, the Silver Streak is included in the Class Pass. If you're a Cal Poly Pomona student and you don't have a pass yet, you can pick one up at the **BRIC (Bronco Recreational Intramural Complex), Bldg 42.** -- just bring your ID and they'll take care of the rest.

### Where can I go on the Silver Streak?

The Silver Streak travels along the 10 freeway between Montclair and downtown Los Angeles. Some of the places along the route include the Montclair Transit Center, Pomona Transit Center, Plaza West Covina, El Monte Station, Union Station, Olvera Street, Walt Disney Concert Hall, Museum of Contemporary Art, Crypto.com Arena, and LA Live.

### Do you have real-time info to see when the next bus will be there?

You can get real-time information by visiting [www.foothilltransit.rideralerts.com](http://www.foothilltransit.rideralerts.com) or by downloading the myStop Mobile app on Android or Apple devices. The app lets customers plan a trip, locate our buses on a map, and find the ETA of the bus nearest you.

To use it, select our agency in the list of agencies in the app (if you’re using it via the website you can skip this step) and select the bus line(s) or bus stop(s) you’re interested in. Then, it’ll show you the ETA of the next bus. The app also has service alerts on any detours or delays that may be impacting service.

 You can also track your buses using other apps like Transit or Moovit.

### Why did you add this stop?

The changes we made to the Silver Streak are part of a pilot. This means that we’re trying this new routing and we’ll consider whether or not to make the changes permanent after the trial period.

One of the reasons that we are testing out this new bus stop is to give more improve connectivity and provide more options for students at Cal Poly Pomona. Making education more accessible for students is an important part of the service we provide. It also helps reduce congestion for people who live and work around campus, so we see college service as a win-win.

Let Foothill Transit know what you think of the new stop. You can send your feedback via email by writing to comment@foothilltransit.org or on their social channels (@foothilltransit).
