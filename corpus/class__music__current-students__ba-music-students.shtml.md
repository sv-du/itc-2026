## Customize Your Music Degree to Match Your Passions

The B.A. in General Studies offers a flexible path for students who want to study music while exploring other academic interests. You’ll build a strong foundation in musicianship, theory, performance, and music history — just like other music majors — but with the added freedom to take courses in areas like education, business, science, or the arts. This option is ideal for students who want to double major, prepare for graduate school, or design a unique career path that combines music with other fields. It’s all about becoming a well-rounded, creative thinker ready for whatever comes next.

![CPP music student performs on piano using a digital score during a live concert or recital.](../img/pictures/piano-ensemble.jpg)

## Considering a Different Music Option? Here's How to Transfer

If you’re admitted as a General Studies in Music major, you can later transfer into Composition, Performance, Pedagogy (Pre-Credential), or Music Industry Studies.

You can find out more about the process by clicking on the following options:

[Bachelor of Music, Composition Transfer Information](bm-composition-students.shtml)

[Bachelor of Music, Performance Transfer Information](bm-performance-students.shtml)
