# Current Students

## Student Success Center

The CLASS Student Success Center provides comprehensive advisement to CLASS undergraduate students. Our professional advisors work closely with departments and campus partners to promote students success and timely graduation.

[Student Success Center](../student-success-center/index.shtml)

## Scholarships

Learn about scholarships available to students in the College of Letters, Arts, and Social Sciences. CLASS offers scholarships for students at any stage in their college journey, from first-year students to seniors and transfer students!

[Scholarships](../students/scholarships.shtml)

## Bronco Mentoring Network

The Cal Poly Pomona Bronco Mentoring Network, your premier online platform for career advice and networking, exclusively designed for Cal Poly Pomona students and alums.

[Bronco Mentoring Network](https://www.cpp.edu/class/students/bronco_mentoring_network.shtml)

## Student Research

Explore Reach - Inspiring Creativity, Discovery, and Innovation, an online showcase of outstanding undergraduate research at Cal Poly Pomona, where students from all disciplines can submit faculty-approved work to be featured.

[Student Research](https://www.cpp.edu/our-cpp/news/reach/index.shtml)

## Internships

CLASS offers internships in the public sector; including a judicial internship where students get to meet judges and district attorneys, the Panetta Congressional Institute Program based in Monterey, CA and Washington, D.C., as well as research opportunities with faculty.

[Internships](https://www.cpp.edu/class/political-science/participate/internships.shtml#judicialinternship "Internships (opens in new window)")

## Commencement

Congratulations on successfully completing this milestone! Commencement takes place each year in May. Apply for graduation, review graduation requirements, and learn about Grad Fair and important deadlines.

[Commencement](https://www.cpp.edu/commencement/index.shtml "Commencement (opens in new window)")

## Clubs

Explore CLASS clubs and organizations such as Model United Nations, CLASS Council, Psi Chi and more. With over 600 clubs and organizations on campus, there is one for you. Visit MyBar to learn more and engage with our campus community.

[Clubs](../students/clubs.shtml "Clubs (opens in new window)")
