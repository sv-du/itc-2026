# Paint Shop

## About

Paint maintenance is provided to all general-fund supported university facilities. Facilities performs painting maintenance on a scheduled basis.  For non-state service requests and any custom color requests, the costs are charged back to the customer. The Paint Shop maintains 5 million square feet of painted surface on the main campus. The team is responsible for painting interior and exterior surfaces, finishing wallboards, plastering, and painting traffic lane stripes.

**What We Do**

Services include but are not limited to:

* Emergency repair work, (leaks, wall damage, open wall access repairs)
* Graffiti removal (priming/painting)
* Safety striping (stairs/curbs)
* Painting and refinishing of all state (and non-state) funded spaces
* New painting projects
* Parking lot painting and restriping
* Accent walls
