# Dean's Student Advisory Board (SAB)

## About the SAB

The SAB provides direct feedback to the dean of the College of Engineering, and represents the college in its premier events.

Members represent the interest and needs of engineering student body, and have the opportunity to develop significant relationships with industry leads.

Applications periodically open in the spring and fall semesters by the virtue of board members graduating. Call for applications will be sent to all engineering students via email.
