# Alumni Success Stories

## One Degree. Endless Possibilities.

Our alumni have found success across a wide range of industries, demonstrating the quality and versatility of an education from Collins College. Their degree in hospitality management opened doors to dynamic and exciting careers not only in hotels and restaurants but also in event planning, tourism, marketing and corporate management. Enjoy their stories!
