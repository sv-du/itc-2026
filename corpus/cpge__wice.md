# Workforce Innovation & Career Engagement (WICE)

![Workforce Innovation & Career Engagement](img/teamwork.jpg)

## Connecting Learning with Work

WICE helps organizations and students build pathways to success through innovative, engaging, and relevant learning solutions. WICE connects Cal Poly Pomona's "Become by Doing" philosophy to the evolving needs of today's workforce.

47%

Increase in Workforce Productivity (Impacts of Employee Training Program)

Source: California Competes Final Legislative Report

57%

Higher Retention in Learning-Focused Companies

Source: LinkedIn Workplace Learning Report, 2025

## BUILDING PATHWAYS THAT CONNECT LEARNING TO WORK

### Workforce Innovation for a Stronger Region

Workforce Innovation & Career Engagement (WICE) is Cal Poly Pomona’s hub for advancing workforce readiness and economic mobility. Powered by the College of Professional and Global Education, WICE bridges education and industry through customized training, strategic partnerships, and real-world career experiences.

## Empowering the Workforce of Today

### Helping organizations grow by investing in their people

WICE partners with employers to help their teams grow stronger from within. Through customized upskilling programs supported by California’s Employer Training Panel (ETP) funds, organizations enhance productivity, boost retention, and build a culture of continuous learning that drives long-term success.

[Work With Us](https://form.jotform.com/253245262014144%20)

![group of professionals meeting at a table](img/investing-in-people.jpg)

## CSU Economic Impact Report

Cal Poly Pomona's economic impacts are felt in four California counties – Los Angeles, Orange, San Bernardino, and Riverside, contributing to $2 billion in economic activities through alumni earnings and support for industry growth. WICE will further solidify the impact model through professional development activities for our industry partners in the region. [Economic Impact Report Pomona (PDF)](https://www.calstate.edu/impact/Documents/acc_economic-impact-report_pomona_final.pdf)
