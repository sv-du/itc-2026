# Engineering Technology & Electromechanical Engineering Technology Alumni

![ET Class](/engineering/et/img/img_5837.jpg)

The Electromechanical Engineering Technology Department has been in existence since 1971 but in the early years there was only the one Engineering Technology major with many options including Electronics. After extensive research and study on needs and expectation of the local and nationwide industry, in the Fall 2016, the Engineering Technology Department was changed to Electromechanical Engineering Technology Department offering two undergraduate programs: Electronic Systems Engineering Technology (ESET) and Electromechanical Systems Engineering Technology (EMSET).

This page is always under construction if you are an Alumni and would like to add your information to this page just send an email to et@cpp.edu.
