# Change of Major Guide

## About This Guide

This Change of Major Guide is intended to help you understand the process of changing your major and the resources available to you as a Cal Poly Pomona student. This guide does not replace an in-person advising session. If you still have questions after reading this guide about what your steps are, contact the [Bronco Advising Center](../bronco-advising-center/index.shtml).
