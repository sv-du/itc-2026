# Student Advising

**For advising you may contact the College of Business Administration (CBA) Student Success Center at Cal Poly Pomona****.**

Be sure your email includes your Bronco ID Number.

Your Student Success Center team  will be monitoring emails Monday through Friday from 8:00am to 7:00pm. We will do our best to respond in a timely manner and thank you for your patience.

Appointments with Academic Advisors can be made via   [CPP Connect](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/cppconnect/index.shtml "https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/cppconnect/index.shtml")  .

## Student Success Center

College of Business Administration
