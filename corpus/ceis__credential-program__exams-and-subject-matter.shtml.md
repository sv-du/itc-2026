# Testing Requirements, Resources and Subject Matter Preparation

### Testing Requirements

1. [Basic Skills Requirement](https://www.ctc.ca.gov/credentials/leaflets/basic-skills-requirement-%28cl-667%29)
2. [Subject Matter Competence Requirement](https://www.ctc.ca.gov/educator-prep/subject-matter-requirements)
3. [Reading Instruction and Competence Assessment (RICA) Written Examination](http://www.ctcexams.nesinc.com/TestView.aspx?f=HTML_FRAG/CA_RICA_Written_TestPage.html)
4. [Reading Instruction and Competence Assessment (RICA) Video Performance Assessment](http://www.ctcexams.nesinc.com/TestView.aspx?f=HTML_FRAG/CA_RICA_Video_TestPage.html)
5. [Teaching Performance Assessments (CalTPA)](https://www.cpp.edu/ceis/credential-program/caltpa/about-caltpa.shtml)
6. [US Constitution Requirement](https://www.ctc.ca.gov/docs/default-source/commission/coded/2011/1101.pdf) is satisfied by a grade of "C" or better in PLS 2010 at CPP, an approved equivalent course, or by passing the US Constitution Requirement [Challenge Exam](https://usconstitutionexam.com/) Submit your [Course Equivalency Request](https://engage.cpp.edu/register/course_equivalency) to see if you have met this requirement.

### Subject Matter Preparation Programs

The subject matter preparation programs are undergraduate degree programs. They allow future teachers to waive the CSET exam, if the prescribed undergraduate coursework is taken and passed satisfactorily. The following undergraduate departments at Cal Poly Pomona offer the subject matter waiver programs:

1. [Agriculture](https://www.cpp.edu/agri/degrees/credentials.shtml)
2. [English](https://www.cpp.edu/class/english-modern-languages/english/education-major.shtml)
3. [Music](https://www.cpp.edu/class/music/curriculum/education-option.shtml)
4. [Kinesiology & Health Promotions](https://www.cpp.edu/sci/kinesiology-health-promotion/academic-programs/undergraduate-program.shtml)
5. [Math & Science](https://www.cpp.edu/sci/cemast/resources/resources.shtml)
6. [Social Science](https://www.cpp.edu/class/history/undergraduate-programs/pre-credential.shtml)
7. [Spanish](https://www.cpp.edu/class/english-modern-languages/languages/precredentials.shtml)
