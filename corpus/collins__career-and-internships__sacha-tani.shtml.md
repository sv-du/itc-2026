# Sacha Tani

## Sports & Recreation

### Sacha's Journey

I was heavily involved in the clubs and the Collins College community which led me to a lot of volunteer roles and my internship at LA Tourism. Since then, I have worked at UCLA, Irvine Company, Universal Studios and my current role at the Los Angeles Memorial Coliseum. Through networking and hard work, I was able to secure every promotion and job through my connections.

![Sacha Tani](../img/sacha-tani.avif)

## In the Spotlight

### How did a Collins College degree help you in your career?

First and foremost, Collins College is a community. The faculty is engaged and invested in the success of the student population and use their personal experiences and connections to help you through. One of the many examples of this was when Dr. Helen Gavin took the time to help me draft a letter and connect me with the CEO of LA Tourism, which led to the launch of my career. The hands-on experience that the classes and clubs provided me were priceless in helping me decide on the career path for my future. I was able to have a glimpse of the different sectors of the industry with the opportunities that were provided. Finally, the community of students created a network of colleagues that would help me grow my career for years to come.

### What do you love about working in hospitality?

Hospitality is an industry unlike any other. You get to create experiences for guests and lifetime memories whether it is an event, dining experience or vacation. No day is the same and you get to weave your creativity with leadership and strategy. It keeps you on your toes and I would not have it any other way!

### How would you convince someone that the field of hospitality management is for them?

There is no limit to where you can take your career. You have to understand how everything works together and you get to meet so many other people in different industries along the way — it is a great place to start. There is always something new to learn, see and do, so it keeps your day-to-day exciting.
