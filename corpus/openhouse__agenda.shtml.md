# BroncoBound Agenda

## Plan Your Visit

**Next Steps Center** | *Bldg. 15 - University Library, 2nd floor*Everything you need to become a Bronco in one place! Visit the Library to **accept your offer with Admissions**, get your financial aid questions answered and take your first official photo as a Bronco. You can even learn more from the Registrar's Office and Cashiering Services or swing by the Maker Studio to explore creative technology available to all students.

**Resource Fair** | *University Quad*Welcome to the herd! Discover departments, student clubs and organizations ready to support your journey at CPP.

**University Housing Tours** | *Bldg. 20 & 74*Discover CPP’s on-campus housing with a tour of our Residential Hall *(Bldg. 74)* for freshmen and Traditional Hall *(Bldg. 20)* available to all students. UHS staff will be on hand to provide tours, share information and answer questions.

**Guided Campus Tours** | *Bldg. 1*Hop on a 30-minute guided shuttle tour to explore our beautiful campus and discover what makes CPP home.

**CPP Enterprises Land** | *University Park*Stop by to learn about meal plans, off-campus housing, jobs on campus and score free swag all at the same time.

**"I Said Yes!" to CPP Celebration** | *University Plaza*Celebrate becoming a Bronco with photo ops, fun giveaways and make sure to bring your family and supporters to mark this exciting milestone.

**ASI Engagement Zone** | *Bldg. 35 - Bronco Student Center*Hang out in the Games Room for fun games and casual conversations with current CPP students. Hear firsthand what it’s like to be a Bronco and how ASI can enhance your student life here at Cal Poly Pomona.

**Bronco Bookstore** | *Bldg. 66 |* 8 a.m. - 2 p.m.Gear up with CPP apparel, supplies and tech with our special event discount of **20% off all CPP merchandise**!

**BRIC Tours** | *Bldg. 42*Tour our 3-story recreational center, equipped with one of the tallest climbing walls in the CSU system, fitness equipment, an indoor track, a pool and multi-activity courts.

**ASI Recreation Programs** | *Bronco Commons*Learn more about workshops, trips, private training, certifications, group fitness classes and sports and competitions with Aquatics, Adventures, Fitness, Intramural Sports, Sport Clubs and Scuba.

## Information Sessions

***Located in the Bronco Student Center (BSC), Bldg. 35***

**Discover Your Path | Room: Ursa Major***9 a.m. - 10 a.m.* | *11 a.m. - 12 p.m.*Future Broncos, hear best practices for your transition to CPP from Orientation, Admissions, Outreach and Office of Student Success.

**Supporting Your Bronco** **| Room: Ursa Major***10 a.m. - 11 a.m.* | *12 p.m. - 1 p.m.*
Family and supporters, learn more about the campus resources from Financial Aid, Parent & Family Engagement, Community Care and Student Wellbeing & Support.

**Undeclared Advising | Room: Ursa Minor**
*10 a.m. - 10:30 a.m.* | *12 p.m. - 12:30 p.m.*
Get ready to explore and grow with the Undeclared Advising Program, here to assist you with academic advising, career and major exploration.
