# Application

We are now accepting applications for Summer 2023.

Application Deadline: 02/05/2023.

The site will be hosted in person at California State Polytechnic University, Pomona.

Dates: **May 30th – Aug 4th 2023**

## Eligibility Requirements:

1. U.S. Citizen or permanent resident.
2. Must be enrolled in an undergraduate institution and graduate after September 2023.
3. Computer Science, Computer Information Systems, Computer Engineering or other related majors.
4. 3.0 or Higher GPA.

## Required Materials:

1. Personal Statement. Please describe your interests in the REU program, and your relevant academic achievements, and your career plan or interests in the long run.
2. Unofficial Transcript
3. A resume which is no more than 2 pages.
4. Two Recommendation Letters from faculty members in your major, minor or course of study, supervisors from your internship, etc.

## How to Apply:

Complete your [NSFETAP Application](https://www.nsfetap.org/award/728/opportunity/763) by 02/05/2023.
