# TEACH Grant

*The Federal TEACH Grant is available for select credential and undergraduate programs and requires recipients to commit to specific teaching obligations. This grant provides funding to students who plan to teach full time in a high-need field at a public or private elementary or secondary school serving a high concentration of students from low-income families, as designated by the U.S. Department of Education.*

*To be considered, you must submit an application to the Office of Financial Aid and Scholarships confirming that you meet and understand the grant requirements. Applications for the 2025–2026 TEACH Grant will become available on July 1, 2025.*

*For more information or to access the application once it becomes available, please visit their website at: [https://www.cpp.edu/financial-aid/credentials/current/aid-programs.shtml](https://www.cpp.edu/financial-aid/credentials/current/aid-programs.shtml#link "https://www.cpp.edu/financial-aid/credentials/current/aid-programs.shtml")*
