## Psychology Advising

Have questions about Psychology? Do you want advice about what psychology classes to take? Do you want to talk with a faculty advisor? Below are some useful resources.

### CLASS Student Success Center

* General Curriculum and GE-related advising
* Transfer-related advising
* Graduation Requirements
* Academic Petitions

[CLASS Student Success Center](https://www.cpp.edu/class/student-success-center/index.shtml)

### Psychology Faculty Advisors

* Major/Minor related advising
* Graduate school and career-related advising
* Advising related to student involvement in the department (for example:undergraduate research, clubs, etc.)
