# Plant Parts

![Parts of the plant showing Flower, fruit, leaf, stem, roots, seeds](../img/plant-parts.jpg)

## Plant Parts Learn the six basic plant parts and their function

Have you ever stopped to look at a plant? They are more complex than you might think. A plant’s various parts are composed of specialized cells and tissues that work together to carry on the plant’s life function. Each plant part has a specific job to perform in order for the plant to grow big and strong.

![fun fact](../img/lightbulb.png)

When we eat broccoli, we are eating the stem and flowers of the plant.
