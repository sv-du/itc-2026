## Directions

We are located at The Collins College of Hospitality Management at Cal Poly Pomona. From University Drive, follow the signs that read The Collins College and Kellogg West Conference Center.  Please park as close to the Collins College building, identified by the silver archway, as possible.  Walk down steps under archway along lit path.  The restaurant entrance is at the end of the walkway to the right through two sets of double glass doors.

**From the 210 Freeway traveling East and the 57 South:**
Take the 210 Freeway to the 57 south, exit on Temple Avenue (make a right). Proceed west on Temple Avenue to University Drive. Turn right on University Drive, proceed north. At Horse Hill Drive, turn right and proceed up to The Collins College.

**From the 57 Freeway travel North:**Exit on Temple Avenue (make a left). Proceed west on Temple Avenue to University Drive. Turn right on University Drive, proceed north. At Horse Hill Drive, turn right and proceed up to The Collins College.

**From the 10 Freeway traveling West or East:**Take the 10 Freeway to the 57 south, exit on Temple Avenue (make a right). Proceed west on Temple Avenue to University Drive. Turn right on University Drive, proceed north. At Horse Hill Drive, turn right and proceed up to The Collins College.
