## WE ARE HERE TO HELP

### Connect with the Right Department for Support

Whether you’re exploring a program or need guidance mid-journey, our departments are here to support you. Each team within the College of Professional and Global Education is dedicated to helping you navigate your next step. From program details and admissions to registration and student services. To obtain the answers you need, we recommend contacting the department that oversees your area of interest directly. If you're not sure where to start, we’ll help point you in the right direction. Please let us know how we can assist you. Your success is our priority.
