# AMM Mission, Vision & Educational Excellence

## Mission & Values

**![Female students measure garments on a table. ](../img/2016-amm-production-vu-920x570.jpg)**

**Vision**

Our vision is to cultivate future global leaders in the fashion business, leveraging technology and innovation to drive sustainable positive change in the industry.

**Mission**

Our mission is to foster global fashion leaders who promote sustainable innovation, technology, and ethical business practices within a culturally responsive environment.

We are committed to:

* State-of-the-art curriculum
* Entrepreneurship-focused experiential learning
* Research, scholarship, and creative activities
* Industry and community engagement

**Program Goals**

Goal 1: Deliver a quality program that promotes experiential learning in fashion, fostering creativity and discovery.

Goal 2: Equip students to integrate innovative technologies and market insights to navigate the global fashion industry.

Goal 3: Prepare students to develop sustainability strategies, promote social responsibility, and drive civic engagement to address community needs.

Goal 4: Foster fashion career readiness through career planning for a culturally diverse environment.

**Student Learning Outcomes**

SLO1: Students will identify materials, develop and produce designs, and evaluate and sell fashion/textile products.
SLO2: Students will assess target market consumer needs and propose fashion/textiles that meet market needs.
SLO3: Students will identify key issues in the fashion industry, synthesize knowledge to conduct research studies, and propose solutions.
SLO4: Students will apply knowledge of ethical reasoning and sustainability practices in the fashion industry.
SLO5: Students will appropriately utilize and adapt innovative technologies used in the global fashion industry.
