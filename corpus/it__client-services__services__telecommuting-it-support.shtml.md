# Telecommuting & Remote Work

## IT Support for Remote Work

In partnership with the [CPP Telecommuting Program](https://www.cpp.edu/eoda-hr/departments/elr/telecommute-program.shtml), the Information Technology Client Services Department provides the technical support assistance for state-side faculty and staff employees who have been approved to telecommute or work from home.

* IT provides standard technical support for all state-owned devices for campus faculty and staff employees.
* IT provides normal remote technical support services through phone, email, chat, Microsoft Teams, Zoom, and TeamViewer.
* All physical device support must be completed on-campus. Telecommuters will be asked to bring their state-owned devices on campus if hardware support is required.
* IT does not provide technical support for remote workers at their residence or remote work location.
* IT does not support or troubleshoot any non-state, personally owned equipment, devices, home office setups, printing, or any Internet/Wi-Fi connectivity configurations.
* Unless approved by IT and the employee's VP office, IT does not ship client device equipment to and from a telecommuting worker's residence.
* Unless approved by IT and funded by the employee's department, remote workers cannot use a state-funded hotspot for remote work Internet connectivity.  In accordance with the CPP Telecommuting Program, Internet access is not provided by Cal Poly Pomona and is the responsibility of the employee to procure and arrange at the employee’s sole expense.

Please refer to the following FAQs for common requests involving the configuration of IT equipment both at home as well as in returning devices to a campus office location.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I extend and position my monitors in Windows?

You can change your monitor or display settings within the Windows "Display" window by clicking **Start**  > **Settings**  > **System**> **Display.** Under the "Multiple displays" section, you may select "Extend desktop to this display".  You may also use the "Display" settings window to change your monitor positions to correspond to how your physical monitors are setup on your desk.

### How do I connect my computer or campus phone to the network over ethernet?

Most [office phones](https://cpp.service-now.com/ehelp?id=kb_article&sys_id=e3fe854e6fe8e600e020f35d5d3ee4cd) are connected by [ethernet cable](https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/I/51PRQ5goFLL._SL500_.jpg) from a wall jack to your phone. A second ethernet cable is then attached from your phone to your computer or docking station.

### I am returning to the office as my primary work location, how do I connect all my devices?

Please remember to bring all of the computing cables with your devices when returning your equipment to the office. If you have any questions in reconnecting your devices or are missing cables, please [contact the IT Service Desk](https://cpp.service-now.com/ehelp) for assistance.

### How do I change my call-forwarding?

Employees often configure their phone extensions to forward to a mobile device while working remote. and many use Jabber to manage calls while working from home. You may adjust office phone extension settings as needed with [Jabber](https://cpp.service-now.com/ehelp?id=kb_article&sys_id=251fc54e6fe8e600e020f35d5d3ee4a2) or by [contacting the IT Service Desk](https://cpp.service-now.com/ehelp) for further assistance.

Please refer to the [Jabber call forwarding and phone management eHelp article](https://cpp.service-now.com/ehelp?id=kb_article&sys_id=251fc54e6fe8e600e020f35d5d3ee4a2) for additional information.

### Moving your phone extension to another location

If you need your phone or extension moved to a new location, please submit a [Move an Existing Phone ticket](https://cpp.service-now.com/ehelp?id=sc_cat_item&sys_id=e0df69c86f0f6200e020f35d5d3ee46d&sysparm_category=3d60feb26f84820025ce9e4eae3ee4ff&catalog_id=-1) or [contact the IT Service Desk](https://cpp.service-now.com/ehelp) for further assistance.  If you need to change an existing phone function such as the caller ID, voicemail, or call forwarding behavior, please submit a [Change an Existing Phone ticket](https://cpp.service-now.com/ehelp?id=sc_cat_item&sys_id=aafad5046f0f6200e020f35d5d3ee4c0).
