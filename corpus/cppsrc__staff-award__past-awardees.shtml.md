### 6th Annual Distinguished Research Staff Award

---

Sandra Gutierrez Magallanez

*Chemistry & Biochemistry Department*

Congratulations to Sandra Gutierrez Magallanez for receiving the 6th Annual Distinguished RSCA Staff Award for her outstanding contributions to research, scholarly and creative activities and for her exmplary efforts to enhance learn-by-doing pedagogy. Sandra has worked for the university over 11 years and is known for dedication to supporting both student and faculty research at CPP.

![Awardee giving speech on podium](../img/staff-award/staff-award-2019.jpg)

### 4th Annual Distinguished Research Staff Award

---

Desiree Vera

*Institutional Review Board Staff*

Congratulations to Desiree Vera who received the Distinguished RSCA Staff Award on March 2, 2018. Vera has worked in the Institutional Review Board office for three years as the compliance analyst. She supports faculty and student research by helping to ensure that their research plans are ethical and comply with federal law. She truly cares about the CPP mission and goals of faculty and students.

![Collage of two images, from left to right: John Olson and Bruce Kennedy](../img/staff-award/staff-award-2017.jpg)
