# Music Degree Programs

## Become by making music.

Our diverse undergraduate programs offer you a comprehensive education in music, preparing you for successful careers in performance, composition, education, and the music industry.

By pursuing one of our degree programs, you will join a vibrant community of musicians and scholars dedicated to excellence and innovation.

## Here's What We Have To Offer

[Visit Our Course Catalog](https://catalog.cpp.edu/preview_entity.php?catoid=69&ent_oid=5780&returnto=5895 )

[![Students performing on French horns during a Cal Poly Pomona brass ensemble rehearsal or concert.](../img/pictures/cards/degree-programs/24-brass-ensemble-french-horns3.jpg)

### Bachelor of Arts in Music

Ready to dive into the world of music? With our General Music option, you’ll thrive in a hands-on environment that combines performance, technology and industry knowledge. With expert faculty guiding you, you’ll explore everything from music theory and history to the latest trends, all while developing your artistic and professional potential. This option allows you to gain a solid foundation while having the freedom to explore interdisciplinary studies.](https://www.cpp.edu/programs/class/music/general.shtml%20 "opens a new window")

[![Student working at an audio mixing console in a Cal Poly Pomona music production lab.](../img/pictures/cards/degree-programs/music-lab-carrillo.jpg)

### Bachelor of Arts in Music Industry Studies

Ready to turn your love for music into a career? Our Music Industry Studies option will give you a behind-the-scenes look at the music business, from the tech side of production to the marketing and management that drives the industry. Learn how to create and perform music while exploring the economics, culture and legal aspects of the music world. Get real-world experience through internships and collaborations with professionals in the Los Angeles music scene.](https://www.cpp.edu/programs/class/music/industry-studies.shtml%20 "opens a new window")

[![Band director and high school music students pose with instruments in a classroom rehearsal setting.](../img/pictures/cards/degree-programs/patino-jose22.jpg)

### Bachelor of Music in Pedagogy (Pre-Credential)

Ready to educate the next generation of great musicians? Our Pedagogy (Pre-Credential) program prepares you to teach music in schools, giving you the skills to inspire future musicians. You’ll learn the theory and techniques of effective teaching while gaining hands-on experience. This program also sets you up to meet the “Subject Matter Competency” requirement for a Single Subject Credential in Music, so you’re ready to lead your own music classroom.](https://www.cpp.edu/programs/class/music/pedagogy.shtml%20 "opens a new window")

[![Student playing keyboard backstage, focused on sheet music during a rehearsal or performance at Cal Poly Pomona.](../img/pictures/cards/degree-programs/bm-composition.jpg)

### Bachelor of Music in Composition

Ready to turn your musical ideas into masterpieces? The Music Composition option is your launchpad to a career in creating original music. You’ll study diverse compositional techniques, orchestration and contemporary music practices while using advanced technology to craft original works. You’ll collaborate with peers and have your pieces performed by talented ensembles, gaining the experience you need to thrive as a composer or arranger.](https://www.cpp.edu/programs/class/music/composition.shtml%20 "opens a new window")

[![Student vocalist performs with Mariachi Los Broncos de Pomona during a concert at Cal Poly Pomona.](../img/pictures/cards/degree-programs/24-mariachi-los-broncos-concert.jpg)

### Bachelor of Music in Performance

Ready to take your music skills to the next level and make a name for yourself in the world of performance? Our Music Performance option will help you fine-tune your technical skills while developing your unique artistic voice. Whether you specialize in guitar, keyboard, classical instruments, voice, or commercial music, you’ll have plenty of opportunities to perform solo and with ensembles, pushing your limits and gaining hands-on experience.](https://www.cpp.edu/programs/class/music/performance.shtml%20 "opens a new window")

[![Students performing on woodwind instruments during a concert with the Cal Poly Pomona band ensemble.](../img/pictures/cards/degree-programs/music-minor-program-card.jpg)

### Music Minor

Majoring in something else at Cal Poly Pomona but still want to incorporate music in your undergraduate experience? The Music Minor is designed for students majoring in other disciplines who wish to gain further experience and knowledge in music. This program offers a flexible curriculum that includes core courses in music theory and history, as well as opportunities for performance.](#modal-0 "Enhance Your Musical Knowledge with the Music Minor")

### Enhance Your Musical Knowledge with the Music Minor

×

The Music Minor is designed for students majoring in other disciplines who wish to gain further experience and knowledge in music.

This program offers a flexible curriculum that includes core courses in music theory and history, as well as opportunities for performance.

**Program Highlights**

* Core courses in music theory and history
* Flexible curriculum tailored to individual interests
* Opportunities to participate in performances ensembles

**Core Courses Include\*:**

* World of Music
* Intro to Music Tech
* Careers in Music
* Intro to Music Tech
* History of American Popular Music
* Various performance ensembles and elective courses tailored to your interests.

[Click Here for a Detailed Course List for the Music Minor](https://catalog.cpp.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=69&poid=19697)
