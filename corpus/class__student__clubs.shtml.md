## Get Involved

Join one of our diverse range of clubs and organizations and discover your passion and purpose! Explore CLASS clubs and organizations such as Model United Nations, CLASS Council, Psi Chi and more. With over 600 clubs and organizations on campus, there is one for you. Whether you're interested in academic advancement, community service, creative expression, or professional development, there's a group waiting for your unique contribution. Getting involved is simple—Check out the Cal Poly Pomona’s myBar or attend our Club Fair at the beginning of each semester.

## Find Your Community

Cal Poly Pomona offers inclusive spaces where every Bronco can find a community. Beyond traditional clubs and organizations, discover our specialized Centers for Transformation, Retention, Equity and Empowerment (T.R.E.E.), which include the Asian and Pacific Islander Student Center, Black Resource Center, César E. Chávez Center for Higher Education, Native American Student Center, Pride Center, and Womxn's Resource Center. For those interested in Greek life, our fraternities and sororities provide valuable community networking and leadership development opportunities. These diverse communities foster connections, support academic success, and celebrate the unique identities that make up our community on the Cal Poly Pomona campus.
