# Philosophers @ RSCA

December 4, 2020

Now’s the time to start thinking about what you might submit to the Student Research Conference (RSCA).  The deadline to apply is January 27, 2021 at 11:59 pm, and you can find information [here](https://www.cpp.edu/cppsrc/current-conference/index.shtml).  And here’s a video offering [Tips for Presenters videos featuring Lauren Ybarra](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zpiEujiCYP0).

CPP Philosophy majors (Jaden Yocom and Lauren Ybarra) have been won the Humanities Session in two of the last three years!
