# International Students

## Admitted International Checklist

Congratulations, you've been admitted! Here are the next steps you need to take before your first semester.

## Checklist

### ✓ Pay Your Enrollment Deposit

are required to submit their **Intent to Enroll** and pay a $150 Enrollment Deposit

You are required to pay an Enrollment Deposit of $150 by **May 1** in order to accept your offer of admission. The deposit will be applied to your tuition. For payment options, visit [Cashiering Services](https://www.cpp.edu/student-accounting/payment-options/cpp-payment-plans.shtml). *Note: The Enrollment Deposit is non-transferable and non-refundable.*

To view step-by-step instructions on accepting your admission offer visit our [tutorial](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7-QVeZ2Z33c).

### ✓ Complete Housing Application

2026-2027 Housing application will be available to ALL admitted students in early **February.**

Those in need of, or interested in, on-campus housing are encouraged to apply early.

CPP offers residence halls, suites, and apartment-style accommodations. If you plan on living on campus, you must complete an application:

[Residence Halls & Suites](https://www.cpp.edu/housing/futureresidents/floorplans.shtml "https://www.cpp.edu/housing/futureresidents/floorplans.shtml")

Please visit the [University Housing Services](https://www.cpp.edu/housing/index.shtml "https://www.cpp.edu/housing/index.shtml") for more information.

### ✓ Register for International Freshmen Student Orientation & Check-in

As an incoming F-1 International student to Cal Poly Pomona, you are required to attend both the **Mandatory University New Student Orientation**and **International Student Orientation.**Failure to register and attend both orientation sessions will result in your admission offer being revoked and withdrawn.

* **Mandatory University New Student Orientation**. Orientation is a mandatory enrollment requirement and will take place in-person during the summer. Please note, that admitted students for Fall 2025 will meet with an academic advisor and enroll for courses during their scheduled orientation session. Please contact the [Office of New Student Programs & Family Engagement](https://www.cpp.edu/orientation/index.shtml) regarding the mandatory orientation registration and deadlines at orientation@cpp.edu or (909) 869-3604.  Should you be unable to attend this New Student Orientation in the summer, please contact [Office of New Student Programs & Family Engagement](https://www.cpp.edu/orientation/index.shtml) immediately to request for accommodations.
* **International Student Orientation (immigration session).** All F-1 International students are required to attend the mandatory immigration and regulatory policies session with the International Center. Please contact the [International Center](https://www.cpp.edu/international/students/about/index.shtml) for additional information regarding session dates and requirements at international@cpp.edu or (909) 869-3604. Visit the [International Orientation Checklist](https://www.cpp.edu/~international/students/international-student-orientation/freshmen-international-orientation.shtml) for more information.

### ✓ Register for the Calculus Placement Assessment (ALEKS PPL)

The deadline to take the ALEKS PPL is **June 15**.

Some students with specific majors are required to take the ALEKS PPL - the mathematics placement program offered by Cal Poly Pomona. It allows you to review, take practice tests, and then come to campus for the official placement test. For more information visit [Mathematics and Statistics department](https://www.cpp.edu/sci/mathematics-statistics/calculus-placement.shtml).

### ✓ Request Your I-20

For more information on the I-20 process and required documents, please visit the [International Center](https://www.cpp.edu/international/students/new-students/pre-departure.shtml).

### ✓ Submit English Proficiency Scores

**Test of English as a Foreign Language TOEFL iBT Home Edition/MyBest**

The institutional code for TOEFL is **4082**. When you enter the code, it may show that it’s only for undergraduates. However, this code is the same for both undergraduate and graduate applicants as all scores sent to this code are received by the Office of Admissions.

* Log on to your TOEFL iBT account <https://www.ets.org/toefl.html>
* On home page, select “Order Score Reports”
* For Product Type, click "TOEFL Services" then click "Continue."
* Select the test date you want to send the scores of and click "Continue."
* Search for and select the schools you want to send your scores to. Cal Poly Pomona School Code: **4082**
* Follow any additional prompts you receive (such as payment, which is discussed more below).

**International English Language Testing System IELTS**

To submit your IELTS scores to Cal Poly Pomona (CPP), have IELTS electronically send your official Test Report Form directly to the university. Be sure to request electronic submission through your IELTS account to ensure the scores are sent to CPP.

* **Request electronic submission:** Log in to your IELTS account and request that an official copy of your Test Report Form be sent electronically to the E-Delivery account called “**Cal State Apply**”.

Cal Poly Pomona also accepts official hard copies of IELTS scores directly from the testing agency.

Please contact your testing center to request for them to send your official TRF (Test Report Form) directly to us at the following address:

Cal Poly Pomona

ATTN: Office of Admissions

3801 W. Temple Ave

Pomona, CA 91768

USA

**Duolingo**

To submit your Duolingo results, please follow these instructions.

* Log in to [duolingo.com](https://englishtest.duolingo.com/) with the email address you used to take the test.
* Click on "My Results" in the menu on the left side of the screen.
* Scroll down to your certified test result and click "Share Score."
* Select whether you are applying to a university, a secondary school, or another type of institution.
* Start typing the name of an institution to search for it and select your choices using the checkbox.
* If your institution uses an Application ID, do not forget to add it!
* Press "Send."

**English Proficiency Waiver**

You may qualify for an English proficiency waiver if you meet any of the following requirements:

* If you have studied at least 3 years of full-time enrollment at a secondary/high school where English is the principal language of instruction.
* Countries where English is the national language include: Antigua, Australia, Bahamas, Barbados, Belize, Bermuda, Botswana, Canada (except Québec), Ghana, Guyana, Ireland, Jamaica, Kenya, New Zealand, Nigeria, Scotland, South Africa, Trinidad, the United Kingdom, the United States, Virgin Islands and Wales.

For more information, visit [International Freshmen Requirements](https://www.cpp.edu/admissions/international/freshmen.shtml)

Do not open transcripts, otherwise, they will be considered unofficial and we will not be able to accept them. After we receive your transcript please allow 2 - 3 weeks for processing time.

To make sure transcripts are received on time, transcripts should be ordered as soon as an admission decision has been made.

**Failure to submit official transcripts by the stated deadlines may result in your application being withdrawn.**

### ✓ Submit AP/IB/CLEP Scores

The deadline to submit official AP/CLEP/IB scores is **July 15**. Please send scores earlier if they are available to ensure timely processing of your credits and Fall course placement.

Advanced Placement (AP)
You can also send scores online through [College Board](https://apscore.collegeboard.org/scores)

College Level Examination Program (CLEP)
Request your CLEP transcripts online through [College Board](https://clep.collegeboard.org/earn-college-credit/your-score)

International Baccalaureate (IB)
Request your IB transcripts or certificates through [IB Transcript Request](https://www.ibo.org/programmes/diploma-programme/assessment-and-exams/getting-results/)

### ✓ Upload your Photo for your Bronco Card (Student ID)

The Bronco Card is the official Cal Poly Pomona photo identification card for all Cal Poly Pomona faculty, staff, students and affiliates. It can also be used for dining services and convenience store locations, as well as access to campus services, buildings, and laboratories.

Visit the [Bronco Card website](https://www.cpp.edu/admissions/bronco-id/index.shtml) for complete photo guidelines, FAQs, and the photo upload link.
