# CPP Years of Service Recognition

## CPP Years of Service Recognition (formerly Service Awards effective 2023)

Note: CPP Years of Service Recognition does not account for service at other CSUs or reflects CalPERS service credit. CPP Years of Service Recognition accounts for years of service at Cal Poly Pomona, ASI, and Enterprise (formerly Foundation).

For the most updated information, please refer to [EODA - Service Recognition](https://www.cpp.edu/eoda-hr/departments/csc/service-awards.shtml)

[View the 2025 Service Awards Wall](service-awards-wall/index.shtml)

## Service Award Pin

Current recipients can stop by the Customer Service Center (CSC) at any time to pick up their pins Monday–Friday, 8 am – 5 pm. For questions regarding Service Pins, please contact the CSC.

General Inquiries
Hours: 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM I Email: hrscsc@cpp.edu I Phone: 909-869-3733
3801 W. Temple Ave, Pomona, CA 91768
Student Services Building 121, Second Floor
[Map and Directions to CSC location](https://www.cpp.edu/maps/?id=1130#!m/594431)

## Bronco Bucks

CPP Enterprises, in collaboration with the University, is pleased to award the 2023-24 recipients Bronco Bucks equivalent to their years of Service (e.g., 25 years of service would equal $25 in Bronco Bucks).  Bronco Bucks will be uploaded to your Bronco ID Card by Wednesday, August 21st,2024.

 For questions regarding your Bronco Bucks, please contact eoda@cpp.edu.

[Bronco Bucks Accepted Locations](https://broncoonecard.com/bronco-bucks-accepted-locations/)
