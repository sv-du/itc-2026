## About Me

Award-winning organizational psychologist with over 15 years of experience in higher education, talent development, and organizational consulting. Proven success designing and delivering impactful learning programs, securing over $6 million in grant funding, and leading organizational change initiatives across public, private, and nonprofit sectors. Recognized for scholarly expertise in leadership fairness, employee engagement, and organizational justice. Certified SHRM-SCP and passionate about advancing inclusive, data-informed workplace cultures through evidence-based strategies.

## Education

M.A., Ph.D., Industrial Organizational Psychology, Alliant International University
M.A. Education Administration (emphasis counseling), University of Redlands
B.A.'s Psychology and Human Development, CSU San Bernardino

## Courses Taught
