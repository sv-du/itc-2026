## The Maker Studio Defined

The Maker Studio is a makerspace—a location dedicated to creativity and creation where people gather to share ideas and to make a variety of products based on their interests. At the Maker Studio, we provide you with the resources and tools necessary to build your projects and bring your ideas to life, whether it be for classes or your own personal use.
