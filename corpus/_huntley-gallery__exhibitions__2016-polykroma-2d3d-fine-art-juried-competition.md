![2d3d](img/2d3d_panel_5.png)The Kellogg and Huntley University Art Galleries welcome the public and the Cal Poly Pomona campus community, students and their families, faculty, staff and administrators to *PolyKroma 2016*, celebrating Fine Arts, Art History and Graphic Design on campus with its *2D3D Juried Art Exhibition* at The Huntley Gallery, AND the Fine Art Senior Shows, Art History Papers Presentation and Industry Night - the senior Graphic Design exhibition - presented concurrently at the Kellogg University Art Gallery. These exhibitions feature the "best of" Cal Poly Pomona's Fine Arts, Art History and Graphic Design Seniors, and the Fine Arts and Graphic Design students of all levels.

[![Photo of Andrea Harris-McGee](img/slcad-gallery-aharrismcgeeheadshot1.jpg)](../../news-and-events/2016-05-23-polykroma-2d3d-fine-art-juried-competition.shtml)

### Andrea Harris-McGee

Andrea Harris-McGee is a Gallery Director at Laguna College of Art + Design, and fine artist and owner-operator at Andrea Lee Harris McGee Press. She earned her MFA in Illustration at Cal State Fullerton and ran the Grand Central Art Center in Santa Ana for ten years.

### [![Photo of Trang Le](img/trang-t-lc3aa.jpg)](../../news-and-events/2016-05-23-polykroma-2d3d-fine-art-juried-competition.shtml)

### Trang Le

Trang Le is a fine artist, represented by Ruth Bachofner Gallery at Bergamot Station. She exhibited her work in Abstract Mystique at the Kellogg Gallery in 2015, and graduated with an MFA from Claremont University.

###

[![Photo of David Kaul](img/dxkaul.jpg)](../../news-and-events/2016-05-23-polykroma-2d3d-fine-art-juried-competition.shtml)

### David Kaul

Graphic designer and fine artist, David Kaul, received his degree at Cornish College of the Arts in Seattle, and is currently a freelance designer with over a decade of full-time experience working at design and printing firms where he conceived of, designed, and prototyped for various brands, including Pacifico, Seattle’s Best Coffee, and City University, and printed show posters for bands including The White Stripes and Pearl Jam.
