# Student Health Services

![Exterior of the health center](img/medical-entrance-student-health-services.jpg)

## Welcome to Health Services

💬 *Your voice matters!* Share your feedback with SHS — take our quick [survey](https://tinyurl.com/y82bzc8y) after your visit or online anytime.

**![Student Health Services](img/student-health-services-banner-01.png)**

###### 🚨 Pharmacy Closure Notice

###### **Pharmacy closed February 12–26.** Clinic remains open. Nearby pharmacies are listed below.

**Cal Poly Pomona Student Health Services** is open during regular business hours: **Monday – Friday, 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM**.

We are conveniently located near a Bronco Shuttle stop. Both the **A** and **B** shuttles stop at our building (**Bldg. 46**).

* To schedule an appointment, please call **(909) 869-4000**.
* For prescription refills, please call **(909) 869-2744** and our health staff will assist you.

## **Emergency & Health Services Information**

### **Health Services**

* **Business Hours:** Monday–Friday, 8:00 a.m. – 5:00 p.m.
* **After-Hours Nurse Advice Line:** (855) 272-1723

  + Available after 5:00 p.m. on weekday;24 hours on weekends, holidays and Campus Closures
  + For **services and advice only** — no appointments
  + Please have your **Bronco ID number** ready when calling

---

### **Emergency Services**

* **University Police Department:**
   A full-service, sworn police agency operating **24 hours a day, year-round.**

  + For **Police, Medical, Fire, or other emergencies:**

    - Dial **9-1-1** from any campus or blue emergency phone
    - If using a cell phone, dial **(909) 869-3070**
    - Program this number into your phone for quick access in emergencies

---

### **Additional Resources**

* **Bronco Wellness Center and Health Education:** (909) 869-5272
* **988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline:** Call or text **988**
* **BHUCC-Behavioral Health Urgent Care Center**
* **[https://www.starsinc.com/bhucc-industry/#Stars Behavioral Health Group](https://www.starsinc.com/bhucc-industry/#Stars Behavioral Health Group "Stars Behavioral Health Group")**
  + **24/7 Outpatient Program**
* **CAPS After-Hours Crisis Support:** (909) 869-3220 (press **Option 2**)

---
