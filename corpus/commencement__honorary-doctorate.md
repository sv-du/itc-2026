# Guest Speakers and Honorary Doctorate Recipients

The following alumni and friends of the university will be part of this year’s commencement celebrations, serving as guest speakers at each ceremony. All messages and Honorary Doctorate addresses will be added to the site after the ceremonies.

## Honorary Doctorate Recipients

![Brian Jaramillo](../img/24jaramillo-brian8.jpg)

### Brian Jaramillo

('87, construction engineering technology)

Chief Operating Officer, Tilden-Coil Constructors

Guest Speaker and Honorary Doctorate Recipient for the College of Engineering

[More on Brian Jaramillo](https://www.cpp.edu/news/content/2024/04/engineering-hall-of-fame-inductee-to-receive-honorary-doctorate/index.shtml)

Ruth Singelyn
 ('84, computer information systems)

Distinguished Alumni and Philanthropists

Guest Speakers and Honorary Doctorate Recipients for the College of Business Administration
