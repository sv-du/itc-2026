## Permission numbers

Permission numbers are only given to students who come to the department office.   **Permission numbers will not be administered over the phone.**

Only one permission number may be given to a student at a time.   **Please know the number or section number before coming in to the department office.**

If you do not meet the prerequisites for the class, you will not be given a permission number.

If you are in concurrent enrollment with two or more universities/colleges, you must have completed the appropriate course and received the appropriate grade, and you must bring in proof to the department office, before receiving a permission number.

 **You MUST bring proof of ALL of the prerequisites required for the course, even if they were taken at Cal Poly.** An unofficial transcript is sufficient.

A permission number will not put you into the class if the class is full, it will only add you to the waiting list.
