# Adrian Laksmono

![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white-mobile.svg)
![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white.svg)

## Lodging & Resorts

### Adrian's Journey

I started working at a resort when I was 14 and liked the industry. I found an interest in revenue management and took the opportunity at The Collins College to help me get my current role by taking internships and extra classes.

![Adrian Laksmono](../adrian-laksmono_edited.avif)

## In the Spotlight

### How did a Collins College degree help you in your career?

The Collins College provided a variety of opportunities for me, like career expos, company showcases, [and] extracurricular activities connecting with alumni, [as well as] having relevant coursework that applies to current industry trends. I felt prepared to tackle the professional post-grad life by taking advantage of everything the school had to offer.

### What do you love about working in hospitality?

I love working in a dynamic work environment, where every day is different, even with the same tasks! Also, everyone that I've worked with is friendly and collaborative.

### How would you convince someone that the field of hospitality management is for them?

I think studying hospitality gives a variety of career opportunities since there are a lot of transferable skills that can be learned. It's also versatile in the sense that you can still apply to roles outside of operations in hotels or restaurants (ex. sales, revenue, supply chain, etc.).

### Share a fun fact about yourself.

I've moved around a lot and have lived in four different countries!
