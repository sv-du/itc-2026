## Education

Ph.D. 2014  Geography, The Ohio State University, Columbus, OH, USA

M.S.   2007  Earth Sciences, The Ohio State University, Columbus, OH, USA

B.S.    2001  Oceanography, Pusan National University, Pusan, Korea

## Research

Tropical Mountain Glaciology / Geography

Climate-glacier-hydrologic dynamics since the Little Ice Age

Physical dimensions of environmental change in glacierized landscapes

Global climate change

Water Resources

Air quality along with all other impacts on populations

Airborne and Spaceborne Remote Sensing

GIScience and GPS Mapping & Spatial Analysis
