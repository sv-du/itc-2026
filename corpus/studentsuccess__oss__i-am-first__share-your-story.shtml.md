# Share Your Story!

## Celebrate Your Journey and Inspire Others

![I am first tabling event ](img/shar-your-story.png)

Many CPP faculty, staff and students self-identify as the first in their families to go to college or be the first to graduate. Share your experience and story as a first-generation college student and advice you may have for CPP students.
