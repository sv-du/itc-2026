## Research Interests

* Earthquake engineering and seismic risk management
* Multi-hazard resilience
* Steel, concrete, and composite buildings and bridges
* Innovative structural systems and structural rehabilitation
* Structural dynamics and control
* Structural health monitoring
* Engineered materials and metamaterials
* Material and large-scale experimental testing
