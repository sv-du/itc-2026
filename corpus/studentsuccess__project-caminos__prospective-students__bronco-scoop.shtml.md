# Bronco Scoop Podcast

![Illustration of Billy holding microphone](../img/updated-bronco-scoop-logo.jpg)

## Our Podcast

### Welcome Listeners!

The Bronco Scoop podcast is dedicated to students and families interested in Cal Poly Pomona. Weekly episodes follow applicant timelines, highlight CPP students in various majors, spotlight program opportunities, share insights from campus departments, and much more. Interested or applying to Cal Poly Pomona? This is the podcast just for you! Subscribe or follow on...

[Subscribe](https://bit.ly/broncoscoop%20 "opens a new window")
