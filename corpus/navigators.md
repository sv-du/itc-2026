# Bronco Navigators

![Three students walk in the Japanese Garden](img/hero.png)

## About the Program

The Bronco Navigators program enhances the college experience by linking students one-on-one with a peer mentor. First-year students are paired with experienced peers invested in their success -- someone ready to listen, inspire, and recognize opportunities. We partner with Mentor Collective to make this program possible.

As a mentee, you will learn what to expect at CPP, how to approach challenges, and gain career advice. At the same time, mentors will build their networks while developing professional and leadership skills. You can discuss common questions that arise for a college student, including:

* How can I get involved on campus?
* How do I balance the demands of school, responsibilities, and socializing?
* How can I set myself up for academic, social, and career success?
* How do I make connections with peers and professional contacts?

## How It Works

Incoming students will be matched with a peer mentor (an experienced CPP student) based on common interests, background, academics, and professional aspirations.
