## A note about the use of AI in this website...

Generative AI tools, such as [Google Gemini](https://gemini.google.com) and [Microsoft Copilot](https://copilot.microsoft.com), were utilized to help create and format parts of this website’s content. In our role of supporting faculty in their use of AI for courses, we are dedicated to the fair and ethical use of generative AI.
