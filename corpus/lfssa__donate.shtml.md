# Donate

## Donate now through December 1st

Unidos plays a vital role in advocating for the Latin community on campus, promoting cultural awareness, and fostering meaningful connections. Be a part of this growing community and help make a lasting impact.

We are launching a **fundraiser  through CPP Launchpad to raise money for our Latin students at CPP to reach their academic goals**. Whether you’re able to donate—no matter the amount—or simply share and repost our fundraiser, we’re deeply grateful for your support.

[![](img/launchpad-donation.png)](https://crowdfund.cpp.edu/project/48180 "Donate Today!")
