# Campus Delegation

## U.S. House of Representatives

![Representative Grace Napolitano](https://www.cpp.edu/gcr/img/napolitano1.jpg)

### Representative Grace Napolitano

**32nd Congressional District of California**

The district includes the cities of Azusa, Baldwin Park, Bradbury, Covina, Duarte, El Monte, Irwindale, La Puente, La Verne, San Dimas, South El Monte, West Covina, Glendora, Industry, Monrovia.

**Committees**:

* Natural Resources
* Transportation and Infrastructure

[Official Biography](https://napolitano.house.gov/about)

![Representative Norma Torres](https://www.cpp.edu/gcr/img/torres.jpg)

### Representative Norma Torres

**35th Congressional District of California**

The district includes the cities of Chino, Montclair, Ontario, Chino Hills, Eastvale, Fontana, Pomona, Rancho Cucamonga, Upland.

**Committees**:

* Appropriations
* House Administration

[Official Biography](https://torres.house.gov/about/full-biography)

![Representative Linda Sánchez](img/licensed-image.jpeg)

### Representative Linda Sánchez

**38th Congressional District of California**

The district includes the cities of Artesia, Bellflower, Cerritos, East La Mirada, Hawaiian Gardens, Lakewood, La Mirada, La Palma, Los Nietos, Montebello, Norwalk, Pico Rivera, Santa Fe Springs, South El Monte, and Whittier.

**Committees**:

* Ways and Means
* Democratic Steering and Policy

[Official Biography](https://lindasanchez.house.gov/about)

## California State Senate

![Senator Susan Rubio](https://www.cpp.edu/gcr/img/rubio.jpg)

### Senator Susan Rubio

**California's 22nd State Senate District**

The district includes the cities of Alhambra, Arcadia, Azusa, Baldwin Park, Covina, El Monte, Industry, Irwindale, La Puente, Monterey Park, San Gabriel, Rosemead, South El Monte, Temple City and West Covina as well as the unincorporated communities of Avocado Heights, Charter Oak, Citrus, East Pasadena, East San Gabriel, Mayflower Village, North El Monte, South Monrovia Island, South San Gabriel, South San Jose Hills, Valinda, Vincent and West Puente Valley.

**Committees**:

* Insurance (Chair)
* Energy, Utilities & Communications
* Governmental Organization
* Health
* Transportation

[Official Biography](https://sd22.senate.ca.gov/biography)

![Senator Josh Newman](img/josh-newman1.jpg)

### Senator Josh Newman

**California's 29th State Senate District**

The district includes the cities of Anaheim, Brea, Buena Park, Chino Hills, City of Industry, Cypress, Diamond Bar, Fullerton, La Habra, La Palma, Placentia, Rowland Heights, Stanton, Walnut, West Covina and Yorba Linda.

**Committees**:

* Education (Chair)
* ​Budget and Fiscal Review
* Transportation
* Energy, Utility, and Communications
* Elections

[Official Biography](https://sd29.senate.ca.gov/biography)

![](bobarchuleta1.jpeg)

### Senator Bob Archuleta

**California's 30th State Senate District**

The district includes the cities of Rialto, San Bernardino, Colton, Fontana, Grand Terrace, Highland, Rancho Cucamonga, Redlands, Upland, Loma Linda.

**Committees**:

* Military and Veterans Affairs (Chair)
* ​Business, Professions and Economic Development
* Transportation
* Government Organization

[Official Biography](https://sd30.senate.ca.gov/biography)

## California State Assembly

![Assemblymember Chris Holden](chrisholden.jpeg)

### Assemblymember Chris Holden

**California's 41st State Assembly District**

The district includes cities of Pasadena, Upland, Altadena, Monrovia, Claremont, San Dimas, La Verne, La Canada Flintridge, Sierra Madre, San Pasqual, Bradbury, as well as communities of Rancho Cucamonga, Phelan, Oak Hills, Pinon Hills, Wrightwood, San Antonio Heights, Hesperia, and Lytle Creek.

**Committees:**

* Appropriations (Chair)
* Communications and Conveyance
* Utilities and Energy

[Official Biography](https://a41.asmdc.org/biography#biography)

![Assemblymember Blanca Rubio](https://www.cpp.edu/gcr/img/b-rubio.jpg)

### Assemblymember Blanca Rubio

**California's 48th State Assembly District**

The district includes cities Covina, Irwindale, West Covina, Baldwin Park, Azusa, Glendora, Monrovia, Duarte.

**Committees:**

* Aging & Long-Term Care
* Budget
* Budget Subcommittee No. 1 on Health and Human Services
* Governmental Organization
* Water, Parks & Wildlife
* Elections
* Rules

[Official Biography](https://a48.asmdc.org/biography)
