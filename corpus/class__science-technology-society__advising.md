# Advising

## Why Advising Is Important for STS

STS is different from every other major at Cal Poly Pomona. Because it is cross-disciplinary, the only listing of STS and AI Ethics and Society Option courses available next term is on this page. Also, because there are so many ways to develop your interests in STS, advising is particularly important. You should be in touch with Peter Ross ([website](https://www.cpp.edu/faculty/pwross/) with office hours) every term.

![Peter Ross giving outstanding advisor address](../img/ross-outstanding-advisor-2011.jpg)

 Ross giving an Outstanding Advisor address.
