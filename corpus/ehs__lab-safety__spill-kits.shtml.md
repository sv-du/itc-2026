## Kit Information

EH&S has developed a standard spill kit for both chemical and oil related spills. EH&S kits include absorbent socks and mats to help contain a small spill as well as basic PPE such as a pair of goggles and nitrile gloves. Each spill kit is uniquely numbered and contains an anti-tamper sticker to ensure kits are promptly replaced once opened. If you need a spill kit, please contact EH&S at x4697.
