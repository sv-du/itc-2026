# California Forum for Diversity in Graduate Education

![forum logo](../img/forumlogo.gif)

## **2024 California Forum for Diversity in Graduate Education**

Dear Students,

You are invited to join students from all over California at the **2024 California Forum for Diversity in Graduate Education**, which will be held in-person, Saturday, October 19, 2024, at UC Riverside.

The CA Forum for Diversity in Graduate Education seeks to acquaint and connect students from underrepresented groups with the career and academic opportunities associated with advanced study in a wide range of disciplines. Students will have the opportunity to meet representatives of the nation's leading graduate schools offering Master's and Ph.D. degrees and to attend workshops and panel sessions. Registered students will have access to open houses from over 150 leading institutions nationwide throughout the fall.

Forum workshops will provide information about selecting the right graduate program; the graduate application process; financial aid opportunities; preparing for the Graduate Record Examination (GRE) and employment prospects. This forum will not benefit those interested in obtaining a medical, dental, pharmacology or law degree.

We urge you to take advantage of this unique opportunity to explore graduate studies either at the master's or doctoral level.

Sincerely,
Rebecca Rivas

Cal Poly Pomona Graduate Forum Coordinator

**Eligibility:** Sophomores, Juniors, Seniors and Master's candidates (GPA of 3.0 is recommended for all students)

**How to Register:** Please see event flyer under Event Information.  Registration will close on Oct. 7th, or once the Forum reaches capacity.

**Cost: This is free event! A lunch will be included for attendees.**

### **Traveling to the Forum**

Students wishing to participate in this year's Grad Forum can either arrange their own transportation, take public transportation, carpool, or go on the bus that CPP will be providing to travel to UC Riverside.

For students traveling by bus, please reserve your seat [here.](https://forms.office.com/r/FbXidc7Q9j)

Reservations are on a first-come, first-served basis and subject to availability.  Please fill out liability release form below and submit to gradstudies@cpp.edu by Oct. 16th. Please note you cannot board the bus without submitting a liability release form. Students will be placed on a waiting list for the bus once all seats have been reserved.

*We hope you will take advantage of this great opportunity!*

**Questions**

Please contact Graduate Studies at gradstudies@cpp.edu.
