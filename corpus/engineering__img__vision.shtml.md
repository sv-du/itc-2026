## The College of Engineering's

### Vision

Become the leader in modern engineering education through robust "Learn by Doing" in the classroom and beyond.

![A vision poster from the College of Engineering with the text: Become the leader in modern engineering education through robust "Learn by Doing" in the classroom and beyond.](poster-vision.png)
