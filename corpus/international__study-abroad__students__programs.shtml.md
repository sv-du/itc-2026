# Program Discovery

CPP currently offers over 100 study abroad programs to over 60 countries all around the world. Programs are academically  focused with credit that counts toward  graduation and your degree. Program descriptions can be found throughout individual program types.

[***Search All Programs here***](https://cpp-sa.terradotta.com/_portal/program-discovery?search=[{%22filterType%22:%22programDiscoveryProgramType%22,%22filterValues%22:[{%22value%22:%221%22},{%22value%22:%226%22}]}]#courseSectionDetails_1026560)

## Types of Programs

![student in class](../img/560-fac-led.jpg)

### Faculty-Led Programs

Students participate in a program directed by CPP faculty, alongside other CPP students. Eligible for CPP or approved transfer credit.

[Faculty-Led Programs](https://cpp-sa.terradotta.com/_portal/program-discovery?search=[{%22filterType%22:%22programDiscoveryProgramType%22,%22filterValues%22:[{%22value%22:%221%22},{%22value%22:%226%22}]},{%22filterType%22:%22programDiscoveryProgramParameters%22,%22filterValues%22:[{%22id%22:10000,%22value%22:%22Faculty-Led%22}]}] "Faculty-Led Programs. ")

![exchange student raising hands](../img/sa-programs-exchstud.jpg)

### Exchange Student Programs

CPP students enroll as a visiting student at an international university and a student from the host institution comes to Cal Poly Pomona. CPP students pay their regular CPP tuition.

[Exchange Student Programs](https://cpp-sa.terradotta.com/_portal/program-discovery?search=[{%22filterType%22:%22programDiscoveryProgramParameters%22,%22filterValues%22:[{%22id%22:10000,%22value%22:%22Exchange%22}]}] "Exchange Student Programs. Explore Programs")

![csu ip](../img/560-sa-csu-ip.jpg)

### CSU International Program (IP)

Offered through the CSU Chancellor's office, these programs are year-long. Students can take courses toward their major or one year of a foreign language.

[CSU International Program](https://cpp-sa.terradotta.com/_portal/program-discovery?search=%5B%7B%22filterType%22:%22programDiscoveryProgramParameters%22,%22filterValues%22:%5B%7B%22id%22:10000,%22value%22:%22CSU%20IP%22%7D%5D%7D%5D "CSU International Program. ")

![paper and pencil](../img/sa-programs-affiliatedpro.jpg)

### CPP-Affiliated Programs

CPP is affiliated with a host of third-party providers who have programs all over the world and have a range of majors and country offerings.

[Non-CSU Programs](https://cpp-sa.terradotta.com/_portal/program-discovery?search=[{%22filterType%22:%22programDiscoveryProgramParameters%22,%22filterValues%22:[{%22id%22:10000,%22value%22:%22Affiliated%20Provider%22}]}] "Non-CSU Programs. ")

## **Other Program Types**

**Internships Abroad**

Internships abroad allow students to gain work experience in a new culture and earn academic credit.

**Research Abroad**

Research abroad can be done in conjunction with faculty or conducted independently, and can encompass a wide variety of fields.

**Service-Learning Abroad**

Service-learning opportunities usually include partnering with local community organizations and address a variety of social, economic, environmental or health challenges facing the community.

**Independent Study**

An individual learning experience abroad (conferences, seminars, etc.) Students can coordinate directly with faculty or department for these opportunities.

**Programs not sponsored by CPP**

Students may have the option to participate in non-CPP programs administered by other institutions or organizations. Students have to proactively make arrangements with a host program and work closely with the Office of Study Abroad and their academic or faculty advisers to ensure approval and credit transfer. Students pay the host institution or organization directly.
