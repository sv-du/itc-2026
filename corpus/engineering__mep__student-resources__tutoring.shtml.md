# MEP Tutoring

![MEP tutors](/engineering/mep/img/mep-tutors.jpg)

## Tutoring Session Times

MEP offers tutoring throughout week.  To maximize effectiveness, we recommend students schedule recurring appointments.

* **Days and Times:** Monday to Saturday; hours vary throughout the week.
* **Location:** See tutoring schedule link for locations and/or Zoom links.

## How to Schedule an Appointment

* Visit the current [tutoring schedule](https://livecsupomona.sharepoint.com/%3Ax%3A/s/mep_tutoring/Ee_bOUncugRLnsEeo_GtkKwB8vHmtjyYfvN3PPvQ5_n2gA). CPP log-in credentials required.
* View the orange tab for supported courses and blue tab for listings.
* Click "Make Appt" to schedule via [CPP Connect](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/cppconnect/index.shtml).
* When you arrive at your appointment, go to [CPP Connect](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/cppconnect/index.shtml) to check in.
