## About Me

Noam Saragosti is a designer and curator whose practice spans architecture, interiors, furniture, preservation, and cultural programming. He is a founding partner of Aunt Studio, a multidisciplinary design practice based in Los Angeles, where he leads projects ranging from buildings and interiors to custom furniture and exhibition design.

He also serves as Resident Director of the Neutra VDL Studio and Residences in Silver Lake, where he leads preservation and rehabilitation work while curating a robust slate of exhibitions, performances, workshops, and residencies. These public programs invite artists, designers, and thinkers to engage the historic site as a platform for contemporary cultural production.

Since 2018, Noam has taught design studios and seminars in the Department of Architecture at Cal Poly Pomona, focusing on adaptive reuse, structural expression, and the intersection of architecture and infrastructure.
