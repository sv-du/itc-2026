# PolyTransfer Summer Transition Program

![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white-mobile.svg)
![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white.svg)

## About the Program

The PolyTransfer Summer Transition Program is designed for incoming transfer students to develop a sense of belonging to Cal Poly Pomona (CPP) through engagement with their peers, CPP faculty, staff, and resources. You will learn more about the CPP community and how to be successful at CPP.

![Students standing on steps wearing PolyTransfer shirts and student in middle holding a "We Love Transfers" sign. ](../img/transfer-student-with-billy.jpg)

## Summer 2026 Transition Program Dates: July 16th -18th

### Learning Outcomes

Students who join us for the Summer Transition Program will have the opportunity to:

* Get familiar with tools, resources, services, and programs
* Receive a peer mentor who can provide guidance during the first academic year at CPP
* Find a sense of belonging and community by connecting with faculty, staff, and other transfer students

## Program Benefits

Benefits of the program include:

* Free meals, parking, housing
* Connect with CPP faculty, staff, and students
* Explore admissions, financial aid, careers, and majors
* Meet peers, enjoy activities, giveaways, and more!
