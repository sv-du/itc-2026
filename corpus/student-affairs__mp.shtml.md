# Free Menstrual Products Now Available

## Free Menstrual Products

Cal Poly Pomona has installed over 45 menstrual product dispensers in first-floor restrooms across campus.

Additionally, ASI has partnered with Flow to bring dispensers to ASI buildings as well, including the BSC, BRIC, and Children's Center. For more info on ASI dispensers, [click here.](https://asi.cpp.edu/facilities/bsc/bsc-amenities/)

All menstrual products are **FREE** for students. To see where dispensers are currently installed, look below, or pick up a map at the Student Health Center!

## Frequently Asked Questions

### A dispenser is defective. Who should I contact?

Please contact Custodial Services at (909) 869-3029.

### A dispenser is out of products. Who should I contact?

Please contact Facilities Planning and Management customer service at (909) 869-3030, Ext. 3030.
