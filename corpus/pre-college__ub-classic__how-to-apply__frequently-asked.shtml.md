# Frequently Asked Questions

![Decorative Banner](../../img/banner.png)

## When does the program start / when can I join?

We provide services **year-round** and recruit students as openings become available, typically in the Fall and the Spring. To begin the process please see "[How to Apply](ub-application.shtml)"

## What type of students are you looking for?

**We are looking for students who are considered [eligible](eligibility.shtml) under federal guidelines and who:**

* have the **desire****and motivation** to attend college
* will continuously work to be a better student
* are dedicated to their academics and learning
* are committed to **participating** **actively** until graduation

## I am sure I am not a low-income student, can I still be eligilbe?

**Yes! Please do not count yourself out.**

Income eligibility is **not** based on your gross income (so you may still qualify under the federal guidelines\*) and you also qualify if:

* your parents did not earn a college degree

**or**

* you meet the criteria of "high risk\*\*"

\*federal guidelines are based on household size and **cannot** be determined by income alone

\*\*please see [eligibility](eligibility.shtml)

## I am already a strong student, are these services for me?

Yes! We strive to provide prescriptive services tailored to the needs and goals of each individual student.

## How will I know when I am accepted?

You should be in regular contact with the advisor assigned to your school for details. The program will notify you personally or via mail whether or not you have been accepted. If you have not heard from us, please do not hesitate to [contact us](../meet-your-advisor/staff-all.shtml).

## Do I have to apply every year?

No, you do not need to reapply each year. Once you have been accepted into the program, you are a participant until you graduate from high school.

## Are there any costs to participate?

No

All services and activities are free as a result of your federal tax dollars. This is your program and you have already paid for it. You will **NEVER** be asked to pay for anything.
