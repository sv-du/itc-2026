# Give to CLASS

![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white-mobile.svg)
![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white.svg)

## Your Support Shapes Tomorrow's Leaders

At the College of Letters, Arts and Social Sciences (CLASS) at Cal Poly Pomona, we believe in the power of humanities, performing arts, and social sciences to transform lives and enrich communities. Each gift, regardless of size, makes an impact in our students' educational journey.

![graduates walking to line up](../img/students/commencement-shadows.jpg)

## Your generosity directly impacts:

**State-of-the-Art Facilities & Equipment**

Modern learning environments and technology are essential for our students' success. Your support helps us maintain cutting-edge facilities where creativity and innovation flourish, from renovated classrooms to specialized equipment for hands-on learning experiences.

**Excellence Funds for Innovation**

The CLASS Excellence Fund and our department-specific funds provide crucial flexibility to seize new opportunities, respond to emerging needs, and invest in promising initiatives. These funds allow us to say "yes" to innovative ideas that might otherwise go unexplored.

**Student Scholarships**

Every student deserves access to quality education regardless of financial circumstances. Your scholarship support removes barriers, reduces student debt, and allows promising students to focus on their studies rather than financial worries.

**Experiential Learning Opportunities**

From community service projects to undergraduate research, study abroad experiences, and paid internships, your support creates transformative learning opportunities that connect classroom knowledge with real-world applications. These experiences build the critical thinking, collaboration, and leadership skills employers value most.

## Your contribution helps achieve our strategic goals:

### Goal 1: Cultivate Trust

Goal 1: Cultivate Trust - Build an inclusive community where diverse perspectives are valued through programs that enhance belonging and transparent communication.

### Goal 2: Advance Student Success

Goal 2: Advance Student Success - Create learning experiences that develop critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills for success in a changing world.

### Goal 3: Elevate CLASS at CPP

Goal 3: Elevate CLASS at CPP - Strengthen the role of humanities, social sciences, and performing arts as essential components of Cal Poly Pomona's polytechnic identity.

### Goal 4: Empower Staff & Faculty

Goal 4: Empower Staff & Faculty - Support professional development and research opportunities that foster innovation and excellence in teaching and scholarship.

### Goal 5: Steward Resources

Goal 5: Steward Resources - Expand our capacity to deliver exceptional educational experiences through strategic investment in our programs and people.
