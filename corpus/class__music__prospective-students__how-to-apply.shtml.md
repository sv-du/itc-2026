## A Quick Guide on How To Apply

We are excited that you are considering joining our vibrant and dynamic community of musicians, educators, and industry professionals.

Here you will find specific information on how to apply to our music programs, including important deadlines, audition requirements, and contact details.

## The Application Process

[How To Apply

### Steps to Join Our Music Community

Ready to Apply?
Click to learn how to apply, schedule your audition, and submit materials on time for the CPP Music program.](#modal-0 "How to Apply: Steps You Need to Take")

### How to Apply: Steps You Need to Take

×

1. **University Application**
   * Submit your application to Cal Poly Pomona through the [Cal Poly Pomona Admissions Office](https://www.cpp.edu/apply/index.shtml).
     + Review the general university admission requirements and deadlines.
2. **Music Department Audition**
   * Complete the [Music Department Supplemental Application](https://forms.office.com/Pages/ResponsePage.aspx?id=HqZLFuw5XU-J_6ofAKUhtHsyie5YOi9LrxijHDAIMllUMzlBSTJMUDRCTzlXSlNPRFVHWUpJVkFMNy4u) to schedule an audition.
     + You need to submit a university application before you schedule an audition.
   * ***Please note: Every student applying for a CPP Music program MUST audition.***
3. **Submit Your Audition Materials**
   * All audition materials and additional documents need to be submitted by the specified deadlines.
   * Late submissions may not be considered.

[Click Here for Audition Requirements and To Schedule Your Audition](audition-information.shtml)

Close

[Important Deadlines

### Key Dates To Be Aware Of

Don't miss a deadline. Click to view important application dates for Spring and Fall 2025 admission to Cal Poly Pomona.](#modal-1 "Important Deadlines: Key Dates for Your Application")

### Important Deadlines: Key Dates for Your Application

×

**Spring 2026 Admission**

Apply online for the Spring 2026 term via [Cal State Apply](https://www.calstate.edu/apply) by August 31, 2025 for all majors.

**Fall 2026 Admission**
Apply online for the **Fall 2026** term via [Cal State Apply](https://www.calstate.edu/apply) between October 1, 2025 - December 1, 2025 for all majors.

Please check the [Cal Poly Pomona Admissions Office](https://www.cpp.edu/apply/index.shtml) for any updates or changes to these deadlines.

[Visit the Office of Admissions For More Information](https://www.cpp.edu/admissions/index.shtml)
