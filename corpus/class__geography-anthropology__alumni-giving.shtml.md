# Alumni and Giving

## Alumni

Our alumni are an integral part of the department – from conducting workshops, visiting classes, meeting students in conferences and events, hosting company tours, connecting students and new graduates with jobs and internship opportunities, to giving generously for scholarships and other educational funds. The support from our alumni is critical in maintaining the high quality of our programs.

Please keep us updated of your career movements and let us know if you would like to be involved in department events or support the department.

[Get involved with CPP Alumni](https://www.cpp.edu/alumni/index.shtml)

## Support the Department

Your support directly funds student scholarships, groundbreaking research, and innovative programs.  Make a meaningful difference today by investing in tomorrow's leaders and breakthrough discoveries that create a lasting impact for future generations!
