# Project SUCCESS

![Students listen to an event](../img/one1.jpg)

## Persistence. Retention. Success.

### About the Program

Through holistic mentoring and support, the Project SUCCESS program fosters academic success.

## Benefits of Joining

### Peer Mentorship

A Project SUCCESS Peer Mentor is a sophomore, junior, or senior who mentors students through their first academic year at CPP. During one-on-one and group meetings (Kin Group Meetings), Peer Mentors will provide direct campus knowledge from their own freshmen year experience. Project Success Mentors play a critical role in the success of our students based on the understanding that peer mentorship is highly effective in increasing students’ sense of belonging and academic transition to the university.

### Access to Free Tutoring

Project SUCCESS students also have access to free tutoring in partnership with the [Educational Opportunity Program's (EOP) Tutorial Services](https://www.cpp.edu/ssep/eop/learning-center_home.shtml). This allows our students to gain a better understanding of their course material by connecting them with academic strategists. Students have the option to create one-time appointments as well as ongoing weekly appointments.

### Course Credit Towards Degree Requirements (Depends on Major)

Project SUCCESS students register for a 3-unit course (LS 1020) in the Liberal Studies department that fulfills Unrestricted Elective Requirements depending on major. Please see your advisor to determine Course Credit. **(Required in Fall only).**

The course is designed to facilitate our students' transition to the university through experiences that outline the university’s academic expectations, support services, social resources, and opportunities.

### Staff and Faculty Connections

Kin Group Leaders are professional staff and faculty mentors who work on campus in various departments. During one-on-one and group meetings (Kin Group Meetings), Kin Group Leaders will focus supporting our students on personal wellbeing, early intervention efforts, and campus resource connections.

### Leadership and Development

Students are lead through engagement workshops that deal with leadership and career development throughout the academic year.

### Eligibility & Apply Now

To Be Eligible:

* Apply and gain admission to Cal Poly Pomona
* Be an incoming first-time freshman at Cal Poly Pomona
* Fully complete and submit Project SUCCESS application on myBAR
* Be a full-time (12 units each semester) registered Cal Poly Pomona student for the entire 2025 – 2026 academic year
* Maintain a minimum of 2.0 cumulative grade point average (GPA) during the program, with the goal to achieve no less than a 2.5 cumulative GPA
* Enroll in the 3-unit Liberal Studies course for Fall Semester
* Attend the Project Success Orientation and other events as described
