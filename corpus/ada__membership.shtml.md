# Membership

## Membership

The AdA is comprised of students, staff and faculty united to increase awareness of, and to promote access and equity for, individuals with disabilities throughout the Cal Poly Pomona campus community.

## Why Join?

Members have the opportunity to lend their expertise to support initiatives aimed at improving access and to participate in programming and events that educate and increase awareness of disabilities.

## Open Invitation

Prospective members are encouraged to contact Cathy Schmitt Whitaker at clschmitt@cpp.edu for current meeting date and time.

## Commit Your Level of Support

Complete the online form provided below to become an active Member or Friend of AdA.
