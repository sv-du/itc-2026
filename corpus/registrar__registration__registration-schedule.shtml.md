# Registration Schedule

## Registration Period:

### Fall 2025 Registration Schedule

**Breakdown by Class Level**

Each student is assigned a registration window within this time period. Your assigned window will be displayed in your [BroncoDirect](https://my.cpp.edu/uPortal/p/broncodirect.ctf1/max/render.uP) - Student Center. prior to the start of registration.

**Fall 2025 Term Session Dates (excludes dates for finals):**

* **August 21, 2025 - December 7, 2025 (*15-Week Session*)**

| **Registration Period** | **Group Level and Start Time** | **Unit Limit** | **Registration Window** |

|  | **Graduating Seniors begins at 8:00 am** | **18** | **April 15 - June 8, 2025** |
|  | **Seniors begins at 8:00 am** | **16** | **April 18 - June 8, 2025** |
|  | **Juniors begins at 8:00 am** | **16** | **April 21  - June 8, 2025** |
|  | **Sophomores begins at 8:00 am** | **16** | **April 24 - June 8, 2025** |
|  | **Freshman begins at 8:00 am** | **16** | **April 28 - June 8, 2025** |
| **Add/Drop Period** |  | **18** | **August 11 - September 4, 2025** |
