## About Me

I was born in Buenos Aires, Argentina. I obtained my Ph.D. in Mathematics from Universidad de Buenos Aires in 2010. In 2009, I moved to Boston, Massachusetts, with my then-girlfriend, now wife and mother of our daughters, Milana and Emilia. After a six-month visit at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology as a Research Affiliate, I had two Visiting Assistant Professor positions at Worcester Polytechnic Institute and the University of California Riverside from 2011 to 2017. Since then, I has been at Cal Poly Pomona. My research interest lies in Analysis, specifically in spaces of functions on irregular domains.
