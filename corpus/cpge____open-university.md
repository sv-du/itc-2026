# Open University | Flexible access to university courses, year-round

![Open University group image](everyone2.jpg)

## Experience Cal Poly Pomona’s hands-on learning — without being admitted to a degree program.

Open University at Cal Poly Pomona gives you the freedom to pursue your goals on your own terms. Whether you’re exploring a new subject, preparing for graduate study, or advancing in your career, you can enroll in real CPP classes taught by expert faculty. Here, learning happens by doing through projects, collaboration, and discovery. It’s your opportunity to join a nationally ranked university community and see what’s possible.

## Who Can Enroll

### Open to Everyone, Ready to Become by Doing

You don’t have to be an admitted student to get started at Cal Poly Pomona. Open University is designed for learners at every stage of life who want to grow, explore, and achieve more. Classes can be taken at the undergraduate and graduate levels with all required approvals. Students may transfer in a maximum of 24 undergraduate units and 9 graduate units to a Cal Poly Pomona degree.

[LEARN ABOUT REQUIREMENTS](faq.shtml)

## Who Does Open University Serve?

### Find Your Path Forward

Open University at Cal Poly Pomona serves learners who want to strengthen their career, explore a new field, finish their degree, or take university classes without formal admission. You’ll find flexible courses, hands-on learning, and a supportive academic community built around becoming by doing.
Select the path that best fits your goals below.

## The CPP Polytechnic Experience (PolyX)

Every Open University student gets access to The [CPP Polytechnic Experience (PolyX)](https://www.cpp.edu/aboutcpp/polyx.shtml) — hands-on learning that connects theory to real-world practice.

## How It Works

### Your Path to Learning, Simplified.

Registering through Open University is easy. Follow these steps to begin your journey:

[#### Choose Your Course

Explore CPP’s Schedule of Classes to find courses that inspire you.](https://schedule.cpp.edu/ "opens a new window")

[#### Request Approval

Confirm prerequisites or request permission from the instructor (if required)](registration/index.shtml)

[#### Pay & Enroll

Complete registration and select a payment option that fits your needs.](tuition/payment-instructions.shtml)

#### Start Learning

Engage in projects, labs, and discussions, and earn transferable university credit.

![Classes are offered during the Fall, Spring, and Summer terms.](img/3terms.png)

## Classes are offered during the Fall, Spring, and Summer terms.

### Registration and enrollment details

Open University offers registration each Fall, Spring, and Summer. Dates vary by term and are listed on the term calendar, and enrollment is finalized once tuition is paid in full or the first payment of an approved payment plan is made.
