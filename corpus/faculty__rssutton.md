## About Me

I am a graduate of CPP and have over 25 years experience in clinical dietetics and clinical management, and I continue to work per diem in the field of clinical dietetics to maintain competency in Medical Nutrition Therapy, one of the courses that I teach to dietetics majors.  As the director of Cal Poly's dietetic internship, I have supervised over 140 interns who successfully completed the program to qualify to take the RD exam.  My passion is to mentor and motivate students and interns to reach their academic and career goals.
