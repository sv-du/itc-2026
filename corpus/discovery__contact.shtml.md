## Contact Us

### *Admin Office*

---

The Discovery Camp website provides comprehensive information on all aspects of the summer camps. We have compiled a list of answers to [frequently asked questions](faq.html) in order to help answer any remaining questions quickly. If you would like to contact us for further assistance, you may send us an email at discovery@cpp.edu or call us at (909) 869-2069. Email gets a quicker response.

### *Staff Info*

---

![girls in science camp](img/sciencecamps.jpg)

Camp counselors are carefully selected based on their personal skills, spirited demeanors and knowledge of their camp topics and/or education so that they can serve as positive role models for our campers. All counselors are subjected to meticulous training on camp operation and counselor roles, and are also highly instructed on adult and pediatric first aid, automated external defibrillators (AED), and cardiopulmonary resuscitation (CPR) by the American Red Cross. Our exceptionally-trained staff is dedicated to engaging campers in great activities, develop both joy and curiosity, provide campers with an inclusive environment, foster new relationships, increase camper self-esteem and confidence in social settings, promote creativity and teamwork in group settings, and provide an exciting & safe environment. The camps staff is comprised of University staff and college students majoring in the specialty of the camps.
