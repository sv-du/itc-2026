## Study Cybersecurity at CPP

![A student presents in front of screen projection that reads: Social Engineering](img/2024-cybersecurity-fair-missa-presentation.jpg)Whether you are a first-time college freshman or pursuing a graduate degree, there are three different programs to choose from. Each department's curriculum is dynamic and hands-on.

**As a Computer Science major,** you'll receive an education with an extensive background in computer programming languages, computer architecture, and the design and application of algorithms.

**Focusing on cybersecurity?** The PolySec lab is a center for education and research in information assurance, security, and forensics. A balanced curriculum, competitions, research, and project opportunities support students in their cybersecurity careers.

**Prefer a business-focused approach?** Our Computer Information Systems department gives students an education that aims to solve business problems with computing technology. Courses include programming, web development, software design, telecommunications, and business fundamentals.

**If you're interested in engineering,** then Electrical or Computer Engineering is just for you! Graduates design next-generation smartphones, create advanced software, and find new ways to transmit power.

Visit our websites for more information:

* [Computer Science, BS](https://www.cpp.edu/programs/sci/computer-science/computer-science.shtml)
* [Computer Information Systems, BS](https://www.cpp.edu/programs/cba/cis/computer-information-systems.shtml)
* [Computer Engineering, BS](https://www.cpp.edu/programs/eng/electrical-and-computer-engineering/computer-engineering.shtml)
* [Electrical Engineering, BS](https://www.cpp.edu/programs/eng/electrical-and-computer-engineering/electrical-engineering.shtml)
