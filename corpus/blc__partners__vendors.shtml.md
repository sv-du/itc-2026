# Vendors

## Welcome!

**We’re so glad you’re interested in joining us as a vendor at Cal Poly Pomona!**

Bringing your business to Cal Poly Pomona is a unique opportunity to tap into a vibrant, diverse community of over 25,000 students, faculty, and staff. With a high foot-traffic campus and a strong culture of supporting local and small businesses, you'll have direct access to a dynamic customer base eager to discover new products and services. Whether you're offering a service, fashion, or other goods, Cal Poly Pomona provides an energetic and engaged audience ready to buy. Join a thriving campus community and showcase your brand to the next generation of leaders, innovators, and trendsetters!

**Note: A new contract, registration, and reservation process has been implemented beginning July 2025.**

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What can I bring? Do I need to submit a layout?

* If you are bringing more than 2x 10'x10' canopies, and a single table, the Bronco Leadership Center must request through Campus Facilities, Planning & Management to approve your booth layout (this process can take 1-3 weeks).
  + The approval is valid for the current academic semester (August-December, January-May).
  + If you are approved in August, you will have to work with the Bronco Leadership Center again in January to be approved until May.
* If you only have up to 2x 10'x10' canopies and a single table the Bronco Leadership Center does not have to request approval from Campus Facilities, Planning & Management.

### When can I sell on campus?

Vendors can sell on campus from August 25, 2025-May 8, 2026 (the academic year), excluding weekends and campus holidays.

### How do I schedule dates?

* There are only three vendor spots available per day.
  + Spots are reserved in advance, and are subject to availability.
* Dates must be at least two weeks (10 business days) from today's date.
  + If the required documents are incomplete five days before the scheduled date, the reservation for the space will be canceled.
* To request date(s), the link to the form is sent to you once your Adobe Sign and materials are approved.
  + We only schedule for the current academic semester.
* **This is only a request, we will contact you if your dates are confirmed.**

The Bronco Leadership Center's vendor program is unavailable during campuswide events.

* Bronco Bound (one Saturday in April)
* Green and Gold Block Party (one Saturday in January or February)
* Explore CPP (one Saturday in November)
* Fall Conference (entire third week of August)
* Orientation (June-August)
* Commencement (mid-May)
* and other large-scale campus events

### How do I pay?

Payment is required on the day of your reservation, or the first day of your consecutive-day reservation. Any returned checks are subject to an additional $25.00 returned check fee. Any cancellation can incur a $75.00 fee.

### What is the process to become a vendor on campus for 2025-2026?

* Review the new contract
* Complete the registration form
* Sign and return contract sent to you
* Make reservations, once you’re a registered vendor

### I do not have AdobeSign? How do I sign my contract?

Print, sign, and either mail or drop it off at the Bronco Leadership Center

### My current insurance policy meets all requirements but expires before May 8th, 2026. What do I do?

* If your insurance coverage expires before May 8, 2026, you are required to email a copy of the new policy to leadership@cpp.edu at least 10 business days before it expires to continue to vend at CPP.

### Will I need to register every year?

Registration will now be an annual process.

### Do I have to register by a certain time every year?

You can register anytime during the academic year (Aug 25, 2025- May 8, 2026), but your registration will only be good until May 8th, even if you register in April.

### Can I reserve a vending space for a year in advance?

No, not at this time. We only allow requests within the current Academic Semester. Semesters run from Late August-December and January-early May. If you request in August for dates in January, your requests will not be approved.

### Are there any promotional rates available for extended stays or multiple sites?

If you request a non-standard layout of 2-3 vendor spaces, there will be a flat fee of $100/day. You will NOT be charged for each space.
