# SCCUR 2025

---

SCCUR is broadly multi-disciplinary including the sciences, humanities, social sciences, arts and performing arts. SCCUR typically draws 700-1,000 participants, undergraduates, faculty mentors, administrators, and community college and high school students. Students present their work either in 15-minute formal panel presentations chaired by faculty members, in poster form, or in exhibition or performance. Undergraduates in all disciplines are welcome including Science, Technology, Engineering, Math, Business, Arts, and Humanities, and Performing Arts. The conference seeks to replicate the conditions of professional and scholarly meetings, and many undergraduate scholars use SCCUR as a "first conference" experience, before going on to present their work at national professional meetings [Learn more about SCCUR](https://www.sccur.org/).

**Benefits of Your Involvement**

* Great experience and opportunity to present scholarly and/or creative activity
* Participation by students from approximately 80 colleges/universities
* Simple abstract submission process with Eventbrite registration
* CPP students can request for their registration fees to be paid by the Office of Undergraduate Research!

Please see the steps below. SCCUR 2025 will be on **Saturday, November 22, 2025** hosted by Cal State Channel Islands.

---

## Present at SCCUR 2025

---

![Step 1 on a blue background](https://i.imgur.com/SSCmSlH.png)

### Abstract Submission

If you have any issues with your abstract submission, please contact SCCUR 2025 directors at sccur2025@csuci.edu. You will receive communication from SCCUR directors regarding your abstract acceptance. Please keep a copy of this email to request a registration fee waiver, Step 2!

**The submission deadline has passed.**

![Step 2](https://i.imgur.com/EQXdtp3.png)

### SCCUR Registration via OUR

The Office of Undergraduate Research (OUR) at Cal Poly Pomona will pay the $90 registration fee for CPP undergraduate students who are accepted to present by the SCCUR organizers. OUR will email CPP students of their acceptance and instructions to register. Look for an email from our@cpp.edu.

Do not register on the SCCUR website. The OUR cannot reimburse students who pay registration fees on the SCCUR website.

## NCUR 2026

---

SCCUR has partnered with the National Conference on Undergraduate Research (NCUR), to provide you an opportunity to present your research again. NCUR will take place April 13-15, 2026, at the Greater Richmond Convention Center.

NCUR brings together thousands of students each year in a unique environment for the celebration and promotion of undergraduate student achievement. To learn more about NCUR 2026 visit their [website.](https://www.cur.org/events-services/ncur/)
