| MIC 201/201L | MIC 201/201L or BIO 2060/2060L  If taken in semester, the extra 2\* will count toward Elective Support. |
| BIO 211/211L | BIO 211/211L or STA 1300; OR  Any STA course (If graduating before S2020)  The excess unit from the semester course (0.5\*) could count toward Elective Support. |
| BIO 230 | Bio 230; OR  BIO 2300 (1\*) & Move BIO 2300 (0.5\*) to Elective Support. |
| BIO 303 | BIO 303; OR  BIO 2400 (4\*) & Move BIO 2400 (0.5\*) to Elective Support. |
| BIO 310 | BIO 310; OR  BIO 3220 (4\*) & Move BIO 3220 (0.5\*) to Elective Core. |
| BIO 441 | BIO 441 or BIO 461 (2\*)  If taken in semester,  student can take either 1 or 2 semester units:  BIO 4410 or BIO 4610 (1.5\*) & Any UD spare 0.5\* unit.  BIO 4410 or BIO 4610 (3\*) where 2\* applies here and 1\* goes to Elective Core. |
| BIO 450 | BIO 450; OR  BIO 4300 (4\*) & Move BIO 4300 (0.5\*) to Elective Core. |
| BIO 451/451L | BIO 451/451/L; OR  BIO 4320 (4.5\*) & BIO 4320L (0.5\*) & Move BIO 4320L (1\*) to Elective Core; OR  BIO 4320 (4.5\*) & Any UD BIO or CHM 0.5\* unit [instructor consent] |
| BIO 490 | BIO 490; OR  Any UD BIO or CHM unit (1\*) |
| Core Electives | Allow all courses listed in the most recent semester Biotechnology Curriculum to count toward the Electives for all quarter curriculum years. |
| B5 Restriction in Support | Waive the restriction; Any BIO or CHM Area B5 GE course will count.  If taken in semester (4.5\*), the excess 0.5\* will go to Elective Core. |
| CHM 121/121L, 122/122L, 123/123L  (Total = 12\*) | CHM 121/121L & 122/122L & 123/123L (original) ; OR  CHM 1210/1210L (6\*) & CHM 1220/1220L (6\*); OR  CHM 121/121L (4\*) & CHM 122B/122LB (2\*) & CHM 1220/1220L (6\*); OR  CHM 121/121L (4\*) & CHM 122/122L (4\*) & CHM 1220C/1220CL (4\*) |
| CHM 314, 315, 316, 317L, 318L, 319L  (Total = 12\*) | CHM 314 & 315 & 316 & 317L & 318L & 319L; OR  CHM 3140 (6\*) & CHM 3150 (4.5\*) & CHM 3140L (1.5\*) & Move CHM 3150L to Elective Core;    These bridge or cap courses should apply wherever appropriate  CHM 315B, CHM 318LB, CHM 3150C, CHM 3150CL |
| CHM 327/327L, 328/328L, 329/329L  (Total = 12\*) | CHM 327/327L & 328/328L & 329/329L (original); OR  CHM 327/327L & 328/328L & CHM 4990/L (4.5\*); OR  CHM 3270/3270L (6\*) & CHM 3280/3280L (6\*); OR  CHM 3270/3270L (6\*) & CHM 3280 (4.5\*) & UD BIO/CHM (1.5\*); OR  CHM 3270/3270L (6\*) & UD BIO/CHM (6\*); OR  CHM 3210 (4.5\*) & UD BIO/CHM (7.5\*) |
| PHY 121/121L, 122/122L, 123/123L | PHY 121/121L & 122/122L & 123/123L (original) ; OR  PHY 131/131L & 132/132L & 133/133L; OR  PHY 1210/1210L & 1220/1220L; OR  PHY 1510/1510L & 1520/1520L  These bridge or cap courses should apply wherever appropriate  PHY 122B/122LB, PHY 1210B/1210LB |
| MAT 120 | MAT 120, or MAT 1200, or MAT 115, or MAT 1140 |
| AREA E Requirement in Support | Waive the restriction; Any Area E GE course will count. |
