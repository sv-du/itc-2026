# Graduation FAQ

## FAQs

### How do I apply for graduation, and how do I know if I qualify?

You can apply for graduation by going to your Student Center via BroncoDirect. On the top left-hand side click the down arrow where it says 'other academic' and select 'Apply for Graduation' and begin the application.

To see if you qualify for graduation, contact your academic advisor to ensure the requirements were completed. You can also look through your DPR to verify completion.

Did this answer your question? If not, please email your inquiry to tga@cpp.edu. A Registrar's Office representative will reach out to help you further.

### If I completed all my major requirements but have a few units left for my minor can I postpone my graduation until I am done?

Once you complete your degree requirements, you should apply for graduation. If you are in the process of completing a minor or double major, please visit your academic advisor for further guidance.

Did this answer your question? If not, please email your inquiry to tga@cpp.edu. A Registrar's Office representative will reach out to help you further.

### What is the Graduating Senior Registration Window, and how do I receive it?

The Graduating Senior Registration Window is a special registration time for students who have applied for graduation before a certain deadline.
The registration window means you will register after students with priority but before all other seniors.

Did this answer your question? If not, please email your inquiry to tga@cpp.edu. A Registrar's Office representative will reach out to help you further.

### What is the graduation application fee?

The graduation application fee is a one-time, non-refundable fee that all students pay when applying to graduate. This fee helps cover the costs of reviewing degree requirements, processing applications, and preparing for commencement. For additional information please visit the [Student Accounting & Cashiering Services (SACS)](https://www.cpp.edu/student-accounting/index.shtml) site.

Did this answer your question? If not, please email your inquiry to tga@cpp.edu. A Registrar's Office representative will reach out to help you further.

### Do I need to pay the graduation application fee again, if I paid already and need to change my expected graduation term?

No, the graduation application fee is only a one-time fee per academic program. You only need to pay for the same degree and academic program once.

However, If you graduated with a bachelor’s degree and paid the application fee and pursuing a master’s degree, once again you will need to pay for the master’s degree application fee.

Did this answer your question? If not, please email your inquiry to tga@cpp.edu. A Registrar's Office representative will reach out to help you further.

### When will the graduation application fee apply to my student financial account?

The graduation application fee will apply to your CPP financial account after the Registrar’s Office starts the degree pre-check process and switch your degree checkout status from applied for graduation to other statuses such as “Approved, Program in Review, Pending coursework.” You may learn more about your graduation application review process by visiting the Steps to Senior Success page.

Did this answer your question? If not, please email your inquiry to tga@cpp.edu. A Registrar's Office representative will reach out to help you further.

### Do I have to pay the graduation application fee, if I apply to graduate out of residency?

Yes, if you have not paid the graduation application fee in the past, you will need to pay it.

Did this answer your question? If not, please email your inquiry to tga@cpp.edu. A Registrar's Office representative will reach out to help you further.
