## Education and Expertise

**Education**

* MBA with Marketing Emphasis; California State Polytechnic University, Pomona, 1999
* B.S. Agribusiness Management; California State Polytechnic University, Pomona, 1997

**Teaching Areas/Expertise:** Specializes in teaching agribusiness economics, the history of agriculture and its importance in the world today, as well as ethical issues in the food, agriculture, and apparel industries.
