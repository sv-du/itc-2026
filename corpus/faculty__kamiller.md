### **Special Projects**

Dr. Miller is also involved in year round work with Centennial Farm, a 3-acre working farm located in Costa Mesa, California on the [OC Fair & Event Center](https://ocfair.com/) grounds. She has collaboratively developed agriculture education curriculum for various events and programs at Centennial Farm and is the voice behind the [Cow Milking video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=plRd4xKSxJw), sponsored by the [Dairy Council of California,](https://dairycouncilofca.org/)that visitors watch while visiting the Millennium Barn at the farm.
