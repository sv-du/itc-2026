## Public Holidays and Special Hours

Please note that the gallery is closed on Campus-observed State Holidays, during required Campus Closures, Intersession Breaks (Summer and Winter Breaks) and between exhibitions when art receiving/retrievals, installations/deinstallations are eminent.

The Gallery is also closed on certain days of the week. During exhibition duration periods some viewing arrangements may be possible for press and large groups only, by appointment, and based on staff availability on those requested times.

For more information, call **(909) 869-4302** or email **artgalleries@cpp.edu**.

Find directions to the general Cal Poly Pomona campus by air, car, and public transportation [here](https://www.cpp.edu/aboutcpp/visitor-information/directions.shtml "directions to Cal Poly Pomona").

## Parking

All vehicles must purchase and display a parking permit. They can be purchased from the Parking Information Booth located on Oak Lane next to Building 109 or from one of the many self-serve booths located throughout campus.  Use Lot C, F9, Parking Structure 1 or 2.

Daily visitor parking permits are $8 Monday through Friday and $5 each day on weekends. $2 hourly permits are also offered. Please visit the campus  [Visitor Parking Information](https://www.cpp.edu/aboutcpp/visitor-information/parking.shtml) page.

Parking Phone: (909) 869-3061

For any other map information go to [Campus Maps](https://map.concept3d.com/?id=1130).

## Walking Directions

From Parking Lot C, go through the Student Services Building (Building 121) Breezway, up the light-grade sloped path that is Olive Lane Walk. The Kellogg Art Gallery and Sculpture Garden will be passed the University Library and to the left. The Huntley Gallery is inside the Library (Building 15) on the 4th Floor (Suite 4435) next to the Library's Special Collections. This is approximately a 5 minute walk.

From [Parking Lot F9](https://www.cpp.edu/maps/?id=1130#!m/276415), or [Parking Structure 1](https://www.cpp.edu/maps/?id=1130#!m/276504) go toward Voorhis Cir. and follow the path to Olive Lane Walk. Follow the walk toward the University Library. It will be on the right. The Kellogg Art Gallery (Building 35A) and Sculpture Garden will be passed the library and to the left. The Huntley Gallery is inside the Library (Building 15) on the 4th Floor (Suite 4435) next to the Library's Special Collections. This is approximately a 10-15 minute walk.

From [Parking Structure 2](https://www.cpp.edu/maps/?id=1130#!m/276389), follow the path to the Bric, and walk towards the Bronco Student Center.  The Kellogg Gallery and Sculpture Garden will be at the top of the hill, to the right. Passed the Kellogg Gallery, and diagonally across is the University Library (Building 15). The Huntley Gallery is inside the Library on the 4th Floor (Suite 4435) next to the Library's Special Collections. This is approximately a 10-12 minute walk.

Accessible parking spaces can be found in Lot A, Lot E1, Lot F4, Lot G, Lot K, Lot L, Lot M, Parking Structure 1 Level 2, Parking Structure 2 Level 1. Adjacent to Building 1, and at the Northeast corner of Building 17. The nearest Lot to both Galleries is located in Lot G. For Mobility Cart assistance, please request it 2 days in advance with the  [Disability Resource Center](https://www.cpp.edu/drc/contact-us/hours-location.shtml).
