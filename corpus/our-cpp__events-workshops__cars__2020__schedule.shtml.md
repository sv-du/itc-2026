### **6th Annual Creative Activities & Research Symposium**

**Location:** Virtual Event through Zoom Meeting

[**Please RSVP for more information!**](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/6th-cpp-creative-activities-research-symposium-cars-tickets-111558973894)

6th Annual CARS Schedule

| **Schedule** | **Time** |
| --- | --- |
| **OPENING RECEPTION**  Keynote by Student Researcher Alumna Vivianne Tabuena | **9:00 to 9:30 am** |
| **ORAL PRESENTATIONS**  [Online Program with session schedules and presenter directory](program/index.shtml)  **Session 1:** Oral, Behavioral and Social Sciences (9:30 - 10:45 am)  **Session 2:** Oral, Biological and Ag Sciences (9:30 - 10:30 am)   **Session 3:** Oral, Interdisciplinary (9:30 - 10:15 am)  **Session 4:** Oral, Engineering and Physical Sciences (11:00 am - 12:15 pm)  **Session 5:** Oral, Business and Administration (11:00 am - 12:00 pm)  **Session 6:** Oral, Biological Sciences (NIH-RISE, 9:30 am - 11:30 am) | **9:30 am to 12:15 pm** |
| **POMONA VALLEY REVIEW SPECIAL SESSION**  Poetry and Creative Writing Readings  [Online Program with session schedules and presenter directory](program/index.shtml)  **Session 7:** Poetry (11:00 am - 1:00 pm) | **11:00 am to 1:00 pm** |
| **P****OSTER & CREATIVE WORKS PANEL**  [Online Program with session schedules and presenter directory](program/index.shtml)  **Session 8:** PCW, Behavioral and Social Sciences (1:00 - 1:45 pm)  **Session 9:** PCW, Bio, Ag, Health, and Physical Sciences (1:00 - 1:50 pm)  **Session 10:** PCW, Engineering and Computer Science (1:00 - 1:55 pm) | **1:00 pm to 2:00 pm** |
| **CONFERENCE ENDS**  Thank you for participating in CARS! | **2:00 pm** |
