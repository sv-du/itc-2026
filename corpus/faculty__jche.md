## Bio.

Dr. Jiangning Che is a professor in the Department of Apparel Merchandising and Management at Cal Poly Pomona. He holds a Ph.D. in Textile Coloration and boasts 10+ years of industrial experience in the textile and apparel industry before joining Cal Poly Pomona as an Assistant Professor in 2013. His expertise lies in industry color application within textile and apparel domains, along with related principles. Dr. Che's research focuses on color science and application, textile dyeing using natural plant resources.
