# Faculty and Staff Forms

## [Graduate Exception Administrative Petition](https://polyform.cpp.edu/Loginformproc/UnityForm.aspx?d1=AfgWogZWtXDoqiQlWmzUrA5F%2fKR33FxZTiVqFhmXqMSapUUrntS%2fdVsySPR2L%2bAkmoZG9yYK9hsni1bN3FvsLgKKixpinu0E8LBp%2bnLZh7%2ftlvQjBV1LQLwB7qdDyZFjkfoEiA0ferfDPTuMvCu2q6mo%2bq19LMduKOTTdF2TN1iQmLMKISDe5bjF%2bkyWneQkBpInAUotvoI0xpeHSce%2fZAkxtMYC1%2btVebhHKzTtkmTn0rMQjloXkmKW3tz0bZahMQ%3d%3d "https://polyform.cpp.edu/Loginformproc/UnityForm.aspx?d1=AfgWogZWtXDoqiQlWmzUrA5F%2fKR33FxZTiVqFhmXqMSapUUrntS%2fdVsySPR2L%2bAkmoZG9yYK9hsni1bN3FvsLgKKixpinu0E8LBp%2bnLZh7%2ftlvQjBV1LQLwB7qdDyZFjkfoEiA0ferfDPTuMvCu2q6mo%2bq19LMduKOTTdF2TN1iQmLMKISDe5bjF%2bkyWneQkBpInAUotvoI0xpeHSce%2fZAkxtMYC1%2btVebhHKzTtkmTn0rMQjloXkmKW3tz0bZahMQ%3d%3d") (E-Form)

The *Graduate Exception Administrative Petition* is a faculty advisor-initiated form used only to request an exception to an administrative policy. This includes:

* Retroactive graduate credits
* Change of pre-requisite courses for conditionally admitted students
* Repeatable course unit limit
* Credit by prior learning for programs with general and advanced standing

**Note:** this petition cannot be used for deviations from Title 5.
