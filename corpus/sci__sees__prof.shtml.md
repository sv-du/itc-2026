# Professional School Preparation

## Overview

![Sydney Perez (optometry), Marlena Lopez (veterinary), Cristal Landa (dentistry), Jordan Berry (CLS)](img/preprof.jpg)

L-R Above: Sydney Perez (optometry), Marlena Lopez (veterinary), Cristal Landa (dentistry), Jordan Berry (CLS)

Since the inception of the SEES program, we have had hundreds of SEES students accepted into professional and graduate schools. It's a distinction we are very proud of. Below, you will find resources that help our students prepare their course of study in order to satisfy the requirements for most programs.

## Professional Schools

SEES has consulted pre-professional offices across the CSU system and the admissions websites of programs across the country in order to provide the following advising sheets for the various careers. **Click on the icon below for each career advising sheet.**

[**=> Click on this chart comparison to see curriculum alignment of most common career programs (PDF)**](https://www.cpp.edu/sci/sees/docs/preprofessionals.pdf)
