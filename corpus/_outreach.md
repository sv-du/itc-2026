## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does Cal Poly Pomona offer scholarships?

Yes! More information can be found on the [Financial Aid and Scholarships website](https://www.cpp.edu/financial-aid/scholarships/index.shtml).

### What does "polytechnic" mean?

Polytechnic translates to our "learn by doing" philosophy, which emphasizes hands-on learning. Learn more at our [PolyX website](https://www.cpp.edu/aboutcpp/polyx.shtml).

### Can I take classes at CPP and not have it count towards a degree?

You can take classes through the [College of Professional and Global Education](https://www.cpp.edu/cpge/index.shtml) which will not count towards a degree.

### Can an outreach counselor help with choosing my CPP classes?

Outreach counselors don’t usually help prospective students pick their CPP classes. This happens during Orientation, when you will meet with an academic advisor from your college who will help you select your courses.

Each academic college has advisors for the majors they house; [see the colleges and departments here.](https://www.cpp.edu/academics/colleges-departments/index.shtml)

### Who can I talk to about financial aid?

For information about Financial Aid, you can visit our [Financial Aid website](https://www.cpp.edu/financial-aid/).

### If I have been denied, who do I contact to appeal?

Any questions regarding an admissions decision must be directed to the Office of Admissions. If you would like to appeal your admissions decision, you can [complete the appeals form](https://www.cpp.edu/admissions/appeals/form.shtml).

Or, contact the [Office of Admissions](https://www.cpp.edu/admissions/index.shtml).

### Can you tell me more about my major?

You can find information about the majors we offer by using the [academic program search page](https://www.cpp.edu/programs/index.shtml). Other resources include our [University Catalog](https://catalog.cpp.edu/content.php?catoid=53&navoid=3972) (be sure to select the correct academic year) or reaching out [directly to the department](https://www.cpp.edu/academics/colleges-departments/index.shtml) to ask questions.

### How can I get an internship or find a job on campus?

For more information about internships and jobs, visit the [Career Center’s website](https://www.cpp.edu/career/index.shtml), where you can log onto Handshake and search for student jobs, including those on and off campus.
