# Bilingual Authorizations

***Promote Multilingual Academic Excellence***

**Join our Bilingual Program!**

**Add a BILINGUAL AUTHORIZATION**

**to your Multiple Subject or Single Subject Credential!**

*At CPP, we offer the Bilingual Authorization (BilA) in English Plus*

**Spanish** **Filipino!** **Mandarin!**

*We partner with the CSU-wide consortium, ALBTEP for Asian Languages: Filipino, Mandarin, Korean, Vietnamese, Khmer, & Hmong.*

**Pre & Post Credential Bilingual Program Pathways**

| --- | --- |
| ***Are you beginning a Multiple or***  ***Single Subject*** ***Credential Program?***  **[*Bilingual Information Flyer*](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1mtcHDZ7wqklL6DphMLqNk9OcM1ExjRZL/edit?usp=sharing&ouid=113745383696481061153&rtpof=true&sd=true)** | ***Do you have a valid California Multiple or***  ***Single Subject Credential?***  **[*Post-Credential*](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1egMzvfuddWGHf5rq3bZ9hDhBPRJucNdw/edit?usp=sharing&ouid=113745383696481061153&rtpof=true&sd=true)**  **[*Bilingual Program Information Flyer*](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1egMzvfuddWGHf5rq3bZ9hDhBPRJucNdw/edit?usp=sharing&ouid=113745383696481061153&rtpof=true&sd=true)** |

*Asian Bilingual Authorization Scholarship Information is available via the CSU’s Asian Languages Bilingual Teacher Educator (*[*ALBTEP*](https://www.fullerton.edu/btep/scholarships/)*) Preparation Consortium.*

**What would you have to do?**

Application Process (see flyer)
Two Courses
One Test
Fieldwork and/or Bilingual Clinical Practice
Final Assessment & Survey
