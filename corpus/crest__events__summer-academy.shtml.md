# Summer Academy

![group of students walking by the CPP mural and a pair of prospective students with their parent](../img/students600x600.jpg)

## Summer 2026

### High School Summer Academy

CREST-RASM in collaboration with the Office of Student Success at Cal Poly Pomona will host the 2026 Summer Academy to bring in 30 high school students to CPP to explore Industrial and Manufacturing Engineering. This grant aimed at igniting interest in STEM students. Building on these experiences and relationships, we will develop a three-day summer academy program.
