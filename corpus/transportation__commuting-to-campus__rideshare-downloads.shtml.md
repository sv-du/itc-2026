# Rideshare Downloads

## Daily Rideshare Records

**How to submit your record to the Rideshare Office:**

* Click the [Online Rideshare Reporting Tool](https://app.smartsheet.com/b/form/c78c41babdda4231abc2ef2ec95fa898)
* Make sure your information is accurate and true
* Click submit

**The Rideshare Office does not accept late records. Records must be submitted no later than the 7th for the prior month. Bronco Bucks will reflect on your account by the 15th.**Submitting your Daily Record for the first time or are having problems? Contact the Rideshare Office.

## Change Form

Rideshare members who move to a new address, change or add carpool partners, change your name, change your work phone number, you must update your information with the Rideshare Office. You may submit via e-mail or print the form and submit it to the Rideshare Office.

* [Change Form (pdf)](https://www.cpp.edu/~parking/documents/faculty-staff-documents/change_form_2012.pdf)
