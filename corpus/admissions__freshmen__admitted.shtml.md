# Freshman Students

![Bronco Bound](../img/cppstudents1)

## Bronco Bound!

Congratulations on becoming a part of the Bronco family! To make sure your transition is as seamless as possible, view your Next Steps below.

## Admitted Freshmen Checklist

### ✓ Complete Housing Application

2026-2027 Housing application will be available to ALL admitted students in early **February.**

Those in need of, or interested in, on-campus housing are encouraged to apply early.

CPP offers residence halls, suites, and apartment-style accommodations. If you plan on living on campus, you must complete an application:

[Residence Halls & Suites](https://www.cpp.edu/housing/futureresidents/floorplans.shtml "https://www.cpp.edu/housing/futureresidents/floorplans.shtml")

Please visit the [University Housing Services](https://www.cpp.edu/housing/index.shtml "https://www.cpp.edu/housing/index.shtml") for more information.

### ✓ Submit your Intent to Enroll

Freshmen are required to submit their **Intent to Enroll** and pay a $150 Enrollment Deposit (if applicable) by **May 1** in order to complete the acceptance of the offer of admission. Admitted students may accept their admission offer via their [**Bronco Applicant Portal**](https://applicant.cpp.edu/).  To view step-by-step instructions on accepting your admission offer (applicable to undergraduate students only) visit our [tutorial](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7-QVeZ2Z33c).  If required to pay the deposit, it wail be applied to student's first term tuition. For payment options, visit [Cashiering Services](https://www.cpp.edu/~student-accounting/payment-options/index.shtml).  *Note: Enrollment Deposit is non-transferable and non-refundable.*

The Enrollment Deposit will be may be waived for some students based on **Student Aid Index (SAI)**derived by the FAFSA.  Other enrollment deposit may waivers include:

* **You qualify for an Athletic Scholarship.**
* **You are a military affiliated student using either Chapter 33 – Post 9/11 GI Bill or Chapter 31 – Vocational Rehabilitation & Employment Program under the Department of Veterans Affairs.  Please contact the [Veterans Resource Center](https://www.cpp.edu/veterans/index.shtml) by e-mailing vetquestions@cpp.edu or calling 909-869-6994.**

If you are eligible for an Enrollment Deposit fee waiver you will not be prompted to pay when submitting your Intent to Enroll.

**Failure to submit your Intent to Enroll and pay your Enrollment Deposit (if applicable) by the stated deadlines may result in your application being withdrawn.**

### ✓ Review Cost of Attendance and Apply for Financial Aid

**Financial Aid Offer notifications are anticipated to begin mid-April.**

The priority deadline for 2026-2027 is March 2, 2026!

**Cal Poly Pomona’s school code is 001144**.

* Visit [studentaid.gov](https://nam11.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fstudentaid.gov%2Fh%2Fapply-for-aid%2Ffafsa&data=05%7C02%7Cjazminef%40cpp.edu%7C438a26f2c2b449bc435e08dc317d05d7%7C164ba61e39ec4f5d89ffaa1f00a521b4%7C0%7C0%7C638439660259231237%7CUnknown%7CTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJWIjoiMC4wLjAwMDAiLCJQIjoiV2luMzIiLCJBTiI6Ik1haWwiLCJXVCI6Mn0%3D%7C0%7C%7C%7C&sdata=CBywMqlFZSgwyP5Qkh%2FBJ66Pxg27JaMAk140MozQb8I%3D&reserved=0) to complete the **Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) form,** the application for U.S. Citizens and Permanent Residents.
* Visit [dream.csac.ca.gov](https://nam11.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fdream.csac.ca.gov%2Flanding&data=05%7C02%7Cjazminef%40cpp.edu%7C438a26f2c2b449bc435e08dc317d05d7%7C164ba61e39ec4f5d89ffaa1f00a521b4%7C0%7C0%7C638439660259240883%7CUnknown%7CTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJWIjoiMC4wLjAwMDAiLCJQIjoiV2luMzIiLCJBTiI6Ik1haWwiLCJXVCI6Mn0%3D%7C0%7C%7C%7C&sdata=bm0R8d5ow93NHSgHtYjmfV%2B391gZgmPGM56%2BGW98ZuY%3D&reserved=0) to complete the **California Dream Act Application**, the application for undocumented & nonresident students who qualify for a non-resident exemption under Assembly Bill 540 (AB 540). to receive certain types of financial Aid.
  If you need assistance completing your aid application, attend one of our [upcoming workshops](https://www.cpp.edu/financial-aid/resources/fafsa-dream-workshops.shtml).

 Visit the [Financial Aid for Newly Admitted Undergraduates](https://www.cpp.edu/financial-aid/undergraduate/newly-admitted/index.shtml) page for additional information on the cost of attendance, financial aid process at Cal Poly Pomona, including the different types of aid available and how to apply for [scholarships](https://www.cpp.edu/financial-aid/scholarships/index.shtml).

### ✓ Register for Orientation

Admitted freshmen are required to attend a mandatory orientation. Registration for orientation will begin after admission decisions have been made. Freshman orientation will run from June through July.

**Students may register for Orientation from April 2 - May 11.**

For more information, visit [Orientation Services.](https://www.cpp.edu/orientation/index.shtml)

**Failure to register for orientation by the stated deadlines may result in your application being withdrawn.**

### ✓ Register for the Calculus Placement Assessment (ALEKS PPL)

**The ALEKS PPL should be completed by your orientation day to ensure proper class placement.**

Some students with specific majors are encouraged to take the ALEKS PPL - the mathematics placement program offered by Cal Poly Pomona. It allows you to review, take practice tests, and then come to campus for the official placement test. For more information visit [Mathematics and Statistics department](https://www.cpp.edu/sci/mathematics-statistics/calculus-placement.shtml).

### ✓ Dual Enrollment Credit

Admitted Freshman who took classes at a college and/or university during high school will need to submit official college transcripts to the Office of Admissions. Without official transcripts, the university cannot grant the college transfer credit. Official transcripts should be sent by the student's orientation date, but no later than **July 15**.

Official transcripts can be sent via:

* [National Student Clearinghouse](https://www.studentclearinghouse.org/)
* [Parchment](https://www.parchment.com/)

### ✓ Submit Final Transcripts

Final high school transcripts will be due to Cal Poly Pomona by **July 15**.

**Electronic Transcripts**

Electronic transcript timeline

| **Methods of Delivery** | **Timeframe & Removal of To-Do List Item\*** |
| --- | --- |
| e-Tran/eTranscript California | 2 business day\*\* |
| Electronic Transcript \*\*\* | 2 - 3 Weeks |

 \* Allow time for processing outside of timeframe of this table

\*\* During peak season, these can take 2 - 3 weeks to process

\*\*\* Includes electronic transcripts that are sent/emailed in PDF format

Admitted students can send electronic transcripts via:

* [National Student Clearinghouse](https://www.studentclearinghouse.org/)
  + Select: California Polytechnic State University - Pomona
* [Parchment](https://www.parchment.com/)
  + Recipient: verify@cpp.edu
* Other Electronic Services
  + If your institution will be sending PDF transcripts then indicate recipient verify@cpp.edu

***Important: Do NOT send transcripts to yourself and then forward to*** ***verify@cpp.edu*** ***or they will be considered unofficial, they must be sent directly to email address above.***

**Non-Electronic Transcripts**

Should these services not be available, have your institution send the transcripts in a sealed, unopened envelope directly to:

> Cal Poly Pomona
>
> Attn: Office of Admissions
>
> 3801 W. Temple Ave.
>
> Pomona, CA 91768

Do not open transcripts, otherwise they will be considered unofficial and we will not be able to accept them. After we receive your transcript please allow 2 - 3 weeks for processing time.

To make sure transcripts are received on time, transcripts should be ordered as soon as an admission decision has been made.

**Failure to submit official transcripts by the stated deadlines may result in your application being withdrawn.**

### ✓ Submit AP/IB/CLEP Scores

The deadline to submit official AP/CLEP/IB scores is **July 15**. AP exams taken this year should be sent to us as soon as scores become available.

Please send scores earlier if they are available to ensure timely processing of your credits and Fall course placement.

**Advanced Placement (AP)**
You can also send scores online through [College Board](https://www.collegeboard.org/)

**College Level Examination Program (CLEP)**
Request your CLEP transcripts online through [College Board](https://clep.collegeboard.org/earn-college-credit/your-score)

**International Baccalaureate (IB)**
Request your IB transcripts or certificates through [IB Transcript Request](https://www.ibo.org/programmes/diploma-programme/assessment-and-exams/getting-results/)

### ✓ Upload your Photo for your Bronco Card (Student ID)

The Bronco Card is the official Cal Poly Pomona photo identification card for all Cal Poly Pomona faculty, staff, students and affiliates. It can also be used for dining services and convenience store locations, as well as access to campus services, buildings, and laboratories.

Visit the [Bronco Card website](https://broncoonecard.com/) for complete photo guidelines, FAQs, and the photo upload link.
