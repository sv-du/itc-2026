# Semester@CPP

**Program Overview**

The Semester@CPP provides an opportunity for international students from around the world to study alongside American students for one or more semesters at Cal Poly Pomona. Qualified students can enroll in courses from 80+ academic programs at Cal Poly Pomona. Credits earned for taking courses at Cal Poly Pomona will appear on the student’s official transcripts.

Download the program flyer [HERE (PDF)](docs/brochure-2025-26.pdf).

**Why You Should Apply?**

* Taking credit-bearing courses without having to be officially admitted by Cal Poly Pomona as matriculated students
* Credit units earned can be transferable to students’ home universities
* Eligibility to choose courses from all the academic program available at Cal Poly Pomona
* Experience of taking university courses alongside American students
* Flexibility of taking online classes virtually in your own country
