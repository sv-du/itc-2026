# Services Offered

## No-Cost and Low-Cost Services

Student Health Services provides Cal Poly Pomona students with affordable, accessible and high-quality health care. Most services are covered by the mandatory student health fee already included in tuition payments.

## Types of Services

### Student Health No-Cost Services Include:

* Unlimited appointments made online, by phone, or in person with licensed & board-certified clinicians, nurses and physician assistants
* Digital X-Rays
* Travel consultations
* Well-patient physicals (1 per academic enrollment; cost of some lab work may be extra)
* Minor surgical procedures (removal of warts, cysts, moles; suturing; repair of ingrown toenail, etc.)
* Well-Womxn Exam
* Wraps, braces, slings, crutches, etc.
* Generic Tylenol through the pharmacy
* Flu Shots
* Tdap vaccine
* Tuberculosis screening
* Common STI/STD testing (Gonorrhea, syphilis, Chlamydia)
* Medication Abortion (call 909-869-4000 to make an appointment)

### Student Health Low-Cost Services Include:

* Prescription medications can be filled at our on-site pharmacy. By request, prescriptions can be printed and given to patients to be filled at an outside pharmacy at their own expense.
* Common non-prescription drug store items through the pharmacy
* Biopsied tissue
* Laboratory tests, including Pap and Cholesterol, sent to an outside lab
* Copies of medical records
* Wraps, braces, slings, crutches
* HIV Testing (expect on Tuesdays)
* All immunizations except Tdap
