## Our Philosophy

### From Seeds to Food and Fashion

Agriculture touches all of our lives, from the food we eat to the clothing we wear.
At the Huntley College of Agriculture, you will receive an education that prepares you to work on some part of that vast chain, whether it’s growing plants or designing clothes in a more sustainable manner, raising and caring for animals, educating people about eating healthier, creating new food products, or marketing and promoting these goods.
