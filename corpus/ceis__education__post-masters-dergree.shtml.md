# Post Master's Degree

Students,

Congratulations on completing your Master of Arts Degree in Education. With your degree, we know you have the foundational tools to become leaders in the profession. There are two other ways that you can continue to advance your leadership and academic careers:

1.      Apply for the [Preliminary Administrative Services Credential Program](https://www.cpp.edu/ceis/edleadership/adminservicescred/application-admission-ac.shtml "https://www.cpp.edu/ceis/edleadership/adminservicescred/application-admission-ac.shtml") as you approach 5 years of teaching experience.

2.      Apply to the [Educational Leadership doctoral program](https://www.cpp.edu/ceis/edleadership/doctoral-program/application-admission-doctoral-program.shtml "https://www.cpp.edu/ceis/edleadership/doctoral-program/application-admission-doctoral-program.shtml"). Click or tap if you trust this link.").
