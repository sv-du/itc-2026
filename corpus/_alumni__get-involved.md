## Get Involved

Interested in getting involved with your alma mater? Cal Poly Pomona's most valuable assets are its students and alumni, whose achievements exemplify the transformative power of education through social mobility, community impact, and meaningful change. Cal Poly Pomona helps anyone who dreams of success achieve it. Our alumni are inspiring the next generation by serving as innovators, leaders, educators, and visionaries who are making a positive impact worldwide. To help current students kickstart their careers, become career-ready, and achieve success, we invite you to volunteer today—sharing your experience, guidance, and insights to empower and uplift the future leaders of Cal Poly Pomona.
