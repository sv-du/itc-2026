# Gainful Employment Disclosure

## Program Name: Cal Poly Pomona Dietetic Internship

This program is designed to be completed in 10 months.

This program will cost $6,400 for in-state tuition and fees and $12,000 for non-residents if completed within normal time. There may be additional costs for living expenses. These costs were accurate at the time of posting, but may have changed since then.

Of the students who completed this program within normal time, the typical graduate leaves with $12,500 of debt.

1. *The program qualifies students to sit for the licensure exam in the following states:  Alabama, Alaska, Arkansas, Delaware, Florida, Georgia, Idaho, Illinois, Iowa, Kansas, Kentucky, Louisiana, Maine, Maryland, Massachusetts, Minnesota, Mississippi, Missouri, Montana, Nebraska, Nevada, New Hampshire, New Mexico, North Carolina, North Dakota, Ohio, Oklahoma, Oregon, Pennsylvania, Rhode Island, South Carolina, South Dakota, Tennessee, Texas, West Virginia.*
2. *The following states do not have licensure requirements for this profession: Arizona, California, Colorado, Connecticut, Indiana, Michigan, New Jersey, New York, Utah, Vermont, Virginia, Washington, and Wisconsin.*

For more information about graduation rates, loan repayment rates, and post-enrollment earnings about this institution and other post-secondary institutions, please click here: [College Scorecard](https://collegescorecard.ed.gov/)
