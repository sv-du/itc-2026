## **Kellogg Ranch Field Trips**

**Update (2/11/2026):** Please note that the information on this page is for the Spring 2025 field trips. We hope to have the details, the availability calendar, and the field trip reservation form for the **Spring 2026 field trips** out in the near future. Please check back later.

Thanks to its sunny weather and fertile soil, California is the #1 state in the nation in agricultural production. The mission of the Kellogg Ranch field trips is to offer visitors a chance to experience this first hand and gain appreciation for the influence of farming on everyday life

**The Petting Farm** - Get up close and personal with Oreo and his friends while experiencing how animals provide food so we can grow up strong and smart, as well as provide fiber like wool to keep us warm and dry

**Wagon Tour** - Learn about the history of the Huntley College of Agriculture and how our farm operations provide Cal Poly Pomona students with learn-by-doing opportunities and produce some of the most sought-after graduates by industry leaders.

**Children’s Garden** - What better way to experience agriculture than by playing in the dirt!  Visit our newly expanded Children’s Garden and explore the life cycle of plants, how some creepy crawlies actually help plants grow and that tasty helps us grow healthy and smart!

**Pick Your Own** - Enjoy the fruits of our labor!  Pick fresh produce and taste the difference with our expanded strawberry garden and revived vegetable fields.

## **Field Trip Packages**

Spring is a great time to visit the Farm! This year, our Spring Field Trips will be available Tuesdays through Thursdays from April 1-24. **Sign-ups will begin on Feb. 3**. The field trips will be more exciting than ever with educational enhancements in our popular Children's Garden!

### Child Package

$15 per participant. Field trips include a basket of strawberries or u-pick vegetables, depending on availability; a walking tour of Discovery Farm; the Hay Wagon Ride, Children’s Garden activities; and the Petting Farm.

### Adult Package

$11 per adult. Gives an accompanying adult access to all activities in the Child Package, except picking a basket of strawberries/vegetables. Only parents/chaperones that have been registered by the school or group may attend a field trip.

### Complimentary

One teacher and one aide for each group of up to 25 children will not be charged to participate in activities. (DOES NOT INCLUDE A BASKET OF STRAWBERRIES/VEGETABLES)
 \*If your group/class has special needs children, their aide will not be charged to participate.

### Home School Groups/Moms Clubs

Required to check-in as one group. Wristbands will be given out to the lead contact to distribute.

*\*Due to the hands-on learning environment of these field trips, groups may need to be split into smaller units.*

## **Signing Up for a Field Trip in Three Steps**

1. Check the **Availability Calendar** below to find dates that work for your group.
2. Fill out the **Field Trip Reservation Form** and request your preferred dates.
3. We will contact you to confirm the details of your trip.

## **Visiting Kellogg Ranch/ADA Accommodations**

This is a working farm, closed shoes and sun/windscreen are highly recommended.  Visitors may come in contact with dirt walkways, and uneven terrain.

If you need accommodations or have questions about accessibility, please contact kelloggranch@cpp.edu. Advance notice of at least 48 hours is recommended to arrange for some accessibility needs. Every reasonable effort will be made to provide accommodations in an effective and timely manner.

## Spring on the Farm Field Trip FAQs

Expand/Collapse All Spring on the Farm Field Trip FAQs

### What is your COVID-19 policy?

We will be following current LA County Health and [Cal Poly Pomona (CPP) Guidelines](https://www.cpp.edu/safer-return/index.shtml). YOU WILL BE NOTIFIED IF THERE ARE ANY CHANGES IN THE GUIDELINES.

### How long is a field trip?

Usually, between 1½ - 2½ hours, allow extra time if you will take a snack or lunch break. We request that you arrive 15 minutes before your reservation time to allow for check-in.

### What about parking?

Buses and cars will be provided with free parking. Directions and information will be provided in advance. Look for signs upon entering Kellogg Ranch (formerly known as AGRIscapes) on South Campus Drive ONLY.

### Do you require liability waiver forms?

Yes, ALL field trip participants including adults must fill out and return the liability waiver form that will be provided – One per family (everyone must be listed). Unfortunately, we will not be able to allow those without the completed forms to participate.

### What methods of payment do you accept?

Payment can be made with cash, checks (payable to the Cal Poly Pomona Foundation), or credit cards. Check and credit card refunds are not possible.

### Is there a picnic area and tables?

Yes, and they are available for free on a first-come, first-served basis.

### Where are you located?

Kellogg Ranch (formerly known as AGRIscapes) is located at 4102 S. University Dr., Pomona, Calif., 91768, next to the Cal Poly Pomona Farm Store. Enter on South Campus Drive from Temple Avenue.

### What's the best way to contact you?

Email us at kelloggranch@cpp.edu.
