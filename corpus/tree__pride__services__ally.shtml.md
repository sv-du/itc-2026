# Ally Education Workshop

## Learn More

After a year-long journey of research and collaboration with Southern California colleges and universities, this pilot program was recently launch **exclusively for student leaders!**

This workshop is designed to empower student employees, as well as members of student clubs and organizations, to become more effective allies to the LGBTQIA+ community.

Email the center at pride@cpp.edu for more information.
