## Benefits of Membership

1. Participating in market research/analytics helps develop critical thinking skills
2. Helps improve strategic decision making
3. Learn insights/data analytics, which is one of the fastest-growing fields in business jobs.
4. Make lifelong friends
5. Networking opportunities
6. Being involved in research shows employers that you are a critical thinker and intelligent.
7. Research/analytics can be applied and is used in many fields of business
