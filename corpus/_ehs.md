# Environmental Health & Safety

![Full view of SSB](img/cpp_arb_img.jpg)

## Welcome to EH&S

Environmental Health & Safety (EH&S), a department in the [Division of Administrative Affairs](https://www.cpp.edu/admin-affairs/index.shtml), is responsible for planning, implementing, and administering the campus Environmental Health and Safety Programs and for providing technical consultation, training, and inspection to ultimately ensure compliance with established laws and maintain a safe place to work, study, and play for faculty, staff, students, and visitors.
