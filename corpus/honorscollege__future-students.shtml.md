# Future Honors Students

## Discover Your Potential with the Kellogg Honors College

Welcome! We're excited you're exploring the Kellogg Honors College at Cal Poly Pomona — a vibrant community where ambition meets opportunity. Here, you’ll find everything you need to learn about the unique benefits, support services, and application process for joining our honors program. Use the links below to take the next step toward shaping your future at CPP.

## The PolyX Advantage

Card 1 of 4

### Apply to the Kellogg Honors College

Whether you’re a first-year student, current Bronco, or transfer applicant, there’s a path into the Honors College. Explore how to apply and take the next step in your academic journey.

Card 2 of 4

### Challenging Courses. Meaningful Connections.

Honors courses are small, discussion-driven general education classes that go deeper—led by dedicated faculty and designed to inspire. You’ll earn the same credits with more opportunities to engage and explore.

Card 3 of 4

### Turn Ideas Into Impact

Through the Engaged Learning Experience, Honors students explore their passions through original research or creative work—culminating in campus-wide presentations that showcase their growth, curiosity, and voice.

Card 4 of 4

### Serve. Connect. Grow.

Civic Engagement connects Honors students with real-world issues and each other through meaningful service. You'll give back, gain perspective, and turn community impact into personal and academic growth.
