# Senior Class Gift /Alumni Association

## Senior Class Gift

Historically, the graduating class provides a gift, funded by graduating students and their families, that contributes to the future of the Cal Poly Pomona community. This year’s Senior Class Gift will support the Presidents Scholarship. Every year, 25 students are selected to receive the President’s Scholarship based on their academic achievement, co-curricular activities, and outstanding application. With your gift, you will help support our most talented students and eventually walk across the commencement stage with their degrees in hand.

If you would like to join the committee or participate with input, please contact Jorge Garduño in Annual Fund at (909) 869-6849.

## Your Alumni Association

We are proud of the quality of our education and the value of our hands–on learning. Cal Poly Pomona is ranked among the best universities in the country year after year, and you can continue to be connected to the best long after you graduate.

Being a member of the Cal Poly Pomona Alumni Association means you are showing support for current and future alumni, and alumni programs that enrich the lives of students. Joining is the best way to stay connected with your peers, former classmates, and other generations of alumni. Members receive benefits, services, invitations to events, university publications and can choose an alumni chapter at no additional cost. Your active membership also helps to elevate the university’s national rankings.

Join today and tap into a network of more than 189,040 Cal Poly Pomona alumni.
