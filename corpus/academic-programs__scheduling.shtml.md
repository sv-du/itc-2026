### Time Module Deviations](#modal-1 "Time Module Deviations")

### Time Module Deviations

×

Standard time modules ensure that students can build a course schedule of major and university requirements. Deviations from these modules thus require significant justification, as they can seriously impact student success. To request a time module deviation, please fill out the form found here

[Time Module Deviation Form](protected/scheduling/time-module-deviation-form-revised-06.13.20231.pdf)

Submit this form to the AVP of Academic Planning, Keith Forward (kmforward@cpp).

## CourseMatch Submissions

CourseMatch is a CSU Chancellor's Office program that aims to provide students across the system with greater access to essential GE courses. CourseMatch courses must be fully online, GE-approved, maintain low DFW rates, and be staffed by trained faculty. Departments receive additional funding for CourseMatch student enrollment.

[Submit Your Course](coursematch.shtml)
