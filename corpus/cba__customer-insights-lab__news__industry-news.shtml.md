## NFTs

In the past few months, non-fungible tokens (NFTs) have become increasingly talked about all over the internet, but what exactly is it and why is it becoming so popular? Maira Genovese at The Drum details the latest buzz surrounding NFTs and the benefits to using it for brands and influencers.

[Click here to read summary](https://www.cpp.edu/cba/customer-insights-lab/news/industry-news-summaries.shtml#nfts "Click here to read summary. ")

## Social Media Trends

## Top 50 Market Research & Data Analytics Industry Report

The IA Association has published a report on the top 50 companies within the research and analytics industry of the US. Within the report, readers can understand more about each companies’ growth and development and its relative standing in the marketplace, as well as the statistics on the market research industry.

[Click here to view the report](https://www.ama.org/marketing-news/2020-top-50/ "Click here to view the report. ")
