## History at Cal Poly Pomona

Welcome to the History Department, where the past is very much alive and present! Our undergraduate and graduate students develop broad content knowledge and hone their research and analysis skills through innovative classes and individualized mentoring from our internationally-renowned faculty.

34

That's how many History Pre-Credential students participated in micro-internships with local secondary social studies teachers in Spring 2025!

## Explore Our Programs

[### Undergraduate Majors

We offer two different undergraduate B.A. options -- the Pre-Credential Option (designed primarily for aspiring secondary or middle school teachers) and the General Option (one with slightly more flexibility in terms of electives and general education courses).](undergraduate-programs.shtml "opens a new window")

[### History Minor

Do you enjoy taking History classes but are already majoring in another discipline? The History Minor gives you formal recognition for taking a set of History classes (and looks good on your transcript!)](#modal-0 "Minor (details)")

### Minor (details)

×

**Requirements**

* Successful completion of HST 1101, HST 1102 and HST 2201
* Successful completion of five additional history classes, of which at least three must be upper-division (HST 3300 or higher)
* Students interested in the history minor should meet with the History Department Chair, Dr. Rob Lewis (rwlewis@cpp.edu)

[History Minor (CPP Catalog, 2025-26)](https://catalog.cpp.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=69&poid=19695&hl=%22History+Minor%22&returnto=search)

[### History Master of Arts (Graduate)

Our History MA program offers you individualized attention from expert faculty, small classes and the support of your incoming cohort of fellow students. It is primarily designed for current and prospective secondary school teachers, but other alums have pursued further graduate study!](graduate-program.shtml "opens a new window")

## Alumni Spotlight

François Tchoyi completed his B.A. in History at CPP in 2016. Originally from Cameroon, Tchoyi came to the United States at the age of 16, and played intercollegiate basketball for five seasons at CPP. Tchoyi now teaches French and coaches three sports at Cushing Academy in Ashburnham, Massachusetts. He fondly remembers his CPP History courses!
