# Gift Shop

**The Farm Store at Kellogg Ranch has a great selection of gift items for any occasion. Items may be pre-ordered by calling (909) 869-4906 or put together while you visit the store.**

***Last day to order 12/18/24.  Not accepting orders from 12/19/24 - 1/2/25.***

***Check out our [gallery of unique gifts](https://cppfarmstore.wixsite.com/website/gift-gallery)that can be included in Custom Gift Boxes and Baskets.*For shipping prices and availability, please contact the store.**

![California mandarin oranges](img/satsuma.jpg)

## Satsuma Gift Pack

This gift pack is loaded with sweet, easy to peel, Cal Poly Pomona mandarins.

![](img/avocadomandarin.jpg)

## Avocado Mandarin Gift Pack

Includes an assortment of Cal Poly Pomona grown avocados and mandarin oranges.

![gift basket with wine and other goodies](img/gift-basket.jpg)

## Custom Gift Basket

Choose from anything in the store to make a unique and personal gift. We'll put your items together in a beautifully wrapped basket.

![gift box containing crackers, cheeses and meats](img/gift-box.jpg)

## Custom Gift Box

These make great, easy to transport, gifts! Boxes can be filled with your choice of items sold in the Farm Store. They can be shipped too! Contact us for pricing.
