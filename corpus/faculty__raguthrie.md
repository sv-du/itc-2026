## Bio

Ruth Guthrie is a Professor of Information Systems at California Polytechnic University, Pomona. She has a background in software test and program management of large scale software programs in the aerospace industry. She is a former Associate Dean, and Director of Graduate Programs, and Her research interests are in educational technology, user interface design, and women in technology careers. She has authored several papers in a variety of areas including two books on Web development.

At Cal Poly, she is a former NSF Advance Scholar for faculty professional development. Recently, she published Support Structures for Women in Information Technology Careers, in the International Journal of e-Politics. She was also a representative for the California OER Council, providing free textbooks to Community, Cal State and University of California systems.
