# Bronco Family Space

As the heart of the campus community and a place on inspiration, discovery, creativity and collaboration, the University Library is excited to introduce our Bronco Family Space, located on the 3rd floor of the Library. The Bronco Family Space is designed for students, faculty, and staff who need to use library services while caring for a younger family member. The Library is proud to support parenting students, and all family caregivers who need a family-friendly campus space. The overall goal of our Bronco Family Space is to provide a family friendly environment and space for the families in our campus community.

![Bronco Familiy Space](img/family-space-logo-l.png)

Our specially designed space features interactive materials for children and caregivers including board and picture books, age-appropriate toys, puzzles, dramatic play items, and blocks providing a rich space for children to explore. In addition, changing tables are available in the men's and women's restrooms next to the Bronco Family Space.

### Children's and Young Adult Collection

Our ever-growing [Children's and Young Adult Collection](https://libguides.library.cpp.edu/children) provides diversity and representation in Children's Literature. Children need to be able to see themselves in our children's and young adult collection and we are working hard to build a collection that highlights the identity of different communities. We are developing a collection with a wide range of children's literature from board books to a young adult collection. We strive to make our collection appeal to all age groups as well as a collection that meets the needs of evolving curriculum in K-12 programs.

### Things You Can Borrow

**Children's iPads**

The University Library now has 3 Children's iPads available for checkout for the Bronco Family Space. The iPads have been configured for children use and include children's apps. Children's iPads may be checked out for a 4-hour loan at 2nd floor Circulation Desk in the University Library (be sure to ask for the Bronco Family Space iPad).

Children's iPads are available for student, faculty and staff to check out on a first come, first served basis. For any assistance, please contact the Circulation Desk, email libcirc@cpp.edu or call (909) 869-3075.

Requests/reservations are not permitted for these devices.

Check out the Library's [Tech Lending Program](https://www.cpp.edu/library/access-services/tech-lending-program.shtml) for information on additional devices.

**Story Time Kits**

**The University Library has [story time kits](https://libguides.library.cpp.edu/children/storytimekits) to check out for course assignments or personal use. The kits are curated, themed collection of educational material designed to foster early literacy and engagement in children. Explore the different themed bags like dinosaurs, music, alphabet, colors, and more.**
