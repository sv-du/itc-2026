# Academic Advising

Chemistry majors (all options) have the option to seek academic advising from a Chemistry faculty member each semester during the designated advising period. Advising for Summer and Fall terms occurs in Spring semester; advising for Spring semester occurs in Fall semester. (The advising period begins approximately two weeks before registration; see Schedule of Classes for exact dates.) Follow the procedure below to schedule an advising appointment with a faculty member:

![Dr. Walton and a student in the lab](img/walton_guzman.jpg)

* Log in to CPP Connect and click on the blue ‘Make an Appointment/View Drop-In Availability’ button on the right-hand side of the page to access the scheduling page.
* Choose ‘**Advising**’ for the type of appointment and ‘**General Advising**’ for the reason to schedule the appointment. You will be best able to further filter your results for finding a Chemistry faculty member for registration advising by choosing ‘**Chemistry Department Advising**’ and setting the meeting type to ‘**Virtual Meeting**’.
* You can also select a desired date for your registration advising appointment.
* Choose a Chemistry faculty member who has an available appointment. Double check time, location, etc. and then confirm the appointment. Please note any pertinent information, such as a Zoom link, time, or request to complete the pre-registration worksheet.

Appointments can book quickly and are first-come, first-served. For video walk-throughs on using CPP Connect, Planner, and Scheduler, please refer to [Student Success Central](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/cppconnect/index.shtml).
