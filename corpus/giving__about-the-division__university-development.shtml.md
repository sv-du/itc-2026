# University Development

## About Us

By giving to Cal Poly Pomona, you strengthen our mission to help anyone who dreams of success achieve it. Students count on supporters like you for mentorship, encouragement and financial support, just as you relied on those who came before you.

Our development officers are here to help you find meaningful and personal ways to engage with students, expand learning opportunities, propel faculty research, enrich community programs, build state-of-the-art facilities or so much more. They are also ready to assist you in finding ways to create an impact on issues that matter to you. (And don’t forget that giving can also provide great tax-savings opportunities.)

Your generosity keeps a high-quality education affordable and within reach.
