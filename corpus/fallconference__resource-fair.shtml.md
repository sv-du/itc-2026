## Resource Fair & Club Fair Info

The Fall Conference Resource Fair & Club Fair is open to all faculty, staff and students. Learn how you can get involved on campus, connect with other Broncos and utilize support services to help you. In addition, come out and meet Interim President Dr. Iris S. Levine, who will introduce the winners of the One Team Award.

 The outdoor event will feature 150+ departments, clubs and orgs in the University Quad.

 **Resource Fair & Club Fair**Open to all faculty, staff and students
9 to 11 a.m. | University Quad
