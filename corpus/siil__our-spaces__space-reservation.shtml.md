## Use our Spaces

If you're looking for a place to hold a meeting or host an event, then SIIL's got you covered. To reserve one of our three spaces, go ahead and fill out the room reservation through 25 Live, and we'll get back to you within a few days!

[Reserve Our Space Today!](https://25live.collegenet.com/pro/cpp%20)

![Maker Studio Card Layouts](../img/spaces/makerstudio4.jpg)

## FAQs

### What are the Location Names for the spaces?

Below are the room names (the Location ID for the search function)

Maker Studio: 015 2000A
iLab: 001 0113
Innovation Orchard (Meeting Room): 999 1008A

### Other Questions?

Contact us via email at siil@cpp.edu.
