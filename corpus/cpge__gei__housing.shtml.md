# Housing

![kw conference center](img/560.gei-kw.jpg)

## Kellogg West Conference Center and Hotel

Over 80 newly renovated guest rooms and suites of Kellogg West possess a warm contemporary charm. Every room has a patio or balcony with views of the picturesque, woodland surroundings.

Kellogg West Hotel Amenities:

* Hot Breakfast Buffet
* High speed Wireless Internet Access
* Daily newspaper
* Fitness Room access
* Pool and Spa access
* In-Room refrigerators
* Flat screen HD TV
* Coffee/tea maker
* Spacious and well-lit work desk
* Parking

![dorms](img/560.gei-dorm.jpg)

## University Dormitory

The Residence Halls are conveniently located on-campus and are within easy walking distance to the academic buildings, library, computer labs, dining areas, the Bronco Student Center and parking lots.

Dorm Facilities

* Laundry room
* Study areas
* Recreation room/TV lounge
* Mailboxes and community kitchenettes
* Satellite television service and high-speed Internet connections

Dorm Features

* Particular theme living community
* Co-ed and non-smoking
* Student Resident Advisors (RA)
* Professional live-in Hall Coordinator

![village](img/560.gei-village.jpg)

## University Village

University Village housing is apartment style rather than dormitory style and is meant as transitional housing for the mature college student. The property is well maintained and provides guests with plenty of recreational areas, smoking locations, and outdoor scenery.

University Village Facilities

* Gated community
* Two or four bedroom floor plans with two bathrooms
* Central air
* Full kitchens
* Fully furnished rooms

University Village Services

* Vending and Laundry
* Telephone - Internet - Cable Television
* Parking and Shuttle Service
* Community Center
* Recreation Center
* Conference Centers
* 24 Hour Professional Staffing
