## About the Program

Cultivate highly sought-after analysis and evaluation skills and become a key asset to any business. The Applied Economics Option provides you with practical knowledge that prepares you for success in the business and government sectors.
Through hands-on learning and problem-solving, you will explore how economics shapes industries and policies. Whether you aim to enter the workforce or pursue a master's degree in business or related fields, you'll gain a strong foundation in real-world applications of economics, including money and banking, government regulation of the private sector and economic forecasting.

## What is the Difference?

### Applied vs Quantitative Economics

The Applied Economics option focuses largely on tangible business applications. It will provide you with an ideal foundation for employment in business or government sectors. The Quantitative Economics option focuses more on the theoretical and technical aspects of the field- preparing you for graduate work in economics, and other technically challenging fields such as finance, actuary science, risk analysis and applied statistics.
