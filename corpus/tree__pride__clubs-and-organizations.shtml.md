# Clubs and Organizations

## Clubs and Organizations

#### **CPP Pride Club**

The Cal Poly Pomona Pride Club is a student-led organization dedicated to creating a safe, inclusive, and supportive environment for LGBTQ+ students and allies at California State Polytechnic University, Pomona

![Instagram Logo](https://www.cpp.edu/common/resources/img/social/instagram-60x60.png) [Follow Us On Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/cppprideclub)        [Register on myBAR](https://calpolypomona.campuslabs.com/engage/organization/cppprideclub)

####

#### **Black Queer Collective**

The Black Queer Collective at Cal Poly Pomona is dedicated to creating a welcoming and safe space where Black queer students can fully express and embrace their authentic selves.

![Instagram Logo](https://www.cpp.edu/common/resources/img/social/instagram-60x60.png) [Follow Us On Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/cpp_bqc)        [Register on myBAR](https://calpolypomona.campuslabs.com/engage/organization/blackqueercollective)

####

#### **oSTEM**

oSTEM is an organization for queer students and allies with the STEM fields (science, technology, engineering, math). They strive to educate, empower, and engage in a diverse community to identify, address, and advocate for the needs of LGBTQIA+ students in the STEM fields. They fulfill these needs through mentorship connections, networking opportunities, strategic collaborations, and professional leadership development. For more information, please contact them.

![Instagram Logo](https://www.cpp.edu/common/resources/img/social/instagram-60x60.png) [Follow Us On Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/cppostem/)      [Register on myBAR](https://calpolypomona.campuslabs.com/engage/organization/qsafe)

**QiB**

Queers in Business (QiB) is Cal Poly Pomona’s College of Business Administration's LGBTQIA+++ professional business association. Our goal is to cultivate a queer-positive business culture by bridging the gap between the queer business community and those in business outside the LGBTQIA+++ community. We do this by providing members with tools to foster inclusivity, diversity, and a strong sense of community within the business setting.

![Instagram Logo](https://www.cpp.edu/common/resources/img/social/instagram-60x60.png) [Follow Us On Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/qib_cpp/)
