# Latin Alumni Network

![group photo](../img/lan-aboutpic.jpg)

## Mission Statement

The four main objectives of the Latin Alumni Network chapter are improving professional development through information and social change; providing financial and moral support to students; providing information on available scholarship programs and employment opportunities; and being a viable resource to Cal Poly Pomona.
