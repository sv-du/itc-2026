# Dates and Deadlines

## Overview

Staying on top of important dates ensures a smooth semester. Here’s everything you need to know about registration, payment deadlines and academic dates at Cal Poly Pomona. Make sure to mark these dates on your calendar to avoid late fees or enrollment issues.

## 2025-2026

Cal Poly Pomona has established key dates and deadlines for each academic term, including important enrollment-related deadlines. As a student, it is your responsibility to review and adhere to these deadlines to ensure a smooth enrollment experience.
