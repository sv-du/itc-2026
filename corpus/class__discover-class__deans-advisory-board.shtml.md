# Dean's Circle

The College of Letters, Arts, and Social Sciences (CLASS) Dean’s Circle is established to develop a mutually beneficial relationship between Cal Poly Pomona (CPP) CLASS and community constituents, emeriti, and alumni representing the humanities, social sciences and the performing arts. The purpose of the Dean’s Circle is to cultivate, advise, inspire, advocate, and collaborate for the betterment of the College and the enhancement of student education.
