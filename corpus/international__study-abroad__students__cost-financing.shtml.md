# Cost and Financing

Study abroad can be affordable, but you have to do your research to make sure the program you choose fits you academically and financially. Start by planning far in advance (we suggest a year). The cost of programs varies depending on location, length, timing, credits, and accommodations.

CPP Study Abroad is dedicated to making international educational programs accessible to students. We provide unique educational experiences by offering classes in various locations around the world. Students are immersed in a new culture and gain insight into that culture in settings outside of a classroom. International experiences and understanding of other people and cultures are highly valuable qualities in today’s diverse environment.

CPP Study Abroad and the [Office of Financial Aid & Scholarships](https://www.cpp.edu/financial-aid/index.shtml) are committed to getting you to your destination and adventure as a study abroad student. We recommend you take care of your finances before you go in order to have a smooth and unforgettable experience. We also strongly recommend applying for [CPP scholarships](https://www.cpp.edu/financial-aid/scholarships/) to help support your journey.
