# Advising

![zero gravity test in vomit comet ](/engineering/aro/img/banner002.jpeg)

### Academic Advising

Embarking on your aerospace education at Cal Poly Pomona is an exciting journey, and our advising system is here to ensure you make the most of every step. Here's what you need to know about our advising resources:

##### Your Advisors:

Each aerospace engineering student is matched with a faculty advisor and a staff advisor based on the first letter of their last name. These advisors are your partners throughout your academic voyage, offering insights, suggestions, and practical guidance. While advising is not mandatory, we encourage you to meet with your advisors at least once each semester. Their expertise can illuminate your path, helping you align your courses and goals effectively.

##### Faculty Advisor:

For questions about technical core courses and elective options, turn to your faculty advisor. They possess the in-depth knowledge to guide you through the specifics of your academic journey. You can find your faculty advisor and their office hours on the side menu on this page.

##### Staff Advisor and Advising Centers:

Navigating the general education requirements and lower-division courses is made easier with the support of the Staff Advisor from the Engineering Advising Center and the Bronco Advising Center. These centers offer a wealth of resources to assist you in crafting a balanced academic plan.

##### Other Resources:

Our advising resources are designed to empower you with the knowledge and guidance needed to thrive in the aerospace engineering program at Cal Poly Pomona. By engaging with your advisors and utilizing the information provided here, you're on a path to make the most of your academic journey. Here are some additional resources to consider. Note that some of these resources changes depending on your curriculum year (the academic year you entered the program). Choose the curriculum year that applies to you when you use advising resources.
