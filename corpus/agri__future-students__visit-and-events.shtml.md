# Discover More About the Huntley College

## How to Visit or Contact Us

![A family gets info at a college booth at open house.](../img/2025-broncobound-open-house-920x570.jpg)

Are you a high school or community college student? Do you want to find out more about the Huntley College of Agriculture and its programs?

Are you a high school counselor or a community college advisor? Do you want information about the Huntley College for your students?

You can learn more through tours, advising, school visits, and special events put on by the college's Recruitment and Retention Office. All are offered in person or virtually.

Here is more information about services and resources you can access:

## Visit the Huntley College

[### College Tours

The college offers personalized tours for prospective students and their families. To schedule a college tour, use this link to fill out and submit the Prospective Student Tour Request Form.](https://forms.office.com/Pages/ResponsePage.aspx?id=HqZLFuw5XU-J_6ofAKUhtJhC0tuMzr9BoAx0r2mRClpUNEY0U0RGUjZUMlhCVzhTVDBCNjBXOENXUSQlQCN0PWcu "opens a new window")

[### Group Tours

The college also offers college tours to high school and community college student groups. You'll get to see the Cal Poly Pomona Farm Store, livestock units, classrooms, and laboratories. Use this link to request a group tour.](https://forms.office.com/Pages/ResponsePage.aspx?id=HqZLFuw5XU-J_6ofAKUhtJhC0tuMzr9BoAx0r2mRClpUOExFRVNBWVhFQk1HRFE4SlJJV1dBVlRPMCQlQCN0PWcu "opens a new window")

### One-on-One Sessions

Chat with our recruitment coordinator and student ambassador about our majors and answer specific questions you have about our programs. Use this link to schedule an appointment with Recruitment Coordinator Sharon Ellis.

### Contact Us

The Recruitment & Retention Office is located in Building 2, Room 114. Office hours are Monday through Friday, 8 a.m. to 5 p.m., year-round (except holidays). The office is closed for lunch from noon to 1 p.m. Staff can be reached at (909) 869-2869 or at agriculture@cpp.edu.

## Visit Cal Poly Pomona

[### University Campus Tours

The university also offers general tours of the Cal Poly Pomona campus. Use this link to schedule a general campus tour.](/outreach/tours.shtml "opens a new window")

[### Explore CPP Day

Held in the fall, Explore CPP Day is a campus-wide event for prospective students to visit the Cal Poly Pomona campus, meet with current students and faculty, and learn more about academic programs, admissions, applying, and financial aid. Learn more about Explore CPP Day at this link.](/explorecpp/index.shtml "opens a new window")

[### BroncoBound Open House

This event, which is held in the spring, is intended for high school and community college students who have been admitted to Cal Poly Pomona for the upcoming fall semester.
You can meet with current students and faculty, and tour student housing on campus. Learn more about BroncoBound Open House at this link.](/openhouse/ "opens a new window")

[### Lodging

Traveling from out of town? Need accommodations? Stay at the Kellogg West Conference Center & Hotel on campus. To make a reservation, call (909) 869-2222 or use this link.](https://www.expedia.com/Los-Angeles-Hotels-Kellogg-West-Conference-Center-And-Hotel.h93162172.Hotel-Information "opens a new window")

## For High School & Community College Counselors

### Huntley College Counselor Day

Are you a high school or community college counselor? If so, then you are invited to attend the Huntley College Counselor Day at Cal Poly Pomona.
Counselor Day, which is held in the fall, is a great opportunity to learn about our programs, explore our facilities, and discover opportunities that await your students.
Keep checking back here. We'll have more information when we have set a date and begin accepting registrations.

[### College Fair Visits or Group Sessions

Our Huntley College Ambassadors are available to visit your high school or community college. They can conduct classroom presentations and participate in college fairs. To request an ambassador to visit your school or college, fill out the form in this link.](https://forms.office.com/Pages/ResponsePage.aspx?id=HqZLFuw5XU-J_6ofAKUhtJhC0tuMzr9BoAx0r2mRClpUOFpaTUpSVlZKVVJOQVpNQTNFUVNOUEtEWiQlQCN0PWcu "opens a new window")
