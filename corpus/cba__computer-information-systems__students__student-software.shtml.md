## Available Software for Students

**Microsoft downloads using your Cal Poly Pomona user name and password**:  **[Microsoft Azure- DevTools](https://azureforeducation.microsoft.com/devtools "Original URL: https://azureforeducation.microsoft.com/devtools. Click or tap if you trust this link.")**

Through the Microsoft Academic Alliance program, CIS students can download the most current versions of Microsoft's platform, server and developer tools. Requests for physical media (e.g., DVDs) involve a nominal cost.

**Office 2019 and Access 2019**

If you are on **PC** or you are on **Mac** but already have **Windows OS** (8.1 or 10) installed, to download and install Office 2019 and Access 2019 :

* Go to [Cal Poly Pomona's homepage](https://www.cpp.edu/),
* Click on the link "Email" at the bottom of the page.
* Log into Outlook email with your Cal Poly Pomona user name and password.
* Click on the button "Office 365" on the top left corner.
* You will be redirected to [MS Office](https://office.com) and remain logged in.
* Then click the button "Install Office" to download the software, which contains Word, Excel and PowerPoint as well as Access.

If you are on **Mac** and **don't have Windows** **Operating System** installed, you can still install Office 2016 including Word, Excel and PowerPoint on your Mac. You can also get Access installed but you first need to install Windows OS (8.1 or 10, 10 is better) on your Mac via either Bootcamp (come with your Mac so it's easier but you just need to reboot into it) or a virtual machine like VMware:

* To get Windows 10 you can go to  [this Microsoft Azure for Education website](https://azureforeducation.microsoft.com/devtools),
* Then log in with your Cal Poly Pomona user name and password.
* Once logged in, under Education - Software, then look up "Windows 10".
* To get VMware (optional) you can go to CIS department's "Current Students" then "Labs and Software" page.
* Click on the link "California State Polytechnic University - Computer Information Systems - Microsoft Imagine", you will be redirected to [OnTheHub.com](https://onthehub.com/)
* Then, log in with your Cal Poly Pomona user name and password.
* Look up "VMware Fusion".
* Click "Add to cart" and follow instructions to download.
