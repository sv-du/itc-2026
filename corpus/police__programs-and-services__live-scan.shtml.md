# Live Scan

**\*\*\* NOTIFICATION TO CUSTOMERS\*\*\***

**Effective Jan 1, 2025 the CPP Livescan Office may only accept Livescan Applications for those that are working or volunteering for programs involving unaccompanied minors. Please contact livescan@cpp.edu if you need help determining if your program qualifies.**

**Live Scan Information**Cal Poly Pomona University Police Department provides Live Scan fingerprinting services only for on-campus entities at the moment.

Live Scan technology allows digitally scanned fingerprints and related information to be submitted electronically to the Department of Justice within a matter of minutes. Please allow at least 30 minutes for processing.

You can learn more about Live Scan fingerprinting on the California Department of Justice website: [https://oag.ca.gov/fingerprints.](https://nam03.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Foag.ca.gov%2Ffingerprints&data=02%7C01%7Cmmquiroz%40cpp.edu%7C075c7e364d9e47bf1fc208d71c282df3%7C164ba61e39ec4f5d89ffaa1f00a521b4%7C0%7C0%7C637008831201927807&sdata=0l5SElvhL9HEIYRy4IUaTjSJPBZzAnlOuRATxoxQe%2F8%3D&reserved=0)

***For General public and off-campus groups,******please visit the [CA DOJ LIVE SCAN SEARCH](https://oag.ca.gov/fingerprints/locations) to look for a Live Scan provider nearby.***

## Hours and Operations

Live Scan Services are open Mon - Fri from 9 am to 4 pm by appointment only.

## Location and Contact Information

**Address:** 109 Cypress Ln. Pomona, CA 91768

**Phone Number:** (909) 869-6738

The Police Department and Parking Service building can be found at the corner of Oak Lane and Cypress Lane. 15 minute parking stalls are available at the front of the building.

## Essentials For Your Live Scan Appointment

### Identification

All Live Scan participants must be able to provide one of the following forms of identification:

* California (or any other state's) driver's license
* Passport

*Please note that copies or temporary licenses are not accepted.*

### Live Scan Form

Prior to your appointment, you must completely fill out a Live Scan Service Form (BCII 8016 Form) available [here](documents/bcia-8016-request-for-livescan.pdf). (PDF)

Please note that Live Scan forms are unavailable in our office. Also, we cannot complete your Live Scan without your employer's Originating Agency Identification (ORI) number and information.

### Forms of Payment

Live Scan accepts applications from CPP department applicants only. Departments are billed on a regular basis via CFS#.

**Fees** The California Department of Justice (DOJ) and/or Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI) charges an applicant criminal history clearance fee for background checks. Applicants are typically required to pay unless a billing account number is provided by their agency. Fees are subject to change without notice. For fee information, access the following file: [Fees](https://www.cpp.edu/police/documents/fees.pdf) (PDF)

If you have questions about the level of service required, please contact the agency that is requesting the live scan.

Cal Poly Pomona University Police Dept. Rolling Service Fee:
 $20.00 Cal Poly Pomona Students/Employees

**Rejection Notice**

If you had your Live Scan done with our agency, contact us so that we can re-take your fingerprints. Bring the rejection notice and the new, completed Live Scan Request Form. THERE WILL BE NO ADDITIONAL FEE FOR THIS RE-SUBMISSION.

**Rolled Ink Fingerprints** Fingerprinting on card for FBI is not available.
