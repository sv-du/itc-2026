## About Our Mentorship Program

In the I Am First mentoring program, CPP first-generation college students are matched with a faculty or staff member who also identifies as a first-generation college graduate. The shared experiences of being first-generation foster a sense of belonging and create a pathway to success for CPP students.

![Sara Garcia (left), a nutrition student, and Ariana Estrada (right), a scholarship coordinator with Financial Aid and Scholarships, were matched through the I AM FIRST mentorship program in 2018.](img/i-am-first3.jpg)

## Mentors Lead and Mentees Succeed in the I Am First Program

[### I Am First Mentor Handbook](i-am-first-mentor-handbook.shtml "opens a new window")

### The Mentor's Impact

Mentors help students transition into Cal Poly Pomona while guiding them in academic and career planning. They make a meaningful impact by connecting students to the university and shaping future leaders through support and encouragement.

### Mentee Benefits

Mentees gain one-on-one mentoring, building lasting relationships with faculty and staff. They receive specialized support and resources to help them succeed academically and personally.
