## Madeline Myers

**Position:** Customer Insights Analyst | Research Assistant

**Major:** Marketing Management

**Interests:** Music, Gaming, Art, Reading, Psychology

**Skills:** Microsoft Office, Qualtrics, Writing

**Short Bio:** Madeline is an undergraduate Marketing major entering her 2nd year at Cal Poly. She is part of the Regulatory Foci Tourism research group, exploring the cognitive processes of consumers and their relation to the tourism industry. She joined the Center in Spring of 2023 to further her research capabilities, aspiring to become a professor in the future. Besides market research, Madeline is interested in both music and psychology.
