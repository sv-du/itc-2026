# Project CAMINOS

![group of students walking by the CPP mural and a pair of prospective students with their parent](img/home-header.png)

## Welcome Future Broncos!

Project CAMINOS, which stands for **C**ultivating **A**ccess and **M**entoring through **I**nstitutional **N**etworks and **O**pportunities for **S**uccess, is dedicated to cultivating a college going culture amongst students interested in applying to Cal Poly Pomona. We help students, and their families, make attending college a reality.
