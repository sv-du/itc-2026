# B2D Symposium

#### B2D/CARS STUDENT SUMMER SYMPOSIUM, 2024

**Cohort 3 & 4 Presenters**

**![symposium2024](img/symposium2024.jpg)**

**Anthony Temm** (Cohort 4)
The role of reactive oxygen species (ROS) in controlling acute *Toxoplasma*
*gondii* infection

**Marco Martinez** (Cohort 4)
Investigating the role of the innate immune system response through the
release of Neutrophil Extracellular Traps (NETs) to entrap and neutralize
*Toxoplasma gondii*

**Nadeem Halasah** (Cohort 4)
DEC-205+ Dendritic Cells Mitigate Viral Replication and Neuropathology
During West Nile Virus Infection

**Sarah Ramirez** (Cohort 4)
Understanding Floral Organ Identity through the Lens of Ranunulales, an earlyDiverging Eudicot Order

**Ximena Corona**
Immunological Interactions with Trichomonads: The Impact of Neutrophil
Degranulation and Macrophage Efferocytosis

**Isabel Romero** (Cohort 3)
Investigating IFN-g mediated autophagy in astrocytes infected with virulent
and avirulent strains of *Toxoplasma gondii*

**Arnel Ibarra**(Cohort 3)
Developing a Novel Triple-Transgenic Reporter Mouse Model to Investigate
the Modulation of the Intestinal Epithelial Barrier by the IBD-associated Gene
PTPN2

**Addyson Hebbert** (Cohort 3)
Role of RNA Helicase During Influenza A Infection

**Ana Alcarez-Echeveste** (Cohort 3, not pictured)
Assessing Climate resilience in *Arctostaphylos glauca*: A Leaf Functional
Traits Perspective in California’s Changing Environment

#### B2D/CARS STUDENT SUMMER SYMPOSIUM, 2023

**Cohort 2 & 3 Presenters**

#### ![CARS 2023 cohort 2 and 3](img/carsall.jpg)

**Isabel Romero** (Cohort 3)
Utilizing blocking antibodies to determine which receptors impact the success
of neutrophil-like cells’ killing of Trichomonas vaginalis
Summer Research PI: Dr. Frances Mercer (CPP)

**Ana Alcarez-Echeveste** (Cohort 3)
Understanding developmental and molecular changes underlying flower
development and floral organ abscission in Delphinium
Summer Research PI: Dr. Bharti Sharma (CPP)

**Arnel Ibarra** (Cohort 3)
Understanding the mechanism of Daple-PDGFRB Gene Fusion in Leukemia
Summer Research PI: Dr. Jason Ear (CPP)

**Sydnie Chase** (Cohort 2)
Comparative analysis of kinetic dynamics between yeast and human eIF4F on
a cross-species mRNA set
Summer Research PI: Dr. Sean O’Leary (UCR)

**Diego Henriquez** (Cohort 2)
Loss of PTPN2 may result in weakened intestinal mucosal barrier and
facilitate E. coli invasion in mice
Summer Research PI: Dr. Declan McCole (UCR)

**Keven Kwok** (Cohort 2)
Alternaria Induced Lung Inflammation triggers changes in the neural
respiratory circuit that are dependent on vagal nerve communication between
lung and brain.
Summer Research PI: Dr. Monica Carson (UCR)

**Addyson Hebbert** (Cohort 3)
Investigating the mechanism of pyramid formation by the C92 protein
Summer Research PI: Dr. Jamie Snyder (CPP)

**Joseph Alas** (Cohort 3)
Studying the Effects of Genistein on the Saccharomyces cerevisiae Cell Cycle
Summer Research PI: Dr. Karine Le Roch (UCR)
