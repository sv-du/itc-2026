# New Metrolink Connect Shuttle Service from Pomona North station available January 21, 2020!

A new hourly round-trip shuttle service will be available on January 21, 2020 for San Bernardino Line riders arriving at the Pomona North station allowing riders to have more options to commute to Cal Poly Pomona.

As part of this schedule change, Cal Poly Pomona has also changed the stop and pick-up location to a more centrally location to connect to on-campus shuttles.  The stop will be located the Student Services Building at the traffic circle where Bronco Express shuttles route D and E stop.

A limited number of complimentary round-trip tickets will be available for students, faculty and staff to ride the Metrolink.  Please contact the Transportation Rideshare office at rideshare@cpp.edu or by phone at 909-869-4781.  Hurry, because these tickets will expire March 1, 2020.

*See the new schedule timetable effective January 21, 2020* *[here](2020metrolinkshuttle.pdf)*
