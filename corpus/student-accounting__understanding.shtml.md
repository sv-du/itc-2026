# Understanding Your Account

## Overview

You can check your student account by logging into the [MyCPP student portal](https://my.cpp.edu/ "MyCPP student portal"). It provides a streamlined dashboard with single sign-on access to BroncoDirect. Your account is organized into several sections, making it easy to track charges, payments, and financial aid activity. Each section gives you a different view of your finances, helping you stay informed throughout the term.

## What’s in Your Student Account?

### Account Summary

View a snapshot of your current charges, payments, pending financial aid, and your total balance due.

### Account Activity

See a detailed list of all transactions, including tuition charges, payments, and refunds. These details support the totals shown in your Account Summary.

### Charges Due

Displays all outstanding charges and deposits, organized by due date.

### Payments

Lists all payments made, including financial aid disbursements and scholarships.

### Pending Financial Aid

Shows financial aid that’s been awarded but not yet disbursed. You can view by term or across multiple terms.

## Payment

### Deadlines & Holds

Payments must be received by their due dates to avoid a hold being placed on your account. Holds prevent registration and are only removed once your fees are paid in full.
Accounts with unpaid balances for 90 days or more may be referred to a collection agency and may incur additional penalties.
