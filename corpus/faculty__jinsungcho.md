* **B.S.** Civil Engineering, Department of Civil Engineering, College of Engineering, Hongik University, Seoul, Korea

* Construction Scheduling (Undergraduate)
* Construction Project Management and Accounting (Undergraduate)

* Virtual Design Construction and Management; BIM (Undergraduate)
* Virtual Design Construction and Management (BIM) Laboratory (Undergraduate)

* Underground Construction and Trenchless Technology (Graduate)
* Temporary Construction Structure (Undergraduate and Graduate)
