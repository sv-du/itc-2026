# Male Success Initiatives

![Students on the way to an event](img/bus.jpg)

## Our Mission

Male Success Initiatives (MSI) at Cal Poly Pomona is dedicated to fostering the academic, personal, and professional success of all students. Through holistic and culturally responsive programming, collaborative cross-campus networks and innovative services, MSI seeks to enhance the retention and graduation rates of men of color at CPP.

Programming and services are available to all students, regardless of how they identify, providing opportunities to explore, learn, connect and build meaningful relationships. Celebrating diversity in all its forms, MSI remains steadfast in its commitment to creating a respectful and inclusive environment where everyone feels valued and supported.

**Our Vision**

MSI scholars will be **prepared**, **connected**and **empowered** to pursue academic success, personal well-being and career readiness at Cal Poly Pomona.

* Prepare: MSI prepares its scholars for the rigors of collegiate life. Through cohort programing, we prepare incoming students in persisting through their first year and beyond.
* Connect: MSI’s collaborative ethos and interdepartmental relationships provide scholars with valuable campus resources and supportive faculty/staff networks.
* Empower: By executing its mission and vision, MSI will develop scholars who are empowered in creating their own realities, fostering a sense of community, and achieving their academic goals.

## Values and Standards

**EXCELLENCE**

* Excellence is striving to do more and be great even if that means making errors along the way.
* Standards of Excellence: We hold all staff, students, faculty, administrators and full- time, to a high standard. Professional staff will discuss an individual’s ability to achieve excellence on a regular basis (1:1s, in- service, staff meetings).

**COMMITMENT**

* Commitment is a pledge to give your time and energy to something or someone you believe in.
* Standards of Commitment: Being present, focused on departmental goals, and doing good work. All staff will put in maximum effort to achieve the tasks at hand.

**COLLABORATION**

* Collaboration helps employees work together to achieve a common goal in ways that benefit a company and its employees.
* Standards of Collaboration: Staff will work with all divisional and external partners to achieve teamwork orientated success. A common goal trumps individual desire.

**SERVICE**

* Service is the occupation or function of serving; contributing to the welfare of others; a helpful act. Simply stated, we put others above ourselves.
* Standards of Service: At MSI, We>Me. We strive to provide support to our department, our colleagues, and the division in all aspects of our shared goals.

**GROWTH**

* Growth is having the drive to continuously improve both oneself and the department. There are no limitations to our success.
* Standards of Growth: All staff will seek campus opportunities that will challenge and hone their professional skills. Student staff will engage in coaching and mentoring opportunities to discuss and strategize growth opportunities.
