# AB 481 Regulated Equipment and Tools Reporting

**AB 481 Annual Report**

Click the link below to read the 2025 AB 481 Annual Report, which provides information regarding the Regulated Equipment and Tools used by the CSU police departments.

To further our transparency and communication efforts, CPP PD will host a community engagement event to discuss information contained within the report.  The event will take place on October 24, 2025, at 3pm in the CPP PD conference room (room 224) located at 3801 West Temple Avenue, Building 109, Pomona, CA  91768.
