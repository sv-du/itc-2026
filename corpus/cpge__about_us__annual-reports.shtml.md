## CPGE Annual Reports

### Advancing Lifelong Learning Through Impact and Innovation

Each year, the College of Professional and Global Education (CPGE) at Cal Poly Pomona reflects on its achievements, partnerships, and progress through its annual report. These reports highlight how CPGE continues to empower learners, strengthen workforce pathways, and expand access to quality education across industries and communities.
