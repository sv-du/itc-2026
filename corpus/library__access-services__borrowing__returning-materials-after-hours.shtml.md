# RETURNING MATERIALS & AFTER HOURS RETURNS

### Returning Library Materials

Library materials may be returned to the University Library, Circulation Desk during the [hours](https://cpp.libcal.com/hours/) the library is open. In addition, library materials (excluding Document Delivery/ILL, CSU+, Course Reserves, and Tech Lending devices) may be returned to the University Library at the 1st floor, Self-Check In machines (located in the 1st floor lobby or outside the entrance on the South wall). **Please return Tech Lending devices at the Circulation Desk only**.

### Returning Library Materials After Hours

As a courtesy, during the hours that the library is closed, there are 2 bookdrops on campus where you may return library materials.

The first outside bookdrop is located in front of the student services building. To drive up to the bookdrop, drive up Red Gum Lane towards the parking structure , turn left into the one-way street and stay in the left-most lane. It will be on the driver's side.

![](../img/return-bin-map.png)

![](../img/book-drop.jpg)

A second outdoor bookdrop is located outside of the Bronco Bookstore near the 24/7 Bronco Lockers, facing parking Lot 'G.'

![](https://www.cpp.edu/lockers/img/lockers-pin-point-map.png)

The library will pick up books periodically throughout the week for check-in. Any items deposited after the pickup will be checked in the following day. **It is the borrower's responsibility to return materials on time**. For immediate check-in, please return at the Circulation Desk during our [hours of operation](https://cpp.libcal.com/hours/).

### Sending your materials in:

If you are out of town, there are still a couple of ways to return your materials:

* If you live near another CSU campus, you can return your items at their campus library. As a courtesy, you can return your books to any CSU campus regardless of where the books were checked out.
* You can mail your library items to:

  **Cal Poly Pomona
  University Library - Access Services
  3801 W Temple Ave
  Pomona, CA 91768**
