# PolyKroma 2016

## PolyKroma 2016

May 23, 2016 to Jun 9, 2016

**Location:** Kellogg University Art Gallery

Press the tab key to view the content. Use the down arrow key to move to the next tab and up arrow key to move to the previous.

[About the Exhibition](#tab-1)
[Program Schedule](#tab-2)
[Installation Views](#tab-3)

![Polykroma 2016](img/screenshot-39.png)The Kellogg and Huntley University Art Galleries welcome the public and the Cal Poly Pomona campus community, students and their families, faculty, staff and administrators to PolyKroma 2016, celebrating Fine Arts, Art History and Graphic Design on campus with its 2D3D Juried Art Exhibition at The Huntley Gallery, AND the Fine Art Senior Shows, Art History Papers Presentation and Industry Night - the senior Graphic Design exhibition - presented concurrently at the Kellogg University Art Gallery. These exhibitions feature the "best of" Cal Poly Pomona's Fine Arts, Art History and Graphic Design Seniors, and the Fine Arts and Graphic Design students of all levels.

Fine Art Seniors are given the opportunity to exhibit a series of their artwork created during their university experience, while exposed to a gallery setting, and gaining professional art world practices in installing, exhibiting and pricing their artwork in a "real world" environment. Industry Night isa special invitation to outside industry professionals to view graduating seniors' graphic design portfolios, projects, and motion graphics presentations with exposure to potential job opportunities being offered. Graduating Art History students are given the opportunity present their abstracts of their final thesis papers.

[![Photo of Reception](img/pk16_2.jpg)](../../news-and-events/2016-03-23-opening-reception-2d3d-juried-art-exhibition.shtml)

##### **Monday, May 23, 2016, 6:00 pm to 7:30 pm**

## [Opening Reception: 2D3D Juried Art Exhibition](../../news-and-events/2016-03-23-opening-reception-2d3d-juried-art-exhibition.shtml)

2D3D Juried Art Exhibition is an annual juried student exhibition, featuring art and graphic design by students at all academic levels. Jurors include experts in the fields of fine art and graphicdesign/illustration.

---

[![photo of polykroma reception](img/pk16.jpg)](../../news-and-events/2016-03-23-opening-reception-fine-art-senior-show.shtml)

##### **Monday, May 23, 2016 - 7:30pm to 9:00pm**

## [Opening Reception: Fine Art Senior Show](../../news-and-events/2016-03-23-opening-reception-fine-art-senior-show.shtml)

Opening Receptions for 2D3D Juried Art Exhibition, the Fine Art Senior Show, and Art History Papers Presentation is Monday, May 23 from 6-9pm. Fine Art/Art History Commemorations from 7:30-9pm at the Kellogg Gallery.

---

![Industry Night. Graphic Design Senior Show](img/12531169_808025889332102_826394214_n.jpg)

##### **Tuesday, May 24, 2016 - 6:00pm to 9:00pm**

## [Industry Night](../../news-and-events/2016-03-24-poly-kroma-2016-industry-night.shtml)

Students get an opportunity to showcase their best works in branding/logo creation, motion graphics and other graphic design portfolios to industry professionals.

---

[![2d3d Juried Art Exhibition](img/screenshot-40.png)](../../news-and-events/2016-05-22-2d3d-family-night.shtml)

##### **Thursday, June 9, 2016 - 4:00pm to 8:00pm**

## [2D3D Family Night](../../news-and-events/2016-05-22-2d3d-family-night.shtml)

A special night is devoted for the friends and family of the students featured in the 2D3D Juried Art Exhibition at the Gallery. It is shown simultaneously with the Senior Fine Arts, Art History and Graphic Design design exhibition at the Kellogg University Art Gallery.

## [Family Weekend: Fine Arts/Art History/Graphic Design Seniors](../../news-and-events/2016-05-22-2d3d-family-night.shtml)

A special night is devoted for the friends and family of the students featured in the 2D3D Juried Art Exhibition at the Gallery. It is shown simultaneously with the Senior Fine Arts, Art History and Graphic Design design exhibition at the Kellogg University Art Gallery.
