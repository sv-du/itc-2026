## Future-Proofing Airport Parking at

### Ontario International Airport (ONT)

Wednesday Oct 22, 2025; 9:30 AM – 4:00 PM,
Bronco Student Center Ursa Major

![Plane Flying from airport](../img/ceo-business-challenge/ontario-airport-plane1.jpg)

## About the CEO Business Challenge

All CPP students are invited to participate in the challenge, presented by the CEO of [Ontario International Airport](https://www.flyontario.com/). For this challenge, Future-Proofing Airport Parking at Ontario International Airport (ONT), you will step into the shoes of a CEO exploring opportunities for growth via strategic and operational excellence, innovation, and sustainability. Analyze the problem in teams of 3-5 students using real-world data and present actionable insights and solutions to a panel of industry and academic leaders!

## Why Participate?

* Build resume-ready skills in real-world problem solving, data analysis, teamwork, and professional presentations
* Network with industry executives
* Earn a digital badge to showcase your achievement [CEO Business Challenge 2025 Presenter](https://cpp.badgr.com/public/badges/VbEd1WujT8aXQDWWpe7mKw)
* Compete for cash prizes awarded to 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place winners [CEO Business Challenge 2025 Distinguished Achievement](https://cpp.badgr.com/public/badges/zQBX0f5ERpKmSz7EyG1SUQ)
