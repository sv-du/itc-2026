# Franco Lossio

![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white-mobile.svg)
![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white.svg)

## Food & Beverage

### Franco's Journey

One day while working at St. Regis in Aspen, a retired couple came from New York. They asked me to prepare them a kosher meal. I knew that I did not have a meal like this for them; however, when I approached the table and engaged with them, they were very understanding. We managed to come up with another solution. The next day for breakfast, I followed up with them and they were the sweetest couple I have encountered in my career. They had a great time in the hotel and I was happy to help and to conquer that.

![Franco Lossio](../img/franco-lossio.avif)

## In the Spotlight

### How did a Collins College degree help you in your career?

It helped me look at the hospitality industry from different angles. It helped me build a sense of professionalism from day one.

### What do you love about working in hospitality?

I am able to travel around and connect with people and provide amazing experiences.

### How would you convince someone that the field of hospitality management is for them?

I won't convince anyone to do that because hospitality is "a call" — either you have it or you don't. I hire people for their optimism and attitude. I can teach everything else.

### Share a fun fact about yourself.

I love camping!
