# Admitted Students

Congratulations! Your acceptance to Cal Poly Pomona and the College of Letters, Arts, and Social Sciences should be a source of pride to you and your supporters. As a Bronco, you are joining a vibrant community where there are opportunities to connect, learn, and become whatever you dream. We look forward to seeing you at one of our spring welcome events – and greeting you in the fall!
