## Parking Information

### Where do I park?

The closest parking we suggest is located in [STRUCTURE 1](https://goo.gl/maps/S2f4KKyRGiCBf7R46) and [STRUCTURE 2](https://goo.gl/maps/5cjcq99jhprzK4bs9) on the CPP Campus. Both lots are a bit of a walk to the theatre (plan 10-15 minutes), so please plan your arrival accordingly.

[Additional parking options and map can be found here.](https://www.cpp.edu/aboutcpp/visitor-information/parking.shtml)

### How far is parking from the theatre?

Both lots are a bit of a walk to the theatre (plan 10-15 minutes), so please plan your arrival accordingly.

[View a PDF with driving and walking directions here.](https://cpp.cascadecms.com/render/file.act?id=153a7b330a00005e2ab5620cceed6a30&type=file&forceDownload=false&rewrite=false)

### Is there accessible parking?

[Accessible Parking](https://www.cpp.edu/maps/?id=1130#!ce/25102?ct/29218?mc/34.05748606007155,-117.82340168952871?z/16?lvl/0) is available by Music Building on both sides of Camphor Lane. From Temple Avenue West bound turn right onto University Drive. From Temple Avenue East bound turn left onto University Drive. Turn right on Camphor Lane until you reach a gate. University Theatre will be on your right hand side. [Here is a dropped pin to help!](https://maps.apple.com/place?coordinate=34.057196%2C-117.822635&name=Dropped%20Pin&_ext=EiYp3ZJmEsUGQUAxvf5mPf90XcA5W2iMbusHQUBBk3Owlk10XcBQBA%3D%3D)

### Do I pay for parking?

Yes! Please read signs wherever you park.

Parking fees are $5 on the weekends and $8 during the week. The easiest way to pay is through PayByPhone - [you can pay through the browser HERE](https://m2.paybyphone.com/parking/start/location) or [download the PayByPhone App for iOS and Android to easily pay on your phone (if you want to pay through the app, we suggest doing this before you arrive.)](https://www.paybyphone.com/)

The PayBy Phone location code for Parking Structure 1 is 29032 and the PayByPhone location code for Parking Structure 2 is 29033. You can use either code to pay for parking in the Accessible Parking lot near the theatre.

## Ticket Information

The box office for all performances is located at the front of our theatre.

### Do you sell tickets at the box office in-person before the shows?

Yes! Tickets are sold at our box office for one hour before showtime.

Please note that if a show is in our Studio Theatre, tickets will be limited. We encourage you to purchase tickets online or reserve directly with our box office to guarantee seats in the Studio Theatre.

### Do you sell tickets online?

Yes! [Tickets for all official Department events can be purchased online.](https://www.tix.com/ticket-sales/tix/3530)

The Department of Theatre and New Dance does not sell tickets for the Department of Music events. To purchase such tickets, please visit their website.

### Do you do group sales?

Yes! The Department of Theatre and New Dance welcomes groups of 20 or more people at a $5/person rate. Group rates must be arranged in advance of the event by calling (909) 869-3929 or emailing the Department Publicist and Box Office Manager Chelsea Sutton at crsutton@cpp.edu.

### Do you do refunds or exchanges?

All ticket sales are final. No refunds will be issued unless a performance is canceled or for a special circumstance or exception. In the event a performance is canceled, refunds will only be issued to original purchasers.

Tickets for a performance may be exchanged for another performance only if the initial performance has not elapsed. No ticket exchanges will be honored if the respective performance has concluded.

Lost tickets can be replaced only if a proof of purchase can be rendered. Order confirmation emails for online sales and credit card machine receipts will be honored as such proof.

To arrange exchanges, email the Department Publicist and Box Office Manager Chelsea Sutton at crsutton@cpp.edu or leave a voicemail for her at (909) 869-3929.

### Do you have wheelchair-accessible seating?

Wheelchair-accessible seats are available for all Main Stage performances. Companion seats are available in the same row as wheelchair-accessible seating.

To arrange seating, email the Department Publicist and Box Office Manager Chelsea Sutton at crsutton@cpp.edu or leave a voicemail for her at (909) 869-3929.

### Who do I reach out to if I have questions?

For questions, email the Department Publicist and Box Office Manager Chelsea Sutton at crsutton@cpp.edu or leave a voicemail for her at (909) 869-3929.
