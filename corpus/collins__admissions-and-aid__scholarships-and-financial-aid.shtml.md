# Scholarships & Financial Aid

## We’re Invested in Your Success

### Get a degree without piles of debt.

The Collins College awards $300,000 in scholarships every year — to both incoming students and current Broncos. These scholarships are made possible by our longtime industry partnerships and generous donors.

![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white-mobile.svg)
![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white.svg)

## Current Broncos

### Scholarship Opportunities

College-based and private industry scholarships are available to current students through the CCHM Scholarship Opportunities course on Canvas.

[Find Scholarships](https://canvas.cpp.edu/courses/30606%20)

![Advisor speaks to students in an office.](../img/24collins-revenue-management2.jpg)

![A man giving a presentation in front of a screen which reads "Paying for College, Office of Financial Aid & Scholarships, Student Accounting & Cashiering Services"](../img/scholarships-2022.png)

## Incoming Freshman, Transfer or Change of Major Student Scholarship

You may qualify for up to $1,000 in scholarship funds with the new Collins Incoming Freshman, Transfer or Change of Major Student Scholarship.

[Apply Now](https://csupomona.academicworks.com/opportunities/20543%20 "opens a new window")
