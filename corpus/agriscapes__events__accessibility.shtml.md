# Accessibility Information (ADA)

This page provides an overview of the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) services and access options available at the Cal Poly Pomona Egg Hunt.

Planning a visit to the Egg Hunt can help make your trip memorable and enjoyable.

ADA-accessible parking is available in Lot U on South Campus Drive, south of Temple Avenue. ADA-accessible restrooms are available at the AGRIscapes Building next to the Farm Store.

![Map showing accessible parking and restrooms](../img/2024-egg-hunt-accessibility-map.jpg)

The Egg Hunt is hosted on an active farm and continues to serve as a commercial operation while offering academic benefits to Cal Poly Pomona students.

As an active farm, there are inherent risks commonly associated with agricultural operations, and considerations should be made in advance as you prepare to visit the farm.

Visitors may see heavy machinery/equipment and animals, and travel on unpaved or wet/soft surfaces in a typical outdoor environment.

Like all farms, it is subject to changing weather patterns. We recommend wearing layered clothing and proper footwear (i.e., closed-toe shoes).

On hot days, we recommend staying hydrated and resting under shade frequently. Shaded seating areas are available in multiple locations.

## Requesting Accommodation

To request an accommodation or for inquiries about accessibility, please contact agriscapesinfo@cpp.edu. Advance notice of at least 48 hours is recommended to arrange for some accessibility needs. Every reasonable effort will be made to provide reasonable accommodations in an effective and timely manner.
