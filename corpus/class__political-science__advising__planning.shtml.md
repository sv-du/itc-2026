# Planning Your Political Science Classes

## Curriculum Sheets, Road Maps, and Checklists

To be able to plan your courses, you need to know the curriculum. This page helps you understand the political science curriculum, in addition to providing you with an exhaustive list of resources: curriculum sheets, checklists, roadmaps, and campus resources. To understand your curriculum, you need to look up the curriculum for the year that you began taking classes (or switched your major) at Cal Poly Pomona.
