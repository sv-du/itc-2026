# College of Engineering Advising Center

![Building 17 second floor with College of Engineering and Engineering Advising Center signage](img/egr-largehero.png)

## About Us

We help undergraduate College of Engineering students with:

* General Academic Advising & Course Selection (GE & Core)
* Registration and Graduation Questions
* Petition Assistance & Information
* Degree Progress Review (DPR) Review
* Transfer Credit Review (TCR) Review
* Graduation Pledge Advising
* Academic Planner Review
* Academic Warning, Academic Notice and Academic Notice with Contract Advising
* Resource Referral

We offer appointments virtually or in-person. Appointments are scheduled in 20-minute increments with your assigned advisor.
