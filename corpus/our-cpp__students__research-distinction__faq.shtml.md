# Frequently Asked Questions

## Frequently Asked Questions

---

## General

### What is the Research Distinction?

The Research Distinction is an award granted to Cal Poly Pomona students that have conducted, presented and published a research project. The [Office of Undergraduate Research](https://www.cpp.edu/our-cpp/index.shtml) facilitates the Research Distinction. Students receive a graduation cord and [digital badge](https://cpp.badgr.com/public/badges/XMMbAjV2Se2Ry486Kw16hA).

### When will I receive my graduation cord and certificate?

The Office of Undergraduate Research distributes the Research Distinction at the end of every semester.

### How is the Research Distinction connected to the Faculty Mentor Research Stars?

The Research Distinction and Faculty Mentor Research Stars work together to recognize students that have been involved in research at CPP and the faculty that have guided them along the process.

As part of the application for the Research Distinction, students must list all faculty members that worked on the project. Faculty members listed here are automatically added to the Faculty Mentor Research Stars and their tally is recorded/updated.

## Application and Requirements

### How can I apply for the Research Distinction?

The application for the Research Distinction is available online throughout the year. Only online submissions will be accepted.

Please refer to the [Application](application.shtml) tab to submit an application.

### Who can apply for the Research Distinction?

Any student, graduate or undergraduate at Cal Poly Pomona, can apply for the Research Distinction. You  ***do not***  need to be a senior or at the end of your program to apply. You can apply to the Distinction at any time in your CPP career.

### I have graduated. Can I apply for the Research Distinction?

You can apply for the Research Distinction. If you have graduated, you must submit your application  ***within 1 year of graduation*** . For example, if you graduated in Spring 2024, you have until the end of Spring 2025 to apply.

### What are the requirements for the Research Distinction?

Students must complete all the requirements listed below at the time of application:

1. **Complete Faculty-Mentored Research** - Complete at a minimum, 6 months of faculty-mentored research, scholarship, or creative activity during your time at CPP OR a semester-long experience (ex. capstone, thesis, creative works).
2. **Present at a Conference** - Present research at the [Cal Poly Pomona Student RSCA Conference](https://www.cpp.edu/~cppsrc/index.shtml), [Cal Poly Pomona Creative Activities and Research Symposium](../../events-workshops/cars/index.shtml) or a Professional Research Conference.
3. **Publish a Full Paper or Scholarly Work** - Publish a full paper or scholarly work related to the research or creative activity to [Bronco Scholar](https://www.cpp.edu/~broncoscholar/about.html) or any Peer Reviewed journal.

### How can I meet the requirements for the Research Distinction?

Sometimes, students may struggle to meet the requirements for Research Distinction. Here are a few things you can do to make it easier:

* Write and polish your research paper. Be sure to get your faculty advisor or professor's approval as they will need to be a part of the process in some way.
* Attend and watch workshops hosted by the Office of Undergraduate Research. [Here](https://www.cpp.edu/our-cpp/events-workshops/workshops.shtml) are a few to get started.
* Presenting at a conference. You can present at one of CPP's conferences, such as [RSCA](https://www.cpp.edu/cppsrc/current-conference/index.shtml) or [CARS](https://www.cpp.edu/our-cpp/events-workshops/cars/current/index.shtml). Students may also present at a regional or international conference. If you present at a conference outside of Cal Poly, you may apply for the OUR's [Conference Presentation Award](https://www.cpp.edu/our-cpp/students/conference-award/index.shtml).

## Deadlines

### What is the Fall deadline?

The Fall deadline is set up for students who are set to graduate that semester or for students who are turning in Graduate applications for December and January.

The Fall dealine falls on the first Monday of December each year.

### What is the Spring deadline?

The Spring deadline is set up for students who want to be recognized at the Scholar Recognition Ceremony in April.

The Spring deadline falls on the first Friday of April each year.

### Are these deadlines "hard deadlines"?

While there are deadlines set, these are not hard deadlines. The deadlines are set for students who wish to have it on their resume/CV or take part in the Scholar Recognition Ceremony. The applications are to set to be on a rolling basis.
