## 2021-22 CPPLA Public Lecture Series — Landscape Architecture and the Science of Climate Change: Extinction

June 03, 2022 to June 03, 2022

“Landscape Architecture and the Science of Climate Change" is a series of talks pairing the research of scientists with the implementation of landscape architects on climate change topics. The final installment of the 2021-22 public event program "EXTINCTION" presents talks by Mia Lehrer, founder and president of Studio-MLA; and Morgan W. Tingley, Associate Professor of Ecology and Evolutionary Biology at UCLA's Institute of the Environment and Sustainability.

[Read more](../news-events/public-lecture-pages/2022-06-03-climate-change-extinction.shtml "2021-22 CPPLA Public Lecture Series — Landscape Architecture and the Science of Climate Change: Extinction")
