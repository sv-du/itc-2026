# Retention & Graduation Specialists

## About

Your Retention and Graduation Specialist (RGS) will help you stay on track toward graduation. If you have an Academic Warning, Academic Notice, or Academic Notice with Contract hold, you may be required to meet with your RGS to develop a plan to improve your GPA and continue to be enrolled at CPP.

Contact your RGS as soon as you see that you have a hold. This will help to you to get back on track faster, as well as help you to avoid long lines during registration periods.

Make an appointment with your RGS using [CPP Connect](http://www.cpp.edu/~studentsuccess/cppconnect/index.shtml).
