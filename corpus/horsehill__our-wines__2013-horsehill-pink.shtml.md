# 2013 Horsehill Pink, South Coast

![2013 Horsehill Pink South Coast](https://www.cpp.edu/horsehill/img/2013-Horsehill-Pink-South-Coast.jpg)

The 2013 Horsehill Pink, South Coast, brings back the off-dry rosé style that Horsehill was so successful with in the inaugural releases.

The pink blend includes:

* 22% Cabernet Sauvignon
* 21% Grenache
* 18% Syrah
* 14% Sangiovese
* 10% Tempranillo
* 7% Zinfandel
* 5% Petit Verdot
* 3% Alicante

Horsehill Pink is a gorgeous blend of red cultivars which captures the very soul of the numerous varietals. Bountiful red fruit aromas, cherry, strawberry, and raspberry all leap from the glass, while lip-smacking flavors are artfully balanced with just the right amount of sweetness and acid balance. A delightful wine to sip while relaxing by the pool or to be paired with some spicy Southwestern cuisine. This is a rose that really shows that sometimes the sum is greater than the parts.
