# Book an Appointment with IT Client Services

## Appointment Types

[### On-Site Computer Setup

This appointment is for setting up of a new computer in a physical office space. The appointment would take place in your office. (Up to 2 hours)](https://outlook.office365.com/owa/calendar/ClientServices%40livecsupomona.onmicrosoft.com/bookings/s/nMFjy3kcPUyN5RqwJO1hUA2)

[### Laptop Setup/Pickup in Bldg 1

This appointment is for setting up of a new or loaner laptop. This appointment will take place in Building 1, Room 211. (Up to 90 minutes)](https://outlook.office365.com/owa/calendar/ClientServices%40livecsupomona.onmicrosoft.com/bookings/s/C9cBgsgQYUSDoVOvoNAdMA2)

[### On-Site Technical Support

An appointment for a tech to visit your office for technical support including troubleshooting, setup, physical access. (45 minutes)](https://outlook.office365.com/owa/calendar/ClientServices%40livecsupomona.onmicrosoft.com/bookings/s/QXjI9F9thk6AHF-cLh5wDw2)

[### Device Pick Up / Return - Bldg 1

This appointment is for picking up or returning of IT devices such as power adapters, monitors, keyboards, mice, loaner laptops. This appointment will take place in Building 1, Room 211 (Up to 30 minutes)](https://outlook.office365.com/owa/calendar/ClientServices%40livecsupomona.onmicrosoft.com/bookings/s/OqVZFv3DKUGtMMBX2VBjmw2)

[### Virtual Technical Support

This appointment is for a 45 minute virtual technical support session. You will meet virtually with an IT Client Services technician.](https://outlook.office365.com/owa/calendar/ClientServices%40livecsupomona.onmicrosoft.com/bookings/s/J5x-ETdkEEm1ELm8E3tniA2)

[### Computer Move Request

Move a computer from one location to another. Max of 3 computers per appointment. There is a $30 per computer fee for this appointment NOTE: If your monitors are connected to monitor arms, please contact Facilities to coordinate the removal and re-install of the monitor arms. IT will disconnect the monitors and re-install them after the arms have been moved. (Up to 2 hours)](https://outlook.office.com/book/ClientServices%40livecsupomona.onmicrosoft.com/s/VkC3zpljBEa5kY4k01R8YA2?ae=true)

[### Computer Upgrade/Reimage

This appointment is for upgrading/re-image a computer. The appointment would take place in your office.](https://outlook.office.com/book/ClientServices%40livecsupomona.onmicrosoft.com/s/4LVqrn_Trku5KtjmrK8_Ug2?ismsaljsauthenabled)

[### Computer Removal

An appointment for a tech to visit your office to collect an unused computer.](https://outlook.office.com/book/ClientServices%40livecsupomona.onmicrosoft.com/s/KgdZaimc1kS-vcED4by92g2?ismsaljsauthenabled)
