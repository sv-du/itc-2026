### Finding Our New President

The CSU Board of Trustees has launched a nationwide search for the next president of Cal Poly Pomona. We invite the campus community and our stakeholders to be part of the process.](https://www.cpp.edu/president-search/ "opens in new window")

[![bronco statue](https://www.cpp.edu/president/img/news-module-images/16bronco-statue-copy.jpg)

### Campus Open Forum Recording

An open forum for the Cal Poly Pomona community was held on Sept. 25. Members of the Trustees’ Committee for the Selection of the President heard feedback on the qualities and attributes desired for our next president.](https://www.cpp.edu/president-search/ "opens a new window")
[![hands typing on a computer](https://www.cpp.edu/president/img/news-module-images/survey.jpeg)

### Stakeholder Survey

Comments regarding professional qualifications, experiences and personal attributes may be submitted via Survey Monkey.](https://www.surveymonkey.com/r/N5TC7VY "opens a new window")
[![CSU logo](https://www.cpp.edu/president/img/news-module-images/csu-logo.png)

### CSU Presidential Search Policy (PDF)

The Board of Trustees of the California State University, in partnership with the Chancellor, is responsible for the
recruitment, selection and appointment of CSU campus presidents.](https://www2.calstate.edu/csu-system/about-the-csu/leadership/presidents/Documents/csu-bot-presidential-selection-policy.pdf "opens a new window")
