# Collecting Areas

Special Collections and Archives serves as the access point for the Library's holdings of rare books and archival collections including the University Archives, the Pomona Valley Historical Collection, the Southern California Wine and Wine Industry Collection, the W.K. Kellogg Arabian Horse Library, and the general rare book collection.
