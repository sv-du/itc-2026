# Transfer Planning

## We can help you reach your goal!

As a transfer student, you know the value of planning for the future and setting goals for your education. At Cal Poly Pomona, we want to help you reach those goals with the right tools and resources to make the most of your time as a Bronco.

## Academic Planning

Knowing how to begin and navigate your academic journey at Cal Poly Pomona is key to your success.

## Academic Planning Resources

[Card 1 of 3

### Prospective Students

If you are considering Cal Poly Pomona, begin your journey by checking out the Apply page.](https://www.cpp.edu/apply/index.shtml)
[Card 2 of 3

### Admitted Students

Once you are admitted, you will want to follow Admissions' Admitted Transfer checklist, as well as the New Student Programs and Family Engagement checklists (for fall or spring admission).](https://www.cpp.edu/admissions/index.shtml)
[Card 3 of 3

### Continuing Students

Find academic planning tools, the academic calendar and much more information on Student Success Central.](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/index.shtml)

## Career/Internship Planning

Check out these resources and links to get you on the path to securing the internship and career of your dreams.

[Career Center](https://www.cpp.edu/career/index.shtml)

[Center for Community Engagement](https://www.cpp.edu/cce/index.shtml)

## Financial Planning and Basic Needs

Make sure you know how to understand your student account, as well as how to find CPP basic needs resources.

[Office of Financial Aid and Scholarships](https://www.cpp.edu/financial-aid/index.shtml)

[Student Accounting & Cashering Services](https://www.cpp.edu/student-accounting/)

[Bronco Care Basic Needs Program](https://www.cpp.edu/basicneeds/index.shtml)

[Rent Affordability Calculator](https://www.rentcafe.com/rent-affordability-calculator/)

[Financial Videos](https://www.consumerfinance.gov/consumer-tools/educator-tools/your-money-your-goals/videos/)

## Graduate School Planning

The Graduate School planning process is most effective when you begin at least one year prior to your intended admittance into a graduate program of your choice. We recommend at least a year’s worth of preparation so you have plenty of time to explore and ask questions.

[CPP Graduate Studies](https://www.cpp.edu/gradstudies/index.shtml)

## Undergraduate Research Planning

If you are considering graduate school or you are going into a professional field where research is a core component, you will want to consider applying for undergraduate research opportunities.

[Office of Undergraduate Research](https://www.cpp.edu/our-cpp/index.shtml)

[PolyTransfer's Undergraduate research Guide](https://www.cpp.edu/polytransfer/transfer-planning/undergraduate-research-planning.shtml)

## Study Abroad Planning

If you would like the opportunity to explore the world while making progress towards your degree, look into the Study Abroad programs we offer at Cal Poly Pomona. Many of our transfer students enter the university believing that they may not have enough time to participate in this program given they only have two years. You have time!

[Study Abroad Website](https://www.cpp.edu/international/study-abroad/index.shtml)

[Study Abroad myBAR](https://mybar.cpp.edu/organization/studyabroad)
