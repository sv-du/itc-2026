# CLASS Scholarships

Through donors' generosity, the College of Letters, Arts, and Social Sciences (CLASS) has the privilege of awarding scholarships to serve our students better. We hope to provide our students with every opportunity to prosper and grow, regardless of their financial circumstances. Applying for scholarships may seem like a daunting task, but we make it our priority to streamline our students' process.

**Applying Has Never Been Easier**: Fill out the general online application located at the Office of Financial Aid's scholarship page: <https://csupomona.academicworks.com>. The system will match you with all the scholarship opportunities available to you. Please be aware many scholarships have additional requirements beyond the general application. To be considered, you must complete all other requirements for the individual scholarships.

[Apply Now!](https://csupomona.academicworks.com "https://csupomona.academicworks.com")
