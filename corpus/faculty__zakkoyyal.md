## About Me

Dr. Koyya joined Cal Poly Pomona in August 2019 in Kinesiology and Health Promotion. She teaches undergraduate and graduate courses within the department exercise science track including Health and Well-being, Movement Anatomy & Kinesiology, Sports Medicine, and Exercise Prescription & Fitness Testing. Her research interests include physical activity promotion, wearable activity monitors and other exercise technologies, exercise prescription, and Exercise is Medicine.

Dr. Koyya is also a faculty director for the University's [BASES Scholars program](https://www.cpp.edu/sci/bases/index.shtml) and a [I AM FIRST](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/oss/i-am-first/index.shtml) mentor. She is also is [Dreamer Ally](https://www.cpp.edu/broncodreamers/index.shtml), [Safe Zone Ally](https://www.cpp.edu/tree/pride/index.shtml), [Basic Needs Ally](https://www.cpp.edu/basicneeds/index.shtml), [Parenting Student Ally](https://www.cpp.edu/studentparents/index.shtml), and [Career Success Ally](https://www.cpp.edu/career/index.shtml).

To learn more about Dr. Koyya's work and achievements, please see her [Curriculum Vitae](curriculum-vitae3.docx)
