# The Undergraduate Journal of Ethics, Policy, and Social Justice

The *Undergraduate Journal of Ethics, Policy, and Social Justice* seeks to provide a space for original undergraduate work that exhibits critical thinking through a social justice lens.

## Participate

### Submit a Paper

Submissions should be 1,500 to 5,000 words and prepared for anonymous review (no indications of who the author is included in the submission).  To submit, please complete [the submission form](https://nam11.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fbit.ly%2F3WsftcW&data=05%7C01%7Cbkim%40cpp.edu%7Cda565c311a6c4d05a1d508db19b93247%7C164ba61e39ec4f5d89ffaa1f00a521b4%7C0%7C0%7C638132055423916449%7CUnknown%7CTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJWIjoiMC4wLjAwMDAiLCJQIjoiV2luMzIiLCJBTiI6Ik1haWwiLCJXVCI6Mn0%3D%7C3000%7C%7C%7C&sdata=Cohy7sYdYSpqjoPC8UY1St7fH%2Bo94BHLj1T7SxbOl%2B0%3D&reserved=0).  Final deadline for submissions is Sunday, March 5.  See the Call for Papers (note the deadline extension to March 5) for more information.  And please contact Ryan Mendoza (rwmendoza@cpp.edu) and/or Dr. Cory Aragon (csaragon@cpp.edu) with any questions.

Submission form link: [bit.ly/3WsftcW](https://nam11.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fbit.ly%2F3WsftcW&data=05%7C01%7Cbkim%40cpp.edu%7Cda565c311a6c4d05a1d508db19b93247%7C164ba61e39ec4f5d89ffaa1f00a521b4%7C0%7C0%7C638132055423916449%7CUnknown%7CTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJWIjoiMC4wLjAwMDAiLCJQIjoiV2luMzIiLCJBTiI6Ik1haWwiLCJXVCI6Mn0%3D%7C3000%7C%7C%7C&sdata=Cohy7sYdYSpqjoPC8UY1St7fH%2Bo94BHLj1T7SxbOl%2B0%3D&reserved=0).

### Become an Editor

Associate Editors take a lecture (3 units) during the Spring semester, facilitated by Dr. Cory Aragon of the Philosophy Department. Here, students learn how to critically evaluate papers and give constructive feedback for submissions. Editors then agree on which research papers will be accepted for publishing in the journal.

Applications for Associate Editors are now closed and will reopen Fall 2024.

### Showcase Art

Once papers have been accepted, students contributing art to the journal will read the accepted papers and create art based on their perception of what they have read.

If interested, please contact Dr. Aragon csaragon@cpp.edu
