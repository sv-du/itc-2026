# Major Information

## Grade Requirements: General Education

General Education Grade Requirements

| **GE Course** | **Grade Required** |
| --- | --- |
| **GE Area A1 or 1C: Oral Communication**   * COM 1100 OR * COM 2204 – Required for Nutrition Majors | C- |
| **GE Area A2: Written Communication or 1A: English Composition**   * ENG 1100 and ENG 1101 – Stretch Composition OR * ENG 1103 – First-Year Composition | C- |
| **GE Area A3 or 1B: Critical Thinking**   * ENG 2105 – Required for Nutrition Majors OR * PHL 2020 | C- |
| **GE Area B4: Mathematics/Quantitative Reasoning or 2: Mathematical Concepts and Quantitative Reasoning**   * See the [University Catalog](https://catalog.cpp.edu/) for a complete list of courses under General Education Program | C- |
