# History Events

## Coming soon!

![Flyer for Sept 25 discussion](../img/on-tyranny-_-amerinst-common-core-i-_-fall-2025.jpg)

### September 25 2025

#### American Institutions Common Core Lecture #1

Please join us for the first American Institutions Common Core Lecture of the year on Sept. 25 at 4 p.m. in the Lyra room (1611) of the Bronco Student Center (Building 35). The event -- On Tyranny: Some Questions for Hannah Arendt & Judith Shklar -- will feature History professor (and The Democracy Institute's Director) Dr. Aishwary Kumar, with another History professor, Dr. Rachael Hill, as the discussant.

## Past Events

![Giancarlo and professors](../img/gin-and-profs.jpg)

### September 4, 2025

#### Fall Kickoff

We jump-started the academic year with our Fall Kickoff on September 4 at U-Hour (12 p.m.- 1 p.m.) -- we introduced faculty and club leaders, and awarded some prizes!

![Michael Slaughter](../img/slaughter-banquet.jpeg)

### September 11, 2025

#### Pre-Credential Q&A

Dr. Michael Slaughter (CPP History Pre-Credential Advisor) hosted a lively information session Sept. 11 at U-Hour (12-1 p.m.) in Building 6, Room 111, all about the Pre-Credential option!

![Aishwary Kumar and Jamelle Bouie](../img/events/bouie.jpg)

### April 10, 2025

#### What is Left of Freedom?

This event was a lively conversation between Dr. Aishwary Kumar (Professor of History, Director of the Democracy Institute) and New York Times columnist Jamelle Bouie. It was one of several "common core" events for students enrolled in sections of HST 3340 (History of American Institutions).
