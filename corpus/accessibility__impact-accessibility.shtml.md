## Experiences of Users with Disabilities

[![Paul Chiou graduating](img/paul_chiou.png)

### Paul Chiou

We Are Cal Poly Pomona computer science alumnus, Paul Chiou, who hopes to one day create more mainstream technology that offers people with disabilities the ability to excel.](https://www.cpp.edu/news/content/2017/09/computer-science-student-receives-top-csu-award/index.shtml%20 "opens a new window")

[![Kelvin Crosby working on pottery](img/kelvin_crosby.png)

### Kelvin Crosby

San Diego State student who is deaf-blind and shares his experience in the classroom and information on accessible instructional materials for the classroom.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qOmNUlWMcEs  "opens a new window")

## Working Together: People with Disabilities and Computer Technology

Working Together: People with Disability and Computer Technology video shows students with various types of disabilities using different adaptive technologies in the academic environment.

![Father laughing with son in a wheelchair](img/boy-wheelchair-embrace-man.jpeg)

## Getty Images

### The Disability Collection

The Disability Collection is a dedicated collection of images that break stereotypes and authentically portray people with disabilities in everyday life from Oath, the National Disability Leadership Alliance and Getty Images.
Check out the experiences of some students with disabilities and how they interact with technology.

[The Disability Collection](https://www.gettyimages.com/collections/thedisabilitycollection%20 "opens a new window")
