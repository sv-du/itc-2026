Alyson Lawrence

![Josiah Cuckler](img/di-student-cuckler-josiah-920x920.jpg)

> "My dietetic internship at Cal Poly Pomona truly set me up for success. The amount of experience and knowledge I acquired through this program helped shape my current clinical practice."

Josiah Cuckler

![Adriana Salinas](img/di-student-salinas-adriana-920x920.jpg)

> "This program was so well-rounded and organized, making me feel prepared when it came time for my exam and the work field ... the tools I was given throughout my time at Cal Poly Pomona helped me to succeed and provided a good base to build upon for my...

Adriana Salinas

![Xinyue Harkless](img/di-student-harkless-xinyue-920x920.jpg)

Xinyue Harkless

![Mackenzie Brewer](img/di-student-brewer-mackenzie-920x920.jpg)

> "The dietetic internship through Cal Poly Pomona was so awesome and definitely set me up for success in my career. I think what really stood out to me was having the ability to select a six-week elective rotation. "

Mackenzie Brewer

![Terrance Tang](img/di-student-tang-terrance-920x920.jpg)
