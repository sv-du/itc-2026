# EOP Student Union

## Who We Are

In association with the Educational Opportunity Program (EOP), we are the EOP Student Union (ESU)! We aim to make a positive difference in the lives of EOP students by offering a sense of Community, Networking Opportunities, and Support for their academic success.

 Stay in the know about upcoming events and General Body Meetings by Connecting with ESU:

* [Follow ESU on Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/esu_cpp/)
* [Follow ESU on Discord](https://discord.com/invite/4qJGW3khng)
* Complete our NEW [Membership Form](https://forms.office.com/r/XGHceqg04i) for a Welcome Email!
