# Upcoming Concerts, Recitals & Music Events

## See What's Happening in the Music Department

The Dr. Andrew G. Chong Box Office #24-188 is open one hour prior to show time for all ticketed events. All events are open to the public and student pricing applies to students from any school with a valid school ID.

* **All Ticketed Events** - $20 General | $10 Senior w/ID | $10 Student w/ID
* **Noon Hour Performances/Guest Artist Master Classes**- Free

***Parking permit required.***

***Artists and programs subject to change.***

*Please do not attend an on-campus event if you are experiencing any COVID-19 symptoms or are under quarantine or isolation orders. Adherence to all campus protocols is required when visiting. For more info, please go to [Safer Return](https://www.cpp.edu/safer-return/index.shtml).*
