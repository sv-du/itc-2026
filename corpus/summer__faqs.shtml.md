# Summer Session FAQs

![Billy Bronco Holding Luggage](img/billy-bronco-dorm.jpg)

## General

Expand/Collapse All General

### Why has registration been rescheduled?

The university needs additional time to set up the new summer course offerings and fee structure.

### How do I know which courses can count for GE?

The BroncoDirect Class Search has Course Attribute filters, which you can use to look for specific GE designations. You can also review a course’s details to see if it has a specific GE designation. You can also see the General Education list in the [University Catalog](https://catalog.cpp.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=65&poid=17161). You can [review your Degree Progress Report in your Student Center](https://nam11.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.youtube.com%2Fwatch%3Fv%3DzXpEbJ3rApg&data=05%7C02%7Civalencia%40cpp.edu%7C9fe80f35c73d474270a508dc3980b471%7C164ba61e39ec4f5d89ffaa1f00a521b4%7C0%7C0%7C638448472156354954%7CUnknown%7CTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJWIjoiMC4wLjAwMDAiLCJQIjoiV2luMzIiLCJBTiI6Ik1haWwiLCJXVCI6Mn0%3D%7C0%7C%7C%7C&sdata=qBpzae4NmCK1r3LD8yXSqJiCCBK5OH9Wkiyh%2BvtJ2dM%3D&reserved=0) to see which GE areas you may need to work on.

 If you have additional questions about General Education, contact the [Bronco Advising Center](http://www.cpp.edu/bac).

### How much will summer tuition and fees cost?

The charts will be available on the [Student Accounting & Cashiering Services website](https://www.cpp.edu/student-accounting/index.shtml "https://www.cpp.edu/student-accounting/index.shtml") in the coming weeks.

### Will I be able to enroll in both major and GE courses?

Yes. You will be able to review the tuition and fee charts to estimate your costs. If you’re enrolling in non-GE courses, your per-unit cost will be different.

### How will I know if I’m eligible for summer financial aid?

Enroll in 6+ units, and [Financial Aid & Scholarships](https://www.cpp.edu/financial-aid/other-programs/summer/index.shtml "https://www.cpp.edu/financial-aid/other-programs/summer/index.shtml") will assess your eligibility. Monitor your email and your financial aid page in your Student Center for updates.

### Will Summer classes be virtual?

All classes being offered in the three summer sessions will be offered in a mixture of online and in-person format.

### I have general questions about Cal Poly Pomona. Who can I contact?

For specific questions about Summer Session, email summer@cpp.edu.

### Is there housing available in the Summer?

Visit the **[housing website](https://www.cpp.edu/housing/futureresidents/apply-to-uhs.shtml)** for more information.

### What is the Library summer schedule?

The University Library is open for the summer semester.  Check the [library homepage](https://www.cpp.edu/library/index.shtml) for current hours and to search for books or journals. The computer lab at the east entrance is open 24/7; Bronco ID is required for entrance to the lab.

### Are there any dining locations open during the summer term?

Yes, summer hours for dining locations around Cal Poly Pomona campus can be found **[here](dining.shtml)**.

## Courses

### I need specific classes this summer. What can I do to make sure that they are offered?

You should talk to your department chair. All departments are going to be collecting information about which classes should be offered this summer. They will be exchanging information about service and GE courses offered by other departments.

## Registration

Expand/Collapse All Registration

### If the College of Professional and Global Education is handling Summer Session, how does that affect my registration?

Registration is exactly the same for current Cal Poly Pomona students. Current students can register and pay fees through Bronco Direct. New and visiting students must contact the College of Professional and Global Education and register through Open University. Read more about registration on the [Registration page from Academic Affairs.](https://www.cpp.edu/registrar/registration/registration-information.shtml)

## Fees

Expand/Collapse All Fees

### Are the fees for summer session the same as fees for other academic terms?

Summer fees do vary from other semesters, and there is a cost savings potential in Summer, depending on the types of courses and/or the number of units you are taking.  Please contact your advisor to discuss. You can also visit the [student accounting & cashiering website](https://www.cpp.edu/student-accounting/tuition-fees/summer2020.shtml) for information.

### I'm in the MBA program.  Do I have to pay the Graduate Business Professional Fee?

MBA courses will be offered as regular session courses and not as part of the self-support session.  The Graduate  Business  Professional fee will be charged.

### What is CPP's Summer Session refund policy?

The refund policy for summer session is different than for other academic terms. For more information, please see the [Cal Poly Pomona Summer Session Payments & Refunds](http://www.cpp.edu/~summer/payment.shtml)[page.](http://www.cpp.edu/~summer/payment.shtml)

### Why do we have additional fees for some types of courses in summer?

Summer classes are self-supported. The state is not providing any funding for the faculty salaries or the other costs of running a course in summer.  Independent Study classes require significant extra fees because the faculty salary needs to be supported by a very small number of students.  The basic tuition fees for lecture and seminar classes do not cover the additional costs of other course types.

### Is there a payment plan available?

Yes, there is a payment plan for summer. There are several ways to pay for Summer, please visit the Payment Options page **[here](https://www.cpp.edu/student-accounting/payment-options/index.shtml)** for more details.

## Financial Aid

Expand/Collapse All Financial Aid

### Am I able to receive financial aid for summer session?

It may be possible to adjust your financial aid disbursements to help cover summer session costs. Students should speak to their financial aid counselor to determine eligibility. There are financial aid options available, please contact the [Financial Aid Office.](https://www.cpp.edu/~financial-aid/index.shtml)

For information about Summer session financial aid, click [here.](https://www.cpp.edu/financial-aid/other-programs/summer/index.shtml)
