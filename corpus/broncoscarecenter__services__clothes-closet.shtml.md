# Clothes Closet

![A student looks through clothes at the closet](../img/2025/care-center-villapando.jpg)

## Put Your Best Fit Forward

The Clothes Closet provides assistance to students who are seeking to obtain proper clothing for an interview, a career fair, a conference, the first week of work, or everyday wear.

## 10 Items of Clothing Per Semester

Any student is welcome to visit the Clothes Closet and can take **10 items of professional clothing per semester**. Students can seek the assistance of a Clothes Closet assistant by appointment or by walking in during our business hours.

Our personalized shopping experience helps you feel comfortable and confident in selecting clothes and accessories from the Closet!

## Visit Us

Inside the Care Center (Bldg. 97)

Monday through Friday: 9:00 a.m. to 4:30 p.m.

Email: carecenter@cpp.edu

## Donate to the Clothes Closet

**Donation Receipts**

Tax receipts are available upon request. Please note the following information:

* The campus Advancement and Gift office provides donation documentation, such as tax receipts.
* The Care Center is not responsible for sending documentation.
* A "Gifts" form will need to be completed by the donor that is provided by the Gifts office.
* Allow up to 30 business days to receive documentation.

**Donation Criteria**

Professional and casual wear items. We ask that items are washed before being brought in.

**We Do** **N****ot Accept**

* Undergarments
* Bathing Suits
* Sweats
* Costumes (Human or Animal)
* Prom dresses/gala/formal dresses
* Worn-out or overused items
