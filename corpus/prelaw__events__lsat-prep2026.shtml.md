# LSAT Prep Course Schedule – Spring 2026

![flyer](../img/events/lsat-prep-sp2026.png)CPP PreLaw Pathways is offering a free LSAT workshop series designed to help students understand the exam, prepare effectively, and practice key skills.

**Time:** 12:00 pm to 1:00 pm
**Location:** Room 1-301
*Schedule subject to change.*

### Workshop Schedule

* **March 5** – Arguments 1 (Logical Reasoning)
* **March 12** – Prep Test Review
* **March 19** – Arguments 2 (Logical Reasoning)
* **March 26** – Reading Comprehension
* **April 14** – Arguments 3 (Logical Reasoning)
* **April 28** – Prep Test Review

### Cost

Free and open to students.
