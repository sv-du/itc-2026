# Office of Academic Innovation

![Group of people posing on a staircase outside a brick building.](img/homepage/header/2.jpg)

## The Offices of Academic Innovation curate experiential learning, discovery and innovation opportunities for the Cal Poly Pomona community.

The Office of Academic Innovation (OAI) is dedicated to fostering student success by cultivating a culture of creativity and innovation in teaching and learning. Aligned with Cal Poly Pomona’s mission to *[promote success through experiential learning, discovery, and innovation,](https://www.cpp.edu/strategicplan/mission.shtml)* OAI supports the university’s strategic goal of becoming the epicenter of creativity, innovation, and discovery. Through collaborative efforts with faculty, staff, students, alumni, and external partners we design and implement innovative curricula that inspire engaged, lifelong learning across the Cal Poly Pomona community.
