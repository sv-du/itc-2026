## Refund Policy

All refund requests must be submitted in written. The refund policy is as follows:

* Full tuition refund given only before the first day of class.
* 50% refund given if withdrawal occurs in the first 5 days.
* Not eligible for refund after the first week (5 days).
* Refund paid only to the person who paid the tuition.
* Application fee is neither refundable nor transferable.

Please allow up to 3-4 weeks for refunds to process.
