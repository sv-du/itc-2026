# Carousel Cards Module Tutorial

## Instructions

In Cascade, you can choose between three carousel card styles: text only, college department (image and text with call-to-action), and image and text. Expand the accordions below to explore each option in detail.

### Carousel Cards (Text)

***Page View***

**![Carousel Card Option 1 View](../img/pageviewcauroseltext.png)**

**For this section you will need:**

* Heading (H2)
* Card Titles + Text
* Card Links (Optional)

***Cascade View***

![Cascade View Pt 1 Instructions](../img/cascadeviewcauroseltextpt1.png)

**Link Option 1**

![linkinstructions1](../img/linkinstructions1.png)

**Link Option 2**
![link instructions 2](../img/linkinstructions2.png)

**No Link**

![no link](../img/nolinkinstruction.png)

### Carousel Cards (College/Department)

***Page View***

![Carousel Card Option 2 View](../img/card21.png)

**For this section you will need:**

* Heading (H2)
* Card Titles + Images
* Card Links (Optional)
* CTA Button + Link (Optional)

***Cascade View***

**![Carousel Card Option 2 Cascade Part 1](../img/collegedeptcascadeviewpt1.png)**

**![Carousel Card Option 2 Cascade Part 2](../img/collegedeptcascadeviewpt2.png)**

### Carousel Cards (Picture and Text)

***Page View***

![Carousel Card 3 View](../img/card3.png)

**For this section you will need:**

* Heading (H2)
* Card Titles + Images + Descriptions
* Card Links (Optional)
* CTA Button + Link (Optional)

***Cascade View***

**![Carousel Card Option 3 Cascade Part 1](../img/cauroselcardspictextcascadeviewpt1.png)**

**![Carousel Card Option 3 Cascade Part 2](../img/cauroselcardspictextcascadeviewpt2.png)**

If you need assistance uploading images or videos, please refer to the Adding Content Instructions page. Visit the page and follow the provided instructions to upload your files correctly.
