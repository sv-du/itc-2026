# Curricula

![Schools That Heal Design with Mental Health in Mind Claire Latane](../img/schools-that-heal-cover.jpg)

Learn about the research behind evidenced-based design strategies that support student mental health and well-being while improving academic success, physical health, inclusiveness, and environmental health. Learn how to conduct conversations to create change and explore creative funding strategies.

See 10 schools that were designed with mental health in mind for inspiration. This curriculum includes a course syllabus and materials to support instructors of college and university level students as well as continuing and professional education.

This course includes a module on design with specific strategies for a range of budgets and timeframes to transform learning environments to provide a sense of belonging, create nature-filled places, and inspire awe. Based on the book *Schools That Heal: Design with Mental Health in Mind*.
