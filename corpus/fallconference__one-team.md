# One Team Award

![One Team Award Banner](img/ota-main.png)

## Introduction

The Fall Conference Committee received approval from campus leadership for an additional component to Fall Convocation that recognizes and celebrates the achievements of cross-departmental teams and the significant impact of work performed by teams (large or small). During Fall Convocation, the One Team Award will be presented as an ***annual*** acknowledgement of organizational and employee excellence.

## Timeline

timeline

| Date | Description |
| --- | --- |
| May 5 – Jun 20 (Survey Period Extended) | Strategic Communications will notify the campus community about the One Team Award and the application submission process. |
| August 18, 2025 | One Team Award announced during Fall Convocation |

[Submit Application](submit-application.shtml)

![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white.svg)

## Purpose

In alignment with our Strategic Plan – Initiative #5, the One Team Award will recognize and reward the achievements of cross-departmental teams focused on the outcome(s) of the work performed by teams and the impact to the campus community with an emphasis on one or more of our Core Values as noted below.

[View Strategic Initiative #5](https://www.cpp.edu/strategicplan/strategic-initiative-5.shtml%20 "opens a new window")
