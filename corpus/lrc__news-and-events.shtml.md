# News & Events

## Tutoring is available Monday-Thursday, 9:00 a.m.-7:00 p.m. and Friday 9:00 a.m.-4:00 p.m. in-person and through Zoom. See [Zoom Instructions](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1QiI85FsLrVHRgQK7jLCrYll0-H0WnlrG_kaqSfHfYpg/edit "link to tutee Zoom instructions") to get started in LRC's online tutoring service. You may find a list of available courses through visiting [CPP Connect](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/cppconnect/index.shtml "link to CPP Connect") or by clicking on the [Available Courses](our-services/subject-tutoring.shtml "link to course list") link.
