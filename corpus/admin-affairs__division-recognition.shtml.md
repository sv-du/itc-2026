## Division Awards

![Gold star award](img/front-facing-gold-star-award.jpeg)

### Customer Service Award

Consistently provides exemplary services to our customers, goes above and beyond to address their needs and resolve their issues and ensures a great customer experience.

[Online Form to Submit Nomination](https://app.smartsheet.com/b/form/e468988ac5e44fbc997637a74a288a00)

Additional Information

### Customer Service Award

×

**Criteria:**

* Positive Attitude: Displays a positive attitude, even in difficult situations, which impacts our customer experience (internal or external).
* Exceptional Service/ Eliminating Barriers: eliminates barriers by communicating and collaborating with the customer to address their needs.
* Creativity or resourceful: demonstrates creativity or resourcefulness in assisting our customers in addressing their needs (within University policy).
* Awards are distributed to eligible CPP Administrative Affairs employees in good standing.

**Metrics:**

* Customer Satisfaction Score / Survey (CSAT): measures customer satisfaction with a service or product at a specific outpoint / includes name of the person who serviced the customer.
* Post-Interaction Surveys: Conducted immediately after a service interaction, these surveys provide immediate feedback from customers about their experience and to capture the name person who serviced the customer.

**Nominations:**

* Submitted on award recognition website, using the nomination link. Nominations may be submitted by CPP campus community specifically for Administrative Affairs Division employees.
* Nominations will be pulled and distributed to the employee's corresponding department AVP for review.
* Each department AVP will select one (1) employee from their nominations as a winner.

### Innovation Award

Brings creative ideas, proposals and solutions, demonstrates creativity in problem-solving, and contributes to workplace innovation and efficiency.

[Online Form to Submit Nomination](https://app.smartsheet.com/b/form/e468988ac5e44fbc997637a74a288a00)

Additional Information

### Innovation Award

×

**Criteria:**

* Initiative and Adaptability: Proactively brings forth new ideas. Embraces and adapts to change.
* Problem solving and implementation: Brings innovative ideas. Creates/proposes innovative, creative solutions to problems. Works with co-workers and leaders to implement solutions.
* Awards are distributed to eligible CPP Administrative Affairs employees in good standing.

**Metrics:**

* Innovative solution impact: Description of innovative achievement and impact to division and/or campus.

**Nominations:**

* Submitted on award recognition website, using the nomination link. Nominations may only be submitted by and for CPP Administrative Affairs Division employees.
* Nominations will be pulled and distributed to the employee's corresponding department AVP for review.
* AVPs will submit their nomination(s) to VP of Administrative Affairs.
* VP of Administrative Affairs will review AVP submitted nominations for selection of winner(s).

### Collaboration Award

Impactful collaboration within a department or across department lines resulting in an outcome that benefits our students, faculty, staff or the overall University.

[Online Form to Submit Nomination](https://app.smartsheet.com/b/form/e468988ac5e44fbc997637a74a288a00)

Additional Information

### Collaboration Award

×

**Criteria:**

* Demonstrated a partnership or collaboration that resulted in a positive outcome or quantifiable benefits for the university.
* Awards are distributed to eligible CPP Administrative Affairs employees in good standing.

**Metrics:**

* Feedback: from their supervisors, peers, subordinates, and clients to provide a holistic view of the employee's collaboration skills.

**Nominations:**

* Submitted on award recognition website, using the nomination link. Nominations may only be submitted by and for CPP Administrative Affairs Division employees.
* Nominations will be pulled and distributed to the employee's corresponding department AVP for review.
* AVPs will submit their nomination(s) to VP of Administrative Affairs.
* VP of Administrative Affairs will review AVP submitted nominations for selection of winner(s).

## Give a High Five

Submit a "high five" to show your appreciation for a staff member or team within the Division of Administrative Affairs. A "high five" can be submitted for any appreciation or recognition you wish to share. Submitted high fives will be reviewed and posted on a monthly basis to the division's "high five" web page. Use the "submit a high five" link below to submit your "high five".
