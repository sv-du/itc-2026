# Announcements

## 2025 Cavin Family Traveling Fellowship: Registration open through March 27

**March 1, 2025** - For more than a decade, the Cavin Family Traveling Fellowship has been awarded to recent architecture graduates to give them the opportunity to independently travel abroad to study architecture.

This year's fellowship will award $50,000 for 16 weeks of travel and research, the theme of which is to be directed by the recipient.

**![2025 Cavin Fellowship poster](../../img/cavin/2025-cavin_poster-corrected-1.jpg)**The awarding of the fellowship is the result of a two-stage design competition held in June. It is eligible for recent graduates (Class of 2025) and early-career professionals up to the age of 35 from the architecture programs of Cal Poly Pomona and the University of Oregon.

**2025 Cavin Competition Schedule**

March 1: Registration Open

March 27: Registration Closes
March 28: Competition Starts
April 1: Submission Deadline
April 13: Finalists announced

May 9: Finalist Presentations
