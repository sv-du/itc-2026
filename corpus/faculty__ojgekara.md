Armed to Farm: Developing Training Programs For Military Veterans in Agriculture. J. Rural Soci. Sci. 29(2):82-93

* Nutritional and medicinal value of plant bioactive compounds to livestock
* Effect of summer/winter annual forages on animal performance
* Effect of concentrate supplements on performance of grazing livestock
