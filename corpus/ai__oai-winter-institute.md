## Learn, Grow, Inspire

### Cultivating Partnerships for Inclusive Academic Excellence

Since 2003, the Kellogg Honors College has fostered hands-on learning through community, civic engagement, and research—leading to high graduation rates and strong student outcomes. The 2026 Winter Institute will highlight KHC as a model for how collaboration advances inclusive excellence across Cal Poly Pomona.

## Institute Schedule & Registration

### Schedule

Wednesday, January 14th - Thursday, January 15th, 2026 in Building 162 Room 1001 from 8:30am - 4:00pm

### Key Themes

Inclusive mentorship, engaged learning, and meaningful connection are at the heart of the Kellogg Honors College model. Discover how community, belonging, and faculty partnerships create transformative student experiences.

### Participants will

Join colleagues from across campus to strengthen mentoring and engagement practices. Learn how collaboration, student leadership, and shared purpose can expand belonging, boost resilience, and enhance success for all Broncos.
