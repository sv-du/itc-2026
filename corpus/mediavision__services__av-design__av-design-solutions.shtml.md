# Services and Solutions

## Academic Spaces

### Classroom Upgrades

From basic setups to complete HyFlex installations that support hybrid and remote learning, we cover all the necessary technology planning for every discipline and make sure you have everything you need to make your lectures more about teaching and less about fiddling with your tools.

[Explore Classroom Options](av-classrooms.shtml)

![picture of a standard classroom](av-images/multiple-display-classroom.jpg)

![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white-mobile.svg)
![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white.svg)

## Non-academic spaces

### Conference Room Upgrades

Need remote viewing capabilities for collaborative spaces, or just simple quality of life enhancements to the technology within a room? We got you covered!

[Explore Conference Room Options](av-conference.shtml)

![a picture of a standard conference room](av-images/large-conference-room.jpg)

![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-green-mobile.svg)
![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-green.svg)

## Bulletins made easier

### Digital Signage

Sometimes setting up a bulletin board can be time consuming, and uses a lot of paper over time. If you want to go green, while also making it simpler to manage displaying event, support, deadlines and other information; digital signage is the way to go!

[Learn about digital signage](av-signage.shtml)

![picture of a standard digital signage install](av-images/digital-signage.jpg)

![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white-mobile.svg)
![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white.svg)

## simple enhancements

### Equipment Only Requests

This would encompass anything within either an Academic or Non-Academic space that does not specifically adhere to the previously mentioned upgrade options.
Due to the miscellaneous nature of one-off upgrades, if the requested device is not part of a standard classroom build, it will be subject to ATI/IT review and additional imposed lean times.

[About ATI/IT](https://www.cpp.edu/accessibility/procurement/index.shtml%20 "opens a new window")
