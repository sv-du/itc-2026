## Accessibility

### Accessible Technology Initiative @ CPP

For guidance in preparing accessible documents, social media, and websites visit our [Accessible Technology webpage](/accessibility/).

### ABBYY FineReader PDF

[ABBYY FineReader PDF](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ABBYY_FineReader) is an optical character recognition (OCR) application for PDF accessibility remediation by converting image documents like photos, scans, and screen captures into accessible file formats, including Microsoft Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Rich Text, PDF/A, searchable PDF, CSV and plain text files. FineReader is available for [Mac,](https://support.abbyy.com/hc/en-us/categories/360003097220) [Windows,](https://support.abbyy.com/hc/en-us/categories/360003111899) and [mobile devices.](https://support.abbyy.com/hc/en-us/categories/360003111959)

* Download and install ABBYY FineReader PDF using the [Software Center](https://cpp.service-now.com/ehelp?id=kb_article_view&sysparm_article=KB0016385&sys_kb_id=a9f15280474eae5094d59d12736d43e2&spa=1).
* Learn more about [Accessibility at CPP.](https://www.cpp.edu/accessibility/)
* Learn more about the CSU's [Accessible Technology Initiative (ATI).](https://ati.calstate.edu/)

### JAWS, ZoomText, and Fusion

JAWS, ZoomText, and Fusion are available on IT-managed Mac and Windows devices, including physical machines, computer labs, and virtual environments. Additionally, everyone with a with a current CPP email address can install Fusion software on up to two personal devices.

**About the Software**

* [JAWS](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/JAWS_%28screen_reader%29) is a screen reader providing text to speech and Braille output.
* [ZoomText](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ZoomText) is a screen magnifier that enlarges text and images.
* [Fusion Suite](https://vispero.com/fusion-accessibility-software/) integrates JAWS and ZoomText into a single software package.

**Resources**

* Download and install Fusion using the [Software Center.](https://cpp.service-now.com/ehelp?id=kb_article_view&sysparm_article=KB0016385&sys_kb_id=a9f15280474eae5094d59d12736d43e2&spa=1)
* View our [eHelp Article.](https://cpp.service-now.com/ehelp?id=kb_article_view&sysparm_article=KB0016418&sys_kb_id=dd90290447deb29094d59d12736d431d&spa=1)
* Learn more about [Accessibility at CPP.](https://www.cpp.edu/accessibility/)
* Learn more about the CSU's [Accessible Technology Initiative (ATI).](https://ati.calstate.edu/)

## Cybersecurity & Phishing Awareness

Cal Poly Pomona and affiliated service providers will never send you a text message or email asking for your credentials or that you provide an MFA authorization; such a request may be a phishing attempt. [**Learn more about protecting yourself from phishing and scams.**](https://cpp.service-now.com/ehelp?id=kb_article&sysparm_article=KB0001163&sys_kb_id=807b57bd874a75103c086e8e0ebb3575&spa=1)

**Never share your BroncoAccount credentials with anyone.**
