### About Subject Tutoring Workshops

An**exam review workshop** is designed to help students prepare for upcoming exams by reviewing key concepts.

A**concept review workshop** does not need to be tied to a specific exam; instead, they provide additional practice and deeper understanding of concepts that students commonly find challenging.

Fulfilling requests depends on tutor and room availability and is, therefore, **not guaranteed**.

To ensure tutors have adequate time to prepare, we kindly ask that workshop requests be submitted at least **five business days in advance** along with:

* A list of concepts or topics to be covered
* Relevant formulas, if any
* Practice problems
* A solution key (for tutor preparation only; this will not be shared with students)

Workshops are 50-minutes long, therefore, the tutor will ask students to prioritize concepts or problems to cover, as it may not be possible to cover all that are provided.

### About Writing Center Workshops

Faculty can request a workshop to be held in their class or at the LRC for the following content:

* Articles
* Developing and Organizing Essays
* Perfect Verb Tense
* Quoting, Paraphrasing, and Responding
* Simple Verb Tense
* Subject Verb Agreement

Fulfilling requests depends on tutor availability and is, therefore, **not guaranteed.**

To ensure tutors have adequate time to prepare, we kindly ask that workshop requests be submitted at least **five business days in advance.**

Workshops are 50-minutes long.
