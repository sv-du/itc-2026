### Foothill Family Shelter

×

**Brief Description of the Organization**: Foothill Family Shelter is a nonprofit organization which houses homeless families with children for a period of up to 120 days, free of rent and utility charges.

**Number of interns each semester**: Varies. Average range is 2-4 per semester. **Process and Requirements for Internship (e.g., TB test, interview, reference letters, etc.)**: Application process. Prospective interns must also attend a new volunteer orientation. They must be interviewed by the Volunteer Coordinator where class requirements are discussed. We also ask for a resume and letters of recommendation when applicable. Not all applicants are a fit for the organization and following these procedures does not mean the student will be granted the internship.

### Generation Her

×

**Brief Description of the Organization:** Generation Her is a non-profit organization operating in Southern California, that seeks to empower teenage mothers, ages 13-23, and their children by connecting them to a supportive community of other teen mothers and adult mentors, equipping them with desired life skills, and, ultimately, impacting their future in a positive way.

**No of interns each semester:**varies

### Jamboree Volunteer Info

×

Jamboree delivers high-quality affordable housing and services that transform lives and strengthen communities. With housing as the platform, we believe in the power of community to bring hope home to those starting out or starting over: working families, seniors, veterans, formerly homeless, and those with special needs. We build award-winning housing with LEED-certified quality they can afford, and provide supportive programs and services that instill both personal accomplishment and community pride. Jamboree's vision is to improve communities throughout California by developing a full range of quality housing affordable to everyone in a community, and provide supportive, life-enhancing services.

**Process and Requirements for Internship**: Application, Interview, Background Check
