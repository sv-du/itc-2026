# Partnering with Industry

## Preparing Students for Success

The CIS program has a “learn-by-doing” approach to teaching in which students participate in technology-based projects through service learning, internships and a mandatory group Senior Project with industry. This ensures that Cal Poly Pomona CIS students are prepared for important roles in the field.

The CIS department strives to have strong relationships within the Information Technology industry. Cal Poly Pomona students are highly sought after due to their practical knowledge and technical skills. If you are looking to hire interns, or seeking students for full-time positions, please contact CIS Department Chair Dr. Zhongming Ma.
