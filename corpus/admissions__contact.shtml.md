# Resources

## Connect with Us

Welcome to the Office of Admissions! We're thrilled to be one of your go-to resources as you pursue your academic journey to Cal Poly Pomona. Our dedicated team is committed to making your experience as smooth as possible, offering the support and assistance you need throughout the admission process. Be sure to explore our [online resources](https://www.cpp.edu/admissions/admission-counselors.shtml) and regularly check your [Bronco Applicant Portal](https://applicant.cpp.edu/)—this will ensure you stay updated with important next steps for your application. If you have questions or need assistance, don’t hesitate to reach out to us through the contact options provided below. We appreciate your patience as we experience a high volume of inquiries, and we look forward to helping you take the first steps in becoming a Bronco!

Got a question? We’re here to help! Reach out to us via email for the answers you need.

For general Inquiries, contact: admissions@cpp.edu

For questions about residency, contact: residency@cpp.edu

Returning students can connect with us at: returning@cpp.edu

For guidance on international admissions, contact: inter@cpp.edu

## Connect with us in "Real Time"

### Phone

The Office of Admissions is available for phone calls from Monday through Friday from 8 am to 5 pm. Please contact us at: (909) 869-5299

### Live Chat through our Chatbot

Live representatives are available to chat with you during the following days and times if the chatbot is unable to provide you with an answer. The Office of Admissions is available through Live Chat from Monday through Friday from 8 am to 5 pm.

### Walk-Ins

The Office of Admissions is available for Walk-Ins from Monday through Friday from 8 am to 5 pm. You can find the Office of Admissions in the Student Services Building (121) on the first floor at the orange counter.
