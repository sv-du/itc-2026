# Freshman Students

![Three Students in Koi Pond Area](../img/cpp-students.jpg)

## Application Process

Cal Poly Pomona wants to make the application process as simple as possible, which is why we have put together an application checklist that lets you know what you need and best practices when applying.

[Begin Your Application](https://www.calstate.edu/apply/freshman%20 "opens a new window")

## Before you Apply

Before you apply, we recommend that you carefully review the Cal Poly Pomona freshmen [admissions requirements](https://www.cpp.edu/admissions/freshmen/requirements.shtml) to be considered. Cal Poly Pomona is impacted at the campus level and for several of our majors/programs. Selection to all Cal Poly Pomona programs is competitive as we have more qualified applicants than we can admit. Cal Poly Pomona will consider you for admission to your alternate major if space is available. However, we cannot guarantee consideration for your alternate major/program. We prioritize applicants within the same academic college. Students who have been admitted should review the [Next Steps Checklist](https://www.cpp.edu/admissions/freshmen/admitted.shtml).

[View Application Status in Bronco Applicant Portal](https://applicant.cpp.edu/).

## Freshmen Application Checklist

### ✓ Explore Majors

Freshmen applicants must declare a major at the time of application. Visit our list of [Academic Programs.](https://www.cpp.edu/programs/index.shtml)

All undergraduate majors are open during the application period. Please explore majors offered by visiting [Cal State Apply.](https://www.calstate.edu/attend/degrees-certificates-credentials/Pages/search-degrees.aspx)

### ✓ Unofficial Transcripts

Have your unofficial transcript(s) on hand when filling out the application as you will be asked to enter all courses you have completed, those in progress and any courses you are planning on taking.

Most students can obtain their unofficial transcripts through their student portal from the High School that they attended or are currently attending.

### ✓ Biodemographic Information

* Make sure to have the correct Social Security Number as entering it incorrectly can impact your financial aid.
* Double-check that your name and date of birth are also entered correctly. Use an e-mail address that you will continue to be able to access. School e-mails are not recommended.

### ✓ Review Application Resources

Applicants are encouraged to review the following helpful resources when completing the Cal State Apply application.

* + [Cal State Apply Resource Page (PDF)](https://www.calstate.edu/apply/freshman)
  + [Freshmen Application Checklist (PDF)](https://www.calstate.edu/apply/freshman/Documents/application-checklist-freshman.pdf "Freshmen Application Checklist (PDF)")
  + [Freshmen Application Guide (PDF)](https://www.calstate.edu/apply/freshman/Documents/cal-state-apply-freshman-application-guide.pdf "Freshmen Application Guide (PDF)")
  + [Non CA resident app guide (PDF)](https://www.calstate.edu/apply/freshman/Documents/cal-state-apply-nonresident-guide.pdf "Non CA resident app guide (PDF)")

### ✓ Apply Online

Apply online for the Fall 2026 semester between October 1 - December 1, via [Cal State Apply](https://www2.calstate.edu/apply).

**Cal Poly Pomona makes admission decisions based on self-reported information from the application, therefore make sure that all of the information entered is accurate.**

Provisionally admitted students who intend to enroll at Cal Poly Pomona will be required to submit an official transcript from all institutions attended, when requested from the Admissions Office.

### ✓ Pay Application Fee

A $70 non-refundable application filing fee (or [fee waiver)](https://www2.calstate.edu/apply/paying-for-college/Pages/fee-waiver.aspx) must be paid at the time of application submission. Instructions for payment can be found on [Cal State Apply](https://help.liaisonedu.com/Cal_State_Apply_Applicant_Help_Center/Submitting_and_Monitoring_Your_Cal_State_Apply_Application/Before_and_After_You_Submit_Your_Cal_State_Apply_Application/01_Submitting_and_Completing_Your_Cal_State_Apply_Application).

### ✓ Check Application Status

The Office of Admissions will communicate your admission status and next steps via the [**Bronco Applicant Portal**](https://applicant.cpp.edu/ "Bronco Applicant Portal").  The portal will allow you to:

* **Monitor your application status**
* **Review your To Do List** for any required documents
* **Find financial aid information**
* Stay informed on **upcoming events**
* Access important **next steps** for your application

Stay tuned for more information in mid-October on accessing this new portal. **Please keep in mind that admission decisions will be made between February - April for Fall applicants**.

Those who have faced academic, financial or personal hardship and cannot attend CPP are encouraged to apply for our [Transfer Success Pathway (TSP)](https://www.cpp.edu/enrollment/resources/tsp.shtml "Transfer Success Pathway (TSP)").

**If you would like to withdraw your application, please submit the [withdrawal form](https://engage.cpp.edu/register/app_withdraw "Withdrawal Form")**.

### ✓ Help with the Bronco Applicant Portal

* Visit the [BroncoAccount Activation for Undergraduate Applicants](https://www.cpp.edu/it/2step/bronco-account-activation-for-undergraduate-applicants.shtml "BroncoAccount Activation for Undergraduate Applicants") page for detailed instructions on how to set up your account.
* Log into your personalized [Bronco Applicant Portal](https://applicant.cpp.edu/ "Bronco Applicant Portal") to view your application status and any action items. For more information, visit the [Bronco Applicant Portal Information](https://www.cpp.edu/admissions/bronco-applicant-portal.shtml "Bronco Applicant Portal Information") page.
