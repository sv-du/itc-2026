# ArcGIS Online Login

## Welcome to AGOL

CPP ArcGIS Online Organization
You may login at [www.arcgis.com](https://www.arcgis.com) or click the button.

[Login](https://www.arcgis.com "ArcGIS Online Login Page")

Login steps are available below.

## Step 1

\*If you are logging into any other Esri web or desktop app, please proceed to step 2.

![ArcGIS Online Homepage](../img/resources/online-steps/1_agol_homepage.jpg)After loading the login page, click on 'Sign In' at either the bottom or top right.

## Step 2

![Sign in login window](../img/resources/online-steps/2_agol_org.jpg)Click on the login option "your ArcGIS organization's URL".

## Step 3

![Sign in window with pomona in input field](../img/resources/online-steps/3_agol_pomona.jpg)Enter "pomona" into the empty field available.

## Step 4

![Sign in window with CPP button](../img/resources/online-steps/4_agol_button.jpg)Click on the CPP button.

## Step 5

![CPP login window](../img/resources/online-steps/5_cpp_login.jpg)Sign in with your CPP credentials as you normally would.
