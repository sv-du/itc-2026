# Administration of Grants and Contracts in Support of Sponsored Programs

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| Subject: | Administration of Grants and Contracts in Support of Sponsored Programs |
| Policy Number: | 1.9 |
| Date Adopted: | December, 2004 |
| Last Revised: |  |

## Policy Statement:

This policy is issued in accordance with Executive Order No. 890 and applies to all Sponsored Programs applied for, awarded to and/or administered by the campus or its auxiliaries.

## Implementation:

[Office of Research and Sponsored Programs](https://www.cpp.edu/research/sponsored-program/index.shtml)
