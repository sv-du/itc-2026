## About Me

**Professor, teaching Organic Chemistry lecture and laboratory classes.**In addition to authoring an introductory organic synthesis [textbook](http://www.amazon.com/dp/0470484098/ref%3Drdr_ext_tmb) ([2nd edition](https://www.wiley.com/en-us/Introduction%2Bto%2BStrategies%2Bfor%2BOrganic%2BSynthesis%2C%2B2nd%2BEdition-p-9781119347248)). I have developed online [lab animations & tutorials](https://www.chemistryconnected.com/ochemlab/index.html), online prelab quizzes, organic chemistry "[clicker](https://www.chemistryconnected.com/local/index.html)" questions, and [online organic chemistry lectures](https://www.educator.com/chemistry/organic-chemistry/starkey/) for Educator.com. As you can probably tell, I am a strong proponent for using [technology](https://www.chemistryconnected.com/references.html) to support student success, and I have created a repository of active learning resources for Organic Chemistry, called [LOCAL](https://www.chemistryconnected.com/local/index.html). I served as a peer reviewer for [MERLOT](http://www.merlot.org/), and I enjoy giving chemistry career [presentations](https://www.chemistryconnected.com/ChemCareers.pps). Please visit my YouTube channel [ChemistryConnected](http://www.youtube.com/user/ChemistryConnected), see my [OrganicERS](https://www.organicers.org/?q=?q=content/laurie-starkey-cal-poly-pomona) spotlight, and visit my collection of [Video Creation & Teaching resources](https://www.chemistryconnected.com/references.html) and [COVID-19 Remote-Teaching resources](https://www.chemistryconnected.com/covid19/index.html)
