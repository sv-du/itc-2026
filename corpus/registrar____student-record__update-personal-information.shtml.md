# Update Contact/Personal Data

## Using BroncoDirect

**Your BroncoDirect: Using Student Center provides an easy way to access, view, and manage your Student Account online, if you are an active student.  You can update your personal information such as address, emergency contact, phone number, and email address on your student record.**

If you are no longer an active student, contact the Registrar’s Office via email at registrar@cpp.edu

![BD Update or phone or via email](../img/update_contact.jpg)

## Your Student Record Data

### Contact Information - Address/Phone Number/Email

As an active student, you may update your address, phone number, and email address via Bronco Direct Student Center.  View [Instructions on how to change contact information](https://cpp.service-now.com/ehelp?id=kb_article&sys_id=efd10fd7db7e94909bba68461b9619de) through BroncoDirect.

If you are no longer an active student, contact the Registrar’s Office via email at registrar@cpp.edu.

**Update your Address:**

*Home:* your home or permanent address, used by Human Resources department

*Mailing:* your current mailing address, used to mail official correspondence

Click the instructions on [How to Update your Address (PDF)](update-personal-info__address-bpg.pdf).

1. To view, add or change an address, **log into**[**BroncoDirect**](https://my.cpp.edu/uPortal/p/broncodirect.ctf1/max/render.uP).
2. **Click** on the **Student Center** link under the Student Self Services menu.
3. To change an address, click on the **edit** box next to the address to be changed
4. Enter the new address in the highlighted field, click **OK**
5. Verify the information and click the **Save** button

**Update your Telephone Number:**

*Cellular:* your mobile phone number if applicable

*Home*: your home phone number

*Main:* your primary phone number for contacts during office hours

*Business:* your work phone number

Click the instructions on [How to Update your Telephone Number (PDF)](update-personal-info-telephone_num_bpg.pdf).

1. Click on the **Add** a Phone Number button
2. Select the **Phone Type** from the drop-down menu
3. Enter the telephone number including the area code
4. Check the **Preferred** box to indicate your primary contact number
5. Click the **Save** button

**Change your Email Address:**

*Home:* your personal email address if applicable

*Campus*: your Cal Poly Pomona email address that ends with @cpp.edu. Official emails from the university will use this email address.

Click the instructions on [How to Update your Email Address (PDF)](update-personal-info_email_address_bpg.pdf).

1. 1. Click on the Email Address link.
   2. **Click** the **Add an Email Address** box.
   3. **Select** the **Email Type**.
   4. **Enter** the **new email address**.
   5. Check the **Preferred** box to indicate your primary email
   6. **Click** the **Save** button.

### Diploma/Affirmed Name Change

**Diploma/Affirmed Name Change**

Cal Poly Pomona recognized that some students may wish to use a different First and Middle name other than their primary name to display on their diploma.  An approved Diploma/Affirmed First and Middle Name is used only on a printed diploma.

A diploma's first and middle name must consist of alphabetical characters, hyphens, and spaces. Cal Poly Pomona reserves the right to deny or remove, with or without notice, a Diploma/Affirmed First and Middle Name when used for inappropriate purposes, including but not limited to misrepresentation, avoiding legal obligation, offensive or derogatory language, or perpetrating fraud. Inappropriation of a Diploma/Affirmed Name may be a Student Code of Conduct violation and referred to the Dean of Students Office. When submitting an Affirmed/Diploma Name change, view instructions through BroncoDirect Student Center.

*You can update your Diploma/Affirmed Name in BroncoDirect Student Center.*

1. Log in to **MyCPP** and open the Self-Service - **BroncoDirect - Student Center**.
2. Click **Personal Information**.
3. Select **My Name**
   1. Under **Name type** drop down, click "**Diploma**" name type, click on the **Add** button.
   2. Enter your Request Diploma First Name and Middle Name.
   3. If you do not have a Preferred/Affirmed First or Middle Name, leave the field BLANK.
      1. Do not enter a period (.) or any other symbol, as the request will be denied.
      2. **Note**: Last Name cannot be edited through Self-Service.
4. Click **SAVE**.
5. After the confirmation page pops-up, click **OK**.
   1. Your request will be routed to the Registrar's Office for approval.
   2. **Requests will be processed within three (3) business days**.

**Diploma/Affirmed Name FAQ**

*1. What is a Preferred/Affirmed Name?*

A Preferred/Affirmed Name is a first and/or middle name that is commonly used that differs from an individual’s legal first name. Unlike the legal name changes in the campus systems, it must be processed by the Registrar’s Office or by Human Resources. Preferred/Affirmed Name changes are changed via the online Student Center and do not require any supporting documentation.

*2. What is a Diploma/Affirmed Name?*

A Diploma/Affirmed Name is a first and/or middle name used to display on a student’s diploma that differs from an individual’s legal first name and other Preferred/Affirmed Name. Unlike the legal name changes in the campus systems, it must be processed by the Registrar’s Office or by Human Resources. Diploma/Affirmed Name changes are updated via the online Student Center and do not require any supporting documentation.

A Diploma/Affirmed Name differs from a standard Preferred/Affirmed Name in that it does not appear on class rosters, Canvas, etc., only on your diploma.

*3*. *Can I change my Diploma/Affirmed Name to whatever I want?*

Students can use any Preferred/Affirmed First and/or Middle Name that may consist of alphabetical characters, hyphens, and spaces. Cal Poly Pomona reserves the right to deny or remove, with or without notice, a Diploma/Affirmed First and Middle Name when used for inappropriate purposes, including but not limited to misrepresentation, avoiding legal obligation, offensive or derogatory language, or perpetrating fraud. Inappropriate use of a Diploma/Affirmed Name may be a Student Code of Conduct violation and referred to the Dean of Students Office.

*4. How do I change the name displayed on my diploma to include accents or special characters?*

Requests for accents, special characters, and corrections to spacing or case lettering that would apply only towards the name displayed on the diploma can be submitted using the Diploma Name Request Form via the diplomas@cpp.edu email.

*5. Where would my Diploma/Affirmed Name appear?*

* Students’ Preferred/Affirmed N*ame* will show on class rosters, BroncoDirect, online Directory Information, Canvas, identification card, and name on the email address.
* Students' Diploma/Affirmed F*irst and Middle Name* is used only on the printed diploma.

*6. Will my Diploma/Affirmed Name appear on any official records?*

No, a Diploma/Affirmed Name is not the same as primary/legal name and it will not be used on any official records.

Only primary/legal name will appear on all official student records such as transcripts, financial aid records, and payroll.

*7. Can I change my email address to match my Diploma/Affirmed Name?*

Students that changed their legal name or identity can request a change to their CPP User Name, which will change their email address. Contact the IT Help Desk for such change.

A Diploma/Affirmed Name change will not change the email address but the Preferred/Affirmed Name, not the Diploma/Affirmed Name, will show as the student name on the email.

*8. May I request a CPP ID card with my Diploma/Affirmed Name?*

Bronco ID Cards issued on the name are active at the time of printing.  Re-issuing a card may be assessed at the standard replacement card fee.  Contact the  [Bronco One Card Office Services](https://broncoonecard.com/bronco-bucks/) for details.

*9. How long will it take to update my Diploma/Affirmed Name on BroncoDirect and Canvas?*

Once updated in PeopleSoft, the student’s Diploma/Affirmed Name will appear on the Class Roster, BroncoDirect, and Canvas after three (3) business days.

*10. Who should I contact, if I have additional questions?*

Students may contact the Registrar’s Office at registrar@cpp.edu.

### Personal Information Update

**Personal Information Update (i.e. Primary Name/SSN/Birthdate)**

CPP must protect the identity of a student and maintain the integrity of their record when changing their personal information including legal/primary name (as it appears on official document), social security number, or birthdate.  Students requesting to change their personal information must submit **two** Government-Issued documentation that support their request and complete a [Change of Information Form (PDF)](../forms/change-of-information.pdf). Upload the *Change of Information Form* along with valid copies of supporting documentation via the [secure file upload system](https://polyform.cpp.edu/Loginformproc/UnityForm.aspx?d1=AQvsCu%2baKwHEe9Mv9EddnAxd4ROfmrmVKrUR9jwbYZ7qFFsUMfS1yehyL6lmtvCoPRxbjq8XvSHU2fVZ8mE7q5xWinc43uPra1bDvpS4prg%2fOL3YgON%2fSgHgkJnkh%2by69BAHJO%2fD9KIZcuy2g3bWMzinAgsxh82wWRTZe4rv1G%2fHabF8U0YnX1XEY1AmEi2DH%2bdlk2Kq4EuWIGZOVFsdmju1yhbx%2bLPf%2fjIFjg8I%2bJuA).

If you are no longer an active student, contact the Registrar’s Office via email at registrar@cpp.edu

The following documents will support personal information update requests:

* Marriage Certificate
* A valid State Driver's License**\***
* A valid State Identification Card
* U.S. or State-Issued Court Order
* Naturalization/Citizenship documents with photo attached
* Signed copy of U.S. Social Security Card
* Birth Certificate
* Signed copy of a valid Government-Issued Passport

**\*** If an out of state driver's license or foreign Passport is submitted, a U.S. Social Security Card is required.

**NOTE**:  *Bronco ID Cards are issued based on the "Preferred/Affirmed" Name active at the time of printing.  Re-issuing cards will be assessed at the standard replacement card fee.  Ensure to check that your desired name is active in BroncoDirect prior to submitting a card request.  Contact the [Bronco Card office](https://broncoonecard.com/bronco-bucks/) for details.*

***How do I change my Primary Name in BroncoDirect to include accents or special characters?***

Requests for accents, special characters, and corrections to spacing or case lettering that would apply to the Primary Name in BroncoDirect would also require documentation (i.e., foreign passport or birth certificate) and can be submitted via the registrar@cpp.edu email.

*8. How long will it take to update my Preferred/Affirmed Name on BroncoDirect and Canvas?*

Once the student’s Preferred/Affirmed Name is updated in PeopleSoft, it will appear on the Class Roster, BroncoDirect, and Canvas after 3 business days.

*9. Who should I contact, if I have additional questions?*

Students may email the Registrar’s Office at [registrar@cpp.edu](https://www.cpp.edu/registrar/index.shtml).

### Pronouns

**Pronouns**

CPP offers students the option to specify their preferred pronouns, which will show up on class rosters for faculty.

Click the instruction on [How to Set or Update your Pronouns (PDF)](how-to-set-pronouns-bpg.pdf) in BroncoDirect using Student Center.

1. Log in to [MyCPP](https://my.cpp.edu/uPortal/f/home/normal/render.uP "MyCPP") and open the Student Center within BroncoDirect
2. In the Personal Information section, click the Pronouns link
3. Select a Pronoun from the drop-down list
4. If selecting Other, enter the Subject/Object/Possessive entries\* in the fields
5. Click Save

Check out these Frequently Asked Questions for more Pronoun usage information.

**Pronouns FAQs**

1. What are Preferred Pronouns?

Preferred pronouns are those that an individual chooses for themself and wants others to use when talking to or about them.

2. Can I set or change my pronouns to whatever I want?

You can set preferred pronouns to be used on class rosters for faculty. The University reserves the right to remove a pronoun if it is inappropriate or being used for misrepresentation or to evade legal obligations. Inappropriate use of a pronouns may be a violation of the Student Code of Conduct and will be referred to the Dean of Students Office.

3. Will pronouns be used for everything at the University?

Right now, preferred pronouns are only being used in PeopleSoft on the class roster and faculty advising pages. If you'd like to see pronoun preferences added somewhere, please let us know!

4. Am I required to set a pronoun?

No. Using a pronoun is entirely optional. You can change this at any time.

5. I still have questions. Who can I talk to?

You can contact the [Registrar's Office](../contact-us.shtml).

**\*The University reserves the right to remove a pronoun if it is inappropriate or being used for misrepresentation or to evade legal obligations. Inappropriate use of a pronouns may be a violation of the Student Code of Conduct and will be referred to the Dean of Students Office.**
