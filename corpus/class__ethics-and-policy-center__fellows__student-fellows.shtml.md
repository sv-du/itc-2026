# Student Ethics and Policy Fellows

Each year, CCEP selects a handful of qualified university students to serve as Ethics and Policy Fellows. Student Fellows spend the year investigating CCEP’s [annual theme](../annual-ccep-themes.shtml). Fellows participate in an intensive thematic seminar, interact with ethics and policy experts, learn about community initiatives related to the theme, and contribute to a student research conference.

We are inviting applications for our 2025-2026 fellowship on **Futures of Work**.  Please see [the call for student fellows](../ccep-call-for-student-fellows-2025-26-futures-of-work.pdf) for details.  Deadline for application is Friday October 3rd and applicants will be notified by the end of the month.  Contact Dr. Alex Madva with any questions.
