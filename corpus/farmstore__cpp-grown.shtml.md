# Cal Poly Pomona Grown

![man picking fruits from tree, bellpeppers, bottles of wines, corn](/farmstore/img/cpp-grown-banner.jpg)

Our selection of produce changes with the seasons. To stay in the loop about our latest offerings, **[join our email list](https://visitor.constantcontact.com/manage/optin?v=001FJi_a6DK_uMdRxd2RWKtmV1n6g0thBM6)** or follow us on **[Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/thefarmstorecpp/)** or **[Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/CalPolyPomonaFarmstore/)**. For real-time availability, please contact us directly at (909) 869-4906.

## Winter

* Satsuma mandarins
* Navel oranges
* Lettuce
* Kale
* Cauliflower
* Spinach
* Carrots
* Avocados (Bacon, Zutano, Fuerte, Pinkerton)
* Grapefruit
* Variegated lemons
* Cilantro
* Celery

## Spring

* Pixie mandarins
* Valencia oranges
* Lemons
* Asparagus
* Artichokes

## Summer

* Summer squash
* Tomatoes
* Peppers
* Cucumbers
* Eggplant
* Corn
* Watermelon
* Dragon Fruit
* Avocados (Hass, Reed, Lamb Hass)
* Ruby Red grapefruit

## Fall

* Persimmons
* Butternut squash
* Guava
* Pomegranate
* Lime
