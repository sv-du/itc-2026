# ECS Practicum Orientation

For all students who wish to take ECS 2100/2100A and/or ECS 4100/4100A, please review all information on this page. This site can be used at any time in any semester, however there is a hard due date for applications to be considered for the next semester:

The due dates for practicum application will remain the same each year:

For placement in the **Spring** semester, applications can open **July 1st** and close **September 1st** at **11:59pm.**

For placement in the **Summer or Fall** semesters, applications can open **December 1st** and close **February 1st** at **11:59pm.**

**Late applications will not be accepted.**

**Required Clearance Documentation**

1. A valid, non-expired, CTC-issued [Certificate of Clearance](https://www.ctc.ca.gov/credentials/leaflets/certificate-of-clearance-%28cl-900%29) or [Child Development Permit](https://www.ctc.ca.gov/credentials/req-child-dev) is required.  Details are included on the application and in links below.
2. Childhood [vaccination verification](https://www.cpp.edu/health/immunizations/index.shtml) is also required. Booster shots for Tdap must fall within 10 years and remain valid through the entire practicum semester.
3. TB Clearance cannot be older than 1 year from the time your practicum semester ends.
4. Flu shot verification or a decline statement (available in the canvas orientation) must be submitted.
5. Completion of relevant coursework requirements are listed on the practicum application.

**[View the application process](ecsdocumentfolder/ecs-practicum-application-infographic-4.30.2024.pdf "ECS Practicum Application Infographic 4.30.2024")**

The orientation below is required for all students who wish to take ECS 2100/2100A and/or ECS 4100/4100A. At the end of the orientation, the link to the Practicum application will be given.

If you have questions about the application process, contact Jeanne Reynaga at the Credential Services Office.

**[View the Canvas orientation](https://cpp.instructure.com/courses/30915)**

 \*\*Completing the Canvas orientation **is only the first step** to a complete practicum application. If you complete this orientation on the application due date, you WILL NOT make the deadline. It is suggested students complete this Orientation *at least* one month before the application deadline.

**Requests to Change a Previously Submitted Application**

If you have already applied and need to request a change to the start term or to withdraw your application completely, please email Karina Sarna in Placement Services. The Credential Services Office should respond within 48-72 hours, excluding weekends.
