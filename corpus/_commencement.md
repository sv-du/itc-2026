## Commencement Schedule

Cal Poly Pomona's Commencement ceremonies will take place in the University Quad on May 15-17, 2026. Schedule will be shared by early December.

![College of Environmental Design graduates smile during the 2025 commencement ceremony. ](img/25env-grad_48-1.png)

The university will hold 12 ceremonies over three days, Friday through Sunday. The four larger colleges - Business Administration, CLASS, Engineering, Science - will each hold two ceremonies. All ceremonies will be about 90 minutes long.
