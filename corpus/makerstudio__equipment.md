# Tools & Equipment

## Team Collaboration Station

### Flip Chart

A flip chart is a large digital whiteboard that can be used as an interactive presentation screen, a massive illustration board, and much more! Great for doodling.

## 2D Design & Fabrication Station

### AxiDraw

An AxiDraw is a pen plotter machine that can use a variety of writing instruments to create precise renditions of text, shapes, or drawings on nearly any flat surface. With this, you can draw out complex designs and write beautiful messages with any pen!

### AlienWare Hardware

AlienWare computers, keyboards, and laptops- ideal for gaming, free to use.

### Digital Sewing Machine

A digital sewing machine has a built-in computer to control different motors, and small monitor displays for ease of operation. Along with its functions as a regular sewing machine, it can also create beautiful embroidered designs.

### Dremel DigiLab - Laser Cutter

A laser cutter is a machine that uses a high-power laser to cut materials, resulting in high quality, dimensionally-accurate cuts. Used for industrial manufacturing or etching your own designs into different materials. With this machine, you can cut out and engrave almost anything in 2D!

### Singer Professional 5 - Serger

A serger is a sewing machine that is used for overcasting to prevent materials from fraying at the edges; it can also sew, trim, and finish a seam in a single step.

### Silhouette Cameo - Vinyl Cutter

A vinyl cutter is a computer-controlled machine that uses a sharp blade to cut out shapes and letters from sheets of vinyl. This can be used to produce stickers, or designs to be pressed into shirts, mugs, steel canisters, and more!

### Letterpress

A letterpress uses metal letters, woodcut type, or polymer plates arranged on a frame and rolled in ink to produce a print. With this device, you can ink press lettering and typography onto paper!

### Light Box Tracer

A light box is a flat surface backlit by LEDs. It is used for tracing or drawing with a reference, and is ideal for illustrators or animators.

## 3D Technology Center

### 3D Printer

A 3D printer is a manufacturing device that can create physical objects from a three-dimensional digital model using a custom material. Great for creating parts & components, figurines, cosplay gear, keychains, and more!

### AlienWare Hardware

AlienWare computers, keyboards, and laptops- ideal for gaming, free to use.

### 3D System Sense 2 Scanner

A 3D scanner is an imaging device that collects distance point measurements from a real-world object and translates them into a virtual 3D object.
