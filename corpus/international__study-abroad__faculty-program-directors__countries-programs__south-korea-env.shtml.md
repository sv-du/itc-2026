# Art, Design and Culture in Korea

![](seoul.jpg)

## About the Program

Experience Seoul — a vibrant global hub where tradition meets innovation! Recognized by UNESCO as a Creative City of Design, Seoul is the perfect place to explore how art, technology, and culture connect.

**Program Highlights**

* Explore Seoul’s Creative Scene:
  + Visit top museums, galleries, and design districts that showcase Korea’s unique mix of history and modern creativity.

* Connect with Local Artists & Designers:
  + Tour art departments at local universities, an animation studio, and an XR (extended reality) studio.

* Dive into Korean Culture:
  + Visit historic landmarks like Gyeongbokgung Palace and Bukchon Hanok Village
  + Join a kimchi-making workshop at the Kimchi Museum
  + Experience a peaceful temple stay at Jogye Temple

* Hands-On Creative Project: Capture your journey by producing a short video, animation, or motion graphic inspired by your time in Seoul, due as a final project.

Don’t miss this exciting opportunity to study, create, and explore in one of the world’s most dynamic cities!

**Program Dates: Summer 2026.**

Two-Week Summer Study Abroad Program to Seoul, Korea

June 11-June 25, 2026

Open to all majors

Eligibility: minimum GPA 2.5

For more information, please contact Professors Jian Lee at jianlee@cpp.edu and Jeffrey T Ray at jtray@cpp.edu
