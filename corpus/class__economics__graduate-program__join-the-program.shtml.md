# Join the Program

![Join the Program Clip Art](/class/economics/img/join-the-program.png)

The Economics Master program is designed to ensure that most students who take three classes a week, can complete the program in 4 semesters.

Most classes are offered in the evenings. The class size is small (usually 5-15 students) and is in a seminar style. It is recommended that new graduate students start in the Fall quarter although it is not required.

An applicant for admission to this program must hold a bachelor's degree from an accredited college or university and satisfy university and departmental requirements for admission to graduate study. An applicant who holds a bachelor's degree in a field other than economics. In undergraduate work, the applicant must have maintained a grade point average of 2.7 overall. Admission to the graduate program in economics requires that the applicant be accepted by the Department of Economics.

If you are interested, please click below and provide your information

[**Graduate Program Interest Form**](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSfWm1oXDSiNiVkTjOqJFx3XOZ11Aa9YYd3q4NIWSDV_kjsavg/viewform)

We will provide you with more detailed information and keep you informed about our program.
