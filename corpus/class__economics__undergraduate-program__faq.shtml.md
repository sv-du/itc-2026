# FAQ

![faq question marks](/class/economics/img/faq.png)

**Below are frequently asked questions (click to see answers)**

## Academic Advising:

Expand/Collapse All for faq number 1

## Where can I receive advising regarding university-wide issues?

The Bronco Advising Center:

<https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/bronco-advising-center/index.shtml>

## Where can I receive advising regarding College-level issues such as General Education requirements, substitution of a higher grade for a lower grade earned, etc.?

The College Student Success Center:

<https://www.cpp.edu/class/student-success-center/index.shtml>

## Where can I receive advising regarding economics department issues?

Email the Department Chair, currently Bruce Brown (bbrown@cpp.edu)

## What is needed to graduate?

Complete all requirements as listed on your Degree Progress Report (DPR) also Shown on the posted Curriculum Sheets:

<https://www.cpp.edu/academic-programs/academic-advising/tools/sheets-roadmaps/2020-2021/class.shtml>

## Where can I find current information about graduation requirements?

The University Catalog:

[https://catalog.cpp.edu](https://catalog.cpp.edu/index.php)

## How can I change my option?

File an online petition under ("registration records forms")

<https://www.cpp.edu/registrar/forms/forms.shtml>

## How can I change my major?

File an online petition under ("registration records forms")

<https://www.cpp.edu/registrar/forms/forms.shtml>

First check with the prospective new major
