# Student Innovation Idea Labs

![Student presenting a project on a large screen in a modern classroom setting, with peers seated and observing attentively.](img/general-photos/siil-8.jpg)

![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white-mobile.svg)
![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white.svg)

## About Us

### We Are Innovators

We're builders, inventors, and entrepreneurs. We make stuff, and we're here to help you do the same. With the Student Innovation Idea Labs (SIIL), you can find all the resources you need to bring your ideas to life!

![An array of 3D printers](img/Maker Studio Images/img_7716.jpg)

## Our Spaces

[![A whiteboard advertising the Maker Studio](img/Maker Studio Images/img_7722.jpg)

### Maker Studio

Our most popular space! We have 3D printers, laser engravers, sewing machines, and more, all for free for students to use. It's also a great place to study, relax, or chat with like-minded people.](our-spaces/maker-studio.shtml "opens a new window")

[![study area in the iLab](img/iLab Images/20241213_141054.jpg)

### iLab

Need a chill place to study? The iLab's got you covered. A quiet area that students are free to study at, and clubs can reserve for free for whatever they need.](our-spaces/ilab.shtml "opens a new window")

[![The laser cutter, CNC router, and manual mill that live in the iLab](https://www.cpp.edu/io/img/about/20180228_103425.jpg)

### Innovation Orchard (IO)

Equipment in the Maker Studio not enough for you? Come to the IO, where you can really start building. Equipped with all the tools deemed too loud or dangerous for a library, here you can bring ideas to reality.](our-spaces/innovation-orchard.shtml "opens a new window")

![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-green-mobile.svg)
![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-green.svg)

## Want to use our Equipment?

SIIL offers training on a variety of different equipment. From 3D Printers to Sewing Machines, SIIL has the resources to get you ready to use our equipment.

[Learn More about Training](equipment-training.shtml)

![Interior of a modern innovation lab with collaborative workstations, high stools, wall-mounted monitors, and large windows providing natural light.](img/spaces/ilab1.jpg)
