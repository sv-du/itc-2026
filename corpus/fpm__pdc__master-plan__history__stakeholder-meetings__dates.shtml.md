# Stakeholder Meetings

**Date:** January 22, 2018

**Time:** 9 a.m. to 10 a.m.

**Information:** Members of the President's Cabinet met with master plan consulting firm Ayers Saint Gross to provide a progress update and overview of previously held workshops.

 [• PowerPoint Presentation (PDF)](../../../docs/01.22.2018.cpp-presidentscabinet_presentation_handout.pdf)

**Date:** January 23, 2018

**Time:**8 a.m. to 4 p.m.

**Information:** The Transportation Advisory Committee and members from Associated Students Inc., university housing, Bronco athletics, health services, childcare center, and student groups met with master plan consulting firm Ayers Saint Gross for individual sessions throughout the day. The topics addressed involved issues that are of particular importance to stakeholders.

**Date:** February 28, 2018

**Time:**4 to 5 p.m.

**Location:** University Library

**Information:** Representatives of the University Library met with Ayers Saint Gross and addressed issues and concerns that are unique to the facility.

**Date:** March 1, 2018

**Time:**8 to 12 p.m.

**Location:** BSC - Centaurus Room

**Information:**The master plan consulting firm met with members of University Police, Office of Student Life & Cultural Centers, and dining to discuss issues that are priorities to students and stakeholders.

**Date:** March 7, 2018

**Time:** 4 to 5 p.m.

**Location:** George P. Hart Academic Senate Offices

**Information:** Members of the Academic Senate were given an overview of the campus master planning process by Ayers Saint Gross. The Campus Master Plan will play a key role along with the Strategic Plan and the Academic Master Plan in the future path of the university.

 [• PowerPoint Presentation (PDF)](../../../docs/CPP-Academic-Senate-Overview-March-2018.pdf "March 7, 2018")

**Date:** March 28, 2018

**Time:**  11 a.m. to 2 p.m.

**Information:**The master plan consulting firm met with members of Registrar's Office, Registrar, Scheduling & Instructional Space, and Governmental Affairs to discuss issues that are priorities to students and stakeholders.

**Date:** April 11, 2018

**Time:**  12 p.m. to 3:30 p.m.

**Information:**The master plan consulting firm met with members of IT, Kellogg West Conference Center, Bookstore, and Alumni Association to discuss issues that are priorities to students and stakeholders.

**Date:** April 23, 2018

**Information:** Members of the President's Cabinet met with master plan consulting firm Ayers Saint Gross to provide updates on progress and timeline. Discussion included space analytics and an overview of previously held workshops.

 • [PowerPoint Presentation (PDF)](../../../docs/04.23.2018.cpp-presidentscabinet_presentation.pdf)

**Date:** June 25, 2018

**Information:** The master plan consulting firm met with the President to discuss space utilization analytics.

 • [PowerPoint Presentation (PDF)](../../../docs/6.25.2018.cpp-preliminary-utilization-review.pdf)

**Date:** August 6, 2018

**Time:** 12 to 2 p.m.

**Information:** The master plan consulting firm held a concepts work session with the President.

 • [PowerPoint Presentation (PDF)](../../../docs/08.06.2018.cpp-pres-concepts_worksession.pdf)

**Dates:** October 26 and November 30, 2018

**Information:** The master plan consulting firm met with the President to discuss master plan strategies for specific sites.

 • [PowerPoint Presentation (PDF)](../../../docs/10.26.2018.cpp-presidents-studies-update.pdf)

 • [PowerPoint Presentation (PDF)](../../../docs/11.30.2018.cpp-presidents-studies-update.pdf)

**Date:** January 24, 2019

**Time:** Noon to 1:30 p.m.

**Location:** Conference Room C. Building 81

**Information:** Transportation Advisory Committee members received updates on the latest measures to address challenging transportation and parking issues facing the campus, including the possible construction of additional parking structures, creation of a bike corridor through the heart of campus and the promotion of alternative modes of transportation. A Metro Rail substation near the university is in the works.

 [• PowerPoint Presentation (PDF)](../../../docs/tac-presentation-jan24.pdf "January 24, 2019")

**Date:** February 4, 2019

**Information:**  Members of the President's Cabinet met with master plan consulting firm Ayers Saint Gross. Topics included the master plan process, schedule, and draft plans.

                • [PowerPoint Presentation (PDF)](../../../docs/02.04.2019_cpp-mp_presidents-cabinet-update_final.pdf)

**Date:** February 20, 2019

**Time:** 9 to 11 a.m.

**Location:** West Multipurpose Room #1969, Student Services Building

**Information:** The Provost's Council, which consists of college deans, received an update on the Campus Master Plan from consulting firm Ayers Saint Gross. Among the issues addressed were the Master Plan timeline, transportation and parking, pedestrian and bike corridors, and space utilization in the Bronco Student Center. Ayers Saint Gross offered findings in a campus space utilization analysis in a second presentation.

 [• PowerPoint Presentation 1 (PDF)](../../../docs/feb20-provost-council-update.pdf "February 20, 2019 1")

 [• PowerPoint Presentation 2 (PDF)](../../../docs/feb20-space-utilization.pdf "February 20, 2019 2")

**Date:** March 22, 2019

**Time:** 3:30 to 5 p.m.

**Location:** West Multipurpose Room #1969, Student Services Building

**Information:** Campus Master Plan from consulting firm Ayers Saint Gross briefed the University Leadership Council, which is composed of college deans and a core group of campus administrators, on the current phase of the process. Topics addressed included the Master Plan timeline, campus pedestrian environment, transportation and parking issues, campus space utilization, academic hubs, and the future of the CLA tower.

 [• PowerPoint Presentation (PDF)](../../../docs/leadership-council-cpp-mp_ulcouncil_21mar2019_web.pdf "March 22, 2019")
