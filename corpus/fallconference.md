# Fall Conference

## President’s Welcome

In her welcome video to open the 2025-26 academic year, Interim President Iris S. Levine shares a message of celebration, collaboration and purpose. Watch the video or read the [transcript (PDF)](documents/fall-conference-video-transcript-2025.pdf).

## Welcome to a New Year

Fall Conference is a week-long series of events, August 18-22, to celebrate the start of a new academic year at Cal Poly Pomona. This schedule webpage lists college and division meetings, workshops and other events for faculty and staff.
