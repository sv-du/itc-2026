# Early Career: First Six Years

## Faculty Success Program

### Tenure-Track Faculty Success Program (summer course design, orientation, and institutional know how presentations)

New tenure-track faculty success begins with the remote summer Course Design experience provided by the Center for Advancement in Faculty Excellence (CAFE). This program provides faculty with connection to other new faculty and initiates and fosters discussions on scholarship of effective teaching and learning at the university level. This program empowers new faculty to design courses informed by knowledge of Cal Poly Pomona’s students, mission, and values. A three-day in-person orientation is provided for new tenure-track faculty the week before their first semester begins.

Throughout the first year Institutional Know-How Zooms on useful topics such as spending startup funds, research support, Library resources, preparing RTP documents, and understanding CPP policies are provided along with an end of the academic lunch reunion.

[For additional resources visit YourLife@CPP](https://www.cpp.edu/yourlife)

## Individual Consultations & Mentoring for All Faculty

**“Everybody’s Mentor”** — Confidential, one-on-one support offered by Ferp (Faculty Early Retirement Program) Faculty Fellows — is here to help you navigate your academic journey. Whether you're new to campus or a seasoned faculty member, we’re here to support you with:

* Career Development and Planning
* Grant proposal guidance
* Understanding university systems, processes and policies
* RTP (Reappointment, Tenure, and Promotion)
* Classroom strategies and teaching questions
* Interest in leadership and service positions
* Work-life balance

## Lecturer Orientation

Our New Lecturer Faculty Orientation is a time to learn about the university and meet new colleagues. A small stipend is available for completion of the orientation.

### Outcomes

* Form relationships with other lecturers that lead to productive collaborations and enjoyable friendships
* Collect a variety of information about Cal Poly Pomona’s priorities, resources, policies, procedures, and opportunities in an easy-to-retrieve format
* Feel valued and welcomed

The orientation covers logistics such as contract issues, California faculty union information, payroll, and evaluation.

The orientation includes best practices on creating an inclusive and equitable classroom to promote student success including syllabus examples and CPP’s learning management system, canvas.

Leave knowing more about the many resources available to enhance yours and your students’ experience at Cal Poly Pomona.

## Parenting Faculty Support Group

Faculty created a Parenting Faculty Support Group to provide the following:

* Holistic support (professional and personal) to enhance productivity and satisfaction
* Connection with other parenting faculty
* Advocacy and support with campus administrators
* Parenting faculty workshops and meetings on topics of interest such as time management, work-life balance, professional growth, etc.

Over 60 faculty have joined the [Parenting Faculty group on Microsoft Teams](https://teams.microsoft.com/dl/launcher/launcher.html?url=%2F_%23%2Fl%2Fteam%2F19%3ABCRM32fLv1Kp3ITdmeGMlbfi2ymC29woyFQBTURU7Rg1%40thread.tacv2%2Fconversations%3FgroupId%3D0d4c9a04-0b84-487e-97d6-6e4b130880b8%26tenantId%3D164ba61e-39ec-4f5d-89ff-aa1f00a521b4&type=team&deeplinkId=d8713bf1-0a9c-4996-a403-5afa760fff60&directDl=true&msLaunch=true&enableMobilePage=true&suppressPrompt=true).
