## About Me

I am currently an Associate Professor in the [Department of Mathematics and Statistics](https://www.cpp.edu/sci/mathematics-statistics/index.shtml) at [Cal Poly Pomona](https://www.cpp.edu/).  Previously I was a software engineer at [Vision Robotics](https://www.visionrobotics.com/) in San Diego, developing robots for use in agriculture.  I completed my doctorate in the [Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering](https://www.ece.ucsd.edu/) at the [University of California, San Diego](https://www.ucsd.edu/), working with [Professor Ken Zeger](http://www.code.ucsd.edu/zeger/) in the areas of network coding and wireless sensor networks.
