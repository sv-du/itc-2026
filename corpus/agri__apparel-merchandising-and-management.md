# Department of Apparel Merchandising & Management

![A female student designs a garment on a computer](img/2024-amm-cad-1160x795.jpg)

## Become by Designing

With Los Angeles as your launchpad, discover where your creativity and education can take you in the $1 trillion global fashion industry. At Cal Poly Pomona, you’ll gain hands-on experience in fashion design, retailing, textiles, sustainability, technology, and entrepreneurship. Create your own fashion line. Manage a student-run boutique. Learn directly from industry leaders through field trips, guest speakers, and internships. Our location gives you access, and your education gives you the edge. This is where you become by doing.

Only

The only TAPAC-accredited apparel program on the West Coast and one of only nine in the U.S.

Textiles and Apparel Programs Accreditation Commission (TAPAC)

### Innovate with Technology

Get hands-on experience with the technology employed by the industry, including digital design and 3D software, body scanning technology, digital textile and 3D printing, and e-commerce and trend forecasting tools.

[Card 2 of 6

![A customer peruses clothing items at a boutique as a student looks on.](img/2016-boutique-920x702.jpg)

### Build Business Skills with ApaprelScapes

Learn the basics behind running an apparel business. ApparelScapes is a student-run retail microbusiness that sells products through various channels. Click on this card to find out more about ApparelScapes via a Poly Post article and video.](https://thepolypost.com/arts-and-culture/2025/12/09/behind-the-seams-of-apparelscapes/)

Card 3 of 6

![Four female students jump in the air at a fashion show.](img/2013-amm-fashion-920x702.jpg)

### Organize a Fashion Show

Students in the Fashion Society club organize an annual fashion show featuring their designs. Other clubs participate in student competitions.

Card 4 of 6

![A female student uses a microscope to examine a textile sample while another looks on. ](img/2018-amm-textiles-920x702.jpg)

### Apply Textile Science

Discover the science behind fiber, yarn, and fabrics, including textile testing, sustainability, coloration, and innovation.

Card 5 of 6

![A student shows President Coley a handbag](img/2016-am2-fashion-line-920x702.jpg)

### Design a Fashion Line

As a senior, you'll have a capstone course that brings all your education and experience together to design and produce a fashion line for the Bronco Bookstore.

### Explore the Global Industry

Students have taken trips to London, Paris, and conventions in Las Vegas.
