## Enjoy Your Life

Want to visit an art gallery? Enjoy fine dining? See a horse show or take in a theatrical performance? Picnic in a rose garden? You can do all of this and more without ever leaving campus. And when you do venture out, you will find mountains, beaches and deserts that are close enough for day trips or full-on adventures.Southern California is unrivaled in its array of cultural, recreational and entertainment options. Whatever your passion, hobby or epicurean interest, you will find it here.
