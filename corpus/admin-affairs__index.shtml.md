# Division of Administrative Affairs Contacts

![](img/ssb-full-view-banner.jpg)

Welcome to the Division of Administrative Affairs! Beyond managing essential administrative and financial responsibilities, our commitment to student success is at the heart of everything we do. This commitment shapes our discussions, guides our decisions, and influences our policies. Through our collective efforts, we strive to create an environment where administrative excellence elevates academic and institutional achievement.
