# CalFresh Outreach

## Get Started

### Make an Appointment

Walk-ins: Visit the Care Center Building 97

### What We Assist With

Eligibility prescreening, application assistance, required verifications, interview preparation, recertification and troubleshooting

### Contact Us

(909) 869-5071 or email carecenter@cpp.edu

## Eligibility Information

Please review this [Eligibility information](calfresh-eligibility.shtml)to see if you may qualify. Eligibility changes semi-regularly.

## Apply for CalFresh Online

The CalFresh application process can be tricky sometimes, so we encourage students to apply through the CalFresh Outreach Program to ensure you are submitting your application correctly.

However, if you prefer to apply on your own, use the link below. You can always reach out to us if you have questions after submitting!

[Apply Online for CalFresh](https://benefitscal.com/r/CPPCareCenter)

## Application Process

## **Step 1**

Complete prescreening with CalFresh Outreach Team during drop-in hours or appointment.

## **Step 2**

If eligible, complete and submit an application with CalFresh Outreach Team.

## **Step 3**

Your 30-day time frame begins when you submit your application online.

## **Step 4**

Within two weeks, you will complete your phone interview.

* The county social service department will call or mail you a date for your interview.
* Answer ALL unknown or private incoming calls because it might be from CalFresh.
* Let the CalFresh Outreach Team know if you have NOT been contacted 2 weeks from your date of submission.

##

## **Step 5**

After your interview, you will be required to submit verification documents to your county office to complete your application. You must submit your documents on time or result in submitting a new application. Examples of verification documentation include:

* Form of ID
* Proof of address
* Utility bills
* Pay stubs
* Financial aid award letter and schedule

##

## **Step 6**

If approved, you can now begin using your CalFresh benefits!

## FAQ's

### Does CalFresh Affect my Financial Aid?

No, CalFresh does not affect your financial aid.

### Does CalFresh Affect my Taxes or my Parent's Taxes?

No, CalFresh does not affect taxes.

### Do I Need to Have a Job to be Eligible for CalFresh?

No, you do not need to have a job in order to be eligible for CalFresh. Although, there are other eligibility requirements that you need to meet. If you would like to learn more about eligibility you can make an appointment with a CalFresh Outreach Student Ambassador. They can answer questions about CalFresh and walk you through the online application.
