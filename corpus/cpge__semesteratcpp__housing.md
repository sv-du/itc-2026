# Housing Information

![residential photo](../img/560.residental-housing.jpg)

## Living On Campus

Since living on campus is very popular, we encourage students to apply as soon as they receive their visa. On the housing application or via email, students should inform the housing office that they will be attending the Semester@CPP.

The Residence Halls and Residential Suites are conveniently located on-campus and are within easy walking distance to the academic buildings, library, computer labs, dining areas, the Bronco Student Center and parking lots.

For information on housing policies, procedures, costs, and how to apply, please visit the

**University Housing Services**

Pomona, CA 91768

## Residence Halls

**Features include:**

* Double or triple occupancy
* Study room, laundry room, recreation room, and kitchenette
* 10, 14, or 19 meal plan required
* Staffed by Residence Hall Advisors and a Residence Hall Coordinator

## Residential Suites

**Features include:**

* Three Floor Plans
* Living room, kitchenette, and full-size bathroom
* Bedroom furnished with bed, bookshelf, desk, chairs and dresser
* Living room/kitchenette furnished with sofa, chair, tables, refrigerator, and microwave
* 10, 14, or 19 meal plan required
* Staffed by Residence Hall Advisors and a Residence Hall Coordinator

## University Village Apartments

The Cal Poly Pomona Village offers apartment living adjacent to the campus. Students who have completed 24 semester units or are 21 years of age or older are eligible to live in the Village.

**How to Apply to Live at the Village**

Please go to [www.cppvillage.com](http://www.cppvillage.com/).  If students do not have a Bronco ID, please use your passport number.

**Community Features**

* Apartments house four students, with either two or four bedrooms options
* Furnished bedrooms, living room, and dining room
* Refrigerator and stove/oven are provided in kitchen
* Meal plan purchase is optional from Campus
* Swimming pool, basketball courts, computer lab and recreation center and free laundry room
* Barbeque and picnic areas
* Staffed by Professional Coordinators and Resident Advisors 24 hours a day
