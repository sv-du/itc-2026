## About the Program

Put your passion for fitness and sports into action while providing youth with healthy exercise habits and teamwork skills they will carry with them for the rest of their lives. Encourage healthy living and make a meaningful and lasting difference. Prepare for teaching credential programs and careers in physical education from elementary to high school.

## The Polytechnic Approach

Receive a broad foundation in health, well-being and human movement, then dive deeper into the practices of physical education. Learn how to teach physical activities to students ranging in age and ability. Develop curriculum and analyze assessment strategies. Here, you receive a comprehensive and inclusive education that prepares you for a teaching credential program and career in the physical education field.

## Career Outcomes

Previous
Next

### Sports Coach

Help athletes develop their skills, strategize for competitions, and encourage communication and teamwork.

### Personal Trainer

Develop and implement exercise plans for individuals with various goals and needs. Set objectives, assess clients’ physical condition and guide them to their desired outcome.

### Physical Education Teacher

Teach students about physical exercise and sports to promote an active lifestyle and develop social skills like teamwork and cooperation. Play an important role in instilling lifelong healthy habits that students can carry into adulthood.

### Wellness Coach

Assist others in achieving their health and wellness goals. Provide education, guidance and support.

### Graduate and Professional School

Prepare for programs including: Public Health Specialist (MPH, DrPH), Physical Education Teacher Credential, Adapted Physical Education Teacher Credential, Physical Therapy (DPT), Occupational Therapy (MOT, DOT), Athletic Training (MSAT), Physicians Assistant (PA), Registered Nurse (RN), Medical Doctor (MD), Doctor of Osteopathy (DO), Doctor of Philosophy, Doctor of Education (Ph.D, Ed.D), Clinical Exercise Physiologist.

## Courses

Most students who select the PETE option will go on to obtain a California teaching credential which will qualify them to teach physical education in the state's public and private schools at either the elementary or secondary level. Those who elect the adapted physical education emphasis will become qualified to obtain the Adapted Physical Education Specialist Credential. Students may prepare themselves to coach athletic teams by selecting appropriate courses.

For the most up-to-date information on courses for this program, visit the course catalog or contact an advisor.

## Learn By Doing

### The Motor Development Clinic

Learn by doing is embedded in programs across campus, and the kinesiology department is no exception with the Motor Development Clinic.
Since 1979, the clinic, located in Kellogg Gym, has been dedicated to improving children's motor skills and helping them fine-tune their cooperation, attention, and social skills. In 2023, the clinic started incorporating a new fitness-based program for adults with disabilities.
