# Getting to the IO

![](../img/about/27.jpg)

## Address of the IO

The IO can be found at **Ganesha High School, 1151 Fairplex Dr, Pomona, CA 91768**

## Getting by Car

![Map on how to get to the IO by car](../img/about/gettingtotheio.jpg)

[**Click here for the interactable car map**](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/viewer?mid=1YoAwV6rzN1U83EkvSgosq0bAQ3bxH47b&ll=34.0622554790566%2C-117.80196191100876&z=15 "Opens map for the innovation orchard")

1. From the Student Services Building take a right onto Red Gum Ln
2. At the second stoplight take a left onto S Campus Dr
3. Continue along S Campus Dr until you see signs pointing towards I-10 East, then turn left
4. Stay on the rightmost lane along I-10 East until you see signs pointing towards Fairplex Dr. from there take exit 43.
5. After exiting, stay on the right lane to turn onto Fairplex Dr.
6. Continue on Fairplex Dr. until you are at the end of the highschool. From there turn right.
7. Follow signs that have the IO symbol on it until you arrive at the Innovation Orchard.

## Biking to the IO

![map of how to bike to the  IO](../img/about/bikingtotheio.jpg)

[**Click here for the interactable bike map**](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/viewer?mid=1vh6CgHk3yoZxc_XoZykLK8WNp_bZh56R&ll=34.06270547737752%2C-117.80328000000002&z=15 "Opens map for the innovation orchard")

1. Head Southwest on Kellogg Dr toward Red Gum Ln
2. Turn left to stay on Kellogg Dr
3. Turn left onto S Campus Dr
4. Turn left onto Ridgeway St
5. Continue onto Murchison Ave
6. Turn right onto Fairplex Dr. until you are at the end of the highschool. From there turn right.
7. Follow signs that have the IO symbol on it until you arrive at the Innovation Orchard.

## Bus Routes

![Bus stops near campus](../img/about/iobus.png)

**[Click here for an interactable bus stop map](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=17wNlZH6J0o345RsGW3rv3cA7Rv_mC9rO&usp=sharing)**

Finding the fastest route to the Innovation Orchard involves routing yourself towards the IO via Google Maps or any major route planning application. You can do so by searching "Innovation Orchard" on the Google Maps app or by clicking [here](https://goo.gl/maps/CCUtNxAtCMWDHWYS8 "Link to Innovation Orchard google maps page") select directions and select the transit icon. The app will preview the fastest routes for getting to a bus stop as well as the bus times. To see the route from the Student Services Building to the IO [click here](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/Student%2BServices%2BBuilding%2C%2BClassroom/Laboratory/Administration%2B%28C/L/A%29%2C%2BPomona%2C%2BCA/Innovation%2BOrchard%2C%2B1151%2BFairplex%2BDr%2C%2BPomona%2C%2BCA%2B91768/%4034.0579624%2C-117.8119794%2C15z/data%3D%213m1%214b1%214m14%214m13%211m5%211m1%211s0x80c32f2e35b2f137%3A0xd69c8309b611bd31%212m2%211d-117.8199464%212d34.0591724%211m5%211m1%211s0x80c32eaf9848cb3f%3A0x3c5994d3f081330b%212m2%211d-117.7898445%212d34.0672386%213e3 "Link to Student Services Building to IO route.").

Otherwise, Foothill Transit Route 482 can take you close to Ganesha High School. There are multiple bus stops located near campus; they are "S Campus Dr and Kellogg Dr" and "S Campus Dr and Temple Ave". The nearest bus stops to Ganesha High School are "Holt Ave and Short E" and "Holt Ave and Fairplex Dr W". You can access the bus route on [myStop](https://foothilltransit.rideralerts.com/myStop) by searching "482" under routes.

You can use myStop to view the bus routes arrival and departure times. Search "482" under routes.
