# Academic Assessment

## What is Academic Assessment?

Academic assessment is the process of gathering and discussing information from multiple and diverse sources in order to develop a deep understanding of **what students know, understand, and can do** with their knowledge as a result of their educational experiences; the process culminates when assessment results are used to **improve subsequent learning.**

## Assessing Student Learning

[### Learning Goals and Outcomes

What students should know and be able to do, guiding curriculum, instruction, and assessment to support intentional learning and institutional alignment.](learning-goals-and-outcomes.shtml)

[### Rubrics

Scoring tools that list the criteria for student work and articulates the levels of quality for each criterion.](rubrics.shtml)

[### Results and Assessment Timeline
