# PolyX Showcase

![group of PolyX students posing for photos](../img/events/showcase-students.jpg)

## About the Showcase

The **Fall PolyX FYE Showcase** provides students with an opportunity to present their in-person research projects developed during their PolyX courses. These courses challenge students to explore innovative solutions to real-world problems within their fields. Through collaborative learning and dedicated mentorship, PolyX engages students beyond the classroom, equipping them with the skills needed for professional and civic success. This event is a partnership between the **[Signature Polytechnic Experience (PolyX) initiative](https://www.cpp.edu/polyx/index.shtml "https://www.cpp.edu/polyx/index.shtml")** and the **[First Year Experience (FYE) program](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/oss/fye/index.shtml "https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/oss/fye/index.shtml")**.

### 2025 PolyX FYE Showcase

×

**Showcase**On **December 2**, Cal Poly Pomona hosted the **8th Annual Fall PolyX FYE Showcase** at Ursa Major, celebrating the creativity, curiosity, and innovation of first-year students. The event featured an in-person poster session where over 500 students presented PolyX projects developed through months of exploration, collaboration, and hands-on learning—bringing CPP’s *Become by Doing* philosophy to life.

“The PolyX FYE Showcase is a wonderful reflection of our *Become by Doing* philosophy in action,” said **Ericka Olguin**, director of the Innovation Incubator. “Our first-year students were not only learning concepts in the classroom but applying them through projects that foster creativity, collaboration, and critical thinking.”

**Dr. Dora Lee**, the associate vice president of academic engagement and success, emphasized the importance of the showcase in the first-year journey. “This event represented the creativity, growth, and potential of our newest students as they applied their learning to real-world ideas and challenges,” Lee said.

Through the continued partnership between the **Office of Academic Innovation** and **First-Year Experience**, the PolyX FYE Showcase invited the campus community to celebrate student innovation and the next generation of changemakers at Cal Poly Pomona.

**Showcase**

On November 21, 2024, Cal Poly Pomona hosted its 7th annual Fall PolyX Showcase, a vibrant celebration of student innovation, creativity, and hands-on learning. Whether you’re a student, mentor, or guest, the PolyX Showcase is a reminder of what’s possible when curiosity meets action!

### 2022 Showcase

×

**Showcase**

On November 17, 2022, the **PolyX FYE Showcase** returned to an enthusiastic, in-person format for the first time since the onset of COVID-19— bringing together over 200 students, faculty, and staff inside the Bronco Student Center’s Ursa Major A, B, and C. The event gave first-year students a dynamic platform to present the Signature Polytechnic Experiences (PolyX) they completed during their first semester at Cal Poly Pomona.

Attendees explored a wide array of student-led projects that reflected the heart of CPP’s *learn-by-doing* philosophy. Through vibrant poster sessions, students shared the questions that sparked their curiosity, the research that shaped their ideas, and the innovative solutions they developed with support from faculty mentors and peers.

**Dr. Olukemi Sawyerr**, associate vice president for Academic Innovation, addressed the audience with enthusiasm and pride: “The hallmark of a Cal Poly Pomona education is learn by doing. Most people who choose to go to Cal Poly Pomona do so because they want to have the learn-by-doing experience,” she said. “PolyX and FYE wanted to make sure that in your first semester here at Cal Poly Pomona you experience the hallmark of Cal Poly Pomona.”

The 2022 Showcase wasn’t just a display of student work—it was a welcoming, empowering moment for first-year students to discover their voices, build community, and begin their academic journeys with confidence and purpose.

### 2021 Recap

×

**Showcase**

Over 500 first-year students engaged in a Signature Polytechnic Experience [(PolyX](https://www.cpp.edu/polyx/index.shtml)) their very first semester at Cal Poly Pomona through the [Learn Through Discovery](https://www.cpp.edu/learndiscovery/index.shtml) (LTD) FYE Showcase.

Happening in person for the first time since 2019, the event gave participating students in the [First Year Experience](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/oss/fye/index.shtml) (FYE) program an opportunity to engage in a hands-on project by creating a research poster on a topic they care about. Half of the students presented in person at the University Library on Nov. 18, while the other half submitted [video presentations](https://www.cpp.edu/learndiscovery/docs/fye/ltd-fye-showcase-2021-polyx-projects-desktop.pdf).

“It was amazing to see so many first-year students sharing the results of their learn-by-doing PolyX projects,” said Winny Dong, faculty director of [LTD](https://www.cpp.edu/learndiscovery/index.shtml). “After more than a year of virtual instruction and a semester of hybrid instruction, it was energizing to be in a space with so many talented students who are excited to talk about how they plan to make a positive impact in their communities.”
