# Upward Bound Application

![Decorative Banner](../../img/banner.png)

## How to Apply

As part of our commitment to guiding students through their educational journey, we ensure that each student receives personalized attention and support. Students will be **provided with an application** and detailed information about the next steps in the process **by their respective advisors**.

**The Upward Bound programs will select those students that:**

* complete the application package (including the requested forms) and an interview with Upward Bound staff
* exhibit commitment to attaining a post-secondary education
* demonstrate a willingness and desire to actively participate in all program activities

To **get in touch with your advisor** and kickstart your academic journey, please drop by the sessions at your school or use the advisor's contact information, **by selecting your school below**. We look forward to assisting you every step of the way!
