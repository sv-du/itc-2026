## Mission

### GEMS Club

The Gender, Ethnic, and Multicultural Studies club exists for the betterment of the California State Polytechnic University, Pomona, and neighboring communities. To initiate and participate in civic, educational, social, and cultural functions enhancing the general welfare of the public addressed. To promote events and strategies directed towards the development of a diverse community including, but not limited to race, ethnicity, gender, sexuality, disability, and immigration status.

![Gems club students with faculty](../img/gems-welcome-2-8-2024.01.jpeg)

![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-green-mobile.svg)
![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-green.svg)

## Student Life

The GEMS club creates a safe space for Cal Poly Pomona Students by welcoming anyone regardless of their identities. In this space, we educate each other and the campus about social inequities within their community and work together to create a better future for those to come. We host film screenings, fundraiser, and guest speakers for anyone interested in social justice causes on the Cal Poly Pomona campus. All majors welcome!
