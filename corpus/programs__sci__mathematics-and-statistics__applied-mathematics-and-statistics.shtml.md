## About the Program

Extend your knowledge and impact as you study mathematical, statistical, computational and modeling methods to solve real-world issues across industries like engineering, finance, health and technology.
With the applied mathematics / statistics program emphasis, you will find extensive opportunities to make a difference in others’ lives.

## The Polytechnic Approach

Develop mathematical and statistical models based on real-world data, design experiments and find solutions to applied problems. Work directly with industrial software and apply mathematical reasoning to fields including engineering, physics and biology. Put theory into practice and graduate ready to take on careers across multiple industries.

## Career Outcomes

Card 1 of 3

### Statistician

Utilize statistical methods and tools to collect, analyze, interpret and present quantitative data. Design surveys, use statistical models and software, interpret results, and prepare reports for healthcare, business, government and academia.

Card 2 of 3

### Actuary

Combine mathematical, statistical and financial theories to analyze financial risks and uncertainties especially in the insurance industry. Help organizations make informed decisions regarding risk management and financial planning.
