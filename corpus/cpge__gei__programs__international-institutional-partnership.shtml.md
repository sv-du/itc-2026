# International Institutional Partnership

## Overview

Building and developing international institutional partnerships connects the world’s leading academic minds so that faculty and students from different countries and regions can jointly cultivate research collaboration, foster diplomacy, build capacity and exchange ideas, which will help enhance the healthy human development and world peace. GEI helps establish and maintain constructive partnerships with international higher education institutions in the world for academic cooperation and exchange.

* Host approximately 50 international visiting delegations per year on average
* Facilitate MOUs with international partner institutions
* Promote Cal Poly Pomona globally

## Highlights

* Sino-U.S. University Presidents’ Forum (<https://polycentric.cpp.edu/2019/11/cpp-global-education-institute-living-up-to-its-name/>)
* Visiting delegation from Babcock University (<https://www.cpp.edu/~ceu/news/articles/babcock-university-nigeria.shtml>)
* Presidents and Administrators from 19 vocational and technical universities from China led by the China Education Association for International Exchange
