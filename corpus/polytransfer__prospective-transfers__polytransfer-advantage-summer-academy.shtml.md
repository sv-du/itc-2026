# PolyTransfer Advantage Summer Academy

## Summer Academy 2026

![Photo of students listening to presentation at summer program from behind](../img/summer-program.jpg)

## About the Program

### 2026 Program Dates: July 9-11

The PolyTransfer Advantage Program at Cal Poly Pomona is a **free** program to empower prospective community college students with a clear pathway to success in their journey toward transferring to Cal Poly Pomona or any California State University (CSU). The PolyTransfer Advantage Summer Academy equips students with the tools, knowledge, and mentorship needed to transition from a community college to a 4-year university seamlessly. Join us for a free, 3-day program and experience university life as a transfer student at Cal Poly Pomona.

**Benefits:**

* FREE meals, parking, and room
* Connecting with CPP faculty, staff, and students
* Learning about the admissions process to a CSU
* Learning about ways to pay for college including financial aid and scholarships
* Learning about different careers and majors
* Meeting other community college students
* Fun activities, giveaways & more!
