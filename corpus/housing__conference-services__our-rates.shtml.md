# Accommodations and Rates

## Traditional Halls

The Traditional Halls are first-year communities located on the north side of campus. The Traditional Halls are close to the colleges, library, and Bronco Student Center. Each Traditional Hall houses one or two students per room, with communal restrooms located in each wing. The Traditional Halls have a laundry room, study areas, common spaces, and community kitchens.

## Traditional Halls Room Type

![Traditional Hall Single](../documents/img/floorplan/trad-halls-layout-sg.jpg)

### Traditional Hall Single

11' x 15' approximately • Closet with above storage • Captains bed - Extra Long Twin • Adjustable height bed • Adjustable height bed • Dresser with three drawers • Desk (42" x 30") and chair

![Traditional Hall Double](../documents/img/floorplan/trad-halls-layout-db.jpg)

### Traditional Hall Double

11' x 15' approximately • Closet with above storage • Captains bed - Extra Long Twin • Adjustable height bed • Adjustable height bed • Dresser with three drawers • Desk (42" x 30") and chair

## Traditional Halls Accommodations and Rates

### Traditional Halls Room Furnishings

**Room furnishings for Traditional Hall Single and Double**

* Room: 11' x 15'
* Bed: 84" L x 38" W x 38" H
* Bed: 80" L x 36" W x 18" H (clearance underneath bed)
* Desk: 42" L x 30" W x 29 1/2" H
* Dresser (double): 30" L x 30" W x 30" H (3 drawers)
* Main Closet: 46" L x 21" W x 78" H
* Above Closet Storage: 46" L x 24" W x 21" H
* Wastebasket
* Carpet

## Residential Suites

The Residential Suites are suite-style communities designed with privacy, comfort and convenience in mind. Offering beautiful views of the campus and the San Gabriel Mountains, all suites have balconies, an interior entrance leading into a living room and kitchenette area, with individual bedrooms. Each building has elevators to the upper levels and large areas for studying.

## Residential Suites Room Type

![Suite Single](../documents/img/floorplan/suites-layout-sg.jpg)

### Suite Single

937 sq. ft. approximately • 2 single occupancy bedrooms • 1 double occupancy bedroom • 2 shared bathrooms • Common living room and kitchenette • 1 shared storage closet

![Suite Double](../documents/img/floorplan/suites-layout-db.jpg)

### Suite Double

1,007 sq. ft. approximately • 4 single occupancy bedrooms • 2 shared bathrooms • Common living room and kitchenette • 1 shared storage closet

## Residential Suites Accommodations and Rates

* Dresser

* Sofa
* Lounge chair
* Coffee table
* End table
* Lamp

**Kitchenette**

* Full size refrigerator
* Electric stove tops

* Cabinet space

## Residential Halls

The Residential Halls are first-year communities located on the south side of campus. The Residential Halls are close to the colleges, library, and Bronco Student Center. Each Residential Hall houses two or three students per room, with communal restrooms located in each wing. The Residential Halls have a laundry room, study areas, common spaces, and community kitchens.

## Residential Halls Room Type

![Residential Halls Single](../documents/img/floorplan/res-halls-layout-sg.jpg)

### Residential Halls Single

143 sq. ft. Approximately • 6’ Closet with shelf and hanging rod • Captains bed - Extra long twin • Adjustable height bed • Dresser with four drawers • Desk (24” X 42”) and chair

![Residential Halls Double](../documents/img/floorplan/res-halls-layout-db.jpg)

### Residential Halls Double

185 sq. ft. Approximately • 6’ Closet with shelf and hanging rod • Captains bed - Extra long twin • Adjustable height bed • Dresser with four drawers • Desk (24” X 42”) and chair

![Residential Halls Triple](../documents/img/floorplan/res-halls-layout-tp.jpg)

### Residential Halls Triple

254 sq. ft. Approximately • 6’ Closet with shelf and hanging rod • Captains bed - Extra long twin • Adjustable height bed • Dresser with four drawers • Desk (24” X 42”) and chair

## Residential Halls Accommodations and Rates

### Residential Halls Room Furnishings

**Room furnishings for Residential Hall Single, Double, and Triple**

* Desk and chair
* Dresser
* Closet: 30" L x 29" W x 72" H
* Wastebasket

* IP television connection
