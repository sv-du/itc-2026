# Celebration of Research

## **7th Annual Celebration of Research**

Welcome to Cal Poly Pomona's Seventh Annual Celebration of Research! The Office of Undergraduate Research (OUR) is hosting a celebration of student research, scholarship, and creative activities on its social media accounts from March to May with events happening across campus. Follow the OUR on Instagram, LinkedIn, or X below to engage with content that recognizes the creativity, discovery, and innovation of CPP students from all disciplines and majors! This includes diverse research profiles, articles, events, faculty mentor recognitions, and updates about the 39th Annual CSU Student Research Competition.

### Collins College Hospitality Uncorked

×

Every year, Hospitality Uncorked celebrates the dynamic social and cultural life that has made hospitality the No. 1 industry in Southern California while raising funds for students from The Collins College of Hospitality Management at Cal Poly Pomona. Organized by The Collins College Board of Advisors, Hospitality Uncorked provides an opportunity to keep Collins College students in front of a broad spectrum of industry professionals, many of whom benefit directly from its gainfully employed students and alumni. Over half a million dollars is raised from generous partners and friends each year to fund scholarships, professional development opportunities, industry trips, innovative programs and more for students to succeed on their journey toward a degree.

---

[Learn about the Event](https://hospitalityuncorked.com/)

### Cal Poly Pomona Artificial Intelligence Fair

×

This year’s theme, AI for All: Bridging Disciplines, Expanding Possibilities, highlights the potential of artificial intelligence as a tool for innovation and growth across all academic and professional fields. The AI Fair will offer workshops, demonstrations, and panel discussions to show how AI can be applied to enhance teaching, learning, and research. Attendees will gain practical insights into using AI to solve real-world problems, streamline processes, and expand their skill sets. By integrating AI into various disciplines, the event aims to equip students, staff, and faculty with the knowledge and tools needed to thrive in an increasingly technology-driven world.

### DBH College of Agriculture Student RSCA Showcase

×

---

All faculty and students are cordially invited to showcase your research, scholarly, and creative projects, so-called RSCA, as a part of the University’s Annual Celebration Week series. As the definition of RSCA stands for “research, scholarly, and creative activities”, all kinds of poster submissions are welcomed for this event, including but not limited to research projects, posters prepared in various classes, such as research methodology with experimental design, product development, new concept design, internship experience when helping the industrial partners on problem solving, as well as critical literature review as a part of research method training.

The Huntley College Student RSCA Showcase will be held in person on **Friday, April 18, 2025 at 11:00 am to 1:00 pm at Cowboy Corner**.

×

The Research Symposium is an opportunity for undergraduate and graduate students in the College of Science to share their research in the form of a poster presentation. All are welcome to attend!

**Art Department Poly-Kroma**

April 26 - May 18, 2025

**Location:**Kellogg University Art Gallery

Learn about Poly-Kroma

### Art Department Poly-Kroma

×

Poly-Kroma consists of two distinct shows that celebrate Visual Communication Design and Fine Art directly resulting from coursework completed within the Art Department. The two components of Poly-Kroma include the Industry Portfolio Showcase – also known as the graduating senior Visual Communication Design majors exhibition – and the 2D3D+ Juried Student Art Show, which is the annual Art Department’s student exhibition.

Some highlights from this year includes:

* Saturday, April 26th
  + Industry Day - Senior Portfolio Showcase (12 - 4pm)
* Thursday, May 1st
  + 2D3D plus opening and awards (6 - 8pm)
  + Motion Design Festival – Screening and awards (8 – 9:30pm)

---
