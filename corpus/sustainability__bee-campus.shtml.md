# Bee Campus

![bees apiary ](img/bigbeepoppy.jpg)

## Bee Campus CPP Certification

Bee Campus USA is a certification program that recognizes colleges and universities committed to supporting pollinators. It encourages campuses to create sustainable habitats, reduce pesticide use, and engage in pollinator conservation through education and community involvement. The program is an initiative of the Xerces Society for Invertebrate Conservation.

[More on Bee Campus USA](https://beecityusa.org/about/ "More on Bee Campus USA. ")
