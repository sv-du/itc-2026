## About the Program

Fuel your passion for health and wellness with the Nutrition and Health option, where the science of nutrition connects with the skills needed to support healthier lives. Whether you’re exploring disease prevention, mental and physical well-being or the psychology of health, this program offers the flexibility to customize your path. Here, your journey blends science, creativity and practical experience to prepare you for a fulfilling career in health and nutrition.

## The Polytechnic Approach

Experience hands-on learning that brings nutrition-focused science to life. From experimenting with ingredients in the kitchen to analyzing data in the lab, you’ll see, smell and taste the connection between food, health and wellness. Internships and service-learning projects give you the chance to apply your knowledge in real-world settings, making an impact while building your skills. Whether you're counseling clients or researching innovative solutions, you'll be prepared to shape healthier communities.

## Career Outcomes

Card 1 of 4

### Wellness Coach

Guide individuals in making sustainable lifestyle changes, addressing nutrition, exercise and mental health to help them achieve their personal wellness goals.

Card 2 of 4

### Sports Nutrition Specialist

Work with athletes and those who love exercise to optimize performance and recovery through personalized nutrition plans tailored to their specific needs.

Card 3 of 4

### Health Educator

Develop and deliver educational programs that teach individuals and communities about nutrition, wellness and disease prevention, empowering them to make healthier choices.

Card 4 of 4

### Nutrition Researcher

Conduct studies exploring the relationship between diet, health and disease, contributing to advancements in nutrition science and influencing public health policies.

## Courses

The Nutrition and Health option offers a versatile education with two distinct emphases. The Nutrition and Health emphasis allows you to delve deeper into disease prevention, mental and physical health, and other specialized areas through elective coursework. The Health Coach emphasis focuses on overall wellness, psychological aspects of health, and coaching techniques. This option is designed for those who wish to explore diverse aspects of nutrition and health, preparing them for roles in public health, sports medicine, or wellness coaching. The program’s flexibility and focus on practical applications make it suitable for those interested in a broad range of health and nutrition careers.

For the most up-to-date information on courses for this program, visit the course catalog or contact an advisor.
