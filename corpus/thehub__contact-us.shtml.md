## Community Innovation Hub Newsletter

Elevate your career and community impact by subscribing to the Community Innovation Hub Newsletter! Dedicated to revitalizing Pomona's workforce, our newsletter offers insider access to transformative Workforce Development initiatives. From skill enhancement in STEAM fields to empowering local entrepreneurs and businesses, and strengthening community ties for a resilient economic future, stay informed and inspired to drive change in Pomona and beyond. Join our community of innovators today!
