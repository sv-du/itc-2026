## Learn more about the Psychology Minor

The Psychology Department offers a minor in Psychology. If you are interested in pursuing a minor in Psychology you can speak with the minor advisor. They will be able to help you process your declaration. Please note: The minor may NOT be taken by majors in Psychology.
