## The Engineering Student Experience Podcast

If you are a high school student who is interested in becoming an engineering student, or a current engineering student who is trying to figure out how to succeed in college and beyond, consider checking out [The Engineering Student Experience Podcast](https://theengineeringstudentexperience.podbean.com/).

The podcast explores topics relevant to future and current engineering students through conversations with practicing engineers, engineering faculty, and engineering students. You can listen to the podcast at the website above and all popular podcast apps such as Apple Podcasts, Stitcher, Spotify, etc.
