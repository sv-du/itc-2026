## Biography

Andrea Watson is a Lecturer in the Department of Animal and Veterinary Sciences at Cal Poly Pomona. She started teaching as a graduate student and has continued to teach in the Animal and Veterinary Sciences Department since graduating in August of 2019.

## Education

M.S. in Agriculture with an emphasis in Animal Science, California State Polytechnic University, Pomona

B.S. in Animal Science with an Equine Science minor, California Polytechnic State University, San Luis Obispo

## Courses Taught

AVS 1000 - Orientation to Animal and Veterinary Sciences
 AVS 1112 - Food Animal Production
 AVS 1113 - Companion, Laboratory, and Exotic Animal Care
 AVS 1125L - Equine Management Laboratory
 AVS 3350L - Anatomy and Physiology Laboratory
 AVS 4610 - Senior Seminar

## Publications
