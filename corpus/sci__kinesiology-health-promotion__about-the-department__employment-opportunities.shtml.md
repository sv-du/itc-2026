# Employment Opportunities

Thank you for your interest in the department of Kinesiology and Health Promotion. If you are looking for a specific position in Kinesiology and Health Promotion, expand the appropriate section for more information.

## Student Assistants

We are not accepting any student assistants at this time.

## Current Faculty Openings

If you are interested in a faculty position at Cal Poly Pomona, please visit the [Faculty Affairs Web Site](https://www.cpp.edu/~faculty-affairs/open-positions/college-unit/sci/index.shtml)

[Application for Employment Form](https://www.cpp.edu/~faculty-affairs/documents/acadapplication_feb2017.pdf)

## Part-Time Faculty Positions in Kinesiology and Health Promotion

We have periodic openings for part-time faculty. If you are interested, please submit:

An application for employment, a letter of interest, CV, 3 recent letters of recommendation dated within the last year, and official transcripts sent to khpsearch@cpp.edu

## Staff and Technical Positions

For information on currently available staff / technical employment opportunties, visit the [Job Opportunities Listing](https://www.cpp.edu/~jobs/) page of the Cal Poly Pomona Human Resources Department.

## Positions for Kinesiology and Health Promotion Majors
