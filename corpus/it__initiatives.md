## Artificial Intelligence

### A Vision for Emerging Technologies.

Through academic excellence, hands-on experience, and ethical foresight, Cal Poly Pomona leads the charge in applying innovations, shaping how AI is taught, built, and used. We prepare our students, faculty, and staff for a transformative future.

[AI at CPP](../advanced-computing/artificial-intelligence-at-cpp.shtml)

![t-shirt reads cybersecurity degree loading](../img/2023-cybersecurity-degree-loading-t-shirt.jpg)

## Cybersecurity

### Careers for the Challenges of Tomorrow.

Students learn and work with industry leaders in creating their digital careers. Through hands-on experiences, research, and nationally recognized programs, we prepare experts in a rapidly evolving industry.

[Cyber Collaborative](https://www.cpp.edu/cyber/%20)

![Cal Poly Pomona Graduate at the 2025 Commencement Ceremony](../img/2025-class-graduate.jpg)

## Geographic Information Systems

### Your Place in the World.

Explore location-based data and how it shapes decisions across industries, through access to advanced mapping tools and guidance from expert CPP faculty to advance your career.

[Center for Geographic Information Science Research](https://www.cpp.edu/class/cgisr/index.shtml%20)
