## Activities

The Egg Hunt and Spring Fun on the Farm Event is a fun time to visit Discover Farm and enjoy a day of exciting activities at AGRIscapes.

The event will run from 9 a.m. to 3 p.m., **Saturday, April 12.** We will have egg hunts at the following times:

* 10 a.m.
* 11:15 a.m.
* 12:30 p.m.
* 1:45 p.m.

General admission will include all the activities listed below:

* Egg Hunts: the egg hunts are open to children from infants up to 12 years of age; each Egg Hunt has a limited capacity. A time-slot reservation is required.
* Petting Farm: does not include feed cups.
* 4H Home Arts Fair and STEM activities
* Photos with the Spring Bunny
* Hay Rides
* Antique tractors and engines

Light food and snacks will be available for sale. In addition, the Farm Store will be open from 10 a.m. to 6 p.m. and will be holding its annual Pepperzania plant sale that day.

## Reservations & Admission

Reservations are required for the egg hunt. Space is limited in each egg hunt time slot. To make a reservation, click on the blue button below.

Although the first egg hunt starts at 10 a.m., we will open at 9 a.m. so visitors can pay for their tickets and participate in the other activities. Egg hunt participants MUST arrive 30 minutes before their reserved time slot.

Following each egg hunt, children will redeem their eggs for prizes.

Reservations only save spots in the egg hunt. Admission is required for everyone entering the event. Tickets are $12 per person, with children under 2 years of age admitted free with a paid adult. **Payment will be taken at the gate only**.

[**Reserve Your Egg Hunt Time Slot**](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSc5wiOKmbAorcp8Z0lnCkx2vaA8raz3d6RTDPQDcFIMYfNHtg/viewform)

## Location & Parking Info

The event will take place from 9 a.m. to 3 p.m. at AGRIscapes, 4102 S. University Drive, Pomona.

Parking is available in the field at the southwest corner of Temple Avenue and South Campus Drive, near the Cal Poly Pomona Farm Store. (see red marker on map below)

**Note:** *Please be aware that Cal Poly Pomona is also holding its BroncoBound Open House event for students admitted for Fall 2025 on the same day. Do not follow signs for BroncoBound parking, which take you away from the Egg Hunt site.*
