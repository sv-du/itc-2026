# Give to Cal Poly Pomona

## Corporate and Foundation Relations

Corporate and Foundation Relations forges strategic partnerships with funders whose missions align with the university’s priorities. By guiding the development and submission of grant proposals, the team helps advance initiatives that create meaningful impact—making it a vital resource for building lasting, mission-driven collaborations. It generates philanthropic support for Cal Poly Pomona, benefiting all nine academic colleges as well as Student Affairs, Bronco Athletics, the W.K. Kellogg Arabian Horse Center, and the University Library.
