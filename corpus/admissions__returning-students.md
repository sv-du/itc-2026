### Graduate Students

If you are a graduate student who left CPP without completing your degree, you must reapply to the university via Cal State Apply. Visit our Graduate Students webpage for more information.](https://www.cpp.edu/admissions/graduate/index.shtml%20 "opens a new window")

## Restarting your Journey at CPP

We’re absolutely thrilled that you’re returning to Cal Poly Pomona! Your decision to continue your journey here shows incredible determination and Bronco spirit. CPP isn’t just a place to learn—it’s a community that celebrates your growth, your dreams, and your future.

As you take this exciting next step, know that we’re here to cheer you on and support you every step of the way. Your experiences, your voice, and your passion make our campus stronger and more vibrant. We can’t wait to see the impact you’ll make as you rejoin the Bronco family and continue shaping the future at CPP.

Welcome home—you’ve been missed, and your journey is just getting started!

## Returning Undergraduate Student FAQs

### Returning Student vs. Returning Transfer

There are key differences between Returning Students and Returning Transfers, please carefully see differences below:

* A **returning student** is someone who left CPP and has not attempted units at another college/university and would like to return under the same major they were initially admitted under.
* A **returning transfer** is someone who left CPP and has attempted units at another college/university, and/or intends to return to a different major from what they were originally admitted under.
  + Returning transfers must meet [transfer requirements](https://www.cpp.edu/admissions/transfer/requirements.shtml) to be considered for readmission

### Leave of Absence (LOA)

If you are returning from an official CPP approved Leave of Absence (LOA), please contact the [Bronco Advising Center (BAC)](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/bronco-advising-center/index.shtml) for next steps. If your LOA has passed the approved timeframe then you will need to submit a [Returning Student Interest Form](https://engage.cpp.edu/register/welcome_back).

### Returning Veterans and Military Service Members

Cal Poly Pomona (CPP) is a proud military friendly campus that is grateful to serve and support its returning Veterans and military service members. As a former student, review the information from our [Veterans Resource Center](https://www.cpp.edu/veterans/index.shtml) to determine if you can return to CPP based on military service.

### Graduate Student

All Graduate students wanting to return to CPP and resume their graduate program will need to follow the steps below as they do not qualify to return to CPP via the Returning Student Interest Form.

* Reapply via [Cal State Apply](https://www.calstate.edu/apply)
* Reach out to your [Graduate Coordinator](https://www.cpp.edu/gradstudies/prospective-students/explore-our-grad-programs.shtml)to ensure that you are eligible or to see what additional steps might be required
