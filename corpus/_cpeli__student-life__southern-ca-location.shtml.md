# Southern California Location

Southern California is one of the most attractive places in the U.S. for international students to come and study! Students often share that the warm, sunny weather and its prime location are some of their favorite things about studying here. When you come to study with us, you will find an endless variety of places to go and things to do -- you'll never get bored!

To get an idea of our location, Cal Poly Pomona is conveniently located about 45 minutes to downtown Los Angeles and 30 minutes to Disneyland.
