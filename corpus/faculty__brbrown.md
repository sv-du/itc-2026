## About Dr. Brown

Greetings,

With almost 25 years of industry experience in Cyber Security, Networking, System Administration, and Project Management, I have a very well-rounded grasp on the Information Technology industry. Couple this with my exposure to multiple industries including Entertainment, Healthcare, Finance, Banking, Integration, Aerospace, and Government, and I bring a multitude of perspectives to the classroom. At Cal Poly, I have taught a multitude of classes in the CIS department of the College of Business Administration. My current assignments include CIS 4670 (Network Security) and CIS 4710 (Internet Security. The latter is my favorite class as I have developed it steadily over many years. The Title "Internet Security" is VERY misleading. In essence, it is an Ethical Hacking Class. I pride myself on assisting and mentoring students to help them with their future goals. This includes help / advice on career searches, independent research, and graduate school selection.

I hope to have you in one of my classes and if you already are, I must say you are in for a treat!

Best.

Dr. B.

**Professional Summary:**

* 24 years of experience in the IT industry. 11most recent years working in mid and upper & executive management implementing business controls, workflows with an emphasis on analyzing and mapping business requirements to technology needs.
* Extensive, Hands-on Network Security background including extensive experience with Cisco, Palo Alto, Juniper, Checkpoint, Fortinet, and other consumer lines.
* Previous Call Center experience building networks of up to 9000 seats in three countries where world-wide telecommunication principles were the cornerstone to success.
* Provided project management and business development guidance on ERP systems such as PeopleSoft, SAP, and MS Dynamics. This included extensive system analysis and project management skills.
* Excellent high level troubleshooting skills for the streamlining of processes.
* Extensive knowledge of IT Service and Support models including ITIL, Six Sigma, Managed Service, Cloud Strategy along with tools, methodologies and help desk related offerings.
* Proven ability to work in diverse environments and with accessibility programs.
* Outstanding documentation skills with an emphasis on attention to detail.
* IT Audit experience with a Big 5 accounting firm. Experience with HIPPA, SOX, FERPA, CIPA, and PCI compliance models.
* Extensive Cyber Security Knowledge and Background to include penetration testing, sandbox modeling, red teaming, policy analysis, and social engineering exercises.
* Hands on technical skills. Willingness to “deep dive” into complex issues to develop root cause analysis profiles and analyze them for the best possible outcome of the end customer.
* Extensive hands on work with networking, wireless, voice, security and Operating System platforms.
