# Registration

## Important 2026 dates for Cal Poly Pomona Students [![bac logo](img/bac-logo.jpg)](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/bronco-advising-center/index.shtml)

Add/Drop Period:

* 10-Week Session: May 13 - June 10. Last Day to Drop and receive 100% refund: June 2
* 5-Week Session 1: May 13 - June 8. Last Day to Drop and receive 100% refund: June 2
* 5 Week Session 2: May 13 - July 14. Last Day to Drop and receive 100% refund: July 8

## Current Cal Poly Pomona Students

Registration is available via [BroncoDirect](https://www.cpp.edu/broncodirect/) from March 12 - May 5, 2025.

* Priority Registration: March 9 - 10
* General Registration: March 12 - May 5

## [Registration Information](https://www.cpp.edu/registrar/registration/registration-information.shtml)

## New or Visiting Students

Registration is available via the Open University process through the [College of Professional and Global Education](https://www.cpp.edu/cpge/). Visit [Open University](https://www.cpp.edu/cpge/open-university/index.shtml) for enrollment details.

## Summer Course Schedule

The [Summer Course Schedule](http://schedule.cpp.edu/index.aspx) is subject to change throughout the registration period and until the first day of classes.

Note: The classes will be offered as Open University sessions. Open University classes have class section numbers that start with an “E” (e.g., E01).
