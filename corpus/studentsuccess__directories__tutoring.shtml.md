# Tutoring Directory

## Getting Academic Support

The following programs offer academic support to Cal Poly Pomona students. Please note any specific eligibility requirements for each program.

![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white-mobile.svg)
![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white.svg)

## Learning Resource Center

### About the LRC

The LRC is open and free to all CPP students.

[Learning Resource Center](https://www.cpp.edu/lrc/index.shtml%20)

![Student checks in at the LRC](../img/directories/tutoring-directory/lrc-cppconnect.png)

![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-green-mobile.svg)
![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-green.svg)

## EOP

### About EOP

Free to EOP & Renaissance Scholar students. Services are also open to students participating in our partner program, Male Success Initiative.

[EOP Learning Center Website](https://www.cpp.edu/ssep/eop/learning.shtml%20 "opens a new window")

![Students in EOP pose for a group photo](../img/directories/tutoring-directory/eop-shirtsgroup-new.jpg)

![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white-mobile.svg)
![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white.svg)

## Maximizing Engineering Potential (MEP) Tutoring

### About MEP

Maximizing Engineering Potential (MEP) offers tutoring to all College of Engineering students. Tutoring supports lower-division math, physics, and chemistry classes for all engineering majors and selected upper-division engineering courses.

[MEP Website](https://www.cpp.edu/engineering/mep/tutoring.shtml%20)

![Two students in MEP pose with Billy Bronco](../img/directories/tutoring-directory/mep-contact.png)

![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-green-mobile.svg)
![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-green.svg)

## Reading, Advising, and Mentoring Program (RAMP) Tutoring

### About RAMP

RAMP tutoring is free to students who are low-income, first-generation, or a student with a disability.

[RAMP Website](https://www.cpp.edu/ramp/%20)

![RAMP students pose for a group photo](../img/directories/tutoring-directory/ramp-contact.png)

![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white-mobile.svg)
![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white.svg)

## Biology Learning Assistant (Bio-LA) Program

### About Bio-LA

The Biology Learning Assistant (Bio-LA) Program is dedicated to helping students succeed and feel like they belong at Cal Poly and in the biology major.

[Bio-LA Website](https://www.cpp.edu/sci/cemast/lap/index.shtml%20)

![A student works in a biology lab](../img/directories/tutoring-directory/cemast-contact.jpg)

![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-green-mobile.svg)
![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-green.svg)

## Science Educational Enhancement Services (SEES)

### About SEES

SEES has a series of opportunities within the program to help students progress and excel. Some of these opportunities are available to anyone in the College of Science. However, due to the financial limitations of offering these programs, some opportunities are for SEES members only.

[SEES Website](https://www.cpp.edu/sci/sees/index.shtml%20)

![two students work in a science lab](../img/directories/tutoring-directory/sees-lab.jpg)
