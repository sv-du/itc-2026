### Scholarships

All undergraduate Psychology majors are eligible to apply for the Behavioral Sciences Scholarship](#modal-0 "Scholarship Information")

### Scholarship Information

×

For ALL undergraduate majors in Psychology

* BEHAVIORAL SCIENCES SCHOLARSHIP (Apply on the [CPP Scholarshipfest](https://csupomona.academicworks.com/ "CPP Scholarshipfest") website)

The requirements are as follows:

* Must have completed a minimum of 60 semester units by the current Spring semester.
* Must be a full time undergraduate student.
* Must not be graduating before Spring of the current academic year.
* Must have completed lower division major requirements by the end of the current Spring semester.
* Must have made some progress in completing upper division requirements in the Psychology major by the end of the current Spring semester.
* Must have a minimum 3.00 Overall GPA at the end of the previous Fall semester.
* Only one application form is required to be considered for any of the departmental scholarships. Students are only eligible to be awarded one scholarship
* Only one letter of recommendation from a Cal Poly Pomona faculty member is required to be considered for any of the departmental scholarships. Please do not submit additional letters of support
