## About the Program

The E-Business (EBZ) option provides a unique and valuable combination of management and technology education with its ever-changing curriculum to keep up with fast-changing business practices. The concentration integrates business and technology through hands-on experience with diverse technologies such as Radio Frequency Identification (RFID), Dreamweaver, Android Application Programming Interface (API) and SAP, to name a few.
The competitive advantages from supply chain management and e-Business are becoming the game changers for the business world. Your choice of Supply Chain Management and E- Business will set the right steps into the future business world.

## Career Outcomes

Previous
Next

### Applications Engineer

An Applications Engineer designs, develops, and supports software applications, ensuring they meet user needs and function efficiently within specific technical environments.

### Business Systems Analyst

A Business Systems Analyst evaluates and improves an organization's technology and processes by bridging the gap between business needs and IT solutions.

### Database Architect

A Database Architect designs and builds database systems, creating structures that ensure efficient data storage, access, security, and scalability for organizational needs.

### Deployment Manager

A Deployment Manager oversees the planning, coordination, and execution of software or system deployments, ensuring smooth implementation with minimal disruptions.

### E-Commerce Product Manager

An E-Commerce Product Manager oversees the strategy, development, and optimization of online products or platforms to enhance user experience, drive sales, and achieve business goals.

### E-Business Consultant

An E-Business Consultant advises organizations on leveraging online platforms and digital tools to optimize business operations, improve customer engagement, and drive growth.

### Entrepreneur

An Entrepreneur identifies opportunities, creates and manages businesses, and assumes financial risks to develop innovative solutions and generate profit.

### Small Business Owner

A Small Business Owner manages all aspects of their business operations, including planning, marketing, finances, and customer relations, to sustain and grow their enterprise.

### Supply Chain Specialist

A Supply Chain Specialist manages and optimizes the flow of goods, services, and information across the supply chain to ensure efficiency, cost-effectiveness, and timely delivery.

### Web Analyst

A Web Analyst collects, analyzes, and interprets website data to improve user experience, optimize performance, and support business goals through actionable insights.

## Overview

EBZ students can get hands-on experience through a variety of organizations, including Operations Management Society (OMS), a business society club that represents the students of the EBZ department. Students can also join the Council of Supply Chain Management Professionals (CSCMP), a worldwide professional organization focused on the aspects and intricacies of supply chain and operations management.
