# Kellogg Art Gallery Collection

![W. Keith and Janet Kellogg University Art Gallery](https://www.cpp.edu/kellogg-gallery/collections/img/keith-and-janet-kellogg-coll.jpg)

Individual artworks that are acquired as part of exhibitions through the W. Keith & Janet Kellogg University Art Gallery include individual mixed media works, sculpture or prints by Jim Morphesis, Kim Abeles, David B. Jang, Thomas McGovern and more.

![Thomas McGovern's New City Project Studies artwork.  navigate down for further details](https://www.cpp.edu/kellogg-gallery/collections/img/kellogg-gallery-coll-thomas-mcgovern-new-city-project-studies.jpg)
**Thomas McGovern**, *New City Project Studies*, 1998-2001, ink jet prints on steel, maquettes of larger scale sculpture installation, 7 parts: 5 x 8 x 3.75”; 4.75 x 8.5 x 3.375”; 4 x 9.25 x 2.5”; 4 x 7.625 x 2.75”; 4.25 x 12.875 x 2.625”; 4.375 x 14.375 x 3.5”; 4.125 x 11 x 4.5”. Exhibited in artist’s exhibition at the Kellogg University Art Gallery. Donated by the artist to the Kellogg University Art Gallery. University Art Gallery Collection, College of Environmental Design, Cal Poly Pomona.
