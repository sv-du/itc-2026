Department of Sociology, College of Letters, Arts, and Social Sciences

## Research Interests

* Criminology
* Life course research
* Social psychology
* Sociology of professions
* Statistics & Methods

## Teaching - Fall 2025

CRM 3306/A Crime Analysis

SOC4611 Senior Seminar - Life Course

SOC4000 Independent Study - Student Research

## Education

Ph.D. Sociology, University of Toronto, Canada

## Recent Publications

* Markovic, Milan, and **Gabriele Plickert.** 2022. "The Gender Pay Gap and High-Achieving Women in the Legal Profession." *Law & Social Inquiry*: 1-32.
* Narayan, Anjana and **Gabriele Plickert**. 2021. "*Connecting the Unconnected:* A Critical Review of Models of Social Work and Police Collaboration in the United States." *Sozial Extra* 45, 167–173 (2021). <https://doi.org/10.1007/s12054-021-00379-7> *(German language publication)*
* **Plickert, Gabriele** and Heili Pals. 2020. “Intergenerational Transmission of Anger: Parental Anger and the Effects on Adolescents and Young Adults.” Journal of Research on Adolescence 30(2):440-457.
* Markovic, Milan and **Gabriele Plickert**. 2019. “The Paradox of Minority Attorney Satisfaction.” International Journal of Law and Economics.60: 105859. <https://doi.org/10.1016/j.irle.2019.105859>
* **Plickert, Gabriele**. 2019. “A Life Course Approach to Workplace Discrimination and Employment: Evidence from a U.S. Sample of Women and Men Lawyers.” Pp. 134-154 in Marta Choroszwicz and Tracey L. Adams (eds.). Gender, Age and Inequality in the Professions. Routledge Press. ISBN 9780815358572
