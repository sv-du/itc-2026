# Open Faculty Positions

## Assistant Professor - Visual Communication Design

The Department of Art welcomes applications for a full-time, tenure-track Assistant Professor to teach Visual Communication Design (VCD) starting in Fall 2022. The application deadline is **December 2, 2021**.

For details, visit [https://apply.interfolio.com/97335](https://apply.interfolio.com/97335 "https://apply.interfolio.com/97335").

## Lecturer Positions

We welcome applications for part-time, temporary faculty in the following areas: Foundations in 5D, Foundations in 4D and Typography.
