# Events

## USS EVENTS

### February 2026

Cal Poly Pomona is a diverse community of individuals who represent many perspectives, beliefs and identities, committed to fostering an inclusive, respectful, and intellectually vibrant environment. All university programs and activities are open and available to all regardless of race, sex, color, ethnicity or national origin.

[#### Connections without Perfection: Healthy

Tuesday, February 3 2026 @12PM . Join us in learning how to set healthy boundaries in relationships to build stronger, more balanced connections. We’ll explore how perfectionism, expectations, and social media pressure affect modern relationships—and how to navigate them in a healthy way RSVP HERE](https://mybar.cpp.edu/event/12082897 "opens a new window")

[#### Imagination Station

Tuesday, February 10 2026 @ 11:30AM Celebrate love in all its forms.
Join us for a cozy Valentine’s craft day with phone charms perfect for besties, cuties, or just treating yourself. Come create something sweet + take home a little reminder you are loved. RVSP HERE](https://mybar.cpp.edu/event/12047660 "opens a new window")

## February 2026

### CARECEN Workshops

CARECEN hosts various workshops that are open to the CPP community throughout the semester. We are also sharing event from Immigration Legal Defense.

[#### Navigating International and Dom. Travel

Friday, February 6 @ 2pm
Join CARECEN to learn about the current state of Advance Parole & options for international and domestic travel for non-citizens. RVSP HERE](https://calstate.zoom.us/webinar/register/WN_LUD-FpEwRciDylwGXsXMmg#/registration "opens a new window")

[#### Emergency Safety Planning for Imm.

Wednesday February 11th @ 3pm
Join us to learn what concrete and proactive steps you can take to prepare for a future potential immigration-related emergency.RVSP HERE](https://calstate.zoom.us/webinar/register/WN_AGc33Bc-TW6vkqWCeg2a6w#/registration "opens a new window")

[#### Marriage Petitions

Friday February 13 @ 2PM
Want to learn more about how a US Citizen or Green Card holder can petition for their spouse? Join CARECEN to learn more about the requirements and other important details you should know during this administration. RVSP HERE](https://calstate.zoom.us/webinar/register/WN_XnKVQh8tT3uuVBKlr4sVCg#/registration "opens a new window")

[#### Special Immigrant Juvenile Status

Thursday February 19 @ 3PM
Join CARECEN to learn about Special Immigrant Juvenile Status (SIJS) and how it may put certain non-citizens under 21 on a pathway to citizenship.. RVSP HERE](https://calstate.zoom.us/webinar/register/WN_f0o8PB3DQQiBjQDVFTUyag#/registration "opens a new window")

[#### Updates in Immigration Law

Friday February 27 @ 12PM
Join us to learn about recent updates in immigration law. RVSP HERE](https://calstate.zoom.us/webinar/register/WN_VvBHNIlWRVyQgb0L7YsVRQ#/ "opens a new window")
