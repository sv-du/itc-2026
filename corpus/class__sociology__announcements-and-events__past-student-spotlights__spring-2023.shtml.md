The Department of **Sociology Peer Mentors** introduced the Department to future students at the Open House. Peer Mentors interacted with students and parents and showcased our two majors, Sociology and Criminology, and minors.

## Spring 2023: AKD and PSA

![](../events/flyers/psa-happy-hour-2023.jpg)

The ***Pacific Sociological Association*** (PSA) Annual Meeting met in Bellevue, WA, in Spring 2023. Twelve AKD students attended the PSA Conference. The conference has been an excellent opportunity for students to learn about cutting-edge research in sociology and network with leading scholars in the field.
