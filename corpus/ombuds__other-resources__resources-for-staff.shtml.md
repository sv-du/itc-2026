# Resources for Staff

## Lots of Support — We Can Help You Find It

The Office of Conflict Resolution and Ombuds offers confidential support to guide staff toward resolving university-related issues. However, the resources listed below can address additional needs and provide specialized assistance beyond our scope.

## Campuswide Staff Resources

[Employee Resource Center](https://cpp.service-now.com/employee_services)

[Employee & Labor Relations](https://www.cpp.edu/eoda-hr/departments/elr/index.shtml)

[EODA / Human Resources Operations](https://www.cpp.edu/eoda-hr/index.shtml)

[University Policies](https://www.cpp.edu/policies/index.shtml)

[Compliance Training](https://www.cpp.edu/eoda-hr/departments/strategic-learning/resource-hub/compliance-training/index.shtml)

[Office of Equity & Compliance](https://www.cpp.edu/officeofequity/)

[Disability Resource Center](https://www.cpp.edu/drc/)

[Emergency Management](https://www.cpp.edu/em/)

[Holiday Observances](https://www.cpp.edu/eoda-hr/departments/payroll/calendars/holidays.shtml)

[Empathia Life Matters Employee Assistance Program](https://www.cpp.edu/eoda-hr/departments/benefits/health-benefits/eap.shtml)

[University Police Department](https://www.cpp.edu/police/index.shtml)

[Parking & Transportation](https://www.cpp.edu/parking/index.shtml)
