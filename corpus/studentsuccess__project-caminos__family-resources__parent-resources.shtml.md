# Parent and Family Resources

## About Us

![Parents and fanily pose on the steps of the CBA Building.](../parents-family.jpg)

Cal Poly Pomona's Project CAMINOS is dedicated to help prospective students and families with their college selection and transition. We know it is not only a transitition for your student, but for you as well.

It is never too early to start talking to your student about college. Whether they are in elementary or high school it is important to keep yourself informed. We are here to support you with any questions you may have.

## Discover Cal Poly Pomona

![A group poses together in the Student Services Building breezeway](../img/parents2.jpg)

Learn how you can start to navigate college websites with this tutorial. Start your web search with these three websites:

[Outreach, Recruitment, and Educational Partnerships (OREP)](https://www.cpp.edu/outreach)

[Office of Admissions](https://www.cpp.edu/admissions)

[Financial Aid & Scholarships](https://www.cpp.edu/financial-aid/index.shtml)

## Our Services

Some of our services include:

* College information
* How to apply to college
* How to apply for financial aid
* Major and career information

Schedule an appointment at (909) 869-4454. Receive email updates about our services:

[Subscribe](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/project-caminos/about-us/email-list.shtml)

## Activities

[### Coloring Pages

It's never too soon to start planting the seed that college is attainable. Download the "I'm Going to College" coloring page with young Billy, Cal Poly Pomona's mascot.](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/project-caminos/documents/cpp-coloring-pages.pdf "opens a new window")

[### Pictionary

Can you guess the college related word? Download our college Pictionary. Play with a friend or in a group and start thinking about college!](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/project-caminos/documents/caminos_pictionary_level-1.pdf "opens a new window")

[### Lotería

Learn about Cal Poly Pomona while playing a fun game. Download our Lotería game and play with a group by printing your cards or play online via video call!](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/project-caminos/prospective-students/loteria.shtml "opens a new window")
