# Huntley College Student Success Center

![Students in a field with a tractor](img/ag-largehero.png)

## About Us

We help undergraduate College of Agriculture students with:

* General Academic Advising & Course Selection (GE & Core)
* Registration and Graduation Questions
* Petition Assistance & Information
* Degree Progress Review (DPR) Review
* Transfer Credit Review (TCR) Review
* Graduation Pledge Advising
* Academic Planner Review
* Academic Warning, Academic Notice and Academic Notice with Contract Advising
* Resource Referral

## Get In Touch With Us

[### Schedule an Appointment on CPP Connect

Advising appointments are 30 minutes long and can be in-person or virtual — just pick what works best for you when booking. Keep in mind that you might not be able to switch formats later. If you're more than 10 minutes late, you'll need to reschedule. You can book up to 2 weeks in advance. Check back Friday afternoons for new openings.](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/cppconnect/index.shtml)

[### Attend Drop-In Hours

Drop-ins with advisors are for quick questions. Click on this card to get details and the Zoom link.](#modal-0 "Drop-In Advising")

### Drop-In Advising

×

No appointment is necessary. Drop-in appointments are informal check-ins (five to 10 minutes) to ask any of the following:

* Registration questions
* University policy/procedure questions
* Academic Planner (single semester)
* To refer you to campus resources
* Change/add major/minor into agriculture

**Please note:** our advisors do not provide Senior Graduation Checks or DPR/TCR Reviews during drop-in hours. You must schedule an appointment on CPP Connect for these services.

Our center also does not provide permission numbers. Please reach out to the department offering the course for a permission number.
