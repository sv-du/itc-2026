# Strategic Plan

The Collins College of Hospitality Management’s mission, vision, goals and values define our purpose and direction. Informed by broad community engagement, they anchor our work around student-centered growth, innovation and lasting impact.

![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white-mobile.svg)
![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white.svg)

## Our Vision

The center for hospitality – connecting industry, education and community.

![A young man in a gray suit stands beside an older woman wearing a green jacket at a formal event. They pose smiling in front of a decorative backdrop reading “The Wizards of Awe” and “Hospitality Uncorked 2024." ](../img/24hospitality-uncorked_18.jpg)

![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-green-mobile.svg)
![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-green.svg)

## Our Mission

We cultivate careers, serve communities and inspire life-long leaders through hospitality learning and innovation.

![Three people stand around a tall table collaborating on laptops in a modern indoor space. A woman gestures while speaking as a man and another woman look at their screens, conveying teamwork, discussion, and professional collaboration.](../img/fogle-elisha21.jpg)

## Our Values

1. ### Collaboration and Relationships

   We invest in relationships and seek out collaborations across all constituencies.
2. ### Inclusivity

   Diverse voices are valued and embraced.
3. ### Career Readiness

   We cultivate career readiness through hands-on experiences and the development of a life-long learning mindset.
4. ### Innovation

   We use our forward-thinking approach to pioneer innovation in the industry.

## Our Goals

![Three adults sit in a modern lounge area having a small group conversation. Two women and one man hold coffee cups while talking face to face, suggesting a professional or community networking discussion in a shared workspace.](../img/goal1.png)

### Goal #1

#### Culture of Communication

The Collins College demonstrates a culture of consistent communication, focusing on clear, intentional, and open conversations.

![A woman wearing a black blazer stands in a bright indoor space while an older man in a blue suit gently adjusts the shoulder of her jacket. Other people are visible in the background holding clothing, suggesting a professional styling.](../img/goal2.jpg)

### Goal #2

#### Culture of Leadership

The Collins College fosters a culture of leadership at every level—developing students, staff, faculty, and alumni to be confident, capable, and leader-ready for growth and learning.

![Three people stand around a table covered with folded clothing, snacks, small items, and printed materials during a busy classroom or workshop event. Other participants sit at tables in the background.](../img/goal3.jpg)

### Goal #3

#### Elevate the CPP Brand Experience

The Collins College leads the CSU system in hospitality excellence through cutting-edge education, dynamic community collaboration, and innovative industry partnerships.

![A culinary student wearing a white chef coat and black gloves carefully plates dishes along a stainless steel counter in a commercial kitchen. Rows of prepared plates extend into the background while another chef works behind them, highlighting hands-on culinary training and professional food preparation.](../img/goal4-2.jpg)

### Goal #4

#### Embed Real-World Learning Across All Programs

The Collins College delivers experiential learning that prepares students for both immediate readiness and long-term career success.

![A group of professionally dressed adults walk together through a modern outdoor shopping and entertainment district. Tall buildings, digital billboards, and storefronts line the walkway, conveying a confident, business-focused atmosphere in an urban setting.](../img/goal4.jpg)

### Goal #5

#### Guide Students Toward Purposeful Career Pathways

The Collins College equips students with the skills and business acumen to chart purposeful career paths and lead with confidence and impact.
