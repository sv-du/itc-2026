Through hands-on learning methods, faculty-mentored research, and exposure to current technology, our students acquire skills applicable to careers in the Geosciences and related disciplines. The Department's programs provide a global perspective for problem solving, decision making and leadership roles in a changing world. Our [**Geology BSc program**](about/degrees-and-classes.shtml "Curriculum") has three different tracks: Geology, Geophysics/Earth Exploration and Environmental Resources. Our [**Geology MSc program**](masters-program/curriculum.shtml "MastersCurriculum") prepares graduates for competitive PhD programs and employment in industry or teaching.
