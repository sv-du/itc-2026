## CSET Online Test Preparation

###### [**CSET Courses/Workshops for Single Subject Mathematics and Science Teachers**:](https://fresnostate.edu/kremen/about/centers-projects/teachmathscience/index.html/documents/CMC-N_09_MSTI_Sundar_Ribeiro.pdf)

California State University, Fresno sponsors free online courses/workshops which review subject matter content included on the CSET subtests for math and science to help prepare future teachers to meet subject matter competency.

###### [**CSET Courses/Workshops for Single Subject Mathematics and Science Teachers**](https://sites.google.com/site/csunmsti/#TOC-GENERAL-SCIENCE---Subtest-I-CSET-215-):

California State University, Northridge is offering free CSET Preparation Workshops in Math-1, Math-2, Math-3, General Science, Physics-2, and Chemistry-2.

###### [**CSET Courses/Workshops for Multiple Subject and Single Subject Mathematics and Science Teachers**:](https://education.sdsu.edu/research-innovation/stemed/msti)

San Diego State University offers free online CSET Preparation for algebra, geometry, foundational sciences, and multiple subjects math and science.

###### **[CSET Math Subtests I, II & III Resources:](https://rfdr266.wixsite.com/matheducation)**

San Jose State University offers free online videos for the Math Subtests I, II & III in addition to other resources and testing strategies.

###### [**CSET – Preparation Resources:**](https://open.uci.edu/collections/california_subject_examination_for_teachers__preparation_resources.html)

The University of California, Irvine offers free math and science online resources to assist prospective secondary math and science teachers to pass the qualifying exams.

## Information about CSET
