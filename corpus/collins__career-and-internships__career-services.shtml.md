# Career Services

![A student meeting a Hilton representative at the hospitality expo over a table indoors.](../img/career-expo-1.png)

## The Hospitality Expo

Behind every unforgettable experience is a team of hospitality professionals making it happen. At our annual Hospitality Expo, students and alumni connect with top industry employers and take the next step toward summer opportunities and post-graduation careers.

[Register Now](https://app.joinhandshake.com/stu/career_fairs/62827%20 "opens a new window")

## Shape Your Future

### Career Advising

Whether you're just starting out or ready to launch your career, our lead career coach can help you build polished application materials, feel confident in interviews and connect with jobs and internships that fit your goals.

[#### Schedule a One-on-One Meeting](https://cpp.campus.eab.com/pal/XydUHuk4tH)

[#### Drop-in Hours

Meet with one of our career advising team members during drop-in sessions for quick help with resumes, job searches, interview prep and more.](#modal-0 "Drop-In Hours")

### Drop-In Hours

×

Tuesdays: 1 p.m. - 2:30 p.m.

Thursdays: 3 p.m. - 4:30 p.m.

Located in building 80, H-Cafe.

Get help with:

* Resume review
* LinkedIn support
* Career tools and resources
