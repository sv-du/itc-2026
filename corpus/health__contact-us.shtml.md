# How to Contact Us

## Note:

Please have your 9-digit Bronco Number ready when calling Student Health Services

## Contacting Us

[### Hours of Operation](#modal-0 "Hours of Operation")

### Hours of Operation

×

* Monday through Friday, 8:00 am - 5:00 pm
* Closed weekends and university holidays.

Close

[### Phone Numbers](#modal-1 "Phone Numbers")

### Phone Numbers

×

**Student Health Services Contact Information**

* **Appointments with a clinician or nurse:**(909) 869-4000
* **Acute Care**: (909) 869-2740
* **Immunization**: (909) 869-4000
* **Bronco Wellness Center and Health Education:** (909) 869-5272
* **Medical Records:** (909) 869-2737 (download a [PDF Release of Medical Records form](img/medical-information-release-form-accessible.pdf))
* **Pharmacy**: (909) 869-2744
* **Laboratory**: (909) 869-2742
* **X-Ray**: (909) 869-2739

**24 hour After-Hours Care**

* **After Hours Nurse Advice Line** (*after 5pm weekdays & on weekends and holidays*): (855) 272-1723
* **988 Lifeline (Suicide Prevention):** [Call or text](https://988lifeline.org/) to 988
* **CAPS****After-Hours Crisis Support:** (909) 869-3220, press Option 2

Close

[### Email](#modal-2 "Email")

### Email

×

* **If you like to file a complaint:** [health@cpp.edu](https://www.cpp.edu/health/contact-us.shtml#mailto:health@cpp.edu "General Health Email")
* **Email general health questions or comments**: [health@cpp.edu](https://www.cpp.edu/health/contact-us.shtml#mailto:health@cpp.edu "General Health Email")
* **Email the Bronco Wellness Center:**[wellnessctr@cpp.edu](https://www.cpp.edu/health/contact-us.shtml#mailto:wellnessctr@cpp.edu "Wellness Center Email")

Close
