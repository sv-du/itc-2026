# Databases

Below are anonymized data samples extracted from **DMDb (the DarkMiner Database)**. The # columns represent the total number of records in the database, not just in the sample. We continuously scrape new data and discover additional websites to make DMDb the **most comprehensive** **criminal hacking database** available for research. Academic researchers and cybersecurity specialists with accredited affiliations can request access to a [full de-anonymized copy of the database here](https://forms.gle/eBYi82mYyEyKaXfH7). Access requires agreement to our **End-User License Agreement (EULA)** to ensure responsible use and prevent misuse. Upon successful credential verification, approved requesters will receive detailed instructions for downloading the complete database.
