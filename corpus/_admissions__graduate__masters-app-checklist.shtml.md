# Graduate Students

## Graduate Application Checklist

Apply online via [Cal State Apply](https://www2.calstate.edu/apply)to become a Bronco! Review application deadlines, explore majors and other application resources before applying.

Before you apply, we recommend that you carefully review the Cal Poly Pomona [admission requirements](masters-requirements.shtml) to be considered. Students who have been admitted should review the [Next Steps Checklist](masters-admitted.shtml).

## Application Checklist Resources

### ✓ Explore Programs

Visit our list of[Academic Programs.](https://www.cpp.edu/programs/index.shtml) To view specific program application filing periods, available application terms, deadlines and department information, you can [explore our graduate programs](masters-explore.shtml).

### ✓ Transcripts

Unofficial transcripts are to be uploaded at the time of application and official final transcripts must be submitted prior to enrollment. If you received your bachelor's degree from Cal Poly Pomona, you do not need to send official transcripts.

### ✓ Biodemographic Information

* Make sure to have the correct social security number as entering it incorrectly can impact your financial aid.
* Double-check that your name and date of birth are also entered correctly.
* Use an e-mail address that you will continue to be able to access. School e-mails are not recommended

### ✓ Review Application Guide

Applicants are encouraged to review the [Graduate Application Guide (PDF)](https://www.calstate.edu/apply/graduate/Documents/cal-state-apply-graduate-application-guide.pdf) when completing the Cal State Apply application.

### ✓ Apply Online

Apply online via [Cal State Apply](https://www2.calstate.edu/apply).

**Cal Poly Pomona makes admission decisions based on self-reported information from the application**! Provisionally admitted students who intend to enroll at Cal Poly Pomona will be required to submit an official transcript with successful completion of Bachelor's degree.

### ✓ Pay Application Fee

A $70 non-refundable application filing fee must be paid at the time of application submission. Instructions for payment can be found on [Cal State Apply](https://www2.calstate.edu/).

### ✓ Check Application Status

The Office of Admissions will communicate your next steps in the admission process via [Bronco Direct](https://www.cpp.edu/~broncodirect/), your student account.

**If you would like to withdraw your application, please submit the [withdrawal form](https://engage.cpp.edu/register/app_withdraw).**

## Technical Issues

#### **For technical issues regarding your application**, contact Cal State Apply customer support by email at CalStateApply@liaisoncas.com or by phone at (857) 304-2087.
