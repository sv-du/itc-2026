## Horse Camp

*A horse! a horse! my kingdom for a horse!*

---

The Discovery Camps are excited to have back the  [W. K. Kellogg Arabian Horse Center](https://www.cpp.edu/wkkelloggarabianhorsecenter/)  and its Horse Camps!!

![horse riding](img/horsecamper.jpg)

The W. K. Kellogg Arabian Horse Center is a showcase facility that houses approximately 70 purebred Arabian horses used in teaching, research, and breeding. The Center includes 38 acres of irrigated pasture, four barns for stabling horses, foaling stalls with an observation room, a breeding barn, a veterinary sciences laboratory, an arena, a farrier shop, and research facilities.

**Children ages 7-12** will have a galloping good time during our 5-day camp. Campers are paired two to one horse. Not just a horse-riding camp, the camp instructors guide campers through a curriculum comprised of basic equitation, horsemanship, grooming, tacking, nutrition, anatomy and humane education. Campers get to work arts & crafts centered on the horse, but are also required to perform chores. These chores include cleaning tack (horse accessories), feeding, boarding and maintenance of the stables. Our horse camp is sure to provide the camper with an unforgettable experience that result in unforgettable memories.

For questions about registration or waiting lists, please contact the Discovery camps at  discovery@cpp.edu. For camp or horse questions, contact the W.K. Kellogg Arabian Horse Center at  arabianhorse@cpp.edu or 909-869-4988.

### Session Dates and Pricing Info

---

The Horse Camps have two sessions (weeks) available. Sessions are limited to 10 children. Camp times are from 9:00am – 4:00pm.

Date and pricing table.

| **Week** | **Dates (2025)** | **Price per camper** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Week A  **SOLD OUT** | 6/2 - 6/6 | $600 |
| Week B  **SOLD OUT** | 6/9 - 6/13 | $600 |

There are no deposits to secure horse camp slots. Horse camp must be paid IN FULL at the time of registration. Lunch is included in the price.

### Cancellation Policy

---

Cancellations made 30 days before start date of your session will be refunded in full. **Cancellations made less than 30 days will be refunded up to 8 days before the start of camp****ONLY if your space is filled by a wait-listed camper, otherwise no refund on the tuition price can be given.** **Cancellations made within 7 days of the start of camp CANNOT be refunded.**

Refunds are not available for vacations, special events, illnesses of three or fewer days, or other personal commitments that prevent attendance. A refund may be available for extended illness of four or more consecutive program days. A doctor's note and written note from the parent or guardian explaining the situation must be received within seven days from the first day of absence.

### Ages

---

The Horse Camp is designed for ages 7-12. Exceptions will not be allowed.

### What To Bring

---

For your camper to have the best experience at the Horse Camp, it is important that s/he receive a good night’s sleep. It is also extremely important that your camper have a hearty meal for breakfast in order to participate in camp activities in a spirited and lively manner. We want your child to gain the most out of their journey into camp activities, horses and fun!

We recommend that campers dress in comfortable clothing that takes into account the high temperatures of summer in the Pomona area. Please insure that your camper arrives with the following:

* Please make sure your child is wearing jeans and boots with a small heel ( **NOT** tennis shoes)
* Helmets will be provided. If your child has an equestrian helmet we would appreciate it if they could bring their own.
* Refillable Water Bottle
* Besides the attire, we ask that your child also bring sunscreen.

### Lunch

---

Cal Poly Pomona has an amazing, new dining commons! Lunch meals are provided by the  [Center Pointe Dining Common](https://www.centerpointedining.com/) , which serves our student population year-round. Whether your camper is vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free, lactose intolerant or simply wants a healthy, balanced diet, Center Pointe will fulfill her/his needs with many tasty options. Registration forms contain areas to identify special dietary needs.

### No Extended Care due to Arabian Horse maintenance

---

The Horse Camp staff understands the challenges of meeting a 9am drop-off and 4pm pick-up schedule. However, our camp counselors have the responsibility to care and maintain for the horses after a long and hot day's work. Because of their effort to safekeep the horses and to attend to the horse's needs, our counselors are not able to care for campers beyond camp hours. We, therefore, require that all campers be dropped off at 8:45am and picked up by 4:15pm.

**From the 10 freeway**  **(east or west)**
 Exit at Kellogg Drive.  Stay in your LEFT lane, you should be on the left side of the large electronic marquee that welcomes visitors (see picture below).  Once you are at the marquee, you will also be at an intersection. Immediately take a left turn at the stop sign onto University Drive. At the next stop sign, turn right onto Eucalyptus Lane. In 800 feet, the Arabian Horse Center main entrance will be on your left (see purple building on map below). You may park in visitor parking to drop-off and pick-up your child.

### *By Public Transportation*

---

Riding the bus to the campus is simple.  Foothill Transit and Metro buses make convenient stops at the corner of Temple Avenue and South Campus Drive.  From there, you can walk through our beautiful campus or ride the  [Bronco Express](https://www.cpp.edu/transportation/img/19fall-shuttle-map.jpg)  (Route A) to the parking structure in Parking Lots F9 or E3, from which the Arabian Horse Center is a short walk.

[Foothill Transit](http://foothilltransit.org/trip-tools/trip-planner/) Bus Routes to Cal Poly Pomona include routes 190, 194, 195, 289, 480, 482, and 486. [Metrolink](https://metrolinktrains.com) Routes to Cal Poly Pomona stop at the Pomona North station, which is serviced by the [Bronco Link shuttles](https://www.cpp.edu/transportation/commuting-to-campus/bronco-shuttle.shtml).
