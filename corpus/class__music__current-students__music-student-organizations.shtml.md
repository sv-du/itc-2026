# Music Student Organizations

![Kappa Kappa Psi Greek letters logo in blue, representing the national honorary band fraternity.](../img/pictures/student-organizations/kappa-kappa-psi.jpg)

## National Honor Band Society

### Kappa Kappa Psi

Kappa Kappa Psi is a national honor band fraternity dedicated to service, leadership, and musicianship. Members support the Director of Bands and ensembles through service, social, and leadership activities. The fraternity enriches student life by fostering growth, community, and a deeper appreciation of music.

![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white-mobile.svg)
![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white.svg)

## National Association for Music Education

### NAfME

The National Association for Music Education comprises college and university students who are interested in working with young people through music. Members explore the many ways of teaching and sharing music in today’s society, network with teachers in the field, and gain experience in actual teaching.

![NAfME logo with stylized letters and musical forte symbol, representing music education advocacy.](../img/pictures/student-organizations/nafme-logo.jpg)
