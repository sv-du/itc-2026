# Graphic Communications Services

![Graphics Banner](/graphics/img/graphics_header)

Crafting innovative communications for our customers is the vision and goal for Graphic Communications Services. Our ability to do so is a result of our specialized knowledge, experience and capability to coordinate public and private partnerships in our industry.

Graphic Communications Services has been crafting innovative communications for its customers for better than 30 years.

In an effort to provide even broader services to our clients, we have developed and implemented a OneSTOP publishing service to meet campus needs. Our OneSTOP service is designed to address all of your reprographic publishing needs in a single location, simplifying the process for you.

## Graphic Communications Services Forms

* [GCS Request Form](faculty-staff/index.shtml) – for submitting GCS order requests. *Available to CPP faculty and staff only (authentication required).*

## About GCS

We invite you to review the many services we provide and have outlined on our webpage. We look forward to meeting with you to discuss your reprographic needs. We're sure that one or more of the formats we offer will enable you to address your audience in a cost effective and timely manner.

We pride ourselves on being competitive and professional and offering complete graphic and printing services to all our clients. Printing Services uses the team concept to improve production. The client is the most important part of the production process. Graphic Communications Services places a high value on its employees, consequently the moral is high and attitudes are positive. This translates to a pleasant and productive work environment that benefits all clients.

You are invited to visit our facilities in Building 5 Room 221 to see for yourself what a great shop we have. We think you will be impressed and will want to trust your printing projects to us! Come on in!

## Mission Statement

Graphic Communications Services exists to support the educational objectives of the University and to enhance its institutional image through quality publications. We apply a customer oriented management style that strives for excellence in quality, economy, and service.

## Department Philosophy

Our customers are our strength and we are proud of the relationships we maintain with them. We promise to give each publication, whether large or small, our undivided attention and will do everything in our power to meet our customer's expectations. Our commitment is to provide the highest quality workmanship at an unsurpassed level of customer service. We understand that our customers have financial constraints and we will strive to deliver the greatest value for their investment. We sincerely appreciate the trust placed in our department and will constantly endeavor to reward our customer's loyalty with excellence in everything we do.
