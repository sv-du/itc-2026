# Maintaining Eligibility

## Requirements to Maintain Eligibility for Continuing Students

**To maintain eligibility you must:**

* Monitor CPP email with program deadlines, registration, upcoming workshops, etc.
* Remain in the same major you selected when you came to CPP (unless Undeclared).
* Successfully complete 30 units toward your degree by the end of summer term every academic year.
* Complete GE Areas A2/1A (English) and B4/2 (Math) by summer term of your first year (if you're a part of the 4-Year program).
* Maintain a minimum grade point average of 2.5.
* Earn a C- or better or CR (Credit) in all course work taken.
* Be continuously enrolled for the fall and spring terms each year.
* Submit your academic plan for priority registration by the deadline each term. Create an appointment via [CPP Connect](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/cppconnect/index.shtml "https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/cppconnect/index.shtml") with your [advisor](http://www.cpp.edu/~studentsuccess/directories/advising-directory.shtml "http://www.cpp.edu/~studentsuccess/directories/advising-directory.shtml") if you need assistance updating your academic plan.
* Enroll in at least 15 units at Cal Poly Pomona in your first fall term.
