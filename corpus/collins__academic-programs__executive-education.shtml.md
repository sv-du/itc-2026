# Executive Education: Professional Development & Certifications

![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white-mobile.svg)
![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white.svg)

## Continue to Grow Professionally

The Collins College is your launchpad to leadership and industry connection. Through our Executive Education programs, gain practical experience, develop a strong network and earn professional certifications. We are the only Southern California college offering the Level One and Two Court of Master Sommeliers Certification exams.

![Students in professional attire walking in front of the Marriott](../img/25collins-marriott3.jpg)

## Expand Your Professional Horizons

[### Society of Wine Educators](#modal-0 "Society of Wine Educators (SWE)")

### Society of Wine Educators (SWE)

×

CCHM students receive discounted group rates on study guides and exams for SWE certifications. Exams are available worldwide through Pearson Testing Centers. SWE also offers online academies, a mobile app and “SWEbinars” to support exam preparation.

**Available Certifications:**

[Certified Specialist of Wine (CSW)](https://societyofwineeducators.org/education-certifications/certified-specialist-of-wine/)

[Certified Wine Educator (CWE)](https://societyofwineeducators.org/education-certifications/certified-wine-educator/)

[Certified Specialist of Spirits (CSS)](https://societyofwineeducators.org/education-certifications/certified-specialist-of-spirits/%20)

[Hospitality/Beverage Specialist Certificate](https://societyofwineeducators.org/education-certifications/hospitality-beverage-specialist/)

Close

[### Court of Master Sommeliers](#modal-1 "Court of Master Sommeliers")

### Court of Master Sommeliers

×

The Collins College is proud to be the *only* college in Southern California offering Level One and Level Two Certification exams through the Court of Master Sommeliers.

Students and alumni interested in participating should contact **collins@cpp.edu**.

Level I: Introductory Sommelier Course & Exam
This two-day course, led by Master Sommeliers, offers intensive training in wine and spirits knowledge, wine service and blind tasting techniques. It concludes with a 70-question multiple-choice exam.

Eligibility Requirements:

* Must be affiliated with Cal Poly Pomona (CCHM students, alumni or CPP students who have completed HRT 312, 315 or 316)

* Prior wine or service experience strongly recommended (completion of HRT 315/316 preferred)

* Not suitable for those with little or no wine/service background

Close

[### ServSafe Alcohol Certification](#modal-2 "ServSafe Alcohol Certification")

### ServSafe Alcohol Certification

×

Students can earn their [ServSafe Alcohol Certification](https://www.servsafe.com/ServSafe-Alcohol) online.

The Association of Beverage Professionals (ABP) at CCHM offers a four-hour in-person training and exam by request. A minimum of 12 students is required to schedule a session.

Close

[### Industry Association Support](#modal-3 "Industry Association Support")

### Industry Association Support

×

The Collins College partners with key hospitality organizations to enhance industry education and professional development. These include:

 [National Restaurant Association Educational Foundation (NRAEF)](https://chooserestaurants.org/)

 [California Restaurant Association (CRA)](https://www.calrest.org/)

 [Club Management Association of America (CMAA)](https://www.cmaa.org/)

Support includes curriculum and course development, workshops and seminars tailored to evolving industry needs.

For more information, contact collins@cpp.edu.
