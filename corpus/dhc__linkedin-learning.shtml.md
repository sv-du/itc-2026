# Link CPP LinkedIn Learning to Personal Account

## Free LinkedIn Learning Access for CPP Students

As a Cal Poly Pomona (CPP) student, you have **free access to LinkedIn Learning**, a platform with thousands of expert-led courses in technology, business, creative fields, and more. Whether you’re looking to boost your resume, learn new technical skills, or explore a creative passion, LinkedIn Learning has something for you.

## Why Connect Your Account

Connecting your CPP LinkedIn Learning account to your **personal LinkedIn profile** ensures that your:

* Completed courses and certificates
* Learning history and progress
* Personalized learning recommendations

... stay with you even after you graduate or lose access to your CPP email.

This connection helps you build a stronger personal brand and showcase your skills and achievements directly on your LinkedIn profile for employers to see.

## How to Connect Your CPP LinkedIn Learning to Your Personal Account

Follow these simple steps to link your accounts:

![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white-mobile.svg)
![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white.svg)

## Step 1

Log in to LinkedIn Learning using your CPP email and password

[LinkedIn Learning](https://www.linkedin.com/checkpoint/enterprise/login/56973593?application=learning&authModeName=CPP  "opens a new window")

![Step 1](img/linkedin-learning-step1.png)

![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white-mobile.svg)
![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white.svg)

## Step 2

Click your profile icon in the top right and go to Settings

![Step 2](img/linkedin-learning-step2.png)

![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white-mobile.svg)
![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white.svg)

## Step 3

Look for the option Connect your LinkedIn account and click it.

![Step 3](img/linkedin-learning-step3.png)

![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white-mobile.svg)
![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white.svg)

## Step 4

Sign in to your personal LinkedIn account when prompted

![Step 4](img/linkedin-learning-step4.png)

![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white-mobile.svg)
![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white.svg)

## Step 5

Confirm the connection. Your CPP learning history and certificates will now be synced with your personal account

![Step 5](img/linkedin-learning-step5.png)

## Setting Up Your LinkedIn Profile

Once your accounts are connected, make sure your LinkedIn profile reflects your skills and accomplishments. If you don’t have a profile yet, or want to improve your current one, check out our guide:

[Setting Up Your LinkedIn Profile](https://cpp.instructure.com/courses/54888/pages/setting-up-linkedin-profile "Setting Up Your LinkedIn Profile")

This guide will help you build a professional, polished profile to maximize the benefits of your LinkedIn Learning experience.
