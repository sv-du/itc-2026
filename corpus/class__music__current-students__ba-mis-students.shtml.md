## Build Your Future in Music, Business, and Technology

The B.A. in Music Industry Studies at Cal Poly Pomona prepares you to turn your passion for music into a career. You’ll gain a strong foundation in music theory, performance, history, and technology—while also diving into industry-focused areas like audio production, recording, business, and marketing. This interdisciplinary program is designed for musicians who want to work in recording, live sound, publishing, artist management, and beyond. You’ll apply what you learn in real-world settings through internships with music and entertainment organizations, giving you the hands-on experience and professional network to launch your career or pursue graduate study.
