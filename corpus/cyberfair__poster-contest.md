## About the CSAF Poster Contest

The field of cybersecurity is evolving with emerging threats and technologies. The annual poster contest aims to help students learn more about cybersecurity through engaging with peers and professionals. By participating, students improve their communication skills, engage with industry leaders, lay a foundation for a future dissertation, or even win a prize. High school and college students are eligible to participate individually or in small teams. Exceptional entries have the chance to present their work at the Cybersecurity and Awareness Fair, based on the quality of their abstract and poster drafts.

Thank you to everyone who participated in and visited the 2025 Cybersecurity & Awareness Fair Poster Contest.
