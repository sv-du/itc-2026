# GIS Day

## Geographic Information System Day

![GIS Day 2025 Career Panel ](img/2025-gis-day-career-panel.jpg)

GIS Day is an international event, and an opportunity for our Cal Poly Pomona (CPP) community, including prospective students, schools, organizations, industry partners, and the public, to share their amazing work and projects. CPP connects our community, making GIS Day a dynamic local and educational experience for everyone.

![GIS Day 2025 banner](img/2025-gis-day-banner.png)

**This year's GIS Day was on November 19, 2025** and included a panel discussion, hands-on activities, and featured CPP students showcasing real-world applications of the latest GIS technology.

### The GIS Day 2025 Agenda

Time and session titles

| **Time** | **Session** |
| --- | --- |
| 10 - 10:10 a.m. | Welcome & Opening Remarks |
| 10:10 - 10:30 a.m. | CPP Geospatial Strategy with Esri |
| 10:40 - 11:20 a.m. | Career Panel |
| 11:30 a.m. - 12:15 p.m. | Lunch & Networking |
| 12:30 - 1:30 p.m. | Hands-on Activities |
| 1:30 - 2:00 p.m. | Feedback Survey & Closing Remarks |

**GIS Day will return to CPP on November 18, 2026!**

### Explore GIS in the University Library This Week

×

We invite faculty, staff, and students to stop by the Library on GIS Day, and **Tuesdays or Thursdays** to check out our new **GIS displays and Surface Studio all-in-one computers.** These interactive stations feature **ArcGIS StoryMaps,** a hands-on way to explore maps, data, and storytelling.

**Location:** Take the escalator, stairs, or elevator up to the library's second floor. Surface Studio displays are past the entrance, near the computers and printing area.

**When:**

* **Tuesdays and Thursdays** – 10:00 a.m. – 3:30 a.m.

**Student GIS assistants** will be available during these times to answer questions, demonstrate the tools, and provide general GIS assistance. Whether you’re completely new to GIS or just curious, stop by to explore how maps can help tell your story.
