# Reasons to Choose the Huntley College

## Get to Know Us

![Assistant Professor Eshwar Ravishankar works with two students in a greenhouse.](../img/2024-greenhouse-ravishankar-920x570.jpg)

Agriculture. Food. Apparel

They're all big business, offering countless career opportunities in California and around the world.

At the Huntley College of Agriculture, you can learn the science and technology involved in each of these fields.

You'll have valuable hands-on learning experiences along the way, whether it's working with animals large and small; growing crops in fields or greenhouses; creating new food products or helping people lead healthier lives; designing and producing a fashion line; or learning what it takes to run a business.

Come join us. Discover your place in the world.

## Program Recognitions

[![Illustration of a briefcase or satchel](../img/icon-abm-920x702.jpg)

### Agribusiness & Food Industry Management

The only bachelor's degree program of its kind in California.](/agri/agribusiness-agricultural-science/programs/agribusiness-and-food-industry-management-bs.shtml%20 "opens a new window")

[![Illustration of a tractor](../img/icon-ags-920x702.jpg)

### Agricultural Science

Offers flexibility to focus coursework on a variety of agricultural industry sectors. The only program of its kind in Southern California.](/agri/agribusiness-agricultural-science/programs/agriculture-science-bs.shtml%20 "opens a new window")

[![Illustration of an animal paw print](../img/icon-ahs-920x702.jpg)

### Animal Health Science

Prepares students to become the veterinary equivalent of a nurse. It's the only four-year program on the West Coast accredited by the American Veterinary Medical Association.](/agri/animal-science/programs/animal-health-science-bs.shtml%20 "opens a new window")

[![Illustration of dresses](../img/icon-amm-920x702.jpg)

### Apparel Merchandising & Management

The only program on the West Coast accredited by the Textiles and Apparel Programs Accreditation Commission (TAPAC), certifying that it provides the highest quality educational experiences to prepare students for careers in the fashion and apparel industries. In addition, the program is one of only 12 recognized by the American Apparel & Footwear Association.](/agri/apparel-merchandising-and-management/programs/apparel-merchandising-and-management-bs.shtml%20 "opens a new window")

[![Illustration of a horse](../img/icon-as-920x702.jpg)

### Animal Science

The only animal sciences or animal and veterinary sciences department in Southern California.](/agri/animal-science/programs/animal-science-bs.shtml%20 "opens a new window")

[![Illustration of a microscope](../img/icon-fst-920x702.jpg)

### Food Science & Technology

The only undergraduate program in Southern California approved by the Institute of Food Technologists (IFT).](/agri/nutrition-food-science/programs/food-science-and-technology-bs.shtml%20 "opens a new window")

[![Illustration of a plate with fork, knife, and spoon.](../img/icon-ntr-920x702.jpg)

### Nutrition

Our dietetics undergraduate program is accredited by the Accreditation Council for Education in Nutrition and Dietetics (ACEND).](/agri/nutrition-food-science/programs/nutrition-bs.shtml%20 "opens a new window")

[![Illustration of a plant with three leaves.](../img/icon-plt-920x702.jpg)

### Plant Science

Our curriculum completes all the academic requirements for the California Pest Control Advisor License. More than 50 percent of our students attain this license.](/agri/plant-science/programs/plant-science-bs.shtml%20 "opens a new window")
