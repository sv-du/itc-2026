# Foothill Transit Class Pass

### ![Free unlimited rides with foothill transit](img/class-pass-banner.png)

## Students at Cal Poly Pomona get FREE RIDES on Foothill Transit Buses

Welcome to Cal Poly Pomona! As a Bronco, you get unlimited FREE RIDES on Foothill Transit buses with Class Pass. Foothill Transit Lines 190,194,195, 289, 295, 480, 482, 486, and Silver Streak all serve the Cal Poly Pomona campus – so you can get to class, downtown LA, or anywhere in between for free!

**Download the TAP App to get your Class Pass today!**

* **Get your eligibility code** that you can find in your [student portal.](https://my.cpp.edu/uPortal/f/home/normal/render.uP) You'll see it on the top right corner of the 'Student Life' tab. If accessing your portal via phone, use the search bar to search for Foothill Transit and select the first option that appears, *Foothill Transit Class Pass.*Keep this code handy throughout the process.
* **Download the app.** Search “TAP LA” on the App Store or Play Store.
* **Create an account.** Follow the prompts on the app to create your account.
* **Click the Cards section to add a TAP card.** Click the icon at the top of the screen then select:
  + **Add a TAP Card to Apple Wallet (iPhone)**
  + **Add a TAP Card to my phone (Android)**
* **Enter your code.** In the app, select “Add Stored Value or Pass” then select “Redeem Code”. Next, enter your eligibility code that you accessed in your student portal.
* **Claim your Class Pass.** The Class Pass will be added to your cart – don’t worry it’ll be free. Press “purchase” and you’re all set to ride! Make sure to use your pass within two weeks to activate it.

Need more help? Click [here](https://www.taptogo.net/Fareless/articles/en_US/GoPass_article/Add-your-GoPass-TAP-Card-to-your-Phone) for more information using the TAP App to load your pass.

Make sure to use your pass on the bus within two weeks of registering online to activate your pass for the school year.

Don’t have a smartphone? Don’t worry. Stop by the BRIC to pick up a TAP card and check your eligibility. Make sure to note down your eligibility code from your student portal, too. You’ll need that to sign up. Then, visit taptogo.net/calpolypomonaclasspass and complete the prompts. Learn more at [foothilltransit.org/cpp](https://www.foothilltransit.org/cpp).

***NEW!* Ride Foothill Transit Bus Line 295 from the San Dimas Metro A-Line station straight to Cal Poly Pomona.**

**Directions to Bus Stop:**

**When exiting the San Dimas station, walk west towards San Dimas Ave., turn right on San Dimas Ave. and continue walking until you reach Bonita Ave. The Line 295 bus stop is located just steps from the corner of San Dimas Ave. and Bonita Ave.**

## Learn More

### Who is Eligible for the Class Pass?

All currently enrolled undergraduate and graduate students with at least 1 credit unit at Cal Poly Pomona are eligible to get a Class Pass. **Continuing Education Unit (CEU) Online Learning, CPGE, and Certificate enrolled students are not eligible at this time.**

### What's a Class Pass?

Class Pass is a program that gives current students unlimited rides on Foothill Transit buses all school year long. It also helps the entire community by reducing traffic and parking congestion around campus. Not bad for a piece of plastic.

The Class Pass is a digital card (TAP card) that is accepted on all Foothill Transit Local and Silver Streak buses, which connect you to 22 different cities throughout the San Gabriel Valley and downtown Los Angeles. Your Class Pass does not include the Commuter Express Lines 490, 493, 495, 498, 499 and 699.

Your class pass is loaded onto a TAP card that is accepted on 26 transit agencies across LA County. If you want to transfer to another agency, you can load cash value onto your card.

### How Do I Get My Class Pass?

**Download the TAP App to get your Class Pass today!**

1. **Download the app.** Search “TAP LA” on the App Store or Play Store.
2. **Create an account.** Follow the prompts on the app to create your account.
3. **Click the Cards section to add a TAP card.** Click the icon at the top of the screen then select
   1. **Add a TAP Card to Apple Wallet (iPhone)**
   2. **Add a TAP Card to my phone (Android)**
4. **Enter your code.** In the app, select “Add Stored Value or Pass” then select “Redeem Code”. Next, enter your eligibility code that you can find in your student portal.
5. **Claim your Class Pass.** The Class Pass will be added to your cart – don’t worry it’ll be free. Press “purchase” and you’re all set to ride! Make sure to use your pass within two weeks to activate it.

Need more help? Click [here](https://www.taptogo.net/Fareless/articles/en_US/GoPass_article/Add-your-GoPass-TAP-Card-to-your-Phone) for more information using the TAP App to load your pass.

Make sure to use your pass on the bus within two weeks of registering online to activate your pass for the school year.

Don’t have a smartphone? Don’t worry. Stop by the BRIC to pick up a TAP card and check your eligibility. Make sure to note down your eligibility code from your student portal, too. You’ll need that to sign up.

### Will my Class Pass still be valid over summer?

Yes! The Class Pass is valid through the start of the next school year. You don't need to be enrolled in summer courses.

Foothill Transit Lines 190, 194, 195, 289, 295, 480, 482, and 486 travel throughout campus, with stops along Campus Dr and Temple Ave — check out the map for locations of stops.

### What If I Don't Know How to Ride the Bus?

1. Use [Google Transit](http://maps.google.com/landing/transit/index.html) or the [Trip Planner](http://foothilltransit.org/trip-tools/trip-planner/ "Trip Planner") at the top of our website to find out what bus line you need and where your nearest bus stop is located — then check [NEXTRIP](http://foothilltransit.org/trip-tools/nextrip/) for real-time updates that show exactly when your bus will arrive.
2. Go to your bus stop — it’ll be marked with a large sign in Foothill Transit blue and green.3. Make sure you board the right bus — when a bus pulls up to the stop, look at its line number and destination on the sign above the windshield.
3. Pay your fare — just touch your Class Pass to the “TAP” symbol right on the farebox and listen for the beep.
4. Move as far to the back as you can, and find a seat or hold onto the handrail — please leave seats at the front open for elderly and disabled passengers.
5. Request a stop — when the bus approaches your stop, pull the cord or press the tape running along the windows, then exit through the doors in the back.

Still have questions? Check out [Bus Basics](http://www.foothilltransit.org/trip-tools/bus-basics/), call at 800-RIDE-INFO (743-3463), or visit our the Foothill Transit website at [foothilltransit.org](http://foothilltransit.org/) anytime!
