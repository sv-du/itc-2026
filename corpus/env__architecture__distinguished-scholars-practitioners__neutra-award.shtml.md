# Richard Neutra Award for Professional Excellence

![Richard Neutra on the roof of the VDL (Julius Shulman Photography  Archive, The Getty Research Institute) ](../img/neutra-julius-shulman.jpg)

*Richard Neutra on the roof of the VDL (Julius Shulman Photography Archive - The Getty Research Institute)*

Richard Neutra was an acclaimed modern architect and contributor to the *Arts and Architecture, [Case Study House Program](https://nam11.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.artsandarchitecture.com%2Fcase.houses%2F&data=04%7C01%7Csbgonzaga%40cpp.edu%7C36388090990a437c47f708d977169841%7C164ba61e39ec4f5d89ffaa1f00a521b4%7C0%7C0%7C637671761187585520%7CUnknown%7CTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJWIjoiMC4wLjAwMDAiLCJQIjoiV2luMzIiLCJBTiI6Ik1haWwiLCJXVCI6Mn0%3D%7C1000&sdata=wXzOGm1w0KNAfXm%2FrC%2FV1ROQV7syRPpl657pGGfBpM8%3D&reserved=0 "Original URL: http://www.artsandarchitecture.com/case.houses/. Click or tap if you trust this link.")*. The Neutra Award recognizes the contributions Neutra made to the practice of architecture through his design research by honoring architects and individuals who have dedicated their career toward the research, design and development of innovative environments in which to work, live and play.

Neutra served as a professor at Cal Poly Pomona in a joint appointment with the departments of Architecture, Landscape Architecture, and Urban and Regional Planning until his passing in 1970.

Cal Poly Pomona and the College of Environmental Design are the owners and caretakers of the [Neutra VDL Studio and Residences](https://www.cpp.edu/env/culture/neutra-vdl.shtml), a Los Angeles house that Neutra built and resided in.
