# Custom Short-Term Programs

**We at CPELI love our short-term programs!**

For more than 25 years, CPELI has collaborated with universities abroad, other educational organizations, and with companies to develop and deliver customized short-term programs. These programs are designed to combine the best in English language development with academic or professionally focused content across a wide array of disciplines.

## What is a short-term program?

Each program is customized to fit the learning needs and interests of the participants, using the resources available at Cal Poly Pomona, a comprehensive university uniquely situated close to all that Southern California has to offer.

Program size may range from small groups of six to eight students to any number -- and for the length of time best suited to meet achievable academic and experiential goals. International short-term program participants may attend with B-1, F-1, or J-1 visas, depending on program specifics.

## How do we create a customized short-term program?

To send a short-term group to CPELI, you will work with the Program Manager. Beginning with program requirements from the sending institution, and incorporating ideas and opportunities we can offer, our Program Manager will work with you to design an interesting, immersive, impactful program that meets your institution’s timeframe, budget and academic requirements.

Information needed to get started:

* Program dates
* Number of participants
* Academic and experiential goals

Get in touch with us by sending an email to cpeli@cpp.edu.

## Program Features

Short term programs often feature:

* Volunteer work opportunities on campus and in the community
* Curriculum supportive excursions, such as the Science Center, observation site visits for hospitality management, etc.
* Lectures by Cal Poly Pomona faculty
* Explore world-famous Southern California attractions such as Disneyland, Hollywood, and Santa Monica and Venice Beaches
* On-Campus housing in residence halls or student apartments, or at Cal Poly Pomona’s campus hotel

## Sample Programs

* English Language Teacher Development
* English for Engineering majors
* English for the Aviation Hospitality professionals
* English for Leadership and Entrepreneurship
* English Language and American Culture
* English for Specific Purpose (you tell us your area of interest)
