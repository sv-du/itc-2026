# Training Opportunities

## Safety/Active Shooter Response Training

Safety/Active Shooter Response Training is available by request to any student or employee group, club, or department on campus.

Please submit requests *at least* two weeks in advance. All trainings/presentations are facilitated by UPD officers and are subject to their availability. If an emergency situation occurs, scheduled trainings may be cancelled without advance notice

[UPD TRAINING REQUEST FORM](https://app.smartsheet.com/b/form/04df9a55d86145c9bd0d6a9d22efdb89)

For more information please contact: mhillman@cpp.edu
