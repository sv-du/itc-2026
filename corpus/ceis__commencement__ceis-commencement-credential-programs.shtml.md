# Commencement Details for Credential Completers

![Students in regalia cheering at commencement behind a greenery. ](../img/0055-0230534-seye-dsc08666.jpg)

## Commencement Information

CEIS Commencement ceremony will take place in the University Quad on May 15, 2026 at 8 A.M.
CEIS Commencement provides credential students an opportunity to celebrate their hard work and commitment to education alongside degree candidates, following the same commencement timeline and event expectations.

## FAQ

Expand all

### Who Is Eligible to Participate?

* Credential Students enrolled in the following courses will receive communication to apply for commencement:
  + EDU 5160, 5260, 5345, 5346, 5347, 5348, 5410, 5420, EDL5470
* Administrative Exception may be granted through the  Credential Office in coordination with the Registrar’s Office.
* Students dual-enrolled in a Credential and a Master’s or EdD program must go through the regular Apply for Graduation process.

### What terms are included in Commencement 2026?

Fall 2025, Spring 2026, Summer 2026 Credential Completers.

### Do I have to participate Commencement Ceremony?

No, credential completers are not required to participate in the Commencement Ceremony. But you will still need to pay the $25 Credential Evaluation Fee.

**Step 1:** Log into BroncoDirect Student Center < click down arrow byAcademics < select Credential Commencement

![instructions](../credonlys1.png)

**Step 2:** Select - To Opt-out, select “Confirm – I will not participate in commencement.”

![instructions](../optouts2.png)

### How do I receive Commencement Updates

After you pay for your commencement fee you will be added to the 2026 Commencement List and will receive regular updates from [commencement@cpp.edu](commencement%40cpp.edu)

### When Does the Application Link Open?

* Spring 2026: Application Period is January 31st - March 6th, 2026
* The application link opens one day after the add/drop period ends and closes on the first Friday of March.
  + *\*Note: The Fall term **closing date** is the offical end of the term.*

### How to apply for Credential Commencement?

[How to Apply for Credential Commencement PDF](../ceis-credential-commencement-in-bd-accessible1.pdf)

1. Students will receive an email from CEIS inviting them to opt in to Credential Commencement.
2. To Apply, log in to your [BroncoDirect Student Center](https://my.cpp.edu/uPortal/f/u25l1s4/p/broncodirect.n9/max/render.uP).
3. Select Academics
4. Select Credential Commencement.
5. Choose Opt-In to Participate in Commencement.
6. Pay the $100 Commencement Fee (non-refundable).
7. Once payment is completed and verified, the student’s name will be added to the Commencement Dashboard.

**Step 1:** Log into BroncoDirect Student Center < click down arrow byAcademics < select Credential Commencement

* To Opt-In:  Select “Yes, I would like to participate in commencement.”

### Does participation in the Commencement Ceremony mean that I have received my credential?

**No.** Participating in the Commencement Ceremony does **not** mean that you have received your credential. Commencement is a celebratory event, while credentials are awarded separately after all program and certification requirements have been officially completed and verified.

* Teaching Credential Completers - certification updates will complete from the Credential Office.
* PASC Completers - certification updates will complete from the Credential Office.

### Is there a fee?

[How to Pay Your Graduation Fees on BroncoDirect (PDF))](https://www.cpp.edu/registrar/forms/how-to-pay-your-graduation-fees-on-broncodirect.pdf).

Your selection in the portal will determine the required fees:

* **Credential-only students**

  + **Participating in Commencement:** **$125 total**
    (**$100** Commencement Fee + **$25** Credential Evaluation Fee)

A $100 commencement fee is required to cover the ceremony. In addition, a separate $25 credential evaluation fee, payable directly to the Credential Office, is required to process state-level recommendations. Regalia must also be purchased to participate in the ceremony.

* **Credential + Master’s/EdD students**

  + **$25** Credential Evaluation Fee

You will also be redirected to complete the *Apply for Graduation* process.

* **Not participating in Commencement:**

  + A **$25** credential evaluation fee, payable directly to the Credential Office, is required to process state-level recommendations.

All fees must be paid at the time of submission in BroncoDirect. Once submitted, you will receive confirmation showing your selection, timestamp, and applied fees.

[Visit: How to Pay Your Graduation Fees on BroncoDirect (PDF))](https://www.cpp.edu/registrar/forms/how-to-pay-your-graduation-fees-on-broncodirect.pdf).

* To confirm commencement participation, you must select Yes and make the commencement payment.

### Do I have to wear a Regalia?

Yes, all credential completers who are participating in commencement are required to wear a CPP Complete regalia (cap, gown, CPP stole, and tassel).

### Where do international students request a letter of invitation for family to attend Commencement 2026?

Students can email Bronco Advising Center (bac@cpp.edu) for any letter requests.
