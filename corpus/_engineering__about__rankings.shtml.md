  + Top 3 in California

## Diversity: Nationally Recognized for Recruiting, Enrolling and Graduating a Diverse Student Body

* Top 5 Top Producers of Hispanic Engineering Graduates, [Diverse Issues in Higher Education 2021](https://polycentric.cpp.edu/2022/10/cal-poly-pomona-ranked-a-top-producer-of-hispanic-graduates/)
* Top 10 College of Hispanics, [Hispanic Outlook on Education Magazine](https://www.hispanicoutlook.com/articles/top-100-colleges-and-universities-for-hispanics)
* Top 10 Nationally for B.S. Degrees Awarded to Underrepresented Minorities, [ASEE Engineering by the Numbers 2022](https://ira.asee.org/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/Engineering-and-Engineering-Technology-by-the-Numbers-2022.pdf#page=19)
