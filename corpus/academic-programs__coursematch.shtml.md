# Office of Academic Programs

## What is CourseMatch?

CourseMatch is a subset of CSU Fully Online courses designed to increase access of GE courses across the system.

**Eligibility**: To be considered a "CourseMatch" course, a department must demonstrate their GE course is taught by a trained faculty member, that student outcomes are reasonably similar to face-to-face instruction, and that the course's DFW rates.

**Process**: At CPP, departments identify fully online courses and their faculty training, and the Office of Undergraduate Studies takes care of the data analysis and submission.

**Benefits**: In addition to providing greater access to students to complete their degrees, departments are incentivized with funds for the CourseMatch students they enroll.
