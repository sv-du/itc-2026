# AI Hackathon

### **Creative AI solutions. Cross-disciplinary collaboration. Community impact.**

---

#### The **AI Hackathon for a Better Future**, hosted annually by the **Mitchell C. Hill Center for Digital Innovation**, brings together **Cal Poly Pomona students, faculty, and industry professionals** to develop innovative, AI-powered solutions to some of society’s most pressing challenges. Designed to be **interdisciplinary, inclusive, and hands-on**, the event promotes learning through experimentation and showcases the power of artificial intelligence to drive change.

---

### **Each year, students form teams to tackle problems in areas such as:**

---

* #### Sustainability and the environment
* #### Public health and wellness
* #### Education and workforce development
* #### Cybersecurity and digital trust
* #### Social equity and ethical AI

  ---

### **Real-World Challenges + Real-Time Innovation**

---

#### *Participants spend two days working intensively on AI-based solutions, guided by mentors from faculty and industry. The event includes:*

* #### Lightning talks and technical workshops
* #### Mentorship from faculty and professionals
* #### Judged presentations and awards
* #### Networking and recruiting opportunities

  ---

### **Get Involved**

#### We invite **faculty** to sign up! There are **opportunities to mentor student teams and serve as judges**. Contact **mhcenter@cpp.edu**

#### We also encourage **students from all majors** to participate. Whether you're new to AI or an experienced coder, this is your chance to learn, build, and compete.

### Register to participate <https://www.cpp.edu/cba/ai-hackathon/index.shtml>
