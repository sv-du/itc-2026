## About Me

VCD 1330 – Foundations in Ceramics

Jen Greenwood is a multidisciplinary artist and educator with over 20 years of teaching experience. Her socially engaged practice blends storytelling, science, and community building, with a current focus on ceramics and instant photography (ask her about my restored 1972 Polaroid Land Camera). She is an active member of professional arts organizations and exhibits work nationally.

She hold a BA in Art from Colorado College and an MFA in Fine Arts from Claremont Graduate University, where she also earned a Certificate in Inclusive Excellence in Professional Practice.

Before returning to academia, she worked as a visual designer and creative director for Fortune 500 companies, public institutions, and nonprofit organizations. Most notably, she spent 14 years at Apple Inc. as a creative and training professional.
