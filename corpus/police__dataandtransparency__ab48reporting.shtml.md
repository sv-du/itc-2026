# AB 48 Reporting - Kinetic Projectiles

## **Kinetic Energy Projectile or Chemical Agent for Crowd Control Use Reporting. AB 48, Penal Code (PC) section 13652.1(a)**

*Penal Code (PC) section 13652.1(a) requires each law enforcement agency to “publish a summary on its Internet website of all instances in which a peace officer employed by that agency uses a kinetic energy projectile or chemical agent, as those terms are defined in [Penal Code] Section 13652, for crowd control,” within 60 days of each incident. Law enforcement agencies “may extend that period for another 30 days if they demonstrate just cause, but in no case longer than 90 days from the time of the incident.”*

**From the initial date of PC 13652.1 going into effect, Cal Poly Pomona University Police Department as had no incidents to report.**

Read PC 13652 in full:  [Penal Code (PC) section 13652.](https://leginfo.legislature.ca.gov/faces/codes_displaySection.xhtml?sectionNum=13652.&nodeTreePath=7.7&lawCode=PEN)

Read PC 13652.1 in full:  [Penal Code (PC) section 13652.1(a)](https://leginfo.legislature.ca.gov/faces/codes_displaySection.xhtml?sectionNum=13652.1.&lawCode=PEN)
