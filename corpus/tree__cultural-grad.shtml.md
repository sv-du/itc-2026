# Graduation Celebrations

## 2025 Celebrations

The Centers for T.R.E.E. Graduation Celebrations are campus-wide events that honor the diverse achievements and academic excellence of all students, celebrate their success and recognize their loved ones and the communities who supported them along the way.

All graduation celebrations are open and available to all, regardless of race, sex, color, ethnicity or national origin. Students may register for more than one celebration.

Join us in celebrating YOU! These ceremonies are not intended to replace Commencement, but offer an additional ceremony for those who wish to participate. We highly encourage participants to also apply for CPP’s official commencement graduation.

*\*Students who have graduated in Fall, are graduating this Spring or will be graduating in Summer are encouraged to register.*

**Ceremonies**

* Native Graduation Celebration
* Raza Graduation Celebration
* WRC Graduation Celebration
* Lavender Graduation Celebration
* Asian, Pacific Islander & Desi-American Graduation Celebration
* Black Graduation Celebration
