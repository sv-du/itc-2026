## Leveraging California State Funding to Power Learning

Access state-funded training through the Employer Training Panel (ETP) and customized upskilling programs designed to enhance performance, retention, and innovation in your organization.

## Employer Training Panel (ETP) Overview

* ETP is a California state funded agency that provides funds for incumbernt woker training
* The funds are collected from California employers through a special payroll tax

Your company has contributed to this. Now is the time for your workforce to benefit!

## ETP Eligibility

Expand all

### Employers

• must contribute to the California Unemployment Insurance Fund

• must have a valid California Employer Account Number (CEAN) or Sales Tax ID

### Employees

• must be full-time (35+ hours/week) and work in California

• must meet the ETP wage requirements

• remain employed for a minimum of 90 days post-training
