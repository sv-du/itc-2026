# Bronco Highlights Module Tutorial

## Instructions

In this Module, it is recommended to have at least four logos. The max number of allowable logos is six.

The module is available for use across the following template pages:

* [College Department Page](../templates-instructions/college-department-page-instruction.shtml)
* [College Landing Page](../templates-instructions/college-landing-page-instruction.shtml)

***Page View***

![](img/bronco-highlights-page-view.png)

For this section you will need:

* 1 Image
* 3 Headings (featured title, story title, logo title)
* 1 Description (story summary)
* 1 Link to a featured story
* 1 Link to an alumni page
* 4 (minimum) to 6 (maximum) high-quality logos of top employers of graduates of your college.
