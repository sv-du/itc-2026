# Financial Aid

Attending summer at Cal Poly Pomona is a great way to make up credits, make up a class you may have dropped, or just get ahead in your coursework and complete your degree!

[Financial aid](https://www.cpp.edu/financial-aid) can help cover some of the costs of taking classes during the summer. Since the summer semester at Cal Poly Pomona is considered the last term of the academic year, your eligibility will be based on your 2025-2026 FAFSA application.  For Undergraduate students, aid funding for the summer comes from the Pell Grant, Cal Grant, or Direct Loans. Your aid options will typically consist of any aid you did not use during the academic year.

Due to limited available funding, your summer aid package may be very different from the awards and amounts you received during the academic year. Specifically, State University Grant (SUG), SEOG, and EOP funding is not available during the summer.

**Full details about financial aid for Summer 2026 will be posted on the [Financial Aid & Scholarships](https://www.cpp.edu/financial-aid/other-programs/summer/index.shtml) website.**

### **Undergraduate**

Students enrolled in at least 6 units for summer are eligible for any remaining federal loan eligibility not used during the academic year.

![illustration of a stack of bills](img/pell-grant-icon.png) Federal Student Loans

![illustration of hands shaking](img/federal-student-loan-icon.png) Federal Pell Grant\*

![illustration of document with writing on it](img/federal-parent-loans.png)Federal Parent Loans

![illustration of document with writing on it](img/private-education-loans.png) Private Education Loans

![illustration of graduation cap](img/department-scholarships.png) Department Scholarships

\*Eligibility varies and is based on enrollement and EFC from the FAFSA.

### **Graduate**

Students enrolled in at least 3 units for summer are eligible for any remaining federal loan eligibility not used during the academic year.

![illustration of a stack of bills](img/pell-grant-icon.png) Federal Student Loans

![illustration of document with writing on it](img/federal-parent-loans.png)Federal Direct Graduate PLUS Loans

![illustration of document with writing on it](img/private-education-loans.png)Private Education Loans

![illustration of graduation cap](img/department-scholarships.png) Department Scholarships

\* A summer Direct Grad PLUS loan can be requested when Unsubsidized Loan eligibility has been exhausted.
