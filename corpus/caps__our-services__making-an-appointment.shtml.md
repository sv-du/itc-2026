# Making an Appointment

## How to Make an Appointment

![](../img/banner-telephone.jpg)

If you are a student seeking support services, or resources from **Counseling and Psychological Services**, please let us know by telephone at (909) 869-3220 during our normal business hours. Have your Bronco ID number ready when calling, and your weekly availability.

**Counseling and Psychological Services** offers a variety of counseling services to help students adjust to college life, cope with personal stressors, and address psychological concerns.  Our services include workshops, group or individual therapy, wellbeing coaching, crisis support, and case management/resource assistance.

In addition, **Counseling and Psychological Services** provides support to the university community through crisis intervention and consultation and outreach training/presentation regarding mental health issues.  Services are available to currently enrolled **Cal Poly Pomona** students only, and who have paid the mandatory student health fee — services are available at no additional costs.  We offer both in-person and telehealth services for our campus community.  Students needing long-term and/or specialty care may be referred to appropriate community providers.

## Inquiring for Services

If you are a student seeking support, services, or resources from **Counseling and Psychological Services**, please let us know by telephone at (909) 869-3220 during our normal business hours.  Have your Bronco ID number ready when calling.  E-mailing is not a preferred method of communication as it is not secure.

## After Your Inquiry

At your inquiry session, the counselor may make any number of recommendations.  If your counselor recommends short-term therapy, you will be referred to the intake process.  While you wait for your intake appointment, we encourage you to check our [workshops](https://www.cpp.edu/caps/our-services/workshops.shtml) and [drop-in groups](https://www.cpp.edu/caps/our-services/groups.shtml). You may also access our [crisis services](https://www.cpp.edu/caps/crisis/crisis-students.shtml) if your needs are urgent.

## Canceling or Rescheduling

We understand that sometimes events come up that may interfere with your ability to attend your scheduled appointment.  If you need to cancel or rescheduled your appointment, please telephone us more than 24 hours in advance (weekends and holidays included) from your scheduled appointment at (909) 869-3220 to assist in rescheduling your appointment.  Feel free to leave a voicemail message with your Bronco ID number, the date/time of your appointment, and reason for cancellation.  We will get in touch with you the next business day in order to assist in rescheduling your appointment.
