# Programs for Middle/High School Students

## Customized Program

* Designed for students aged **12 and above**
* Group size: **20–25 participants** for personalized attention
* **Customizable program topics** including:

* + STEM subjects
  + Leadership innovation and entrepreneurship
  + American culture
  + And much more

* Flexible program length: from short workshops to extended sessions
* Proven success with engaging, high-quality instruction
* Focus on academic growth and cultural exchange

**UPCOMING PROGRAM: Summer Academy – Science & Technology Exploration Program**

**Summer Academy 2026 - Science & Technology Exploration Program** at Cal Poly Pomona is designed for middle and high school students (in 7th to 12th grades).  This program provides high school students with a unique opportunity to explore cutting-edge fields, including cybersecurity, finance, artificial intelligence (AI)/computer science, and biology. The program combines theoretical knowledge with hands-on experience through seminars, workshops, team projects, and lab work. Students will engage with real-life scenarios and participate in visits to on-campus facilities, gaining a deeper understanding of these vital fields. A **Certificate of Completion** from the College of Professional and Global Education at Cal Poly Pomona will be awarded upon successful completion of the program.

**Program Dates & Fees**

* Dates: July 6 to July 17, 2026 (no classes on weekends)
* Time: 9:00am to 3:00pm on weekdays
* Extended Hours: 8:00am to 5:00pm on weekdays
* Program Fees:
  + With lodging (11 nights): $2,900/student
  + Without lodging: $1,450/student
  + Extended Hours: $2,050/student

## Program Highlights

* **Academic Exploration**: Introduce students to key concepts and emerging trends in cybersecurity, finance, AI/computer science, and biology through in-classroom sessions and hands-on activities.
* **Skill Development**: Equip students with critical thinking, problem-solving, and collaborative skills as they work on team projects and lab activities.
* **University Experience**: Offer participants an experience of campus life at Cal Poly Pomona (CPP), including interactions with faculty and current students.
* **College Readiness**: Provide insights into university programs and the admissions process to encourage students to consider applying to CPP in the future.
