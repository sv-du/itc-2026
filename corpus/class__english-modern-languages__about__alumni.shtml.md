# Alumni

## Our excellent graduates

These are only *some* of our talented alumns. We would love to include you in the list if you are not currently on it. Please contact us with any additions, corrections or updates.

### English B.A.

* Bryan Nakawaki. 2008. Earned an M.A. in English from the University of North Carolina, Charlotte. Currently a Ph.D. Candidate in English at Purdue University, specializing in Shakespeare in performance.
* Deborah Takahashi. 2006. Earned an M.A. in Library and Information Science at San Jose State University. Has worked at  Alhambra Civic Center Library.

### Spanish B.A.

* Cynthia Cea. 2015. Pursuing PhD in Spanish at UC Riverside.
* Laura Arias. 2014. Pursuing Master’s in Education at Portland State University.
* Iliana N. Garcia. 2014. Earned an MA in Public Administration U. of La Verne (2015). Currently working at City Hall Los Angeles.
* Jaime Cortés. 2013. Pursuing Spanish Teaching Credential. Works as Spanish Teacher at King City High School.
* Alejandra Pulido. 2013. Pursuing Master’s in English at Cal Poly Pomona.
* Meloddye Carpio. 2013. Master’s in Latin American Literatures and Culture at UC Davis 2015. Currently pursuing a PhD degree in Hispanic Literary and Cultural Studies at the University of Illinois-Chicago.
* Leah McCollough. 2013. She is currently working as African American Community Outreach Assistant.
* Irene Conde. 2013. She is currently completing her Spanish Teaching Credential at Cal Poly Pomona.
* Andrea Sarria. 2013. She is currently working as a Spanish and ESL tutor at Chaffey College.
* Valentin Mercado. 2013. Spanish Teaching Credential at Cal Poly Pomona 2015. He is currently a teacher at Collins Family College-Ready High School.
* Diana E. Zapata. 2013. Spanish Teaching Credential at Cal Poly Pomona 2015. She is currently a teacher at Collins Family College-Ready High School.
* Fátima Quiñones. 2012. She is currently working as a caseworker in Chino.
* Erica Velasco. 2011.Spanish Teaching Credential at Cal Poly Pomona 2015. She is currently a Spanish teacher at Jurupa Valley High School.
* Heidi Alcalá. 2011. Master in Spanish at Fullerton 2015. She is currently teaching Spanish as an adjunct at Cal Poly Pomona.
* Edgar Orlando. 2011. Pursuing Teaching Credential-Special Education. Works as a substitute teacher in the Baldwin Park district.
* Fátima Luna. 2011. Freelance translator.
* Cyntia Castro. 2010. Certificate in Translation at UC Riverside 2014. Currently pursuing a Master’s Degree in Spanish at Cal State San Bernardino and working as a School District Translator.
* Dalia Gomez. 2009. MA Spanish at CSU Long Beach 2012. Currently pursuing a PhD in Spanish at UC Riverside.
* Melody Sánchez. 2009. After graduation, moved to Mexico D.F. where she taught 5th grade English in an all-girls Catholic school.
* Karen Bossano. 2008. Earned her teaching credential after graduation. Spanish teacher at Upland High School in Upland.
* María Azpeitia. Spanish BA 2007. Earned an M.A. in TESL and English Rhetoric and Composition from Cal Poly Pomona in 2009. Has taught at various area colleges.
* Ivette Gómez. 2002. Earned her M.A. and Ph.D. in Spanish from U.C. Irvine in 2009 with a specialty in 20th & 21st century Latin American Literature.
* Rafael Durán. Spanish BA 2001. Earned a Bilingual Education Credential, M.A. in Bilingual Education, and M.A. in Spanish. Spanish Teacher at Ganesha High School in Pomona. An accomplished vocalist, Rafael has sung in English and Spanish in a variety of settings including the L.A. Opera Chorus.
