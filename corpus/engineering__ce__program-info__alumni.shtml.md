# Industry, Alumni & Friends

![Commencement group 2012](/engineering/ce/img/graduation.jpg)

The Civil Engineering Department graduated its first class in 1963, and now has over 3000 alumni. Many of our alumni have found social media to be the best way to remain connected with one another and with the department. If you are a member of LinkedIn, please join our [CPP CE Alumni group](https://www.linkedin.com/groups?home=&gid=1909417).

The Civil Engineering Department maintains ties with practicing engineers in many ways, including the Civil Engineering Industry Advisory Council (IAC).
