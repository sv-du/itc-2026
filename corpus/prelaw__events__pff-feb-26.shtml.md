# Partners for Justice Info Session: Become a Client Advocate

![flyer](../img/events/pfj.png)CPP PreLaw Pathways welcomes Partners for Justice for a virtual information session about their Client Advocate program. Learn how this paid, two-year position works, what the organization looks for in applicants, and how and when to apply.

### Date and Time

Thursday, February 26, 2026
12:00 pm to 12:50 pm

### Location

Zoom (link provided upon registration)

### About the Client Advocate Program

Client Advocates are paid, full-time team members who work alongside public defenders nationwide, including in Los Angeles, San Diego, and San Bernardino.

Participants:

* Support clients and defense teams
* Develop practical skills valuable for law school and legal careers
* Join a national network of Partners for Justice alumni

### Who Should Attend

* Students interested in public interest law
* Pre-law students seeking hands-on legal experience
* Anyone curious about working with public defenders before law school

### Registration

Join via Zoom using the event link or QR code. Advance registration is encouraged.
