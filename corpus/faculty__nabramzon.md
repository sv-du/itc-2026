## Bio

I am a Professor and Associate Chair of the Physics and Astronomy Department at California Polytechnic University. I earned a B.S. in Physics from Bar Ilan University, Israel (1994) and PhD in Physic in experimental Atomic and Molecular physics from City University of New York( 1999). I then worked as a postdoctoral fellow at JILA/University of Colorado in Boulder Colorado, and at Stevens Institute of Technology in Hoboken New Jersey. I have also worked at Plasma Sol Coporation in Hoboken, New Jersey and  taught at Sara Laurence college in Bronxville New York as a visiting faculty and at Cooper Union in New York as lecturer. In 2004 I joined the Department of Physics and Astronomy at Cal Poly Pomona.
