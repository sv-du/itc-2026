This is a comprehensive list of workshops that the Office of Undergraduate Research plans to host/attend on an annual basis. We invite Faculty Mentors to encourage their students to take part in these activities so that they can take full advantage of the many services and opportunities the office provides. To view upcoming workshops, please visit our [Events page](events.shtml).

## Ways to Get Started in Undergraduate Research

**Hosted by:**OUR | **Offered in:**Fall, Spring

For students interested in understanding what undergraduate research is, how it can be used to fulfill your goals at Cal Poly Pomona and the resources available through the office. Students will learn about our four-step process which includes a self-evaluation and tips on reaching out to faculty.

**Note:**There is a recorded version available. Please refer to the [Recorded Workshops](#recorded) to view the workshop.

## Institutional Review Board for Students

**Hosted by:**IRB Office | **Offered in:**Fall

Introduction to the office that reviews and approves project proposals that involve human animal subjects.

## Narrowing the topic of your research project

**Hosted by:**OUR | **Offered in:**Fall

This workshop is designed to help students understand how to scope a project to be manageable.

**Note:**There is a recorded version available. Please refer to the [Recorded Workshops](#recorded) to view the workshop.

## How to write a Literature Review and Abstract for your project

**Hosted by:**OUR | **Offered in:**Fall

Learn how to develop a literature review and abstract for your research project.

**Note:**There is a recorded version available. Please refer to the [Recorded Workshops](#recorded) to view the workshop.

## Incorporating Your Research Experience into your Resume and Cover Letter

**Hosted by:** Career Center and OUR | **Offered in:**Fall

Special workshop hosted by staff from the Career Center

## Writing a Research Analysis

**Hosted by:** OUR | **Offered in:**Spring

Learn strategies that will help you with writing a research analysis for a class project or final paper.

## Bronco Scholar and Professional Research Presentations

**Hosted by:**Library and OUR | **Offered in:**Spring

This workshop will cover the different types of presentations at the Student RSCA Conference, traits of good presentations, and Cal Poly Pomona's own online repository, Bronco Scholar.

## Library Resources for Student Researchers

**Hosted by:**University Library | **Offered in:**Spring

Hosted by Faculty from the University Library
