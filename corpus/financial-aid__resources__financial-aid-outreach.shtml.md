# Resources

## Financial Aid Presentations and Outreach Requests

Getting the word out about financial aid is important.  The Office of Financial Aid & Scholarships conducts many workshops on campus and in the community on a variety of aid topics throughout the year. The presentations offered vary and change as the aid year progresses so check back here to find new presentations and materials.

## Financial Aid Presentations

### 2022 Recorded Presentations

### **FAFSA CADAA Tips for Staff Video**

### Presentation Request Information

We provide presentations to various on and off-campus groups. Our most common audiences include high school students and their families, campus visitors, academic advisors, and campus departments.

### **Presentation Guidelines**

To best accommodate your request, please note the following guidelines:

* We are currently only offering virtual / online presentations.
* Requests must be submitted at least 3 weeks in advance. This allows us adequate time to find an appropriate presenter and to prepare the presentation.
* Our office will make every attempt to accommodate your request.
* If you need multiple workshops in different time slots within one day, please submit a separate request for each time slot.
* The Financial Aid & Scholarships team member assigned to your presentation will contact you to refine the details and logistics of the presentation.

### **Popular Presentation Topics**

* **Financial Aid Essentials****- High School Seniors:**Review of ffinancial aid and scholarship opportunities and pprocesses (approximately 1 hour)
* **Financial Aid****Essentials – Transfer Students****:**Review of ffinancial aid and scholarship opportunities and processes - with a focus on transfer students (approximately 1 hour)
* **Financial Aid Essentials****–****Newly Admitted Students****:**Review of financial aid and scholarship opportunities and processes (approximately 1 hour)
* **Financial Aid Essentials****–****Parents & Families**:  Review of topics we often receive questions about from parents & families, such as the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA), types of aid, how to apply, cost of attendance, payment options, and available resources (approximately 1 hour).
* **General F****financial****A****id and****S****scholarship****o****overview** (approximately 25 – 35 minutes)
* **How to fill out the FAFSA****or DREAM Application** (approximately 30 – 45 minutes)
* **Satisfactory Academic Progress****for Financial Aid Applicants** (20-30 minutes)
* **How to apply for scholarships** (approximately 25 minutes)
* **Financial****A****id question and answer sessions** (flexible time allowance)

We are happy to discuss other presentation topics and time frames as needed.

## Request a Presentation

Complete our [online request form](https://forms.office.com/pages/responsepage.aspx?id=HqZLFuw5XU-J_6ofAKUhtFEqj6ADOE5EolyOPcFgoodUOVg3T1E0SDVFUVVaNkJHUjVRV1pBVkJZQyQlQCN0PWcu) to submit your request.

Our office has diverted the majority of our resources to our [FAFSA / CADAA workshops](fafsa-dream-workshops.shtml), which are taking place during February and March, 2024. **It is unlikely that we will be able to satisfy any requests for additional workshops during those months** - or the month of April.
