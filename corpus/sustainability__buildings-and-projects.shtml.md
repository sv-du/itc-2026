# Buildings and Projects

![Student Services Building](img/ssb-2020.jpg)

## Green Building Student Services Building Earns Platinum LEED Certification

Cal Poly Pomona incorporates sustainable building practices into small renovations and large construction projects alike. Building design and construction takes a broad spectrum of green building strategies into account. To accomplish this, the University uses the U.S. Green Building Council’s Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design (LEED) Certification System to track opportunities in transportation, site development, water efficiency, energy efficiency, waste reduction, materials and resources, and indoor environmental quality.

[Read More](http://polycentric.cpp.edu/2019/10/student-services-building-earns-university-first-leed-platinum-certification/ "Read More. ")

### Other LEED Certified Buildings at Cal Poly Pomona

* The three-building [College of Business of Administration complex](http://polycentric.cpp.edu/2012/10/new_cba_complex_opens_for_business/) has an LEED Silver certification.
* Phase 2 of the 150-unit [Residential Suites](http://polycentric.cpp.edu/2011/05/event_residences_marks_environ_accomplishment/) has an LEED Silver certificate.
* I-Poly High School holds LEED Silver certification.
* At Innovation Village, [Southern California Edison Phase 4](http://polycentric.cpp.edu/2012/02/innovation_village_building_earns_leed_gold/) garnered LEED Gold certification.
* The [Bronco Recreation and Intramural Complex (BRIC)](http://polycentric.cpp.edu/2016/04/bric-receives-leed-gold-certification/) has LEED Gold certification for the high level of environmental sustainability used in its construction.
* The [Collins College of Hospitality Management expansion](http://polycentric.cpp.edu/2016/06/collins-colleges-new-building-earns-leed-gold-certification/) holds LEED Gold certification.
* [Parking Structure 2](http://polycentric.cpp.edu/2017/10/parking-structure-2-becomes-first-california-facility-to-earn-certification/) is the first parking facility in California to earn a certificate from Parksmart for sustainability and methods that promote green building practices. Parksmart is a component of the LEED rating system.
* The Student Housing Replacement Project (Secoya and Sicomoro) and [CenterPoint](https://polycentric.cpp.edu/2022/11/centerpointe-dining-commons-and-neighboring-residence-halls-certified-gold/) dining commons have been awarded LEED Gold certification.
