# Black History Month

## About BHM

Black History Month is an annual celebration of the achievements of Black people throughout history. The BRC hosts a variety of programs that focused on culturally engaging spaces for Black students, elevating Black identities/lived experiences, and showcasing the current achievements of Black students.

## BHM 2025 Events

### Black History Sprit Week: Feb. 3 - Feb. 7

Kick off the celebration with special spirit days all week long!

### BHM Kick-Off Event: Feb. 4

Black Resource Center from 12:00 p.m. to 1:00 p.m.

### Black Dodgeball: Upperclassmen vs. Underclassmen

At the BRIC MAC Court from 6:00 p.m. to 7:00 p.m.

### Centerpointe Dining: Taste of the Black Diaspora: Feb. 6

At Centerpointe from 5:30 p.m. to 8:30 p.m.

### Haircare with the APISC: Feb. 11

In the Bronco Student Center, Andromeda from 12:00 p.m. to 1:00 p.m.

### Dropping GEMS - The Exploitation of Black Creativity: Feb. 13

Black Resource Center from 12:00 p.m. to 1:00 p.m.

### Off-Campus Community Service with BWE and BSU: Feb. 15

Contact the Black Resource Center for details.

### Internships with Microsoft and LATech.org: Feb. 18

At the Black Resource Center from 2:00 p.m. to 5:00 p.m.

### Dropping GEMS: The History of the 'N' Word: Feb. 20

At the Black Resource Center from 12:00 p.m. to 1:00 p.m.

### Dinner with the Deans: Feb. 24

At the Bronco Student Center, Ursa Minor from 6:00 p.m. to 8:00 p.m.

### Black History Experience: Feb. 27

In the Bronco Student Center, Ursa Minor from 12:00 p.m. to 1:00 p.m.
