## Adrian Cruz

**Customer Relationship Management Specialist**

**Web Analytics Specialist**

**Research Assistant**

**Major:** Computer Information Systems

**Interests****:** Data Analytics, Marketing Analytics, Business Analytics, Consumer Insights, Data Visualization and Data Storytelling

**Skills****:** Microsoft Office, Excel, Tableau, SQL

Business Strategy has been a sustained passion of Adrian’s throughout his life and has only grown deeper during his years in college. Through his internships, leadership positions, and on-campus organizations such as CCIDM, he has learned about Excel, R, Tableau, SQL, Python, and more to deepen his understanding of large-scale business and marketing strategy.
