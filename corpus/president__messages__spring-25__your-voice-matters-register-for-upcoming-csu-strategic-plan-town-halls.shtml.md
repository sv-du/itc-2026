# Your Voice Matters: Register for Upcoming CSU Strategic Plan Town Halls

## February 10, 2025

Dear Campus Community,

As we continue to shape the future of the California State University through our systemwide strategic planning process, your insights and perspectives remain vitally important. As we enter the second phase of stakeholder engagement, you are invited and strongly encouraged to participate in an upcoming virtual town hall session to share your thoughts on the future direction of the CSU.

These interactive sessions will provide an opportunity for faculty, staff, students and community members like you to ask questions, contribute ideas and engage in meaningful dialogue about our systemwide priorities.
