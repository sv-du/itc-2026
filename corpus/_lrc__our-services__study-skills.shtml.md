# Study Skills

The Learning Resource Center (LRC) offers a variety of Study Skills resources such as handouts, videos, and tutoring tips to handle stress, improve communication skills, and provide strategies for test-taking. While working with your tutor, you can discuss techniques to improve your study skills. Please see the links below and visit the LRC for these and additional handouts. Please also visit the LRC [Online Tutoring](https://www.cpp.edu/~lrc/our-services/tutoring.shtml#online "link to online tutoring info") Blackboard organization for help in writing and course-specific concepts.
