## Events

[![Flyer for a Cal Poly Pomona Pre-Law Zoom event with Partners for Justice titled “Become a Client Advocate.” Scheduled for Thursday, Feb 26, 2026, 12:00–12:50 pm. It highlights paid two-year Client Advocate roles with public defenders and includes a QR code and Zoom link.](img/events/partners4justice.png)

### Info Session with Partners for Justice 2/26/26

Cal Poly Pomona Pre-Law is hosting a Zoom info session with Partners for Justice on February 26, 2026, from 12:00–12:50 pm about becoming a paid Client Advocate. Attendees will learn about the two-year roles working with public defenders, what the program looks for, and how to apply.](events/pff-feb-26.shtml)

[![Flyer for a CPP PreLaw UC Irvine Law School campus visit on Friday, Feb 27, 11:00 am–1:30 pm at UC Irvine Law School. Includes a photo of the law school building, event details, a 40-student limit, and a QR code to sign up.](img/events/ucilawschool.png)

### Visit UC Irvine Law School on 2/27/26

CPP PreLaw is hosting a campus visit to UC Irvine Law School on Friday, February 27, from 11:00 am to 1:30 pm. Students will tour the campus and meet current law students. Space is limited to 40 participants, and a QR code is provided to sign up.](events/ucirvine-law-school-campus-visit.shtml)

### CLASS & PreLaw Pathways hosts:

[Alumni Attorney Panel](img/events/prelaw-panel_final-3.19.25.png "Alumni Attorney Panel")

3/19/25 12pm-1pm in the Bronco Student Center, Andromeda Suite

Almni attorneys - Michael Bidart (Economics Major - class of ’72), Jamaar Boyd Weatherby (Philosophy Major - class of ’00), Amy Oakden (English Major - class of ’03) and Raul Sabado (Economics Major - class of ’00)
