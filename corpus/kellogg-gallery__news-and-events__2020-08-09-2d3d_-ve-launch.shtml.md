# 2D3D+ Virtual Exhibition Launch

August 8, 2020

![a virtual exhibition coming Fall 2020.  Website launch Sept. 1, 2020. 2d3d+ annual student juried art exhibition. Visit 2d3dplus2020.com](../img/2020-2d3dplus-ve-ig-square.jpg)

## About our Virtual Exhibition

***Poly-kroma 2020***, celebrating Fine Arts and Visual Communication Design on the Cal Poly Pomona campus with its ***2d3d+ Juried Student Art Exhibition***, and ***Industry Day*** —a Senior Student Visual Communication Design portfolio showcase event and exhibition.

***2D3D+*** is a competitive exhibition that features Fine Art and Visual Communication Design work by students at all academic levels. The jurors, who consist of art, design and curatorial industry professionals, select the best work by students and determine the award-winning entries. This year’s ***2d3d+ Student Art Exhibition***Jurors are: Fine Artist and Sculptor David Jang, Graphic Designer and AIGA President Patrick Fredrickson; and Student Aluma and Graphic Designer Kathryn Izquierdo-Gallegos.

Go to [2d3dplus.com](http://2d3dplus2020.com/index.html) for the ***Annual 2d3d+ Juried Student Art Exhibition*** - showcasing the fine art and visual communication design work from 2019-2020 of Art Department students at all academic levels. Both on-line and virtual exhibitions launching September 1, 2020.

Website is Live Now. Launching with Virtual Exhibition on **Sept. 1, 2020**.

Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, this year's ***Poly-kroma 2020***(***Industry Day***and***2d3d+ Student Show***) is being postponed to Spring 2021, or until we can safely return to campus. As a contingency plan for 2020's on-site exhibition venue, we are hosting a virtual exhibition this year for the first time.
