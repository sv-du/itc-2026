# Transfer Success

##

![Transfer Success](../img/transfer-success-banner.png)

## Your Journey to Success Starts Here!

Transitioning from a community college to Cal Poly Pomona? The Transfer Success Program is here to make sure you don’t just land on your feet, but that you *thrive*.

Our program is designed specifically for transfer students, providing the resources, support and community you need to navigate your new campus with confidence. From tailored workshops and networking events to insider tips and academic guidance, we’re all about helping you make the most of your time at CPP.

Whether you’re looking to connect, get involved or just figure things out, the Transfer Success Program is your go-to hub for a smooth and successful transition. Welcome to your next chapter — we’re excited to be a part of it!

## Program Components

### Community Gatherings

Each monthly community gathering is created with youin mind. These events offer a chance to connect with fellow Transfer Success scholars, engage with supportive staff and build lasting relationships with faculty and mentors who want to see you thrive, all while sharing a meal.

We will also have key campus partners cover real-life topics that matter to transfer students — from academic success to personal growth — so you’re equipped not just to survive, but to succeed at Cal Poly Pomona and wherever your journey takes you next.

### Group Peer Mentoring

As a Transfer Success scholar, you’ll be part of a powerful support network! You’ll be grouped with a dedicated Transfer Success student staff as well as other Transfer Success scholars navigating their own transfer journey. Together, you'll dive into key academic strategies, share experiences, and build the skills you need to thrive at Cal Poly Pomona and beyond.

### A Network of Support

At Cal Poly Pomona, you're never alone. Through the Transfer Success program, you'll connect with a strong community — students, faculty and professionals — all working together to help you reach your full potential.

### Career Skills Development

Transfer Success scholars dive into hands-on workshops and guided learning that boost your career readiness. You’ll discover essential campus resources, grow your professional skillset and become a strong candidate for internships, careers and graduate programs.

### Mentors Who Move You Forward

Research shows that strong connections with faculty and staff lead to a better college experience. Through Transfer Success, you’ll start building those relationships from day one. Meet faculty and staff who are here to support your academic goals, explore your career interests and even write those crucial letters of recommendation when opportunities come your way.

### FREE On-Campus Tutoring

Transfer Success scholars will gain access to one-on-one sessions with EOP (Educational Opportunity Program) tutors at no additional cost.

## Interested in Joining?

### How to Apply

**Requirements**

* Must attend all Transfer Success gathering programs scheduled for the semester.
* Must meet with your peer mentor for group peer mentoring 4 times a semester.
* Must maintain a GPA of at least 2.0.

Open to all Cal Poly Pomona students. Submit a [**myBAR application now**](https://mybar.cpp.edu/submitter/form/start/687696)**.** MSI staff will be contact shortly.
