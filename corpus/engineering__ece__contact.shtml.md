## Visiting the ECE Department

Visitors are always welcome. If you would like to visit the ECE Department, please contact Ms. Margel M. Torrence in the electrical and Computer engineering office before your arrival: ece@cpp.edu or 909-869-2511.

The Electrical and Computer Engineering Department is located in Building 9. The department office is in Room 9-407.
