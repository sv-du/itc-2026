# Steps to Apply to EOP

## Please review the following steps and their deadlines:

### CSU Application

Complete and submit the online undergraduate California State University Admissions application at Cal State Apply and indicate "Yes" if interested in applying to EOP. CSU Applications will open between Oct. 1st – Nov. 30th of each cycle. Monitor the CSU website for updated dates.

### EOP Application via CSU Apply

**The dates below apply to students applying for Fall 2026 admission to Cal Poly Pomona.**

Complete the EOP application by the priority deadline of Nov. 30, 2025 via your CSU Application Portal.

Applicants must complete the EOP portion of the application, including:

* Two names and email addresses for the letters of recommendation

* Autobiographical portion of the EOP sections

Note: Final deadline to complete the EOP application to Cal Poly Pomona is January 15, 2026. The final deadline for recommenders to submit electronic EOP recommendation forms is January 31, 2026. We strongly recommend that you ask your reccomenders if they will be able to meet the letters of recommendation deadline.

Decisions will be made on a rolling basis. For the Fall 2026 class, the deadline to submit the EOP Participation Agreement is June 1st, 2026.

### Financial Aid Application (FAFSA or CADAA)

The priority deadline for the [FAFSA](https://studentaid.gov/#https://studentaid.gov/) and [CADAA](https://dream.csac.ca.gov/landing#https://dream.csac.ca.gov/landing) is typically March 2nd of each cycle. For the 2026-27 cycle only, the priority deadline in California will be March 2nd, 2026. We encourage that you complete your financial aid application as soon as possible before the deadline and to review their respective websites for updates.

**Please make sure to add Cal Poly Pomona's school code (001144) to your financial aid application, otherwise, your financial aid application will not be received by CPP.**

Which application should I file?

* Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA)
  + U.S. Citizen or eligible non-citizen (i.e., Permanent Resident)

* CA Dream Act Application (CADAA)
  + You must **not meet** eligibility criteria to file a FAFSA
  + You must meet the conditions of California AB540 status
  + You must not hold a valid non-immigrant visa (F, J, H, L, A, B, C, D, E, etc.).

If you have questions regarding which financial aid application to submit or need additional assistance, we encourage you to visit the [Cal Poly Pomona Financial Aid and Scholarships website](https://www.cpp.edu/financial-aid/index.shtml#https://www.cpp.edu/financial-aid/index.shtml) for updates, contact information and drop-in (in-person and virtual) services. You can also visit the [Cal Poly Pomona Financial Aid and Scholarships website - Prospective Undergraduate Students Page](https://www.cpp.edu/financial-aid/undergraduate/prospective/index.shtml#https://www.cpp.edu/financial-aid/undergraduate/prospective/index.shtml), to learn more.
