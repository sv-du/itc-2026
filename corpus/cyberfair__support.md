## Your Support is Valuable

We cordially invite organizations seeking to foster awareness of the cybersecurity industry to consider sponsoring the 21st Annual Cal Poly Pomona Cybersecurity and Awareness Fair.

CSAF takes place Thursday, **October 7, 2025**, at the Cal Poly Pomona Bronco Student Center from 9:00 a.m. to 2:00 p.m.

This year’s theme, **Guardians of the Grid** empowers our role in defending the vast digital infrastructure of modern life, including critical systems, enterprise networks, and our global internet. This event offers workshops, demonstrations, and discussions to explore the evolving challenges for cybersecurity professionals, advocates, and enthusiasts and how it enhances teaching, learning, and research. Attendees gain practical insights into real-world digital security challenges, tools, and expand their knowledge. CSAF aims to equip students, staff, and faculty to thrive in an increasingly technology-driven world and is held annually during National Cyber Security Awareness Month.

### Vendor Tables

CPP provides an audience of upcoming cybersecurity career professionals and technology enthusiasts.

* Engage with or hire Cal Poly Pomona STEM students.
* Showcase your products and services through exhibition tables, demos, or presentations.
* 'Maximize brand visibility to an industry relevant audience.

### Faculty/Student Participation

Faculty and student clubs will table to demo their cybersecurity related research and projects. There will also be hands-on hacking demonstrations.

### Logistics

* When: Thursday, October 7, 2025
* Time: 9 a.m. - 2 p.m.
* Cybersecurity Workforce Job Fair: 3 p.m. to 6 p.m.
* Location: Bronco Student Center at Cal Poly Pomona

Join us in shaping the future of cybersecurity by sponsoring our 21st Annual Cybersecurity and Awareness Fair.

Elevate your brand, engage with industry leaders, and support the education of future cybersecurity professionals - all while making a lasting impact on our community's security landscape.
