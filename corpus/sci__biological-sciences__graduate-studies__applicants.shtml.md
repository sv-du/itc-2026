# Applicants

Admission to Biological Sciences Department Graduate Program is determined by the members of the graduate faculty and is based on the total academic record of the applicant. Applications will be screened by graduate faculty members, who will advise the Graduate Coordinator when an applicant qualifies for admittance. Admission to the program is competitive, therefore, it is important that all applicants adhere to deadlines, requirements, and sponsor policy.

The application information below is designed to aid those applying to the Graduate Program during the submission process. Follow each step to ensure that all requirements are completed.

## Step 1 – Check requirements and deadlines.

* Review the [curriculum requirements](gradprog/degree-requirements.shtml) for the Master’s program.
* Review the [faculty sponsor policy](gradprog/sponsor.shtml).
* Review [admission requirements](gradprog/admission-requirements.shtml).
* Read the [FAQ](gradprog/faq.shtml) page before proceeding with the application process.
* **Application** **deadlines for online applications** (<https://www.calstate.edu/apply>)

**Fall Semester applications:** 1 February of the preceding Spring Semester

**Spring Semester applications:** 15 October of preceding Fall Semester

## Step 2 – Find your faculty sponsor.

* Find a [faculty sponsor](gradprog/sponsor.shtml) who will guide and supervise your thesis research.
* Once you have confirmed your sponsor, inform the [Graduate Coordinator](contact.shtml) by email.

## Step 3 – Complete the online application.

* Review the detailed information on the [application requirements](gradprog/admission-requirements.shtml)
* Apply to the Cal Poly Pomona Master’s in Biological Sciences on CSU Apply: <https://www2.calstate.edu/apply>
* Include contact information for **three references** in CSU Apply. The referees will be contacted to directly to upload their letters of recommendation.
* Include a **personal statement** with your CSU Apply application. In your personal statement, identify your faculty sponsor.
* Upload your **unofficial transcripts** on CSU Apply.
* Note: GRE scores are not required for application to the Biological Sciences Master’s program.

## Step 4 – After acceptance to the program, send official transcripts to Cal Poly Pomona.

* For final admittance to the program, official transcripts must be submitted directly to Cal Poly Pomona. **Do NOT submit official transcripts to the Department of Biological Sciences or the Graduate Coordinator, or on CSU Apply**.
* Cal Poly Pomona requests that you send your final official transcripts electronically. If your sending institution does not offer electronic transcripts, we recommend using the following services:
  + National Student Clearinghouse
  + Credential Solutions
* When sending PDF transcripts, please indicate the recipient email as verify@cpp.edu.
* Should these services not be available for your sending institution, please mail to:

Cal Poly Pomona

Attn: Office of Admissions

3801 W. Temple Ave.

Pomona, CA 91768

## Additional Information

* Review the departmental Graduate Program [Policies](policies.shtml).
* Review the [Graduate Studies resource page](https://www.cpp.edu/gradstudies/future-students.shtml) for applicants.
* Consider reviewing the online [Orientation](https://www.cpp.edu/~orientation/) for new graduate students, which was developed by Student Affairs. Although intended for students who have been admitted to the university, applicants may find useful information here.
* If you are a Cal Poly Pomona student you can check your admissions status in [Bronco Direct](https://www.cpp.edu/broncodirect/).
