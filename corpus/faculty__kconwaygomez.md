## About Me

Dr. Conway-Gómez regularly teaches *Regenerative Concepts and Social Practices* (RS 5100) in the MSRS program. Her research interests focus on:

* Natural resource conservation from social and ecological perspectives
* Latin American resource conservation
* Cross-cultural research

Dr. Conway-Gómez received her Bachelor of Arts in Environmental Studies and Religious Studies from Macalester College, and her M.A. in Latin American Studies/Tropical Conservation and Development and Ph.D. in Geography from the University of Florida.

She is a professor of geography in the Department of Geography and Anthropology at the College of Letters, Arts and Social Sciences. Learn more about her teaching and mentoring philosophies and view pictures of her field work at her faculty page [here](http://www.cpp.edu/~kconwaygomez/ "Kristen Conway-Gomez, Ph.D.").
