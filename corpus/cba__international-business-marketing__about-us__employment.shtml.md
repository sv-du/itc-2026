# Work with us

In the International Business and Marketing Department, we pride ourselves on a collegial work environment where we support each others' unique research interests and commitment to teaching excellence. We are known in the College for our social lunches at our monthly faculty meetings, and when we hire we look for a good fit with the department culture.
