## Payroll Services

Welcome to the Payroll Services page! Our department supports the campus by processing, balancing and reconciling payroll data for all state-side employees. Our team is available Monday - Friday between the hours of 8 a.m. and 5 p.m.

[Meet the Team](meet-the-team.shtml) [Payroll Inquiry Form](https://app.smartsheet.com/b/form/bc0d2326f90b483b8c1d29e98d73ed33)
