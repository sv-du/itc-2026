# Requesting a Certificate of Insurance

Strategic Enterprise Risk Management (SERM) can provide a Certificate of Insurance (COI) to a third party when an Agreement has been properly executed by an authorized signor between the University and the third party when a COI is required.

## How to Request a Certificate of Insurance

Complete the Certificate of Insurance Request Form and upload a scanned copy of the applicable agreement.

[Certificate of Insurance Request Form](https://app.smartsheet.com/b/form/e8f04df4478740a6bc086c2e541a85d7)

## Questions

**For questions please contact Strategic Enterprise Risk Management** at
riskmanagement@cpp.edu
