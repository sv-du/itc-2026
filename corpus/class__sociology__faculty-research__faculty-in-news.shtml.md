# Faculty in the News and Public/Academic Engagement

The Department of Sociology at Cal Poly Pomona comprises faculty experts in a wide range of specialized research areas who engage and lead public discussions/debates about current issues in sociology and criminology. For example, our faculty publish in magazines/newspapers with a wide range of interests or appear as experts on various public news networks, or radio news programs.
