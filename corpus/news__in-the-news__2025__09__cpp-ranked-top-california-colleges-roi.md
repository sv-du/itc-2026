# CPP ranked among top California colleges for ROI

Posted on September 18, 2025

Cal Poly Pomona was ranked among the top California colleges for return on investment, with graduates earning a projected $260,000 more over 20 years compared to peers who did not pursue higher education. According to the publication’s analysis, CPP delivers strong ROI through affordable tuition, hands-on learning and career outcomes that allow alumni to quickly recoup the cost of their degrees — making it one of the state’s best values for students seeking long-term economic mobility.

This article was originally published by *[San Francisco Chronicle](https://www.sfchronicle.com/projects/2025/best-california-colleges-return-on-investment/)*on September 18, 2025.
