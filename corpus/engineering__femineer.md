# Femineer

![Cal Poly Pomona Femineer College of Engineering](img/femineer-logo-1200x400.jpg)

***The Femineer Program is open to all K-12 students interested in engineering, science, and agriculture regardless of sex, gender, and gender expression.***

**Program Change.** As part of our college/institutional pivoting—including changes in budget and resources—our Femineer program will only be able to support our local school sites.  We do not have the staff bandwidth and resources to support schools outside of our local area.

![Young Femineers Posing for a Pan Pic](img/femineer-group.jpg)

## History and Background

The Femineer Program was created and funded by Cal Poly Pomona’s College of Engineering in 2013 to inspire students to pursue STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math) in their education and career. The program provides K-12 students with project-based learning, engineering student mentors, and opportunities to visit Cal Poly Pomona, while building a sustainable program and community for current and future STEM leaders. The program engages participants through highly creative, hands-on activities. By engaging with real-world applications, Femineers acquire the technical knowledge and practical skills necessary to become confident and strong in their future careers.
