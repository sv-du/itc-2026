# Donate

In order for us to continue our good work and further our mission of advancing environmental sustainability through regeneration, we need the   generous support of contributors like you. We are proud to count students, alumni, workshop and tour participants, community members, small and large businesses, foundations, and government agencies among our contributors.

[**You can support the work of the Center by giving today**](https://www.cpp.edu/env/lyle/support-the-center/give.shtml "Support the Lyle Center (opens in new window)")  .

We have a variety of opportunities available, each of which can be tailored to meet your personal or professional needs. Please consider one of the options described below.  For assistance or additional information regarding other opportunities for contribution, please contact the Lyle Center at (909) 869-5155 or crs@cpp.edu.

![HRM Architects](../img/hmc-architects.jpg)

**HMC Designing Futures Foundation supports the continuation of an extensive tour and outreach program at the John T. Lyle Center for Regenerative Studies**  which includes the completion of the interpretive signage for the program.

The Lyle Center also thanks the following donors:

* Steve and Marian Dodge
