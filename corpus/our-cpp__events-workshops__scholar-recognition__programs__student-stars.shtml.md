![](../../../img/events/scholar-recognition/dividers/dividers-02.png)

## About Student Success and Transfer Articulation through Research and Support Services

The Student Success and Transfer Articulation through Research and Support Services (STARS) Program helps low income students in in STEM partake in undergraduate research early connect to Peer and Faculty mentors to learn about research and career development opportunities.

Visit the [STARS](../../../students/stars/index.shtml) website to learn more about the program.
