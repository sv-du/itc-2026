# March 8, 2023 Academic Senate Meeting

Time: 3:00 to 5:00 p.m.
Location: CLA Building 98-P2-007 (classroom off the Paseo)

**Safer Return Task Force recommends masking indoors**

[March 8, 2023 Academic Senate Agenda](academic_senate_agenda_03.08.23.pdf)

1. Academic Senate Minutes - February 15, 2023 - there will be a motion to postpone the minutes

2.  Information Items

1. Chair's Report
   * [Academic Senate Vacancies and Election Timelines](election_timeline_03.08.23.pdf)
   * Rules for Debate and Discussion -  each member has the right to speak twice on the same question on the same day but cannot make a second speech on the same question so long as any member who has not spoken on the question desires the floor. A member who has spoken twice, on a particular question on the same day has exhausted their right to debate that question for that day. To ensure that members have an opportunity to speak, each member will have a 3-minute speaking time limit.
