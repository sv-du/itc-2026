## A Community of Support In and Out of the Classroom

Keep your connection to the Kellogg Honors College after you graduate. You are now part of a network of over 2000 students that have completed our program. Our alumni can be found across the country and in countries throughout the world over.
As an alumnus of KHC, you are a valuable part of our community, and we want you to stay in touch and support our mission and vision through mentorship, sharing your success with us, and in other ways that have meaning to you.

## Inspire

### Share your Success

* Nothing inspires students more than seeing our alumni grow and succeed
* To share about your success with the KHC, please fill out this [form.](https://forms.office.com/Pages/ResponsePage.aspx?id=HqZLFuw5XU-J_6ofAKUhtLbS61b9IChHsHRsqth3fPVUNDhQQkI5U041ODA1SDYwOE5GU0w4MlVNSiQlQCN0PWcu)

### Apply to be our Convocation Speaker

* Every year we celebrate our graduates of KHC, consider serving as a speaker to inspire them as they launch the next chapter of their future
* To join us as the Convocation speaker, [apply here.](https://forms.office.com/Pages/ResponsePage.aspx?id=HqZLFuw5XU-J_6ofAKUhtFFTugcCXTlGiZw9aUJYCztUOTYzSE81TExTUFI0RDJLTkJGMFZHVk1UMC4u)

### Serve on our Advisory Council

* Apply to serve as an alumni representative on our KHC Advisory Council
* We currently have two alumni representatives
* Representatives  attend monthly meetings, work with the KHC Director in an advisory role, and support alumni engagement
* Terms of service are for 1 academic year (September-May), but are renewable for up to an additional year
* If interested in applying, please email the KHC Director Dr. Claudia Garcia-Des Lauriers at clauriers@cpp.edu

### Share ideas for innovative change

* Let us know how we might benefit from your expertise

## Donate

* Share your success by giving to KHC. Donations of any size are welcome to help us continue to grow our program and provide the high quality and impact of our KHC community to more students.
* For any donations and transformative gifts, please [click here.](https://www.cpp.edu/giving/kellogg-honors.shtml)
* Please reach out to our CPP Advancement team if you wish to make an impactful transformative gift to KHC.
