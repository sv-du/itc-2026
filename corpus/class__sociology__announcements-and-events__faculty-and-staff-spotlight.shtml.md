New Book

Her new book Metamorphosis: Who We Become after Facial Paralysis," Dr. Faye Wachs takes us on a captivating journey, exploring the physical, emotional, and psychological transformations experienced by individuals with facial paralysis

![](../img/book-cover-ocampo2022.jpg)

ASA Section Award

Dr. Anthony Ocampo is the co-winner of the 2023 ASA Section of Latina/o Sociology Award for his groundbreaking book "Brown and Gay in LA," a distinguished contribution to research in the field.
