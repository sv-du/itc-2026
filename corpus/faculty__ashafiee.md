## Selected Publications

**Google Scholar: <https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=ViU5ePEAAAAJ&hl=en>**

**Research Gate: <https://www.researchgate.net/profile/Ali_Shafiee7>**

1. **Shafiee, A.**, R Dabiri and F Askari (2017). “Dynamic Properties of Firoozkooh Sand-Silt Mixtures” *Journal of Seismology & Earthquake Engineering (JSEE)*, 19(4), 273-284.
2. Yniesta, S., Brandenberg, S.J. and **Shafiee, A.** (2017). “ARCS: A one dimensional nonlinear soil model for ground response analysis.” *Soil Dynamics and Earthquake Engineering*. 102, 75-85.
3. **Shafiee, A.,** JP Stewart, R Venugopal and SJ Brandenberg (2017). “Adaption of broadband simple shear device for constant volume and stress-controlled testing,” *Geotechnical Testing Journal,* 40(1), 15-28
4. **Shafiee, A**, JP Stewart, and SJ Brandenberg (2014). “Reset of secondary compression clock for peat by cyclic straining,” *ASCE Journal of Geotechnical and Geoenvironmental Engineering,* 141 (3), 02815001.
5. **Shafiee, A**, SJ Brandenberg, and JP Stewart (2013). “Laboratory investigation of the pre- and post-cyclic volume change properties of Sherman Island peat,” *Geo-Congress 2013*, San Diego.
6. Stewart, JP, SJ Brandenberg, and **A Shafiee** (2013). "Laboratory evaluation of seismic failure mechanisms of levees on peat," *UCLA-SGEL 2013/04*, Structural and Geotechnical Engineering Laboratory, Civil & Environmental Engineering Department, UCLA.
7. **Shafiee, A**, M Kamalian, MK Jafari, and H Hamzehloo (2011). “Ground motion studies for microzonation in Iran,” *Natural Hazards*, 59(1), 481-505.
8. **Shafiee, A**, M Bahador, and R Bahrami (2008). “Application of fuzzy set theory to evaluate the effect of pore pressure build-up on the seismic stability of Karkheh embankment dam, Iran,” *Journal of Earthquake Engineering*, 12(8), 1296-1313.
9. **Shafiee, A** (2008). “The influence of clay core composition on the seismic stability of embankment dams,” *ICE Geotechnical Journal*,61(6), 283-290.
10. **Shafiee, A** (2008). “Permeability of granule-clay mixtures,” *Engineering Geology*, 97(3-4), 199-208.
11. **Shafiee, A,** and A Azadi (2007). “Shear-wave velocity characteristics of geologic units throughout Tehran City, Iran. *Journal of Asian Earth Sciences*, 29, 105-115.
12. Jafari, MK and **A Shafiee** (2004). “Mechanical behavior of compacted composite clays,” *Canadian Geotechnical Journal*, 41(6), 1152-1167.
13. Jafari, MK, **A Shafiee,** and A Razmkhah (2002). “Dynamic properties of fine grained soils in south of Tehran”, *JSEE*, 4(1), 25-35.
