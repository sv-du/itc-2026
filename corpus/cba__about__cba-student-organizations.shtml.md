# CBA Student Organizations

## Get Involved

Cal Poly Pomona's College of Business Administration features more than 25 clubs and organizations governed by the United Business Student Senate (UBSS). Compete in cybersecurity competitions by joining Students With an Interest in the Future of Technology or connect with high-achieving academics by getting involved with national business honor society Beta Gamma Sigma. The CBA is filled with opportunities to Become By Doing!

Contact CBA Organization Liaison
