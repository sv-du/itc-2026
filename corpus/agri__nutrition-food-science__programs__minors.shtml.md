# Minors

## Become by Experimenting

Expand your skills and knowledge with these minors!

Whether you are in hospitality management, nutrition, food science and technology or another STEM-related major, there is something for you here.

Combine culinary arts and food science with the Culinology minor. Learn to keep your workplace safe with the food safety minor.

Majoring in food science and technology? Add to your knowledge with the nutrition minor – or specialize even further with the plant-based food and nutrition minor.

Majoring in nutrition? Learn more about food processing with the food science and technology minor.

Find out more about the courses related to these minors in the University Catalog:

[Culinology](https://catalog.cpp.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=69&poid=19579&returnto=5890)

[Food Safety](https://catalog.cpp.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=69&poid=19580&returnto=5890)

[Food Science & Technology](https://catalog.cpp.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=69&poid=19581&returnto=5890)

[Nutrition](https://catalog.cpp.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=69&poid=19582&returnto=5890)

[Plant-Based Food & Nutrition](https://catalog.cpp.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=69&poid=19796&returnto=5890)

Make sure that you select the most recent edition of the catalog in the upper right corner (see the circled menu in the picture below).
