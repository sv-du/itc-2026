# Preparing for an AV Request

## Who can make AV requests?

Anyone can request a consultation; however, it is important to note that final approval for any project must go through the respective owner of a particular space which will be a College Dean along with Academic Affairs or a Department VP.

## Ownership and Approvers

The distinction between these two is required as it will determine what funding options are available, and who can ultimately approve the project. The relevant college and Academic Affairs approve academic spaces, while the relevant department approves Non-Academic spaces. If you request a combination of academic and non-academic spaces, please submit separate requests for each type.

Expand all

### About Academic Spaces

For academic room modifications, please consult with your Department Chair, Associate Dean, and/or your Dean first before going through Academic Resources to obtain approval to modify the space. Contact Academic Resources ar@cpp.edu for more information on approving classroom projects.

### About Non-Academic Spaces

Non-academic room modifications are approved by your Division’s VP or Authorized Account Signer.

## Information Requirements

Expand all

### Scope

What are the different component needed to accomplish goals defined by the objectives set in place to achieve your anticipated outcome?

### Project Goals

What is it that you are trying to accomplish? Are you trying to improve student accessibility or modernize the way you present content in your classes?

### Objectives

How are you planning to accomplish your goals? Do you have any specific ideas or use cases that you are trying to accommodate or plan for?

### Anticipated Outcomes

What do you expect as a result of your efforts? Will this improve the existing room functions for everyone who uses this classroom?

### Background Information

Is there any information about the room that you are concerned with or would like to give special attention to? Are there any existing issues?

### Other Parties

How far have you already gone in the process on your own? Is facilities already involved? Has funding already been allocated to this for a larger project?
