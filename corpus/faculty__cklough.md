## About Me

Cheryl brings 20 years of experience as a landscape architect to her practice.  Her work spans projects large and small, international and domestic. Dedicated to evidence-based design, Cheryl specializes in biologically diverse placemaking. She designs unique, meticulously curated experiences for her clients. Her background in academia and project management provides her with detailed knowledge about issues associated with multiple project types in the ever-transforming design industry.

Cheryl is especially well-versed in the advancement of systems to address the environmental imperatives facing our urban conditions, and has worked extensively across multiple projects to reconnect infrastructures with the surrounding natural environment for a mutually supportive relationship between nature and the urbane.

Cheryl is a registered Landscape Architect (California, Virginia, Missouri) and LEED BD+C certified. She acquired her BS Landscape Architecture from West Virginia University and her Master’s in Landscape Architecture with honors from Louisiana State University.
