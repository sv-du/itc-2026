# Faculty and Staff Resources

## Start Here

The Veterans Resource Center is committed to providing members of the campus community with the resources and information needed to help support our student veterans in and outside of the classroom.

If you would like to be more involved with supporting our student veterans and/or have questions about veteran processes or issues, please contact the Veterans Resource Center at 909.869.6994 or at vetquestions@cpp.edu. You are also welcome to visit the Veterans Resource Center during our business hours which are Monday - Friday,  8 a.m.- 5 p.m.

Please click on the following links for additional information:

* [Academic Related Support](academic-related-support-for-veterans.shtml)
* [Listing of veteran liaisons across campus](campus-liaisons.shtml)
* [Publications](publications.shtml) that may interest you
