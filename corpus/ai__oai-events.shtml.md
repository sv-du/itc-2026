## Upcoming Event

### Faculty & Staff Winter Institute 2026

The Faculty and Staff Winter Institute provides participants with dynamic professional development opportunities. Through interactive workshops and expert-led discussions, participants gain new strategies to enhance teaching, foster collaboration, and drive student success at Cal Poly Pomona.

[Learn more](oai-winter-institute/index.shtml)

## Fall 2025 Calendar

[Card 1 of 5

September 29 - November 5th, 2025

### Employer Engagement Opportunities

Beginning this fall, the Career Center is shifting to a new approach designed to meet your needs now and in the future. Instead of one large career event, we’re creating smaller, targeted career programs focused on specific majors and industries, while also offering shared virtual opportunities open to all students.](https://careercenter.cpp.edu/employer-engagement-opportunities/)
[Card 2 of 5

September - December 2025

### Community Innovation Hub

Dive into a rich lineup of free events and hands-on workshops at the Community Innovation Hub — from 3D printing to home-buying basics, Dia de los Muertos celebrations, and more. Sign up now to reserve your spot and join us in building, learning, and connecting in Pomona.](https://www.cpp.edu/thehub/programs-and-workshops.shtml)
[Card 3 of 5

November 22nd, 2025

### Southern California Conferences for Undergraduate Research

SCCUR is broadly multi-disciplinary, including the sciences, humanities, social sciences, arts and performing arts. SCCUR typically draws 700-1,000 participants, undergraduates, faculty mentors, administrators, and community college and high school students. Students present their work either in 15-minute formal panel presentations chaired by faculty members, in poster form, or in exhibition or performance.](https://www.cpp.edu/our-cpp/events-workshops/sccur/index.shtml)
[Card 4 of 5

December 2nd, 2025

### PolyX Showcase

Our Fall PolyX Showcase highlights the innovative work of Cal Poly Pomona students as they present research and creative projects developed in PolyX courses. Join us at the Bronco Student Center (BSC) in Ursa Major from 11:30 a.m. to 1 p.m. to celebrate the impact of experiential education and the dedication of our students and faculty mentors!](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/8th-annual-polyx-showcase-tickets-1471053573839?utm-campaign=social&utm-content=attendeeshare&utm-medium=discovery&utm-term=listing&utm-source=cp&aff=ebdsshcopyurl)
[Card 5 of 5

TBD

### Bronco Startup Challenge

The Bronco Startup Challenge includes a series of workshops, networking events, and business pitch competitions, on and off campus, throughout the academic year. The ecosystem is designed to challenge and support students from different backgrounds, specialties, and interests to create a unified vision and bring their ideas to life.](https://www.cpp.edu/siil/events-and-programs/cpp-startup-challenge.shtml)
