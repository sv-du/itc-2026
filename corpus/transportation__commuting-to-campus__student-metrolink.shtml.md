# Metrolink Tickets for Students

One advantage of being a student is the wide variety of discounts you may receive – including purchasing a Metrolink ticket at a discounted price.  Students receive a 25% discount off the regular adult fare for a one-way ticket, round-trip ticket, 7-day pass, or a monthly pass.  Student discount tickets or passes must be purchased at the Metrolink stations.  For help planning your trip stop by the Rideshare Office, we have maps and schedules and will share our expertise in commuting by rail.
