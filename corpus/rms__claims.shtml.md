# Claims Against the University

A claim against The California State University (CSU) or one of its campuses must be filed at the address below within six (6) months after which the incident or event occurred along with a $25 claim filing fee made out to "**Trustees of CSU**".

Completed claims must be delivered or mailed to:

**The California State University - Office of the Chancellor**
Risk Management and Public Safety
401 Golden Shore, 5th Floor
Long Beach, CA 90802-4210

Visit the [CSU Systemwide Risk Management](https://www.calstate.edu/csu-system/administration/business-finance/systemwide-risk-management/Pages/file-a-claim.aspx) website for further information or to obtain a claim form.
