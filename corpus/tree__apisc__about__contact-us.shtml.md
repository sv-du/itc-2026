# How to Contact Us

## Contact Us

Email: apisc@cpp.edu
Phone: (909) 869-5025

## Office Location

**Cal Poly Pomona****Asian & Pacific Islander Student Center****Building 95-101**

3801 West Temple Avenue, Pomona, CA 91768

The [Asian & Pacific Islander Student Center](https://www.cpp.edu/maps/dynamic/) is located in Building 95 across from the Career Center and the Campus Center Marketplace, behind the Womxn’s Resource Center, the César E. Chávez Center for Higher Education, and the African American Student Center. Building 95 is referred to as the Cultural Centers.

## Office Hours

**Fall and Spring Hours**

Monday – Friday  8:00 a.m. – 5:00 p.m

**Summer Hours**

Monday - Thursday 7:00 a.m. - 6:00 p.m., Closed on Friday
