# Effective Presentation Skills for Competitions and Conferences

## Soft Skills: Presentation Skill

This workshop is presented by the AMI Club’s Vice President of Operations, Jillian Munoz, and focuses on how to create effective presentations for competitions and academic conferences with a goal of supporting students who were planning to compete in a data science competition. Jillian will cover topics from selecting your template to how to handle the Q&A session. At the time of the presentation, Jillian held a Social Media Marketing Specialist position at the Center for Customer Insights and Digital Marketing.
