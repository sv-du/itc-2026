# The Hub in the News

![Students at Dia de los muertos altar](img/photos/news/cpp-latinx-heritage-month.jpg)

## CPP Recognizes Latinx Heritage Month

Cal Poly Pomona celebrates Latinx Heritage Month and Hispanic-Serving Institutions Week with a variety of events. From a luncheon to a panel discussion to fun activities, campus organizations and resource centers from Sept. 15 to Oct. 15 will highlight and honor the contributions of the Latinx community, as well as acknowledge the history and culture of more than 20 Latin American countries. The entire Bronco community is welcome to participate in these events, regardless of identity.

[Read more about CPP Recognizes Latinx Heritage Month](https://www.cpp.edu/news/content/2025/09/cpp-recognizes-latinx-heritage-month/index.shtml)

![panelists at cafe con conversaciones](img/photos/news/community-innovation-hub-news-2.jpg)

## Community Innovation Hub Uplifts Latinx Voices for Latinx Heritage Month

Cal Poly Pomona’s [Community Innovation Hub](https://cpp.edu/thehub) in Downtown Pomona welcomed a crowd of over 40 community members on Sept. 17 for Cafe y Conversaciones, an engaging event highlighting Latinx Heritage Month. Seven Latinx entrepreneurs and community leaders shared their experiences, insights, and reflections on the impact of small businesses and the role of cultural identity in shaping entrepreneurship.

[Read more about Community Innovation Hub Uplifts Latinx Voices for Latinx Heritage Month](https://www.cpp.edu/news/content/2025/09/community-innovation-hub-uplifts-latinx-voices-for-latinx-heritage-month/index.shtml)
