# Become a Donor

## Make an Impact

Several sponsorship opportunities are available to support the Ahimsa Center. The Center welcomes donations in any amount.

If you are interested in joining the family of [Ahimsa Center Donors and Sponsors](about/sponsors.shtml "Ahimsa Center Donors and Sponsors"), please [contact the Ahimsa Center](about/contact.shtml "Contact the Ahimsa Center").
