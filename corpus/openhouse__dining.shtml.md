## On-Campus Options

**Bronco Student Center (Bldg. 35)** | *9 a.m. - 2 p.m.*

* Subway
* Qdoba
* Hibachi San
* Saddles
* The Patio
* BRIC Break
* Poly Fresh

**Campus Center (Bldg. 97)**| *9 a.m. - 2 p.m.*

* Carl's Jr.
* Panda Express
* Takorean
* Choolaah
* Campus Marketplace Pony Express

**Library (Bldg. 15)**

* Starbucks | *7:30 a.m. - 2 p.m.*
* Poly Trolley 2 | *9 a.m. - 2 p.m.*
* Tacos Chicanx | *9 a.m. - 2 p.m.*

**[Centerpointe](https://centerpointedining.com/) (Bldg. 72)**

* Lollicup | *9 a.m. - 5 p.m.*
* Centerpointe Dining | *11 a.m. - 8 p.m.*

**Vista Market (Bldg. 52)** | *7:30 a.m. - 10 p.m.*

**CBA Pony Express (Bldg. 164)** | *10 a.m. - 3 p.m.*

**BRIC Break (Bldg. 42)** | *9 a.m. - 2 p.m.*

**[Innovation Brew Works](https://www.ibrewworks.com/) (Bldg. 220A-C)** | *11 a.m. - 10 p.m.*
