# Safety and Emergency Information

![Stylized SSB and Clouds](img/20ssb-and-clouds2.jpg)

## Campus Safety Information

In the event of an emergency, this Safety website ([www.cpp.edu/safety](http://www.cpp.edu/safety)) would provide critical information to the university community.

* If this is an emergency, dial 911 on a campus phone or dial 909-869-3070 from a mobile device
* Blue light emergency phones on campus connect with University Police
* [Emergency Management](https://www.cpp.edu/em/)
* [University Police](https://www.cpp.edu/police/)

**There is currently no emergency on campus.**

## Regional & Emergency Info & Alerts

* [Los Angeles County Emergency Information](https://lacounty.gov/emergency/)
* [San Bernardino County Emergency Information](https://oes.sbcounty.gov/)
* [Orange County Resources During Emergency](https://www.ocsheriff.gov/AirportFire) and [Emergency Alerts](https://www.readyoc.com/)
* [Riverside County Emergency Information](https://rivcoready.org/active-events)

## Seeking Resources & Support

The safety and well-being of our campus community is our highest priority. Support resources are available for those in need.

* **Students:** For assistance, including housing support, please contact the [Care Center](https://www.cpp.edu/broncoscarecenter/).
* **Faculty and Staff:** Employees can access resources through the [Employee Assistance Program by Empathia](https://www.cpp.edu/eoda-hr/departments/benefits/health-benefits/eap.shtml). For disaster relief resources, visit the [Benefits webpage](https://www.cpp.edu/eoda-hr/departments/benefits/index.shtml). For individual support, email eoda@cpp.edu.
