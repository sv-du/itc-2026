# Visit Us

## Welcome to Cal Poly Pomona

Welcome to a place like no other. Nowhere else can you ride an Arabian horse, design and construct an award-winning Rose Parade float and launch a rocket into space.

Nestled in the vibrant San Gabriel Valley, just beyond the bustling energy of Los Angeles, our campus is a place of discovery, culture and community—where every student gets [The CPP Polytechnic Experience](../polyx.shtml) as they [become by doing](../become-by-doing.shtml).

Whether you’re coming for a tour, an event or just passing through, we invite you to experience the unique blend of tradition and innovation that defines Cal Poly Pomona.
