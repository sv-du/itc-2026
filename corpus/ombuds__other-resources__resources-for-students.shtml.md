# Resources for Students

## Lots of Support — We Can Help You Find It

The Office of Conflict Resolution and Ombuds offers confidential support to guide students toward resolving university-related issues. However, the resources listed below can address additional needs and provide specialized assistance beyond our scope.

## Other Student Resources

[Student Health Services](https://www.cpp.edu/health/)

[The Care Center](https://www.cpp.edu/broncoscarecenter/)
