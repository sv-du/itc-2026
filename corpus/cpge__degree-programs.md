![Diverse group of professionals smiling and collaborating around a table with laptops and a tablet in a modern office setting](main_image_landing.jpg)

## Masters Degrees

Cal Poly Pomona offers a range of master’s degree programs developed in collaboration with the university’s academic colleges and delivered through the College of Professional and Global Education (CPGE). These programs combine academic rigor with applied learning and flexible formats designed to support working professionals and career-focused learners.

## Explore Master’s Degree Programs

[![three people standing in front of a tech room wearing badges ](img/eng_img.jpg)

### Master of Science in Systems Engineering

Prepare for leadership in complex technical environments through advanced study in systems design, integration, and analysis.](https://www.cpp.edu/cpge/degree-programs/systems-engineering/index.shtml%20 "opens a new window")

[![two people walking downstairs of federal building](img/mpa-wywl2-img.jpg)

### Online Master of Public Administration

Develop leadership and management skills for careers in public service, government, and nonprofit organizations through flexible online learning.](https://www.cpp.edu/cpge/degree-programs/public-administration/index.shtml%20 "opens a new window")

[![dietician talking to client ](img/msd-main-image.jpg)

### Online Master of Science in Dietetics

Gain advanced knowledge in nutrition and dietetics to support clinical practice, community health, and leadership roles in the field.](https://www.cpp.edu/cpge/degree-programs/dietetics/index.shtml%20 "opens a new window")

[![group of hospitality workers standing in a line](img/hospitality_main.jpg)

### Master of Science in Hospitality Management

Build strategic leadership and operational expertise for management roles across hospitality, tourism, and service industries.](https://www.cpp.edu/cpge/degree-programs/hospitality/index.shtml%20 "opens a new window")

[![two architects looking at a design](img/master-of-interior-architecture.jpg)

### Online Master of Interior Architecture

Advance professional design practice with a master’s degree focused on interior architecture, applied research, and industry relevance.](https://www.cpp.edu/cpge/degree-programs/interior-architecture/index.shtml%20 "opens a new window")

[![person-looking-at-computer](img/msis-hybrid-24.jpg)

### Master of Science in Information Security

Develop technical and strategic skills to protect information systems and manage cybersecurity risks in public and private organizations.](https://www.cpp.edu/cba/graduate-business-programs/programs/master-science-information-security-face-to-face.shtml%20 "opens a new window")

[![person working at their desk in front of two screens](fa-img.jpg)

### Master of Science in Financial Analytics

Develop advanced analytical and quantitative skills to support financial decision-making, risk analysis, and strategic planning.](https://www.cpp.edu/programs/cba/gp/masters-financial-analytics.shtml%20 "opens a new window")

[![team in a meeting and person standing in front of a white board](img/mhr-updated-2025.jpg)

### Master of Science in Human Resources Leadership

Prepare for strategic HR leadership roles by building expertise in talent management, organizational development, and workforce strategy.](https://www.cpp.edu/programs/cba/gp/masters-human-resources-leadership-mshrl.shtml%20 "opens a new window")

[![person-working-in-a-warehouse](img/program-msdscm-24.jpg)

### Master of Science in Digital Supply Chain Management

Build expertise in supply chain strategy, analytics, and digital operations to lead complex global supply networks.](https://www.cpp.edu/programs/cba/gp/masters-digital-supply-chain-management-msdscm.shtml%20 "opens a new window")

[![person presenting numbers on a screen](img/msa-program-24.jpg)

### Accelerated Master of Science in Accountancy (Saturdays Only)

Complete an intensive accounting master’s degree designed for working professionals through a structured, Saturday-only format.](https://www.cpp.edu/programs/cba/gp/masters-accounting.shtml%20 "opens a new window")

[![group of business professionals in a meeting](img/business-analytics-24.jpg)

### Master of Science in Business Analytics

Develop data-driven problem-solving skills using analytics, modeling, and business intelligence tools applied across industries.](https://www.cpp.edu/programs/cba/gp/masters-business-analytics-msba.shtml%20 "opens a new window")

[![person on computer working on digital marketing](img/digital-marketing-app-24.jpg)

Gain advanced skills in digital strategy, analytics, and customer engagement to lead marketing initiatives in a digital-first environment.](https://www.cpp.edu/programs/cba/gp/masters-digital-marketing.shtml%20)
