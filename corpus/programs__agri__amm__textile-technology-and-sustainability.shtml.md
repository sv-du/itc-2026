## About the Program

Revolutionize the way we produce and consume fashion with an eye toward sustainability. After building a comprehensive foundation in apparel design and production, discover ways to reduce the environmental impact of this massive global industry and join a growing movement of sustainable practices in fashion. With our pairing of theory and practice, you will become an innovator of textile choices, dyeing and print methods, and supply chain management.

## The Polytechnic Experience

Test textile strength, flammability and weather resistance with state-of-the-art equipment in our textile and garment testing lab. Study the physical and chemical properties of fibers and dyes, then test application methods. Experiment with innovative textiles, analyze their uses and determine their place in the current market. And do it all while evaluating sustainability in practices and processes.

## Career Outcomes

Card 1 of 4

### Textile Sustainability Manager

Develop and implement strategies to reduce the environmental impact of textile production. Focus on sustainable sourcing, waste reduction and eco-friendly manufacturing practices.

Card 2 of 4

### Textile Development Specialist

Research and test new materials and technologies to create innovative, sustainable fabrics. Work on improving textile performance while reducing environmental harm.

Card 3 of 4

### Eco-friendly Product Designer

Design textile products with sustainability in mind. Use eco-friendly materials and production methods to create environmentally responsible products.

Card 4 of 4

### Textile Supply Chain Analyst

Optimize the flow of sustainable raw materials within the textile supply chain. Ensure ethical sourcing, reduce waste and promote transparency from production to retail.

## Courses

The Textile Technology and Sustainability option equips students with expertise in fiber and fabric selection, digital design, and sustainable product innovation. This specialization focuses on integrating advanced textile technologies with sustainable practices to meet the growing demand for eco-friendly fashion solutions. You will engage in hands-on projects, such as working in digital design labs and garment construction facilities, and benefit from real-world experiences equivalent to two years of industry work. Located in Los Angeles County, a key fashion and textile hub, the program offers unique opportunities to develop and test sustainable textile innovations and participate in industry tradeshows. With a curriculum that combines technical skills with a strong emphasis on sustainability, graduates are prepared for roles in textile development, product design, and sustainability management. The program’s small class sizes and dedicated faculty provide personalized instruction and support, ensuring that you are well-prepared to address the challenges and opportunities in the evolving textile industry.

For the most up-to-date information on courses for this program, visit the course catalog or contact an advisor.
