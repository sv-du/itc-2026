## About the Program

Power the future through studying the ever-evolving world of electronics, computers and communications. Seek to understand digital electronics, computer hardware and software, communications electronics, controls, instrumentation and robotics. Through a strong background in applied science, teamwork and exploration, you will develop the necessary technical and communication skills to be successful in the industry.

## The Polytechnic Approach

Here, you get hands-on experience with various electronic systems. Explore concepts in advanced digital design using Field Programmable Gate Arrays (FPGAs), radio frequency and optical communication. Achieve advanced programming fluency in Java. Develop autonomous vehicles. Through these practical applications and more, you will become a technically-focused, solutions-oriented contributor to any engineering team.

## Career Outcomes

Previous
Next

### Electronics Engineer

Design and develop electronic equipment, systems and technologies, from microchips to broadcast systems. Contribute to advancements in renewable energy, telecommunications and smart technology.

### Test Engineer

Design and conduct tests to evaluate products, systems and software before they are released to customers or deployed in the field. Support industries like software development, electronics, automotive, manufacturing and aerospace.

### Digital Engineer

Use digital skills to create and capture data, then use that data to model and develop new systems and products or to improve existing applications.

### Automation Robotics Engineer

Combine mechanical and electronic engineering, computer science and artificial intelligence to create, program and maintain autonomous robotic systems that operate anywhere from manufacturing floors to outer space.

## Courses

Electronic Systems Engineering Technology focuses on applying theoretical concepts to practical systems involving electronics, embedded software, communications, controls and robotics. The program emphasizes hands-on learning and teamwork, preparing you for careers as Electronics Engineers, Digital Engineers, Avionics Software Engineers, Systems Test Engineers and Automation Robotics Engineers. The curriculum provides a strong foundation in applied sciences, mathematics and engineering principles. You will gain technical and communication skills through project-based learning and be trained to solve industry-specific technical problems efficiently. The program is designed for those with a strong background in science and mathematics and includes instruction in design, manufacturing processes and the environmental impact of technology. You will be well-equipped for application-oriented careers in a rapidly evolving technological landscape.

## Accreditation

### ABET Accreditation

The Department of Electromechanical Engineering Technology offers an electromechanical systems engineering technology program leading to a Bachelor of Science degree.

The electromechanical systems engineering technology program is accredited by the Engineering Technology Accreditation Commission of ABET, [https://www.abet.org](https://www.abet.org/), under the General Criteria, and the Program Criteria for Electromechanical Engineering Technology and Similarly Named Programs.

For more information, please visit our [accreditation page.](https://www.cpp.edu/engineering/et/emset/outcomes.shtml)
