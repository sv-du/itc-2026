# Faculty and Staff Opportunities

![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white-mobile.svg)

## Innovate. Lead. Inspire.

### Empowering Faculty & Staff Through Innovation

Discover opportunities to grow, collaborate, and innovate. The Office of Academic Innovation supports faculty and staff with grants, workshops, and resources to foster creativity and professional development.

![test](img/homepage/faculty-opportunities/pop-up-courses.jpg)

## Innovate in Teaching & Learning

### Transform the classroom experience with new strategies

Explore innovative teaching approaches that enhance student engagement and learning outcomes. From technology integration to hands-on learning models, we provide resources and funding to help bring your ideas to life.

### Attend the Academic Innovation Summer Conference

### Teach in the Kellogg Honors Community

### Apply to be a KHC Faculty Fellow

### Mentor Honors Student Engaged Learning Experiences (ELE)
