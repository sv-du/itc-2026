## About

### What are Honors Classes?

Honors (H) classes are special sections of mostly General Education classes available only to Kellogg Honors College (KHC) students. H classes are limited to 20 students.
KHC students must complete a minimum number of H classes before graduation. The minimum is determined by how students joined the KHC and includes the Engaged Learning Experience project.

![students with books](img/bookbbq1.jpg)

## Current Honors Students

### How many Honors Courses should I take?

There is no maximum limit on Honors classes. KHC students are advised to take a Honors class every semester, but it is not required. Starting Spring 2024, honors class lists and Priority Registration instructions are available on the KHC Canvas page.

#### Freshman Admits

KHC scholars admitted as freshmen must complete a total of 7 Honors classes before graduation: 6 GE Honors courses plus CPU 4990H, the required course that accompanies the Engaged Learning Experience (ELE) requirement. Freshmen are also required to take CPU 2990H during their first Fall semester, a 1-unit course that does not count toward the 7-course total.

#### Sophomore Admits

KHC scholars admitted as sophomores must complete a total of 5 Honors classes before graduation: 4 GE Honors courses plus CPU 4990H, the required course that accompanies the Engaged Learning Experience (ELE) requirement. The one-unit course, CPU 2990H, is optional but encouraged for sophomore admits and does not count toward the 5-course total.

#### Transfer Admits

KHC scholars admitted as transfer students must complete a total of 4 Honors classes before graduation: 3 GE Honors courses plus CPU 4990H, the required course that accompanies the Engaged Learning Experience (ELE) requirement. CPU 2990H is optional but encouraged for transfer admits. The one-unit course, CPU 2990H, is optional but encouraged for transfer admits and does not count toward the 4-course total.
