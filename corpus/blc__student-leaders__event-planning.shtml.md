# Event Planning

## Planning an event

If you are a registered student club or organization, this page contains important information about planning events. You must enter in your organizations events both on and off campus. After reading the below Process page and FAQs, please visit [25Live (collegenet.com)](https://25live.collegenet.com/pro/cpp#!/home/dash) to start an event form.

Do you need video tutorials for how to use [25Live (collegenet.com)](https://25live.collegenet.com/pro/cpp)? View our [recorded how-to videos.](event-planning-videos.shtml)

If you want to learn what is required for different event reservations (off-campus, on-campus, out-of-state, outdoor, etc.) check out the [space helper tool (office.com).](https://forms.office.com/r/HqZTtmWuTb)

Remember, your request in 25Live is not complete until the entire reservation is marked as "Confirmed" and not "Tentative." Receiving a Facilities Planning and Management Permit for outdoor events, is NOT confirmation that your event can occur.

## Event Planning FAQs

### What is considered an event?

Club and Organization events are defined as anything taking place on or off campus that is not a class sponsored by a department, organization, or recognized campus activity.

Events can be on or off campus.

### What is 25Live?

[25Live (collegenet.com)](https://25live.collegenet.com/pro/cpp) is a full-featured scheduling tool that allows faculty, staff, and students to search, check availability, and make scheduling requests of spaces on campus.

### How far in advance do I need to request an event?

Standard events include information tabling by a singular campus group, regular meeting spaces (with no off-campus speakers or guests involved), study hall sessions, and/or fundraisers (to last no longer than 4 hours) sponsored by students, employees, or University-affiliated departments or groups.

* The requesting individual or group must request within 25Live **at least 10 business days in advance** (no same day reservations allowed)

Tabling by multiple campus groups at the same event, and unpaid speaking engagements with off-campus speakers or attendees publicized outside the sponsoring organization but intended solely for a CPP audience.

* The requesting individual or group must request within 25Live, **at least 15 business days in advance**.
* If reserving a location that requires staffing the requesting individual or group must request in 25Live **at least 30 business days in advance**.

Engagements with paid speakers and programs or events publicized outside the sponsoring organization but intended solely for a CPP audience where an authorized University official has determined, on a viewpoint neutral basis, that the program or event requires ticketing or entry by invitation only.

* The requesting individual or group must reserve within 25Live **at least 25 business days in advance**.

Events are programs and events that are open to the general non-CPP public and may include youth, ticketing, and national conferences.  This tier typically includes contracts with off-campus vendors and/or with speakers who are expected to attract Non-Affiliates to campus.

* The requesting individual or group must request the event in 25Live, **at least 50 business days in advance**.

### Why do I need to request so far in advance?

For events, there are factors that many checks by different on campus departments. The event review group ensures that all regulations are being followed.

* Police presence
* Fire Marshall requiring law enforcement presence
* CA Fire Code requires trained staff for crowd control. CA Fire Code 403.11.3
* CPP University Alcohol Policy
* Risk Assessment from Risk Management

### How do I add my organization's event to the Campus Calendar?

Posting to the [campus calendar](https://www.cpp.edu/events/index.shtml) is optional. Posting is meant for events open to all campus members to enjoy. Usually, private events and e-board meetings are not placed on the campus calendar. The BLC reviews all events before adding them to the calendar.

**Requirements**

* Event Description in 25Live
  + Clear Event Title
  + The title has no acronyms or abbreviations unless written out with an abbreviation or in the description.
  + Event description must be well written and error-free
  + Call to Action
    - Where to go to get more information
    - How to sign up/acquire a ticket for the event
* Upload an image in 25Live
  + You can upload the image to 25Live
  + One of the questions will be, what is the URL for the image
  + Use the URL within 25Live for your uploaded image

### What is the Facilities Request Form and what can I request?

Facilities can provide services for an additional fee through the [event support form](https://www.cpp.edu/fpm/forms/index.shtml). This form must be submitted at least **10 business days prior to the event.**

**Depending on the size and type of the event, on-site support and additional Custodial and Landscape Services may be required in order to maintain campus sanitation.**

Custodial Services

* Restroom use outside of building operating hours
* Custodial cleaning during your event
* Heating/Cooling systems on for events outside of building operating hours.
* Electrical Assistance:
* Provide power cables & cable covers
* Verify outlets have power

Landscape Services

* Provide additional sets of trash and recycle bins
* Reschedule irrigation (rotating daily schedule from 10pm – 6am)
* Cleaning during your event
* Paint soccer/football lines

Maintenance Support

* Setting up temporary snow fencing around light towers and generators

### How do I create an event layout?

You can go to the [CPP Campus Map](https://www.cpp.edu/maps), take a screenshot, and use any photo editing or desktop publishing tool to add items. Below is a sample layout.

On your event layout, you must label the below items. Note: this is what you or a vendor will bring to your outdoor event. **Items on your event layout will not be provided by any campus department.**

* Tents, canopies, ez-ups, and their size (e.g. 10'x10')
* Chairs
* Tables
* Wooden letters and their size (e.g. 3 letters, 10'x5')
* Any heating device (gas burner, induction cooktop, etc.)
* Any other equipment

![A picture of University Park from the CPP Campsu Map. There are 8 rectangles spaced in the center of the park which denotes tents. There are 8 ovals within each rectangle to note tables. Each tent also has two black dots that signify chairs. Lastly, there are three purple rectangles that signify wooden 4'x3' letters.](../img/2025/sample-event-layout.jpeg)

### Why would I need to meet with the Campus Events Review team?

Any on‐campus or off‐campus event that entails additional risk, need for lighting or restrooms, reviewing campus safety, fire Marshall event plan review, or require additional campus personnel, may be required to have a pre‐planning meeting with the campus's Events Review team.

The Events Review will contact you if they would like to review your event. This is why it is important to get events into 25Live multiple weeks before your event.

### How do I request in 25Live an event that we are having off-campus?

All students traveling off campus must review and fill out these forms, and return them to the group's scheduler. Scheduler, please ensure your attendance list has the same order as the liability forms and upload the packet to your 25Live off-campus event. **Reviewing the attendance list and liability form takes at least 10 business days.**

* [Student Travel Release Notice: Release of Liability, Promise Not to Sue, Assumption of Risk and Agreement to Pay Claims (PDF)](https://www.cpp.edu/rms/forms/student-travel-release-notice-2022_encrypted.pdf)
* [Form: Release of Liability Promise Not to Sue, Assumption of Risk and Agreement to Pay Claims (PDF)](https://www.cpp.edu/rms/forms/release-of-liability-2022.pdf)

**Off Campus Event 25Live Process**

* In 25Live use building "999"
  + Ensure you uncheck the boxes for "hide conflicts" and "enforce headcount"
* Upload these items into your 25Live event
  + Scheduler must have a list of attendees with first name, last name, and CPP email address
  + Each CPP student's Release of Liability form
* You can use the ASI Travel Packet as long as it is complete and correct
* There is a step where you need to add a task for your advisor to acknowledge the event
  + Create a new task using the toolbar in 25Live for your event, on the task page, name it "Advisor Acknowledgment" and assign it to your advisor using their CPP email address
  + Copy the URL for your event's task list and email it to them
  + The advisor can use the URL, login with their CPP credentials, and Confirm the task that was assigned to them to acknowledge the event

### What is a Special Event Permit and why is it required for all outdoor events?

All outdoor events are subject to review to comply with risk, environmental health and safety, and time, place and manner regulations. Request must be made **10 business days prior to the event.**All outdoor events are subject to a Special Events Permit. This is part of the 25Live event request process.

If a Special Event permit needs to be issued, your event may be subjected to inspection during your event set up or during the event.

If the required documents are not submitted in time, your event may need to be postponed or find other accommodations.

### What is a State Fire Marshal (SFM) Permit?

Any outdoor setup that includes tents, shade sails, the usage of multiple tents or a single which sums up to 400 square feet or more, and stages require an SFM permit.

All outdoor event set up may be subject to additional State Fire Marshal review depending on the type of event and estimated attendance for the event. The agency having jurisdiction over campus is the Office of the State Fire Marshal not local agencies.

This is **an additional permit**that may be issued with the Special Events Permit at the discretion of Facilities Planning & Management Department and the Office of the State Fire Marshal.

In this process, you must list the name of the company or vendor you are working with in 25Live.

**Requires supporting documents from vendors**

• Valid Flame-Resistant Certifications (tent/shade sails)

• Valid Structural Engineering Certification from California (tents over 400 square ft and stages)

• If a SFM permit needs to be issued, your event will be subjected to inspection by the State Fire Marshal during your event set up or the day of the event.

**Timeline**

• Submit an Event Support Form, Layout and Additional Supporting Documents **at least one (1) month prior to your event date.**

• If the required documents are not submitted in time, your event will need to be postponed or find other accommodations.

### When is an SFM permit or a Special Events Permit needed?

**Facilities Planning and Management Special Event Permit & State Fire Marshal Permit**

* (4) 10'x10' tents tied together (400 sq.ft.)
* (1+) 20'x20' tent(s) (400 sq. ft)
* Food Truck
* Propane/Electric cooking appliance
* Shade sails
* Light tower/Generators

**Only Facilities Planning and Management Special Event Permit**

* 1 or more Table & 1 or more Chair
* (1) 10'x10' tent
* (2+) 10'x10' tents, with each tent set up 12 feet apart
* Stage (under 400 sq. ft.)

### I have a guest coming to my event, what do I need to do?

25Live will require you list all guest speakers and attendees. Guests may be required by Risk Management to also sign a release of liability form.

### Do I need to maintain a list of attendees of my event?

It is recommended that all events must maintain an attendee list which includes attendee full name and CPP email.

## 25Live FAQs

### Why is my event still listed as "Pending" and not "Confirmed"?

Event confirmation involves a detailed process. You should reserve and plan for spaces at least a month before the event. **Remember:** you cannot proceed with your event until your event is fully confirmed in 25Live. Parts of your event may be confirmed, but remember: **the overall status of the event must say "Confirmed" at the top of the event page.**

* **Space reserved:**Before you can start your tasks, you must get the space reserved by the assigned scheduler for the space. If it is outdoors it is likely the Bronco Leadership Center. If it is indoors it could be the Bronco Leadership Center or it could be someone in that building or college. If it is in the Bronco Student Center or the Bronco Recreation Intramural Complex you need to contact [ASI Conference & Events services (cpp.edu)](https://asi.cpp.edu/facilities/facility-reservations/).
* **Task List:** You must clear your task list within your 25Live event to proceed with confirmation.
  + Each task item has a department or contact name so you can ask that are for assistance.
  + Sample departments are, University Police, Environmental Health and Safety, Facilities Planning and Management, University Risk, and others
* **Task List cleared**: Please contact the Bronco Leadership Center at (909) 869-2841 or leadership@cpp.edu to fully confirm your event.

### Something changed while I was planning the event. How do I change the 25Live request form?

If your event is marked as tentative, you can still update your 25Live reservation to reflect new answers to your previously answered questions. However, you may require additional approvals based on your new answers. If items on your task list already have been confirmed, you will be asked to put in a new event as new answer may change who may need to review your event. Contact the Bronco Leadership Center at leadership@cpp.edu or (909) 869-2841 for assistance.

### I do not see the "Event Form" in 25Live / I cannot see any requestable rooms / I do not see the event type "Student/Club Event Meeting"

There are a few reasons why you might not have access:

* **Registration Status:** Your club or organization registration might not be approved
  + Please check with your President to ensure they received a Step 4 Registration approval email from myBAR
* **Club Registered Approval Time:** It takes 1-3 business days after your club or organizations registration is approved.
* **Register Tag:** The Bronco Leadership Center needs to add the “Registered” category to your myBAR organization
* **Scheduler Listing on MyBAR roster:** If your scheduler is not on the organization roster at all, or if your scheduler is on the roster, but the word "Scheduler" does not appear next to their name as an assigned role, this will cause a delay

### I am not sure on some of the questions 25Live is asking me, can I write TBD or make it up?

No, you must be as accurate as possible by planning all the details with others. Do not make up or put false data into the form. Doing so may be a violation of the [CSU Time, Place and Manner policy](https://www.cpp.edu/free-speech/policies.shtml).

### Where is the scheduler training? I want to watch it again.

If you already completed the Scheduler workshop, you can view your training transcript to review the workshop again on [CPP Learning (sumtotal.host).](https://csu.sumtotal.host)

If you have not completed the training yet, please contact us at leadership@cpp.edu.

[How to use CPP Learning from your iOS/Android device (DOCX)](https://www.cpp.edu/blc/img/leadership-toolkit-mobile.docx "https://www.cpp.edu/blc/img/leadership-toolkit-mobile.docx")

Do you need video tutorials for how to use 25Live? View our [recorded how-to videos](event-planning-videos.shtml).

### Can I use my 25Live access from my on-campus job to schedule events for my club?

No, all club reservations must be input by the club scheduler only. The event type for all clubs and organizations must be “**Student Club Event/Meeting**.”

### I previously had a position on-campus as a student assistant where I had departmental scheduling privileges. How do I make sure my access works for my club/org?

Please inform us ahead of time so we can ask IT&IP can reset your 25Live account. If you run into problems, please email us at leadership@cpp.edu.

### I am an event lead, Club President, or Advisor. Can I put in an event in 25Live instead of the scheduler?

No, only the scheduler is authorized to submit events.

### My advisor put in an event for our club/org and it was canceled. What do I do now?

Advisors cannot submit event requests on behalf of your student clubs or organization. Go back into 25Live and put in the request for your event.

### My event is in the Bronco Student Center (BSC) or the Bronco Recreational Intramural Complex (BRIC). It's marked as "Pending," what do I do?

Please contact the [Associated Students, Inc. (ASI) Conferences & Events (C&E) team (cpp.edu)](https://asi.cpp.edu/facilities/facility-reservations/) for assistance.
