### I want to create an internship course, what do I need to do?

Your department will need to nominate the course and submit a proposal to Academic Affairs.

### Do students needs to complete forms on BroncoSERV?

Students placed at an academic internship site must complete the following forms:

* Academic Internship Learning Plan
* Academic Internship Release of Liability
* Visual Audio Image Release Form

### What forms do I need to submit to CCE?

You will need to submit the following forms:

* [Request to Initiate Partnership - Academic Internship](https://app.calstates4.com/cpp/node/470663) (**NEW** community partners ONLY)
* [Internship Agreement](https://app.calstates4.com/cpp/internship-partner-agreement) (Must have employer’s signature)
* [Academic Internship Learning Plan](https://app.calstates4.com/cpp/node/335540) (Must be completed by student, signed by supervisor, and approved by academic dept.)

PLEASE NOTE: For non-academic internship contact the [Career Center](https://www.cpp.edu/career/student/job-search/internships.shtml) - Handshake.

### Where do I access the internship database?

The internship database is found in [BroncoSERV](../learn-by-doing-good/broncoserv.shtml) website.

### How do I work with a new community partner or organization that is not located in the BroncoSERV database?

A [Request to Initiate Partnership – Academic Internship](https://app.calstates4.com/cpp/node/470663) must be completed to begin a formal campus partnership. CCE will review requests according to the university’s academic internship policy. Once the request is approved by CCE, the Partner Agreement will be sent directly from CCE to the partner. Please note the review process may take 2-3 weeks at the beginning of the semester due to high volumes of activity. It is best to submit the request 4-6 weeks prior to the start of the term.

### How can I learn move about BroncoSERV database?

The [Faculty User Guide](https://documentcloud.adobe.com/link/track?uri=urn:aaid:scds:US:ca2f1c24-67c7-4aa8-99a7-8f1a30f566c3) has detailed instructions for navigating the BroncoSERV database.
