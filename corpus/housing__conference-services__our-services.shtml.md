# Welcome!

## Conference and Events Services

The Conference & Event Services team is a unit under the University Housing Services Department in the Division of Student Affairs.

Our team takes pride in offering superior service and creative solutions to assist in the planning of successful events. We are committed to providing a comfortable, pleasant, and an affordable guest experience on campus.

Conference and Events Services specializing in the planning and coordination of events hosted on campus. With decades of combined experience and strong partnerships with campus service providers, our team ensures every aspect of your conference or event is handled seamlessly. You can book with confidence, knowing that the Conference Services team will manage all the details, allowing you to focus on what matters most.

## Our Services

[![Bldg 73, Room 2](../documents/img/uhsmeetingspaces/73mm-2-sm.jpg)

### Indoor and Outdoor Spaces

Our indoor meeting spaces and outdoor venues are designed to accommodate a variety of events, offering modern amenities and a comfortable environment. Whether you’re planning your next meeting or event, our versatile spaces provide the perfect setting. Enjoy the fresh air and natural surroundings of our outdoor venues or the productive atmosphere of our indoor facilities.](indoor-outdoor-spaces.shtml "opens a new window")

[![students smiling while on staircase](../documents/img/cs/2025-team-photo.jpg)

### Summer Conferences

Our program offers a solution for your summer events, allowing guests to book our comfortable residential spaces, enjoy meals at our dining commons, and utilize a variety of campus facilities. Whether you’re organizing a conference or retreat we provide the perfect blend of convenience and hospitality. Experience the vibrant campus life while taking advantage of our top-notch amenities and dedicated support services.](summer-conferences.shtml "opens a new window")
