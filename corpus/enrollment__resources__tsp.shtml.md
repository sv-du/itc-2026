# Transfer Success Pathway (TSP) at CPP

![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white-mobile.svg)
![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white.svg)

## Discover your path to Cal Poly Pomona from a California Community College​

Cal Poly Pomona is offering the Transfer Success Pathway (TSP) program for all graduating high school students beginning with the Class of 2023. To learn about this opportunity and to receive more information download our brochure or email: tsp@cpp.edu.

[TSP Brochure](https://www.calstate.edu/apply/counselor-resources/Documents/CSU-TransferSuccessPathwayBrochures.pdf%20)

![tsp logo](../img/tsp_logo.jpg)

## Student Eligibility

The new TSP program is designed to increase access to CPP. If you faced academic, financial, or personal hardships that prevented you from attending CPP, this program could help you get to CPP to earn your degree. All freshmen who opted not to enroll at a CSU campus can apply to the TSP. TSP applicants can choose from over 40 selected majors at CPP.

## Apply

Students can submit at TSP application through the [Transfer Planner](https://csutransfer.myliaison.com/). The TSP application filing dates are the following:

* Participating Majors: Opens: August 1, Closes: November 30
* Limited Capacity Majors: Opens August 1, Closes: When Capacity Reached

[See our full list of Participating and Limited Capacity majors here.](tsp-programs.shtml)

[See our list of programs not participating in TSP here.](tsp-programs.shtml)

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What do students receive from completing the CPP TSP program?

Students will have guaranteed admission to Cal Poly Pomona and their degree program upon completion of the transfer requirements.

### Is a student eligible to apply to multiple TSP programs?

TSP applicants can attend multiple community colleges; however, a student can only apply to one program and have one TSP agreement.

### Is it possible for a student to end their CPP TSP agreement?

If a student decides to change to another campus or program in the future, they must submit a request for release and allow time for the campus to review. Students can request to cancel their agreement at any time. However, the ability to select another campus is only during their enrollment period between August 1 and September 30th. CPP will allow students to change programs within the first year of their TSP agreement to allow flexibility to choose a program during their college exploration time at the community college.

### Is it possible to switch TSP agreements to a different program?

CPP will allow students to change programs within the first year of their TSP agreement to allow flexibility to choose a program during their college exploration time at the community college. Students can only switch to another TSP eligible program.

### How does a student submit a TSP agreement?

Students will be able to view available TSP agreements by campus and enroll in a TSP agreement through the Transfer Planner portal.

### How long does the student have to fulfill the requirements of the agreement?

Students must meet the requirements of the agreement and transfer within 3 years to a CSU campus.

### If a student has college credit earned while in high school, are they eligible for TSP?

Yes.

### Can a student take summer courses after high school graduation and still be eligible for TSP?

Yes.

### Who can I contact if I have additional questions about TSP or need guidance on my TSP program?

You can contact us at tsp@cpp.edu.
