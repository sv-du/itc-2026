## Internships

Complete the internship application form if you would like to receive course credit for an internship. Department approval is required  ***prior***  to starting the internship for which you need course credit for.

To receive internship course credit...

1. Complete the pages of the internship form titled; form 1, form 2, and form 3.
2. Submit these completed forms to the Department Chair and department ASC for approval, along with the attached job description.
3. Once you have approval for the internship, you will receive a permission number to enroll in either TOM 4410 or EBZ 4410, depending on your declared option.
4. To complete the course, turn in form 4 of the application, along with the required final report to the course instructor on the assigned due date.

 All instructions can be found on the application. Please reach out to the department ASC for additional questions or clarification.

* [TOM/EBZ 4410 Internship Form (PDF)](../docs/2022semester-internship_course-app.pdf)

## Prerequisite Permission Numbers

Prerequisite permission numbers are required when student transcripts are not up to date therefore when registering, there is an error message saying that the student has not completed the prerequisite requirements. If you have completed the required prerequisite with a passing grade, you may receive a permission number to override this message and help to either enroll you or place you on the waitlist of the desired course. Please complete the prerequisite permission number request form to obtain a permission number. The form needs to be turned in to the department ASC for review. Only one permission number is issued to student per course. The department will not issue multiple numbers to help you in enroll in multiple sections of a course.

Please note that this process is only for overriding a prerequisite issue. If a permission number is required to override the class capacity to help you enroll, faculty consent is required. Reach out to the faculty and forward written approval to the department ASC for a class capacity overriding permission number.

Permission numbers to help you enroll into an internship course are only department approved. The internship application must be completed and turned in to the department chair for approval. Please refer to the internship process if you require to be enrolled in an internship course.

## SAP Student Recognition Award

**Submit the following to the TOM/EBZ Department ASC via email:**

* Completed Application
* Unofficial Transcript

\*The three required minimum courses  ***must be completed and grades must be posted***  on unofficial transcript for consideration when application is submitted. If grades are not reflected for those courses on your transcript, your request will  ***not*** be processed. Submit application once all courses are completed and grades posted.

## College of Business Administration Student Success Center

The    [Student Success Center](https://www.cpp.edu/cba/student-success-center/index.shtml "Student Success Center")    (SSC) in Building 164, Room 1060, is designed to help all CBA majors by:

1. providing general academic information
2. assisting in the completion/processing of various university forms (e.g., general academic petitions, drop petitions)
3. maintaining a supply of most university petitions and forms. Please visit SSC and find out what services it offers.
