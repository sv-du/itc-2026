## Welcome to the Customer Service Center page!

We are here to support you with new hire onboarding for faculty and student employment, employment verifications and all other human resources inquiries. We are located near parking lot C, in the smaller building of the Student Services Building complex (Building 121 West). You may enter the building through the sliding glass doors, and you will find our lobby on the 2nd floor, room 2700. Feel free to stop by anytime Monday - Friday from 8 a.m. - 5 p.m. and we would be happy to assist you.
