# Permission Number Request

[Please complete and submit this form](https://www.cpp.edu/ceis/education/masters-programs/permission-number-request-form.shtml%20) to request permission numbers for Master’s courses (ECI, EMM, ERA) courses only.

Please be sure to check any holds placed on your account before submitting this form. For more information, please check the [Registrar's Office Registration Website](https://www.cpp.edu/registrar/registration/enrollment.shtml)

**Requests will receive responses to the CPP email account within 24 hours during the active registration period.**

[Graduate Studies website](https://www.cpp.edu/gradstudies/steps-towards-completing-your-thesis-or-project.shtml) to follow the remaining steps toward your degree.
