# Alumni Association

## Vision and Purpose

**Founded in 1972, The Cal Poly Pomona Alumni Association is an independent 501 c 3 that supports the mission and vision of Cal Poly Pomona, our alumni and students.**

**Vision Statement:**

Cultivating, building, and fostering a culture of partnership and investment

**Purpose:**

The primary purpose of the Cal Poly Pomona Alumni Association is to establish connections between alumni and their alma mater, as well as among each other. Its objectives include supporting alumni and the university through networking, career readiness programs, events, fundraising initiatives, and student engagement efforts such as mentoring and scholarships. Per the Cal Poly Pomona Alumni Association bylaws:

1.      To assist in continued educational and cultural growth for Cal Poly Pomona alumni.

2.      To promote the educational goals set forth by California State Polytechnic University, Pomona, and to engage in educational and charitable plans, projects, programs and activities in furtherance of these goals in ways, actions and activities as approved by the president of the university or his/her authorized representative to the board.

3.      To promote, establish, and maintain appropriate communication patterns and other mutually beneficial relationships between the California State Polytechnic University, Pomona, and its alumni, as approved by the university president and /or his/her authorized representative.

4.      To maximize the use of; alumni association resources, to support and advance the educational and charitable purpose and goals, as determined by California State Polytechnic University, Pomona and its president or authorized representative.

**Initiatives:**

Annually, the Alumni Association Board of Directors comes together at their annual retreat to identify initiatives, based on our annual alumni engagement survey.

**Current Initiatives:**

1.      Alumni Recognition- Celebrating the success of our Alumni

2.      Engagement – Inspiring support

3.      Career Readiness- Providing opportunities

[### Engagement](#modal-1 "Engagement")

### Engagement

×

***The goal of the Alumni Engagement Strategy is to ensure that alumni feel inspired to support their alma mater in a variety of ways. It is a dynamic road map that begins with cultivating a relationship with our students; continues through their time at Cal Poly Pomona; and grows stronger after commencement.***

***The Alumni Engagement Strategy is in tune with industry standards and benchmarks through The Council for Advancement and Support of Education (CASE), The Alumni Association and CSU Alumni Council. Alignment is not possible without University Advancement, and attention to campus initiatives and the University Strategic Plan.***

* Communication
* Experiential
* Volunteerism
* Philanthropy
