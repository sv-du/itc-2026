# Internships

## MHR Internship Requirement

To fulfill graduation requirements, MHR students must complete **3 units** through one of the following options:

* **MHR 4410 / MHR 4420: Internship**
* **MHR 4610 / MHR 4610S: Senior Project**

> *Students are not required to complete both. If you secure an internship, enrollment in the Internship course is recommended.*

## Internship Policy & Eligibility

* Internship units **must be earned while concurrently working** at an approved internship.
* **Previous internship experiences cannot be credited** retroactively.
* The internship must be **a learning-focused experience** with training, mentorship, and feedback provided by the employer.

### Internship Course Options

**1. Part-Time Internship (MHR 4410)**

* 1 or 2 units per semester
* **Minimum 18 hours/week** during the semester
* Two semesters required to complete 3 units (can be with the same or different organizations)

**2. Full-Time Internship (MHR 4420)**

* 3 units
* **Minimum 36 hours/week** during the semester

### Eligible Internship Roles

Internship positions must involve core responsibilities in **management, human resources, or entrepreneurship**. Examples include:

* Assistant Store Manager
* Department Manager
* HR Assistant / Recruiter
* Business Owner (with new learning objectives)

**Not eligible:**
 Roles such as sales associate, financial advisor, administrative assistant, or warehouse associate.

> If you're currently working in a management, HR, or entrepreneurial role, you may use your job for the internship *only if* new learning responsibilities are assigned.
>
> ## Finding an Internship
>
> Students are encouraged to explore opportunities through:
>
> * [**Career Center**](https://careercenter.cpp.edu/)
> * [**Handshake**](https://cpp.joinhandshake.com/login)
> * **Student clubs** (e.g., [PIHRA](https://www.cpppihra.org), [CEO](https://sites.cpp.edu/ceo/index.shtml), [NSLS](https://www.calpolypomonansls.com))
> * **Campus career fairs**
> * **External platforms** (e.g., [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/), [Indeed](https://www.indeed.com/))
>
> ## Internship Approval Process
>
> **For the Spring 2026 semester,** after securing an internship, submit the following to **Dr. Nastaran Simarasl** at nsimarasl@cpp.edu:

1. [MHR Internship Pre-Approval Form (PDF)](../documents/mhr-internship-form.pdf)
2. A signed copy of the job announcement from the supervisor. If unavailable, a detailed job description specifying duties and tasks, also signed by the supervisor, is acceptable. Here is a sample [Internship/Job Description (PDF).](../jobdescription.pdf)

The MHR Department hosts **two info meetings per semester** about the Internship and Senior Project.
Please check the **MHR homepage** for the current schedule.
