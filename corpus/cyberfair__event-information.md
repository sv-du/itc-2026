## Getting Here

The Cal Poly Pomona Cybersecurity and Awareness Fair is where awareness and the advancement of the cybersecurity industry is happening on October 7, 2025 at the [Bronco Student Center at Cal Poly Pomona.](https://asi.cpp.edu/facilities/bsc/bsc-info/)

### Directions from Los Angeles

* Take I-10 East to Pomona
* Exit Kellogg Drive
* Turn Right onto Kellogg Drive
* Merge Right onto University Drive
* Turn Left onto Magnolia Lane
* Turn Left into Parking Structure 1

### Directions from the Inland Empire

* Take I-10 West to Los Angeles
* Exit Kellogg Drive
* Turn Left onto Kellogg Drive
* Merge Right onto University Drive
* Turn Left onto Magnolia Lane
* Turn Left into Parking Structure 1

### Directions from Orange County

* Take SR-57 North to Pomona
* Exit Temple Avenue
* Turn Left onto Temple Avenue
* Turn Right onto University Drive
* Turn Right into Parking Structure 2

### Directions from Pasadena / San Dimas

* Take I-210 East to SR-57 South
* Exit Temple Avenue
* Turn Right onto Temple Avenue
* Turn Right onto University Drive
* Turn Right into Parking Structure 2

Visitors must purchase a $8 parking permit from the [parking dispensers](https://www.cpp.edu/maps/?id=1130#!ce/25102?ct/25111,25103,0,29218?mc/34.05655044903938,-117.82282186994755?z/16?lvl/0) located throughout campus in visitor parking areas. We recommend attendees park in [parking structure 2,](https://www.cpp.edu/maps?id=1130#!m/673111?share) pay stations are available on every floor.

For deatailed information on visiting Cal Poly Pomona, please visit the [Parking Permit page.](https://www.cpp.edu/parking/permits/pay-stations.shtml)
