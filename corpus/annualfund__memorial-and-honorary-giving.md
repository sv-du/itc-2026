# Annual Giving & Stewardship

## Memorial & Honorary Giving

A tribute gift is a meaningful way to convey sympathy to the bereaved, memorialize a loved one, commemorate a special occasion or honor a person’s life and accomplishments. Your gift to Cal Poly Pomona, made in honor or memory of a special person, ensures a legacy that extends far into the future.

## FAQs

### How do I make my gift?

Please provide the following information:

* The name of the person being honored or remembered
* The name and mailing address of the individual or family to be notified about the tribute gift
* The [philanthropic fund](https://www.cpp.edu/giving/search.shtml) you wish to designate for this gift

There are three ways to make a gift:

1. Make a [gift online](https://host.nxt.blackbaud.com/donor-form/?svcid=renxt&formId=52d8b781-451e-473d-adfa-a190179592dd&envid=p-U8cWxcSPwE-IVTDHD3u4WA&zone=usa) and provide the name of the person you are honoring.
2. Call us at 909-869-6809.
3. Mail a check, payable to Cal Poly Pomona Philanthropic Foundation or CPP-PF, to Cal Poly Pomona Gift Processing, PO Box 3121, Pomona, CA 91769.

### What will my gift support? Can I select the area that I would like my gift to impact?

You may designate your gift to any fund that is particularly meaningful to you, the occasion or the honoree. You can find a full list of all philanthropic funds at Cal Poly Pomona on the [Giving Search page](https://www.cpp.edu/giving/search.shtml). If you do not have a preferred area, we suggest the Bronco Fund, which supports the university’s greatest needs.

### How do I mention this tribute gift opportunity in an obituary or recommend that gifts be made to CPP in lieu of flowers?

We suggest that you use text similar to this:

“The family requests that memorial gifts be made in support of [fund name] at Cal Poly Pomona. You may make a gift online at [cpp.edu/giving/cpp](https://host.nxt.blackbaud.com/donor-form/?svcid=renxt&formId=52d8b781-451e-473d-adfa-a190179592dd&envid=p-U8cWxcSPwE-IVTDHD3u4WA&zone=usa) or make a check payable to the Cal Poly Pomona Philanthropic Foundation (CPP-PF). Please indicate that the gift is in memory of [honoree's name] in the memo area on your check. Mail to: Cal Poly Pomona Gift Processing, PO Box 3121, Pomona, CA 91769.”

### Should I alert anyone at Cal Poly Pomona if I am coordinating or requesting tribute gifts?

Notifying us in advance ensures we are able to appropriately inform the honoree (for gifts in honor) or family member (for memorial gifts) about any tribute gifts we receive. Please contact us at 909-869-6809 or annualgiving@cpp.edu to provide the following information:

* The name of the person being honored or remembered
* The name and email or mailing address of the individual or family to be notified about the tribute gifts
* The [philanthropic fund](https://www.cpp.edu/giving/search.shtml) you wish to designate for these gifts

### How can Cal Poly Pomona support my efforts?

We feel privileged to join you in honoring or remembering a loved one. Please contact us at 909-869-4970 or annualgiving@cpp.edu to discuss options.

### May I create a named fund or endowment to honor a special person?

Endowments are a wonderful way to honor someone and leave a charitable legacy. To begin an endowment, a minimum contribution of $50,000 is needed to generate an annual impact that will continue to support your or your honoree’s passions in perpetuity. There are opportunities for named spaces on campus, scholarships, program support and more. To get connected or to speak to an advancement officer to see if this opportunity is right for you, contact  Jeff Cox, Interim Executive Director of Institutional Strategy and Development, College of Business Administration at jecox@cpp.edu or 909-869-2755.

### I have seen naming opportunities on campus. What options are there?

There are multiple opportunities across campus to commemorate or honor your loved one.

* **Alumni bricks in the Rose Garden** can be engraved with your name, major and graduation year. For more information, please contact the Office of Alumni and External Relations at alumni@cpp.edu.
* Student-built **Bronco Engineering Competition Vehicles** — including the SAE Formula and Baja cars, Liquid Rocket, Concrete Canoe and Autonomous Robot — offer opportunities for an honoree to be recognized. To learn more, contact Carrie Geurts, Senior Director of Development, College of Engineering, at clgeurts@cpp.edu or 909-869-4468.
* The **Don Miller and Ron Simons Rose Float Lab** has multiple naming opportunities. Please contact Sam Moore, Director of Development for Student Affairs, at samoore@cpp.edu or 909-869-5191.
* **Project Benchmark** invites you to sponsor a bench within the College of Business Administration complex to create a welcoming space that inspires future business leaders. To learn more, contact Jeff Cox, Executive Director of Development, at jecox@cpp.edu or 909-869-2755.

If you have additional questions or would like to learn more about other opportunities including named scholarships on campus, please contact Jeff Cox, Executive Director of Development, at jecox@cpp.edu or 909-869-2755.

## Further Questions?

We would be happy to speak with you and assist you through this process. Please contact us at annualgiving@cpp.edu or 909-869-6809.
