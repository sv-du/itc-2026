## **11th Annual Creative Activities & Research Symposium**

**Location:** Bronco Student Center

[View Online Program](program/index.shtml)

11th Annual CARS Schedule

| **Schedule** | **Time** |
| --- | --- |
| **WELCOME AND KEYNOTE**  Welcome and Keynote by Kathleen Blakistone (Ursa Major) | **9:00 to 9:30 am** |
| **PRESENTATIONS**  [Online Program with session schedules and presenter directory](program/index.shtml)  **Session P1:** Posters (9:45 - 10:45 am, Ursa Major)  **Session 2:** Oral - Physical and Mathematical Sciences (10:30 - 11:30 pm, Andromeda A)  **Session 3:** Oral - Physical and Mathematical Sciences (10:30 - 11:45 am, Orion A)  **Session P2:** Posters (11:10 - 12:10 pm, Ursa Major) | **9:30 to 12:10 pm** |
| **LUNCH BREAK**  Lunch will take place outside of Ursa Major. | **12:10 to 1:00 pm** |
| **PRESENTATIONS**  [Online Program with session schedules and presenter directory](program/index.shtml)  **Session 1:** NIH (1:00 - 2:15 pm, Orion A)  **Session 4:** Oral - Behavioral, Social Sciences, and Public Administration (1:00 - 2:00 pm, Andromeda A)  **Session 5:** Oral - Biological and Agricultural Sciences (1:00 - 1:45 pm, Andromeda C)  **Session 6:** Oral - Humanities and Letters (1:00 - 2:00 pm, Perseus)  **Session 7:** Oral - Engineering and Computer Science (1:00 - 2:00 pm, Centaurus)  **Session 8:** Oral - Engineering and Computer Science (2:15 - 3:30 pm, Centaurus)  **Session 9:** Oral - Engineering and Computer Science (2:15 - 3:30 pm, Andromeda C) | **1:00 to 3:30 pm** |
| **CONFERENCE ENDS**  Thank you for participating in CARS! | **3:30 pm** |
