## Directions to Cal Poly Pomona

The Philosophy Department is on the 3rd floor of Building 1, located on the north end of campus.

**From Los Angeles:** Take the 10 freeway EAST and exit at Kellogg Drive.

**From Orange County:** Take the 57 freeway NORTH to Temple Avenue and exit north (left-hand turn) to the campus.

**From The Inland Empire:** Take the 10 freeway WEST and exit at Kellogg Drive.

**From the Pasadena area:** Take the 210 freeway EAST to 57 Freeway SOUTH. Exit on Temple Avenue and turn north (right-hand turn) to the campus.
