# Review Summary Available: Christina Gonzales, Vice President for Student Affairs and Dean of Students

## August 28, 2024

Dear Campus Community,

Per university policy ([No. 1325](https://www.cpp.edu/~academic-manual/1300-1399-academic-personnel-policies/1325-1349/policy_1325_mpp_periodic_review_process.pdf)), the committee charged with conducting the periodic performance review of Vice President for Student Affairs and Dean of Students Christina Gonzales has completed its work.

The summary report of the evaluation is available to members of the campus community for review on request with presentation of their Bronco ID at the [University Library](https://www.cpp.edu/library/index.shtml) Circulation Desk on the 2nd floor from now through November 13 during library hours.

I want to thank Vice President Gonzales for her participation in this important review process. Her strong leadership continues to serve and strengthen our university community. In addition, I want to sincerely thank the chair of the review committee, Cecilia Santiago-Gonzalez, Associate Vice President for Student Success, and the entire evaluation committee for their service. To all those involved in promoting excellence and continuous improvement at Cal Poly Pomona, I thank you.

Sincerely,

Soraya M. Coley, Ph.D.
President
