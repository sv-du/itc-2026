# California Ethics and Policy Center

## Our Mission

The California Center for Ethics and Policy (CCEP) investigates pressing national and global challenges—such as climate policy, healthcare, artificial intelligence, immigration, and racism—through a Californian lens. How do these challenges impact Californians in all their diversity and complexity, and what opportunities are there for California to take leadership in addressing them?
