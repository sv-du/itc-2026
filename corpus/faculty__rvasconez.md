## Research Interests

My research interests are Seismic Design of Bridges, Seismic Analysisis of Structural Walls, Protective (Blast) Engineering, Earthquake Engineering, Structural Retrofitting, High-Performance Fiber Reinforced Concrete, Sustainable Concrete, Sustainable Civil Engineering Materials, Sustainable Built Environments and Engineering Education.
