## Bio

Dr. Shuo Zeng is an Associate Professor of Technology & Operations Management at the College of Business Administration. His teaching interests include operations management, information systems, and business analytics. His research interests include the application of game theory in operations management, business analytics in e-commerce platforms, and sharing economy. His work appeared in journals such as Decision Sciences, Mathematical Methods of Operations Research, International Journal of Production Economics, IEEE Intelligent Systems, and Expert Systems with Applications.
