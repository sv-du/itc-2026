## About the Program

Become one of the innovators that create products pivotal to modern society, from combustion engines to HVAC systems. Build a strong foundation in mathematics, pure science, mechanics, machine design, energy systems, fluid flow, heat transfer, and thermodynamics.
With small class sizes, hands-on labs, faculty who bring their industry experience to the classroom, and your choice of technical electives, you can shape your coursework toward your dream career and gain the support and skills necessary to get there.

## The Polytechnic Approach

Learn theoretical principles in the classroom and apply them with state-of-the-art equipment. Measure turbine performance in your thermal-fluids lab, design a race car with Formula SAE or participate in industry-sponsored competitions. Gear up for a career in countless industries and gain the experience needed to make an impact on day one.

## Career Outcomes

Previous
Next

### Mechanical Engineer

Draw upon thermodynamics, mechanics and material science to design, develop, build and test mechanical devices, ranging from household appliances to robots and aerospace components.

### Robotics Engineer

Combine mechanical and electrical engineering, computer science, and artificial intelligence to create, program and maintain robots and robotic systems that operate anywhere from manufacturing floors to outer space.

### Automotive Engineer

Apply elements of mechanical, electrical and software engineering to develop, manufacture and test safe and efficient vehicles. Analyze vehicle performance to optimize design for acceleration, handling, fuel efficiency and more.

### Aerospace Engineer

Design, develop, and test commercial and military aircraft, spacecraft, satellites, missiles and rockets. Innovate technologies used in aviation, defense and space exploration.

### Patent Lawyer

With an understanding of complex machinery and the ability to assess inventions, mechanical engineering majors can make excellent patent lawyers and help inventors protect their intellectual property and rights.
