# Contact Us

## Contact Our Office

Have a question about Orientation at CPP? Don't hesitate to reach out! You can reach us via our website chat, the contact form below, email, or phone. When sending an email or leaving a voicemail, include your full name, 9-digit Bronco ID number, specific question and contact information.

If you have difficulty or are unable to use the Orientation chat service, please call (909) 869-3604 or email orientation@cpp.edu for assistance, Monday to Friday, 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m., Pacific Time.

## Additional Information

Email: orientation@cpp.edu

(909) 869-3604

Location: Student Services Building (SSB) 121-West, Room 1730, 1st Floor

**Office Hours**

Monday through Friday, 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. We are closed on [official campus holidays.](https://www.cpp.edu/eoda-hr/departments/payroll/calendars/holidays.shtml)

**Parent and Family Hotline**

Email: broncofamily@cpp.edu

(909) 869-3009

Location: Student Services Building (SSB) 121-West, Room 1730, 1st Floor

[Parent and Family Portal](https://www.cpp.edu/family/index.shtml)

[Find Us on Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/Cal-Poly-Pomona-Parents-Family-and-Supporters-116055193513326/)

**E-Student Services**

To chat with a Bronco Advising Center advisor, Financial Aid & Scholarships staff member or Registrar's Office staff member, visit e-Student Services.

[More Info](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/bronco-advising-center/index.shtml)
