# Resources for Transgender Students

## Resources are Available

**On-Campus Housing Options**

* Gender Inclusive Housing
* Contact: 909-869-3307
* [https://www.cpp.edu/officeofequity/gender-identity/housing-gender.shtmlhttp://www.cpp.edu/housing/contact.shtml](https://www.cpp.edu/officeofequity/gender-identity/housing-gender.shtml)

**Gender-affirming Care**

* We do not offer HRT or the continuation of it. However, due to a reciprocity agreement among CSU campuses, as a CPP student, you can access basic health services at any other CSU campus at no additional charge, which may include gender-affirming services. Here are some nearby CSU campuses that offer gender-affirming care:
  + **CSU Long Beach**
  + **CSU LA**
  + **CSU Fullerton**
* [Planned Parenthood](https://www.plannedparenthood.org/get-care/our-services/transgender-hormone-therapy) continues to be a great resource for folks and is another option for gender-affirming care.
* [Use this link to find a Queer friendly doctor.](https://www.lgbtqcenteroc.org/linkage-to-care/) Courtesy of the OC LGBTQ Center.

**Gender Inclusive Restrooms**

* Across from the Pride Center is an All-Gender Multi-Stall Restroom in Building 26 (Old Stables)
* Please see the full list of restrooms[HERE](https://www.cpp.edu/officeofequity/gender-identity/list-gir.shtml)

**Affirmed Name**

* If you are looking to update your gender and name on the official records system so that they appear on your transcript, payroll records, and financial aid documents, you're going to need to submit a Change of Information form to the registrar's office. [Here is a link to that form.](https://www.cpp.edu/registrar/forms/change-of-information-form.pdf)
* For most official registrar changes, you will need proof of legal identity change such as a government-issued identification card, marriage license, divorce decree, or other court document.
* However, there are quite a few places where you don't need official documents of a legal name change. [Here is the link to access this.](https://www.cpp.edu/registrar/student-record/update-personal-information.shtml)
* Students affirmed name may be used for class rosters, appear on BroncoDirect, online Directory Information, Canvas, student identification card, and name on the student’s email. It may also be disclosed as directory information unless the student declines to permit such disclosure.
* For more information, please reach out to the Pride Center Coordinator at pride\_center@cpp.edu
