# Seminars & Workshops

![Group of students in the classroom](../img/seminar.jpg)

## Seminars & Workshops

CREST-RASM will host annual seminars and professional development workshops for graduate and interested undergraduate students, offering quarterly research seminars, quarterly skill-building workshops, and bi-annual NSF GRFP sessions to strengthen technical, communication, and leadership abilities for academic and industry success.

## Seminars & PD Workshops

### Spring Semester 2026

We invite you to join our quarterly seminar and professional development workshop, offering valuable learning and networking opportunities for undergraduate and graduate students.

### Research Seminar

#### April 23, 2026

From Traditional to Smart Manufacturing: Components, Data & Cloud Platforms, and Migration Pathways](#modal-4 "From Traditional to Smart Manufacturing: Components, Data & Cloud Platforms, and Migration Pathways")

### From Traditional to Smart Manufacturing: Components, Data & Cloud Platforms, and Migration Pathways

×

**Date:** Thursday, April 23, 2026

**Time:** 1:00 - 2:30 PM

**Location:** Vy and Tim Li Automation and CIM laboratory (Bldg. 17, Room 1659)

#### From Traditional to Smart Manufacturing: Components, Data & Cloud Platforms, and Migration Pathways

**Presenter:** Dr. Ellips Masehian

This seminar provides a clear, systems-level overview of smart manufacturing, highlighting its core components, the central role of data, and the enabling power of cloud platforms. Participants will learn why smart manufacturing is becoming essential for competitiveness and resilience, and will gain practical insights into realistic pathways for transitioning from traditional manufacturing environments to data-driven, connected, and intelligent production systems.
