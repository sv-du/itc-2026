# X-Rays

## X-Rays

All x-rays taken at Health Services are free of charge and covered under your basic services. Digital X-rays are performed by a licensed radiologic technologist, reviewed by your clinician, and then sent to a professional radiologist for interpretation. They become part of your permanent medical record for 7 years.

If needed, copies are available for a fee, upon receipt of a signed [Release of Medical Information form](../img/medical-information-release-form.pdf). Please contact the X-Ray department for details at (909) 869-2739. Services are closed from 12-1pm for lunch.
