# Employee Safety Training

![students observing instructor operate machinery](../img/employee-safety-training.jpg)

## Employee Training

## Introduction

Below are the courses in the Base Learning Plan for Faculty, Staff and Student Employees.  Supplemental Employee Safety Training Plans depend on the employees job classification and work to be performed. **For all other campus required training's, please contact [Employee and Organizational Development and Advancement (EODA) and Human Resources Operations](https://www.cpp.edu/eoda/index.shtml).**

[Training Assignment Form for New Personnel](https://app.smartsheet.com/b/form/cc74066e4d5c4718abb897d58fe99838)

## Base Learning Plan for Faculty, Staff and Student Employees.

### EH&S Base Learning Plan

The *Base Learning Plan*  includes the following general safety training courses for all Cal Poly Pomona employees (faculty and staff).

* [CSU - Injury and Illness Prevention Program (25 mins)](https://nam11.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fcsu.sumtotal.host%2Fcore%2FpillarRedirect%3FrelyingParty%3DLM%26url%3Dapp%252Fmanagement%252FLMS_ActDetails.aspx%253FActivityId%253D67092%2526UserMode%253D0&data=05%7C01%7Clainarda%40cpp.edu%7Cd8b69c8fe7024d7e166808db25716506%7C164ba61e39ec4f5d89ffaa1f00a521b4%7C0%7C0%7C638144941179354174%7CUnknown%7CTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJWIjoiMC4wLjAwMDAiLCJQIjoiV2luMzIiLCJBTiI6Ik1haWwiLCJXVCI6Mn0%3D%7C3000%7C%7C%7C&sdata=mltu3KfmH1jT2%2BCTO99vdSGodDkr408EX6uZ9d%2FTX5k%3D&reserved=0)
* [Fire Safety and Prevention (36 mins)](https://csu.sumtotal.host/core/pillarRedirect?relyingParty=LM&url=app%2Fmanagement%2FLMS_ActDetails.aspx%3FActivityId%3D82330%26UserMode%3D0)
* [CSU-Workplace Violence Prevention (25 mins)](https://csu.sumtotal.host/Core/pillarRedirect?relyingParty=LM&url=core%2Factivitydetails%2FViewActivityDetails%3FActivityId%3D134225%26UserMode%3D0)

The *Supplemental Employee Safety Training Plans* listed below are for employees working or participating in all classes, labs, research activities, technical work areas, and campus-sponsored clubs and organizations classified by Environmental Health & Safety as a Tier 1 elevated hazard level.  These plans are designed to cover hazard-specific training more relevant to the activities/work being performed.

*Additional Site-Specific Training* on standard operating procedures (SOP’s) or unique equipment can vary from one area to another and must be identified by the Principal Investigator (PI), if applicable.
