# Textbooks Available as eBooks in the Library: Fall 2016

Below is a list of textbooks for **Fall 2016** that are available to students for **free from the library ebook collection**. In partnership with the Bronco Bookstore, each term the Library will be providing you with a list of links to ebooks for the assigned texts that is owned by the library. In the comments column, those noted as “Single user” can only be read by one student at a time. The specific ebook collection is also noted (e.g., ebrary, EBSCO Ebooks, JSTOR ebook Collection, Safari Books Online, and Wiley Online Library), as well as information about a different edition or ISBN.

For other assigned or recommended readings that may be available in the library, check [course reserves](http://reserves.calstate.edu/pomona/) by course or professor.

For information on searching for ebooks, check answers to frequently asked questions about [eBooks](http://libanswers.library.cpp.edu/search/?t=0&q=ebooks).

For books from a previous term, check the [index](ebook-lists.shtml) of available and archived e-book lists.

Click on the book title for access to the ebook.
