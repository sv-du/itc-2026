# Reserved Parking

Parking & Transportation Services understands the need to have guests visit the campus for various reasons. Departments have the option to pay for parking on behalf of guests using the parking Guest Reservation System.

Use the request link below to submit your request. You can indicate whether you want your guest to pick up a parking permit at the [Information Booth located on Oak Ln](https://maps.app.goo.gl/QowABhuNSvC4PJXt9) OR you want us to send you a PDF printable permit via email.

A parking representative will verify your information as well as billing account number. Acceptable forms of payment include Campus CFS# or Foundation Account#. Please allow 2 business days for processing. If you have last minute needs please contact Parking & Transportation Services at 909-869-5417.

[Submit a Guest Reservation Request](https://app.smartsheet.com/b/form/68ebfbb5623b4b059a0e1057364efd4d)

[Guest Reservation Tracker](https://app.smartsheet.com/dynamicview/views/7ba2366c-5174-4e01-840a-eb6d1c156c65)
