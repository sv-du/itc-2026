## About Me

Claudia Wainer received her MS in Design Theory and Pedagogy in 2018 and her MA in 2017 from SCI- Arc . She coordinates and teaches architecture design studios. Her research interests include fusing ancient material wisdom with digital fabrication in both studio and seminar.
