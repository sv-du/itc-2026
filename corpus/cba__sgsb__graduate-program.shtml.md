# Singelyn Graduate School of Business

![program lead](../test/singelyn-program-lead-2024.jpg)

## About Us

The Singelyn Graduate School of Business at Cal Poly Pomona is a regional leader in graduate education. SGSB’s AACSB-accredited programs are taught by expert faculty in a supportive environment. Students gain access to hands-on experiences, mentorship, and networking opportunities with the region’s top executives and global thought leaders.

AACSB

Less than 6% of business schools in the world earn AACSB accreditation,
Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business
