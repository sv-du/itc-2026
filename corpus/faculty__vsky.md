## Education

* Ph.D. in Applied Linguistics, Northern Arizona University, Flagstaff, AZ

* B.A. in Secondary Education, Teaching English and German as Foreign Languages

## Selected Research and Creative Activity

* **Sky, V.**, & Ortiz Moreno, A. M. (2023, October). AI-Generated Images for L2 Pronunciation and Speaking Instruction. *SPLIS Newsletter, As We Speak*. <https://my.tesol.org/news/725477>
* **Sky, V.** (2023, August). Accent-based explicit and implicit biases. *MindBrainEd Think Tanks: How Bias Works in the Brain and What it Means for Language Teachers*, *9*(8), (26-32). <https://www.mindbrained.org/2023/08/accent-based-explicit-and-implicit-biases/>
* **Sky, V.** (2023, March.). *Imaginary Accents; or The Power of the Subconscious Biases*. Independently organized TED Talk at Arizona Western College (TEDxAWC), Yuma, AZ. <https://youtu.be/zhUmjU83K0s>
* Kermad, A., & **Sky, V.** (2022). Using statistical transformation methods to explore speech perception scale lengths. *Language Teaching Research Quarterly, 29,*(pp. 65-91. <https://doi.org/10.32038/ltrq.2022.29.05>
