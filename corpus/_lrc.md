# The Learning Resource Center

## Our Mission

The Learning Resource Center’s mission is to promote student success by providing programs and services that advance students’ critical thinking, problem solving, and analytical skills.

## About Us

Located on the second floor of the University Library (Building 15) in Rooms 2919 and 2921, the LRC is the University’s most comprehensive tutoring service, promoting the development of students’ critical thinking, problem solving, and analytical skills through the Subject Tutoring Program and the Writing Center. The LRC is one of the programs within Academic Engagement & Success under the **[Office of Student Success](https://www.cpp.edu/~studentsuccess/oss/index.shtml "link to the Office of Student Success")** and is certified through the **[College Reading & Learning Association](https://www.crla.net/ "link to CRLA")** (CRLA).

## Our Services

Tutoring services are offered both in-person and online. Email **lrc@cpp.edu** for general questions. Visit **[CPP Connect](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/cppconnect/index.shtml)**, email **lrcscheduling@cpp.edu**, or call **(909)869-3502** to make an appointment. Drop-ins are welcome.

## Faculty & Staff Requests

* The Learning Resource Center can provide LRC Overview presentations, concept review workshops, writing workshops, and embedded tutoring to provide further support to your students.
* Please click on the [**Faculty & Staff Request**](https://forms.gle/mWgeab7Y6x6mAX9bA) link to request the LRC's services.

## Test Proctoring

* Available to Cal Poly Pomona students who need to make up an exam **due to serious and compelling reasons**.
* Faculty must submit a Faculty Request Form (located on **[Testing Services](https://www.cpp.edu/testing-services/index.shtml "link to testing services webpage")** website).
* Test proctoring available to incoming freshman for **ALEKS PPL Assessment**.
* See [**Testing Services**](https://www.cpp.edu/testing-services/index.shtml "link to testing services webpage") website for more information.

[Test Proctoring](https://www.cpp.edu/testing-services/index.shtml "Test Proctoring. ")
