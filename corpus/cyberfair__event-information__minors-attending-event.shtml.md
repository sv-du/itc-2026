## All minors in attendance must be escorted by a parent or guardian, or a qualified sponsoring organization.

A qualified sponsoring organization must provide the following:

* At least two mandated reporters from the sponsoring organization in attendance at the event with/or supervising children.
* All staff and volunteers from a youth sponsoring organization escorting minors is expected to have completed and passed a fingerprint background check ([Penal Code 11105.3 reference in SB 506](https://leginfo.legislature.ca.gov/faces/codes_displaySection.xhtml?lawCode=PEN&sectionNum=11105.3)).
* All staff and volunteers from the qualified youth sponsoring organization must have received training on child abuse and neglect identification and reporting.
* The qualified youth sponsoring organization will need to provide representatives (mandated reporters) when minors use the designated restrooms.
