# Protocol Application Process

## Step 1: CITI Training

* Register with CITI
* Add CPP **[Required CITI Animal Care and Use Courses](https://livecsupomona.sharepoint.com/%3Aw%3A/s/research_compli/EVZKFHIOZ05PrCNBvGChMl8B1ja_xClY0mKk3od3aPtllQ?e=7QRzIT)**

[Register with CITI](https://support.citiprogram.org/s/article/updated-guide-to-getting-started#Registering "Register with CITI. ")

## Step 2: Risk Assessment Screening Questionnaire (eRASQ)

* Complete the eRASQ
* Must be completed by all animal users
* Must renew every 3-years

[eRASQ](https://app.smartsheet.com/b/form/8c07bb2149a8457cbdac2214ace9d50e "eRASQ. ")

## Step 3: iMedRIS Protocol Management System

* Access iMedRIS via Single Sign-On (SSO)
* [iMedRIS Resources](https://livecsupomona.sharepoint.com/%3Af%3A/s/research_compli/Esk1BFubUdJMv-Y8B3eo4S8B6bSLYK9MKuD5zcP7rsfziQ?e=s2DpdA)

Note: All protocol personnel must log into iMedRIS using their SSO before submitting a protocol or amendment.

[iMedRIS Protocol Management System](https://cpp.imedris.net/Application_Main.jsp?s=1763677652155 "iMedRIS Protocol Management System. ")
