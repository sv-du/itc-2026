# EOP Learning Center

## Learning. Empowerment. Belonging.

The EOP Learning Center (EOPLC) provides an inclusive learning community and academic support by combining 1-1 tutoring and academic coaching through a holistic approach. We specialize in supporting students through learning, empowerment, and belonging.

## Learn More

[### Services](learning-services.shtml)

[### Tutoring Subjects](#modal-0 "Tutoring Subjects")

### Tutoring Subjects

×

**The EOP Learning Center offers general learning assistance for writing and presentations as well as course-based/subject tutoring for STEM/Business courses you are currently enrolled in.**

Step 1: Check **[Tutoring Subjects (PDF)](img/documents-accessible/eoplc-subjects-accessible.pdf)**by Learning Strategists.
Step 2: View **[Learning Strategist Schedule (PDF)](img/documents-accessible/eoplc-availability-in-accessible.pdf)**and if it works with your schedule.
Step 3: Make appointment on [**CPP Connect**](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/cppconnect/index.shtml). Check out our [CPP Connect Guide (PDF)](img/documents-accessible/eoplc-cpp-connect-guide-accessible.pdf) for a step-by-step guide on how to make an appointment.

**Writing & Presentations Learning Strategists** provide general support for academic skills, aiding in papers, presentations, and scholarship essays. You do not need be enrolled in specific course to meet with them.

**Business/ STEM Learning Strategists** support students based on subjects like Chemistry, Math, and Physics, or Business Calculus. You must be enrolled in the following courses to access appointments on CPP Connect.

[### EOP Learning Strategists](#modal-1 "EOP Learning Strategists")

### EOP Learning Strategists

×

An EOP Learning Strategist is the official name of EOP tutors who are students equipped to provide guidance by building on a student's academic skills. EOP Learning Strategist's focus is not just on grades but on sharing the skills and knowledge needed to learn meaningfully and independently.

Step 1: Check **[Tutoring Subjects list](img/documents-accessible/eoplc-subjects-accessible.pdf)** and if you don't see the subject you're looking for, please email us at **eoplc@cpp.edu.**

Step 2: View**[Learning Strategist Schedule](img/documents-accessible/eoplc-subjects-accessible.pdf)** and if it works with your schedule.

Step 3: Make appointment on CPP Connect. Check out our **[CPP Connect Guide.](img/documents-accessible/eoplc-cpp-connect-guide-accessible.pdf)**for a step-by-step guide on how to make an appointment.

**Visit our [Contact Us](learning-team.shtml) page for full list of EOP Learning Strategists.**
