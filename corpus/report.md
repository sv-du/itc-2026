# What Are My Options?

![Report @ CPP](img/report-at-cpp-ver2.png)

Report @ CPP allows you to share information related to concerning incidents or behavior happening within the Cal Poly Pomona community. Numerous reporting forms can be found on this website, with descriptions for each. Once submitted, the appropriate team will take next steps.

**NOTE: If you are experiencing an emergency situation that requires immediate attention, please call University Police at (909) 869-3070 from a cell phone on campus, or 9-1-1 using a campus landline.**

**Common Reporting Options (click to submit):**

**[Threatening Behavior](https://cm.maxient.com/reportingform.php?CalPolyPomona&layout_id=34) [Students of Concern](https://cm.maxient.com/reportingform.php?CalPolyPomona&layout_id=7) [Discrimination, Harassment or Retaliation](https://cm.maxient.com/reportingform.php?CalPolyPomona&layout_id=8) [Student Misconduct](https://publicdocs.maxient.com/reportingform.php?CalPolyPomona&layout_id=1)**

[Academic Dishonesty](https://publicdocs.maxient.com/reportingform.php?CalPolyPomona&layout_id=6)

**Unsure which report you need? Scroll down for all available options.**

## **For Students**

## Experienced discrimination, sexual violence, or retaliation (Office of Equity and Compliance and Discrimination, Harassment, or Retaliation, DHR)

This form is used for reporting issues of Sexual Harassment, Sexual Misconduct, Dating/Domestic Violence, and Stalking and will be handled by the Office of Equity and Compliance.

This form is also for reporting discrimination, harassment, or retaliation (DHR) based on [protected status.](https://www.cpp.edu/officeofequity/documents/Protected%20Categories1.pdf)

If you are in immediate danger or require medical attention, please dial 911 or call campus police at (909) 869-3070.

[Submit a Report](https://cm.maxient.com/reportingform.php?CalPolyPomona&layout_id=8)

## Experiencing concerns, in distress, or do not know where to begin (Care Center)

The Care Center is the first place to send students who are of concern or in distress. We connect students to resources they need to succeed (both on and off campus), support them through crises, provide advocacy, and offer Basic Needs services.

[Report a Student of Concern or Submit an Assistance Form for Yourself](https://cm.maxient.com/reportingform.php?CalPolyPomona&layout_id=7)

## Behavioral misconduct, academic dishonesty, i.e., cheating or violation of student code of conduct (Office of Student Conduct & Integrity)

The Office of Student Conduct & Integrity responds to a variety of incidents that may include behavioral misconduct, academic dishonesty, and concerning student behavior.

Members of the campus community have the opportunity to report issues that they believe may be a violation of the Student Conduct Code. The Office of Student Conduct & Integrity will investigate reports to determine appropriate action.

[Report Student Misconduct](https://publicdocs.maxient.com/reportingform.php?CalPolyPomona&layout_id=1)

[Report Academic Dishonesty Incident](https://publicdocs.maxient.com/reportingform.php?CalPolyPomona&layout_id=6)

## Reporting non-emergency crimes on campus (University Police Department)

Contact the University Police Department (UPD).

[Access UPD Website](https://www.cpp.edu/police/)

## Threatening Behavior

For any high level behavioral concerns involving faculty, staff or students, please [refer here.](https://cm.maxient.com/reportingform.php?CalPolyPomona&layout_id=34)

Please note: if there is an immediate concern for health and safety contact University Police at (909) 869-3070.

## Report a Student Death

The Dean of Students office works to support the family of the student and those directly impacted by the death such as close friends, roommates, professors, advisors and classmates.

[Submit a Notice of Student Death](https://cm.maxient.com/reportingform.php?CalPolyPomona&layout_id=72)

## Experience hazing activities

**If you are unsure if the activities are hazing, please fill out this form so that a professional staff member can follow-up with you**. While you are **not required** to enter your name or contact information to report allegations of hazing, please note that anonymous submissions may limit our effectiveness in responding to the behavior.

[Submit Hazing Reporting Form](https://cm.maxient.com/reportingform.php?CalPolyPomona&layout_id=25)

## Reason Not Listed (Dean of Students)

Submit a form through the Dean of Students Office.

[Submit Now](https://cm.maxient.com/reportingform.php?CalPolyPomona&layout_id=32)

## Employee Assistance Program (EAP) - Staff and Faculty

MyLifeMatters by Empathia offers staff, faculty, and their households individual assistance and support services.

When you or your family need helpful guidance, short-term counseling, financial and legal consultation, referral to local resources, your Life Matters program is just a phone call away, and it is available on a live basis, 24/7. Direct Assistance is free and confidential.

* ­Call 800-367-7474
* ­Log on to: [www.mylifematters.com](http://www.mylifematters.com/)
