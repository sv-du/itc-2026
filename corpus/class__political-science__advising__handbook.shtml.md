# PLS Student Handbook

## Guidance and Resources for Political Science Majors

Every year, the department prints a handbook for undergraduates. While it is distributed at orientation, it can be used by political science majors at any point in their academic careers. The handbook discusses tips and tricks for students at each phase of their undergraduate career. Make sure to [download a copy](../docs/handbook/24-25.pdf) of the handbook to your hard drive.
