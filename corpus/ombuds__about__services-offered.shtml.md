# What We Offer

## Support on Your Terms

The Office of Conflict Resolution Services and Ombuds is here for you — whether you are a student, faculty, staff member or administrator at Cal Poly Pomona — our services are confidential, voluntary and tailored to your needs. We will not tell you what to do or keep records that identify you. We listen, explore options and support you as you decide what comes next.

## Your Choice, Your Pace, Your Voice

The use of Ombuds services is always voluntary, confidential and informal — we do not investigate, take sides or make decisions — and will not replace any other services or options. However, we are available to help you navigate. You decide what to share, whether to meet and what steps (if any) to take.

Want to learn more or make an appointment? Contact us at ombuds@cpp.edu or visit our [scheduling page](https://calendly.com/ombuds-officer).
