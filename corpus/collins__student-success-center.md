# Collins College of Hospitality Management Student Success Center

![Collins students walk in front of college buildings](img/collins-largehero.png)

## About Us

We help undergraduate Collins College of Hospitality Management students with:

* General Academic Advising & Course Selection (GE & Core)
* Registration and Graduation Questions
* Petition Assistance & Information
* Degree Progress Review (DPR) Review
* Transfer Credit Review (TCR) Review
* Graduation Pledge Advising
* Academic Planner Review
* Academic Warning, Academic Notice and Academic Notice with Contract Advising
* Resource Referral

## Get in Touch with Us

[### Schedule An Appointment on CPP Connect

We offer appointments virtually or in-person. Appointments are scheduled in 30-minute increments with your assigned advisor. You may not be able to switch from an in-person appointment to a virtual appointment after scheduling. If you are more than 10 minutes late, you will need to reschedule. Appointments can be scheduled no more than 2 weeks into the future. If we are fully booked, please check on Friday afternoons for new appointments and/or check for cancellations.](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/cppconnect/index.shtml)
