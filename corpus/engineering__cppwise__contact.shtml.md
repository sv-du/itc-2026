WiSE activities and programs are coordinated and facilitated by full-time professional staff. Please contact us at wise@cpp.edu for WiSE-related inquiries.

**For admissions** **questions**, please contact the Admissions & Outreach Office at (909) 869-5299 or admissions@cpp.edu.

**For campus tour arrangements**, please contact the Campus Tours Office at (909) 869-3529 or tours@cpp.edu.
