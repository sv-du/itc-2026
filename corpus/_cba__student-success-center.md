# College of Business Administration Student Success Center

![Students walk in front of College of Business Administration building](img/cba-bighero.png)

## About Us

We help undergraduate College of Business Administration students with:

* General Academic Advising & Course Selection (GE & Core)
* Registration and Graduation Questions
* Petition Assistance & Information
* Degree Progress Review (DPR) Review
* Transfer Credit Review (TCR) Review
* Graduation Pledge Advising
* Academic Planner Review
* Academic Warning, Academic Notice and Academic Notice with Contract Advising
* Resource Referral

Graduate students can connect with their [Graduate Coordinators](https://www.cpp.edu/cba/sgsb/contact.shtml) for advising.

## Get in Touch with Us

[### Schedule An Appointment on CPP Connect

We offer appointments virtually or in-person. Appointments are scheduled in 30-minute increments with your assigned advisor. You may not be able to switch from an in-person appointment to a virtual appointment after scheduling. If you are more than 10 minutes late, you will need to reschedule. Appointments can be scheduled no more than 4 weeks into the future. If we are fully booked, please check on Friday afternoons for new appointments and/or check for cancellations.](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/cppconnect/ "opens a new window")

[### Attend Drop-In Hours

Virtual Zoom and in-person drop-in hours are available to all currently enrolled undergraduate College of Business Administration students.
Our drop-in hours are quick 10-15 minute meetings with advisors and operate on a first-come, first-serve basis. Click on this card to get details and the Zoom link.](#modal-0 "Spring 2026 Drop-Ins")
