## About the Program

Contribute to transformational medical and technological advancements at the intersection of physics and biology. The Biophysics option provides multidisciplinary preparation to apply physical principles to biological and medical problems, including basic and applied research, careers in biomedical and biotechnology industries, professional studies for clinical careers, and science education.

## The Polytechnic Approach

Explore biological systems and the physical mechanisms that help them function. Research biosensors that identify proteins, viruses and bacteria. Participate in labs on Newtonian mechanics and human anatomy. With Biophysics, you will get an education as refined as a single cell and as expansive as the universe. You will be well-prepared for diverse pathways, including research, biomedical and biotech positions, and further professional education.

## Career Outcomes

Previous
Next

### Environmental Scientist

Study and problem-solve in areas like climate change, conservation, pollution and alternative forms of energy in the public or private sector.

### Biophysicist

Apply physics concepts to biological systems, studying the processes of living organisms and contributing to areas like medical research.

### Science Educator

Be a mentor for students with a passion for science, passing on your knowledge and helping others grow.

### Imaging Scientist

Develop, maintain and assist with medical imaging technology which helps doctors diagnose and monitor conditions.

## Courses

The Biophysics Option is designed to provide you with multidisciplinary preparation for a variety of careers and advanced programs of study that involve the application of physical principles to biological and medical problems, including basic and applied research, careers in biomedical and biotechnology industries, professional studies for clinical careers, and science education.
