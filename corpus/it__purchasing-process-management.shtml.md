## Purchasing & Requesting Computer Hardware and Software

Policies vary by item and employee status. Contact IT if you have any questions.

[Contact IT](https://cpp.service-now.com/ehelp?id=kb_article_view&sysparm_article=KB0001064&sys_kb_id=0295ca71c3d6d6904f7fbfc6050131a8&spa=1 )

## Primary Use Computers

[### Campus Computer Refresh Cycle

Departments purchase the computing equipment for both new and existing staff on a 4-year cycle.](campus-computer-refresh-program.shtml)

[### Faculty Computers

The Faculty Laptop Program coordinates laptops for all new and current full-time tenured and tenure-track faculty members.](https://cpp.service-now.com/ehelp?id=kb_article_view&sysparm_article=KB0011592&sys_kb_id=f3b67112876721504f5098e73cbb3502&spa=1)
