# Welcome to Summer Conferences

## Summer Conference Program

Welcome to Cal Poly Pomona! We are thrilled at the opportunity to host your upcoming conference program.

Enjoy comfortable overnight accommodations, dining and banquet services, versatile classroom and meeting spaces for groups of all sizes, and top-notch recreational and athletic facilities. We are dedicated to providing a seamless and memorable experience for all attendees.

We look forward to making your stay with us one to remember!

## Why Choose Cal Poly Pomona

[![View of Cal Poly Pomona](../documents/img/img_8839.jpg)

### Location

Cal Poly Pomona is a short distance to the vibrant downtown Pomona, 20 miles from Anaheim, and 27 miles from downtown Los Angeles.](https://www.google.com/maps/search/34.060851%2C%2B-117.820118?coh=219680&utm_campaign=tt-rcs&entry=tts&g_ep=EgoyMDI0MTEwNS4wIPu8ASoASAFQAw%3D%3D  "opens a new window")

### Housing Communities

Find our current housing rates here! Our cozy accommodations are nestled within our residential communities, just a short walk away from our conference spaces and dining facilities](https://www.cpp.edu/housing/conference-services/our-rates.shtml%20 "opens a new window")

[![student residential with view of the lawn](../documents/img/uhsmeetingspaces/7374lawns-4.jpg)

### Variety of Spaces

We offer a variety of indoor and outdoor spaces for your next meeting or event within University Housing Services and on-campus!](https://www.cpp.edu/housing/conference-services/indoor-outdoor-spaces.shtml%20 "opens a new window")
