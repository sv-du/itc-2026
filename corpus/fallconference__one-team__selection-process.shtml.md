# One Team Award Selection Process

* [CPP](https://www.cpp.edu)
* [Fall Conference](../index.shtml)
* [One Team Award](index.shtml)
* Selection Process
* [...](../index.shtml)

## Process

* Nominations must be submitted on the One Team Award application (1-2 pages).
* Nominations can be submitted by one team member or a group nomination.
* Nominations should explicitly explain how the team being nominated meets the criteria for a cross-departmental team and specifically indicates the outcomes achieved and the impact to the campus community.
* All nominations will be reviewed by the selection committee and scored based on a rubric focused on the campus impact and emphasis on one or more of our Core Values.
* The top 3-5 highest team scores will be considered for the One Team Award.
* The selection committee may choose to interview the top 3 teams to determine the One Team Awardee.

## Criteria

* Team members must be from at least 2 departments.
* There must be a minimum of 3 individuals on the team.
* A group is a team when key attributes exist. Examples include, but are not limited to:
  + Team norms
  + Common purpose and goals
  + Balanced participation
  + Clearly-define roles and responsibilities
  + Mutual support and trust
  + Open and honest communication
  + Respect for differences
  + Well-planned and productive meetings
  + Team Spirit
* One Team Award projects must have been completed in the past academic year, or a defined major milestone of a project was completed within the last academic year.
* One Team Award winners cannot be nominated again for the same project in for consecutive years.
* Presidential appointed teams with multiple leadership and staff members, e.g., Safer Return Task Force, do not qualify.

## Award

* The One Team Award will be announced by President during Fall Convocation.
* Each team member will receive a medal, certificate of appreciation, and $50 Bronco Bucks.
* One Team Award members will join President and the University leaders for a celebratory lunch.
* One Team Award members will be featured on the EODA webpage for one year.
* One Team Award members may be requested, on a voluntary basis, to provide feedback to the selection committee to refine the application and selection process.
* One Team Award members may be requested to participate in a select Leadership events to assist other campus community members with overcoming challenges to meet team objectives.
