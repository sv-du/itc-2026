# Womxn's Leadership Institute

## About the Institute

The Womxn's Leadership Institute is a 1-day in-person leadership development conference focused on empowering womxn students at CPP through community building, mentorship, networking and workshops facilitated by industrious womxn leaders.

Our goal is to foster womxn's leadership and mentorship, and **all Cal Poly Pomona students** interested in furthering this mission may participate in the Institute, regardless of their race, color, national origin or sex.

![Students at the WLI](../img/wrc5.jpg)

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the Womxn's Leadership Institute?

The Womxn's Leadership Institute is a 1-day in-person leadership development conference focused on empowering students at Cal Poly Pomona through community building, mentorship, networking and workshops facilitated by industrious womxn leaders.

### Will Meals be Provided?

Breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks will be provided.

### What Are the Attendee Requirements?

* Must successfully complete the [sign-up form](https://tinyurl.com/WLI23) and be the first 75 to attend
* Must be a Cal Poly Pomona student
* Must be available for the entirety of the institute

### I Am a Faculty/Staff Member. How Can I Support?

Facilitators are trusted womxn leaders and role models who lead small cohorts of WLI student participants throughout the event, help answer questions and concerns, and facilitate open dialogues and activities among their cohort. We intentionally match facilitators with their cohort group based on shared lived experiences so that our student participants feel seen, heard, valued, as well see someone like themselves in in a leadership role.

***Thank you for your interest -- submissions are now closed.***

Volunteers support the WLI committee with tasks before and during the event. All folx, regardless of gender identity, are encouraged to volunteer.
