Offered in Partnership with the College of Engineering, the Master of Science in Systems Engineering (MSSE) teaches students how to design engineering systems, solve operational problems, and shape organizational structures. The Master of Science in Information Security (MSIS) program teaches advancement in management or executive roles in security and computer systems. Total 31 units.](https://www.cpp.edu/cba/graduate-business-programs/programs/master-science-information-security-face-to-face.shtml)

### Master of Science in Human Resources Leadership

The Master of Science in Human Resources Leadership (MSHRL) program offers an industry-relevant curriculum developed based on input from HR managers and industry leaders to align with industry standards. The Master’s Program in Public Administration (MPA) is exclusively online. ### Master of Science in Digital Marketing

The Master of Science in Digital Marketing (MSDM) program is designed for those who want to be equipped with knowledge in digital marketing and marketing data science/analytics to inform strategic marketing planning and execution processes critical in the digital economy. ### Professional Development for Every Career Stage

Whether you're entering a new field, upskilling for your current role, or preparing for a promotion, our Professional Development programs are built to meet you where you are. With flexible online options, expert instructors, and industry-aligned certificates, you’ll gain practical, job-ready skills that fit into your busy life. ## Your Gateway to Global Learning

### Programs That Cross Borders and Build Connections

Our Global Campus programs offer international students and professionals a pathway to academic, cultural, and career success at Cal Poly Pomona. From English language learning to custom training and study abroad opportunities, Global Campus connects learners from around the world with California’s hands-on education and global networks. This experience is ideal if you’re looking to expand your global perspective or if you value international collaboration and cross-cultural learning.

[Learn More](https://www.cpp.edu/cpge/global-campus/index.shtml%20)
