## About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in the [Computer Science Department](http://www.cpp.edu/~cs/) at [California State Polytechnic University - Pomona](http://www.cpp.edu/) (Cal Poly Pomona). I earned a B.S. in Computer Science from [Pontifical Catholic University of Goias](https://www.pucgoias.edu.br/), Brazil (2001), an especialization in Software Quality Assurance and Management from [Pontifical Catholic University of Goias](https://www.pucgoias.edu.br/), Brazil (2013), a M.S. in Computer Science from [Federal University of Goias](https://www.ufg.br/), Brazil (2013), and a Ph.D. in Computer Science from [Arizona State University](https://www.asu.edu/), USA (2020), where I proposed a hacker-centric perspective to empower cyber-defense. After defending my Ph.D. dissertation in April 2020, I joined Cal Poly Pomona in Fall 2020. I have also worked from 2001 until 2010 in my own software factory [Marin Solutions](http://marin.inf.br/), where my team designed custom-built, requirements-oriented, high-performance software solutions for different type of companies.

---

My research focuses on **AI for Threat Intelligence (ATI)**, which develops predictive and adaptive learning models to anticipate emerging threats in adversarial online environments. Detailed descriptions of my research projects and lab activities are available on the [Research](research.shtml) page.
