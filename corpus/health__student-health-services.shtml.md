# Health Services

## Let Us Keep You Healthy

![](img/health-services.gif)

No matter where your illness or injury occurred, know that as a Cal Poly Pomona student, you are always welcome to use our services. Most of what we offer is at no charge to you, and whatever is offered at low-cost is priced at ***our cost*** —not marked up like you'd expect from outside facilities. Take advantage of what you pay for with your health fee!
