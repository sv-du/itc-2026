# Application

## Admission Requirements

Admission to this program is based on the holistic view of the following criteria below. Weakness in one area can be made up by strengths in other areas.

* **Transcript showing a Bachelor’s Degree.** Unofficial transcript can be submitted for admission decisions at the application portal. Once accepted, an official transcript must be submitted to the Office of Admissions. See the [admitted students page](https://www.cpp.edu/admissions/graduate/masters-admitted.shtml).
* **2.5 GPA or higher (Transcript)**
* **Statement of Purpose:**In this statement (with a word limit between 250 and 500 words) you may state things that would reveal your motivations and preparations such as: Why you are pursuing an MS in Digital Marketing; How you have prepared yourself academically or professionally in the field of digital marketing or/and analytics; What you can contribute to the program; Your career objectives and how this program would help you achieve this goal.
* **One letter of recommendation**
* **Demonstrated Proof of English Proficiency (For International Students only):** See international office’s site for details about [English proficiency](https://www.cpp.edu/admissions/international/graduate.shtml).

## Optional Documents or Qualifications (Not Required)

**The following documents or qualifications are not required. Not having them will not disqualify you from  admission.**

* **GMAT/GRE**
* ****Other supporting documents:** Resume, certificates, etc.**
* ****Working Knowledge of College Algebra and Statistics:** Students without official coursework in college-level Algebra or statistics courses are strongly encouraged to apply. Students who are accepted into the program without having a sufficient working knowledge of math (College Algebra) or/and statistics are encouraged to take a [crash course](resources/crash_courses.shtml) to refresh their memory by the first semester. Contact the directors to get information about cost-effective solutions to better prepare for the program curriculum.**
* **Exposure to Computer Programming Languages Such as R or Python is Encouraged:**Students without programming experience are also strongly encouraged to apply, as we will provide [DWV in R Boot Camp](https://www.cpp.edu/cba/customer-insights-lab/msdm/resources/crash_courses.shtml) to supplement course work at no extra cost. All registered students will be taught basic and intermediate skills such as literature coding and Tidyverse family package, equivalent to [DWV100](https://www.cpp.edu/cba/customer-insights-lab/curriculum/certificate/dwv/index.shtml) in their first semester.

## Application Submission Deadlines

* For International Students: May 1, 2024
* For Domestic Students: July 1, 2024
