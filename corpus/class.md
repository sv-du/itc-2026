## You + CLASS

### Be Ready for the World Beyond the Classroom

In the College of Letters, Arts, and Social Sciences (CLASS), we embrace the Become by Doing philosophy, offering dynamic, hands-on courses that foster collaboration, creativity and problem-solving. Our polytechnic approach ensures that you gain a deep understanding of your field and develop the practical skills needed to thrive in your career. Whether you’re studying social justice in sociology, advancing technology in geography or conducting innovative research in the humanities, you will graduate with both theoretical knowledge and real-world experience. Here, you become by doing.
