# How to Join the Hatchery

## Application

To apply, fill out an application that describes your:

* Team (can be an individual)
* Idea and why it's innovative
* Target community
* Your plan for putting your idea into action

[Application Now Open](https://forms.office.com/pages/responsepage.aspx?id=HqZLFuw5XU-J_6ofAKUhtIA4mUkTPiRKi2czkQDGumlUN1VLNlJIVTdQTFVET1NCMFlMSlVCRDQ3WSQlQCN0PWcu)

## Eligibility

* Masters and doctoral thesis and citations are **not eligible** for the Project Hatchery.
* Student's competitions are **not eligible** for the Project Hatchery.
* Pre-existing senior or capstone projects are **not eligible** for the Project Hatchery.
* Only projects spanning more than one semester are **eligible**.
* The Hatchery encourages creative, exploratory projects across all class levels.

\*While the Hatchery is open to every student, there are some restrictions on projects. If a team that applies has only graduating seniors, they must be approved for Phase 3 by the beginning of the last semester they are going to be on campus.

\*Limited Funding: Funding is prioritized for projects that align with the Hatchery's focus on exploration and innovation.

\*Please see our [handbook](https://acrobat.adobe.com/id/urn%3Aaaid%3Asc%3AUS%3A0524dbe6-8bef-4936-8d32-dad62cd0555d) for details.

## What to do once you've applied

Please keep an eye on your email as a confirmation email will be sent after you submit your application. This email has the next steps (**pretest survey and a team action plan meeting**) that you must take to continue on your application process. Please allow for 5-10 business days to receive a response regarding your status.

If you have any questions or concerns, please email the Projects Hatchery Coordinator at hatchery@cpp.edu.

![student photo](img/handing-photo.jpg)

### Benefits

The Projects Hatchery is a collaborative network of students, faculty, staff, and external partners. As a participant in the Hatchery you will be able to:

* Build lasting relationships with mentors and future colleagues
* Engage with and give back to their communities
* Build professional experience utilizing innovative techniques, project development skills, and problem-solving
* Receive up to $5,000 in funding for teans if 3 or more
* Receive up to $3,00 in funding for teams of 2 or less
