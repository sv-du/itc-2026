# Scholarships

The below scholarships are available to the Civil Enigneering and Construction Engineering and Management students. The requirements, eligibility, and deadline for application of each of the scholarships might be different, which can be found below.

Please visit the following webpage for submiting your application:      <https://csupomona.academicworks.com/>

## Applications

### Eng - CE - Civil Engineering Department Outstanding Student Scholarship

The Civil Engineering Department Outstanding Student Scholarship is available to all continuing CE students that hold a full-time status. Financial need is not considered. The purpose of this scholarship is to recognize students that have excellent academic records, and outstanding work ethic, while being active in extracurricular activities.

## Engineering - CE - Geomatics Engineering Scholarship by Wood Rogers Inc.

The Geomatics Engineering Scholarship by Wood Rogers Inc. was created and funded by Wood Rogers Inc. to promote the interest in the geospatial and land surveying profession. Cal Poly Pomona Civil Engineering department will select two students for this scholarship. Each will receive $1,000 to help cover college fees and living expenses.

\*Selection Criteria:\*
 \* Open to all class level: Freshman, Sophomore, Junior, Senior and Graduates (including first-freshman and transfer)
 \* Major must be Civil Engineering major with declared Geospatial Engineering option
 \* Must be enrolled full-time (12 or more units) during the entire awarding period
 \* Must have a minimum cumulative GPA of 3.0 or higher

\*Funds Available:\*
 \* One scholarship in the amount of $1,000 for incoming freshmen or transfer student
 \* One scholarship in the amount of $1,000 for a continuing student

Selected applications will be auto-matched based on their completed general application. Eligible winner(s) will be notified via email.

## Engineering - CE - Huff Family Scholarship

This scholarship is open to continuing freshman in the College of Engineering whose demonstrate an interest in civil engineering.

\*Selection Criteria:\*
 \* Open to class levels Freshman Only
 \* Major must be Civil Engineering
 \* Must have a minimum cumulative GPA of 3.0 or higher
 \* Submit a copy of your unofficial transcript with your Fall grades
 \* Demonstrate financial need by completing the FAFSA for 2020-2021
 \* Submit a 500-word essay detailing your financial need and your motivation to become a civil engineer
 \* Must be enrolled full-time (12 or more units) during the awarding academic year
 \* California residency is required

Award winners will be determined based on their academic ability, motivation, and financial need.

## Engineering - CE - James Gates Scholarship Endowment

This scholarship is offering awards to undergraduate students whose a Civil Engineering major and have completed at least 30 or more CE program units.

\*Selection Criteria:\*
 \* Open to all class level: Freshman, Sophomore, Junior and Senior
 \* Student must be first generation
 \* Must be major in Civil Engineering
 \* Must have a minimum major and cumulative GPA of 3.0 or higher
 \* Demonstrate financial need by completing the FAFSA for 2020-2021
 \* Submit a one-page essay describing your entrepreneurial activities, experiences or plans in civil engineering
 \* Must be enrolled full-time (12 or more units) for the fall and spring semester of 2020-2021 AY
 \* California residency required.

Selection will be based on students' entrepreneurial POTENTIAL, essay, and financial need.

## Engineering- CE - Donald G. Wells Memorial Scholarship

Donald G. Wells was a Civil engineering professor and a leader in the environmental and water resources group within the Civil Engineering Department. This scholarship was established to provide support and reward those who have some involvement in their profession using engineering skills/knowledge in some way beyond taking classes.
This scholarship is renewable (dependent on available funds) for the awarding recipient who continues to meet the criteria.

\*Selection Criteria:
 \* Open to current grade level: Junior and Senior including first-time transfers
 \* Major must be in Civil Engineering
 \* Must have worked or intern in the field of civil engineering or participated in networking with the industry/employers.
 \* Must maintain a minimum of 3.0 GPA or higher
 \* Answer the supplemental question(s)

For more information on this scholarship opportunity, please visit this website at https://givingday.cpp.edu/campaigns/donald-g-wells-memorial-scholarship

## RASC - Railway Association of Southern California

The Railway Association of Southern California (RASC) Board is committed to supporting students seeking careers in the rail transportation industry. This scholarship program is offered once a year to local students in the Southern California Region. This year RASC will be awarding two $3000 scholarships to undergraduate students and three $3000 scholarships to graduate students for a total of five.

The scholarships are competitive and are based on the applicant's specific involvement in transportation, career goals, and academic record. Please visit below for more information and application process:

<https://www.railwayassociation.org/scholarship>
