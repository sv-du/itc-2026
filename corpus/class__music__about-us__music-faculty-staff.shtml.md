# Music Faculty & Staff

## Guiding Your Musical Journey

### Faculty and Mentorship: Personalized Guidance for Your Success

Our faculty at the Cal Poly Pomona Music Department are distinguished performers, composers, and researchers, dedicated to providing personalized instruction and mentorship. We ensure that you receive the support and guidance needed to reach your highest artistic and professional potential.

We take pride in our distinguished faculty and dedicated staff, integral to your success. Our faculty bring a wealth of expertise and experience, deeply committed to fostering an environment where you can thrive both academically and artistically.

### Expertise and Achievements: Learn from Industry Leaders

**Performers**
Many of our faculty are active performers, regularly participating in concerts and recitals, both nationally and internationally.

**Composers**
Our composition faculty have their works performed by leading ensembles and orchestras around the world.

**Researchers**
Faculty members contribute to the field of musicology with cutting-edge research, published in prestigious journals and presented at international conferences.

**Educators**
With a strong focus on teaching excellence, our faculty members have received numerous awards and recognitions for their contributions to music education.

### Roles Within the Department: Teaching, Mentorship, and Community Engagement

**Teaching**
Faculty members lead a variety of courses ranging from music theory and history to performance and technology.

**Mentorship**
Faculty provide one-on-one guidance, helping you navigate your academic and professional journeys.

**Community Engagement**
Faculty and staff organize and participate in numerous events, including concerts, workshops, and masterclasses, enriching the cultural life of the campus and community.
