## Education

* Ph.D. Finance, Rutgers University

## Courses Taught

Undergraduate and graduate courses in Investments, Portfolio Management, Fixed Income Securities and Markets, and Derivatives.

## Most Recent Publications

* "Investment Commonality across Insurance Companies: Fire Sale Risk and Corporate Yield Spreads," with Vikram Nanda and Xing (Alex) Zhou,  *Journal of Financial and Quantitative Analysis*, forthcoming.
* "The Wealth Effects of Merger and Acquisition Announcements on Bondholders: New Evidence from the Over-the-Counter Market," with Fan Chen and Krishnan Ramaya,   *Journal of Economics and Business*, forthcoming.
* "Value of Tax Benefits of a Dynamic Debt Policy with Regime Switching," with Michael Dothan,   *Applied Economics Letters*, 24(12): 888-892, 2017.
* "The Fair Value Option for Liabilities During the Financial Crisis: An Option for Lemons?" with Nicole Thibodeau and Robert Couch,   *Journal of Accounting, Auditing & Finance*, 31(4): 441-482, 2016.
* "The Fair Value Option for Liabilities and Stock Returns During the Financial Crisis" with Robert Couch,  *Quarterly Review of Economics and Finance* , 59(1): 83–98, 2016.
* "Interest Tax Shields: A Barrier Options Approach," with Robert Couch and Michael Dothan,   *Review of Quantitative Finance and Accounting*, 39(1): 123-146, 2012.
* "Private Investment and Public Equity Returns," with Robert Couch,   *Journal of Economics and Business*, 64(2): 160-184, 2012
