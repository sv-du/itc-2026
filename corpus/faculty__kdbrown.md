## Profile

Dr. Brown is a Professor of Landscape Architecture at California State Polytechnic University, Pomona. He previously served as Director of the Lyle Center for Regenerative Studies for 14 years, Interim Dean for the College of Environmental Design, and chair of the University’s Environmental Sustainability Task Force.  Dr. Brown is highly active in the community, having served as a Planning Commissioner for the City of Pomona from 2017-2024, and as a board member of Clean & Green Pomona, a local environmental action group, from 2011-Present.  He has been recognized with numerous awards, including the George P. Hart Award for outstanding faculty leadership, multiple community Service Awards from the City of Pomona, Pomona Unified School District, Clean & Green Pomona, and Community Service and Professional Merit Awards from the American Society for Landscape Architects.

[In 2019, Dr. Brown launched Green City Blog](http://greencityblog.com/), to showcase the work of his Green City Studio at Cal Poly Pomona.
