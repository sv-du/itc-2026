## Biography

Maha Ghosn is a dedicated educator, researcher, and academic innovator with over a decade of experience teaching marketing and business strategy at Cal Poly Pomona. She currently serves as a Lecturer in the International Business and Marketing Department within the College of Business Administration and in the Singelyn Graduate School of Business at Cal Poly Pomona, where she specializes in principles of marketing, market analysis, marketing research, strategic marketing problems, and marketing management applications. Professor Ghosn brings a multidisciplinary perspective to the classroom. Her signature course, “Prof. G.’s MRX,” has been recognized as a Signature Polytechnic Experience (PolyX), enabling students to explore data-driven decision-making and applied marketing research through real-world projects and collaborative learning.

Professor Ghosn is currently the Director of the CBA Dean's Teaching Seminar Series (DTSS), a new platform for faculty to exchange strategies, showcase innovation, and strengthen the College of Business’ and Singelyn Graduate School of Business’ culture of teaching excellence. She is also the CPP Faculty Lead for Online Course Services and Student Quality Assurance Impact Research (SQuAIR) Faculty Fellow with the CSU Chancellor's office in collaboration with the Center for the Advancement of Faculty Excellence (CAFE), where she supports quality assurance and equitable teaching initiatives across campus.

Beyond academia, Professor Ghosn has over 15 years of industry experience in marketing, consulting, and business management, including leadership roles in automotive services, real estate, and healthcare.
