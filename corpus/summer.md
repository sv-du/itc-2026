# Summer session at Cal Poly Pomona, open to students who want to finish their degree and stay on track

## ![19summer-t-shirt.jpg](img/19summer-t-shirt.jpg)

### Summer 2026 Dates

## **10-Week Session:** June 3 - August 7 (Finals will be August 10 -14)

## **5-Week Session I:** June 3 - July 3 (Finals will be July 6 - 7)

## **5-Week Session II:** July 9 - August 7 (Finals will be August 10 - 11)

Financial Aid & Scholarships is working on awarding summer aid. Visit the [**Summer Financial Aid webpage**](https://www.cpp.edu/financial-aid/other-programs/summer/index.shtml) for more information, and check your CPP email for future updates.

### Important Dates for Students

**Priority Registration:** March 9-10
**General Registration:** March 12
 **Advising Period Ends:** April 22
 **Fees Due:** May 5
**Add/Drop Period – 10 Week Session:** May 13 - June 10. Last Day to Drop and receive 100% refund: June 2.
**Add/Drop Period – 5 Week Session I:** May 13 - June 8. Last Day to Drop and receive 100% refund: June 2.
**Add/Drop Period – 5 Week Session II:** May 13 - July 14. Last Day to Drop and receive 100% refund: July 8.

The University Library is open for the summer semester.  Check [this page](https://libguides.library.cpp.edu/library-services-available-covid19) for current hours and services or check the [library homepage](https://www.cpp.edu/library/index.shtml) for current hours and to search for books or journals. The computer lab at the east entrance is open 24/7; Bronco ID is required for entrance to the lab.
