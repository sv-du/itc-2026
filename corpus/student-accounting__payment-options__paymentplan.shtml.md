# Cal Poly Pomona Payment Plans

## Overview

CPP payment plans help you split tuition into interest-free installments. Whether you’re a student or an authorized user, you can enroll in a plan that fits your budget. Payments can be made by eCheck (no fee) or credit/debit card (2.95% fee). All plans require a $35 enrollment fee.

[Enroll in a Payment Plan](https://secure.touchnet.net/C20365_tsa/web/caslogin.jsp "Enroll in a Payment Plan")

## Enrollment, Payments & Support

Have questions? Explore answers to common enrollment and payment concerns.

### How do I sign up for a payment plan (students)?

You can enroll in an available plan through your Student Center in BroncoDirect. Just click on the “[Enroll in a Payment Plan](https://secure.touchnet.net/C20365_tsa/web/caslogin.jsp "Enroll in a Payment Plan")” link, select your term, and follow the steps.

Need help? Check out the [Payment Plan Enrollment Instructions (PDF)](cpp-payment-plans.shtml "Payment Plan Enrollment Instructions (PDF)").

### How do parents or authorized users enroll in a plan?

Students must first add parents or other payers as Authorized Users. Once added, they can view balances, make payments, and set up a payment plan.

Steps to add an Authorized User:

1. Go to the [TouchNet site, payment plan provider](https://secure.touchnet.net/C20365_tsa/web/caslogin.jsp "TouchNet site, payment plan provider")
2. Click the Authorized Users link under My Account
3. Enter their email address
4. They'll get a link and temporary password to log in

Note: Authorized users cannot see class schedules or grades.

### What if I don't have an account balance yet?

No problem! You can enroll in an estimated payment plan and enter the amount you expect to owe. Once your actual balance is posted, your plan will update automatically.

### Can I still enroll if I have financial aid?

Yes. If you've already received aid, your plan will be based on your remaining balance. If aid is awarded after you enroll, the system will evenly adjust your remaining payments.

### What payment methods are accepted?

You can use:

* eCheck (no extra fee)
* Credit/debit cards (2.85% service fee applies)

### Can I pay in cash?

Yes. Visit the Cashier's Office to pay in person. Cash payments will automatically apply to your plan.

### When will my payments post?

Payments post within minutes once they're processed successfully.

### Can multiple family members help pay?

Yes. A student can add more than one authorized user. Each person can log in separately and pay their portion of the plan. Just make sure the student signs up first.

### Who can I contact for help?

Have questions? Call Payment Plan Support at 833-269-3675 for help with enrollment or plan management.

## CSU Fee Policy

The CSU makes every effort to keep student costs to a minimum. Tuition and fees listed in published schedules or student accounts may need to be increased when public funding is inadequate. Therefore, CSU reserves the right, even after tuition or fees are initially charged or initial payments are made, to increase or modify any listed tuition or fees. All listed fees, other than mandatory systemwide tuition, are subject to change without notice, until the date when instruction for a particular semester or quarter has begun. All CSU-listed tuition and fees should be regarded as estimates that are subject to change upon approval by the Board of Trustees, the chancellor or the presidents, as appropriate. Changes in mandatory systemwide tuition will be made in accordance with the requirements of the Working Families Student Fee Transparency and Accountability Act (Sections 66028 through 66028.6 of the California Education.)
