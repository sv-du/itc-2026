## About Me

Dr. Zhu received his Ph.D. in Supply Chain and Operations Management from McCombs School of Business, the University of Texas at Austin in 2005. He also received Master’s degree in Management and Bachelor’s degree in Electric Engineering. His main teaching interests are supply chain management related areas and e-business. His research areas are new product development and supply chain management. His work has appeared in journals such as *Management Science, Production and Operations Management*

## Selected Publications
