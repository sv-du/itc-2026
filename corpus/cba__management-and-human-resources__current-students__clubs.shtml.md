## [Collegiate Entrepreneurs Organization](https://calpolypomona.campuslabs.com/engage/organization/cppceo)

The **Collegiate Entrepreneurs’ Organization (CEO)** is a student-run club at Cal Poly Pomona.

We invite you to join the CEO club if you are considering:

* Connecting with a group of entrepreneurially-minded and fun CPP students across campus.
* Starting your business one day.
* Developing your creativity and entrepreneurial mindset.
* Starting or improving your side hustle.
* Working or finding an internship at a startup or a small business soon.
* Taking your family business to the next level.

## [Professionals in Human Resources Association](https://www.pihra.org/find-pihra-chapters/pihra-cal-poly-pomona/)

Founded in 1994, the Cal Poly Pomona Professionals in Human Resources Association (CPP PIHRA) is a non-profit student organization dedicated to the field of Human Resource Management. We are a SHRM and PIHRA Student Chapter that offers various opportunities for all members to become more educated in Human Resources. Members can participate in the many professional activities we plan every year, including community service outings, socials, fundraisers, seminars, workshops, and presentations.

**Mission Statement:**To prepare students for the advanced business world by providing networking opportunities, leadership development, and resources to develop a better understanding of the human resources field.

**Meetings Held:**Every Thursday
**Time:** 11:45 AM - 12:45 PM
**Location:** Building 163 Room 2015

## [National Society of Leadership and Success](https://mybar.cpp.edu/organization/nsls)

The National Society for Leadership and Success (NSLS) is a nationwide organization that offers a key leadership program for college students, regardless of their major.

The mission of NSLS is to help students achieve personal growth and career success by giving members tools to make a positive impact in their communities. Two core values guide us. First, we recognize, nurture, and honor leaders while creating lasting, positive change in the world. Second, we strive to help members identify and achieve their goals, and we provide real-world experience that gives our members the best chance to succeed in their future endeavors.

Some of the benefits students gain by becoming members of NSLS include, but are not limited to:

• Provides access to benefits including scholarships, awards, and letters of recommendation

• Exclusive on-campus professional development events

• Employer recruitment through an online job bank

• Exposure to different faculty across the different colleges here at Cal Poly Pomona

• Create and foster networking opportunities with students and alums at Cal Poly Pomona

• Provide leadership development and career readiness opportunities through club interactions, community service, and guest speakers
