# Who to Contact for Your Project and Thesis

## Format Reviews

Prior to submitting their final thesis to Bronco ScholarWorks, students are required to have it reviewed for formatting errors and to ensure that it meets the University's standards for publication in the institutional repository. Please review this [sample guide (pdf)](../documents/sample-guide.pdf) , [Thesis Formatting Checklist (PDF)](../documents/formatting-checklist.pdf), and the [Master's Thesis Formatting Guidelines](formatting-guidelines.shtml) BEFORE requesting a formatting approval.

Once you have formatted your work, submit an email request that includes your thesis as a PDF (.pdf) attachment to formatreview@cpp.edu.

## Bronco Scholarworks Submission

For questions on the project and thesis electronic submission process please email: broncoscholar@cpp.edu

## Writing Center

Writing assistance is available from the [LRC University Writing Center](https://www.cpp.edu/lrc/index.shtml)
