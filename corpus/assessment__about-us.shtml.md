# About Us

## Who We Are

### Mission

The Office of Assessment and Program Review empowers academic programs to use meaningful, evidence-informed practices that improve student learning and academic effectiveness.

### Vision

We are a campus where a shared commitment to assessment drives academic excellence and strategic decision-making.

## Committees

[### Academic Assessment Committee

The Academic Assessment Committee’s purpose is to promote and facilitate meaningful advancements in building an institutional culture of assessment to strengthen teaching and student learning at Cal Poly Pomona. The Academic Assessment Committee allows for open communication and coordination of efforts in learning outcomes assessment at the program and institutional levels, including the General Education program.](academic-assessment-committee.shtml)

[### Program Review Committee

The Program Review Committee is an advisory body to the Academic Senate and the Office of Assessment and Program Review. Members provide feedback to assigned academic programs on matters related to the substantive elements of Cal Poly Pomona’s program review with emphasis on institutional-specific issues not addressed by external reviewers. The Program Review Committee also provides feedback and suggests improvements to the Office of Assessment and Program Review concerning the program review process, strengthening the relationship between the two entities to maintain the integrity, significance, and relevance of the program review.](program-review-committee.shtml)
