# Plan your next Conference

## Information Request

Thank you for taking an interest in hosting your summer conference with University Housing Services at Cal Poly Pomona. The Conference Service (CS) team takes great pride in providing a “one-stop shop” for all of your conference planning needs. Please fill out the information below and one of our Conference Services staff members will create an estimate for you within 5 business days.
