# SAW Luncheon

## Information

**Date:** Wednesday, May 20, 2025

**Time:** 11:30 AM - 1:30 PM

**Location:** Centerpointe

**Register here on Tixtree:** Coming Soon

*Note: Raffle has been moved to the Basketball Game.*

---

**BRONCO CARDS REQUIRED FOR LUNCHEON**

Bronco cards are required to enter Centerpointe for the luncheon. If you do not have your card available, you can access your virtual Bronco card through the **Bronco One Card Management Portal.**

**WILL NEED SMARTPHONE OR TABLET TO ACCESS VIRTUAL ID**

Click to access:**[Bronco One Card Management Portal](https://connectcpp.atriumcampus.com/)**

Choose the following to access Virtual ID

* Bronco One Card Management Portal
* Menu (3 lines upper left of screen)
* Display Virtual Bronco ID (bottom of menu options)
* Virtual ID
* Employee is required to complete 2-step duo verification before ID can be accessed

This can be on a smartphone or tablet but requires 2-step duo verification (duo push). You will need to have your verification device available.

NOTE: **SCREENSHOTS WILL NOT WORK!** A screenshot of your Bronco ID card is not recognized by the scanner, only Bronco One Card Virtual ID.
