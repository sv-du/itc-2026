## About the Program

Use movement and musicality to convey emotion and make larger statements about life, culture and society. Learn the history of dance and adopt a more global perspective of the art. With practice across forms and styles of dance, as well as technical skills in theatre, you will gain a comprehensive education that will prepare you for a career as a performer or versatile professional in the entertainment industry.

## The Polytechnic Approach

Explore the intersection of dance, theater and performance, gaining an integrated understanding of both the physical and theatrical elements of the craft. From practicing dance techniques to learning the intricacies of choreography and technical production, you’ll develop skills that apply beyond the stage and studio. Using hands-on experience and collaborative projects as your foundation, you’ll excel in a wide range of creative roles as a performer and behind the scenes. Combining artistic expression with technical expertise, you’ll be ready to contribute to every aspect of the performing arts, telling powerful stories through movement.

## Career Outcomes

Card 1 of 4

### Choreographer

Create and arrange original dance routines for theatre, film, television, music videos and animation.

Card 2 of 4

### Movement Director

Guide actors and performers through body language during scenes even without music or dance numbers, helping to convey the nonverbal signs of emotion more effectively in scenes.

Card 3 of 4

### Dance Photographer

With expert knowledge of dance, production and technique, you’re perfectly positioned to capture stunning commercial and industry-ready photos of the human body in artistic movement.

Card 4 of 4

### Dance Company Director

Manage and run your own dance company, helping others fulfill their dreams.

## Courses

The Dance Theatre option combines the rigorous study of dance techniques with a strong foundation in theatre, allowing you to explore the intersection of these two art forms. You will receive training in various dance styles, choreography, and movement while also engaging in theatre production and performance. The program provides numerous opportunities for you to showcase your talents, including annual student/faculty dance concerts and other campus-wide performances. You will be well-prepared for careers in dance, theatre, or further study in dance-related disciplines.

For the most up-to-date information on courses for this program, visit the course catalog or contact an advisor.
