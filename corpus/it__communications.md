### Be Alert of Phishing and Scams as the Holidays Approach

Be cautious when responding to an email or other messages requesting information. Phishing attempts to acquire sensitive information, including usernames, passwords, financial information, and assets often impersonate members of an organization you trust, such as Cal Poly Pomona (CPP). A phish can use any method of communication and these attempts tend to pick up during times of stress like finals and the holiday season.](#modal-5 "Be Alert of Phishing and Scams as the Holidays Approach")
