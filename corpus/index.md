## Why Cal Poly Pomona

### A Place to Learn By Doing

[![Two students working on Rose Float](img/19rose-float-deco-phun2.jpg)

#### The Polytechnic Experience

Here, you’ll roll up your sleeves, dive in and hit the ground doing. You’ll put learning to work in practical, hands-on ways, and you’ll gain the confidence and skills needed to become career ready.](https://www.cpp.edu/aboutcpp/polyx.shtml%20%20%20%20%20%20)

[![Graduate Lauren with her family](img/24ag-grad_91.jpg)

#### Brighter Financial Futures

Some college degrees hang on a wall. Get one that also paves the way to a brighter financial future — from the #1 polytechnic university for economic mobility.](https://www.cpp.edu/news/content/2023/09/elevating-students-cpp-is-top-polytechnic-for-value-economic-mobility-in-new-rankings/index.shtml%20%20%20%20%20%20)

[![Javyion Campbell and his friends](img/jayvion-campbell-and-friends.jpg)

#### Belonging

Be inspired every day, inside and outside the classroom, by a diverse, supportive and creative community that will help you discover who you are and what you’re capable of.](https://www.cpp.edu/student-affairs/topics/diversity.shtml%20%20%20%20%20%20)

[![Faculty assisting two students during orientation](img/24orientation-mares.jpg)

#### A Culture of Care

Navigating the ins-and-outs of the college experience can feel overwhelming when you’re not following familiar footsteps. Follow your path to greatness with a whole community behind you every step of the way.](https://www.cpp.edu/campus-life/index.shtml%20%20%20%20%20%20)

The CSU Board of Trustees has launched a nationwide search for the next president of Cal Poly Pomona. As the #1 polytechnic university for diversity & economic mobility, Cal Poly Pomona seeks an exceptional leader who will champion our students, mission and values. We invite the campus community and our diverse stakeholders to be part of the process.
