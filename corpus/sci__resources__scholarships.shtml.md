# Scholarships

![Professor Arlo Caine poses with scholarship recipient Mark Maldonado and representative of the Armando Gallegos Memorial Scholarship.](../img/math-scholarship-2025.jpg)

All College of Science students are encouraged to participate in research, and apply for scholarships and grants. Please note that all scholarship applications are to be made through the [Bronco Scholarship Portal](https://csupomona.academicworks.com).
