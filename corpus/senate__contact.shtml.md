### Location

**George P. Hart Academic Senate Offices**
 Bldg. 98-P2-8 (CLA Paseo)
 x2283

George P. Hart was a well-respected member of the Political Science Department for 30 years and an Associate Dean in the College of Letters, Arts & Social Sciences. Hart was active on the Academic Senate for 15 years serving two non-consecutive terms as Chair.
