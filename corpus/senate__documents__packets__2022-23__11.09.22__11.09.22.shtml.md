# November 9, 2022 Academic Senate Meeting

Time: 3:00 to 5:00 p.m.
Location: CLA Building 98-P2-007 (classroom off the Paseo)

**Safer Return Task Force recommends masking indoors**

[November 9, 2022 Academic Senate Agenda](academic_senate_agenda_11.09.22.pdf)

1. Academic Senate Minutes - [September 21, 2022](academic_senate_minutes_09.21.22_posted.pdf) and October 19, 2022

* There will be a motion to postpone the October 19th minutes

2.  Information Items

1. Chair's Report
   * Available Service Opportunities - <https://www.cpp.edu/senate/service-opp-new.shtml>
2. President's Report (President Coley will not be in attendance)
3. [Provost's Report](final_provosts_report_to_academic_senate_2022-11-09.pdf)
4. [Vice Chair's Report](2022.11.09_report_status_summary.pdf)
   * [Report Status](report_status_11.09.22.xlsx)
5. [CSU Academic Senate Report](ascsu-senator-report.pdf)
   * [Engaging Intersegmental Discussions Regarding College Preparatory Coursework in Mathematics](3571-apep---area-c-post-plenary.pdf)
   * [On the Intersegmental Committee of Academic Senates (ICAS) CAL-GETC Proposal: Action](xxx3569-apep---cal-getc-proposal-post-plenary.pdf)
6. [Budget Report](budget-report-11_2022.pdf)
   * [Division of Student Affairs Budget Report](2022-budget-cover--academic-senate-presentation.pdf)
7. CFA Report
8. ASI Report
9. [Staff Report](11.09.22---staff-report.pdf)
10. [Safer Return Task Force Report](presentation_situational-awareness_academic-senate_11.09.22.pdf)

3. Academic Senate Committee Reports - Time Certain 3:45 p.m

1. [AP-003-212, Revised Charter for Science, Technology, and Society Major and Minor](ap-003-212_report_first_reading_revised-charter-for-sts-major-and-minor_11.09.22.pdf) - **FIRST READING**
   * [Revised Charter with Changes](charter_for_sts_major_and_minor_with_markups_indicating_modifications-rev002-w-reformatting-of-section-2e-2.pdf)

4. New Business

1. [Adoption of Dean Erik Rolland's Resolution for Faculty Emeritus](emeritus_resolution_rolland.pdf)
2. [Sense of the Senate Resolution, In Support of Academic Freedom and Solidarity with Iranian Women - "Woman, Life, Freedom" - University Communities, and International Students](cpp-resolution.draft.2022-011-09.docx) (agenda will be amended from floor to include resolution)

5. Old Business

1. Brand Building Presentation - Amon Rappaport, Senior Associate Vice President and Chief Communications Officer
