# Getting Started

## There are a variety of ways to learn about the education abroad opportunities available through Cal Poly Pomona.  Our office has the information you need to start your search and select the program to fit your academic needs.

![two girls looking at laptop](../img/sa-getting-started-advising-500.jpg)

## [Advising](advising.shtml)

Our staff is available to walk you through the first steps of choosing to study abroad.

![finger and earth](../img/sa-getting-started-program-500.jpg)

## [Compare Programs](programs.shtml)

Whether this is your first time abroad or you are looking for a more independent experience, we offer a range of program types that will suit your academic interests.

![advisor course checklist](../img/sa-advisor-course-aproval-500.jpg)

## [Course Approval and Transcript](../advisors/course-approval-transcripts.shtml)

Learn about how a student's coursework abroad connects their degree requirements with Study Abroad programs.

![global money](../img/sa-getting-started-costfinance.jpg)

## [Costs & Financing](cost-financing.shtml)

There are many ways to help pay for education abroad and maximize the resources that are available to you.

![visa passport](../img/sa-getting-started-passport-500.jpg)

## [Passports and Visas](passport-visa.shtml)

U.S. and non-U.S. citizens need a valid passport both to enter other countries and to return to the United States. Certain countries may also require a visa that allows you to enter and remain in that country for a specific reason.

![faqs](../img/sa-faq-block.jpg)

## [Frequently Asked Questions](../faqs1/index.shtml)

Find answers to the frequently asked questions on education abroad at Cal Poly Pomona.

![](../img/study_abroad_101_300.jpg)

## [Events and Workshops](events-workshops.shtml)

Study Abroad 101: This is the first step for Broncos to Study Abroad! Learn about types of programs, course pre-approval, how credit applies to your DPR, Financial Aid & Scholarships, getting connected with a Global Ambassador, and more! SA101 is a ~30-minute presentation +15 minutes of Q&A

![person working on a laptop](../img/how-to-apply.jpg)

## Submit Your Application

Interested in studying abroad? **[Start your application today](how-to-apply.shtml)**.

!["health safety" yellow road sign](../img/health_safety_security.jpg)

## Health, Safety, and Security

 In partnership with our office, SERM prepares students and faculty going abroad with health insurance, pre-departure orientations, emergency and crisis management protocols, travel guidelines, and the best **[health, safety and security practices](health-safety-security.shtml)**.
