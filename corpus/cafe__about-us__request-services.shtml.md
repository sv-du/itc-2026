# Request Services

[![person on wheelchair icon](../img/icons/icon-accessibility.svg)

## Accessibility Consultation

Request a consultation regarding making instructional materials accessible or in response to a notification from the Disability Resource Center (DRC).](https://cpp.service-now.com/ehelp?id=sc_cat_item&sys_id=355426dd6f3c43007996073aea3ee401)

[![computer os window with silhouette of teacher](../img/icons/icon-lms.svg)

## Canvas Requests

Request assistance with Canvas, which can be used to supplement a traditional class, support a hybrid or blended course, or be the main delivery structure for an online course.](https://cpp.service-now.com/ehelp?id=sc_cat_item&sys_id=df0d4725dbffa0d0f0eed2e3ca9619b7&sysparm_category=d6f972c313ab130027a43ff18144b0a4)

[![computer os window with checkboxes](../img/icons/icon-instructionaldesign.svg)

## Instructional Design (ID) Consultation

Request a consultation with an experienced instructional designer to redesign a course or module, integrate active learning strategies, or develop a new teaching idea that you would like to try.](https://cpp.service-now.com/ehelp?id=sc_cat_item&sys_id=c2cfa1e26fe18b007996073aea3ee47e)

[![computer os window with a lightning bolt](../img/icons/icon-multimedia.svg)

## Multimedia Development

Request development of interactive multimedia or animations, known as Multimedia Learning Objects (MLO).](https://cpp.service-now.com/ehelp?id=sc_cat_item&sys_id=0547f5e66fe18b007996073aea3ee499)

[![computer os window with hand clicking a remote device](../img/icons/icon-technology.svg)

## Learning Technologies

Request assistance with Camtasia, iClicker, and much more! Help is also available at Studio 6, our faculty/staff computer lab.](https://cpp.service-now.com/ehelp?id=sc_cat_item&sys_id=7d49fd2a6fe18b007996073aea3ee4d1)

[![computer os window with silhouette of people on Zoom conference call](../img/icons/icon-workshops.svg)

## Workshops

Request a CAFE workshop tailored to your department or college's needs.](https://cpp.service-now.com/ehelp?id=sc_cat_item&sys_id=a8c0ede66fa18b007996073aea3ee482)
