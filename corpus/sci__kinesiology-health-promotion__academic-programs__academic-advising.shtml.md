# Academic Advising

For basic advising questions, submission of graduation plans, and approval of petitions, please see the College of Science advisors:

**College of Science Advisors**Location: Building 3 Room 1645
909-979-5654
sci\_advising@cpp.edu

**Advising Hours for College of Science Advisors**
Please visit the [College of Science Advising Center](https://www.cpp.edu/sci/advising-center/contact.shtml) website for more information regarding hours and appointments. When scheduling through CPP Connect, please select the College of Science Advising Center as the location not the Kinesiology department.
