# Black Alumni & Friends Chapter

![alumni presenting during mixer event at innovation brew works](../img/baaf-aboutpic.jpg)

## Mission Statement

The Black Alumni and Friends chapter welcomes all alumni and students, regardless of race, color, national origin, or sex and is committed to assisting in continued educational and cultural growth and development of students. The chapter promotes social activities that enhance the educational and charitable purpose of the association.
