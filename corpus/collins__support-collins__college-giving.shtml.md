## Support the Future of Hospitality

YOU make the success of The Collins College of Hospitality Management possible! Join our community of alumni, faculty, staff and industry partners who champion excellence in hospitality education.

![Three 2025 Collins College grads smile during commencement.](../img/25collins-grad_79.gif)

![Krista Spangler](../img/krista-spanglersqaure.jpeg)

## Your Giving Contact

Krista Spangler, senior director of development, is here to help you explore giving opportunities and answer any questions. Reach out at 909-869-4450 or email klspangler@cpp.edu to get started.

## Areas of Need

1. ### Unrestricted resources

   or Excellence Funds support the college’s greatest needs.
2. ### Scholarships

   create opportunities for students from all walks of life to attend Collins and reach their full potential — regardless of their financial circumstances.
3. ### Student/faculty research

   support fosters “learn-by-doing” experiences and serving others through discovery and impact.
4. ### Program support

   enables students to explore their passions both in and out of the classroom and maximize their Cal Poly Pomona experience. Examples include workshops, academic counseling and internships that foster student success.
5. ### Facilities and equipment

   play a vital role in meeting our educational mission — empowering teaching, learning and outreach.
6. ### Lasting legacy or endowed funds

   provide support in perpetuity. The initial gift is invested, and funds are awarded from the investment income, preserving the principal so funds continue to generate income in future years.

## Give Your Way

Cal Poly Pomona’s gift planning professionals can work with you to discuss how The Collins College can be included in your financial and estate plans to support the college, provide substantial tax benefits and maximize charitable giving. Gifts to the college are tax deductible to the full extent of the Internal Revenue Code.

### Instant Online Giving

To make an instant gift to the Annual Fund, simply complete the form above and provide your credit card information.

### Check or Money Orders

Send a check or money order made payable to the Cal Poly Pomona Foundation, and we will send you the receipt:

Cal Poly Pomona Gift Processing
PO Box 3121
Pomona, CA 91769

### Pledges

If you’d like to make a pledge that can be paid off in consecutive monthly installments, contact Raven Carter, annual fund assistant, at 909-869-4321 or rccarter@cpp.edu.

### Gifts of Securities

Please contact Vince Fraumeni at 909-869-4825 or vjfraumeni@cpp.edu.

### Planned Giving

Planned gifts can be designed to meet your personal philanthropic and financial goals. Learn more by visiting our [Planned Giving website](https://cpp.giftlegacy.com/).

### Matching Gifts

Multiply the effects of your generosity by using a corporate matching gift program. To find out if your company has a matching gift policy, please visit our [Matching Gifts page](https://www.cpp.edu/giving/ways-to-give/matching-gifts.shtml).

### In-kind Gifts

Gifts of tangible personal property, such as equipment, materials, crops and company products or inventory.

### Payroll Deductions

Faculty and staff can demonstrate support for students and pride in their work and the impact of CPP. Contact gifts@cpp.edu.

### The Mark Peel Culinary Scholarship Endowment

Please join us in continuing Mark’s legacy and commitment to the industry and students by supporting the [Mark Peel Culinary Scholarship Endowment Fund](https://crowdfund.cpp.edu/project/29109).
