# Alumni & External Relations

## Bronco Network Events

### Upcoming Events

The Cal Poly Pomona Alumni Association hosts over 60 events per year ranging from alumni panels, mixers, networking events, career readiness workshops, and industry discussions.

[![baaf alumni gathering ](../img/baaf-gather26-thumb.jpg)

### Black Alumni & Friends Alumni Gathering

Let's reconnect, reminisce, and celebrate at the Black Alumni & Friends Alumni Gathering 2026!
Join us Saturday, February 21, 2026 as Cal Poly Pomona's Black Alumni and Friends Chapter sponsors an alumni gathering at Kellogg House Pomona. This year's keynote speaker is Beverly Kuykendall, Owner & Chief Strategy Officer at Kuykendall & Associates.](https://baafgathering26.eventbrite.com  "opens a new window")

[![mastering debt and maximizing your workplace benefits](../img/masteringdebt26.jpg)

### Mastering Debt & Maximizing Your Workplace Benefits

Feb. 24 from 12 pm to 1pm: In our final Financial Literacy session, we’ll break down the fundamentals of debt, repayment strategies, and workplace benefits so you can make confident, informed money decisions. Topics include; good debt vs bad debt, student loans and the Rule of 72.](https://FinanciallyFeb.eventbrite.com )

[![mock interview workshop feb 24 6 pm ursa major ](../img/mock-interview26-thumb.png)

### Mock Interview Workshop

Feb. 24: The College of Business Alumni Chapter will provide mock interviews to students and recent alumni! Practice interviewing and get real-time feedback from professional alumni who conduct hiring in their current positions. Bring along your resume for on-the-spot feedback.](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/1849410870699?aff=oddtdtcreator  "opens a new window")

[![career advancement alumni panel ask me anything](../img/askmeanything26.jpg)

### Career Advancement Alumni Panel- Ask Me Anything

Mar. 12 from 6pm to 7:30pm: Our alumni panelists will share their personal experiences, tips, and tricks to help you navigate the professional world. This is a virtual event via ZOOM, link will be sent to registered guests.](https://askmeanythingcbachapter.eventbrite.com )

[![collins alumni society painting mixer march 12, 2026](../img/cas-paintmix26.jpg)

### Collins Alumni Society Painting Mixer

Mar. 12 from 6pm to 8pm: Exclusive for Collins Alumni Society Members only! Network and paint at Pinot's Palette in Brea, no painting experience needed.](https://casPaintingMix26.eventbrite.com )

[![Latin Alumni Network bowling mixer](../img/lan-bowlingmix26.jpg)

### LAN Bowling Mixer

Mar. 19 from 6pm to 8pm: The Latin Alumni Network is hosting a networking mixer for alumni and guests. All tickets include 2 games of bowling, shoe rental, pizza and soda.](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/latin-alumni-network-bowling-mixer-tickets-1981250883727?aff=oddtdtcreator )

[![cpp night at angel stadium april 21, 2026](../img/angels26-thumb.jpg)

### CPP X Angels Night

4/21: Join fellow Broncos for an evening of baseball and school spirit at the CPP Angels Game. Purchase tickets for your family and friends and visit our pregame Bronco Village beginning at 5:08 p.m. for activities and community connection. MUST purchase at angels.com/cpp to receive our promo package with co-branded jersey.](https://angels.com/cpp%20)
