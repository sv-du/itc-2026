# LinkedIn Badge for Mentors

## Add Bronco Navigators to Your LinkedIn Profile

Mentors who successfully complete their mentor training will be eligible to receive a LinkedIn badge. This badge is a great way to signal key skills to potential employers -- letting them know you have hands-on experience in leadership and more. If you don't already have a LinkedIn account, now might be the perfect time!

## How do I receive my LinkedIn Badge?

Mentors must first complete their mentor training. Upon completion, you will receive a post-training email. Once you receive your email, [go to LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/signup/cold-join?session_redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fwww%2Elinkedin%2Ecom%2Fprofile%2Fadd%3FstartTask%3DCERTIFICATION_NAME&trk=login_reg_redirect) to get started on adding your badge. You will need to enter the following:

* Name - Trained Mentor
* Issuing Organization - Mentor Collective
* Issue Date - Current Month and Year
* Expiration Date - Does not Expire
* Click 'Save'

If you are unable to access the link, you can easily access it by logging into your LinkedIn profile and select 'Add Profile Section' ---> 'Licenses & Certificates' ---> fill out the fields.
