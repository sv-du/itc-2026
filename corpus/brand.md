# Our Brand

## We help anyone who dreams of success achieve it.

As the #1 polytechnic university for diversity and economic mobility, our brand purpose is to help anyone who dreams of success achieve it — from first-generation college students to those who come from a long line of Broncos.

Our *brand* is more than a logo. It’s the collective perception people hold in their minds about Cal Poly Pomona — what we do and stand for, what makes us special and the feelings we evoke.

Our *branding* includes the tangible assets, such as logos, colors and fonts, that visually represent and remind people about our university and brand.

If you represent Cal Poly Pomona as a communicator or in a similar role, use the guidelines and tools on this site to tell powerful stories about our purpose and impact.

## About the Campaign

### The “Become by Doing” Enrollment & Awareness Campaign

Cal Poly Pomona can no longer afford to be a “hidden gem” or “best kept secret.” We have a great reputation for high-quality academics, our learn-by-doing ethos and success in improving our students’ financial futures. But we have low awareness among prospective students and others. That’s why we’re undertaking the multi-year, “Become by Doing” college enrollment and university awareness campaign — to attract students, talent and dollars, and bring more resources to support current students, impact society and reinvest in CPP for future generations.

[Learn More](about-the-campaign.shtml)
