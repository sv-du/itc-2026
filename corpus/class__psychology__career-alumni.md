## Our Featured Alumni

[![Lowell Overton](../img/re-lowell-overton.jpg)

### Lowell Overton

Class of 1997](#modal-0 "Lowell Overton")

### Lowell Overton

×

Mr. Lowell Overton is a CPP alumni who graduated with a BHS major back when Psychology and Sociology were still one department called *Behavioral Sciences*. This is why we have students from both departments (Psychology and Sociology) presenting together at the symposium. Mr. Overton has used the knowledge and skills he developed at CPP to build a successful real estate career. In 2008, he was ranked #1 nationally in sales for Coldwell Banker Affiliates and #10 in the world. He has been an amazing friend and benefactor to the University, the Sociology and Psychology Department, and symposium supporter.

Close

[![Le Ondra Clark Harvey](../img/rele-ondra-clark-harvey.jpg)

### Le Ondra Clark Harvey

Class of 2003](#modal-1 "Le Ondra Clark Harvey")

### Le Ondra Clark Harvey

×

Dr. Clark Harvey holds a B.A. from Cal Poly Pomona and an M.S. and Ph.D. in counseling psychology from the University of Wisconsin–Madison, with clinical training at CHLA and UCLA. She has led major policy and legislative efforts in California, including serving as CEO of the California Behavioral Health Association and Executive Director of the California Access Coalition. She has been appointed to numerous state and national boards, reflecting her deep commitment to mental health advocacy and leadership.

Close

[![Shea Duarte](../img/reshea-duarte.jpg)

### Shea Duarte

Class of 2018](#modal-2 "Shea Duarte")

### Shea Duarte

×

A Cal Poly Pomona alumna and former McNair Scholar, Dr. Shea Duarte went on to earn her Ph.D. in cognitive psychology at UC Davis, using VR to study audiovisual perception and memory. She has conducted UX research at Google and Meta Reality Labs on emerging technologies and is committed to creating inclusive devices that enhance everyday life.

Close

[![Jonathan A. Pedroza](../img/rejonathan-pedroza.png)

### Jonathan Pedroza

Class of 2015](#modal-3 "Jonathan Pedroza")

### Jonathan Pedroza

×

Jonathan A. Pedroza (JP) is a Cal Poly Pomona alumnus and CSU Chancellor’s Doctoral Incentive Program scholar whose work focuses on using data to address health disparities. He holds graduate degrees in Health Psychology and Prevention Science, with expertise in program evaluation, implementation science, and advanced statistical and machine-learning methods. JP now mentors global cohorts in R and Python at Posit Academy and researches socio-ecological and cultural influences on health.

Close

[![Allison Vaughn](../img/reallison-vaughn.jpg)

### Allison Vaughn

Class of 2002](#modal-4 "Allison Vaughn")

### Allison Vaughn

×

Dr. Allison Vaughn, a Cal Poly Pomona Psychology alumna, is a Professor whose research explores social relationships, stigma, and health. She is committed to supporting student development and has played a key role in creating campus programs such as the New Faculty Success Programs, the Ally Teaching program for Young Men of Color (YMOC), and the Introduction to College Teaching graduate course.

Close

[![Ngoc H. Bui](../img/rengoc-bui.jpg)

### Ngoc Bui

Class of 1994](#modal-5 "Ngoc Bui")

### Ngoc Bui

×

Dr. Ngoc H. Bui, a Cal Poly Pomona Psychology alumna, is a tenured full professor at the University of La Verne and currently serves as Associate Dean for Effectiveness, Planning, and Faculty Affairs. She earned her M.A. and Ph.D. in social psychology from the University of Nebraska–Lincoln and previously taught at Metropolitan Community College. Her research spans teaching and learning, social cognition, procrastination, and media psychology, and she has an extensive record of publications, student mentorship, Psi Chi leadership, and university service.

Close

[![Jaydee Tumambing Hughes](../img/rejaydee-tumambing-hughes.jpg)

### Jaydee Tumambing Hughes

Class of 2003](#modal-6 "Jaydee Tumambing Hughes")

### Jaydee Tumambing Hughes

×

Dr. Jaydee Tumambing Hughes, a Cal Poly Pomona M.S. Psychology alumna and Filipina American LMFT, has nearly two decades of experience working primarily with survivors of childhood abuse and complex trauma. She is trained in TF-CBT and psychodynamic approaches and emphasizes culturally responsive care in her practice and supervision. A longtime clinical supervisor, she is passionate about expanding access to quality mental health services with authenticity, compassion, and creativity.

Close
