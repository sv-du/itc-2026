# Poly-Kroma 2014: 2D3D Fine Art Juried Competition

## Poly-Kroma 2014: 2D3D Fine Art Juried Competition

May 19, 2014 to Jun 14, 2014

**Location:** Don B. Huntley University Art Gallery

Press the tab key to view the content. Use the down arrow key to move to the next tab and up arrow key to move to the previous.

[About the Exhibition](#tab-1)
[Program Schedule](#tab-2)
[Installation Views](#tab-3)

***![2d3d A Juried Art Show](img/10369124_632565823515385_925739288413152787_n.png)Poly­‐Kroma***, celebrates the Fine Arts, Art History & Graphic Design on campus with this ***2014 2D/3D Juried Art Exhibition* at The Huntley Gallery**, the ***2014 Fine Art Senior Show***, AND ***Industry Night***‐the senior Graphic Design exhibition, presented concurrently at the **Kellogg University Art Gallery**. *2014 2D/3D Juried Art Exhibition* features the “best of” Cal Poly Pomona’s the Fine Arts and Graphic Design students at all academic levels. This year’s exhibition is juried by staff member and alumnus **Kenny Flores**, the Interactive Content Producer for ASI Gas Creative Group; alumna **Trinkel De La Paz**, a Brand Designer for eBay Enterprise; and staff member **Michele Cairella Fillmore**, the Gallery Director/Curator of the Kellogg and Huntley University Art Galleries. Of **250** submissions reviewed in three rounds of eliminations, the jurors deliberated at length to make the **42** selections of artworks exhibited. These Fine Arts & Graphic Design events celebrate the legacy of the arts on campus in conjunction with the**University’s 75th Anniversary**.
