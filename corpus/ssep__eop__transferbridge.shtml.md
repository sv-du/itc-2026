# Transfer Bridge

## About Transfer Bridge (TB)

Transfer Bridge (TB) is a FREE comprehensive, two-day summer transition program designed to assist Educational Opportunity Program (EOP) **transfer students in transition from****community college to the university** setting by providing a wide range of educational, personal, social, and cultural enrichment opportunities.

We understand our EOP transfer students come to Cal Poly Pomona with unique lived experiences and a wealth of knowledge and skill sets yet transitioning to a new college environment can be intimidating. In just**two days**, students will be provided with campus resources, community connections, and additional support services that will enhance their skills and success as a Cal Poly Pomona Bronco.

**Transfer Bridge Program Dates 2026:**

**Wednesday, July 29, 2026 through Thursday, July 30, 2026 @ Cal Poly Pomona**

*\*\*If selected to participate, all students MUST attend all days of the program, unless approved by program staff. For accommodations, please contact us at transferbridge@cpp.edu.\*\**

## Program Model

Transfer Bridge (TB) is a structured two day virtual program, where students are engaging in many different components to learn more about CPP services, resources, and to connect with peers and staff. See program details below.

* **Program Dates: July 30 - July 31**
* **In-Person Program Schedule:** 9:00 am - 5:00 pm at Cal Poly Pomona
* **Includes:**
  + Connecting with EOP staff and peers
  + Overview of key resource areas such as:
    - Academic Resources
    - Campus Tour
    - Campus Resources
    - Connect with EOP Advisors
    - Networking
    - Campus Speakers and Student Panels

## Learn More

[### Transfer Bridge Benefits](#modal-0 "Why participate in Transfer Bridge?")

### Why participate in Transfer Bridge?

×

**Program Goals:**Transfer Bridge is designed to assist participants in getting a head start in their college journey, build support systems with faculty, staff, and peers, and gain essential skills that will facilitate their transition and success at Cal Poly Pomona.

**Program Cost: FREE** to the student. The program offers a wide range of resource and support services at no cost to the students, as fees are covered by university funding. To fully benefit from TB, we encourage students to actively engage in all that TB has to offer.

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Program Benefits Include:** | |
| **Educational Support:**   * Connecting with your EOP Support team: Advisor, Learning Center, and Community Events * Workshop on Understanding Transfer Credit Report | **Student Development:**   * Student Success Coaching * Personal Development and Wellbeing Workshops |
| **Community and Social Events:**   * Community Building Activities * Student Leadership Opportunities * Building Support Networks * Exposure to Campus Life | **Cultural and Academic Enrichment Opportunities:**   * Workshops with Campus Partners (Financial Aid, Career Center, Basic Needs, Children's Center, and much more!) * Motivational Speakers |

[### Transfer Bridge Eligibility](#modal-1 "How am I eligible for Transfer Bridge?")

### How am I eligible for Transfer Bridge?

×

Eligible incoming EOP transfer students are invited to learn more about Transfer Bridge via email in early June and are provided with additional information regarding our program application process.

In order to be eligible to apply to Transfer Bridge, students must:

1. **Be admitted to both CPP and EOP**
   1. After you have been admitted to both CPP and EOP you will receive an email with more information about Transfer Bridge.
2. **Be available to attend all program dates**
   1. **TB 2026 Program Dates:** July 29th - July 30th from 9am - 5pm at Cal Poly Pomona
      * *Applicants must commit to all program dates and be available to participate in all program components, unless excused by the program staff.*
3. **Have attended CPP orientation session prior to Transfer Bridge program dates**
   1. Review your CPP email for more information about [orientation](https://www.cpp.edu/orientation/transfer/start-here.shtml).

Close

[### Transfer Bridge Application](#modal-2 "How can I apply to Transfer Bridge?")

### How can I apply to Transfer Bridge?

×

To be fully considered, all EOP and Transfer Bridge program deadlines and applications steps must be met.

**APPLICATION TIMELINE**

* Application OPENS: June 9th @ 8:00 am
* Application CLOSES: June 30th @ 12:00pm (noon)

Review your CPP email daily for all EOP and Transfer Bridge notifications. Transfer Bridge invitations will only be sent out to admitted incoming EOP transfer students at Cal Poly Pomona.

* Applicants will be notified of their application status by July 15 or sooner.

Close
