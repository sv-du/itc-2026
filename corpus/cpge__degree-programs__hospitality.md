## Program Overview

The Master of Science in Hospitality Management (MSHM) degree exposes students to advanced management concepts, including accounting and finance, marketing, strategic management, and human resources management, as well as qualitative and quantitative analytical skills as they pertain specifically to the hospitality industry.

![hospitality students walking](../img/hospitality_main.jpg)

## Award

![Best Master's in Hospitality Management Degree Programs of 2025 by Intelligent](../img/rank_intelligent.jpg)

## Program Overview

The program is designed to prepare graduates with the depth of hospitality knowledge and skills that will equip them to make an immediate impact in the highly competitive hospitality industry or to provide the foundation for the pursuit of a terminal degree at an academic institution. The MSHM program is sufficiently flexible to recognize the full range of skills and knowledge needed for significant contributions in all segments of the hospitality industry.

## Ranking

The Collins College of Hospitality Management is ranked No. 10 in the [CEO World Magazine](https://ceoworld.biz/2025/04/06/best-hotel-schools-2025/ "2025 Ranking by CEO World")’s annual list of “Best Hospitality and Hotel Management Schools In The World For 2025.” It is the only program in California to make the Top 10.
