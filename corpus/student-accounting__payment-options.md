# How to Pay

## Overview

Paying for your education should be clear and convenient. Whether you’re paying online, using a college savings plan, or receiving financial aid, this page will guide you through the options available at Cal Poly Pomona.

## How to Pay Your Tuition and Fees

[### Pay Online

Use an eCheck, credit card, or FlyWire International Payment in BroncoDirect.](https://my.cpp.edu/)

[### Payment Plan

Split your tuition into smaller, monthly payments.](https://secure.touchnet.net/C20365_tsa/web/caslogin.jsp)

[### Pay with Financial Aid

Grants, loans, and scholarships may cover all or part of your balance.](https://www.cpp.edu/financial-aid/)

[### Flywire - International

Make an international payments with Flywire](https://my.cpp.edu/)

[### PayMyTution - International

Make an international Payment with PayMyTution](https://payment.paymytuition.com/payment-welcome/CPP "opens a new window")

## Payment Methods Explained

### eCheck (ACH Transfer)\*

* Withdraws directly from checking or savings.
* Most recommended method.
* Do not enter debit or credit card number

\*eCheck (electronic funds transfer from your bank account) / ACH (Automated Clearing House)

### Credit Card

* Accepted online only.
* Credit card payments for tuition and fees include a 2.65% processing fee.
* Credit card payments made through the installment plan include a 2.85% processing fee.

### 529 College Savings Plan

* Pay Online via Bronco Direct (if your 529 plan provider is available).
* If your 529 provider is not available in the payment page drop down, please follow your plan provider instruction on how to request a physical check made payable to cpp).
* If mailing a physical check, please allow at least 10 business days before the due date.

### Cash / Check / Money Order

* In-person or drop-box payments accepted.

**\*\***Include your Bronco ID.

### International Payments

* FlyWire and PayMyTuition allow local currency conversion.
* Pay online via BroncoDirect.

## Waivers & Deferments

### Qualify for Reduced Tuition?

Some students are eligible for tuition waivers or deferred payment options based on military service, employment, or third-party sponsor. The California State University offers eligible employees the opportunity to enroll in the CSU Employee Fee Waiver and Reduction Program. This program provides a waiver or reduction of certain fees for employees and eligible dependents. Employees may enroll in job-related courses offered by the CSU for the purpose of improving skills for existing jobs, or advancement in accordance with a career development plan. Applicants must meet all university admission requirements. Admission to a CSU campus is not guaranteed. Learn more about these options below.
