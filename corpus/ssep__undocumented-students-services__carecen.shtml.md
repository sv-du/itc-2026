# Free Immigration Legal Services

![carecen logo](img/carecen-3720.png)

## CARECEN- Free Immigration Legal Services

**CARECEN Immigration Legal Services**
CARECEN provides free immigration legal services to the Cal Poly Pomona community through the CSU Project, funded by the California Department of Social Services (CDSS). Services are available both in-person and virtually and include:

* General consultations
* DACA renewals (apply at least 180 days before expiration; fee assistance available)
* Family petitions
* Humanitarian relief
* Removal defense
* Special Immigrant Juvenile Status (SIJS)
* Naturalization
* Advance Parole
* Other immigration relief

**Who Qualifies?**

* Currently enrolled CPP students
* CPP faculty and staff
* Immediate family members (parents, siblings, children, spouses)
* Alumni who graduated within the last two years

For additional support, please contact:

* Email: **dream@cpp.edu**
* Phone: **(909) 869-2728**

## Book an Appointment

**Book an Appointment with CARECEN**

To schedule an in-person or virtual appointment with CARECEN’s CSU Project, please use the online booking system. Appointments may fill up to a month in advance, so plan ahead.

[Schedule Appointment](https://carecenla.simplybook.me/v2/)

**Click "Book Now" then select California State Polytechnic University, Pomona**

Please watch this helpful video to assist you further. This link will redirect you to our step-by-step guide on our social media: [CARECEN Guide](https://www.instagram.com/reel/DMf9hcGP39N/?utm_source=ig_web_copy_link&igsh=MzRlODBiNWFlZA==)

If you’re experiencing difficulties scheduling an appointment, Please reach out to us at **dream@cpp.edu**  Or call us directly at **909‑869‑2728**
