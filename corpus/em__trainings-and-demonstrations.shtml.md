# Trainings and Demonstrations

Currently Available
Thank you for interest in a Cal Poly Pomona University Emergency Management Training/Demonstration.

Submission of the request forms does not guarantee your presentation date/time is confirmed. Availability is based on staff availability and scheduling. Please submit 14 days ahead of the date requested.
