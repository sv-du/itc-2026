## Bio

Dr. Mohammad Salehan is an Associate Professor of Computer Information Systems at Cal Poly Pomona. With a strong background in software engineering, including five years of industry experience, Dr. Salehan has evolved his expertise to encompass Machine Learning, Big Data, and Cloud Computing. His particular focus lies in implementing advanced analytics solutions in cloud environments, a proficiency validated by his AWS Certified Machine Learning Specialty. This unique blend of software engineering experience and cutting-edge data science skills positions Dr. Salehan at the forefront of data-driven business solutions. Dr. Salehan's contributions to the field have been recognized in premier journals such as Journal of the Association for Information Systems, European Journal of Information Systems, Decision Support Systems, and Information & Management.
