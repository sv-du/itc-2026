# Programs for International University Students

## These custom programs can be tailored to meet international students’ specific needs. Based on the program lengths, Cal Poly Pomona will provide invitation letter or Form I-20 for the students to travel to the United States on a B1/B2 visitor's visa, or an F1 international student visa. Students will also have the option to take the programs virtually from their home countries.
