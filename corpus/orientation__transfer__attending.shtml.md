# Attending Your Orientation Day

## We're So Excited To Welcome You to CPP!

**If you do not attend Orientation Day, your admission to CPP will be withdrawn. If you can no longer attend your reserved Orientation Day, [contact us](https://www.cpp.edu/orientation/contact-us.shtml) AS SOON AS POSSIBLE. Be sure to include your Bronco ID so we can assist you quickly.**

**What to Expect**

* At orientation, you will have the opportunity to learn about your college, receive academic advising, register for your first semester of CPP classes and meet students, staff and faculty.
* During advising and registration, you will meet in a small group with your advisor and other students in your major and then register for your first semester classes. Before you leave advising and registration, make sure you are registered for 12-15 units, have your Plan complete and know how to contact your advisor!
* You selected your Orientation Day when you make your orientation reservation. As a reminder, you only attend **one** Orientation Day.

**What to Bring**

* Laptop or tablet, with charger, to use during advising and registration (if you have one), **[WITH EDUROAM ALREADY INSTALLED](https://www.cpp.edu/it/wifi/index.shtml)**
* A copy of your unofficial transcripts and/or test scores, in case you need to reference them during advising and registration
* Comfortable shoes
* Reusable water bottle
* Sunscreen and hat
* Backpack, notebook, and pen
* Light sweater or jacket for the morning, evening, and indoors

**Coming to Campus**

## **Parking Registration**

Parking on Orientation Day is included in your Student Orientation Fee and will be in Parking Structure II. You don't need a parking permit, but you must register your car on the form linked in the parking slide of the [Pre-Orientation modules.](https://orientation.cpp.edu)

## **Parking Location**

Parking for orientation attendees is in [Parking Structure 2](https://www.cpp.edu/maps/?id=1130#!m/276389#!m/276389). You MUST park in this location with your Orientation Parking Permit. All vehicles must be registered in order to park, including those with a disabled placard. If you have any concerns about parking, please contact our office directly.
