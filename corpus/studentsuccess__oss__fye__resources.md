### FYE Learning Committee Information

×

The FYE Learning Community brings together faculty and staff interested in supporting first-year students through collaborative professional development. Participants will create or refine FYE course modules aligned with student learning outcomes, share best practices, and receive guidance from the CAFE team.

**Who should apply:** Faculty (tenure-track and adjunct) and staff working with first-year students.
 **Meeting Format:** In person, Thursdays from 11:45 a.m. to 1:00 p.m.
 **Meeting Dates:**

* September: 9 & 26
* October: 9 & 23
* November 6 & 20
* December 4

**Stipend:** $500 for eligible faculty upon completion

**Facilitators:** Dr. Nina Abramzon, Dr. Dora Lee, and Dr. Donya Rahimi

[Apply Now!](https://forms.office.com/pages/responsepage.aspx?id=HqZLFuw5XU-J_6ofAKUhtKHzEZbWxoRClS96C7xkreJUNjRHN1BUVEE3VlFZNVQyQ0RKVVJMV05OSC4u&route=shorturl)
