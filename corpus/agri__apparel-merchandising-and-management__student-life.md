# Student Life

## Get Involved. Get Connected.

![A group of students jumping in the air.](../img/2022-viewbook-920x570.jpg)

A college education should be more than attending classes. Develop leadership skills, team building skills, and make friends amongst your classes. There are two clubs in this major, and multiple honor societies that this major can participate in.

## Clubs & Honor Societies

### AMMA

The Apparel Merchandising & Management Association (AMMA) is the club run by AMM students. The club organizes social and professional activities throughout the academic year, providing networking opportunities for students with alumni and industry professionals. The club meets at least one Thursday a month at noon in Building 45: Room 102. For more information on the club, follow its TikTok (https://www.tiktok.com/@ammacpp) and Instagram (http://www.instagram.com/ammacpp/) accounts. You can also use this link to send them an email.

### AATCC SoCal Broncos

The AATCC So Cal Broncos Club was established in 2015 as a student chapter of the industry professional association AATCC (American Association of Textile Chemists and Colorists). The club works to provide networking opportunities for students with industry professionals, while also providing opportunities to help other Cal Poly Pomona students advance their careers. The club meets every other Thursday at noon, in Building 45: Room 102. Follow the club's Instagram account at https://www.instagram.com/aatcc\_cpp/. Contact the club by clicking on this link to send an email.

### Fashion Society

Fashion Society was founded by a group of AMM students who wanted to create a hub for students of all majors who have a passion for fashion. The club meets on Tuesday at noon in Building 6, Room 111. Follow the Fashion Society on Facebook at https://www.facebook.com/fashionsocietycpp and Instagram at https://www.instagram.com/fashionsocietycpp/. To contact the club by email, click on the card.
