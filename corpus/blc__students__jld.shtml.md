# Job Location and Development

## About JLD

The **Job Location & Development (JLD) Program** at Cal Poly Pomona connects businesses and organizations with motivated students seeking **part-time, off-campus employment** that supports their academic and career goals. JLD provides resources and guidance on **competitive pay, student engagement**, and how to **successfully collaborate with young professionals**.

Through **Project Link**, employers can also team up with **student clubs and organizations** on **short-term projects**, gaining fresh ideas while giving students valuable real-world experience. It’s a meaningful way to support workforce development and engage with the next generation of talent.

## For Students

**Some of the ways JLD helps students:**

* Find flexible, paid job opportunities that fit your schedule
* Develop professional skills and networks to jump start your career
* Gain real-world experience in your field of interest
* Work with local and regional employers invested in student success

**Schedule an appointment to discuss any career services or support you may need.**

## Workshops

**Career Ready Workshop Series**

* Personal Branding: Stand Out in Your Job Search
* Mastering the Interview: Speak with Confidence & Showcase Your Strengths
* Strategic Job Searching: Find the Right Opportunity for You
* Resume Excellence: Craft a Powerful & Strength-Based Resume

[**Please reach out to us if your club or organization is interested in scheduling a workshop.**](https://support.joinhandshake.com/hc/en-us/articles/115011431228-Getting-Started-With-Handshake-Employers)

## ProjectLink

**For Students:**
 Looking to apply your skills outside the classroom? Project Link gives you the chance to work on short-term, meaningful projects with real employers while representing your club or organization. Build your resume, gain hands-on experience, and make valuable professional connections—all while doing what you love with your peers.

**For Employers:**
 Project Link is your opportunity to collaborate with motivated student clubs and organizations on short-term projects that bring fresh ideas and energy to your company. From marketing to research to design, our students are eager to contribute. We'll help guide you through the process and provide support on best practices, including student engagement and fair compensation.

Let’s work together to create experiences that matter—for students and employers alike.
