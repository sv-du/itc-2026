# Eligibility

![Decorative Banner](../../img/banner.png)

#### To be eligible for UB / UBMS the student must:

* be a U.S. citizen or permanent resident

* be a 9th, 10th, or 11th grade student enrolled at one of the following high schools:

-      Chaffey High School (UB Classic only)

-      Chino High School (UB Classic only)

-      Don Lugo High School (UB Classic only)

-      Fremont Academy of Engineering and Design (UB/UBMS)

-      Ganesha High School (UB/UBMS)

-      Garey High School (UB/UBMS)

-      Montclair High School (UB/UBMS)

-      Ontario High School (UB/UBMS)

-      Pomona High School (UB/UBMS)

-      Village Academy High School (UB/UBMS)

In addition, the student must qualify under at least **ONE** of the following:

*\*\*Speak with a staff member first. DO NOT disqualify yourself!!!*

1. meet the family income guidelines established by the federal government
   * BASED ON  TAXABLE INCOME  ONLY
     + 1040,  line15
2. student is a potential first-generation college student
   * neither parent has a four-year college degree **from the U.S.**
3. student is at high risk for academic failure
   * GPA is at 2.5 or below **OR**
   * student did not achieve at the proficient level in the ELA **OR** Math state standards tests (CAASPP) **OR**
   * student has not completed pre-algebra/algebra by the beginning of 10th grade
