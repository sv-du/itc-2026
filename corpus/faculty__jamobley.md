## About Me

Dr. Mobley is proud alumna of Cal Poly Pomona's Animal Science Pre-Vet program and a graduate of Western University of Health Sciences College of Veterinary Medicine. Upon receiving her Doctorate of Veterinary Medicine in 2014, Dr. Mobley completed a small animal rotating internship in internal medicine, surgery, and emergency. She has since worked in small animal general practice and emergency. She enjoys providing high quality general practice care and forming life-long relationships with her clients and patients, while also loving the exciting pace of emergency medicine and it's complex cases. Dr. Mobley's primary focus is on providing excellent compassionate care to both her patients and their owners. Her love for education and a commitment to the future of the veterinary industry was a primary motivator to become faculty in the amazing Animal Health Science department.
