## About me

Dr. Ben Steichen is an Associate Professor in the Computer Science Department at [California State Polytechnic University, Pomona](http://www.cpp.edu/). He received his PhD from [Trinity College Dublin](https://www.tcd.ie/) (TCD), Ireland, an M.Sc. degree in Computer Science from TCD, and a First Class Honours B.Sc. degree in Computing Sciences from the [University of East Anglia](https://www.uea.ac.uk/), UK.

Dr. Steichen leads the [hapii lab](https://hapii-lab.github.io/#https://hapii-lab.github.io) (human-centered, adaptive, and personalized information interaction), which researches novel information access solutions that support each individual person. This research involves techniques and concepts from the fields of Human-Computer Interaction, Information Retrieval & Visualization, Artificial Intelligence, and Web & Information Science.
