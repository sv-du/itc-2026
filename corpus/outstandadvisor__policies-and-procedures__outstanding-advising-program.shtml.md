# Outstanding Advising Program

## About This Award

![Photo of WISE team with Alison Baski](../img/program.png)

## Selection Process

One Outstanding Advising Program will be selected by Provost Gomez and Vice President Gonzales and honored at the reception.

## Nomination Process

Submit your nomination [through our form](https://cpp.az1.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_eg38JXZxMNrRxrw). Nominations for 2025-26 are open until Friday, March 13.

The nomination should include:

1. The name of the program;
2. The name and contact information of a student who is part of the program;
3. A detailed description of the contributions of the program together with supporting documentation; and
4. A description of the process that led to the nomination.

The nomination materials for Outstanding Advising Program, including supporting documentation must not exceed 20 pages. Materials exceeding this limit will not be forwarded for consideration.
