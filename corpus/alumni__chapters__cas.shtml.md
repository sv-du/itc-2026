# Collins Alumni Society

![students gathered around a table listening to an alum share his career trajectory](../img/cas-aboutpic.png)

## Mission Statement

With more than 6,200 alumni around the country and the globe, the chapter is striving to bring Collins alumni back to The Collins College family. As part of this continuous effort to build the network through increasing membership, the chapter is creating a calendar of events throughout the year which will include alumni networking events, social mixers, and special membership discounts.
