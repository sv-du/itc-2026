# Faculty Leadership Development

## Individual Consultations & Mentoring for All Faculty

**“Everybody’s Mentor”** — Confidential, one-on-one support offered by Ferp (Faculty Early Retirement Program) Faculty Fellows — is here to help you navigate your academic journey. Whether you're new to campus or a seasoned faculty member, we’re here to support you with:

* Career Development and Planning
* Grant proposal guidance
* Understanding university systems, processes and policies
* RTP (Reappointment, Tenure, and Promotion)
* Classroom strategies and teaching questions
* Interest in leadership and service positions
* Work-life balance

## [Faculty Learning Community (FLC) on Leadership](faculty-leadership-development/flc.shtml)

The goal of the Academic Leadership FLC is to increase leadership confidence and skill by exploring higher education and CPP, self-awareness, and interpersonal relationships within a supportive cohort.

![](../../img/general/divider.png)

## Department Chair Support

CAFE provides support to department chairs through facilitated peer-support groups, individual consultations, workshops, and other resources, working collaboratively with the University Council of Chairs and the Office of Faculty Affairs.
