# Electroencephalography Biometric For Android OS

## About

Electroencephalography (EEG) data describe the activity of neuron populations in the brain and are acquired via electrodes placed on specific positions of the skull. **EEG signals have been shown to be sufficiently different between individuals and therefore suitable for use in the authentication process.**

The relatively **recent introduction of consumer-grade EEG** acquisition devices has made the utilization of EEG research **more accessible to ubiquitous computing devices** such as smartphones; however, the **software developed in EEG research lacks specific implementations for seamless collection** and signal level analysis of the raw data on the Android platform.

**EEG Workbench (EEGW) aims to provide developers with a tool** to facilitate the development of an EEG-based Recognition System (EEGRS) as a part of computational neuroscience and brain computer interface projects.
