## Fall 2025 Graduate Student Welcome Virtual Orientation

To help newly admitted graduate students get off to a good start, the CPP Graduate Studies Office invites students to our The Fall graduate Student Welcome Orientation to learn about guidelines, resources, and opportunities available to them. Please view the [Fall 2025 Graduate Student Welcome Orientation recording](https://streaming.cpp.edu/media/2025%2BWelcome%2BEvent/1_bfifp7xp).
