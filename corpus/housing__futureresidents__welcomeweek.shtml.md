# Welcome Week

## Launch Into the Year

### Bronco Welcome Experience

We’re excited to have you join our campus community. Bronco Welcome Week is your first step into life at Cal Poly Pomona — a chance to settle in, make connections, and have fun. Check out this year’s events and activities to kick off your college journey the right way!

![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-green-mobile.svg)
![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-green.svg)

## Welcome Week 2025

From Sunday, August 17 to Wednesday, August 20, we're kicking off the year with four days of non-stop energy and community fun with Welcome Week! Get to know your new home, meet fellow Broncos, and dive into campus life. You won’t want to miss a moment!

![Students posing](../documents/img/uhs-events/sl-hiring-resident-advisors.jpg)

## Mandatory Safety Meetings

All new residents are required to attend one **Mandatory Safety Meeting** during Welcome Week. Sessions will be held daily at **6:00 PM from August 17 to August 20** and hosted via Zoom by your Area Coordinator. **You only need to attend one session.**

## Sunday and Monday

### August 17th -August 18th

We’re kicking things off with high-energy icebreakers from Playfair and time to connect in the Bronco Student Center. It’s all about meeting new people, having fun, and starting your campus journey with good vibes.

[Sunday

#### August 17th

Playfair and Late Night at Centerpointe. Click here to learn more!](#modal-0 "Sunday, August 17th Event Schedule")

### Sunday, August 17th Event Schedule

×

**6:00 p.m.**Mandatory Safety Meeting for new residents (hosted by the AC via Zoom). Residents only need to attend one session during Welcome Week.

**6:40 p.m.**Meet your RA outside the Community entrance to walk together to Bronco Commons.

**7:00 p.m. – 9:00 p.m**.
Playfair at Bronco Commons. Connect with fellow Broncos through fun, high-energy icebreakers facilitated by Playfair.

**9:00 p.m. – 11:00 p.m.**Late Night at Centerpointe (meal swipe required). Enjoy late-night snacks and Bingo.

Close

[Monday

#### August 18th

Rose Garden activities, BSC hangout, and Late Night at Centerpointe. Click here to learn more!](#modal-1 "Monday, August 18th Event Schedule")

### Monday, August 18th Event Schedule

×

**3:00 p.m. – 5:00 p.m.**Crafts at the Rose Garden. Relax with snacks, crafts, and lawn games in the scenic Cal Poly Pomona Rose Garden.

**6:00 p.m.**Mandatory Safety Meeting for new residents (hosted by the AC via Zoom). Residents only need to attend one session during Welcome Week.

**6:40 p.m.**Meet your RA outside the Community entrance to walk to the BSC.

**7:00 p.m. – 9:00 p.m.**BSC Night at the Bronco Student Center. Enjoy:

* Photo Booth (open at 7:00 p.m.)
* Magician in Ursa Major (show begins at 7:15 p.m.)
* Trivia in Ursa Minor (starts at 7:30 p.m.)

**9:00 p.m. – 11:00 p.m**.
Late Night at Centerpointe (meal swipe required). Enjoy snacks and Karaoke.

Close

## Tuesday and Wednesday

### August 19th - August 20th

The excitement continues with a lively carnival hosted by our Resident Student Association, filled with games, treats, and great energy. We’ll wrap up Welcome Week with a relaxing movie night — the perfect way to unwind before the first day of classes.

[Tuesday

#### August 19th

Resident Student Association carnival and Late Night at Centerpointe. Click here to learn more!](#modal-2 "Tuesday, August 19th Event Schedule")

### Tuesday, August 19th Event Schedule

×

**3:00 p.m. – 5:00 p.m.**Crafts at Sycamore Grove. Enjoy snacks, crafts, and lawn games in the shade of the Sycamore Grove.

**6:00 p.m.**Mandatory Safety Meeting for new residents (hosted by the AC via Zoom). Residents only need to attend one session during Welcome Week.

**6:40 p.m.**Meet your RA outside the Community entrance to walk to Engineering Lawn.

**7:00 p.m. – 9:00 p.m**.
Resident Student Association (RSA) Bronco Bash at Engineering Lawn. Experience inflatable activities, games, and free food.

**9:00 p.m. – 11:00 p.m.**Late Night at Centerpointe (meal swipe required). Enjoy snacks and a friendly game of Uno.

Close

#### August 20th
