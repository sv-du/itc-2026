# Trainings for Title IX Investigators & Coordinators

## Training Information

The Office of Equity and Compliance's investigation team receives annual Title IX training from the CSU Chancellor's Office. Trainings are available at calstate.edu/titleix.

### 2024-2025

[CSU Nondiscrimination Policy Updates 8/9/2024 (PDF)](../documents/csu-nondiscrimination-policy-updates-8.9.2024.pdf)

[AWI Workplace Investigations Basics-Module 1 (PDF)](../documents/awi-workplace-investigations-basics-module-1-1.28.2025.pdf)

[AWI Workplace Investigations Basics-Module 2 (PDF)](../documents/awi-workplace-investigations-basics-module-2-2.4.2025.pdf)

[AWI Workplace Investigations Basics-Module 3 (PDF)](../documents/awi-workplace-investigations-basics-module-3-2.11.2025.pdf)

### 2023-2024

[T9 Title IX: Gathering Facts on Dating Violence, Partner Violence and Stalking (PDF)](https://www.cpp.edu/officeofequity/documents/manual-sep2023-dating-violence-final.pdf)

[Clery Center's Clery Act Training Seminar: Title IX-Related Content (PDF)](../documents/clerycenters-cleryact-training-seminar.pdf)

[Responding to Charges of Discrimination (PDF)](https://www.cpp.edu/officeofequity/documents/2348_presentationslides_20240104081624.pdf "Responding to Charges of Discrimination")

10 Commandments for Chief Diversity Officers

[T9 Putting Facts into Focus: A Nuts & Bolts Writing Workshop (PDF)](https://www.cpp.edu/officeofequity/documents/report-writing-course-manual-feb2023.pdf "T9 Putting Facts into Focus: A Nuts & Bolts Writing Workshop")

[T9 Part 1: A Close Look at Affirmative Consent (PDF)](https://www.cpp.edu/officeofequity/documents/manual-consent-pt1-may2023.pdf)

[T9 Part 2: A Close Look at Incapacitation (PDF)](https://www.cpp.edu/officeofequity/documents/manual-consent-pt.2-may2023.pdf)
