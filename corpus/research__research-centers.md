## Center Creation and Annual Review Guidelines

The establishment of Centers at the California State Polytechnic University, Pomona, supports the teaching, scholarly and research activity, and service missions of the University. The types of activities conducted by Centers are important to the development of new initiatives, to the partnering of students and faculty, and to the integration of the community into university partnerships.
[Centers Guidelines (Docx)](docs/centers-guidelines-12-13-18.docx "Five-Year Review Template") (Word File)

## Center and Institutes Review Guidelines

Per the agreement in the Centers/Institute document, each center/institute will be evaluated annually and must submit a Five Year Performance Review to be reviewed through Academic Affairs. This submission includes performance evaluation based on the original goals and objectives, and business plan as needed, to identify success of the unit, modification of the mission or business plan, and role in the academic strategic plan of department, college or Academic Affairs. Please complete the following review, attach the signature review sheet, and submit per the directions in the guidelines.
