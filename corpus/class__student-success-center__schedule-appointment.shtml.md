# Schedule Appointment

## Schedule your advising appointment on CPP Connect!

Play the above instructional video and [log on to CPP Connect](https://cpp.campus.eab.com/ "log on to CPP Connect") when you're ready.

**Please note that our advisors schedule appointments only two weeks out from today. If no appointments appear within the next two weeks, they have all been booked by other students.**

Keep checking back every morning as new appointments open up and as cancellations occur. If you encounter other errors, please contact us or the [Bronco Advising Center](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/bronco-advising-center/index.shtml "Bronco Advising Center") for assistance.

Thank you for your patience!
