# Industry Advisory Board

![Aerospace Engineering Industry Action Council](/engineering/aro/img/banner015.jpeg)

### Aerospace Engineering Industry Advisory Board/Council (IAC)

Welcome to Cal Poly Pomona's Aerospace Engineering Department, strategically located in Southern California, a region renowned for its robust aerospace and defense industry, along with related government agencies. We take immense pride in the invaluable support provided by our Industry Advisory Board (IAC), comprising experienced managers and engineers from prominent aerospace companies and government agencies.

Twice a year, our dedicated IAC members convene with our faculty to engage in dynamic discussions encompassing a diverse range of crucial topics. These encompass curriculum development, state-of-the-art laboratory advancements, faculty recruitment, annual design reviews, research initiatives, proposals and grants, noteworthy publications, participation in national competitions, accreditation standards, internship opportunities, and job placements, among other key areas.

We invite those with a passion for aerospace engineering and a desire to contribute to our community to consider joining our Aerospace Engineering IAC. To express your interest or inquire further, please reach out to us at aro@cpp.edu.

By collaborating closely with industry leaders and experts, we remain at the forefront of advancements in aerospace engineering education, ensuring our students receive a cutting-edge learning experience that prepares them for thriving careers in this ever-evolving field. We look forward to welcoming you to our vibrant aerospace community at Cal Poly Pomona!
