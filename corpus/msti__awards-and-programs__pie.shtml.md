# Partners in Education (PIE)

![Partners in Education 2019 group](../img/pie-scholarship-conf-group-20.jpg)

Cal Poly Pomona has a unique scholarship provided by the Cal Poly Pomona Partners in Education (PIE) Board. The organization raises money to support teacher candidates while completing their Clinical Practice. The PIE Scholarships are up to $4,000 per year and 6 to 15 scholarships are available each year. The number of scholarships awarded is based on the amount of money PIE raises for this purpose.

The PIE Scholarship application process generally takes place in late fall. Be sure to check the college web site and your email, as the announcement will come to all who are fully admitted to the credential programs.
