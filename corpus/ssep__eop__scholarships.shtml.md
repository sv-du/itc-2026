# EOP Grants and Scholarships

## EOP Grant

AMOUNT: EOP students who qualify for financial aid may receive an EOP grants average $600 per year. Priority is given to our incoming first year and transfer students and then awarded to eligible continuing students.

### Criteria/Eligibility

* Student Aid Index (SAI) is between $0 - $1,500
* Student must be enrolled in at least 12 units each term.
* To be considered for the grant, EOP students must apply for financial aid by filing the *[Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA)](https://studentaid.gov/ "Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA)")*  or the *[California Dream Act application](https://dream.csac.ca.gov/)*.
* EOP grants will only be awarded as long as funds are available.
* The FAFSA or Cal Dream Act application is a separate process from the EOP admission process.
* For more information on applying for FAFSA or the Cal Dream Act application, please contact Cal Poly Pomona’s [Office of Financial Aid & Scholarships](http://www.cpp.edu/~financial-aid/index.shtml "Office of Financial Aid & Scholarships").
* No additional application is needed for consideration.

## EOP Scholarships

2026-2027 EOP Scholarships are now available and can only be accessed via private hyperlinks sent to current EOP student's CPP emails.

### EOP Eric Lara Family Foundation Scholarship

**RECIPIENT:** 1
**AMOUNT:**$1,500
**DEADLINE:**March 2, 2026
**CRITERIA/ELIGIBILITY:**

* Must be a current undergraduate EOP student
* Must be currently enrolled full-time (12 or more units at time of application and award disbursement) and must maintain full-time enrollment and be in good academic standing for the awarding fall semester and spring semester
* Minimum 2.0 CPP or cumulative GPA
* Open to all undergraduate majors
* Special consideration will be given to those students coming from donor’s hometown in the La Puente area, but all EOP students are encouraged to apply
* Must submit a 250-500 word supplemental personal statement/essay

***Note: Link to access scholarship application is sent to student's CPP email address***
