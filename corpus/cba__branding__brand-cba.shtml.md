# CBA + SGSB Brand

## CBA and SGSB Brand Guidelines

The Cal Poly Pomona brand encompasses the work of many different people. When we use the same elements, and speak with a shared voice, we can tell a more powerful story.

Brand Guidelines are designed to help you tell this story more effectively — and harness the power of the CPP brand in your work. The following tools are for CBA and SGSB. To learn more about the Cal Poly Pomona “Become by Doing” campaign, visit [about the campaign.](https://www.cpp.edu/brand/about-the-campaign.shtml "https://www.cpp.edu/brand/about-the-campaign.shtml")

Share your news and story ideas with us by clicking [here!](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1Gg2x1hUypBjGScrLkkXD-2kmh16btY_8-vRu_7bUauI/edit)

For any questions about CBA or SGSB, please email CBA communications at cbacomms@cpp.edu
