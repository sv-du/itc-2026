# Contact Us

## Office Location and Hours

**Black Resource Center**

Building 95, Room L110
3801 West Temple Avenue
Pomona, CA 91768

The BRC is located in Building 95, Room L110 across from the Care Center and the Campus Center Marketplace. Building 95 is referred to as the Cultural Centers.

Monday - Friday
8 am - 5 pm
