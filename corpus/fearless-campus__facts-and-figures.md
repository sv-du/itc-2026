# Fearless Facts

![](../img/logo/fcdataproven.png)

* 54,682 students enrolled in courses where the professor attended a Fearless Classroom training since January 2020
* 16,801 students in enrolled in courses identified as Fearless Classrooms by the professor since January 2020
* 11,518 students participating in the end of semester exit surveys since May 2020
* 20 Fearless Classroom workshops since November 2019
* 302 faculty attended Fearless Classroom workshops since November 2019
* 2,588 Fearless Classrooms created since January 2020
* 292 faculty implemented Fearless Classroom concepts in their courses since January 2020
* 8 colleges and the university library represented in the Fearless Classroom project since January 2020
* 43 college departments represented in the Fearless Classroom project since January 2020

As of 2024, 92% of students agreed their instructor created a comfortable (fearless) learning environment that supported their ability to succeed in the course (n=13,361).

## Student Exit Survey May 2020 - December 2024 (N= 13,361; 51% response rate)

## **94%** Trust

94% of students agreed they trusted the instructor to provide the knowledge, skills, and abilities to succeed in the course

## **93%** Respect

93% of students agreed they felt respected in their class

## **92%** Empathy

92% of students agreed the instructor displayed empathy being patient with their ability to learn the course material

## **88%** Compassion

88% of students agreed the instructor helped them learn from their mistakes

## **95%** Equity

95% of students agreed they were treated equally in their class

## **91%** Inclusion

91% of students agreed they felt included in their class

## **85%** Engagement

85% of students agreed they felt comfortable participating in the course discussions

## **92%** Vulnerable

92% of students agreed they felt comfortable asking the instructor for help
