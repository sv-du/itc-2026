# CSU Fully Online/CourseMatch

## (Cross-Campus Enrollment)

![CSU Fully Online banner](../img/csu-fully-online-banner.png)

## Review the Registration Window

The Full-time students enrolled at Cal Poly Pomona have access to fully articulated online courses at other semester CSU campuses through [CSU Fully Online website](https://www.csufullyonline.com/).The Courses designated as ***CourseMatch*** has undergone an extensive review process through the CSU Office of the Chancellor. These courses have been deemed eligible based on the curriculum and student success rates. In addition, these courses carry a small number of guaranteed seats for visiting students. This registration access is offered without students formally admitted to the CSU host campus and without paying additional fees. Students interested in participating should consult with their academic advisor to determine how such a course may apply to their degree program before enrolling at another CSU campus. Credit earned at the CSU campus offering the online course is automatically reported to your home campus and included on your transcript.

## Enrollment Process, Eligibility Requirements, FAQ, Welcome Letter

### Enrollment Process

The **CourseMatch** enrollment process is **fully automated**, allowing Cal Poly Pomona students to  register for **CSU Fully Online** courses offered across the CSU system.

**How to Enroll**

Students may enroll in CSU Fully Online courses through their **home campus student self-service portal**.

* **Cal Poly Pomona students:**
   Log in to **BroncoDirect** → **Student Center** → click **“Enroll in CourseMatch.”**
* A comprehensive list of CSU Fully Online courses is available on the [CSU Fully Online](https://www.csufullyonline.com/ "CSU Fully Online") website.

**After You Enroll**

Once you select a course and your home campus approves the request:

* A **course placeholder** will appear on your class schedule:
  + **CM2990** for **Undergraduate** students
  + **CM4990** for **Graduate** students
* The placeholder confirms that your enrollment request has been approved at CPP and is going through approval process at the host campus.

**Host Campus Review**

The **host campus** (the CSU campus offering the course) will then review your enrollment request through its process.
 Once approved:

* You’ll receive a **welcome email from the host campus** with instructions on how to **set up your student account** and access your course materials.

**Advising Reminder**

Before enrolling, please consult with your **academic advisor** to ensure that the selected course meets your **General Education (GE)**, **major**, or **graduation requirements** at your **home campus**.

### Elgibility Requirements

1. Student has completed at least one semester at Cal Poly Pomona as a matriculated student and earned at least 12 semester units.
2. Student has attained a GPA of 2.0 in all work completed at Cal Poly Pomona and is in good standing.
3. Student is enrolled at Cal Poly Pomona during the period of concurrent enrollment and has paid tuition fees as a full-time student.
4. Enrollment is limited to another semester CSU campus.  (This excludes Cal Poly San Luis Obispo.)

The labeling of General Education areas in [CSU Fully Online](https://www.csufullyonline.com/ "CSU Fully Online") follows the General Education structure for semesters.  If you are on a quarter curriculum, refer to the chart below for the changes to General Education.  Consult with your academic advisor to ensure that the course you want to take satisfies your degree requirements.

### FAQ and Welcome Letter

Questions about CSU Fully/Online and/or CourseMatch?  Please view the [CSU Fully Online frequently asked questions](https://www.calstate.edu/attend/student-services/Pages/CSU-Fully-Online-FAQs.aspx "CSU Fully Online frequently asked questions"), or you can email the Registrar's Office at registrar@cpp.edu and include ***Fully Online/CourseMatch*** in the subject line.  Be sure to specify whether you are a Cal Poly Pomona student, or a student transferring from another semester CSU campus.  More details at [CSU Fully Online](https://www.csufullyonline.com/ "CSU Fully Online").
