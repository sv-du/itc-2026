# Bronco Scholars Program

![Group of 4 Bronco Scholars students pose wearing Bronco Scholars t-shirts](img/bspgroup1.png)

## About Our Program

In the Bronco Scholars Program at Cal Poly Pomona, first-time freshmen receive comprehensive academic support throughout the first year to ensure success in completing required math and English coursework. As a Bronco Scholar, you'll have exclusive access to summer opportunities that give you head start on earning college credit, begin your General Education math requirements, learn about campus resources, and connect with faculty, staff, and other students.

Bronco Scholars is open to Fall 2025 first-time freshmen, with eligibility based on math and/or English placement. Please monitor your email for an invitation to our optional Summer 2025 program and details about fall academic support. If you do not get an invitation but think you are eligible, email us at bsp@cpp.edu.
