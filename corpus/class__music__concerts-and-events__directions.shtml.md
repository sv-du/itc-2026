## Start Here

### The CPP Recital Hall is located in Building 24

Please keep in mind that **it takes about 10-12 mins to walk to the Cal Poly Pomona Recital Hall** from the main parking structures, so plan extra time to make the concert on time.

## Parking On Campus

Parking permits are required to avoid getting a parking ticket!

Anyone parking on the Cal Poly Pomona campus must obtain and display a permit. They can be purchased from the Parking Information Booth located on Oak Lane next to Building 109 or from one of the many [self-serve booths](https://www.cpp.edu/parking/permits/pay-stations.shtml) located throughout campus.

Limited short-term parking spaces are available. Daily visitor parking permits are $8 Monday through Friday and $5 each day on weekends.

Visitors may park in any student parking lot, with the exception of those lots reserved for on-campus housing students (lots F-1, P, and parts of B). View the [campus map](http://www.cpp.edu/maps/) to learn more.

[More Information on Purchasing a Parking Permit](https://www.cpp.edu/parking/index.shtml)

## Accessible Parking

**Accessible parking is available by the Music Building on both sides of Camphor Lane.**

From Temple Avenue turn North onto University Drive. Turn right on Camphor Lane, proceed until you reach a gate. Parking is on left and right. The Music Building and Recital Hall are to your right.

[Accessible Parking by the Music Building Map](https://www.cpp.edu/maps?id=1130#!m/282699?share)
