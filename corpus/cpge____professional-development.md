# Professional Development Programs

![women in a meeting](img/design-thinking.jpg)

## Professional Development Courses

At the College of Professional and Global Education (CPGE), we provide accessible, career-focused learning opportunities for adult learners through professional development certificates and workforce-aligned training that support lifelong learning and career growth.

## How Our Programs Are Organized

CPGE offers multiple ways to learn, depending on your goals, schedule, and experience level. Whether you are seeking a certificate, exploring a new skill, or learning online at your own pace, our programs are designed to support your next step.

## Become by Doing

### Industry and Technical Certificates

Hands-on programs supporting technical, engineering, construction, and manufacturing careers.

[![Instructor pouring beer](img/brewery.jpg)

### Assistant Brewer Training

Build hands-on brewing skills and prepare for entry-level roles in commercial breweries through applied training and industry-standard practices.](brewery/index.shtml "opens a new window")

[![laptop on architectural diagram](img/autocad-banner.jpg)

### Auto CAD

Learn industry-standard AutoCAD tools and drafting techniques used in construction, engineering, and design environments.](construction/autocad-certificate.shtml "opens a new window")

[![construction worker](img/construction-management.jpg)

### Construction Management Certificate

Develop practical skills in construction planning, coordination, and project oversight for today’s built environment.](https://www.cpp.edu/cpge/professional-development/construction/index.shtml%20 "opens a new window")

[![deciphering plans and specifications](img/dcp-banner.jpg)

### Deciphering Construction Plans and Specifications

Strengthen your ability to read and interpret construction drawings, specifications, and technical documents with confidence.](https://www.cpp.edu/cpge/professional-development/construction/deciphering-construction-plans-specifications.shtml%20 "opens a new window")

[![workers in safety equipment looking at laptop](img/upc.jpg)

### Electric Utility Planner

Gain essential planning and analysis skills for utility infrastructure, permitting, and public agency coordination.](construction/utility-planner-certificate.shtml "opens a new window")

[![worker at a plastic manufacturer](img/plastics-engineering.jpg)

### Plastics Engineering Technology

Explore materials, processing, design, and tooling fundamentals used in plastics manufacturing and product development.](https://www.cpp.edu/cpge/professional-development/engineering-manufacturing/index.shtml%20 "opens a new window")

[![Hardhat on table outdoors](img/pole-loading.jpg)

### Pole Loading Certificate

Learn to analyze utility poles using SPIDA®calc software and industry-approved pole loading methodologies.](https://www.cpp.edu/cpge/professional-development/construction/spida-calc-pole-loading-certificate.shtml%20 "opens a new window")

[![silicone class](img/silicone.jpg)

### Silicone Elastomers Technology and Fabrication

Develop hands-on knowledge of silicone elastomer materials, processing techniques, and fabrication applications.](https://www.cpp.edu/cpge/professional-development/engineering-manufacturing/silicone.shtml%20 "opens a new window")

### Public Sector and Government Programs

Certificates designed for city, county, and public agency professionals.

[![LGL course with instructor](img/lgl.jpg)

### Local Government Leadership

Prepare for leadership roles in city and public agency settings through focused training in communication, strategy, and collaboration.](local-government/local-government-leadership-academy.shtml "opens a new window")

[![local government supervisory class](img/lgs.jpg)

### Local Government Supervisory

Build effective supervisory skills to lead teams, manage performance, and navigate workplace challenges in public sector environments.](https://www.cpp.edu/cpge/professional-development/local-government/index.shtml%20 "opens a new window")

[![image of woman smiling](img/human_resources_tile.jpg)

### Human Resources Management in the Public Sector Certificate Program

Learn public sector HR practices, policies, and workforce strategies and more.](public-sector-human-resources-certificate.shtml "opens a new window")

### Education and Professional Credentials

Programs designed for educators, administrators, and working professionals focused on leadership development, project delivery, and career advancement.

### Project Management

Prepare for the CAPM certification while building foundational project management knowledge and career-ready skills.](capm-certification-prep.shtml "opens a new window")

[![Teacher in a classroom with children](img/tpa-cpp-cpge.jpg)

### Teaching Performance Assessment (TPA)

Receive guided support and preparation to complete California’s Teaching Performance Assessment requirements.](teaching-performance-assessment.shtml "opens a new window")

## Learn in Partnership

### Online and Self-Paced Courses

Cal Poly Pomona offers short, flexible online learning opportunities through CPGE’s trusted partners for individuals who want to build job skills, explore new career paths, or expand their knowledge at their own pace.

[![IT Professional looking at screen](img/it-bootcamp.jpg)

### Online IT Boot Camp Training Courses

Cal Poly Pomona IT boot camps will prepare you for in-demand, high-paying jobs in the fast-moving technology space. Gain the skills employers are looking for in an accelerated, 100% online format.](online-learning/it-bootcamp/index.shtml "opens a new window")

[![A lady looking at her laptop for more info](img/more-courses.jpg)

### Career Training Courses by ed2go

Explore Career Training Courses by ed2go, offered through CPGE’s online learning partners. These short, flexible programs are designed to help you build practical job skills, prepare for career changes, or strengthen your professional knowledge at your own pace.](online-learning/career-training/index.shtml "opens a new window")

[![A lady looking at her laptop](img/fundamental-classes.jpg)

### Fundamental Courses with ed2go

Explore short, self-paced online courses offered through CPGE’s partner, ed2go. These introductory courses support skill building, personal enrichment, and career exploration across a wide range of topics. You will be redirected to an external site.](https://www.ed2go.com/cpge/%20 "opens a new window")

## Disclaimer

Online learning programs are offered through the College of Professional and Global Education (CPGE) at Cal Poly Pomona in partnership with approved third-party providers, including [**ed2go**](https://www.ed2go.com/cpge/). These programs are sponsored by [Cal Poly Pomona Enterprises](https://cppenterprises.org/).

These programs are NOT taught by California State Polytechnic University, Pomona (CPP) or CPGE faculty. Courses are non-credit, do not apply toward a degree, and are not eligible for academic credit.

Course schedules, formats, meeting times, and availability are subject to change without notice. The University reserves the right to cancel a course due to insufficient enrollment or other operational considerations.

Courses are self-paced and designed for working professionals seeking flexible learning options. Content focuses on practical skills and knowledge that can be applied in professional settings. Registration is completed through the external course provider.
