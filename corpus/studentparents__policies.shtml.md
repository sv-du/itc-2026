# Student Parents Have Rights Under Title IX.

## Learn More

[### Assembly Bill 2881

AB 2881 aims to help student parents attending public post-secondary institutions. It was signed into law by Governor Gavin Newsom in Sept. 2022. Click card to read the bill.](https://leginfo.legislature.ca.gov/faces/billPdf.xhtml)

[### Know Your Rights - Title IX

Title IX is a federal law that prohibits discrimination on the basis of sex, including pregnancy and parental status, in educational programs and activities. Learn more about your rights under Title IX by clicking this card.](https://www.ed.gov/laws-and-policy/civil-rights-laws/sex-discrimination/know-your-rights-pregnant-or-parenting-title-ix-protects-you-from-discrimination-at-school)

[### Institutional Equity and Compliance

The Office of Institutional Equity and Compliance at Cal Poly Pomona helps ensure students who are pregnant, parenting or nursing are free from discrimination on campus. They also maintain a number of resources for student parents. Click to visit their office website.](https://www.cpp.edu/officeofequity/resources/pregnancy-nursing-parenting.shtml)
