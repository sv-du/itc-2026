## Bio

Dr. Hui Shi is an Associate Professor of the Computer Information Systems Department at Cal Poly Pomona. She received a B.S. in Computer Science and Technology from Hefei University of Technology, China, a M.S. in Computer Application Technique from Hefei University of Technology, China, and a Ph.D. in Computer Science from Old Dominion University. Her research interests include Data Mining, Machine Learning, Social Media Analysis, Information Visualization, Semantic Web and Computer Supported Cooperative Work.

## Awards

* 2018-2021: Co-PI, NSF-MRI: Acquisition of a Mass Storage System for Data Intelligence Research
* 2017-2018: Co-PI, Internal Pre-proposal of NSF Major Research Instrumentation program
* 2017-2018: PI, 2017 RSCA faculty Mini-Grant
* 2016: PI, 2016 Provost Office Summer Pilot Grant
* 2015: Co-PI, 2015 Faculty Research Creative Work Grant
* 2015: PI, 2015 Summer Research Award for Junior Faculty
* 2014 -2016: Co-PI, National Science Foundation for Young Scholars (China): Study on smooth transformation control of heterogeneous crowd formation: a geometry-constrained perspective.
