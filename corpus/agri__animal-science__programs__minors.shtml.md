# Minors

## Become by Specializing

![A female student works with a dog manikin](../img/2017-animal-handling-920x570.jpg)

Strengthen your career prospects by specializing in one of our minors.

The **animal and veterinary science minor** is designed for those in other majors who are considering working with animals and want to add versatility to their skills.

With **equine studies**, you can concentrate your education on working with horses, learning more about their physiology and care. You’ll work with the animals at the university’s W.K. Kellogg Arabian Horse Center.

Find out more about the courses related to these minors in the University Catalog:

[Animal & Veterinary Science](https://catalog.cpp.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=69&poid=19571&returnto=5890)

[Equine Studies](https://catalog.cpp.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=69&poid=19763&returnto=5890)

Make sure that you select the most recent edition of the catalog in the upper right corner (see the circled menu in the picture below).

![A screengrab of the University Catalog page on the animal and veterinary science minor](../img/2025-avs-minors-920x388.jpg)
