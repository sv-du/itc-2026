# Curriculum 2025 - 2026

## Business Core Courses

Students are required to take the 14 core courses in several business disciplines ([See College of Business Administration 2025-2026 Course Catalog](https://catalog.cpp.edu/index.php) for more information). This foundation gives students rudimentary knowledge of accounting, finance, human resources, marketing, operations management, and international business. A 2.0 cumulative GPA is required in core courses including sub-plan courses for the major in order to receive a degree in the major.

## CIS Career Tracks

The CIS Department has established three career tracks in the major. Every CIS major student selects one of these tracks. To graduate with a major in CIS, students complete the track recommended courses and proceed to complete some track elective courses. **Students can take classes from different tracks.** Consultation on track selection is available through the track coordinators or [the department chair](https://www.cpp.edu/cba/computer-information-systems/faculty/office-hours.shtml).

* Applications Development Track - (coordinator: [Dr. Hui Shi](https://www.cpp.edu/cba/computer-information-systems/faculty/office-hours.shtml))
* Business Intelligence Track - (coordinator: [Dr. Mehrdad Koohikamali](https://www.cpp.edu/cba/computer-information-systems/faculty/office-hours.shtml))
* Information Security and Forensics Track - (coordinator: [Dr. Indira Guzman](https://www.cpp.edu/cba/computer-information-systems/faculty/office-hours.shtml))

[See Faculty Office Hours](../../faculty/faculty-staff-bios.shtml)
