# Science, Technology, and Society Minor

## A Broader Perspective for Scientists and Technologists

![Globe banner for STS](img/sts-global-banner-green-500x233.jpg)

The STS Minor provides students who are intending to be scientists or technologists (such as software engineers, medical technologists, and engineers) with a sense of how science and technology exists in a broader human context.

The STS Minor's interdisciplinary approach to science and technology provides background enabling scientists and technologists better understand the goals and methods of co-workers such as accountants, human resource professionals, compliance officers, and lawyers, and to make better use of 'soft skills' like communication, adaptability, and non-technical problem solving in the workplace.

[More description of the STS Minor and the STS Minor curriculum sheet.](https://catalog.cpp.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=69&poid=19565&returnto=5895)
