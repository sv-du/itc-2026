Professor

Finance, Real Estate, and Law, College of Business Administration

## Education

* B.A. Finance, Minors: Economics and Sociology: Mississippi State University
* M.B.A. Finance: Western Illinois University

## Courses Taught

Undergraduate and graduate courses in Managerial Finance, Investment Analysis, Evaluation of Financial Policy, Commercial Banking and Corporate Finance Theory.

## Most recent publications

* "The Effect of Federal Reserve Interest Rate Policies on the Returns of Commercial Banks Stocks," Journal of Financial Services Marketing, May 2007, pp. 349-359.

* "Effect of the Federal Reserve Interest Rate Change on the Returns of Commercial Banks Stocks," Proceedings of the Western Decision Sciences Annual Meeting, March 2006.

* "Trade Decimalization: A Comprehensive Review," Proceedings of the Hawaii International Conference on Business, June 2004.

* "A Short-run Analysis of Barron's Expert Positive Recommendations," Proceedings of the 33rd Annual Meeting of the Decision Sciences Institute, November 2002, pp. 809-14.

* "The Fulbright Program: An Experience in Portugal," Proceedings of the 9th International Business Association, May 2000
