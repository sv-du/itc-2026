# Status Reports

Beginning in Fall 1996, a short report on the status of the graduate program has been issued annually. These reports provide data on enrollment and admissions, as well as provide information and/or discussion on other topics relevant to the program.

Please be advised that some tables of information are not available on the web version of the status reports. Some tables contain information considered private. If you wish to view the tables, contact the [Graduate Coordinator](https://www.cpp.edu/sci/biological-sciences/graduate-studies/contact.shtml) to discuss the matter. Assessment data are available. The figure showing long-term enrollment and graduation data is available. Other figures or tables may be made available, as appropriate.
