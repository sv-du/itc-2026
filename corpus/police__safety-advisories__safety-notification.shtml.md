# Safety Notification

Date: September 27, 2022
To: Active Students, Faculty, Staff, Auxiliary Staff
Subject: Safety Notification

**THIS BULLETIN IS POSTED TO ADVISE YOU OF AN INCIDENT THAT CONCERNS THE SAFETY OF THE UNIVERSITY COMMUNITY.**

On Friday, September 23, 2022, at approximately 1:00 a.m., a male student exited his car in Parking Lot B when two males approached him, one exhibiting a knife. After taking his wallet, keys, and cell phone, the suspects entered the victim’s vehicle and immediately drove off campus and away from the area. Although this appears to be an isolated incident, we strongly encourage increased vigilance and proactive safety measures.

**DESCRIPTION:**
Suspect 1:  Male, White, 45 – 50 years old, 5’10”, average build, wearing a grey shirt, black shorts, shoes, and baseball cap.
Suspect 2:  Male, Hispanic, approximately 35 years old, 5’10”, average build, wearing a dark blue hoodie, and dark colored pants.

If you have any information that might be helpful in this investigation, please contact:
Cal Poly Pomona University Police Department (UPD)
Telephone: (909) 869-3070
In Person: Building 109 – Citrus Ln. & Magnolia Ln. (adjacent to Parking Structure 1)

You may also report anonymously by calling the UPD Anonymous Tips line at (909) 869-3399 or Crime Stoppers Los Angeles Regional at (888) 222-TIPS.

**SAFETY TIPS:** Be aware of your surroundings at all times. Park in well-lit areas whenever possible. Avoid walking alone late at night.  If you see suspicious activity or a situation where you or another person may be in danger, contact law enforcement immediately by dialing 911 or (909) 869-3070 (if using a cell phone on campus) to reach a UPD Dispatcher 24/7.  Campus Code Blue emergency poles/phones are located throughout the campus and parking lots and may also be used to contact UPD.  You may also request safety escorts at any time, day or night, by calling (909) 869-3070.

**SUPPORTIVE SERVICES:**  We are here to assist and support you. Victims of crime are strongly encouraged to report the incident to UPD, who will provide information on rights, options and supportive services. Students are also strongly encouraged to seek support from Cal Poly Pomona’s Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS is a confidential resource) or from the Care Center.

**Contacting CAPS:**
Counselors are available 24/7 at (909) 869-3220. If you are calling between 5:00pm and 8:00am, dial 2 when prompted to speak with a live crisis counselor.
In person: Building 66, Rm. 116 (Bookstore Building) from 8:00am-4:30pm, Mon-Fri.
Online at cpp.edu/caps

**Contacting the Care Center:**
(909) 869-5071 from 8:00am—5:00pm, Mon-Fri.
In person: Building 97 (1st floor of Career Center) from 8:00am-5:00pm, Mon-Fri.
Online at cpp.edu/broncoscarecenter
Faculty and staff may contact CPPLife Matters by Empathia at 1 (800) 367-7474.

Off campus, the L.A. County District Attorney’s Office Victim Services may be reached at (800) 380-3811.
