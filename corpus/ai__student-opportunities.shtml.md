# Student Opportunities

## Explore. Learn. Create.

### Empowering Students through Innovation

Incoming and current Cal Poly Pomona students can engage in a variety of academic opportunities to develop and gain different technical and soft skills through research or campus programs.

![CPP Students smiling at camera.](img/opportunity-pages/23cars3.jpg)

## Hands-On Learning & Research

### Gain real-world experience beyond the classroom

Take your education to the next level with hands-on learning, research projects, and innovation challenges. Whether you're working on faculty-led research, developing creative projects, or tackling industry problems, we provide opportunities to apply your knowledge in meaningful ways.

Previous
Next

[Card 1 of 6

![Bronco Advisor meeting a student.](img/homepage/student-opportunities/student-opportunites-carousel-imgs/24bac-advising5.jpg)

### Become a Project Learning Assistant

Support hands-on learning and mentor fellow students. Gain valuable experience while serving as a student mentor and assist faculty in project-based learning in the First Year Experience (FYE) courses.](https://www.cpp.edu/cpp-invests/index.shtml)

[Card 2 of 6

![CPP student presents research poster to President Coley.](img/homepage/student-opportunities/student-opportunites-carousel-imgs/2.jpg)

### Explore Polytechnic Experiences (PolyX)

Take learning beyond the classroom with hands-on, interdisciplinary projects that tackle real-world challenges. Work with peers, apply your skills, and make an impact through immersive experiences.](https://www.cpp.edu/polyx/students.shtml)

[Card 3 of 6

![CPP students sitting in a lecture hall.](img/homepage/student-opportunities/student-opportunites-carousel-imgs/4.jpg)

### Discover Pop-Up Courses

Explore short-term courses designed to introduce emerging topics and skills. These fast-paced, engaging experiences help you stay ahead in a rapidly changing world.](https://www.cpp.edu/ii/pop-up-course/about-course.shtml)

[Card 4 of 6

![CPP Student participates in a food drive.](img/homepage/student-opportunities/student-opportunites-carousel-imgs/6.jpg)

### Make a Difference Through Service Learning

Turn knowledge into action by applying what you learn in the classroom to real-world challenges. Service learning connects you with communities, broadens your perspective, and builds meaningful skills.](https://www.cpp.edu/cce/students/service-learning.shtml)

[Card 5 of 6

![Student reading pages of blueprints.](img/homepage/student-opportunities/student-opportunites-carousel-imgs/7.jpg)

### Combine Work and Learning with Cooperative Education

Get the best of both worlds—earn hands-on experience while working toward your degree. Cooperative education blends academic learning with practical work opportunities, setting you up for success.](https://www.cpp.edu/cce/students/co-op-education/index.shtml)

[Card 6 of 6

![Undergraduate research student explains research poster to a person visiting their table.](img/homepage/student-opportunities/student-opportunites-carousel-imgs/15.jpg)

### Participate in Undergraduate Research Opportunities

Work alongside faculty and peers on research that enhances your academic and professional journey. Gain hands-on experience, develop critical skills, and open doors to new opportunities.](https://www.cpp.edu/our-cpp/students/getting-started.shtml)

## Workshops & Professional Development

### Build Skills for Career Success

Sharpen your skills with workshops, mentorship programs, and leadership development opportunities. Learn from industry experts, faculty, and peers as you prepare for the next step in your academic or professional journey.

Previous
Next

[Card 1 of 6

![Student volunteers to help paint furniture.](img/homepage/student-opportunities/student-opportunites-carousel-imgs/8.jpg)

### Volunteer at Bronco Stampede of Service

Be part of something bigger and join the CPP community in service events that support local organizations and create lasting change.](https://www.cpp.edu/cce/learn-by-doing-good/bronco-stampede-service.shtml)

[Card 2 of 6

![Student shakes hand with recruiter at career fair.](img/homepage/student-opportunities/student-opportunites-carousel-imgs/3.jpg)

### Earn Digital Credentials

Showcase what you know and what you can do with digital credentials that validate your skills and achievements. Stand out to employers and take the next step in your career.](https://www.cpp.edu/ii/digital-badging/earn-a-badge.shtml)

[Card 3 of 6

![Student maintaining the plants.](img/homepage/student-opportunities/student-opportunites-carousel-imgs/9.jpg)

### Serve with College Corps

Make an impact while earning financial support. College Corps provides meaningful service opportunities in education, climate action, and food security, helping you grow while giving back.](https://www.cpp.edu/cce/students/college-corps-fellows-poly-corps.shtml)

[Card 4 of 6

![Two students listening in class.](img/homepage/student-opportunities/student-opportunites-carousel-imgs/19.jpg)

### Join Career-Based Workshops

Boost your career confidence with workshops designed to sharpen your skills, from interview prep to salary negotiation. Learn directly from industry experts and career advisors.](https://careercenter.cpp.edu/channels/workshops/)

[Card 5 of 6

![Career counselor holds a resume and listens to student.](img/homepage/student-opportunities/student-opportunites-carousel-imgs/20.jpg)

### Perfect Your Resume and Cover Letter Writing

Get expert feedback and to highlight your strengths and stand out to employers.](https://careercenter.cpp.edu/channels/writing-resume-cover-letter/)

[Card 6 of 6

![Student signs up at the USC Graduate School table.](img/homepage/student-opportunities/student-opportunites-carousel-imgs/21.jpg)

### Receive Guidance with Graduate School Planning

Thinking about grad school? Get guidance through the application process, from researching programs to preparing for exams and securing letters of recommendation.](https://careercenter.cpp.edu/channels/graduate-school/)

## Internships & Career Connections

### Bridge the gap between education and career

Gain practical experience and make valuable industry connections through internships, apprenticeships, and networking events. Our programs connect students with employers and professionals who can help shape your career path.

Previous
Next

[Card 1 of 6

![Office of Academic Innovation team seated at a table. ](img/homepage/student-opportunities/student-opportunites-carousel-imgs/5.jpg)

### Gain Experiences with Micro-Internships

Build your resume with short-term, project-based internships that provide real-world experiences and valuable industry connections. Flexible, hands-on, and designed for busy students.](https://www.cpp.edu/ii/micro-internships/for-students.shtml)

[Card 2 of 6

![Students wearing lab equipment conduct an experiment.](img/homepage/student-opportunities/student-opportunites-carousel-imgs/10.jpg)

### Apply for Academic Internships

Earn academic credit while gaining hands-on experience in your field. Academic internships let you apply classroom learning to real-world challenges, giving you a competitive edge.](https://www.cpp.edu/cce/students/internships.shtml)

[Card 3 of 6

![Kellogg Honors Community students talking at a meeting.](img/homepage/student-opportunities/student-opportunites-carousel-imgs/12.jpg)

### Join the Kellogg Honors Community

Connect with like-minded, high achieving students in a supportive environment. Engage in research, leadership, and service while pushing yourself to new heights.](https://www.cpp.edu/honorscollege/index.shtml)

[Card 4 of 6

![Kellogg Honors Community students talking at a meeting.](img/homepage/student-opportunities/student-opportunites-carousel-imgs/16.jpg)

### Connect with Employers at Career Fairs

Make valuable connections and explore career opportunities at the career fairs. Meet recruiters, learn about job openings, and take the next step toward your future.](https://careercenter.cpp.edu/channels/career-fairs-recruitment/)

[Card 5 of 6

![Student speaking to an audience.](img/homepage/student-opportunities/student-opportunites-carousel-imgs/17.jpg)

### Find Internships that Fit Your Goals

Gain real-world experience through internships that align with your career internships. Whether you’re exploring new fields or building on your expertise, the Career Center can support you in finding the right opportunity.](https://careercenter.cpp.edu/channels/internships/)

[Card 6 of 6

![Student meeting with a career guidance counselor.](img/homepage/student-opportunities/student-opportunites-carousel-imgs/18.jpg)

### Get Personalized Job Search Support

Meet with career advisors to help with search strategies, networking tips, and resources to land the position that’s right for you.](https://careercenter.cpp.edu/channels/search-for-a-job-internship/)

## Innovation & Entrepreneurship

### Turn ideas into action

Whether you're launching a startup, designing a prototype, or working on a groundbreaking solution, we support student entrepreneurs and innovators. Access resources, mentorship, and funding to bring your vision to life.

Previous
Next

[Card 1 of 5

![Attendees of a workshop in the Hub looking at the projector.](img/homepage/student-opportunities/student-opportunites-carousel-imgs/11.jpg)

### Attend a Workshop at the Community Innovation Hub

Explore hands-on workshops designed to spark creativity, build skills, and connect you with a community of innovators.](https://www.cpp.edu/thehub/programs-and-workshops.shtml)

[Card 2 of 5

![SIIL Ambassadors work on the iLab sign ](img/homepage/student-opportunities/student-opportunites-carousel-imgs/maker-studio-ilab.jpg)

### Bring Your Ideas to Life at the Maker Studio and iLab

Turn creativity into reality with cutting-edge tools, collaborative spaces, and expert guidance. Whether you’re prototyping or designing, these labs are here to support your vision.](https://www.cpp.edu/siil/our-spaces/index.shtml)

[Card 3 of 5

![SIIL Ambassador teaches trainees how to operate the sewing machine.](img/homepage/student-opportunities/student-opportunites-carousel-imgs/ambassador.jpg)

### Become an Ambassador

Inspire and support fellow students by becoming an ambassador for innovation and entrepreneurship. Lead workshops, connect with industry professionals, and grow as a leader.](https://www.cpp.edu/siil/what-we-offer/ambassador-program.shtml)

[Card 4 of 5

![Student holding mic presents to an audience.](img/homepage/student-opportunities/student-opportunites-carousel-imgs/13.jpg)

### Launch Your Startup

Get the support, mentorship, and resources you need to build and grow your business from concept to reality.](https://www.cpp.edu/siil/events/istartup-academy.shtml)

[Card 5 of 5

![SIIL Ambassador teaches student how to use sewing machine.](img/homepage/student-opportunities/student-opportunites-carousel-imgs/14.jpg)

### Build Your Skills with Equipment Training

Gain hands-on experience with cutting-edge tools and technology. Complete equipment training sessions to learn tools such as 3D Printers, Laser cutting, soldering, and more.](https://www.cpp.edu/siil/what-we-offer/equipment-training.shtml)
