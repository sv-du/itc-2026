## Application

---

The Research Distinction application is open year-round and awarded at the end fall and spring semesters. CPP students must fulfill the following criteria to be eligible for the Research Distinction:

1. Participate in faculty-mentored research or creative activity for a semester
2. Present at a research conference or symposium. CPP [RSCA](https://www.cpp.edu/cppsrc/current-conference/index.shtml) and [CARS](https://www.cpp.edu/our-cpp/events-workshops/cars/index.shtml) are acceptable.
3. Publish the research in a journal or deposit on [Bronco ScholarWorks](https://www.cpp.edu/library/digital-collections/bronco-scholarworks/faq.shtml "https://www.cpp.edu/library/digital-collections/bronco-scholarworks/index.shtml")

Please view the application for more information.

[Apply for Research Distinction](https://forms.office.com/Pages/ResponsePage.aspx?id=HqZLFuw5XU-J_6ofAKUhtIA4mUkTPiRKi2czkQDGumlUMVpTRkZWRktVN0JNMFcyOVBUU1NTVVFQTiQlQCN0PWcu) [Submit Faculty Mentor Form](https://forms.office.com/Pages/ResponsePage.aspx?id=HqZLFuw5XU-J_6ofAKUhtIA4mUkTPiRKi2czkQDGumlUM05QQU8wNzY3TkNTVEhBNzE0MVBUODRVTSQlQCN0PWcu)
