# PreLaw Pathways

![A group of students standing in front of Pomona Court building](img/mock-trialstudents.jpg)

## Welcome to PreLaw Pathways

Cal Poly Pomona PreLaw Pathways offers a variety of opportunities to help CPP students interested in attending law school explore legal careers and navigate the law school application process.

Here, you can find information about prelaw advisors, events and workshops organized by CPP PreLaw Pathways, LSAT and other law school applications related information, and suggested prelaw courses offered at Cal Poly Pomona.

No matter where you are in the process, from thinking about going to law school to getting ready to apply, please reach out to the CPP PreLaw Pathways advisors and subscribe to our newsletter to receive resources and workshop information.

## Areas of Expertise

I am interested in how we as a society respond to crime. My research focuses on police interactions such as investigatory stops and the phenomenon of racialized police violence. I gladly welcome students who are interested in collaborating on research projects with me. My research projects with students have included: analyzing police use-of-force policies, analyzing violence committed against Blacks in the South during Reconstruction, experimental studies of how perceptions of dangerousness and criminality are influenced by race and how rule-breaking is evaluated.

## Explore Law in Action: Hands-On Legal Experiences at CPP

[### Ethics Bowl

Join CPP’s nationally recognized Ethics Bowl team to debate real-world issues, sharpen your critical thinking, and compete against top schools.](https://www.cpp.edu/class/philosophy/student-organizations/index.shtml "opens a new window")

[### Judicial Internship

Get inside the courtroom with the Cal Poly Pomona Judicial Internship—gain firsthand experience, connect with judges and lawyers, and explore the legal system up close while earning academic credit.](https://www.cpp.edu/class/political-science/participate/judicial-internship.shtml "opens a new window")

[### Mock Trial

Step into the courtroom with Cal Poly Pomona’s award-winning Mock Trial Team—build real trial skills, compete across the region, and experience the excitement of litigation firsthand.](https://www.cppmocktrial.com/tryout "opens a new window")
