# Department Information

## Major and Minor Information

![ENV Resource Fair](img/env-student-success-fair.jpg)

Use the [Change of Major tool](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/guides/changeofmajor/requirements.shtml) for more information on declaring or changing into a College of Environmental Design major. If you have more questions about an ENV major, contact the department chair.

* Architecture: Robert Alexander (ralexander@cpp.edu)
* Art History (for Spring 2026): Kevin Moore (kemoore@cpp.edu)
* Landscape Architecture: Claire A. Latané (calatane@cpp.edu)
* Urban & Regional Planning: Dohyung Kim (dohyungkim@cpp.edu)
* Visual Communication Design (for Spring 2026): Kevin Moore (kemoore@cpp.edu)

For more information about declaring a College of Environmental Design minor, use the [Minor Coordinator Directory](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/directories/declaring-a-minor.shtml). You can also contact the minor coordinator.

* Art History:
  + For Fall Term Requests: Alison Pearlman (apearlman@cpp.edu)
  + For Spring Term Requests: Kevin Moore (kemoore@cpp.edu)
* Landscape Architecture: Barry Lehrman (blehrman@cpp.edu)
* Regenerative Studies: Andy Wilcox  (aowilcox@cpp.edu)
* Studio Arts: Kevin Moore (kemoore@cpp.edu )
* Urban and Regional Planning: Dohyung Kim (dohyungkim@cpp.edu)
