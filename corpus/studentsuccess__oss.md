## About Us

### Serving All CPP Students From Admission to Graduation

The Office of Student Success at Cal Poly Pomona drives institutional transformation to facilitate persistence, retention, and graduation. We proactively connect students with advising, targeted academic support, and critical resources throughout their educational journey.

[Learn More about OSS](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/oss/about.shtml%20 "opens a new window")
