# Community Building

## Community Building Events

![Students attend a NASC event](../img/community.png)

To build community, we plan events that are intentionally focused on the three R’s that are integral to Native ways of being: Respect, Relations and Reciprocity.

These events include: Welcome Back Socials to kick-off each semester, Native Nights events including game nights, movie nights, or Mario Kart tournaments, Food and Finals Prep events to allow students to refuel and study together, and many others. All events are open to anyone in the campus community.
