# Electric Vehicle Parking

## Electric Vehicle Charging Stations

If you have an electric vehicle, you may use one of our 22 charging stations in [Lot H](https://www.cpp.edu/maps/?id=1130#!m/276396), which is located on Red Gum Lane between the CLA Building and the College of Business Administration, or in [Parking Structure II](https://www.cpp.edu/maps/?id=1130#!m/277494), where 24 spots are available.

Please note there is a 4-hour limit on charging your vehicle.
