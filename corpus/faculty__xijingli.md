## About Me

Xijing Li is an assistant professor in Department of urban and regional planning in CPP. He gained his Ph.D. degree in the Department of City and Regional Planning at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, Aug, 2024. His research critically examines environmental injustice under the urban retrofitting process, particularly in communities burdened by historical planning practices like redlining.
