# Clubs and Organizations

## La Union Leadership Collective

La Union is a César E. Chávez Center for Higher Education leadership development program that serves as the representative and collaborative body for the Chicano/Latino student organizations, clubs, fraternities and sororities.

La Union is a space where Chicano/Latinx student organizations and clubs can learn from one another, take advantage of co-programming opportunities and can be informed of programs, services and events that each organization is planning through out the year.

Through La Union the César E. Chávez Center for Higher Education administrates the needs of the Cal Poly Pomona Chicano Latinx student leaders by providing leadership development through workshops, retreats and leadership and program advising.

### Phi Lambda Rho Sorority, Inc.

Phi Lambda Rho Sorority, Inc. is committed to developing and empowering strong Chicana/ Latina leaders who will make a positive impact in their community. As an organization, we strive to provide women with the resources to grow personally, academically, and professionally in order to reach their ultimate goal. ​ The purpose of Phi Lambda Rho Sorority, Inc. is to provide a lifelong sisterhood based on facilitating the advancement of women of color through higher education. ​ Our mission is to give back to our community, especially our marginalized youth while celebrating and promoting the Chicana/Latina culture.

### Latino Business Students' Association

The mission of the Latino Business Students' Association is to enable Latino students, as a whole, to increase their knowledge of the many opportunities in the business field and further develop each individual's skills and abilities through participation in club events. This includes our guest speaker series where get to hear from guest speakers experiences in the field of business, to workshops, and social mixers to expand our network. The main emphasis is placed on further developing business students as a whole. We want our members to be seen as Latino students who can professionally, personally and socially interact and participate successfully in the business world.

[Latino Business Students - Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/lbsapomona/)

### Hermanos Unidos de CPP

To advance young Latino males into academic scholars, community leaders, and young professionals. By instilling the values represented through our three pillars - Academic Excellence, Community Service, and Social Interaction - our members will work to break down cultural stereotypes and strive to become "El Nuevo Hombre."

### Hermanas Unidas de CPP

Hermanas Unidas is an organization that provides undergraduate, graduate, and professional leadership opportunities and resources to predominately Latina/Chicana students by participating in and organizing academic, community service, and social events/workshops with different chapters/clubs and orgs. Our organization strives to create a safe space for all of our members and encourage/welcome everyone with open arms.
