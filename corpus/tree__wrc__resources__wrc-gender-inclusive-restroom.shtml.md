# Gender Inclusive Restroom

## Locate Gender Inclusive Restrooms

Below is a list representing the areas on campus in which single-stall/gender-inclusive restrooms are located. Single-stall/gender-inclusive restrooms are located in the following buildings:

**Building 4A**
Bio-Trek - near front door (2)
\*\*limited hours

**Building 7**

Environmental Design – 102B
Environmental Design – 102C

**Building 15**

Library (24-Hour Computer Lab) - 1800A & 1800B

**Buildings 20, 21, 22, 23** (Residence Halls)

\*\*must be a resident to gain building access
1st Floor, either side of entrance - 2

**Building 24**

Music - 203

**Building 29**

W.K. Kellogg Arabian Horse Center - 103D

**Building 30**

Agricultural Unit

**Building 32**

Beef Unit
**Building 37**
Swine Unit

**Building 38**

Sheep/Wool Unit

**Building 42**

Bronco Intramural Recreation Complex (BRIC) - 1st floor
Universal Changing Room (shower included)

**Building 45**

Agricultural Engineering - R130 & R140 (sign from hallway reads room R110)

**Building 46**

Student Health Services Center - 1st Floor Lobby

**Building 52**

Denny's
Multipurpose Room (MPR)

**Buildings 54, 60, 61, 62 and 63** (Residential Suites)

\*\*must be a resident to gain building access
1st Floor Lobby
**Building 70**
Los Olivos Commons - 2nd Floor
\*Male/Female, Single Stall
\*\*must be a resident to gain building access

**Building 91**

Student Affairs Information & Technology Services - 216

**Buildings 94**

Room 106 (Male, single use)
Room 107 (Female, single use)

**Building 95**

African American Student Center
Asian and Pacific Islander Student Center
Cesar E. Chavez Center for Higher Education
Womxn’s Resource Center

**Building 109**

University Police Department - 1st Floor Lobby

**Building 164**

Einstein’s/Pony Express – 1st Floor

**Building 193**
Chilled Water Center Plant - 1st floor

**Building 200**

University Village (2) - Village Community Center
\*\*must be a resident to gain building access
