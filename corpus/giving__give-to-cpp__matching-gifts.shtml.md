# Give to Cal Poly Pomona

## Matching Gifts

[Matching Gift](https://doublethedonation.com/matching-grant-resources/matching-gift-basics/) and [Volunteer Grant](https://doublethedonation.com/matching-grant-resources/volunteer-grant-basics/) information provided by
[![Powered by Double the Donation](https://doublethedonation.com/api/img/powered-by.png)](https://doublethedonation.com)

Did you know that your support for Cal Poly Pomona can be doubled without you giving a dollar more? Many employers will also match any volunteer hours made by their employees. Find out if your employer participates in a Matching Gifts Program to maximize the impact your gift will have on our students, programs and resources.

Many corporations match employee donations or the tax deductible component of your membership to our university. Search for your company above to see if this opportunity is available for you or look at our FAQs provided below.

**Thank you for discovering how you can partner with your company to uplift our student experiences!**
