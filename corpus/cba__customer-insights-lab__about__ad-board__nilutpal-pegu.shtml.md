# Nilutpal Pegu

![erantzeri](../../img/advisory-board/nilutpal-pegu-headshot.jpg)

## **Role in the Center:**Advisory Board Member

---

Nilutpal Pegu is a transformative digital leader with over 15 years of experience spearheading large-scale digital transformations and driving exponential revenue growth within Fortune 500 companies across the pharmaceutical, biotechnology, and insurance sectors. His proven expertise lies in architecting and executing innovative digital strategies that elevate customer experiences, optimize operational efficiencies, and deliver exceptional financial outcomes. A key driver throughout his career has been a passion for bridging the gap between cutting-edge academic research and practical industry application.

Adept at building and inspiring high-performing global teams, Mr. Pegu fosters a culture of digital innovation, leveraging emerging technologies such as AI/ML and data analytics to create disruptive business value. His career is marked by a demonstrated ability to lead e-commerce growth, omnichannel customer engagement, and data-driven marketing optimization, consistently exceeding expectations and driving market leadership.

Currently, as Vice President, Global Head of Digital at BioMarin Pharmaceutical Inc., Mr. Pegu leads the development and execution of the company's global omnichannel customer engagement and go-to-market strategy. He has a strong track record of modernizing customer experience ecosystems, implementing agile digital transformation programs, and developing best-in-class data science and analytics capabilities.

Mr. Pegu's previous roles include Global Head, Digital Sales & Marketing at Merck KGaA, where he led a $2.5B global digital and e-commerce business, and Head, Growth & Performance Marketing at Allstate, where he built and led a high-performing digital and performance marketing organization.

His leadership has consistently driven significant revenue growth, improved ROI, and enhanced customer engagement across diverse industries.

## Education

Nilutpal Pegu earned a Master of Business Administration (MBA) with a specialization in Finance from The Wharton School at the University of Pennsylvania. Prior to his MBA, he completed a Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering from MNIT, Jaipur.

## Area of Experience

Nilutpal Pegu is an accomplished leader with expertise spanning Digital Strategy, AI & Machine Learning, and Data Analytics. His career reflects significant achievements in driving E-commerce Growth through sophisticated Omnichannel Marketing and optimized Customer Experiences. With robust experience in P&L Management and Performance Marketing, he has consistently delivered notable Revenue Growth. Nilutpal is also skilled in forging Strategic Partnerships, enabling organizations to achieve sustained success and operational excellence in rapidly evolving markets.

## Notable Awards and Recognitions

Nilutpal Pegu has earned notable professional recognitions, including Liberty Mutual’s prestigious President’s Award in 2015, the company’s highest honor for exceptional contributions. Throughout his career at BioMarin, Merck KGaA, Allstate, and Liberty Mutual, he consistently achieved the highest performance rating, placing him among the top 5% of employees. His expertise and industry leadership have also earned him invitations to the Forbes Technology Council and the IA Forum Advisory Board.

## Involvement in the Center

Nilutpal Pegu serves on the Advisory Board at the Center for Customer Insights and Digital Marketing (CCIDM) at Cal Poly Pomona. A strong advocate for bridging the gap between academia and industry, he will provide guidance and expertise to the Center, contributing to its mission of advancing knowledge in consumer behavior and digital marketing within the digital economy. He will collaborate with other industry leaders and faculty to address emerging challenges and opportunities in the field, enrich the academic environment, and foster practical learning experiences for students, drawing from his experience as a Guest Speaker at CPP.
