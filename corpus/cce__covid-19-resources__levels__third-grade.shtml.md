## Science

### 3D Paper Flowers

Using construction paper, children will create 3D paper flowers for spring time.

[YouTube Video](https://youtu.be/Ht3yEyZfss4 "YouTube Video . ")

### Rain Cloud - Shaving Cream Fun

Children will learn about weather and how rain clouds work.

[YouTube Video](https://youtu.be/tCutJQqZego "YouTube Video. ")

### How to Make a Bird Feeder

Children will learn how to create their very own bird feeder (Designed by a Cal Poly Pomona Student).

[YouTube Video](https://youtu.be/DPn2lm50yfU "YouTube Video. ")

### How to Make a Catapult Box

Chidlren will learn how to make a catapult box using critical thinking skills while having fun! (Designed by a Cal Poly Pomona Student).

[YouTube Video](https://youtu.be/TX7MJsRF2G8 "YouTube Video. ")

### How to Make a Balloon Catapult

Did you know there are so many different way to create energy? This fun way to launch a balloon is simple and fun! (Designed by a Cal Poly Pomona Student).

[YouTube Video](https://youtu.be/OCJwR6_Jdgw "YouTube Video. ")

### Rainmaker

Description: The child will be able to create a miniature water cycle in a bottle and record the process of how long it takes for it to “rain”.

[PDF Document](https://www.cpp.edu/cce/covid-19-resources/files/3-rainmaker.pdf "PDF Document. ")

### Optics 4 Kids

Description: Explore the Science of light!

[Website Link](https://www.optics4kids.org/home "Website Link. ")

### Quarked

Description: THE QUARKED! PROJECT has a wide range of engaging multimedia and hands-on materials to introduce the exciting world of particle physics to kids ages 7 and up, and their families.

[Website Link](http://www.quarked.org/parents/ "Website Link. ")

### Science games for kids

Description: Learn about forces in action as you experiment how gradients, weights, motion and resistance affect the movement of various objects with this fun science activity.

[Website Link](https://www.sciencekids.co.nz/gamesactivities/forcesinaction.html "Website Link. ")

### Well, are you sure about that?

Children will learn about scientific conepts such as the layering of various material compositions underneath the surface of the Earth. (Designed by Cal Poly Student)

[PDF Document](https://www.cpp.edu/cce/covid-19-resources/files/3-well-are-you-sure-about-that.pdf "PDF Document. ")

### Cloud Watching

Have kids recreate their favorite cloud with cotton balls to show the thickness of a real cloud. (Designed by Cal Poly Student)

[PDF Document](https://www.cpp.edu/cce/covid-19-resources/files/4-cloud-watching.pdf "PDF Document. ")
