# Instructional Technology Support

![An overhead view of a group of male students studying. Laptops, phones and homework are spread across the table](img/tech-support.jpg)

## Technology Support

Technology is a crucial piece of the educational landscape today. As we return to more in-person instruction on campus, we know that WiFi, computer labs, and tech support are more important than ever. We’ve used the Student Success Fee to bolster these efforts in the following ways.

![A student uses a laptop in class](img/laptop.jpg)

## Strengthen WiFi and Network

In 2021-22, Information Technology installed 95 wireless access points in and around campus buildings and parking lots.

![The IT Tech Support desk in the CPP library](img/supportdesk.jpg)

## Expand IT Service Desk

The professional staff and 62 student assistants who helm the IT Service Desk are essential to our tech health: they troubleshoot software problems, lost passwords and computers on the fritz. Visit [the Service Desk](https://www.cpp.edu/it/help/index.shtml) website to view support options available to all students.

![Students use a computer inside a lab at CPP](img/lab.jpg)

## Upgraded Technology

In 2021-22, we upgraded over 150 classroom audio/visual setups, including presentation technology, projectors and document cameras. The fee also supported funding for the [Virtual Software Lab](https://www.cpp.edu/it/virtual-software-lab/index.shtml). Funds also go towards software licenses, maintenance and other[student benefits.](https://www.cpp.edu/it/students/)
