# Undeclared Event Calendar

## Attending Undeclared Events

Events will be held via Zoom or in-person. Be sure to check the location when registering for an event. Please contact undeclared@cpp.edu if you have any questions.

**To RSVP for an event,** [accept your MyBAR invitation](https://mybar.cpp.edu/) to the Undeclared Advising organization. If you have trouble, email undeclared@cpp.edu.

## Spring 2026 Events

[### February 3: How to Declare a Major

Learn the steps to declare your major.](https://mybar.cpp.edu/event/12077074)

[### March 3: Major Fest

Dive into the world of majors, meet amazing staff, faculty, student success ambassadors and discover tons of exciting opportunities and majors! Plus, FREE food and light refreshments will be provided while supplies last!](https://mybar.cpp.edu/event/12077095)

[### April 30: Class Registration Assistance

Registering for Summer and Fall 2026 classes doesn’t have to be stressful: come to the Undeclared Class Registration Assistance Workshop and get the guidance you need!](https://mybar.cpp.edu/event/12097460)

### May 11-15: Fuel for Finals

Stop by the Student Services Building, first floor, blue counter to grab finals snacks and supplies.
