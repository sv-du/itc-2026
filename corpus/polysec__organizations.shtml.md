## Resources

### Information Directory

* [Information Directory](http://www.cyberdegrees.org/)

### Training

* [Cybrary](https://www.cybrary.it/) - Open-source(free) cyber security and IT learning and certification preparation platform
* [SANS Insitute](https://www.sans.org/work-study/) - Institute for cyber security training
* [Meetups](https://www.meetup.com/) - Join a cybersecurity group near you, search "cyber"
* [BSides](http://www.securitybsides.com/) - Community-driven framework for building events for and by information security community members
* [HackEducate](https://hackeducate.com/) - 6 week, intensive hands-on-keyboard cybersecurity training program

### Online Competitions for Training

* [CyberAces](http://www.cyberaces.org/) - Free courses offered by SANS Insitute
* [Over the Wire](http://overthewire.org/wargames/) - Hacking Challenges via games
* [Linux Survival](http://linuxsurvival.com/) - Free beginning training on Linux
* [CTFtime.org](https://ctftime.org/) - Lists Cyber competitions around the world

### Certifications

* [CompTIA](https://www.comptia.org/) - Network+, Security+, Linux+ - good starting point
* [EC-Council](https://www.eccouncil.org/) - Various certificates, C|EH|CND|ECSA
* [ISC2](https://www.isc2.org/) - CISSP | SSCP | CCSP | CAP | CSSLP | and ISC2
* [ISACA](https://www.isaca.org/) - CSX-P - entry-level certification
* [Offensive Security](https://www.offensive-security.com/) - OSP - senior league certificate
* [CISCO](http://www.cisco.com/) - CCNA - other sysad-type certificates
* [GIAC](https://www.giac.org/) - Various certificates, in compliance with SANS Insitute
