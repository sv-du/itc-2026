# Alumni Opportunities

## Mentor. Connect. Grow.

### Unlock Meaningful Alumni Opportunities

Your journey with Cal Poly Pomona doesn’t end at graduation. Whether you’re mentoring students, engaging with the Bronco community, or advancing your career, there are countless ways to stay involved and make an impact. Explore opportunities to inspire the next generation, support innovation, and continue your professional growth as a proud Bronco.

![test](img/homepage/alumni-opportunities/ashish.jpg)

## Mentor & Inspire the Next Generation

### Empower students and young alumni through mentorship

Your experience and insights can shape the future of fellow Broncos. Whether you mentor students, provide micro-internships, or engage with emerging scholars, your support helps create career-ready graduates. Share your expertise, make lasting connections, and inspire the next generation.

### Offer Micro-Internships to CPP Students

## Engage with Community & Service

### Make a lasting impact through service and innovation

Bronco alumni are changemakers, and CPP offers opportunities to give back. From community partnerships to hands-on service projects, your involvement enriches both student experiences and local organizations. Find meaningful ways to contribute and stay connected with CPP’s mission.

Next

![bronco stampede of service](img/opportunity-pages/alumni-opportunities/bronco-stampede.jpg)

### Join the Bronco Stampede of Service

Participate in university-wide service events that support local communities while connecting with fellow alumni.](https://www.cpp.edu/cce/learn-by-doing-good/bronco-stampede-service.shtml)

[Card 2 of 3

![grand opening of community innovation hub](img/opportunity-pages/alumni-opportunities/parter-with-the-hub.jpg)

### Partner with the Community Innovation Hub

Use your expertise to support Pomona’s workforce through technology and entrepreneurship programs.](https://www.cpp.edu/thehub/contact-us.shtml)

![student participating in coop](img/opportunity-pages/alumni-opportunities/co-op.jpg)

### Support Service Learning & Cooperative Education

Help bridge classroom theory with practical applications by integrating real-world experiences into academic coursework.](https://www.cpp.edu/cce/partners/co-op-education/index.shtml)
