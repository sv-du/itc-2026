# Exploratory Students

## Interested in Pursuing a Different Major?

[### Explore Your Major Options at CPP

Look at the list of majors and degrees CPP offers to learn more about your options.](https://www.cpp.edu/programs/index.shtml)

[### Review Curriculum Sheets

Look at curriculum sheets for the major(s) you're interested in to see what courses you'd need to take and read course descriptions.](../../../advising/index.shtml)

[### Make an Advising Appointment

Visit the Undeclared, Exploratory, and Pre-Health Professional Advising Coordinator to talk about your exploration. You can make an appointment on CPP Connect.](../../../cppconnect/index.shtml)

[### Visit the Career Center

Once you have found a major of interest, the Lead Career Coach from that major's college can help you use career exploration tools, review your resume, and find internships and employment opportunities and more. Visit the Career Center website for more information.](https://careercenter.cpp.edu/)
