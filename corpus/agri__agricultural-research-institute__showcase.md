# Showcase

**Cal Poly Pomona Agricultural Research Institute Showcase****Kellogg West Conference Center, 25 April 2025 1:00pm – 5:00pm**
[Register Here](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/cal-poly-pomona-agricultural-research-institute-showcase-2025-tickets-1304699343859?aff=ebdsshcopyurl&utm-campaign=social&utm-content=attendeeshare&utm-medium=discovery&utm-term=organizer-profile&utm-share-source=organizer-profile)

1:00  **Poster Session**

[ARI-funded projects (active projects).](poster_titles_25.pdf)  Principal Investigators and students available for questions & discussion.

2:00 **Opening Remarks**

* **Dr. Soraya M. Coley**, President, Cal Poly Pomona
* **Dr. Ethan Orr**, Dean, Don B. Huntley College of Agriculture, Cal Poly Pomona
* **Dr. David Still**, Executive Director, California State University Agricultural Research Institute
* **Dr. Ondieki Gekara**, ARI Campus Coordinator, Cal Poly Pomona

2:10 **Oral Presentations**

1. **Subodh Bandari**, Aerospace Engineering, [“Application of AI/ML Techniques for Weed Detection and Removal using Aerial and Ground Robots”](abstracts_2025_bhandari.pdf)
2. **Bonny Burns-Whitmore**, Nutrition and Food Science, [“The Effects of Freeze-Dried Whole Grape Powder on Chronic Disease and Cardiovascular Risk Factors, Hunger, Satiety, and Body Composition in Free-Living People-a Pilot Study”](abstracts_2025_bbw.pdf)
3. **Sean Liu**, Chemistry and Biochemistry, [“Studies towards Development of Portable Devices for Detecting Citrus Fungicides and HLB using LFA and LAMP Platforms”](abstracts_2025_sl.pdf)
4. **Jiangning Che**, Apparel Merchandising & Management, [“Almond Waste for Textile Coloration with Potential Antimicrobial Performance”](abstracts_2025_che1.pdf)
5. **Gabreil Granco**, Geography & Anthropology, [“Multisensors and Multiplatform Assessment of Vineyard Health”](abstracts_2025_gg.pdf)

3:00 **Break/posters**

3:20 **Oral Presentations (continued)**

6. **Steven Archambault**, Agribusiness and Food Industry Management, [“Is It Eco-friendly and Safe? Investigation of Consumers’ Perceived Ecological Benefits, Contagion Beliefs, and Safety Concerns of Hydroponic, Aquaponic, and Organic Produce”](abstracts_2025_sa.pdf)
7. **Alex John,** Chemistry and Biochemistry, “[Catalytic Methods for Production of Chemical Feedstock from Biomass-Derived Molecules"](abstracts_2025_aj.pdf)
8. **Xu Yang**, Nutrition and Food Science, [“The Utilization of Bacteriophage as a Biocontrol Agent for *Escherichia coli*Decontamination in Microgreens”](abstracts_2025_xy1.pdf)
9. **Priti Saxena**, Plant Science, [“Effect of *PSY 2* and *SFSR* Genes on the Organic Tomato Fruit Quality”](abstracts_2025_ps2.pdf)
10. **Elizabeth Scordato**, Biological Sciences, [“Incorporating Native Vegetation into a Landscape-Scale Integrated Pest Management Program in Ventura County”](abstracts_2025_es1.pdf)

4:10 **Oral Presentation Closing Remarks**

4:15**Poster Session (continued)**
