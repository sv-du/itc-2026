# Before You Apply

## Eligibility Requirements

![The Bronco Bound Open House for prospective students at Cal Poly Pomona ](img/bronco-bound.png)

To be eligible to participate in RAMP, you must be enrolled or accepted for enrollment at Cal Poly Pomona; a U.S. citizen or national or meet the residency requirements for Federal student financial assistance; have an academic need for services; and meet at least one of the following criteria:

* First-generation college student (neither parent or guardian graduated with a bachelor's or higher degree)
* Low-income student ([Federal TRIO Programs Current-Year Low-Income Levels](https://www.ed.gov/about/ed-offices/ope/trio/federal-trio-programs-current-year-low-income-levels#https://www.ed.gov/about/ed-offices/ope/trio/federal-trio-programs-current-year-low-income-levels) can be found)
* Student with a documented physical or learning disability
