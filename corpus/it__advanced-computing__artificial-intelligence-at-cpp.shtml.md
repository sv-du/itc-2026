# Artificial Intelligence at CPP

## Prompt by Doing.

**![GenAI image of astronaut looking at an alien landscape](../img/ai-at-cpp-banner.png)**

**Cal Poly Pomona (CPP) is a leader in exploring artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning education.** At CPP, we build partnerships with leading technology companies to provide innovative academic resources, host research competitions, and prepare tomorrow's professionals for an AI-aligned workforce.

A Vision for Leadership in Higher Education: As a leader in academics, research, and workforce development, the university is positioned at the forefront of the AI revolution. Our commitment to providing equitable access and industry collaboration shapes the future of education while securing the responsible and ethical use of AI technologies. CPP supplies AI tools campuswide as part of our dedication to preparing students for careers in this emerging industry.

* Provide educational opportunities for all students to graduate with AI literacy and real-world experience.
* Supporting faculty in integrating AI into teaching, learning, and research methodologies.
* Partnering with industry leaders to provide students with internship and career opportunities.
* Promoting ethical AI use by addressing bias, privacy, and academic integrity concerns.
* Leveraging AI to improve institutional efficiency and decision-making.
* Establishing an AI Innovation Center with industry partner Avanade, serving as a hub for AI development and research.

Cal Poly Pomona is not just embracing AI — we are shaping its role in higher education. As CPP continues to advance our AI initiatives, we remain focused on empowering students, faculty, and staff to harness its transformative potential responsibly and equitably.
