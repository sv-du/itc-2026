# Computer Science Supplementary Authorization (CSSA)

The Computer Science Department and CEMaST are offering FREE tuition for multiple and single subject teachers to obtain their [supplementary authorizations in computer science](https://www.ctc.ca.gov/docs/default-source/credentials/manuals-handbooks/supplement-auth.pdf).  Both programs will be available for K-12 teachers. Unfortunately, we are not offering these programs in 2025-26 AY.

##### **Benefits of the Programs**

* Online coursework with both synchronous and asynchronous components
* Tuition is FREE through generous grants funded by SchoolsFirst FCU and MSTI
* 10 upper division university units which may increase your salary
* Cohort-based with program completion in 2 semesters (4 courses)
* CTC application fees will be reimbursed
* Easy enrollment through Cal Poly's College of Extended University
* No computer programming experience required
* Courses offered by experienced college professors

##### **Program #1:****Introductory Supplementary Authorization in Computer Science**

Introductory Supplementary Authorizations **may be added to a Single Subject or Multiple Subject Teaching Credential** and authorizes the holder to teach the supplementary subject matter content typically included in curriculum guidelines and textbooks for study in grades 9 and below. The list of courses include:

* Visual Programming for Teachers (3 units)
* Impacts of Computing (2 units)
* Advanced Programming for Teachers (3 units)
* Computer Systems (2 units)

##### **Program #2:****Specific****Supplementary Authorization in Computer Science**

Specific Supplementary Authorizations **may be added to a Single Subject Teaching Credential** and authorizes the holder to teach the specific subject in grades preschool through 12th, and classes organized primarily for adults. The list of courses include:

* Computer Programming for Teachers (3 units)
* Computer Systems and Networks (3 units)
* Data Structures for Teachers (2 units)
* Software Engineering for Teachers (2 units)
