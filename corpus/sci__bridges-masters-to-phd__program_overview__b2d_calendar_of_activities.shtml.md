# B2D Calendar of Activities

[**TIMELINE AND ACTIVITIES FOR EACH TWO-YEAR B2D COHORT**](../img/calendar-of-activities_08202021.pdf "B2D Timeline and Activities")

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **First Summer**   --- | |
| **Weeks 1 & 2 9AM-5PM** | * **Monday-Thurs: Molecular Biology Techniques workshops** * **Friday: Professional Skills Workshops (2 hours per workshop)** * **Begin work on Individual Development Plan (IDP)**  --- |
| **Week 3 9AM-5PM** | * **Monday-Friday: Professional Skills Workshops (2 hours per workshop)**  --- |
| **Academic Year 1 (end of August through mid-May)**   --- | |
| **Fall, & Spring Semesters** | * **CPP MS research projects and academic coursework** * **Introduce CPP STEM undergraduates to benefits of B2D experience through peer mentoring, seminars and selected workshops** * **Continue work on IDP**  --- |
| **Fall** **Semester** | * **Wednesdays 5:30PM-7PM (2 unit-CPP BIO 6800 B2D workshop – Dr. Nancy Buckley): PhD Application Workshop; Fridays 12PM-1:30PM (2 unit-CPP BIO-6800 B2D seminars & Journal Club – Dr. Wei-Jen Lin): Guest Speaker Seminars (3X/Semester), Journal Club (1.5h/session, 3X/Semester) and Grant Writing (2h/session, 3X/Semester)**  --- |
| **Spring Semester** | * **Wednesdays 5:30PM-7PM (2 unit-CPP BIO 5990 B2D workshops – Dr. Nancy Buckley): Manuscript Writing Workshop; Fridays 12PM-1:30PM (2 unit-CPP BIO-5990 B2D seminars & Journal Club – Dr. Wei-Jen Lin): Guest Speaker Seminars (3X/Semester), Journal Club (1.5h/session, 3X/Semester) and Grant Writing (2h/session, 3X/Semester), URM Graduate campus coordinator visits (1.5h/session, 2X/Semester); Present research at CPP & UCR student research symposium**  --- |
| **Second Summer (end of May through beginning of August-10 weeks)**   --- | |
| **40h/week** | * **Execute research project in laboratory of UCR FRM; Give oral research presentation at UCR and CPP summer symposia; Continue work on IDP**  --- |
| **Academic Year 2 (end of August through mid-May)**   --- | |
| **Fall & Spring Semesters** | * **CPP MS research projects and academic coursework** * **Introduce CPP STEM undergraduates to benefits of B2D experience through peer mentoring, seminars and selected workshops** * **Continue work on IDP**  --- |
| **Fall Semester** | * **Complete and submit PhD applications** * **UCR predoctoral graduate course enrollment** * **Fridays 12PM-1:30PM (2 unit-CPP BIO 6800 B2D seminars & Journal Club – Dr. Wei-Jen Lin): Guest Speaker Seminars (3X/Semester), Journal Club (1.5h/session, 3X/Semester) and Grant Writing (2h/session, 3X/Semester)** * **Continue work on IDP**  --- |
| **Spring Semester** | * **Present research at STEM local, state and/or national meetings** * **Begin to write MS thesis** * **Complete IDP**  --- |
| **Third Summer (end of May through beginning of August-10 weeks)**   --- | |
| **20h/week** | * **Finish last experiments for MS project** * **Complete writing MS thesis, give oral Defense of MS Thesis, make final corrections on MS thesis and submit thesis to CPP Graduate Office**  --- |
