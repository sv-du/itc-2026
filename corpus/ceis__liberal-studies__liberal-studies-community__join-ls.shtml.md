# Join Liberal Studies

## Follow these steps

### Check out our Programs

Check out Our Programs to learn about the General Studies, Pre-Credential, and ITEP program options.

### Attend an Info Session

Attend an info session. Bring your questions! The session is designed for you to learn whether the major is compatible with your academic, career, and life goals.

### Info Session Schedule

Info sessions typically happen twice a semester. For details related to info session scheduling, please contact the Liberal Studies coordinator. After you RSVP, you will receive the event details.

### Fill out Change of Major Form

If you decide to join us, complete the Change of Major e-form. Then, email lsadmincoord@cpp.edu to notify the department that you intend to become a Liberal Studies major.

### Process and Results

Once we receive your form, we will verify you have attended the info session and are in good academic standing with at least 2.0 overall GPA. If so, your form will be forwarded to the Department Chair for approval.
Check in a few weeks later to see if your official major has been changed. If not, contact lsadmincoord@cpp.edu.

### Meet with an Academic Advisor

Make a plan to complete your Liberal Studies degree with a CEIS academic advisor.

## Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I meet with an Advisor before I transfer to Liberal Studies? ?

Unfortunately, you cannot schedule an appointment to see an academic advisor until you are officially a LS major. CEIS College Advisors sometimes have drop-in hours, those you can attend as long as it is not during a registration period.

### Can I fill out a Change of Major before I attend an information session?

Yes. Completing the form only indicates your intention. It will remain “on hold” until after the info session and you indicate your final decision. We remove all change of major forms "on hold" two weeks after each info session date. If you attended a session and decided to change at a later time, submit the form again. Before it can be approved,  email lsadmincoord@cpp.edu notifying her of:

* *the date you attended the information session,* and
* that you *now* want to be considered as a change of major.

Assuming your GPA is 2.0 and above, she will verify your attendance and forward the form.

### Can I take Liberal Studies courses before I join the major?

We don’t recommend it. In the event you attend the info session and decide not to become a major, you are stuck with a course that likely will not count toward another major. In the future, this will change and only LS majors will be able to take LS courses. At which time, the answer will be 'no.'

### Can I become a Liberal Studies major is my GPA is below 2.0?

It depends. It is best to make an appointment with the department chair to discuss your situation. Contact **lsadmincoord@cpp.edu**to make an appointment with Dr. Christina Chavez-Reyes. Come prepared to make your case for why becoming a Liberal Studies major is best for you and a plan for how you intend to increase your GPA.

### How do I change my major from Liberal Studies to another?

**It’s great that you have found where you want to contribute! Here are the steps to change your major:**

1. **View the change-of-major requirements for the major you want.**Here is the [Student Success Office Change-of-Major Guid](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/guides/change-of-major.shtml)e for other information and a change-of-major tool to find the requirements.*Don't worry if you do not have everything you need right now. You can work at it and change when you do.*It is important to get good information to make the best decision. Changing a major can affect your time to graduation. Seek help from an academic advisor to help determine any change in how long it will take to graduate.
2. **Meet with a faculty advisor** in the new department. Search for the department contact office using Bronco 411 Directory or find the department webpage. That advisor might help you make a plan to transition to the new major
3. [**Complete the Change of Major E-form**](https://polyform.cpp.edu/Loginformproc/UnityForm.aspx) once all requirements are met
4. **Start your new major!**

Email [CEISadvising@cpp.edu](https://nam11.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fmailto%3ACEISadvising%40cpp.edu%2F&data=04%7C01%7Cashleyjones%40cpp.edu%7Cf3b213a83b564313f91908d93b36a988%7C164ba61e39ec4f5d89ffaa1f00a521b4%7C0%7C0%7C637605928244599938%7CUnknown%7CTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJWIjoiMC4wLjAwMDAiLCJQIjoiV2luMzIiLCJBTiI6Ik1haWwiLCJXVCI6Mn0%3D%7C1000&sdata=bLxgCLnuG%2FtVWxVhDPRoXESGHECSu3YPd5foXfI8qwI%3D&reserved=0 "Original URL: https://mailto:CEISadvising@cpp.edu/.
