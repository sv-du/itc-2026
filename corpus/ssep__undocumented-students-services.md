# Undocumented Students Services

![Palm trees near the old stables](img/union-plaza-tower-2.jpg)

## About Us

Since its inception in 2017, Undocumented Students Services (USS) has served as a brave space where students can build relationships with faculty and staff that support their academic success, build community, and be connected with on- and off-campus resources throughout their educational journey. We provide virtual and in-person services to accommodate the needs of students.

USS professional staff are located in building 26, room 101. Through our programs and initiatives, we foster a sense of belonging, create opportunities for personal and professional growth, and empower students to thrive in an inclusive and supportive environment.

Cal Poly Pomona is a diverse community of individuals who represent many perspectives, beliefs and identities, committed to fostering an inclusive, respectful, and intellectually vibrant environment. All university programs and activities are open and available to all regardless of race, sex, color, ethnicity or national origin.
