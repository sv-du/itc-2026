# Ernest Prete Jr. Fellowship Program

#### **The application period is now OPEN.**

#### **Applications due by Friday, Sept. 12, 2025!**

[APPLY HERE](https://csupomona.academicworks.com/opportunities/17970)

---

##### The Prete Fellowship program is an opportunity for College of Science students to engage with students from an elementary school in our community, support the sustainability of urban gardening, gain an understanding of the expectations of teachers in a modern society, and explore if the teaching profession may match their career aspirations.
