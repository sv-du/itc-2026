## Executive Committee

**2025**

* August 27, 2025
* September 3, 10, and 17, 2025
* October 1, 8, 22, and 29, 2025
* November 12 and 19, 2025

**2026**

* January 21 and 28, 2026
* February 11 and 18, 2026
* March 4, 11, and 25, 2026
* April 8, 22, and 29, 2026

Meetings are held in Building 98, room P2-008 from 3:00 pm - 5:00 pm during the academic year, except on days when the Senate meets, academic holidays, and during final examinations. **The Executive Committee meetings maybe held via Zoom. Check with the Executive Committee Chair who will provide additional information regarding meeting dates and location.**
