# Transfer Students

## Begin Your Application

[### Fall 2026 Application

Application Filing Period: October 1- December 1. All admission requirements must be completed by the end of Spring 2026 (prior to enrollment at CPP).](https://www.calstate.edu/apply/transfer "opens a new window")

## Admissions Information

*Students that have completed coursework at a college or university since leaving high school, but have not yet completed a bachelor’s degree are considered transfer students.  Applicants must have 60 semester/90 quarter transferable units at a 4-year or community college.*

## New GE Requirements for Fall 2025

[### New GE Requirements: Fall 2025

The CSU board of trustees approved changes that will impact students attending CPP beginning with the fall 2025 incoming class. See below for more information.](https://www.calstate.edu/csu-system/administration/academic-and-student-affairs/academic-programs-innovation-and-faculty-development/program-development/Documents/csu-ge-comparison-trifold.pdf "opens a new window")

## Apply to Cal Poly Pomona

### Become a Bronco

Cal Poly Pomona is #2 in the west for public universities and #1 in the nation for economic mobility amongst polytechnic universities.  Our caring and inclusive campus community is committed to helping anyone who dreams of success achieve it. At CPP, you can explore different career paths, apply your knowledge to real-world situations, and gain the experience you need to succeed and stand out in your field from day one.
