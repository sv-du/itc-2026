# Awards & Accolades

![Horsehill Vineyards' award winning wines](https://www.cpp.edu/horsehill/img/zinfandel-2016-awards.jpg)

* 2018 Red Blend: **Silver Medal** - 2023 Los Angeles International Wine Competition
* 2018 Red Blend: **Gold Medal, Packaging & Design: A Cut Above** - 2023 Los Angeles International Wine Competition
* 2017 Zinfandel: **Bronze Medal, Zinfandel Category** - 2022 Los Angeles International Wine Competition
* 2017 Zinfandel: **Silver Medal,**  **Packaging Design: Color & Type**   - 2022 Los Angeles International Wine Competition
* 2016 Zinfandel:   **Silver Medal**  - 2019 OC Wine Competition
* 2016 Zinfandel:   **Bronze Medal**  - 2019 Los Angeles International Wine Competition
* 2016 Zinfandel:  **Silver Medal**   - 2018 Los Angeles International   Wine Competition
* 2016 Zinfandel:  **Honorable Mention**   - 2018 Inland Empire Magazine Wine Competition
* 2016 Zinfandel Rosé:  [**Gold Medal**](http://www.cpp.edu/~collins/news/zinfandel_rose.shtml) - 2017 Los Angeles International Wine Competition
* 2013 Rosanna:   **Silver Medal**  - Los Angeles International Wine Competition
* 2009 Zinfandel Rosé:    [**Gold Medal**](http://polycentric.cpp.edu/2010/06/calpolypomona_wine_second_gold/)  - 71st Annual Los Angeles International Wine Competition
* 2008 Rosé:    [**Best of Class Gold Medal**](http://polycentric.cpp.edu/2009/06/horsehill_vineyards_win_gold_bronze_medals/) - 70th Annual Los Angeles International Wine Competition, Best Rosé Limited Production Category
* 2008 Zinfandel:    [**Bronze Medal**](http://polycentric.cpp.edu/2009/06/horsehill_vineyards_win_gold_bronze_medals/) - 70th Annual Los Angeles International Wine Competition,   Limited Production Zinfandel from 2007 or Later Category
