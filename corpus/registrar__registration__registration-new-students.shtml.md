## FOR

### NEW STUDENTS

## Next Step - Register for Classes

![Profile of students register for classes](../img/new_student_registration.jpg)

Incoming Freshman and Transfer Students may begin to register for classes after they complete their orientation and academic advising, please refer to the [Student Success New Student Registration Guide](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/guides/new-student-registration.shtml) for more information.

![students studying in the classroom](../img/students_classroom.jpg)

## Undergraduate Students

## Registration Window

New freshman and transfer students will be able to register for their first term of classes at the [orientation session](https://www.cpp.edu/orientation/) that they attend. Students will be able to access [BroncoDirect](https://my.cpp.edu/uPortal/p/broncodirect.ctf1/max/render.uP) at designated periods during their orientation session. To ensure a trouble-free registration, new students are advised to submit documents required by the [Office of Admissions](https://www.cpp.edu/admissions/index.shtml) as early as possible before their scheduled orientation session.

![students studying in the classroom](../img/faculty-photo_11.png)

## Graduate Students

## Registration Window

The Registrar’s Office assigns fall and spring term incoming graduate students' registration window as reflected below.  To view your registration window, log in to your [BroncoDirect](https://my.cpp.edu/uPortal/p/broncodirect.ctf1/max/render.uP) account and click on Student Center under the Student Self Services menu. Please watch this [Enrollment Window how-to video](https://nam11.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.youtube.com%2Fwatch%3Fv%3DAEnASjuvZEU&data=05%7C02%7Cisam%40cpp.edu%7C4ba16c33e7f6424af76108dd51d76c28%7C164ba61e39ec4f5d89ffaa1f00a521b4%7C0%7C0%7C638756707849931715%7CUnknown%7CTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJFbXB0eU1hcGkiOnRydWUsIlYiOiIwLjAuMDAwMCIsIlAiOiJXaW4zMiIsIkFOIjoiTWFpbCIsIldUIjoyfQ%3D%3D%7C0%7C%7C%7C&sdata=y74JVkxEXh7VoTBCKsc3tSPulWmB%2F%2BudKWhQiyUkA0Q%3D&reserved=0) to check your registration window. Also, you may view:  [How to register for classes](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/guides/registration/register.shtml). If you have questions about which courses you should enroll in, please do not hesitate to contact your [Department Graduate Coordinators](https://www.cpp.edu/gradstudies/prospective-students/explore-our-grad-programs.shtml). To further assist you in your CPP journey, click on [resources and next steps](https://www.cpp.edu/gradstudies/current-students/new-graduate-students.shtml) for more information. Check the [Class Search](https://schedule.cpp.edu/) for Course Offerings. If you miss this general registration window, the next opportunity to register for classes will be during the term Add/Drop Period reflected below. Visit the [Registration Schedule](https://www.cpp.edu/registrar/registration/registration-schedule.shtml) page for more information.

Registration windows for new incoming graduate students are typically assigned as follows:

* **Spring semester:** Mid-October to the end of the Spring Add/Drop period
* **Summer term:** Mid-April to the end of the Summer Add/Drop period (*Credential and EDD programs only*)
* **Fall semester:** Mid-April to the end of the Fall Add/Drop period

For more details about the **Graduate Studies** resources, please visit the [Graduate Studies](https://www.cpp.edu/gradstudies/index.shtml#:~:text=GRADUATE%20STUDENT%20WELCOME%20ORIENTATION%20FOR%20FALL%202022&text=Please%20save%20the%20date%3A%20Saturday,Fall%202022%20Virtual%20Welcome%20Event.) website.
