# About the Scholarship for Service Program

The **CyberCorps®: Scholarship for Service (SFS)** program, funded by the **NSF** and co-sponsored by the **DHS**, aims to increase the number of cybersecurity professionals in government. The program provides full tuition and fees for up to three years, annual stipends, and a professional allowance.. Upon graduation, participants must work for a government agency in a cybersecurity role for a period equal to the length of their scholarship. **More information is available on [sfs.opm.gov.](https://sfs.opm.gov#sfs.opm.gov)**

**We are currently accepting applications until February 28th, 2026.**

## Scholarship Components

* Up to 3 years of support for undergraduate and graduate (MS or PhD) education.
* Academic-year stipends of $27,000 per year for undergraduate students and $37,000 per year for graduate students.
* Tuition and education-related fees (do not include items such as meal plans, housing, or parking).
* Professional allowance of $6,000 for SFS Job Fair and other travel, professional certification, etc.
* Participation in virtual and in-person job fairs in Washington, D.C.
* Post-graduation government service requirement for a period equivalent to the length of the scholarship.

## Student Eligibility

* Be within the final two years of an eligible cybersecurity degree program at Cal Poly Pomona at the time of acceptance.
* Have a minimum undergraduate GPA of 3.0 or a minimum graduate GPA of 3.2.
* Be a United States Citizen or Permanent Resident.
* Be eligible for federal employment.
* Participant in cybersecurity activities while attending Cal Poly Pomona.
