## Local Area Preference

Applicants are considered “local” if they graduated from a high school historically served by Cal Poly Pomona. First-time freshmen applying to impacted majors receive a "Local Preference" with an eligibility index bump based on our local area designation to enhance admission consideration. In addition, CPP grants "Local Preference" to all veterans.

## Local Area High Schools

## **Applicants who apply to non-impacted majors and are from our local area high schools and who meet the minimum [CSU eligibility requirements](https://www.cpp.edu/admissions/freshmen/requirements.shtml) will be admitted. Those applying to [impacted majors](https://www.cpp.edu/admissions/freshmen/impacted-majors.shtml) will receive a "local preference".**
