# Academics

## Colleges & Departments

Ranked among the top public universities in the western U.S., Cal Poly Pomona provides a rich academic experience steeped in hands-on learning in every program, course and activity on campus. The university features eight academic colleges and community access to traditional extended programs through the College of Professional and Global Education.
