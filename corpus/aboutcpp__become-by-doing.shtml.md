# Dreams of Success

## Learn. Do. Become.

Cal Poly Pomona is the #1 polytechnic university for diversity and economic mobility, where we help anyone who dreams of success achieve it. Here, every student gets The CPP Polytechnic Experience (PolyX) — hands-on learning inside and outside the classroom that positively impacts their careers and communities for generations to come. CPP students don’t just learn by doing. They become by doing. That’s why Cal Poly Pomona is where careers begin, mentorship thrives, and leaders learn to lead.
