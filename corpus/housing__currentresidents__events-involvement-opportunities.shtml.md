## Past Events from RSA and UHS

[See more photos from RSA and UHS events](https://www.flickr.com/photos/cppuhs/%20)

![Video Game Night](../documents/img/uhs-events/video-game-night.jpg)

### Mario Kart Tournament

Resident Student Association (RSA) gathered to bring fellow gamers together to host a fun, social video game program. Residents met fellow gamers and played on the Nintendo Switch while enjoying snacks and socializing.

![Group photo of residents](../documents/img/uhs-events/program-pictures/lnb-pictures.png)

### Late Night Breakfast

RSA hosted their Late Night Breakfast event for Housing residents. This event is held each seemster where residents enjoy breakfast food at night, play games, win prizes, and relax before finals.

![Hikers](../documents/img/uhs-events/hike-to-kellogg-22)

### Hike to Kellogg

Our Student Leader Event Leads took residents on a fun, relaxing on-campus hike to Kellogg House. Students enjoyed a leisure walk discovering this hidden gem of a trail, one of many Cal Poly Pomona has to offer.

![Yoga Night](../documents/img/uhs-events/yoga-night-with-bric)

### Yoga with the BRIC

Students took a moment to relax, stretch, and meditate during midterms. The Student Leader Event Lead team collaborated with ASI to bring a BRIC (Bronco Recreation and Intramural Complex) Yoga Instructor to Housing to lead a class.

## RSA Eboard Positions

IHC Eboard is elected each school year and falls under University Housing Student Leader positions. Any resident can run for an eboard position.

### President

RSA President oversees the entire Resident Student Assocation.

**Position Requirements**

* 2.0 or higher semester & cumulative GPA
* Must be in good judicial standing with UHS & CPP
* Must be enrolled as a full-time student at CPP for the 2025-2026 Academic Year
* Must complete Bronco Leadership Center Registration Education process

### Vice President

Also known as the NACURH Communications Coordinator (NCC), this position oversees conference planning logistics with NACURH (National Affiliate of College and University Residence Halls) and for regional conferences with PACURH (Pacific Affiliate of College and University Residence Halls). In this position the NCC represents RSA at monthly Regional Chats and ensures the council is up to date on all communications within NACURH.

**Position Requirements**

* 2.0 or higher semester & cumulative GPA
* Must be in good judicial standing with UHS & CPP
* Must be enrolled as a full-time student at CPP for the 2025-2026 Academic Year
* Must complete Bronco Leadership Center Registration Education process

### Director of Administration and Finance (Treasurer)

The Director of Administration and Finance (Treasurer) oversees RSAs budget, meeting minutes, and other administrative requests.

**Position Requirements**

* 2.0 or higher semester & cumulative GPA
* Must be in good judicial standing with UHS & CPP
* Must be enrolled as a full-time student at CPP for the 2025-2026 Academic Year
* Must complete Bronco Leadership Center Registration Education process

### Director of Media and Public Relations Coordinator

The Media and Public Relations Coordinator (MPRC) focuses marketing, design, and social media for RSA.

**Position Requirements**

* 2.0 or higher semester & cumulative GPA
* Must be in good judicial standing with UHS & CPP
* Must be enrolled as a full-time student at CPP for the 2025-2026 Academic Year

### Director of Social Programming

The Director of Social Programming (Event Planner/Scheduler) oversees all programming efforts for Inter-Hall Council. They work alongside the DAA (Director of Administrative Affairs) to stay within their programming budget, reach out to campus partners for collaborative programming, and guide the council on program planning and initiatives.

**Position Requirements**

* 2.0 or higher semester & cumulative GPA
* Must be in good judicial standing with UHS & CPP
* Must be enrolled as a full-time student at CPP for the 2025-2026 Academic Year
* Must complete Bronco Leadership Center Registration Education process

### Director of Cultural Engagement

The Director of Cultural Engagement (Event Planner) oversees all cultural programming efforts alongisde UHS' Equity, Diversity, and Inclusion Coordinator. This position focuses on collaboration with campus departments and partners.

**Position Requirements**

* 2.0 or higher semester & cumulative GPA
* Must be in good judicial standing with UHS & CPP
* Must be enrolled as a full-time student at CPP for the 2025-2026 Academic Year
* Must complete Bronco Leadership Center Registration Education process

### Associated Students, Inc. Resident Student Association Senator-at-Large

The ASI Senator-at-Large of IHC is a representative that sits on the executive board and participates in eboard and general meetings. They serve as a liason between the Resident Student Association (RSA) and Associated Students Inc. (ASI).

**[Visit ASI CPP for Position Requirements](https://asi.cpp.edu/student-government/get-involved/become-a-student-leader/elected-positions/)**
