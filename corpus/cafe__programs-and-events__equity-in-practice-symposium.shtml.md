# Equity in Practice Symposium

![Equity in Practice Symposium](/cafe/img/events/equity-in-practice/equity-in-practice-symposium.jpg)

## About the “Equity in Practice” Symposium

The [Center for the Advancement for Faculty Excellence (CAFE)](../index.shtml), the  [Office of Student Success, Equity and Innovation (OSS)](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/oss/), and [Institutional Research, Planning, and Analytics (IRPA)](https://www.cpp.edu/data/) invite the campus community to join us at the Equity in Practice Symposium. We will celebrate CPP's progress towards student success, share effective practices, and spread the word about campus resources. We’ll also present the [Wall of COOL 2024](../cool/index.shtml) awards!

**When:**Friday, April 19, 2024 **Where:** In person at [Kellogg West](https://www.cpp.edu/maps/?id=1130#!m/276537)

![](../img/general/divider.png)

## Agenda at a Glance

**📅 Symposium Date: Friday, April 19, 2024**

| Time | Item or Session |
| --- | --- |
| 8:00 – 9:00 | Breakfast & Networking |
| 9:00 – 9:15 | Plenary Welcome: CAFE, OSS, and IRPA |
| 9:15 – 9:45 | Plenary Speaker Desdemona Cardoza, CSU Chancellor's Office: *The Course Equity Portal: A Tool to Inspire Faculty to Create More Inclusive and Equitable Learning Environments for All Students* |
| 10:00 – 11:30 | Faculty & Staff Concurrent Session Presentations: 20-Minute Talks and 5-Minute Lightning Talks |
| 11:30 – 12:45 | Lunch |
| 12:00 – 12:30 | CAFE’s Wall of COOL Presentations over Lunch |
| 1:00 – 2:00 | Faculty & Staff Poster Session |
| 2:15 – 3:00 | Plenary Speaker David Webb, Dept. of Physics & Astronomy UC Davis: *Course Structure for Equity*, based on [Webb & Paul (2023)](https://journals.aps.org/prper/abstract/10.1103/PhysRevPhysEducRes.19.020126) |
| 3:15 – 3:30 | Call to Action and Conclusion: CAFE, OSS, and IRPA |
| 3:45 – finish | Informal Gathering at Innovation Brewworks (No-host bar) |
