# Resources

## Resources

Articles, modules, workshops and more to assist you in mentoring undergraduate researchers.

Questions? Contact Dr. Winny Dong (winnydong@cpp.edu).

![](../../img/getting-started/faculty.jpg)

##### Faculty Mentoring Handbook

Handbook created by the Office of Undergraduate Research to teach you about Faculty Mentoring.

[View the Handbook](https://livecsupomona.sharepoint.com/%3Ab%3A/r/sites/our_team/Shared%20Documents/OUR%20Websites%20and%20Online%20Programs/Faculty%20Mentoring%20Handbook.pdf?csf=1&web=1&e=cUy79F "View the Handbook. ")

![](../../img/getting-started/studentresources.jpg)

##### Getting Started in Research

A guide created by the OUR to assist undergrads starting their research journey.

[View the Resources](https://www.cpp.edu/our-cpp/students/getting-started.shtml "View the Resources. ")

![](../../img/events/workshops/mentorstudent.jpg)

##### Faculty Mentoring 101 with Dr. Winny Dong and Isabella Hoang

Learn more about mentoring with a focus on diversity, equity, and inclusion.

[View the Workshop](https://streaming.cpp.edu/media/Faculty%20Mentoring%20101/1_zxgshccu "View the Workshop. ")

![](../../img/getting-started/mentoring.jpg)

##### Salient Practices of Undergraduate Research Mentors
