![A student works with test tubes](img/hero.png)

![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white-mobile.svg)
![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white.svg)

## About

### Academic Advising for Careers in Healthcare

Are you interested in pursuing a career in healthcare after getting your bachelor's degree from CPP? The Pre-Health Professional Advising team can help you explore your options, make an academic plan, and gain experience to apply for professional programs in medicine, dentistry, veterinary medicine and more.

![Two veterinary students work with a dog](img/feature.png)

[### Current Students: CPP Connect

Make an appointment to see a Pre-Professional Health advisor.](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/cppconnect/index.shtml "opens a new window")

### Visit Us in Person

Our office is in the Student Services Building, First Floor (Blue Counter). We are open weekdays 8 a.m. to 5 p.m.

### Email Us

Email us at prehealth@cpp.edu. Please email us from your CPP email address and include your Bronco ID number in your message.
