## IMPORTANT DEADLINES​

![Student working with a plane prototype](../img/admissions-aid/22uav-lab-cha2.jpg)

### Undergrad Applications

Open Oct 1 – Dec 1, 2025 for fall 2026 start

![Students working with Professor in front of their laptops](../img/admissions-aid/fogle-elisha21.jpg)

### Spring Transfer Applications

Open Aug 1 – Aug 31, 2025 for spring 2026 start

![student setting on their desks in a lecture](../img/admissions-aid/22first-day-robinson6.jpg)

### FAFSA

2026-27 Free Application for Federal Student Aid now available through March 2, 2026.\*

![student speaking at a lecture](../img/admissions-aid/22edd-classes15.jpg)

### Graduate Applications

Open Oct 1, 2025 for fall 2026 / ​Open Aug 1, 2025 for spring 2026

![Adult talking to a child](../img/admissions-aid/project-renaissancespelling.jpg)

### Credential Applications​

Open Oct 1, 2025 for both summer or fall 2026 start

![student cheering at a graduation ceremony ](../img/admissions-aid/24science-two-grad_29.jpg)

### CADAA

Priority filing deadline for California Dream Act students in the 2026-27 year is March 2, 2026.\*

## Note:

\* **Students residing in Los Angeles and Ventura counties** have a state priority deadline of April 2, 2025, for the 2025-26 FAFSA and CADAA due to the recent wildfires.
