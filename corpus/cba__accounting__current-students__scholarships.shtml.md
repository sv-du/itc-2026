# Scholarships

## Annual Recognition Scholarship

Accounting majors at Cal Poly Pomona have a yearly opportunity, in the spring, to apply for consideration of a scholarship, donated by a local accounting firm or organization and presented at the Annual Student Recognition Night.   Applications are done online through Financial Aid.
