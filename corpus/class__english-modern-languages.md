# English & Modern Languages

![Entrance to CPP building 24, where EML is on the second floor](img/cpp-bldg-24.jpg)

## Welcome!

We are an integrated department of languages, linguistics, literature, and writing. EML trains students in the academic disciplines dealing with language itself, the medium of all social and cultural life.
