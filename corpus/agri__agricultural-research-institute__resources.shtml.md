## ARI Resources

Learn what you need to do to submit a proposal for research funding from ARI.

* [2026-2027 Guidelines (Request for Proposals)](https://csuari.infoready4.com/PlatformServicesV2/Commons/getDocument/FY%202026-27_Request%20for%20Proposals.pdf?documentName=d56cab55-125e-4b3b-8fd8-dd2a48573fe6.pdf&applicationId=7)
* [Policies & Procedures](https://csuari.infoready4.com/PlatformServicesV2/Commons/getDocument/FY%202025-26%20ARI%20Policies%20and%20Procedures%20Manual%20%28PPM%29.pdf?documentName=5e0003d6-8c1d-4bd6-9cf1-5b38a8f0ac89.pdf&applicationId=7)

Please see the [Request for Proposals](https://csuari.infoready4.com/PlatformServicesV2/Commons/getDocument/FY%202026-27_Request%20for%20Proposals.pdf?documentName=d56cab55-125e-4b3b-8fd8-dd2a48573fe6.pdf&applicationId=7) for guidelines and instructions for information on the topics listed below.
