# Canvas Frequently Asked Questions

## Can a faculty member or staff member get an empty course shell created just to mess around with?

Yes. We call them “sandboxes.” Just email canvas@cpp.edu and request it.

## What does Canvas have?

Canvas has all the features of a typical learning management system including the ability to accept and grade assignments, conduct discussions in forums, administer quizzes and exams, store and organize course resources, communicate with students, etc. Canvas has multiple grading tools including rubrics, audio/video feedback, and a dedicated grading app. Students can access course materials, view course calendars, to-do lists and set their own preferences to receive notifications on mobile devices or laptop/desktops. Canvas has a course calendar that automatically displays assignments and events from all courses in one place. These calendar entries can be synced with other web service calendars such as Outlook.

## Can I use my Bronco login with Canvas?

Yes. Your Bronco credentials will be used to log in to Canvas. You sign into Canvas at [canvas.cpp.edu](https://canvas.cpp.edu)or via [MyCPP](https://my.cpp.edu).

## Are there mobile apps for Canvas?

Yes. Instructors and TA’s can download the Canvas Teacher App for [iPhone, iPad](https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/canvas-teacher/id1257834464?mt=8) or [Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.instructure.teacher) devices, while students can download the [Canvas iOS Student App](https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/canvas-by-instructure/id480883488?mt=8) for iPhone and iPad or [Android Canvas App](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.instructure.candroid&hl=en).

## Can CAFE do a Canvas workshop for our department faculty or staff?

You bet!

Please contact us through our [request form](https://www.cpp.edu/cafe/about-us/request-services.shtml), indicating some days and times that would work for your department.

## How do faculty get help with Canvas?

• Email canvas@cpp.edu
• Contact Studio 6 at 909-869-3099 or by email at studio6@cpp.edu. See the Studio 6 website for our current hours of operation.

## How do students get help with Canvas?

Canvas has lots of student-facing resources through their [Canvas Student Guides](https://community.canvaslms.com/t5/Canvas-Student/ct-p/canvas_student). Your professor can also help you. If your professor can’t help you, please contact the [IT Service Desk](https://cpp.service-now.com/ehelp?id=kb_article_view&sysparm_article=KB0001064&sys_kb_id=e0618355474ac110230f5a5c346d432e&spa=1).
