# Customer Insights Consultancy (CIC) Program Synopsis

![CIC](../../img/internship/cic-member.png)

*A Real World Project Driven Class over two semesters, Fall and Spring.*

Since 2012, students who have taken our consulting project-driven classes have been hired by world-class insights and analytics companies such as Kantar Research and Materials Plus, in part, thanks to the hands-on nature of the program (to see which companies hired our alumni, go to our [placement record](https://www.cpp.edu/cba/customer-insights-lab/student-success/outcomes/placement-record.shtml)). For this program, we will select a small number of promising students (see [former participants](https://www.cpp.edu/cba/customer-insights-lab/student-success/cic/cic-alum.shtml)) to consult our paying clients (see [past clients](https://www.cpp.edu/cba/customer-insights-lab/industry-partnership/services-to-business-community.shtml)). Outstanding students with strong detail-orientation and excellent communication skills are strongly encouraged to contact the professors with (1) resume with GPA and (2) a cover letter stating why taking this class will enhance your career preparation. Applicants will be vetted and admitted based on merit.

## Additional Program Information

### Who should take this program?

* CBA students whose primary emphasis are Data Analytics, Business Intelligence, Consumer Insights and Analytics, and Marketing Research are especially welcome.
* All CBA majors and minors with a minimum GPA of 3.0 (preference will be given to 3.4 and above).
* Anyone who wants to expand knowledge in data analytics, entrepreneurship, market research, and consulting.

### What can you gain from taking the program?

* Learn the consulting process from start to finish, including proposal development,  insight generation from data, and generation of actionable recommendations.
* Gain experience with questionnaire design, data wrangling and visualization, modeling, and storytelling.
* Learn software such as Qualtrics, R, RStudio and many packaged needed, SPSS, and Excel’s advanced functions
* Prepare for a job involving some aspects of market research, data science, new product development, pricing strategy, promotions, and consulting.
* Interaction with professionals and learn professionalism.
* Invaluable consulting experience that you can bring to job interviews.

### How will the class be conducted?

* Both Dr. Stein and Dr. Jung will be in both classes, teaching students customized content.
* No textbook. All course materials will be provided through Canvas, customized for the project.
* The professors will guide students through forming the goals of the project, selecting the analytics method, designing a study, collecting data, analyzing data, and creating a final presentation slide.
* Class time is T/H 1:00–2:15 pm during Fall and Spring.
* Students will register for IBM 4092 (Advanced Marketing Research) and IBM 4952 (Marketing Research Practicum).
* **Prerequisites**: IBM 3012 (Principles of Marketing Management) and TOM 3020 (Foundations for Business Analytics).

### What do I do if I’m interested in enrolling the class?

* **Step 1**: Email both Dr. Randy Stein (rbstein@cpp.edu) and Dr. Jung (jmjung@cpp.edu) an application packet consisting of (1) your resume with your GPA and (2) a cover letter stating why taking this class will enhance your career preparation
* **Step 2**: We will contact you to set up an interview late this week or next week.
* **Step 3**: Once you are admitted into the program, you will enroll in IBM4092 and IBM4990 for fall, and IBM4952 and IBM4990 in the spring. All enrollments must be approved by [Dr. Stein](https://www.cpp.edu/cba/customer-insights-lab/about/member/stein-page.shtml) and [Dr. Jung](https://www.cpp.edu/cba/customer-insights-lab/about/member/jung-page.shtml)
