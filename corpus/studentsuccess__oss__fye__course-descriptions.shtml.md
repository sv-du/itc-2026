# Course Options

## Course Options by College

### Agriculture

* AGS 1000
* AHS 1104
* AVS 1000
* AMM 1000
* FST 1000
* NTR 1000
* PLT 1000

### Business Administration

* BUS 1010

### Education and Integrative Studies

* IGE 1020
* LS 1020

### Engineering

* EGR 1000/1000L

### Environmental Design

* ARC 1010/1010L
* ARC1020/1022A
* LA 1771
* RS 1110
* URP 1040/1040L
* URP 1050
* VCD 1321A

### Hospitality Management

* HRT 1010

### Letters, Arts and Social Sciences

* CLS 1101/1101A
* ENG 1000
* EWS 1020
* MU 1040
* TH 2030

### Science

* SCI 1010/1010A and SCI 1020A
* SCI 1100/1100A and 1110A

### Alternative options for students that are not part of a college/major that offers a FYE course:

* LIB 1500
* CPU 1100
