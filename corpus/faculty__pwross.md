## Recent Scholarly Activities

* "[Spectrum Inversion](ross-spectrum-inversion-routledge-handbook-of-phil-of-color.pdf)," in D. H. Brown and F. Macpherson (eds.), *The Routledge Handbook of Philosophy of Colour*, Abington, Oxon, UK:  Routledge, 2021, 226-243.
* "[Phenomenal Externalism's Explanatory Power](ross-phenomenal-externalisms-explanatory-power-ppr.docx)," *Philosophy and Phenomenological Research*, XCVII, 3 (November 2018): 613-630.
* "[What the Mind-Independence of Color Requires](ross-what-the-mind-independence-of-color-requires.docx)," in M. Silva (ed.), *How Colour Matters for Philosophy,* Cham, Switzerland: Springer International Publishing AG, 2017, 137-158.
* “[Primary and Secondary Qualities](ross-primary-and-secondary-qualities-oxford-handbook-of-phil-of-perception.doc),” *The Oxford Handbook of Philosophy**of Perception*, Mohan Matthen (ed.), Oxford: Oxford University Press, 2015, 405-421.
* “[Existence Problems in Philosophy and Science](ross-and-turner-existence-problems-in-philosophy-and-science-synthese.doc),” *Synthese*, 190, 18 (December 2013):  4239-4259. Co-authored with Dale Turner.
* "[Perceived Colors and Perceived Locations: A Problem for Color Subjectivism](ross-perceived-colors-and-perceived-locations-apq.pdf),"  *American Philosophical Quarterly*, 49, 2 (April 2012):  125-138.
* [Review of  *The Senses: Classic and Contemporary Philosophical Perspectives*](http://ndpr.nd.edu/news/25597-the-senses-classic-and-contemporary-philosophical-perspectives/), editor Fiona Macpherson,  *Notre Dame Philosophical Reviews*, 2011.08.24.
* "[Fitting Color into the Physical World](ross-fitting-color-into-the-physical-world-phil-psych.pdf)," *Philosophical Psychology*, 23, 5 (October 2010): 575-599.
* "[Common Sense about Qualities and Senses](ross-common-sense-about-qualities-and-senses-phil-studies.pdf)," *Philosophical Studies*, 138, 3 (April 2008): 299-316.
