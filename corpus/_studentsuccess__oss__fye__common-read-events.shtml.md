## 2025-26 Common Read

### I Never Thought of It That Way by Mónica Guzmán

In this thoughtful and practical book, journalist and author Mónica Guzmán provides a practical guide and toolkit to navigate conversations and connect despite differences. With real-world examples and everyday tools, Guzmán encourages readers to listen actively, stay curious, and remain engaged with the world around them, especially when faced with differing perspectives. The book was nominated by Jason Wang, visitor experience counselor in the Office of Outreach, Recruitment and Educational Partnerships, who appreciated how its practical insights support thoughtful conversations. He believes the book offers meaningful support for first-year students as they encounter new perspectives and adjust to college life.

## Join us for the Common Read Curiosity Workshop

Mónica Guzmán will host a workshop for faculty and staff on using curiosity to navigate productive disagreement. Through interactive exercises and reflection, participants will build skills in listening with curiosity. Space is limited, register today!
