# Kellogg Honors College Application

## Why Join the Kellogg Honors College?

The Kellogg Honors College (KHC) is a community of scholars at Cal Poly Pomona where you’ll explore big ideas, engage in meaningful discussions, and connect with faculty mentors who care about your success. As a KHC student, you’ll take exclusive honors courses, participate in civic engagement, and gain access to special events that enhance your learning experience. Whether you're an incoming freshman, a first-year student finishing your first year at CPP, or a transfer student, KHC provides an inclusive and supportive environment where you can challenge yourself and thrive.

To apply, you must first be admitted to Cal Poly Pomona (CPP) and meet the eligibility criteria for your student type.

The application process for incoming first-year, second-year, and non-HTCC-affiliated transfer applicants includes submitting a cover letter, essay, and resume. Applications submitted in Round 2 are typically reviewed in early June, with decisions released shortly thereafter.

### First-Time Freshmen & Sophomores

For incoming first-year and second-year students, there are two rounds of applications each year:

* Round 1: January to March
* Round 2: April to May

### [First-time freshmen & Sophomore Application](https://cpp.az1.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_9StIL3uwLjtZapo) Transfer Students

The Kellogg Honors College (KHC) accepts applications from CPP transfer students year-round. The Transfer Application includes two pathways within the same form:

1. Honors-to-Honors Transfer Pathway – for students transferring from an HTCC-affiliated honors program (may qualify for automatic admission).
2. General Transfer Application – for students transferring from a non-HTCC-affiliated community college.

Transfer applicants may apply at any time using the following link:

[KHC Transfer Student Application](https://cpp.az1.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_4U8bQbmgIbLaoF8)

### Application Status

The application cycle for incoming first-year and second-year students is currently open. Application link is located above and below.

For incoming first-year and second-year students:

**Round 1:** Open from January 2, 2026, to March 2, 2026. Application decisions should be released approximately by March 31, 2026. **Secure a spot early!**

**Round 2:** Open from April 1, 2026, to May 4, 2026. Application decisions should be released approximately by June 1, 2026.

Applications for transfer students remain open year-round.

## Honors-to-Honors Transfer (HTCC & KHC)

For students in community college honors programs who are members of the Honors Transfer Council of California (HTCC) you may transfer directly into KHC. All you need is to be accepted into CPP, an unofficial transcript, and an HTCC Report of Student Participation in Honors Form from your transferring institution. Find the links below to submit your paperwork and welcome to CPP and KHC!

* [KHC application form for HTCC members](https://cpp.az1.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_4U8bQbmgIbLaoF8)
* [HTCC Report of Student Participation in Honors Form PDF](documents/honors-transfer-form.pdf)
* [List of eligible partner Community Colleges](https://www.honorstransfercouncil.org/membership)

## Application Tracks:

### First-Year Freshman

Must be admitted to Cal Poly Pomona. No minimum GPA to apply, but you must maintain a 3.30 GPA once admitted.

### Sophomore Students

Must have started at CPP as a first-year freshman in Fall 2025 and expected to have a minimum 3.30 GPA and 24 completed units at CPP by Spring 2026.

### Transfer Students

Must apply before starting their first semester at CPP. No minimum GPA to apply, but you must maintain a 3.30 GPA. We accept applications year-round. Apply today!

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What are the benefits of joining the Kellogg Honors College?

* Access to exclusive **honors courses** with small class sizes.
* Priority registration and personalized academic advising.
* Opportunities for **research, internships, and leadership development**.
* A **Capstone Project** requirement that enhances academic and career growth.

### Will being in KHC help my chances of getting into Cal Poly Pomona?

* No, admission to CPP and KHC are **separate processes**.

### Are there scholarships for KHC students?

* Yes! Scholarships are available through the **Bronco Scholarship Application**. Visit the [Office of Financial Aid & Scholarships](https://www.cpp.edu/financial-aid/scholarships/cal-poly-pomona-scholarships.shtml) for details.

### Does KHC charge a fee?

* No, applying and being a member of KHC is **free**.

### What happens if I don’t maintain the GPA requirement?

* Students must maintain a **3.30 GPA** each semester to stay in good standing.

### What are honors courses?

* These are specially designed CPP courses with a maximum of **20 students**, fostering discussion-based learning and stronger faculty connections.
* Most **honors courses** fulfill **General Education (GE) requirements** but not major core courses.

### What are the academic requirements to stay in KHC?

* Maintain a **3.30 GPA**.
* Complete **five honors courses** before graduation (including a Capstone Project).
* Participate in **civic engagement** activities.
* Present your **Capstone Project** at the KHC Convocation.

### Can I apply to KHC as a current CPP student?

* If you started at CPP as a **first-year freshman in Fall 2024**, you can apply through the **Sophomore Application**.

### Can transfer students apply?

* Yes, but **only before their first semester at CPP**.
* Current CPP transfer students **cannot** apply.
