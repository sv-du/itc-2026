# Years of Service Recognition Ceremony

## Celebrating Faculty and Staff in 2025

Each year, Cal Poly Pomona hosts the Years of Service Recognition Ceremony to celebrate the milestone anniversaries of our outstanding faculty and staff. The 2025 ceremony will be held on Monday, Aug. 18 from 10 to 11:30 a.m. in Ursa Major, Bronco Student Center.

[View Recipient List](https://www.cpp.edu/eoda-hr/departments/csc/service-awards.shtml)
