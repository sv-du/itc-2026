# Outstanding Advising Partner Award

## About This Award

![Photo of Terri Gomez, Jinah Young, Jason Shieh, and Cecilia Santiago-Gonzalez](../img/partner.png)

## Selection Process

Outstanding Advising Partners will fulfill at an exceptional level, in a manner consistent with their position, criteria from the following list:

* Exceptional partnership and collaboration with members of the advising community to support student success
* Dedication to eliminating institutional barriers to student success and progress to degree improve the interpretation, communication and implementation of policies, procedures, and information to assist students to make well-informed academic decisions
* Contribute to creating innovative solutions to students' problems
* Maintain clear lines of communication with those who have responsibility and authority for decisions regarding academic advising.

## Nomination Process

Submit your nomination [through our form](https://cpp.az1.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_02mnefraCgouyB8). Nominations for 2025-26 are open until Friday, March 13.
