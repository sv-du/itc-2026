**Sabbatical leave** proposals will be submitted this cycle using InfoReady (an online platform).

* [How to Complete Your Sabbatical Application in InfoReady (pptx)](../documents/how-to-complete-your-sabbatical-application-in-infoready-mjs-updated-4th-screen%2C-5th-slide-re-apply-button-4.pptx)
* To access InfoReady, please go to: [https://cpp.infoready4.com/](https://nam11.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fcpp.infoready4.com%2F&data=02%7C01%7Cmmslavin%40cpp.edu%7Cf5074bba728b49427ee608d85c0490cc%7C164ba61e39ec4f5d89ffaa1f00a521b4%7C0%7C0%7C637360522473961236&sdata=DR0cZm73isjF%2F9VR5y6kixs4ha0fLkSJaMtZGlW1otA%3D&reserved=0)

*Please note:  If you have completed the word version of the sabbatical application, you can cut and paste your info. in the fields/textboxes in InfoReady, but you cannot upload or transfer the whole form into InfoReady.*

**Difference-in-Pay (DIP) leave** proposals will continue to be submitted via email to the Department Chair. The remaining steps will continue via email (Department Leave Committee, Dean, Faculty Affairs).
