# CEIS Student Success Center

![Students walking outside of CEIS building](img/ceis-largehero.png)

## About Us

We help undergraduate College of Education and Integrative Studies students with:

* General Academic Advising & Course Selection (GE & Core)
* Registration and Graduation Questions
* Petition Assistance & Information
* Degree Progress Review (DPR) Review
* Transfer Credit Review (TCR) Review
* Graduation Pledge Advising
* Academic Planner Review
* Academic Warning, Academic Notice, and Academic Notice with Contract Advising
* Resource Referral

## Get In Touch With Us

[### Schedule An Appointment on CPP Connect

We offer appointments virtually or in-person. Appointments are scheduled in 30-minute increments with your assigned advisor. Contact your advisor if you need to switch from an in-person appointment to a virtual appointment after scheduling. If you are more than 10 minutes late, you will need to reschedule. Appointments can be scheduled no more than 2 weeks into the future. If we are fully booked, please check on Friday afternoons for new appointments and/or check for cancellations.](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/cppconnect/ "opens a new window")

[### Attend Drop-In Hours

Drop-ins with advisors are for quick questions. Log in to CPP Connect to find our hours and location.](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/cppconnect/ "opens a new window")

### Email Us

Email ceisadvising@cpp.edu. Email from your CPP email address and include your Bronco ID number in your message.

[### Visit Us

Our center is in Building 94, room 275. Check in at our main office.](https://www.cpp.edu/maps?id=1130#!m/276501?share "opens a new window")
