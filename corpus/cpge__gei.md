## GEI Highlights

* Building and developing international Institutional Partnerships
* Offering high-quality programs for international educators, students, and working professionals
* Hosting 50+ visiting delegations each year
* Organizing 10+ university promotional events annually to enhance CPP’s prestige globally
* Delivering 50+ high-quality programs annually with personalized services to program participants

## GEI Strengths

### Experienced and Devoted Team Members

* The team has more than 30 years of experience and expertise in the field of international program development and partnership building
* Team members are diversified, devoted and committed

### High Quality Programs

* Programs with rich contents, frontier information and practical knowledge
* “Learn by doing” philosophy
* Academic seminars plus field trips

### Personalized Services

* Culturally-relevant activities
* Customized programs to meet the clients’ specific needs
* Considerate logistical arrangements including airport pick-up, guided tours, etc.
