### Visit Reflection Form

This private form is for your personal reflection after your visit with the Ombuds. It’s intended to help you process and apply insights once you’ve returned to the environment where the issue arose — such as your workplace, classroom or group setting. This worksheet is yours alone. Fill it in and download/print it to use during your visit to assess how the situation looks now, whether any changes have occurred, and what adjustments or follow-up might be helpful.](https://livecsupomona-my.sharepoint.com/personal/mhpatterson_cpp_edu/_layouts/15/onedrive.aspx?id=%2Fpersonal%2Fmhpatterson%5Fcpp%5Fedu%2FDocuments%2FAttachments%2FOmbuds%5FVisit%5FReflection%5FForm%20v2%201%2Ehtml&parent=%2Fpersonal%2Fmhpatterson%5Fcpp%5Fedu%2FDocuments%2FAttachments&ct=1752191105812&or=OWA%2DNT%2DMail&cid=fdcc0160%2D481c%2D6893%2D182c%2D5de0cf391874&ga=1)
