# Narcan Locations on Campus

## **Below is a list of the departments where Narcan can be found on campus in the event Narcan is needed for an emergency.**

List of Departments & Locations

| Department | Location |
| --- | --- |
| BRIC | Building 42 |
| Bronco Student Center | Building 35 |
| Collins College | Building 79 |
| Care Center | Building 97 |
| Counseling and Psychological Services | Building 66 |
| Student Health and Wellness Services | Building 46 |
| University Housing | Various locations |
| University Library | Building 15 |
| University Police | Building 109 |
| University Village | Building 200 |

Departments who would like to have Narcan in their area, can complete the department narcan request form.

[Department Narcan Request Form](https://forms.office.com/Pages/ResponsePage.aspx?id=HqZLFuw5XU-J_6ofAKUhtKyxaQXRgR1OqnDYsSfrIJFUMDhRVTBCSTU0SE5RV09ERUpISzkwNzdJTyQlQCN0PWcu)

Faculty, staff and students who would to obtain a box of free Narcan for their own use, complete the narcan distribution program training.

[Narcan Distribution Program Training](https://forms.office.com/pages/responsepage.aspx?id=HqZLFuw5XU-J_6ofAKUhtKyxaQXRgR1OqnDYsSfrIJFUQ01aNzRBSEdTQkxLU1ZPTlc5NERIWkdSRSQlQCN0PWcu)
