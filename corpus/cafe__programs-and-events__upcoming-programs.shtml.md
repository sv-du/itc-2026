### Resources for New Lecturers

×

#### Getting classes ready

**If lecturers want to begin preparing courses in Canvas before they have access to CPP’s Canvas, they can do so:** Go to [Instructure.com](https://www.instructure.com/) and sign up for a free teacher account. Build courses there, then when ready download the course package and upload it to CPP Canvas (CAFE Canvas team can help with this).

#### Using Canvas

* **CAFE’s self-enrollable [Introduction to Teaching with Canvas](https://canvas.cpp.edu/courses/39147):** A basic introduction for faculty who are new to using Canvas. Learn to maintain a current syllabus, maintain an up-to-date gradebook, communicate with students, and provide instructional materials that are accessible to students with disabilities. *Requires CPP login.*
* **LinkedIn Learning [“Learning Canvas”](https://www.linkedin.com/learning/learning-canvas-2022/create-and-manage-courses-with-canvas):** A basic introduction for faculty who are new to using Canvas. Learn how to set up a class, add assignments, grade in Canvas, communicate with students, add multimedia, and leverage Canvas for student success. *Requires CPP login or a LinkedIn Learning account.*
* **CAFE’s [Canvas support](../teaching-and-learning/lms-support/index.shtml) page:** Video playlists, tutorials, and CAFE contact information. *Does not require CPP login.*
* **canvas@cpp.edu:** Send a request for Canvas help or consultation; a member of CAFE will respond

#### Effective Teaching Strategies

* **[Getting Started: Basics of Course Design](https://www.cpp.edu/cafe/teaching-and-learning/getting-started.shtml):** No matter what format you are teaching in -- face to face, hybrid, synchronous, asynchronous, hyflex, or something else -- backwards design is the way to create an effective course.

#### Getting Help in General

* **Everybody's Mentor**: Lisa Kessler, CAFE faculty associate, provides individual, confidential consultation and coaching. Email Lisa to set up an appointment.
* Get help Mon-Fri 8:00am-5:00pm via [CAFE’s Studio 6](../about-us/studio-6.shtml) at 909-869-3099

### Fundamentals of Accessibility

×

All students should have the joy of learning, the freedom to grow, and the benefits of education! Accessibility of course materials and Universal Design for Learning helps make that vision a reality. In this self-paced, self-enrollable, certificate course get background and practical strategies, and also develop empathy for the needs of all students.
