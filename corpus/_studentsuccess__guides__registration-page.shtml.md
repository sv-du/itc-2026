# Cal Poly Pomona Registration Guide

## Term Information: Spring 2026

[More Dates and Deadlines](../calendar/index.shtml)

### October 13

Advising Period Begins

### October 13-14

Priority Registration

### October 15

Student Parent Priority Registration

### October 16-December 3

General Registration

### January 5-30

Add/Drop Period

### January 17

First Day of Classes

## Class Standings

### Freshmen

0-29 completed units

### Sophomores

30-59 completed units

### Juniors

60-89 completed units

### Seniors

90+ completed units

## Unit Enrollment Limits

### Priority/General

16 units

### Add/Drop Period

18 units

### Waitlisted Units

10 units (waitlisted units are included in your overall term unit limit)
