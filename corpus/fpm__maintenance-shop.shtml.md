# Maintenance/Carpenter Shop

## About

![](img/commencement-stage.jpg)

The Maintenance/Carpenter Shop provides responsive services to the campus that are of light construction as well as small repairs and installations. The services they provide include installation, repair, and routine maintenance of public spaces, including walls, floors, ceilings and doors, plus all service calls that are generated by the campus community.

**What We Do**

Services include but are not limited to:

* Baseboard, door, drywall and plaster repairs
* Ceiling tile replacement and repair
* Restroom partitions
* Floor tile repair and replacement in public spaces
* Replacement and/or repair of vinyl floors, wooden floors and other floor surfaces in public spaces
* Mirror replacements in restrooms and public spaces
* Wooden railings and steps repair and replacement
* Stair tread, guard replacements, safety strips
* Window and glass repairs on building exteriors
* Roof leak repairs
* Pothole and concrete repairs
* Install white boards in classrooms
* Hang artwork
* Furniture assembly, repair, and moving
* Clean gutters

**Carpenter Recharageable Services**

In addition to routine maintenance and repair, the Carpenter Shop also provides billable-recharge services. Such services are typically those that fall outside the scope of routine maintenance and baseline services and are customer funded. Payment for billable services is the responsibility of the requesting department or it is included in a project’s scope of work.

## Service Levels

Work provided by the department falls into two categories.

### Routine Service (non-billable)

* Broken Windows
* Missing or damaged ceiling tiles
* Doors not opening or closing properly
* Exit door/gate problems

### Extra Service (billable)

* Construction/repair/installation of cabinets, bookshelves or misc. casework
* Door replacements and/or conversions
* Earthquake restraints: shelves, cabinets, gas cylinders, bookshelves, file cabinets, freezers or other furniture or equipment
* Picture framing, picture/mirror hanging, white board/chalk board installations or moving
* A/V installation
* Keyboard tray installation
* Installation of name/desk/door plates
* Office furniture repair
* Replacement and/or repair of carpet squares, vinyl floors, wooden floors and other floor surfaces in departmental spaces
* Purchase and/or installation, maintenance and repairs of venetians blinds or draperies
* TV / Monitor / AV equipment mounting
* Office furniture repair, assembly and moving
