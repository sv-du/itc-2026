# Auto Insurance for Driving in Mexico

Mexican law requires that you have Mexican auto liability insurance if you drive in Mexico.  This insurance is to be coordinated through Strategic Enterprise Risk Management.

Note that the procedures for requesting approval of international travel apply and the purchase of [Foreign Travel Insurance](../forms-archive/3-17-21-revised-foreign-travel-insurance-requirements.pdf) (PDF) is required.

For information on Mexican Auto Insurance, contact the Strategic Enterprise Risk Management at**extension 2495.**
