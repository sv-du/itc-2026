## About Me

Rohan Guyot-Sutherland is a designer, builder, teacher and advocate of Regenerative Practices around the world. With a keen eye for the inherent synergies found in nature, he uses biomimicry to shape the built environment in an efficient, eco-friendly way. He has managed a wide range of projects relating to self-sufficiency and adaptation to climate change, either for individuals, companies, non-profits or international institutions. Rohan also believes in giving back to the planet and communities in need. By sharing his knowledge, he tries to promote a regenerative lifestyle whenever he can.
