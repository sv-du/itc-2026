# How to apply : Semester@CPP

## How to Apply

The Semester@CPP program application for Fall 2026 is now open. The application deadline will be June 12, 2026.

Before you click the "Apply Now" button, please make sure you have all the application documents, indicated below, ready.

[Apply Now](https://cpge.cpp.edu//portal/applications/applicationProfile.do?method=loadApplicationIndex&applicationProfileId=1069377 "Apply Now. Apply Now")

## Application Documents

**1. Copy of the picture page of your passport**
**2. Most updated transcript from your home university**
**3. Official bank statement** (bank statement needs to be in English and the amount in U.S. dollars. The bank statement needs to be dated within the last six months). The minimum account amount in the bank statement needs to be:

* $16,267 (if you plan to study at Cal Poly Pomona for 1 semester)
* $32,534 (if you plan to study at Cal Poly Pomona for 2 semesters)

If you are using your parents' or someone else's bank statement, please click [HERE (PDF)](../docs/statement-financial-support.pdf) to download the Statement of Financial Support Form and have them fill out this form for you.

**4. English proficiency Proof** (either one of the below):

* TOEFL iBT: 71
* IELTS: 6.0
* Duolingo: 95
* CEFR: B2
* DAAD Certificate
* International Baccalaureate (IB) Diploma
* TOEIC: 640
* Danish High School Completion of English A or English B
* CET-6: 425
* Student may qualify for a waiver of English proficiency proof if he/she attended high school or university for at least three years where English is the medium of instruction

After the application is submitted, please make the application fee of $175 via the payment method of "Flywire". [HERE](https://www.cpp.edu/cpge/semesteratcpp/dates-fees/index.shtml "Instruction of payment methods") is the instruction.

Your application will only be processed once Cal Poly Pomona receives the application fee.
