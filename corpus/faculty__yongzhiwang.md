## About Me

Dr. Yongzhi Wang received his Ph.D. degree from the School of Computing and Information Sciences at Florida International University and received my M.S. and B.S. degrees in Computer Science at Xidian University, China. Prior to joining Cal Poly Pomona, he was an Assistant Professor at Texas A&M University, Corpus Christi, TX, and an Associate Professor at Park University, Kansas City, MO. His research interests include Cybersecurity, Software and System Security, and Artificial Intelligence.

**Recent Courses**

* CIS 2650 - Contemporary Operating System
* CIS 2670 - Cloud Infrastructure

**Research Interests**

Computer System and Software Security, AI Security, Cybersecurity,  Machine Learning , On-Device AI.
