# Department of Theatre & New Dance

![theater](img/home-images/home-large1.png)

## The Future of Theatre

Cal Poly Pomona's Department of Theatre and New Dance awards undergraduate degrees in theatre, emphasizing theatrical production and the "doing" of theatre. After completing the theatre major at Cal Poly Pomona, students are prepared to begin their careers or specialized training in professional theatre, dance, themed entertainment, television or film; to go for advanced training in graduate schools; or to go into teaching.
