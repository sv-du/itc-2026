## About Me

Dr. [Iman Hemmatian](https://imanhemmatian.com) is an Associate Professor of Strategy and Entrepreneurship in the Department of Management and Human Resources at Cal Poly Pomona’s College of Business Administration.

His primary research interests are in the areas of technology innovation, technology entrepreneurship, and Innovation Policy. His work has been published in top innovation and management journals, including *Research Policy*, *Global Strategy Journal*, *Economic Development Quarterly,*and *PLOS ONE*.

He received my PhD in Strategy, Entrepreneurship, and Innovation and an MBA in Global Operations from Oregon State University. He holds a BS in Mechanical-Mechatronics Engineering from Sharif University of Technology.

Before joining academia, he worked as a mechanical engineer in a variety of industries such as oil and gas, automotive, and heavy machinery.
