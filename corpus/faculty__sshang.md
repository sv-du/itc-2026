## Most recent publications

**Selected Publications (Peer-Reviewed Journal Articles)**

* Carrie Shu Shang, “Access to Justice for the Chinese Consumer – Handling Consumer Disputes in Contemporary China” (*Book Review*), *Conflict Resolution Quarterly* (forthcoming 2021)
* Denis Iurchenko, Jeffrey S. Petty, Carrie (Shu) Shang and Joern Block, “Enabling Online Equity Crowdfunding: Understanding the Approval Process Across Four Countries”, *International Review of Entrepreneurship* (forthcoming 2021)
* Carrie Shu Shang and Wei Shen, “Embracing Lex Mercatoria Through Resistance: Trade Customs, ADR and Laws Governing Commercial Relationship in China”, *Asia Pacific Law Review*(forthcoming 2021)
* Wei Shen and Carrie Shu Shang, “Conceptualizing Unilateralism, Fragmentationism and Statism in a Populism Context – A Rise of Populist International Law?”, *Brazilian Journal of International Law* (forthcoming October 2020)
* Shu Shang and Wei Shen, “Trajectory of Centralized Judicial Control in the Context of Tackling Local Protectionism in Enforcing Foreign Arbitral Awards in China”, *The China Quarterly*(March 2020)
* Shu Shang and Wei Shen, “When the State Sovereign Immunity Rule Meets Sovereign Wealth Funds in the Post Financial Crisis Era: Is There Still a Black Hole in International Law?” *Leiden Journal of International Law* 31(4) (2018)
* Shu Shang, “Investor Protection in the China (Shanghai) Pilot Free Trade Zone”, *Transnational Dispute Management the Pacific Rim and International Economic Law Special Issue*(2015)
* Shu Shang, “An Incomplete Negative List”,*Jurisprudence Monthly (**法学**)*, Vol.1 (2014)

**Selected Publications (Law Journals and Book Chapters)**

* Carrie Shu Shang and Wenli Guo, “The Rise of ODR-Led Justice in China – An Initial Look”, *ANU Journal of Law and Technology* (forthcoming 2020)
* Shu Shang, Wenli Guo and Charles Mak, “Two Paths Leading to the Same End? A Discussion of Development and Regulation of Online Mediation under the COVID-19 in China and the United States”, *World Arbitration and Mediation Review* (forthcoming 2020)
* Julien Chaisse and Shu Shang, “Promoting a Greater Use of Mediation or Hybrid Dispute Resolution in the EU: The Resolution of International of International Commercial and Financial Disputes”, *Multi-Tier Dispute Resolution*, co-edited by Anselmo Reyes and Weixia Gu, Cambridge University Press, forthcoming 2021
* Shu Shang, “Implementing Investor-State Mediation in China’s Next Generation BIT”, *China-European Union Investment Relationships*, edited by Julien Chaisse, Edward Elgar, 2018

**Selected Research Awards and Fellowships**

* Invited Scholar, American Business Law Journal (ABLJ) Invited Scholar Colloquium, Academy of Legal Studies, 2020
