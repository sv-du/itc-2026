# Caltrans

The I-10 Freeway High Occupancy Vehicle Lane Project will add one carpool lane in both directions of the 10 Freeway from Citrus Avenue to the 57 Freeway. Work on the 5.2-mile section is scheduled to be completed in 2022.

Please plan accordingly to ensure safe and timely travel to campus.

Additional information about the project is available on the Caltrans District 7 website at [http://www.dot.ca.gov/d7/projects/10/10-hov.html](https://na01.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.dot.ca.gov%2Fd7%2Fprojects%2F10%2F10-hov.html&data=02%7C01%7Cestherchou%40cpp.edu%7Cc9aa04cda86d49bd09b108d693763278%7C164ba61e39ec4f5d89ffaa1f00a521b4%7C0%7C0%7C636858533496455396&sdata=NuXVjl0dF2WpMI66lHfbh1gy0Uy2vVXyFHgTsTrMygI%3D&reserved=0).

Additional FAQs and a map of the closures is available on the university’s [Compas](https://www.cpp.edu/~compass/)[s](https://www.cpp.edu/~compass/) [website](https://www.cpp.edu/~compass/). Updates and more information, including alternate routes, can be found at the university’s [Compass website](https://www.cpp.edu/~compass/).
