# Volunteer with SAS!

The SAS Peer Ambassador program is a great way for students to gain experience in serving the community, and looks great on your resume. Ambassadors' primary role is to support Peer Educators and Social Work Interns with their programs, events, and projects. This includes preparations as well as helping on the day of an event.

We are currently not accepting application at this time, but stay tuned to our social medias[(@cppsurvivoradvocacy)](https://www.instagram.com/cppsurvivoradvocacy/?hl=en) for announcements when applications open.
