# 2040 Campus Master Plan Update Focused Studies

![The Bronco Student Center (BSC) at Cal Poly Pomona](/fpm/pdc/img/bsc-exterior-stairs2.gif)

2040 Campus Master Plan Update consulting firm Ayers Saint Gross undertook studies of university facilities to determine best usages that would benefit students, faculty and staff, and further advance the mission of Cal Poly Pomona. The studies, which range from re-imagining the possibilities for the shuttered CLA Tower to expansion of the Bronco Student Center to designing a transportation corridor through campus, have been incorporated into the draft 2040 Campus Master Plan.
