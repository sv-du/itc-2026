## About Me

### Specializing in Computer Forensics and Management Information Systems

I joined the faculty at Cal Poly Pomona in the Fall term of 2006 from the University of Hawaii, where I earned my MBA and Ph.D. and taught undergraduate and graduate courses in the College of Business Administration. In keeping with Cal Poly Pomona's "learn by doing" approach, I use a *teach by doing* approach in the field of Computer Forensics, as in addition to conducting research and publishing within this field, I am also a practitioner with an active caseload. For more information on these forensic services, please click the link on the left of this page.

###

### Computer Forensics Professional Certification Program

I am pleased to announce Cal Poly Pomona's new Computer Forensics Professional Certification Program. This program consists of a series of one-day and two-day courses scheduled on selected Fridays and Saturdays, beginning Friday, April 2, 2010. A web site with additional details on the program will be available soon, and you can obtain additional details and registration information now simply by sending me an e-mail message requesting information on this program.
