# Transfer Students

## Transfer Application Checklist

### Application Process

Cal Poly Pomona wants to make the application process as simple as possible, which is why we have put together an application checklist that lets you know what you need and best practices when applying. You can apply via Cal State Apply.

[Begin Your Application](https://www.calstate.edu/apply%20 "opens a new window")

## Transfer Application Checklist

### ✓ Explore Majors

Transfers must declare a major at the time of application.  Visit our list of[Academic Programs.](https://www.cpp.edu/programs/index.shtml)

**Spring 2026:** August 1 - 31

**Fall 2026:** October 1 - December 1

Please apply via [Cal State Apply.](https://www2.calstate.edu/apply/choosing-a-degree)

### ✓ Unofficial Transcripts

Have your unofficial transcript(s) on hand when filling out the application as you will be asked to enter all courses you have completed, those in progress and any courses you are planning on taking.

Most students can obtain their unofficial transcripts through their student portal from the College/University that they attended or are currently attending.

### ✓ Biodemographic Information

* Make sure to have the correct social security number as entering it incorrectly can impact your financial aid.
* Double-check that your name and date of birth are also entered correctly.
* Use an e-mail address that you will continue to be able to access. School e-mails are not recommended.

### ✓ Review Application Resources

Applicants are encouraged to review the following helpful resources when completing the Cal State Apply application:

* [Transfer Application Guide (PDF)](https://www.calstate.edu/apply/transfer/Documents/cal-state-apply-transfer-application-guide.pdf)
* [Transfer Application Checklist (PDF)](https://www.calstate.edu/apply/transfer/Documents/application-checklist-transfer.pdf)
* [Transfer Academic Record Update Guide (PDF)](https://www.calstate.edu/apply/transfer/Documents/cal-state-apply-transfer-academic-update-guide.pdf)
* [Cal State Apply Resource Page](https://www.calstate.edu/apply/transfer)
* [How To: Reporting Institutions Attended (Video)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ThaFNEI9axs&t=10s)
* [How To: Entering College Coursework (Video)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=l9GQlJqR3Jc&t=133s)
* [How To: Understanding Quarter vs. Semester (Video)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sdnJYeH1y9g&t=1s)
* [How To: Reporting Grades (Video)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Zv7SMxsIvhM&t=1s)
* [Non CA resident app guide (PDF)](https://www.calstate.edu/apply/freshman/Documents/cal-state-apply-nonresident-guide.pdf "Non CA resident app guide (PDF)")

### ✓ Apply Online

Apply online for the**Spring 2026**term via [Cal State Apply](https://www2.calstate.edu/apply) between August 1 - August 31.

**Cal Poly Pomona makes admission decisions based on self-reported information from the application, therefore make sure that all of the information entered is accurate.**

Provisionally admitted students who intend to enroll at Cal Poly Pomona will be required to submit an official transcript from all institutions attended, when requested from the Admissions Office.

### ✓ Pay Application Fee

A $70 non-refundable application filing fee (or [fee waiver)](https://www2.calstate.edu/apply/paying-for-college/Pages/fee-waiver.aspx) must be paid at the time of application submission. Instructions for payment can be found on [Cal State Apply](https://help.liaisonedu.com/Cal_State_Apply_Applicant_Help_Center/Submitting_and_Monitoring_Your_Cal_State_Apply_Application/Before_and_After_You_Submit_Your_Cal_State_Apply_Application/01_Submitting_and_Completing_Your_Cal_State_Apply_Application).

### ✓ Academic Record Update (January)

Cal State Apply will reopen between January 1-31, 2026 for **Fall Transfer applicants** to update their application with updated coursework and grades.  All applicants are required to update their record with the most current academic information. More information about updating your academic record can be found [here](https://www.calstate.edu/apply/transfer/Documents/cal-state-apply-transfer-academic-update-guide.pdf). **Failure to do so may result in ones admission application being withdrawn.**

**Please note, this is only for Fall applicants and does not pertain to Spring applicants.**

### ✓ Check Application Status

The Office of Admissions will communicate your admission status and next steps via the [**Bronco Applicant Portal**](https://applicant.cpp.edu/).

* **Monitor your application status**
* **Review your** **To Do List** for any required documents
* **Find financial aid information**
* Stay informed on**upcoming events**
* Access important **next steps** for your application

Stay tuned for more information in mid-October on accessing this new portal. Please keep in mind that admission decisions will be made mid-October for Spring  applicants and February - April for Fall applicants.

**If you would like to withdraw your application, please submit the [withdrawal form](https://engage.cpp.edu/register/app_withdraw).**

### ✓ Help with the Bronco Applicant Portal

* Visit the [BroncoAccount Activation for Undergraduate Applicants](https://www.cpp.edu/it/2step/bronco-account-activation-for-undergraduate-applicants.shtml) page for detailed instructions on how to set up your account.
* Log into your personalized [Bronco Applicant Portal](https://applicant.cpp.edu/) to view your application status and any action items. For more information, visit the [Bronco Applicant Portal Information](https://www.cpp.edu/admissions/bronco-applicant-portal.shtml) page.

## General Information

Before you apply, we recommend that you carefully review the Cal Poly Pomona transfer [admission requirements](requirements.shtml) to be considered. Admission into Cal Poly Pomona is competitive as we have more qualified applicants than we can admit. Cal Poly Pomona will consider you for admissions to your alternate major if space is available, but cannot guarantee consideration for your alternate major/program. We prioritize applicants within the same academic college. Students who have been admitted should review the [Next Steps Checklist](admitted.shtml). [Begin your application here.](https://www.calstate.edu/apply)
