# Clinician Visits

## Clinician Visits

You may make ***unlimited*** [appointments](https://www.cpp.edu/health/make-appointment.shtml "appointments") to see a physician, a physician assistant or a nurse. Appointments can be made online, by phone or in person. Treatment for colds, flu, stomach ache, back ache, sore throat, upper respiratory infections, sprains, minor cuts or broken bones, etc. are just many of the common reasons to see a clinician.

Well-patient physicals are also available (1 per academic enrollment; some lab work may be at cost) if you're looking to get a 'clean bill of health' before you graduate.
