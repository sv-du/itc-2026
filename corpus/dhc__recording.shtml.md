## Library Media Studio

The University Library's Media Studio is your destination for recording professional quality videos and podcasts. You can record yourself practicing for presentations or introducing yourself as part of an online portfolio, and the green screen makes it easy to add your own backgrounds using video editing software.
At your reserved time, come to the 2nd floor Circulation Desk to check out the Media Studio Kit for access. CPP students can make one 3-hour reservation each day. The Library Media Studio is on the 2nd floor, room 2434.

[Reserve Online](https://cpp.libcal.com/spaces?lid=16744  "opens a new window")

## 24-113 Media Studio

The 24-113 Media Studio is available for reservation, fitting 1-2 students at a time, to record audio or, with your own camera, green screen for ease of adding backgrounds to video.
