# Water Shutdown Scheduled in Music Building Starting at 7 a.m. on Friday, July 14

**Summary of disruption:** Water to the Music Building (Building 24) will be shut down on Friday, July 14 from approximately 7 a.m. to 5 p.m. Campus crews will be on standby to restore water service.

**Reason for disruption:** Allow crews to replace an auxiliary water valve for the water hydrant.

**How does this affect me?** Water to restrooms, faucets, filling stations and water fountains will be out of service.

**What do I need to do?** Use restrooms, faucets and filling stations in nearby buildings. Please share this information with others in the building who may have not received this message.

**For any questions or more information, please contact:** James Stansberry at (909) 203-2540.
