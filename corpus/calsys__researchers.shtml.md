| ![](img/photo.jpg) | **Dr. Ericsson Marin**  Dr. Marin is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at California State Polytechnic University, Pomona (Cal Poly Pomona). As the director of CALSys Lab, Dr. Marin leads a team of researchers who explore proactive cyber-threat intelligence. He can be reached at   **santanamarin@cpp.edu**  for research inquiries. His faculty site is available at  [https://www.cpp.edu/faculty/santanamarin/](https://www.cpp.edu/faculty/santanamarin/#https://www.cpp.edu/faculty/santanamarin/) |

He can be reached at **whkwan@cpp.edu**for research inquiries, and his personal website is available at [www.wesleyhkwan.com](https://www.wesleyhkwan.com) |
