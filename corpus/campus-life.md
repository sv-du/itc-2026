## Culture of Care

Previous
Next

Card 1 of 4

![A student receives help at the Care Center](img/2025/campus-life-care-center.jpg)

### Care Center

The Care Center is the first place to send students who are of concern or in distress. We connect students to resources they need to succeed (both on and off campus), support them through crises, provide advocacy, and offer Basic Needs services.

Card 2 of 4

![Inside the meditation room at CAPS](img/2025/campus-life-caps.svg)

### Counseling and Psychological Services

Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS) is a confidential, comprehensive, short-term mental health facility with licensed mental health clinicians/therapists who provide basic clinical services.

Card 3 of 4

![A student talks with an advisor](img/2025/campus-life-academic.jpg)

### Disability Resource Center

The DRC removes barriers to access for students with disabilities to have equal opportunity to participate in all aspects of the university experience.

Card 4 of 4

![A student receives a flyer at a Health event](img/2025/campus-life-health.jpg)

### Student Health Services

Student Health Services provides Cal Poly Pomona students with affordable, accessible and high-quality health care. Most services are covered by the mandatory student health fee already included in tuition payments.

![Students inside of their residence hall](img/housing_dining6.png)

## Recreation and Fitness

Previous
Next

Card 1 of 5

![Students climb the rock wall inside of the BRIC](img/2025/campus-life-bric.jpg)

### Bronco Recreation and Intramural Complex

Currently enrolled students get a BRIC membership for free. Discover Bronco Peak (climbing wall), fitness studios, courts, a pool and so much more.

Card 2 of 5

![Students play chess in the Games Room](img/2025/1sports.jpg)

### The Games Room

Play billiards and catch up on gaming, air hockey and more. This dynamic venue caters to a variety of recreational interests!

Card 3 of 5

![Students at the SCUBA certification course](img/2025/2sports.jpg)

### Recreational Certifications

Ready to plunge into the extraordinary world of scuba diving and earn your Professional Association of Diving Instructors (PADI®) scuba diving certification? You can at the BRIC!

Card 4 of 5

![An intramural volleyball game](img/2025/3sports.jpg)

### Intramural Sport Leagues

From bouldering to pickleball, you'll find a league that ramps up the competition and fun.

Card 5 of 5

![A student playing dodgeball](img/2025/4sports.jpg)

### Sports Clubs

The Sport Clubs program provides high-quality, structured, and competitive activities at a collegiate level that offer its members the opportunity to experience physical, social, and emotional growth.
