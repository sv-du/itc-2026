# STEAM Camps

In response to the public health mandates intended to reduce the spread of COVID-19, the decision has been made to offer our exciting hands-on Bronco Academy STEAM Camps at a time when students will be able to fully benefit from the college experience by attending these weeklong programs on-campus at Cal Poly Pomona.

As a result, our Summer Bronco Academy STEAM Camps have been rescheduled for Summer 2022.

Please know that the health and wellbeing of our campus community and our students continues to be our highest priority. Thank you for understanding during this uncertain time.  We look forward to seeing you soon!

![students and robots](../img/560.steam.jpg)

## STEAM Camps

Our Bronco Academy STEAM Camps offer exciting weeklong day camps where students engage in hands-on learning in Science, Technology, Engineering, Art, and Mathematics (STEAM).

Each course incorporates the Cal Poly Pomona *Learn by Doing* philosophy and contains grade appropriate content specially designed for middle school students.

Created to immerse, engage and inspire young minds, Bronco Academy STEAM Camps provide middle school students a unique learning experience at one of the top rated public university’s in the Western United States.

Our standards-based learning, for 6th - 8th grade students, focuses on high-tech subjects such as applied mathematics and robotics, coding and programming, storyboarding and game development, CAD and 3D modeling, coding with Minecraft Education, and virtual reality!

![3 students working](../img/560.steam-2.jpg)

## Our program offers:

* An opportunity for incoming 6th - 8th grade students to learn more about what engineers, scientists, computer science, and mathematics professionals do.
* Workshops taught by instructors with extensive backgrounds in science, technology, engineering, art, and math.
* Curriculum correlated with state and national standards.
* Extended learning opportunities (field trips) in which students are immersed in hands-on activities in math, physics, engineering and more!

Students participating in the program leave with a sense of accomplishment, new friends and fond memories of a wonderful week spent at vibrant and engaging university where learning is celebrated!

Throughout the week, Bronco Academy students gain new academic knowledge, participate in social and recreational activities, and experience college life first-hand!

Please check our website often in the coming months for information regarding our Summer 2022 Bronco Academy STEAM Camps.
