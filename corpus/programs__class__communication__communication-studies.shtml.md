## About the Program

Develop key critical analysis skills to solve complex organizational challenges across careers in corporate training, consulting, and organizational development. The program provides a research-based foundation in how communication shapes human interaction across personal, organizational, and cultural contexts. Blending theoretical understanding and practical application, students graduate ready to lead communication initiatives.

## The Polytechnic Approach

Embrace hands-on learning through research projects, communication simulations, and real-world problem-solving. You will apply communication theory and research methods to analyze community issues and develop evidence-based solutions. Through coursework in interpersonal, organizational, and intercultural communication, students gain practical experience that prepares them for diverse careers in corporate training, consulting, organizational development, or advanced academic study.

## Courses

The Communication Studies option equips you with the skills needed for careers in business, government, education and beyond. This program focuses on developing a deep understanding of communication patterns and strategies, which are crucial for roles that require effective human interaction. You will engage in a rigorous curriculum that includes practical classes like Interpersonal Communication, Organizational Communication, and Group Communication. These courses provide you with the ability to analyze and improve communication processes within various professional settings. Additionally, you will gain valuable experience through research projects and events such as the annual COM Day and Alumni Night, where you apply theoretical knowledge to real-world scenarios. This option is particularly beneficial for those planning to pursue graduate studies or enter professional fields that demand strong communication skills and critical thinking.

For the most up-to-date information on courses for this program, visit the course catalog or contact an advisor.
