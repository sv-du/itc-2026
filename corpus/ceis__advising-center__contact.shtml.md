# Appointments & Drop-in Hours

The CEIS Advising Center's regular office hours are Monday - Friday from 8:00am - 5:00pm.

For general advising on **Early Childhood Studies (ECS)** or **Liberal Studies (LS)** programs you can email us at ceisadvising@cpp.edu or meet with either advisor during the drop-in hours listed below.

**IMPORTANT:** Drop-in hours are **NOT** for general advising in **non-CEIS** majors. If you need advising for a major other than ECS or LS, please visit the [advising directory](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/directories/advising-directory.shtml) to find your major's advising center.

## Scheduling Advising Appointments

Appointments can be scheduled no more than 4-weeks in the future. If we are fully booked, please check on Fridays afternoons when we open a new week in the calendar and/or continue checking for cancellations.

[CPP Connect](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/cppconnect/index.shtml)

## Canceling an Appointment

To cancel or reschedule, you can cancel it by clicking on "Upcoming Appointments" located on the right-hand side of the CPP Connect homepage after logging in.

## Advising Holds

If you have an advising hold, you should check your email to find instructions on how to complete your advising requirement.

During registration weeks you may e-mail ceisadvising@cpp.edu to request a temporary hold removal after you have scheduled an appointment.

Be aware that if you miss or cancel your appointment, your hold will reappear and no future courtesy hold removals will be given

## New & Prospective Students

New students and prospective CPP students will not be able to schedule an appointment through CPP Connect until their first semester.

You will receive advising at orientation during the registration portion of your program. If you have general questions, please connect with us via email: ceisadvising@cpp.edu
