## Biography

Candice H. Huynh is a Professor of Technology and Operations Management in the College of Business Administration at California State Polytechnic University, Pomona. She received her Ph.D. in Management in 2014 from the Paul Merage School of Business at the University of California, Irvine and B.A. in Mathematics and Economics with *magna cum laude* in 2009 from the University of California, San Diego. She teaches Operations Management, Managerial Statistics, and Business Analytics. Her research is primarily in the area of operations management and decision analysis.
