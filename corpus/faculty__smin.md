## Research Statement

Design research integrating theories from design and psychology forms the foundation of my scholarship, with an emphasis on practical implications for apparel design. The research seeks to expand understanding of design and its impact on individuals and society, while my creative scholarship explores new technical methods that advance designers’ practice.
