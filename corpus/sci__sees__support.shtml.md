# Support SEES

## Support the Program!

![Discovery Camp Counselors](img/counselors.jpg)

SEES is running many programs and activities to help the new generation of students advance through college and excel in their careers. You can support the SEES program in many ways. We have a variety of opportunities available, each of which can be tailored to meet your personal or professional availability. Please consider one of the following options:

* Send your kids to the SEES-run [Discovery Camps](http://www.cpp.edu/~discovery/ "Discovery Camps for kids") during summer
* Make a ONE-TIME GIFT to support specific SEES program initiatives
* Become a corporate or business partner

For assistance or information on opportunities for contribution, please contact the SEES Director or Bill Burrows, Director of Development at (909) 869-4160 or by e-mail at bdburrows@cpp.edu.

Thank you for your support!
