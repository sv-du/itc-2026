### Access Your Student Portal

MyCPP is the hub for all online things a CPP student could need. Your BroncoDirect Student Center, Canvas, and CPP email all live here. You can also access many other online student resources and information through your portal.
CPP uses Canvas for classwork. Monitor you...](https://my.cpp.edu/)

[Card 2 of 12

![Two students look at a campus map](img/2-campus-map.jpg)

### Use the Campus Map

Your BroncoDirect Student Center’s class schedule will tell you where your classes are located. Use the campus map to find their buildings.](https://www.cpp.edu/maps)

[Card 3 of 12

![Photo of Billy in a convertible going into the parking structure](img/3-parking.jpg)

### Learn About Parking

In order to park on campus, you need a parking permit. Summer parking permits will be available for purchase on the Parking website. You can also use the PaybyPhone app for hourly and daily parking. You can park in any of the student lots.](https://www.cpp.edu/parking/index.shtml)

[Card 4 of 12

![Student grabs textbooks from shelf](img/4-textbooks.jpg)

### Get Your Textbooks

You can buy or rent textbooks, stock up on school supplies or get CPP swag at the bookstore. Use the Bookstore’s textbook tool to look up the books you’ll need for your Bronco Scholar classes. You can buy them through the Bookstore or wherever you find your bo...](https://www.broncobookstore.com/default.asp)

[Card 5 of 12

![Two students look at laptops in the library](img/5-wifi.jpg)

### Connect to WiFi

Eduroam is our Campus WiFi network. You can set it up ahead of coming to campus.](https://www.cpp.edu/it/wifi/index.shtml)

[Card 6 of 12

![Billy stands in front of a foothill transit bus](img/6-busrides.jpg)

### Take Free Bus Rides

Enrolled students can get a Class Pass for free, unlimited rides on Foothill Transit buses all year long. The CPP Class Pass also works on Silver Streak bus lines, which stop on campus and can get you to Downtown L.A. in less than an hour.
You can also use the Bronco Sh...](https://www.cpp.edu/transportation/classpass.shtml)

[Card 7 of 12

![Photo of exterior of University Library](img/7-library.jpg)

### Visit the University Library

The University Library is a popular spot for students to study. You can check out books and materials and use the computer labs.](https://www.cpp.edu/library/index.shtml)

[Card 8 of 12

![Student checks into Learning Resource Center](img/8-lrc.jpg)

### Use the Learning Resource Center

Located on the second floor of the University Library, the LRC is CPP’s university tutoring center. You can come here for subject tutoring, writing tutoring and academic skills workshops. Email lrcscheduling@cpp.edu to make an appointment.](https://www.cpp.edu/lrc/index.shtml)

[Card 9 of 12

![Students eating together at Centerpointe](img/9-food-on-campus.jpg)

### Eat on Campus

Dining services during the summer are limited, so be sure to check the hours to see what’s open.](https://cppenterprises.org/dining-hours/)

[Card 10 of 12

![Student looks at a computer](img/10-microsoft.jpg)

### Download Microsoft Office for Free

Download a free license of Microsoft Office to use during your time at CPP.](https://www.cpp.edu/it/client-services/software/software-students.shtml)

[Card 11 of 12

![Student runs on a treadmill](img/11-bric.jpg)

### Work Out at the BRIC

Your Bronco Scholar fees include access to the BRIC, our campus gym.](https://asi.cpp.edu/campusrec/)

[Card 12 of 12

![Photo of exterior of Health Center](img/12-health-center.jpg)

### Use the Health Center

If you aren’t feeling well or need non-emergency health services, head to the Health Center. Make an appointment through your Health Portal or call 909-869-4000.](https://www.cpp.edu/health/student-health-services.shtml)
