## Info for High School and Community Partners

### Early Academic Preparation for Local Schools

Thank you for helping students prepare to pursue their education at Cal Poly Pomona! CPP’s EAP supports early academic preparation in our local admissions area. High Schools within the local admissions area may contact us to request a member of our team to schedule a CSU Academic Preparation Presentation or participate in a district meeting.

[Local Admissions Area Info](https://www.cpp.edu/~admissions/freshmen/local-area.shtml%20 "opens a new window")

### High School A-G Course List

Based on certain Standardized Test scores, students may be asked to successfully complete a 12th grade approved year-long English or math course. Verify which approved year-long courses are listed in your high school A-G course list.

![CAASPP logo](../img/caaspp-logo.png)

### CAASPP EAP Results

The CAASPP Student Score Report includes EAP results. In order for any of the CSU campuses to see these scores, the student must release their scores during the SBAC testing or when they apply via Cal State Apply. District and school CAASPP aggregate data may be found on the CDE website.

![Graphic that shows pathway from CSU to high schools](../img/csu_hs_dashboard.jpg)

### CSU High School Dashboard

Use the CSU high school dashboard to view the academic paths of your students.
