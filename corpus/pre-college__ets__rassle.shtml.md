# R.A.S.S.L.E.

![Decorative Banner](../img/banner.png)

During the summer, 50 ETS participants will be selected to attend the RASSLE Program (Rigorous Academic Support Summer Learning Experience).  Students will attend classes through Cal Poly Pomona’s ETS Program at Cal Poly Pomona during this six-week academic experience.  The culminating piece of RASSLE is a three-day residential life experience where students will attend workshops and live in college/university dormitories with college residential advisors and the ETS Program staff.
