## Study Abroad News

**Panama Study Abroad Trip Offers Students Lessons in Culture,** **Opportunities to Serve**

Read about how CPP students traveled to Panama for a spring study abroad fieldwork trip, where they participated in cultural exchange, volunteered at a local food bank, conducted field research, and gained firsthand insight into biodiversity, sustainability, and social service alongside Panamanian communities.

Read the article on Polycentric [**here**](https://www.cpp.edu/news/content/2025/05/panama-study-abroad-trip-offers-students-lessons-in-culture-opportunities-to-serve/).
