## Eligibility for Open University

Expand all

### Who is eligible to enroll through Open University?

Open University allows individuals who are not currently admitted to Cal Poly Pomona to enroll in undergraduate- and graduate-level courses through the College of Professional and Global Education. Enrollment is offered on a space-available basis and does not result in admission to the university.

### Are international students eligible to enroll through Open University?

International visa holders may be eligible to enroll through Open University if they are able to maintain their non-immigrant status through a sponsoring organization. Open University does not issue [Form I-20](https://studyinthestates.dhs.gov/students/prepare/students-and-the-form-i-20) or [Form DS-2019](https://j1visa.state.gov/participants/how-to-apply/about-ds-2019/), and enrollment does not confer full-time matriculated student status.

### How many units of Open University coursework can be used to meet degree requirements at Cal Poly Pomona?

Undergraduate students may apply up to 24 semester units of Open University coursework toward a degree. Graduate students may apply up to 9 semester units. Unit limits vary by program.

### If I have been disqualified from Cal Poly Pomona or have a registration hold on my account, will I be allowed to register?

Students who have been academically disqualified from Cal Poly Pomona may enroll through Open University to improve their GPA and meet readmission requirements. Students with registration holds are reviewed on a case-by-case basis. For assistance, contact the CPGE Registration Office at CPGEReg@cpp.edu.

## Registration

Expand all

### Why do I need to activate an account every term?

Open University enrollment is managed on a term-by-term basis. Students must activate their account each term they plan to enroll in order to access BroncoDirect and complete registration.

### Where do I look to see if a course has a prerequisite?

View the Course Catalog to search for course prerequisites by the class's abbreviation and number.

### How do I find out if my course(s) meet degree requirements?

If you are a disqualified or former student at CPP, make an appointment with your academic advisor to verify that your class meets degree requirements. If you’re a visiting Open University student, you may use the Course Catalog to verify course descriptions.

### How will I know if I'm registered in class?

Courses in which you have registered will be visible in the Student Center section of your BroncoDirect account.

### How do I find a class?

Classes that are available for registration for the term can be viewed online on the [Public Schedule of Classes](https://schedule.cpp.edu/) page.

### What is your withdrawal and refund policy?

View the [Refunds](https://www.cpp.edu/cpge/open-university/withdrawals-and-refunds/index.shtml) page for more information.

## Fees & Payment

Expand all

### What are the fees for Open University courses?

Fees for Open University course can be viewed at course fees and payment options.

### What types of payment do you accept?

View the [Payment Options](https://www.cpp.edu/cpge/open-university/tuition/payment-options.shtml) page for more information on the types of payments we accept.

### Is financial aid available to students taking classes through Open University?

Financial aid is not accepted for courses taken through Open University.

### How do I make a payment?

Payments for Open University courses are made online through BroncoDirect. Accepted payment methods and payment plan options are listed on the Open University [tuition page](tuition/payment-options.shtml).

### What is your withdrawal and refund policy?

View the [Refunds](https://www.cpp.edu/cpge/open-university/withdrawals-and-refunds/index.shtml) page for more information.

## Parking

### Do I need a parking permit to park on campus and where do I purchase one?

Visit the Cal Poly Pomona [Parking](https://www.cpp.edu/~parking/index.shtml) website for more information.

## Transcripts

### How do I get official/unofficial transcripts of my coursework?

Official transcripts can be purchased online through the [National Student Clearinghouse](https://tsorder.studentclearinghouse.org/school/select). Unofficial transcripts can be accessed through BroncoDirect if you still have access.
