# Graduate Studies

## Graduate Programs in CLASS

[![Carsten Lange in front of map](../img/faculty/lange-carsten.jpg)

### M.S. in Economics

The Master of Science in Economics offers intensive education in economic theory and analysis, with specialized classes in areas like Money and Capital Markets and Environmental Economics, preparing graduates for immediate employment in business and government, research positions in fields such as finance and marketing, teaching roles at secondary and community college levels, or further doctoral studies.](https://www.cpp.edu/class/economics/graduate-program/join-the-program.shtml%20 "opens a new window")

[![student on laptop](../img/students/ma-english.jpg)

### M.A. in English

The Master of Arts degree in English offers the opportunity for students to engage in advanced study in the fields of literature, rhetoric and composition, and/or the Teaching of English to Speakers of Other Languages (TESOL). The program prepares students to pursue careers in high school and postsecondary teaching in community college and university settings, to continue on to doctoral studies, and to enter career fields requiring superior proficiency in written and oral communication, critical thinking, analysis, and research.](https://www.cpp.edu/class/english-modern-languages/graduate-programs/index.shtml%20 "opens a new window")

[![student on laptop](../img/students/first-day-photo.jpg)

### M.A. in History

The Master of Arts in History is designed for secondary school teachers who wish to pursue professional development and increase their academic content knowledge and to obtain a higher degree, though candidates with other goals will also be considered. The department also provides a solid training for students in historical methods, including training in the latest internet-based research and teaching techniques, historiography, and historical research.](https://www.cpp.edu/class/history/graduate-program/index.shtml%20 "opens a new window")

[![Hilda Solis in conversation](../img/alumni/hilda-solis.jpg)

### Master of Public Administration (M.P.A.)

The Master's Program in Public Administration prepares students for higher degrees and administrative leadership positions in government at all levels. This MPA program equips graduates for careers not only in public service but also in international organizations, nonprofits, interest groups, community institutions, and businesses.](https://www.cpp.edu/class/mpa/%20 "opens a new window")

[![Viviane Seyranian teaching a group of students](../img/students/seyranian460x351.jpg)

### M.S. in Psychology

The Master of Science Program in Psychology provides students with coursework and supervised practice in marriage and family therapy (MFT), preparing them for eventual MFT licensure in California. This professional training equips graduates for diverse counseling careers, from industrial programs to clinic settings and private practice, developing skills beyond the theoretical knowledge gained in the classroom.](https://www.cpp.edu/class/psychology/psychology-course/masters-program.shtml%20 "opens a new window")
