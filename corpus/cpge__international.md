# International | College of Professional and Global Education, CPP

## Departments

![International Messages](img/560.group-cpp-students.jpg)

### American Semester Program (ASP)

* Study for a semester or a year alongside American students
* Choose from 9 colleges offering 130+ degrees
* Receive an official university transcript
* Combine university credit courses and language classes, if needed
* Complete prerequisites for a Master's Program

[Read More about American Semester Program (ASP)](https://www.cpge.cpp.edu/asp)

![welcome to cpeli](img/560.cpeli-main.jpg)

### Cal Poly English Language Institute (CPELI)

* Intensive English Program – Six levels; Five 8- week terms per year
* Customized programs combining English and university major of interest
* Programs that increase confidence and ability in English for high school and university students
* TOEFL/IELTS waiver to Cal Poly Pomona undergraduate and graduate programs
* University application advising and cultural orientation for international students

[Read More about Cal Poly English Language Institute (CPELI)](https://www.cpeli.cpp.edu)

![International Messages](img/560.gei-kw-group-shot.jpg)

### Global Education Institute (GEI)

* International institutional partnership building
* Programs for international university students
* Programs for international high school students
* Programs for educators and other working professionals

[Read More about Global Education Institute (GEI)](https://www.cpp.edu/cpge/gei)

![International student jumping](img/560.ic-student-sand.jpg)

### International Center (IC)

* Adminstraters study abroad and exhange programs
* Oversees incoming international student services
* Coordinates the visiting scholar program
* Partners with academic colleges to support global engagement

[Read More about International Center (IC)](https://www.cpp.edu/international/international-students/index.shtml)

![Student housing](img/560.student-housing.jpg)

### Housing Information

University Housing Services offers three living communities - Residential Halls, Residential Suites, and The Lyle Center for Regenerative Studies.
