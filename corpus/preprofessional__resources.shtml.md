# Resources

![Photo of career fair](img/career.jpg)

## Career Center

Connect with the Career Center to review your personal statements, schedule a mock interview, or work with a career coach on career exploration. You can also use Handshake to see upcoming career fairs and a list of participating employers.

[Visit the Career Center website](https://careercenter.cpp.edu/%20 "opens a new window")
