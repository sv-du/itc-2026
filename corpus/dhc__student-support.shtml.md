# Student Support & Resources

## Explore Opportunities and Support at DHC

The Digital Humanities Consortium (DHC) at Cal Poly Pomona is a collaborative space where students explore the intersection of technology, creativity, and the humanities. We offer hands-on opportunities through workshops, research projects, and digital storytelling, helping students build practical and creative skills. To support their learning journey, we also highlight valuable campus resources, such as free access to LinkedIn Learning, Adobe Creative Cloud, and other digital tools available through the university, ensuring students have what they need to grow academically and professionally.

## Academic Support

Our Academic Support programs help students build skills, gain hands-on experience, and get guidance through office hours, mentorship, workshops, and research opportunities.
