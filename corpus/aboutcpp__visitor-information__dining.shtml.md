# Dining

## On Campus Dining

There are countless dining options on campus, from elegant hilltop dining at the Restaurant at Kellogg Ranch to convenient fast food options.

### Campus Dining

* [Campus Restaurants](https://cppdining.com/dining-locations/)
* [Dining Hours](https://cppdining.com/hours/)
* [Centerpointe](https://cppdining.com/centerpointe/)
* [Restaurant at Kellogg Ranch](http://www.cpp.edu/~rkr/index.shtml)
* [Kellogg West Conference Center and Hotel](http://www.kelloggwest.com/)
* [Farm Store](http://www.cpp.edu/~farmstore/)
