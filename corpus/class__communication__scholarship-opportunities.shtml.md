## COM Scholarships

![awards-at-com-day](img/awards-at-com-day.jpg)

The following scholarships are currently offered through the Department of Communication:

1. Legacy Scholarship
2. Gary Keele Memorial Scholarship
3. Frank A. Tennant Endowed Scholarship
4. Ben F Thompson Memorial Scholarship
5. Kaufman-Yarbrough Memorial Scholarship
6. Patricia A Kurtz Memorial Scholarship
7. Wayne Rowe Memorial Scholarship
8. Cecil B. Brown Memorial Scholarship (*CLASS*)

All scholarships have an online application process accessed through Bronco Scholarships. To apply, please visit [Cal Poly Pomona Bronco Scholarship Portal](https://csupomona.academicworks.com/) and search for COM.

Scholarship inquiries can be directed to Dr. Ivana Cvetkovic, student scholarships committee chair, at icvetkovic@cpp.edu.

Note that scholarship calls are typically posted 6-8 weeks prior to the application deadline.

## Scholarship FAQs

Expand all

### What are the criteria for receiving a scholarship?

Scholarships are awarded based on academic merit (GPA), leadership, and/or community service. Financial need is considered for some scholarships. We advise reviewing the scholarship criteria listed in the description section of the application, but students who are active in student clubs and organizations on campus are particularly encouraged to apply.

### I am a scholarship recipient. When will the scholarship be disbursed?

The Communication Department usually announces the scholarships recipients during the COM Day in April (join us for the COM Day to hear whether you are among the scholarship recipients). The scholarships will be disbursed on recipients’ accounts in late July or early August. Students who are also receiving financial aid will receive the scholarship at the same time.

### Can I apply to more than one scholarship?

Yes. We encourage students to apply to all scholarships for which they are eligible.  Students can check the eligibility in advance on the CPP scholarships website. Once a student logs in, each student will complete the general questionnaire and the system will display all the scholarships a student is eligible for.

### Is the process of application for scholarships demanding?

The process is not demanding, but we advise that students prepare the application materials in advance. First, each student will fill out the general questionnaire. Some scholarships request additional essays (usually between 250-500 words) and/or a resume. Please read the scholarship criteria in advance.

### If I already received a scholarship, am I eligible to apply again?

It depends on a scholarship. Since each scholarship is awarded for the upcoming academic year, current graduating seniors are not eligible to apply. The Frank Tennant Scholarship recipients may reapply for the scholarship if they met the criteria from the scholarship description.

### Do I have to be a full-time student to apply for scholarships?

Please check the scholarships description and criteria. Some scholarships require students to be full-time students, whereas other scholarships can be awarded to part-time students.

### I am graduating in December. Am I eligible to apply for scholarships?

Please carefully read the scholarships description and criteria. Some scholarships require full-time status during the upcoming academic year, while others require enrollment in courses only during the Fall semester.

### Will each scholarship be awarded to one student only?

No. Depending on the scholarship description and criteria, some scholarships are annually awarded to a single recipient, while other scholarships are awarded to multiple recipients. We encourage students to apply to multiple scholarships based on eligibility.

## COM Scholarships

Card 1 of 8

### Frank A. Tennant Endowed Scholarship

Eligibility: High school students who are already accepted to Cal Poly Pomona (Proof will be needed before the start of the 2024-25 academic year), first-years, sophomores, and juniors (seniors are not eligible to apply). Minimum GPA 3.25. Only available to students pursuing Communication with an emphasis in Multimedia Journalism and PR.
Amount of Award: $7,780.22

Card 2 of 8

### Patricia A. Kurtz Memorial Scholarship

Eligibility: Current Communication majors with an interest in Journalism, Magazines and Feature Writing (Seniors are not eligible to apply). Minimum GPA 3.0.
Amount of Award: $500

Card 3 of 8

### Cecil B. Brown Memorial Scholarship

Eligibility: This is a CLASS Scholarship for current Communication Majors or Minors with a minimum 3.25 GPA.
Amount of Award: $3,000

Card 4 of 8

### Wayne Rowe Memorial Scholarship

Eligibility: The Wayne Rowe Memorial Scholarship was created to memorialize Dr. Wayne Rowe, a Cal Poly Pomona Communication faculty emeritus member from 1990 to 2017. Communication students are eligible to apply with a GPA of 2.0 or higher.
Amount of Award: $1,000

Card 5 of 8

### Gary Keele Memorial Scholarship

Eligibility: Full-time Communication majors in the Communication Studies Option (previously Organizational or Interpersonal Communication) with a minimum GPA of 3.5. Graduating seniors are not eligible to apply.
Amount of Award: $1,000

Card 6 of 8

### Ben F. Thompson Memorial Scholarship

Eligibility: Current Communication majors in the Multimedia Journalism option with a minimum 3.0 GPA and a demonstrated commitment to community service.
Amount of Award: $500

Card 7 of 8

### Kaufman-Yarbrough Memorial Scholarship

Eligibility: Current Communication majors with a minimum 3.25 GPA.
Amount of Award: $500

Card 8 of 8

### Legacy Scholarship

This scholarship is funded from donations secured by the COM 4446 Event Planning Class.
Eligibility: Junior or senior Communication majors with a minimum GPA of 3.0.
Amount of Award: $1,000
