# CPU 4010

## **Due to COVID-19, the GWT requirement has been suspended as a condition of graduation for spring 2020 and summer 2020.  If you have questions about the GWT, please email studentsuccess@cpp.edu.**

CPU 4010 is a course offered each semester to students who have taken the GWT two or more times, but have not passed.  Students who pass the course have satisfied the GWT requirement.

* Students will focus on writing, revising, and proofreading strategies and produce five essays, from which they will select the best three to submit in a portfolio to be assessed by GWT readers.  If the portfolio passes, then the student’s transcript indicates that the GWT requirement has been met.

* Students wishing to apply for enrollment into CPU4010 should complete a [CPU 4010 application](https://www.cpp.edu/~lrc/documents/applications-recommendations/CPU401%20Application_Rev_%201-28-2014.pdf "Link to the CPU 401 application") and submit it to lrc@cpp.edu.

NOTE: Due to the high number of applications submitted for each semester, students that are admitted into the class typically submit their applications **one full academic semester before the semester they wish to enroll.** (For example, a student wishing to enroll in spring semester should submit an application at the beginning of fall semester)
