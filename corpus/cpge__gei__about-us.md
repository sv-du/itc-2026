# About Global Education Institute (GEI)

![professional in conference room](../img/560.gei-professional.jpg)

## GEI Mission

GEI at Cal Poly Pomona (CPP) is a unit of dedicated professionals in the field of international education. GEI supports university mission by

* Establishing international institutional partnerships
* Offering high-quality university programs to international students, educators and other professionals
* Engaging CPP students, faculty, staff and administrators
* Enhancing CPP’s global awareness and prestige
* Facilitating academic and cultural interaction between the U.S. and other countries

## Program Scope

GEI provides high-quality university-based programs for

* University students
* University faculty and administrators
* K-12 school principals and teachers
* High school students
* Government officials
* Other working professionals
