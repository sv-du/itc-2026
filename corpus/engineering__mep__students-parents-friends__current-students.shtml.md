# Current Students

![SHPE board members](/engineering/mep/img/2015-shpe-e-board.jpg)

There have been many new changes and we want you to stay as updated as possible. If you are a freshman, be sure to stay up to date with [Academic Advising](https://www.cpp.edu/engineering/advising/index.shtml). All other MEP students are welcome to come in for advising as well. However, MEP is able to advise on general academic issues, policies, and processes only, and cannot provide curriculum advising nor major related advisement as this is best addressed by your faculty or department advisors.

## Program Benefits:

* \* [Advising](https://www.cpp.edu/~engineering/advising/index.shtml)
* Early Identification & Monitored Success Plan
* Engineering Industry Tours & Field trips
* Graduate School Exploration & Preparation Workshops
* High-Tech Engineering Learning Center
* \* [Part-time Employment](../summerjobs.shtml)
* [Peer Mentoring](../student-resources/mentoring.shtml)
* \*Milestone & Academic Recognition Events
* [Scholarships](https://csupomona.academicworks.com/opportunities?utf8=%E2%9C%93&term=mep&commit=Search)
* “Dollars for Scholars” (textbook stipend for high-achieving students)
* \*Tutoring
* \*Workshops

\* The asterisked benefits are open to all students.
