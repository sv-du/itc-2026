# Ambassador Program

![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-green-mobile.svg)
![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-green.svg)

## Discover Your Spark

### Become a SIIL Ambassador

Do you enjoy helping others, exploring big ideas, and bringing projects to life? At Cal Poly Pomona, we believe that every student has the potential to lead—and that leadership starts with action.
As a SIIL Ambassador, you’ll support innovation spaces like the Maker Studio, iLab, and the Innovation Orchard, where students turn ideas into prototypes, startups, and solutions to real-world problems. You’ll join a vibrant community of makers and mentors who believe in learning by doing and in supporting one another’s growth.
Whether you’re interested in design, engineering, entrepreneurship, or want to be part of something meaningful, this is your chance to shape the future at CPP.

[Become an Ambassador](https://forms.office.com/r/V3tNFV3Nbe%20 "opens a new window")

![SIILL Student showing student how to use a machine.](img/general-photos/siil-3.jpg)

## Become by Leading

### Join a Community

Join a team of student leaders who help guide peers in innovation spaces like the Maker Studio and iLab. Support creativity and collaboration while building your own leadership skills.

### Grow Your Skills

Gain hands-on experience, connect with mentors, and help your fellow Broncos bring their ideas to life. No technical background needed—just a passion for helping others.

### Make an Impact

At CPP, we believe in learning by doing—and leading by serving. As a SIIL Ambassador, you’ll be part of a welcoming, student-powered community shaping the future of innovation.

## What You’ll Gain

1. ### Access on Your Terms

   Work on your personal projects in the Maker Studio or iLab—even during after-hours. Ambassadors get flexible access to spaces that inspire.
2. ### Free Weekly Printing

   Enjoy up to 50 hours of free printing per week to bring your ideas to life—whether you're prototyping, personalizing, or practicing.
3. ### Skill Building & Portfolio Development

   Gain hands-on experience with tools like 3D printers, embroidery machines, laser cutters and more. Receive training and grow your personal portfolio along the way.
4. ### Career-Ready Growth

   Develop marketable skills, build your résumé, and show future employers that you take initiative—and that you lead by doing.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What are the expectations for SIIL Ambassadors?

**SIIL Ambassadors are expected to:**

* Support SIIL staff and lab techs in creating a welcoming, service-oriented environment.
* Volunteer for shifts at the Maker Studio and iLab to assist with operations and maintenance.
* Learn how to use key equipment like 3D printers, laser cutters, vinyl cutters, and sewing machines.
* Demonstrate professionalism, a customer-service mindset, and strong communication—or show a willingness to grow in these areas.

### What kind of duties will I have?

**As a SIIL Ambassador, you may:**

* Help foster creativity, innovation, and entrepreneurship in our innovation spaces.
* Assist with maintaining equipment and tracking inventory.
* Monitor and care for the spaces during shifts (typically 2–4 hours per week).
* Answer student or visitor questions and offer project support.
* Offer hands-on help with equipment, if you're comfortable.
* Help host events or participate in outreach that supports SIIL’s mission.

### What perks come with the volunteer role?

**Ambassadors receive:**

* Flexible, even after-hours access to innovation spaces.
* Up to **50 hours of free printing** per week.
* Personalized training on all available equipment.
* Opportunities to grow their résumé and develop a creative portfolio.

### Who can I contact for more information?

Reach out to us at siil@cpp.edu
