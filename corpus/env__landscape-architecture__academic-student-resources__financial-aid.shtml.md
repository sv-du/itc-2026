# Financial Aid

Cal Poly Pomona's Office of Financial Aid & Scholarships' mission is to provide financial assistance to help students and families achieve their academic goals.

All students should visit the [Office of Financial Aid and Scholarships](https://www.cpp.edu/financial-aid/index.shtml "Office of Financial Aid and Scholarships (opens in new window)") to learn if they qualify for aid.

**CPP offers numerous types of financial assistance to students:**

* [Grants](https://www.cpp.edu/financial-aid/undergraduate/current/aid-programs.shtml "Grants (opens in new window)") - Aid that does not need to be repaid.
* [Federal Work Study](https://www.cpp.edu/financial-aid/federal-work-study/student-information.shtml "Federal Work Study (opens in new window)") - A work program in which the student earns a salary for hours worked.
* [Scholarships](https://www.cpp.edu/financial-aid/scholarships/cal-poly-pomona-scholarships.shtml "Scholarships (opens in new window)") - Aid that does not need to be repaid but requires a separate application from the FAFSA.
* [Bronco Scholarship Application (BSA)](https://csupomona.academicworks.com/ "Bronco Scholarship Application (BSA) (opens in new window)")  - Online scholarship applicaton for scholarship opportunities from colleges, departments, clubs, and offices throughout campus. For specific deadlines, go to the [Bronco Scholarship Application](https://csupomona.academicworks.com/ "Bronco Scholarship Application (opens in new window)").
* California Middle Class Scholarship - Tuition reduction program for California residents.
* [Loans](https://www.cpp.edu/financial-aid/resources/loan-information.shtml "Loans (opens in new window)") - Aid that need to be repaid.
* TEACH Grant  - A grant/loan combination that requires a TEACH Grant Agreement to Serve (ATS).

With a learn-by-doing philosophy, Cal Poly Pomona offers a variety of programs that provide our students with real life, hands-on experience and adventure!

Financial Aid requirements and qualifications vary for the special programs we offer, and may require the student to submit additional documents for processing:

* [Study Abroad](https://www.cpp.edu/financial-aid/other-programs/study-abroad.shtml "Study Abroad (opens in new window)") - Financial Aid Availability and Instructions for Study Abroad Program
* [Summer Aid](https://www.cpp.edu/financial-aid/other-programs/summer/index.shtml "Summer Aid (opens in new window)") - Application for Financial Aid for the Summer Quarter
* [Veteran Benefits](https://www.cpp.edu/veterans/ "Veteran Benefits (opens in new window)") - Instructions on Applying for Veterans Benefits
* [Teacher Credential Program](https://www.cpp.edu/financial-aid/credentials/prospective-students.shtml "Teacher Credential Program (opens in new window)")**-**Eligibility and Procedures for Financial Aid for Teacher Credential Programs
* Second BA/BS & MA**-** Eligibility and Procedures for Financial Aid for Second Baccalaureates and Master’s Degree Programs
* Ed.D. Program - Eligibility and Procedures for Financial Aid for the Ed.D. Program
* Early Start - Financial Aid Availability and Instructions for Early Start
