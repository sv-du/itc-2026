# Matheus Ragghianti Cordeiro De Garcia

![](../../../img/members/matheus-image.png)

## Matheus Ragghianti Cordeiro De Garcia

## **Junior Marketing Scientist**

**Major:** Master in Science in Business Analytics

**Interest: Data Science, Data visualization, and machine learning models**

**Skills: Programming languages (Python, R, SQL), data visualization tools (Power BI, Tableau), deadline-oriented and team collaboration**

Matheus is a Brazilian international student, currently in his first year of Master in Science in Business Analytics. He has a lot of interest in programming languages and machine learning models specifically in business/ marketing areas. This means that his focus is on using his analytical skills to give companies strategic insights to improve their business. On the side, Matheus loves to watch different types of sports, including soccer, basketball, volleyball, with hiking being one of his favorite hobbies. He plans to get a job as a data scientist and apply this experience to a Ph.D. program.
