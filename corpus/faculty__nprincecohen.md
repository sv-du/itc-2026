## About

Dr. Prince-Cohen teaches hybrid graduate courses focusing on understanding and creating research in education.  Her interests are eclectic including the sociology of education, social presence in online learning, history of education, and world history. Dr. Prince-Cohen is a reviewer and associate editor for the formerly *The International Journal of Science in Society* now *Online Learning: Official Journal of the Online Learning Consortium*. She was the co-principal investigator for the development of an online master in education degree awarded by some CSUs throughout the state.
