# Bronco Applicant Portal

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How can students access the Bronco Applicant Portal?

All CPP undergraduate applicants starting with Fall 2025 should receive a message to their personal email instructing them on how to claim their Bronco Account. At that point, prospective students can access the portal. The link to the Bronco Applicant Portal is on the [Admissions home page](https://www.cpp.edu/admissions/index.shtml "https://www.cpp.edu/admissions/index.shtml"). Additionally, the Ask Billy Bronco chatbot on the Admissions website can direct students to log into the portal when prompted.

### What can students find in the Bronco Applicant Portal?

The Bronco Applicant Portal houses updates on students' applications, a to-do list of items that can impact their enrollment, information on their major, financial aid, upcoming events, and Orientation. It is also where students are notified of their admission decision and can formally indicate their intent to enroll, ensuring they stay on track with key enrollment milestones.

### What students should be using this portal?

The Bronco Applicant Portal is available to undergraduate applicants beginning Fall 2025. Additionally, applicants will only have access to this portal until two weeks before the start of their first semester at CPP. Please direct them to the Bronco Applicant Portal to stay up to date on their application status.

### Who should I contact if the student has a problem with something in the portal?

Please contact the Office of Admissions with any concerns or issues with the Bronco Applicant Portal. The Office of Admissions can be contacted via email or phone at (909) 869-5299.
