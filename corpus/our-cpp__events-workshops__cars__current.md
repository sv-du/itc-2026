# Creative Activities & Research Symposium (CARS)

![Annual CARS Symposium](../../../img/summer/carsbanner-thin-generic.png)

---

#### Welcome to the 11th Annual

## Creative Activities and Research Symposium

**August 7, 2025**
In-Person Event at the Bronco Student Center at Cal Poly Pomona

**Time: 9:00 am - 3:30 pm**

Hosted by the [Office of Undergraduate Research](https://www.cpp.edu/our-cpp/index.shtml), the **Creative Activities and Research Symposium (CPP CARS)** is a celebration of *student investigation*, *exploration*, and *inquiry* completed by Cal Poly Pomona (CPP) and community college students participating in summer research programs. Students can present the work they completed during the academic year and summer while they were a student at CPP. There are two presentation formats students can chose from: 1) An Oral Presentation session with a 10-minute Oral Presentation followed by a 4-minute Q&A. 2) A 1-hour Poster interactive session where each student will present in-person next to their poster.
