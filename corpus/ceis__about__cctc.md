# CCTC Accreditation and Assessment

The College of Education and Integrative Studies is located on the eastern edge of Los Angeles County. It partners with surrounding schools and districts to provide preliminary and advanced credential programs to prepare dynamic, innovative, skilled, and socially responsible educators and school leaders. Cultivated through a polytechnic approach, CEIS graduates enact purposeful interdisciplinary education, create culturally relevant student-centered learning, and address social justice and equity issues in classrooms and schools.

This website supports accreditation with the California Commission on Teacher Credentialing (CCTC) and is under development.
