## Advising Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I change my major to Psychology?

The change of major forms for Psychology are now submitted via E-FORM on the [Registrar's website under Forms & Services](https://www.cpp.edu/registrar/forms/forms.shtml). Please see the Change of Major information on our website regarding GPA for Psychology.

### How do I get one of my classes that I took at another college to fulfill one of my courses here at Cal Poly Pomona?

Academic Advisors must initiate the Course Substitution or Acceptance of Transfer Coursework Petition for students. Please visit your advisor to submit the petition.

### How do I get a course I took at Cal Poly Pomona to count for another course at Cal Poly Pomona?

Academic Advisors must initiate the Course Substitution or Acceptance of Transfer Coursework Petition for students. Please visit your advisor to submit the petition.

### I took the same course twice because I did not pass it or get an adequate grade(C-, D+, D, D-, F, WU, or IC) the first time and I would like to replace the grade I originally received. How do I replace the first grade with my now passing grade?

* A Grade Forgiveness form is available on the [Registrar's website under Student Forms & Services](https://www.cpp.edu/registrar/forms/forms.shtml).
* Take the completed form to your Academic Advisor for their signature.
* If the catalog course number has **NOT** changed, the Department Chair **DOES NOT** need to sign the form and you can take the form directly to the Registrar's Office for processing.
* If the catalog course number **HAS** changed (Ex. 1st attempt: BHS 204-2nd attempt: PSY 204), then the Department Chair signature is **required**. After, the Department Chair's signature is obtained, you can then take the form to the Registrar's Office for processing.
* **NOTE:** You cannot complete a Grade Forgiveness through Course Repetition Form if you received a W in the course.

### I am currently taking a course and I would like to Withdraw, but the Withdrawal period to receive a W has already passed. At this point, what can I do to still receive a W in the course?

The Class Withdrawal form is now submitted via online on the [Registrar's website under Student Forms & Services](https://www.cpp.edu/registrar/forms/forms.shtml).

### I would like to take a Leave of Absence, how would I go about doing that?

The Leave of Absence is now submitted via online on the [Registrar's website under Student Forms & Services](https://www.cpp.edu/registrar/forms/forms.shtml).

### I would like to minor in Psychology. How do I obtain the minor?

See [Adding, Changing or Dropping a Minor](https://www.cpp.edu/studentsuccess/directories/declaring-a-minor.shtml)
