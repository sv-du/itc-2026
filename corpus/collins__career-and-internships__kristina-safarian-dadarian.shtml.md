# Kristina Safarian Dardarian

![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white-mobile.svg)
![](https://www.cpp.edu/common//green-and-gold/assets/images/linework-combo-offset-white.svg)

## Travel & Tourism

### Kristina's Journey

My journey in hospitality began with a deep passion for event planning, but I sought a more balanced career path. I transitioned into event and hotel sales, which led me to complete Marriott International’s Leadership Development Program and work with their hotels for five years. During the pandemic, I decided to go back to school to earn my MBA, aiming to pursue more strategic and business development roles. Today, I work in member engagement for PS, a private luxury terminal serving commercial flights at LAX and ATL. Each step of my journey has shaped my career and allowed me to blend my love for hospitality with strategic business growth.

![Kristina Dardarian](../img/kristina-safarian-dardarian.avif)

## In the Spotlight

### How did a Collins College degree help you in your career?

The Collins College provided invaluable resources both in and out of the classroom, propelling my career forward. The comprehensive curriculum, combined with world-class hospitality experience and a strong professional network, equipped me with skills to earn my MBA and excel in sales and strategic partnerships at a pioneering company reimagining the commercial experience.

### What do you love about working in hospitality?

I love working in hospitality because it allows me to combine my quantitative expertise with building meaningful client relationships. It's incredibly rewarding to collaborate with amazing people in a cutting-edge company, delivering exceptional experiences.

### How would you convince someone that the field of hospitality management is for them?

Hospitality management is an exciting field if you enjoy working with people and creating memorable experiences. With each day being unique, you will find real satisfaction in making a positive impact on others. Plus, the industry offers amazing opportunities to grow, move around and work in different parts of the world, giving you the chance to build a career anywhere you choose.

### Share a fun fact about yourself.

A fun fact about me is that I'm on track to walk 1,500 miles this year!
