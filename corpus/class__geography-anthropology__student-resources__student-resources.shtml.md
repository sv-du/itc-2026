## Welcome

We're thrilled you're interested in a **Geography** or **Anthropology** major!  Our dynamic programs offer a wide range of resources for current and prospective students, including information on **academics, career paths,** and **faculty**.  We invite you to explore all our department has to offer and join our community.

## Scholarships

[### Department Scholarship

The Department of Geography and Anthropology scholarship is generously supported by both the department and various donors who contribute to its funding.](#modal-0 " The Department of Geography and Anthropology Scholarship")

### The Department of Geography and Anthropology Scholarship

×

**Description**

Qualified students majoring in Geography or Anthropology (good academic performance, involvement in geography- or anthropology-related activities, any other facts about you that you want us to consider) are encouraged to apply.

**Criteria**

* Must be a Geography or an Anthropology major
* Must submit a current resume

For questions regarding the scholarship, please contact the Department Chair

Close

[### The Kun Chen Endowed Memorial

Kun Chen made a significant impact during her time as a Professor in the Department of Geography and Anthropology from 2011 to 2015. Her dedication to teaching and research left a lasting impression on students and colleagues alike](#modal-1 "The Kun Chen Endowed Memorial Scholarship")

### The Kun Chen Endowed Memorial Scholarship

×

**Description**

Dr. Kun Chen joined Cal Poly Pomona in September 2011 after receiving her Ph.D. from UC Berkeley. She instantly became one of the most popular professors among students. Her dissertation research on transnational Chinese entrepreneurs was a ground-breaking study bringing together issues in migration theory, cultural change, and emerging technologies.

Recipients will be awarded $500, distributed in two semesters one in Fall and one in Spring.

**Criteria**

* Anthropology major
* Minimum Cal Poly Pomona GPA of 3.25
* Resume
* Address how your study of anthropology will contribute to your immediate or long-range career plans
* Recipients must maintain full-time status to receive the award for the following academic year
* Previous recipients are eligible to apply

For questions, please contact the Department Chair.

To begin the application process, please complete the general Bronco Scholarship Application first then proceed to search this scholarship in the “Opportunities>>Recommended” Tab and APPLY

Close

[### Swenson Scholarship

The Wayne Abbott Swenson Scholarship is funded by an alumnus of the department](#modal-2 "The Wayne Abbott Swenson Scholarship")

### The Wayne Abbott Swenson Scholarship

×

**Description**

The Wayne Abbott Swenson Scholarship is for geography and anthropology majors with a minimum Cal Poly Pomona GPA of 3.25.

Recipients will be awarded $1000, distributed in two semesters in Fall and Spring. Recipients must maintain full-time status to receive the award. Previous recipients are eligible to apply.

**Criteria**

* Geography or Anthropology major
* Minimum Cal Poly GPA of 3.25
* Resume
* Recipient must maintain full-time status in the following academic year

For questions regarding the scholarship, please contact the Department Chair.

To begin the application process, please complete the general Bronco Scholarship Application first then proceed to search this scholarship in the “Opportunities>>Recommended” Tab and APPLY.
