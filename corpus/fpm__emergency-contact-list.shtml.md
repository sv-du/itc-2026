# Emergency Response

## FP&M Contact List

The monthly listing provides after-hours contact information for Facilities Planning & Management (FP&M) managers and University Police Department (UPD) to respond to non-routine and critical after-hours emergencies. In an emergency, call the University Police Department at Ext. 3070 or (909) 869-3070.
