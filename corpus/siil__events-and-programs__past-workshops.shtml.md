# Past Workshops and Webinars

## I-Startup Academy Webinars

[![iStartup Academy](images/optimized/wp1.png)

### iStartup Kickoff Fall 2021

Do you have an innovative idea that has the potential to change daily tasks or our way of life? Want to learn how to take that special idea and turn it into a reality? The Student Innovation Idea Labs (SIIL) wants you to be a part of our iStartup Academy! The iStartup Academy was created with the purpose of providing students with the training, resources, and connections to help you launch your idea into the marketplace.](https://streaming.cpp.edu/media/iStartup%2BKickoff%2BFall%2B2021/1_8rjaw4sy%20 "opens a new window")

![Thursday October 14th, 12-1pm](images/optimized/wp2.png)

### Mapping Value to Customer Needs

This workshop is designed to help you identify all the benefits your product or services have to offer. You will then identify your customer’s main problem your value proposition helps solve. By connecting this value to your buyer’s challenges, your value proposition will become clearer.

[![Presented by Dr. Olukemi Saywerr](images/optimized/wp3.png)

### Customer Discovery

The main objective of customer discovery is to help you identify your first customers by testing your assumptions about who your customer is, the exact problem you are solving for the customer, and how the customer will buy from you.](https://streaming.cpp.edu/media/Customer%2BDiscovery%2BWorkshop%2B-%2BSpring%2B22/1_iv3iezfz?st=0  "opens a new window")

![Thursday, November 4th from 12-1pm](images/optimized/wp4.png)

### Competitor Analysis

A competitive analysis is a strategy where you identify major competitors and research their products, sales, and marketing strategies in order to improve upon them and identify potential opportunities where you can out-perform them

[![Thursday November 11th from 12-1pm](images/optimized/wp5.png)

### Patents, Trademarks and Market Research

Interested in learning how to search for a patent or trademark using the US Patent and trademark office website? Want to learn how to conduct market research for your business or research project? If this is something you are curious about join us as we dive deeper into learning the fundamentals of patents, trademarks and market research.](https://streaming.cpp.edu/media/Patents%2C%2BTrademarks%2Band%2BMarket%2BResearch/1_qwypzggn?st=0  "opens a new window")

![Presented by Laura Pohopian](images/optimized/wp6.png)

### How To Effectively Pitch Your Business Idea

Join us as we teach you how to effectively prepare a pitch and sell your idea through the art of pitching. You will practice how to present your thoughts clearly and learn the do's and don'ts of pitching your idea to investors or in any business setting. Whether you have a business idea or thinking about launching a business this workshop is open to everyone!

[![Thursday September 8th, 2025](images/optimized/wp7.png)

### A Venture Capitalist View on Startups - Ray Chan

Come join the conversion as we invite a venture capitalist to talk about the different kinds of innovative ideas he enjoys working with. Ray Chan is an active angel investor with a successful 25 year track record as a king/queen maker, Ray is committed to partnering with entrepreneurs to lead them to success. Ray Chan is part of K5 Ventures - a group of experienced investors, entrepreneurs, and technologists that have successfully built and sold companies in southern California. K5 ventures is an early stage venture fund that partners with and invests in technology - driven businesses led by impact and growth focused on founding teams.](https://streaming.cpp.edu/media/K5%2BVentures%2B-%2BRay%2BChan/1_mpm0n81l?st=0  "opens a new window")

[![Guest speaker Jennifer Silbert](images/optimized/wp8.png)

### Clean Tech Energy Conversation with LA Clean Tech Incubator

Not sure what Clean Energy is all about? Want to learn how to start a Clean Tech Energy business? Are you interested in Clean Energy? Then this workshop is for you! Join us as we invite LA Clean Tech Incubator (LACI) introduce the conversation of Clean Tech Energy in our campus. This workshop will discuss everything you need to know about clean energy along with the different resources they have available so you can begin thinking about potential clean tech energy solutions.](https://streaming.cpp.edu/media/Clean%2BTech%2BEnergy%2B-%2BLACI/1_f2r9bsrw?st=0  "opens a new window")

## Entrepreneurial Series

[![Presented by Matt Ryczek](images/optimized/wp9.png)

### Part One

#### Startup: A Guide to Starting Anything

This workshop will introduce you to the foundations of entrepreneurship with an overview of the workshop series as well as the process of starting your own business.](https://streaming.cpp.edu/media/StartupA%2BA%2BGuide%2Bto%2BStarting%2BAnything/1_fpktixeh%20 "opens a new window")

[![Presented by Trayan Kushev](images/optimized/wp10.png)

### Part Two

#### Understanding the Business Model Canvas

In this workshop you will learn about the Business Model Canvas (BMC): a tool that will help you design your business models that have a competitive edge, leverage opportunities in your environment and test your ideas through experiments with customers.](https://streaming.cpp.edu/media/Understanding%2Bthe%2BBusiness%2BModel%2BCanvas/1_vuqsnhwo%20 "opens a new window")

[![presented by Deniz Bayramoglu](images/optimized/wp11.png)

### Part Three

#### Intellectual Property (IP) Workshop

The Intellectual Property (IP) workshop was created to provide you with knowledge about trademarks, patents, copyrights, Internet law, disputes, resolutions and much more in order to help you protect your most creative innovations.](https://streaming.cpp.edu/media/Intellectual%2BProperty%2BBoot%2BCamp%2B%28IP%29/1_dqaqxvct%20 "opens a new window")

[![Presented by Olukemi Sawyerr](images/optimized/wp12.png)

### Part Four

#### Customer Discovery - Part I

The main objective of customer discovery is to identify your first customers by testing your assumptions about who your customer is, the exact problem you are solving for the customer, and how the customer will buy from you.](https://streaming.cpp.edu/media/Customer%2BDiscovery%2B/1_iii5uec2%20 "opens a new window")

[![Presented by Edmond Wu](images/optimized/wp13.png)

### Part Five

#### Competitor Analysis

A competitive analysis is a strategy where you identify major competitors and research their products, sales, and marketing strategies in order to improve upon them and identify potential opportunities where you can out-perform them.](https://streaming.cpp.edu/media/Competitor%2BAnalysis/1_jzxdyguo%20 "opens a new window")

[![Presented by Dr. Nasatran](images/optimized/wp14.png)

### Part Six

#### Customer Discovery - Part II

During the second session of customer discovery, we will go more in-depth on how to conduct customer interviews and prepare questions to ask a potential customer.](https://streaming.cpp.edu/media/Customer%2BDiscovery%2BPart%2B2/1_xuqt7b9j%20 "opens a new window")

[![presented by Dr. Nastraran Simarasi](images/optimized/wp15.png)

### Part Seven

#### Crowdfunding

Crowdfunding is the process of raising money from a large number of people in order to fund a project, a company, or a cause. In this workshop, we talk about how successful crowdfunding campaigns are executed.](https://streaming.cpp.edu/media/Crowdfunding%2B/1_abjn52gw%20 "opens a new window")

[![presented by Dr. Denis Iuchenko](images/optimized/wp16.png)

### Part Eight

#### Building Your Pitch Deck

Building your Pitch Deck is a workshop on what you should include in your presentation to investors. This will cover the key information you need to have ready on the screen and in your presentation notes.](https://streaming.cpp.edu/media/Building%2BYour%2BPitch%2BDeck/1_alkeifpu%20 "opens a new window")

[![presented by Dr. Laura Pohopien](images/optimized/wp17.png)

### Part Nine

#### Pitching Your Idea to Investors

This workshop is designed to teach you how to prepare a pitch and sell your idea. You will practice how to present your thoughts clearly and learn the do's and don'ts of pitching your idea to investors!](https://streaming.cpp.edu/media/Pitching%2BYour%2BIdea%2Bto%2BInvestors/1_6mkc2fg2%20 "opens a new window")

[![Presented by Namakkal Balakrishman](images/optimized/wp18.png)

### Part Ten

#### The Phase Gate Process - New Product Introduction

The phase-gate process is ideal for projects that involve teams across multiple departments, and is typically used for new product developments, software/app/website launches and business-wide changes.](https://streaming.cpp.edu/media/The%2BPhase%2BGate%2BProcess%2B-%2BNew%2BProduct%2BIntroduction/1_mdykk2lv%20 "opens a new window")

### Past Event:

#### Pomona Innovation Weekend

Pomona Innovation Weekend was a two day, learn-by-doing campus workshop that taught new venture creation skills to university students in an extreme hands-on environment. This workshop allowed students to experience what it takes to turn a bright idea into a for-profit, nonprofit, or social venture.
