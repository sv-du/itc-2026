# Our Mission & Values

## Overview

We partner with you to navigate your university finances, offering guidance and flexible solutions tailored to your needs. Our team supports your success by assisting with tuition payments, payment plans, financial aid disbursements, and more, so you can stay focused on your education and worry less about the process.

We work closely with campus partners to ensure your account is managed responsibly and transparently. Our specialists are available for one-on-one consultations to help you explore options and better understand your financial responsibilities.

In addition to student services, we also support campus departments through guidance on e-Market processes and cash handling compliance.

From accepting your tuition payment to generating your IRS 1098-T tax form, our goal is to provide services that contribute to a financially informed and supportive university experience.

## Available Resources

[### Tuition & fee guidance

Paying for your undergraduate degree shouldn't be confusing. Visit the Cost of Attendance by Category page](../tuition/estimate-your-costs.shtml)

[### Payment plan support

CPP payment plans help you split tuition into interest-free installments. Whether you’re a student or an authorized user, you can enroll in a plan that fits your budget](../payment-options/cpp-payment-plans.shtml)

[### Refunds & financial aid disbursements

Students who withdraw from all classes may be eligible for a tuition and mandatory fee refund. Refund eligibility and amount depend on the date of withdrawal and are governed by CSU policy and Title 5 of the California Code of Regulations](../refund-and-holds/refund-policy.shtml)

[### 1098-T forms & tax-related questions

Form 1098-T is an annual IRS form completed by Cal Poly Pomona as required by the Taxpayer Relief Act of 1997, which is furnished to students to assist them in the preparation of their annual income tax returns.](1098-t-introduction-and-questions.shtml)

## How We Support Your Financial Success

### Our Mission

We're here to help you manage your university finances with clarity and confidence. From tuition payments and payment plans to financial aid disbursements and refunds, our team provides step-by-step support so you can stay focused on your education.
Our specialists are available for one-on-one consultations to help you understand your account, navigate payment options, and make informed decisions. We also work with departments across campus to ensure financial processes are accurate, secure, and student-friendly.
Whether you’re paying your first tuition bill or requesting your IRS 1098-T tax form, we're here to make sure your experience is smooth, transparent, and supportive.
