## Post-Award Forms

### Progress Reports

* [ARI Annual Assessment](https://californiastateuniversityari.formstack.com/forms/ari_annual_assessment_fy2024_25)
  + This is required for all ARI-funded projects. Yearly submission of an online ARI Annual Assessment is required for all projects. It is due on August 15 every year regardless of the project’s start date. The Project Director must complete the ARI Annual Assessment for each project. In the year the project is completed, in addition to the ARI Annual Assessment, a final report is due within 90 days after the project’s scheduled completion date.
* [Final Report](https://californiastateuniversityari.formstack.com/forms/ari_finalreport_fy24_25)
  + Required for all projects. Submission of a final report is required within 90 days after a project’s scheduled completion date.

### **No-Cost Extension**s

* [No-Cost Extension Request/Justification form](ari-system_no-cost-extension-request-form.pdf)
  + Requests for no-cost extensions must be submitted to Ondieki Gekara  ojgekara@cpp.edu and Wei Bidlack, wwbidlack@cpp.edu via email with an appropriate technical justification. No-cost extension requests must be submitted at least 30 days prior to the current project expiration date. No-cost extensions can be granted on a case-by-case basis for 1 year with a maximum of 2 per project.

### Budget Revisions

A budget revision is required if more than 10% of the total budget is being redirected. A project director is only allowed two budget revisions in one year. Please submit your budget revision to wwbidlack@cpp.edu. All budget revisions must be approved by the campus coordinator and then will be forwarded to Foundation.

Please submit the following:

* [Budget Revision Spreadsheet](../fy22-23/budgetrevisionspreadsheet_000.xls) - Please enter your current budget and your requested re-allocations.
* [Revised Budget Narrative](../fy22-23/revisedbudgetnarrative.docx) - Please describe how you are reallocating your funding.
