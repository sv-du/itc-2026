## Human Resources Policies

The employment terms and conditions, benefits and obligations of Cal Poly Pomona employees may be governed by an applicable collective bargaining agreement, laws at the federal, state or local level, and policies of Cal Poly Pomona or of the entire CSU system. [View the collective bargaining agreements](https://www.calstate.edu/csu-system/faculty-staff/labor-and-employee-relations/Pages/collective-bargaining-agreements.aspx).

The [Policies Checklist and University Resource Guide (PDF)](docs/policy-acknowledgment-checklist-april-2025-final.pdf) contains summaries and links to important work-related policies that new and existing employees need to know.

Below is a more extensive collection of work-related policies. Some are applicable to all CSU employees while others are campus-specific policies that only apply to Cal Poly Pomona employees. All CSU Employment policies can be found and searched through [The California State University PolicyStat](https://calstate.policystat.com/ "Opens in a new window") system.
