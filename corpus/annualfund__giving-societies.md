# Annual Giving & Stewardship

## Giving Societies

Cal Poly Pomona giving societies recognize the visionaries, leaders and loyal supporters whose generosity fuels progress and transforms lives. Whether through annual support, lifetime contributions or future commitments, these donors expand opportunity and enrich the Cal Poly Pomona experience.

When you join a giving society, you become part of a passionate community that champions Cal Poly Pomona’s mission. Your commitment advances the programs and priorities that matter most, and inspires others to give boldly and meaningfully.

If you are interested in becoming part of one of these societies or learning more, email donor\_relations@cpp.edu.

## Explore Our Giving Societies

### President’s Circle

The President’s Circle is a community of dedicated supporters who cumulatively give $2,500 or more annually, between July 1 and June 30, to Cal Poly Pomona. These leadership gifts provide flexible, immediate resources that drive academic excellence, fund student scholarships, power hands-on learning and respond to emerging university priorities. Members may direct their support to the college, program or scholarship that matters most — ensuring their gift reflects their values and passions. President’s Circle donors are essential partners in cultivating student success and strengthening the university’s day-to-day impact.

[Give Now to Join](https://www.cpp.edu/giving/cpp/)

### Kellogg Voorhis Legacy Society

The Kellogg Voorhis Legacy Society honors individuals who have included Cal Poly Pomona in their estate plans through a will, trust, retirement account, life insurance policy or designated or other planned gift. Inspired by university founders W.K. Kellogg and Charles B. Voorhis — whose [landmark gifts helped establish the campus](https://libguides.library.cpp.edu/c.php?g=686944&p=5013734) — the society recognizes donors whose lasting commitment strengthens Cal Poly Pomona’s future. Their generosity ensures that generations of students will continue to benefit from a hands-on education rooted in innovation, access and opportunity. Visit the [Planned Giving website](https://cpp.giftlegacy.com/) for more information.

### Founders’ Society

The Founders’ Society recognizes Cal Poly Pomona’s most generous donors — individuals and organizations whose lifetime contributions of $250,000 or more represent a deep and enduring commitment to the university. Named in honor of those who helped establish Cal Poly Pomona, Founders’ Society members shape the university’s growth, impact and future. Their generosity fuels bold initiatives, empowers students and advances the university’s ability to lead with vision and purpose.
