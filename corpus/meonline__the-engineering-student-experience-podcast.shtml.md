# The Engineering Student Experience Podcast

![microphone, soundwaves, headset](/meonline/img/the-engineering-student-experience-podcast-banner.png)

This podcast was started in 2018 to increase awareness of engineering as a major and a career option, allowing current and future students to take the necessary steps to enhance their success in school and beyond. Each episode consists of interviews with students, practicing engineers, and faculty members discussing topics that are relevant to high school and college students.
