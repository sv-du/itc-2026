# Lodging

## Where to Stay

There are many wonderful choices in selecting a hotel while you visit Cal Poly Pomona.

### Kellogg West Conference Center & Hotal

Conveniently located on the university campus. It is a limited-service hotel and conference facility located on a natural and tranquil tree-lined hilltop. Featuring well-appointed guest rooms, [Kellogg West](https://kelloggwest.com/) offers special rates for guests affiliated with the Cal Poly Pomona Campus. Accommodations include complimentary parking and daily brewed coffee and tea. Guest amenities include free high-speed internet access, in-room coffee, pool/Jacuzzi and cardio-fitness center.

For more information about Kellogg West and to make reservations, please call (800) 593-7876 or (909) 869-2222.

### Local Lodging (Google)
