## Research Interest

Dr. He received his Ph.D. in Operations from Weatherhead School of Management, Case Western Reserve University in 2005. He also received Master degree in System Engineering from Tianjin University in 2000. His main teaching interests are supply chain management related areas. His research areas are supply chain risk management and sustainable supply chain management. His work has appeared in journals such as *Organization Science*, *European Journal of Operational Research*, and *International Journal of Production Economics*.
