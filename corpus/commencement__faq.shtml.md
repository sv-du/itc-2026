# Frequently Asked Questions

Congratulations to Class of 2026!

## Student Information

Expand/Collapse All Student Information

### What terms are included in Commencement 2026?

Fall 2025, Spring and Summer 2026.

### When will I receive ceremony information?

All graduates, including Fall 2025, Spring and Summer 2026 will need to monitor their CPP email for all communications leading up to the event.  Communications will be shared monthly and move to bi-weekly in March.

### Where do I purchase my cap and gown, and other graduation items?

Graduation items will be available at Bronco Bookstore.  Online orders will begin in December.  In person shopping will begin at Grad Fair in March 2026.

### Where do I pick-up my Honor Cord?

Distribution will be in March 2026 at Grad Fair.  Qualifying students will be emailed prior to event.

### Where do international students request a letter of invitation for family to attend graduation in May?

Students can email Bronco Advising Center (bac@cpp.edu) for any letter requests.
