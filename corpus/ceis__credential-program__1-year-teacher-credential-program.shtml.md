# 1-Year Teacher Credential Program

There are two ways to obtain a Single Subject Credential in one year, one for Cal Poly Pomona undergraduates and one for all other students. Cal Poly Pomona undergraduates\* can begin taking credential courses in their undergraduate studies to earn a Single Subject Credential in 2 semesters with full-time attendance after completing their bachelor’s degree. Students with degrees from other universities can take all foundations courses through Summer Admission, followed by 2 semesters of full-time attendance. See below for more information.

\*Junior or senior standing

*Cal Poly Pomona undergraduates with junior or senior standing may take up to four credential foundation courses (up to 12 units). A maximum of 9 units may be petitioned to be applied towards graduate credit if the credential courses are taken with senior standing. Please see the [Graduate Credit Petition Form](https://www.cpp.edu/gradstudies/documents/graduate-credit-petition.pdf) for more information or visit the [Graduate Studies website](https://www.cpp.edu/gradstudies/forms-and-procedures/graduate-forms.shtml).**Credential foundation courses taken as an undergraduate count towards the credential regardless of how the units are applied: undergraduate credit or graduate credit.*

| **Completed in Summer School or as a CPP Undergraduate** | **Units** |
