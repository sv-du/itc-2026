# Curriculum Maps

Each course in the Master in Regenerative Studies (MSRS) program corresponds to particular program learning goals outlined in the program's [Student Learning Outcomes](learning-outcomes.shtml).

Learn about the graduate studies curriculum  **[here](ms-regenerative-studies.shtml "Masters in Regenerative Studies")** for details such as:

* The admissions process, including prerequisites and important information for international students
* Curriculum overview, including a list of core courses and first- and second-year roadmaps
* Registering for thesis or a final project
