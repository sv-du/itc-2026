# 2017 Zinfandel

![](https://www.cpp.edu/horsehill/img/2017-red-bottle.png)87% Zinfandel, 9% Petite Sirah, 3% Tempranillo, 1% Touriga Nacional

Bright berry fruit dominates the aromas and rich flavors of blackberry jam and coffee round out the palate. Aged in French and American oak barrels for 20 months, this Zinfandel offers classic structure and refrained finesse while delivering a berry scented treasure from the past. Cheers to you and Broncos everywhere!
