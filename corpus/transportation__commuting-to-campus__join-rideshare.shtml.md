# Join Rideshare

Joining Rideshare is quick and simple.  Registration forms are available from the Rideshare office.  Once the information has been verified and approved by the Rideshare office, a brief orientation is required to explain the benefits you are entitled to as a member of the program.  The office must have an original copy of the registration form.

#### Eligibility Requirements

Members must meet the following requirements to enroll in the program.

* State, ASI or Foundation employee, part time or more
* 12 month permanent or probationary status
* Temporary lecturers with one quarter appointment or more
* Temporary appointment with 90 day contract or more
* Temporary emergency with 90 day contract or more
* You must share the ride at least 51% or more of the total trip distance
* Arrive to work on campus between 6 a.m. to 10 a.m., Monday through Friday

Students are not eligible to join the program.
