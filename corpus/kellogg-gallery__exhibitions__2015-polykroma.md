# Polykroma 2015- Annual Senior Fine Arts Show &amp; Graphic Design Seniors Show

## Polykroma 2015- Annual Senior Fine Arts Show & Graphic Design Seniors Show

May 18, 2015 to Jun 14, 2015

**Location:** Kellogg University Art Gallery

Press the tab key to view the content. Use the down arrow key to move to the next tab and up arrow key to move to the previous.

[About the Exhibition](#tab-1)
[Program Schedule](#tab-2)
[Installation Views](#tab-3)

***![Polykroma](img/poly_kroma_0.png)PolyKroma 2015*,**celebrating Fine Arts, Art History and Graphic Design on campus with its ***2D3D Juried Art Exhibition***at **The Huntley Gallery**, AND the ***Fine Art Senior Shows***, ***Art History Papers Presentation***and ***Industry Night****-*the senior Graphic Design exhibition - presented concurrently at the **Kellogg University Art Gallery**. These exhibitions feature the "best of" Cal Poly Pomona's Fine Arts and Graphic Design Seniors. **Fine Art Seniors**are given the opportunity to exhibit a series of their artwork created during their university experience, while exposed to a gallery setting, and gaining professional art world practices in installing, exhibiting and pricing their artwork in a "real world" environment. ***Industry Night***is a special invitation to outside industry professionals to view graduating seniors' graphic design portfolios, projects, and motion graphics presentations with exposure to potential job opportunities being offered. Graduating **Art History**students are given the opportunity present their abstracts of their final thesis papers.
