## New Employee Onboarding

Welcome to Cal Poly Pomona! As a new or returning member to our campus, you will need to complete the new employee sign up process. This process is necessary to comply with federal and state laws, as well as to obtain the necessary information to put you on the University's payroll. The content on this page is intended to help guide you through the process and make it as convenient as possible. Welcome aboard and go Broncos!

## Employment Types

### Faculty, Teaching Associates and Graduate Assistants

Congratulations on your new position with Cal Poly Pomona! The HR Customer Service Center team will be assisting you through the hire process. Newly hired employees need to complete the onboarding forms and complete the I-9 process. This process is necessary to comply with federal and state laws, as well as to obtain the necessary information to place you on the University's payroll.

**Step #1:** Email a PDF or scanned copy of your Faculty or Lecturer contract to the HR Customer Service Center and mention “Faculty Contract” in the subject line.

**Step #2:** Gather important documents that you will need to complete your Human Resources Forms and the I-9 Process.

* Identification & eligibility documents required for completing the I-9 form. You will be uploading copies your documents to us through our secure link when completing the onboarding forms and you will need to bring the original documents with you to your in-person appointment. ([Reference page 3 of the I-9 form for a list of acceptable documents](https://www.uscis.gov/i-9))
* Social Security Card and additional identification & eligibility documents required for completing the I-9 form. This requirement is for verification of name and Social Security Number, and accurate payroll reporting under the Code of Federal Regulations and the US Code, the US Dept. of Labor and the IRS. If you do not have an original Social Security Card, you may request one from the Social Security Administration, please go to [Social Security number & card](https://www.ssa.gov/ssnumber/ "Opens in a New window") for additional information.

**Step #3:** Complete your Human Resources Forms. An email will be sent to you with a link that will guide you through the various steps of completing your forms. It is recommended that you navigate through this course while simultaneously completing your forms as the course provides helpful tips for completing and uploading the forms to our secure digital filing portal.

**Step #4:** Complete the I-9 process. This process will include an in-person appointment with an HR Customer Service Center team member to verify your documents. After booking your in-person I-9 appointment, check your confirmation email for important details about our location and steps that need to be completed prior to attending your appointment.

Should you have any questions, need assistance, or prefer an in-person onboarding experience, you may contact us by email at hrscsc@cpp.edu or by calling 909-869-3733.

### Student Assistants

Congratulations on your new position with Cal Poly Pomona! The HR Customer Service Center team will be assisting you through the hire process. Newly hired employees need to complete the onboarding forms and complete the I-9 process. This process is necessary to comply with federal and state laws, as well as to obtain the necessary information to place you on the University's payroll.

**Step #1:** Book a virtual appointment with the HR Customer Service Center to complete your onboarding process. Your appointment will be a combination of audio, video and screen sharing, please ensure your device is compatible. After your book your appointment, check your confirmation email for details how we will connect with you.

**Step #2:** Prior to attending your onboarding appointment, scan or take a picture of the necessary documents below that you will be presenting to us before your appointment and have these files saved to your computing device. During your appointment, we will provide you a link to upload these files to our secure document database.:

* Identification & eligibility documents required for completing the I-9 form. [Reference page 3 of the I-9 form](https://www.uscis.gov/i-9 "Opens in a new window") for a list of acceptable documents.
* Your Social Security Card and additional identification & eligibility documents required for completing the I-9 form. This requirement is for verification of name and Social Security Number, and accurate payroll reporting under the Code of Federal Regulations and the US Code, the US Dept. of Labor and the IRS. If you do not have an original Social Security Card, you may request one from the Social Security Administration, please to go [Social Security number & card](https://www.ssa.gov/ssnumber/ "Opens in a New window") for additional information.

**Step #3:**Book an in-person I-9 appointment as soon as possible to complete the process by coming into our office and showing us the I-9 documents that were discussed during the virtual new hire appointment. As an employer of the state, we are federally required by the Department of Homeland Security, to verify a hard-copy of your I-9 documents. Reference page 3 of the [I-9 Form (PDF)](https://www.uscis.gov/sites/default/files/document/forms/i-9-paper-version.pdf "Opens in a new window") for a “List of Acceptable Documents” provided by the U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services.

### MPPs and Support Staff

Congratulations on your new position with Cal Poly Pomona! Newly hired MPPs and Support Staff employees will be contacted by a member of our Talent Acquisition to start the onboarding process and complete all new hire forms in Adobe Sign prior to your first day.

### Setting up DUO

Protect your Cal Poly Pomona account security by installing DUO, our 2-Step Authentication, which will verify your identity before allowing access to campus systems. The CSU and Cal Poly Pomona are using the DUO application for the 2-Step Authentication process, also known as Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA). We recommend you download the mobile application and secure your account today.
