## About us

### Driven by Student Learning

The office of Academic Programs role is to support faculty in developing programs that are intentionally designed, driven by student learning, and committed to student success. By supporting departments’ course and curriculum design, administering academic policies, and engaging in robust program review and assessment, the Office maintains both the University’s Catalog and WSCUC accreditation.

## Generative AI in the Classroom

### Emerging Technologies

Emerging technologies like Generative AI (gAI) are transforming education, offering new ways to engage students, enhance learning experiences, and develop future-ready skills. The Generative AI Course Integration Grant, funded by the Kellogg Legacy Endowment, supports tenured and tenure-track faculty in integrating AI into new or existing courses.
