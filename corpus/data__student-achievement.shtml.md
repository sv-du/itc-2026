### Student Success

Graduation Initiative 2025 is the California State University (CSU) system institutional initiative to increase graduation rates for all CSUs by 2025. Each campus has its own set of goals and strategies to encourage innovation and student success. Here we show progress towards targets for graduation rates of traditional freshmen.

## Reports

### Degrees Conferred

Cal Poly Pomona reports degrees conferred information on an annual basis. Since 1956-1957 through Summer 2024, over 201,000 bachelor's degrees, master's degrees, and doctorates have been awarded.

[Degree Trends Dashboard](dashboards/degrees.shtml "opens a new window")
