## Wi-Fi Setup

### Connecting your Device

Cal Poly Pomona offers a setup wizard for registering your Windows, Mac, Chromebook, iOS, or Android device for the eduroam network; your device will also have access from all other participating locations off campus.

[Wi-Fi Setup Wizard](https://cloud.securew2.com/public/93050/eduroam%20)

![person working at a laptop](../img/2019-library-study.jpg)

## Campus Wi-Fi for All Your Devices

Cal Poly Pomona (CPP) uses eduroam for Wi-Fi and internet access. We encourage you to prepare all your devices, including tablets, laptops, and computers, from home before arriving; this allows them to connect to Wi-Fi automatically when on campus.

For our guests without a BroncoAccount, please visit our [CPPGuest guide](https://cpp.service-now.com/ehelp?id=search&spa=1&q=CPPGuest) for your device.
