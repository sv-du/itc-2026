# Alumni Spotlight

The chemical and materials engineering department's alumni are a diverse group of accomplished engineers. From working in industry to researching new and innovative chemical and material compisitions that better the world, check out just a few of the thousands of our alumni.

## Hey, alumni!

We'd love to include you on this page! Fill out this [short questionnaire](https://cpp.formstack.com/forms/alumni_spotlight) and we'll add your spotlight to this page.
