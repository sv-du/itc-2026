# AY 2024-2025 Events

### Oct 09 2025

All Member Meeting

***Location**: TBA
**Registration**: Members will receive an "All-Member Meeting" registration email.*

11:30 AM

### Oct 15 2025

Lecturer Essentials

This workshop covers entitlement, reappointment, one- and three-year appointments, and evaluation. This is a great way to understand your rights as a lecturer so you can advocate for them.

***Location**: Library
**Registration**: Forthcoming for CFA members*

1:00 PM

### Oct 30 2025

Lecturer Lunch

> This is an informal gathering in support of lecturers and to continue strengthening our community. Please come, meet your colleagues, and ask any questions you may have.  Food will be provided.
>
> ***Location**: Kellogg West
> **Registration**: Forthcoming for CFA members*

11:30 AM

### Nov 17 2025

Lecturer Essentials (Zoom)

> This workshop covers entitlement, reappointment, one- and three-year appointments, and evaluation. This is a great way to understand your rights as a lecturer so you can advocate for them.
>
> ***Location**: Zoom / online
> **Registration**: Forthcoming for CFA members*

6:00 PM

### Nov 06 2025

CFA - Dept Rep Meeting

Department representative meetings allows departments to directly connect with CFA leaderships, voice concerns, receive updates, and coordinate action across campus.  If you would like to attend, contact us to become one of your department's CFA representatives.

***Date & Time**: First Thursday of the Month at 12pm*

***Registration**: Forthcoming for CFA Depts Reps*
