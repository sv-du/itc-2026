# Student Accounting & Cashiering Services

![The Student Services Building at Cal Poly Pomona, featuring a distinctive curved roof and glass exterior under a partly cloudy sky](img/ssb-750-500.jpg)

## ‎About Us

Welcome to Student Accounting & Cashiering Services. We help you manage your financial responsibilities while attending Cal Poly Pomona. Our goal is to make your financial journey clear and stress-free. From accepting tuition payments to helping you understand your financial aid disbursements, we’re here to support you every step of the way.

At Student Accounting & Cashiering Services, we partner with you to navigate your university finances by offering guidance, flexible solutions, and one-on-one support tailored to your needs. We collaborate closely with other campus departments to ensure your account is managed responsibly and transparently, so you can stay focused on your education and worry less about the process.

Use the quick links below to take action or explore our most popular resources.

## How We Help

### SACS Makes It Easy

Student Accounts & Cashiering Services (SACS) is your go-to resource for managing your student account and making the financial side of college easier to understand. We help students view and pay tuition and fees, understand their account balance, set up flexible payment plans, enroll in direct deposit for faster refunds, resolve holds that could impact enrollment, and access tax forms, billing details, and step-by-step guides.
If you ever have questions about your account, you’re not alone. We're here to help.

## FAQs

Managing your student account can raise a lot of questions, especially around payments, holds, refunds, and financial aid. We have gathered the most commonly asked questions to help you better understand your student account, payment options, policies, and support services. Use the list below to find quick answers and helpful resources for managing your finances at Cal Poly Pomona.

### When is the bill due?

Your tuition and mandatory fee payment deadline will now be based on when you register for a specific term. Please see below for Spring 2026 specific deadlines:

Due Date Schedule

| **If you register for Spring 2026 between:** | **Your fees are due:** |
| --- | --- |
| October 13th  - December 3rd, 2025 | December 4, 2025 |
| December 4, 2025 - January 14, 2026 | January 15, 2026 |
| January 15 - January 30, 2026 | Immediately after you register |

### How do I make a payment?

Paying for your education should be clear and convenient. Whether you’re paying online, using a college savings plan, or receiving financial aid, this page will guide you through the options available at Cal Poly Pomona. See [Payment Options.](payment-options/how-to-pay.shtml "Payment Options")

### What happen if I do not pay my bill by the due date?

If you do not pay your bill by the due date, your enrolled classes may be subject to **cancellation for non-payment**. A hold may also be placed on your account, which can prevent you from registering for future terms or receiving your diploma. Late fees may apply.

To avoid these consequences, be sure to pay your bill on time or consider enrolling in a **payment plan** through Student Accounting & Cashiering Services.

### How do I change my mailing address?

You can update your mailing address through your **[BroncoDirect](https://my.cpp.edu/ "BroncoDirect Module") Student Center**. Here’s how:

1. Log in to your BroncoDirect Student Center.
2. Go to the **Personal Information** section.
3. Click on **Addresses**.
4. Select **Mailing Address** and click **Edit** to update it.

Be sure to save your changes. Let us know if you have any trouble!

### Where can I check my due date?

You can check your due dates by logging in to your **[BroncoDirect](https://my.cpp.edu/ "BroncoDirect Module") Student Center**:

1. Go to BroncoDirect.
2. Click on **Student Center**.
3. Under the **Finance** section, select **Account Inquiry**.
4. Then click on the **Activity** or **Charges Due** tab to see your due dates and amounts.
