# Ahimsa Center Nonviolence in Thought and Action

![civil disobedience-A massive crowd of people, all facing forward, fills a wide city street between rows of buildings. The photo is in black and white, and the scene stretches into the hazy distance, giving a sense of scale and density.](img/civil-disobedience-movement-coverresized.jpg)

## Welcome

*Ahimsa connotes reverence for all life. It is nonviolence rooted in courage and compassion, fearlessness, and forgiveness. It evokes civility and trust and promotes among all a sense of oneness and amity leading to social justice, lasting peace, and ecological sustainability*.
