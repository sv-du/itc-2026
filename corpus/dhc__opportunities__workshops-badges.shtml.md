# Workshops & Badges

## DHC Professional Development Badges

Digital badges are a great way to demonstrate your knowledge and skills in emerging fields. By earning these badges, you gain credentials that highlight your expertise in podcasting and artificial intelligence.

![katie-podcasting-2-27](../img/2025-02-27-dhc-katie-podcasting101.jpg)

## Available Badges

![podcasting](../img/podcasting-badge.jpg)

### Podcasting

A growing number of people, worldwide, get their news and entertainment from audio and visual sources. Learn the secrets of how to get started in podcasting.
To earn this badge, you must attend a certified Podcasting workshop covering the fundamentals of podcasting, from developing a series or episode, to tips on content scripting and/or storyboarding, recording, and an introduction to resources to get started.

![AI literacy badge](../img/ai-literacy-badge.png)

### A.I. Literacy

Artificial Intelligence is a rapidly evolving field. This badge is awarded to participants in a workshop that explains foundational A.I. concepts and applications, discusses ethical and societal implications, and considers how A.I. is shaping work.
This badge is awarded to individuals who complete an A.I. Literacy workshop covering core definitions of Artificial Intelligence with a particular focus on generative A.I.; a brief look at the history of A.I., current tools, and future directions; and discusses specific case study-based examples to help consider both utility and ethical considerations. Workshops may include an introduction to prompt engineering or other applied examples.
