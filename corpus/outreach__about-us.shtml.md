# About Us

## We're Here to Help Future Broncos

![Student smiles during Orientation](img/23open-house_15-1.gif)

The Office of Outreach, Recruitment and Educational Partnerships connects with future Broncos and provides you the guidance needed to succeed in your academic journey. Through our office you can:

* Tour campus
* Receive pre-admission advising
* Experience events for prospective and admitted students

If you are a counselor, teacher or administrator, we encourage you to:

* Take part in one of our conferences and events
* Connect with one of our outreach representatives to schedule a visit to your institution

## Mission and Vision

Our mission is to connect with prospective students, families and educational partners to cultivate an inclusive and welcoming campus community. We strive to expand access to higher education, promote academic excellence, build meaningful partnerships, and offer comprehensive support services to ensure the success and growth of every student.

Our vision is for a diverse and dynamic university where every prospective student has the opportunity to thrive. We aspire to be a leader in fostering an inclusive campus culture, breaking down barriers to higher education, and nurturing lifelong academic and personal growth. Through innovative outreach, strategic partnerships and unwavering support, we empower our next generation of learners and leaders to become by doing.
